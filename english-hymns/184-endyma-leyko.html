184 ENDYMA LEYKO

Greek Hymns - No. 184 | "ΕΝΔΥΜΑ ΛΕΥΚΟ"
Verse 1:
Endyma lefko, organo chryso,
(Garment white, instrument gold,)
ena palati, ena stefano,
(a palace, a crown,)
alithini chara, ouranio thisafro
(true joy, heavenly treasure)
etoimas' o Christos mas!
(prepare our Christ!)
Einai Sotiras mou, m' eleftherose,
(He is my Savior, set me free,)
pliros' akriva gia mena sto Stafro,
(pay dearly for me on the Cross,)
mesa stin agapi Tou me odigise
(in His love he led me)
kai tora zo sto fos Tou!
(and now I live in His light!)
Chorus:
Kai vadizo, vadizo eftychis!
(And I walk, I walk happy!)
Vadizo eftychis, vadizo eftychis!
(I walk happy, I walk happy!)
Kai vadizo, vadizo eftychis!
(And I walk, I walk happy!)
Vadizo pros Afton ston ourano!
(I walk to Him in heaven!)
Verse 2:
Tin Kyriaki eftychis!
(Happy Sunday!)
Deftera me chara!
(Happy Monday!)
Tin Triti echo pantote
(I always have Tuesday)
eirini stin kardia!
(peace in the heart!)
Tetarti olimeris
(Wednesday all day)
kai Pempti zo sto fos!
(and Thursday I live in the light!)
Paraskefi kai Savvato
(Friday and Saturday)
sti gi ein' o ouranos!
(on earth is heaven!)
