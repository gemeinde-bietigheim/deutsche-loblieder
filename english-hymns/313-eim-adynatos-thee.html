313 EIM' ADYNATOS, ThEE

Greek Hymns - No. 313 | "ΕΙΜ' ΑΔΥΝΑΤΟΣ, ΘΕΕ"
Verse 1:
Eim' adynatos, Thee,
(I'm skinny, God)
Sy 'sai dynami gia me,
(You are strength for me,)
krata me, parakalo,
(hold me please)
panta me Se na perpato!
(always with You to walk!)
Chorus:
Pos potho na prochoro
(How I long to move forward)
sti odo me Se, Christe,
(on the street with You, Christ,)
Se mono n' akoloutho
(I'll just follow you)
ferontas pantoy to stafro!
(carrying the cross everywhere!)
Verse 2:
Thlipseis an tha synanto,
(Sorrows if I meet,)
poion tha epikalestho;
(who shall I invoke?)
Mono Sy, o Lytrota,
(Only You, O Redeemed One,)
me lytroneis ap' ta desma!
(you free me from the shackles!)
Verse 3:
Dose mou ti dynami
(Give me the strength)
tou Agiou Pneymatos,
(of the Holy Spirit,)
pantote gia se na zo
(always for you to live)
olo chari ki agiasmo!
(all grace and sanctification!)
Verse 4:
Ki otan pleon afichtho
(And when I arrive now)
ston oraio ourano,
(in the beautiful sky)
Sy, Christe, tha m' odigeis
(You, Christ, will lead me)
se piges aionias zois!
(to fountains of eternal life!)
