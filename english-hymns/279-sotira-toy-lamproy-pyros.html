279 SOTIRA TOY LAMPROY PYROS

Greek Hymns - No. 279 | "ΣΩΤΗΡΑ ΤΟΥ ΛΑΜΠΡΟΥ ΠΥΡΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Sotira tou lamproy pyros,
(Savior of the bright fire,)
steile pyr, theio pyr, steile pyr!
(send fire, god fire, send fire!)
Esy mas eferes sto fos,
(You brought us into the light,)
steile pyr, theio pyr, steile pyr!
(send fire, god fire, send fire!)
Elthe kai vlepse eis emas,
(Come and see us,)
zitoyme theia dorea
(we ask for divine donation)
gia eftychia kai chara,
(for happiness and joy,)
steile pyr, theio pyr, steile pyr!
(send fire, god fire, send fire!)
Verse 2:
O! tou Ilia o Theos,
(Oh! of Elias the God,)
steile pyr, theio pyr, steile pyr!
(send fire, god fire, send fire!)
Afto na m' odigei sto fos,
(May this lead me to the light,)
steile pyr, theio pyr, steile pyr!
(send fire, god fire, send fire!)
Na katakaysei to kako,
(To burn the evil,)
na ferei ouranio thisafro
(to bring heavenly treasure)
kai ti chara ap' ton ourano,
(and joy from heaven,)
steile pyr, theio pyr, steile pyr!
(send fire, god fire, send fire!)
Verse 3:
To Pneyma sou zitoym' emeis,
(We ask for your Spirit,)
steile pyr, theio pyr, steile pyr!
(send fire, god fire, send fire!)
Na mas fylaei sti zoi,
(To guard us in life,)
steile pyr, theio pyr, steile pyr!
(send fire, god fire, send fire!)
Na oplisthoyme me Afto,
(To arm ourselves with It,)
n' antistathoyme ston echthro,
(to resist the enemy,)
na martyroyme to Christo,
(to bear witness to Christ,)
steile pyr, theio pyr, steile pyr!
(send fire, god fire, send fire!)
