295 NAI, PROThYMOS PARADINO

Greek Hymns - No. 295 | "ΝΑΙ, ΠΡΟΘΥΜΩΣ ΠΑΡΑΔΙΝΩ"
Verse 1:
Nai, prothymos paradino ti zoi mou sto Christo!
(Yes, I willingly surrender my life to Christ!)
Dia Afton thelo na ziso fernontas psyches s' Afton!
(Through Him I want to live bringing souls to Him!)
Chorus:
Paradinomai, Iisoy Christe!
(I surrender, Jesus Christ!)
Olokliros eis Esena paradinomai!
(I surrender completely to You!)
Verse 2:
Iisoy mou, paradino ola tapeina se Se!
(My Jesus, I surrender everything humbly to You!)
Kosmou idoni afino, gia na zo pleon gia Se!
(World pleasure I leave, to live now for You!)
Verse 3:
Paradinomai, Christe mou, eis Esena entelos!
(I surrender, my Christ, to You completely!)
Me to Pneyma plirose me, gia na martyrei entos!
(With the Spirit fill me, to testify within!)
Verse 4:
Paradino sto Christo mou soma, pneyma kai psychi!
(I surrender my body, spirit and soul to Christ!)
Dos' agapi stin kardia mou, plirose me dynami!
(Give love to my heart, pay with strength!)
Verse 5:
Paradinomai, Sotira, ti chara aisthanomai!
(I surrender, Saviour, what joy I feel!)
Dexou oli ti kardia mou, os thysia dia Se!
(Accept my whole heart, as a sacrifice for You!)
