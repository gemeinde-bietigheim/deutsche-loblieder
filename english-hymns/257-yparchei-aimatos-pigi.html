257 YPARChEI AIMATOS PIGI

Greek Hymns - No. 257 | "ΥΠΑΡΧΕΙ ΑΙΜΑΤΟΣ ΠΗΓΗ"
Verse 1:
Yparchei aimatos pigi ek ton flevon Christoy,
(There is a fountain of blood from the veins of Christ,)
kathe kilida tha svisthei,
(every stain shall be blotted out,)
ama loustheis aftoy!
(if you wash it!)
ama loustheis aftoy!
(if you wash it!)
ama loustheis aftoy!
(if you wash it!)
Kathe kilida tha svisthei, ama loustheis aftoy!
(Every stain will be erased if you bathe in this!)
Verse 2:
O epi tou Stafroy listis agnistike ki ego,
(The thief on the Cross purified himself, too,)
ki an molyntho, pali mporo
(and if I get infected, I still can)
amesos n' agnistho!
(I will immediately confess!)
amesos n' agnistho!
(I will immediately confess!)
amesos n' agnistho!
(I will immediately confess!)
Ki an molyntho, pali mporo amesos n' agnistho!
(And if I get infected, I can immediately cleanse myself again!)
Verse 3:
I theia chari kai eme stin idia tin pigi
(Divine grace and me at the very source)
odigise kai ektote
(has driven ever since)
ymno to Lytroti!
(I praise the Redeemer!)
ymno to Lytroti!
(I praise the Redeemer!)
ymno to Lytroti!
(I praise the Redeemer!)
Odigise kai ektote ymno to Lytroti!
(He led and I have been praising the Redeemer ever since!)
Verse 4:
Otan stou tafou ti sigi to soma mou tha mpei,
(When in the silent grave my body shall enter,)
to pneyma mou stous ouranoys
(my spirit in the heavens)
aionia tha ymnei!
(forever shall he praise!)
aionia tha ymnei!
(forever shall he praise!)
aionia tha ymnei!
(forever shall he praise!)
To pneyma mou stous ouranoys aionia tha ymnei!
(My spirit in the heavens forever shall praise!)
