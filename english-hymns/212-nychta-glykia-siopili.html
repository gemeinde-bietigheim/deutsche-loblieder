212 NYChTA GLYKIA, SIOPILI

Greek Hymns - No. 212 | "ΝΥΧΤΑ ΓΛΥΚΙΑ, ΣΙΩΠΗΛΗ"
Verse 1:
Nychta glykia, siopili, prosdoka ol' i gi...
(Sweet, silent night, all the earth waits...)
Mes sti fatni gennietai ftochos
(He is born poor in the manger)
o Sotiras tou kosmou, o Christos!
(the Savior of the world, Christ!)
Egennithi Christos! Egennithi Christos!
(Christ is born! Christ is born!)
Verse 2:
Nychta glykia, siopili, aggelos omilei
(Night sweet, silent, angel speaks)
se voskoys pou poimenoun makria
(to shepherds far away)
aggelia ti charopoia!
(happy ad!)
Egennithi Christos! Egennithi Christos!
(Christ is born! Christ is born!)
Verse 3:
Nychta glykia, siopili, endoxi, foteini,
(Night sweet, silent, glorious, bright,)
psalloun aggeloi ston ourano
(angels are singing in heaven)
eis ti gi chara kai sto lao!
(joy to the earth and to the people!)
Egennithi Christos! Egennithi Christos!
(Christ is born! Christ is born!)
Verse 4:
Nychta glykia, siopili, tis gis olis chara,
(Night sweet, silent, of all the joy of the earth,)
astro feggei sta skoti tis gis,
(a star shines in the darkness of the earth,)
aggeloi psalloun sto Lytroti!
(angels sing to the Redeemer!)
Chairei oli i gi! Chairei oli i gi!
(All the earth rejoices! All the earth rejoices!)
