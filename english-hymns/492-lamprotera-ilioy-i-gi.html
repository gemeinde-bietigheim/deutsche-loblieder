492 LAMPROTERA ILIOY I GI

Greek Hymns - No. 492 | "ΛΑΜΠΡΟΤΕΡΑ ΗΛΙΟΥ Η ΓΗ"
Verse 1:
Lamprotera iliou i gi,
(The earth is brighter than the sun,)
pou me pisti thoroyme makria,
(which with faith we cast away,)
lefkoforon aggelon moni,
(white-robed angels monastery,)
kosmimeni me theia chara!
(adorned with divine joy!)
Chorus:
Nai ekei! Nai ekei!
(Yes there! Yes there!)
Tha synantithoyme oloi ekei!
(We'll all meet there!)
O, patrida ourania
(Oh, heavenly homeland)
doxasmeni kai trispothiti!
(glorified and three-pointed!)
Verse 2:
Filous posous tha doyme ekei
(How many friends will we see there?)
pou epigan eis ton ourano!
(who went to heaven!)
Asma psalloun to neo glyky
(Song they sing the new sweet)
anymnontas ekei ton Amno!
(praising the Lamb there!)
Verse 3:
O Sotiras prosmenei ekei
(The Savior waits there)
ki adelfoi mas poly pothitoi,
(and our brothers much desired,)
pote arage thelo vrethei
(when do I want to be found?)
kai ego met' afton asfalis!
(and I am safe with them!)
Verse 4:
San to dromo mou trexo edo,
(As my way I run here,)
me ekeinous tha synantitho
(with them I will meet)
kai tous pothous mou olous tha do,
(and all my desires I will see,)
aionios sti doxa tha zo!
(forever in glory will I live!)
