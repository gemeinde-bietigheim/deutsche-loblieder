45 O KYRIE MOY, POSO SE ThAYMAZO

Greek Hymns - No. 45 | "Ω ΚΥΡΙΕ ΜΟΥ, ΠΟΣΟ ΣΕ ΘΑΥΜΑΖΩ"
Verse 1:
O Kyrie mou, poso Se thafmazo
(Oh my Lord, how I admire You)
ki ekstatikos ta erga Sou koito!
(and ecstatic I look at Your works!)
Vlepo to fos, ton ilio kai t' asteria,
(I see the light, the sun and the stars,)
pou Sy eskorpises ston ourano!
(that you scattered in the sky!)
Chorus:
Tot' i psychi mou psallei kai ymnei
(Then my soul sings and praises)
ti doxa Sou ti thafmasti!
(Thy glory the admirable!)
Kai mes' ap' tin kardia mou tapeina
(And from the bottom of my heart humbly)
S' efcharisto gia ola afta!
(Thank you for all this!)
Verse 2:
Otan koito ta plati tou pelagou,
(When I look at the backs of the ocean,)
ta ori ta psila, tous kerafnoys,
(the high mountains, the lightning,)
megaloprepeia eisai ntymenos
(magnificence thou art clothed)
kai kyvernas Esy tous ouranoys!
(and You rule the heavens!)
Verse 3:
Ki otan skeftho oti Esy, Thee mou,
(And when I think that You, my God,)
gia me ton athlio ki amartolo
(for the wretched and sinful)
to Mono Sou Yio den elypitheis,
(Your Only Son you do not pity,)
alla Ton eides pano sto Stafro!
(but you saw Him on the Cross!)
Verse 4:
San thymitho pos o Sotiras tha 'rthei
(As if I remember that the Savior will come)
na me arpaxei gia ton ourano
(grab me for heaven)
ki ekei mprosta sto throno tou Patera
(and there before the Father's throne)
dikaios ki agios tha parastatho!
(righteous and holy I will appear!)
