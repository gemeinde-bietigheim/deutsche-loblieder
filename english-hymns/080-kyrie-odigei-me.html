80 KYRIE, ODIGEI ME

Greek Hymns - No. 80 | "ΚΥΡΙΕ, ΟΔΗΓΕΙ ΜΕ"
Verse 1:
Kyrie, odigei me, krata to cheri mou na prochoro.
(Lord, lead me, hold my hand to go forward.)
Dose mou dynami kai anazopyrosi na S' akoloutho!
(Give me strength and revival to follow You!)
Verse 2:
To Pneyma t' Agio, Afto tha m' odigei pros to Christo.
(The Holy Spirit, This will lead me to Christ.)
Mono s' Afton konta kai eis to Logo Tou vrika chara!
(Only near Him and in His Word did I find joy!)
Verse 3:
Mesa sto Logo Sou oi yposcheseis Sou einai pistes.
(In Your Word Your promises are faithful.)
Gi' afto ki ego, Christe, panta sto dromo Sou tha zo m' aftes!
(That's why I too, Christ, will always live on Your path with them!)
Verse 4:
S' efcharisto, Christe, s' efcharisto, Christe, giati se Se
(I thank you, Christ, I thank you, Christ, because to You)
vrika o,ti potho kai Se efgnomono, Agapite!
(I have found what I desire and I thank You, Beloved!)
Verse 5:
Ki otan ston ourano tha fthaso asfalos, tote, Christe,
(And when I will surely reach heaven, then, Christ,)
tha zo panta me Se sti doxa tou Patros, Agapite!
(I will always live with You in the glory of the Father, Beloved!)
