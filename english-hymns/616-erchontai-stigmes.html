616 ERChONTAI STIGMES

Greek Hymns - No. 616 | "ΕΡΧΟΝΤΑΙ ΣΤΙΓΜΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Erchontai stigmes sto diava tis zois,
(There are moments in life,)
pou ola gyro feygoun chanontai.
(that everything around leaves is lost.)
Ametrites fores s' Esena erchomai
(Countless times I come to You)
kai Sou zito me mia prosefchi.
(and I ask You with a prayer.)
Chorus:
Nai, dos' mou dynami, dynami,
(Yes, give me strength, strength,)
otan vretho mesa stin mpora,
(when I'm in the rain,)
san erthei i dyskoli i ora,
(when the hard time comes,)
na kratitho gera ap' to logo Sou,
(to hold fast to Your word,)
mechri tin teleftaia mou pnoi!
(until my last breath!)
Verse 2:
Erchontai stigmes sto diava tis zois,
(There are moments in life,)
pou to karavi kymata chtypoyn.
(where the ship waves hit.)
Ametrites fores ta gonata lygoyn,
(Countless times the knees bend,)
kai Sou psithyrizo ap' tin psychi.
(and I whisper to You from the soul.)
