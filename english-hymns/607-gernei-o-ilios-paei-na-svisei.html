607 GERNEI O ILIOS PAEI NA SVISEI

Greek Hymns - No. 607 | "ΓΕΡΝΕΙ Ο ΗΛΙΟΣ ΠΑΕΙ ΝΑ ΣΒΗΣΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Gernei o ilios, paei na svisei piso ap' ta psila vouna
(The sun is setting, going to disappear behind the high mountains)
kai i thalassa chrysizei, isychasan ta kymata!
(and the sea turns golden, the waves calmed down!)

Chorus:
Vlepo s' ola afta ti doxa,
(I see the glory in all this,)
Thee, kai ti sofia Sou!
(God, and Your wisdom!)
Ki i kardia mou gonatizei
(And my heart is on its knees)
mprosta stin parousia Sou!
(before Your presence!)
Verse 2:
Gernei o ilios, paei na svisei ki eisai tora edo xana,
(The sun goes down, it goes out and now you're here again,)
kati mesa fterougizei me tis eirinis ta ftera!
(something inside is fluttering with wings of peace!)
