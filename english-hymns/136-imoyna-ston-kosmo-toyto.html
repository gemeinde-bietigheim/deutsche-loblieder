136 IMOYNA STON KOSMO TOYTO

Greek Hymns - No. 136 | "ΗΜΟΥΝΑ ΣΤΟΝ ΚΟΣΜΟ ΤΟΥΤΟ"
Verse 1:
Imouna ston kosmo toyto dystychis ki amartolos,
(I was in this world unhappy and sinful,)
eicha thlipsi stin kardia mou, alla m' esose o Christos!
(I had sorrow in my heart, but Christ saved me!)
Eicha thlipsi stin kardia mou, alla m' esose o Christos!
(I had sorrow in my heart, but Christ saved me!)
Verse 2:
M' esose kai echo ginei filos Tou pantotinos.
(He saved me and I have become His friend forever.)
T' onoma Tou tha doxazo sti gi kai ston ourano!
(I will glorify His name on earth and in heaven!)
T' onoma Tou tha doxazo sti gi kai ston ourano!
(I will glorify His name on earth and in heaven!)
Verse 3:
Kathe mera pou pernaei, kathe ora kai lepto
(Every passing day, every hour and minute)
o Christos ein' odigos mou kai Afton th' akoloutho!
(Christ is my guide and I will follow Him!)
O Christos ein' odigos mou kai Afton th' akoloutho!
(Christ is my guide and I will follow Him!)
Verse 4:
Tora pia den echo fovous oyte thlipseis stin kardia!
(Now I have no more fears or sorrows in my heart!)
O Christos m' echei lytrosei! Alliloyia, ti chara!
(Christ has redeemed me! Hallelujah, what a joy!)
O Christos m' echei lytrosei! Alliloyia, ti chara!
(Christ has redeemed me! Hallelujah, what a joy!)
Verse 5:
File mou, tora ki esena o Christos se proskalei
(My friend, now Christ is inviting you too)
na se plynei, na se sosei, na se kanei eftychi!
(to wash you, to save you, to make you happy!)
Na se plynei, na se sosei, na se kanei eftychi!
(To wash you, to save you, to make you happy!)
