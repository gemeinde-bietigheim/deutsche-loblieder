663 FOS TOY OYRANOY

Greek Hymns - No. 663 | "ΦΩΣ ΤΟΥ ΟΥΡΑΝΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Fos tou ouranoy, irthes mes sto skotadi,
(Light of heaven, you came into the darkness,)
ta matia mou anoixes!
(you opened my eyes!)
Tin omorfia sou i kardia mou echei niosei.
(My heart has felt your beauty.)
Elpida gia mia zoi!
(Hope for a lifetime!)
Chorus:
Irtha na latreyso! Na se proskyniso!
(I came to worship! To worship you!)
«O Theos mou eisai!», na sou po.
("You are my God!", let me tell you.)
Eisai o,ti oraio! Eisai oti axizei!
(You are everything nice! You are worth it!)
Eisai Esy gia mena Thafmastos!
(You are amazing to me!)
Verse 2:
O Vasilias ston psilotero throno!
(The King on the highest throne!)
O Endoxos t' ouranoy!
(The Glorious One of Heaven!)
Irthes sti gi, tapeinothikes toso
(You came to earth, you were so humbled)
ki edeixes pos agapoyn!
(and you showed how they love!)
Verse 3:
Ma den mporo na fantasto
(But I can't imagine)
to kostos pou eiche o stafros! (2)
(the cost of the cross! (2))
