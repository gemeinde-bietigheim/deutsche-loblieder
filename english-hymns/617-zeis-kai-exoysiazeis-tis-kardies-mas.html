617 ZEIS! KAI EXOYSIAZEIS TIS KARDIES MAS

Greek Hymns - No. 617 | "ΖΕΙΣ! ΚΑΙ ΕΞΟΥΣΙΑΖΕΙΣ ΤΙΣ ΚΑΡΔΙΕΣ ΜΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Zeis! Kai exousiazeis tis kardies mas,
(You live! And you rule our hearts,)
kardies athanates, kardies pou Se ymnoyn.
(hearts immortal, hearts that praise Thee.)
Zeis! Kardiochtypas stis symfores mas,
(You live! Heartbeat in our misfortunes,)
me mia agapi pou ti dineis se aftoys pou Se kaloyn.
(with a love that you give to those who call you.)
Chorus:
Zeis! Gia mena zeis kai vasileyeis
(You live! To me you live and reign)
mes stin ourania tin poli me tous aggelous Sou mazi!
(into the heavenly city with Thy angels together!)
Zeis! Kai ta paidia sou plimmyrizei
(You live! And your children are flooded)
mia chara gia ti stigmi tis arpagis!
(fine for the rapture moment!)
Verse 2:
Zeis! Kai ola lene pos tha ertheis
(You live! And everything says you will come)
sa na 'sai kleftis mes stis nychtas ti siopi.
(as if you were a thief in the silence of the night.)
Zeis! Kai 'meis tha Se ypodechtoyme
(You live! And we will welcome you)
m' ena chamogelo sta cheili ston ourano san tha faneis.
(with a smile on your face in the sky as if you will be seen.)
