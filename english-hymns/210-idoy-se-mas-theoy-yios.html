210 IDOY SE MAS ThEOY YIOS!

Greek Hymns - No. 210 | "ΙΔΟΥ ΣΕ ΜΑΣ ΘΕΟΥ ΥΙΟΣ!"
Verse 1:
Idoy se mas Theoy Yios!
(Behold the Son of God!)
Idoy, gennatai o Christos,
(Behold, Christ is born,)
ptocho paidio epi gis,
(poor child on earth,)
o ton apanton Poiitis!
(the poet of the answers!)
Verse 2:
O ypsistos Dimiourgos,
(The supreme Creator,)
o monos alithis Theos,
(the one true god,)
aioniotitos Patir,
(eternal Father,)
Archon eirinis kai Dotir!
(Lord of peace and Giver!)
Verse 3:
Ta tropaia tou foveroy
(Trophies of awesomeness)
tis anthropotitas echthroy
(of humanity's enemy)
katakrimnizei en ripi
(it precipitates in a gust)
en ti avysso ti frikti!
(in the abyss the horrible!)
Verse 4:
Tas pylas de ton ouranon
(And the gates of heaven)
anoigon kamnei koinonon
(of openings becomes communion)
pasan fylin kai patrian
(every tribe and country)
eis ton aggelon tin charan!
(to the angels the joy!)
Verse 5:
O! Pasa, Kyrie, timi,
(Oh! Pass, Lord, honor,)
ainos kai doxa prepei Soi
(praise and glory to You)
ston plasti kai dimiourgo
(to the shaper and creator)
kai krataio imon Theo!
(and our mighty God!)
