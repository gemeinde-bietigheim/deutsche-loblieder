228 ARGA KRATONTAS TO STAYRO

Greek Hymns - No. 228 | "ΑΡΓΑ ΚΡΑΤΩΝΤΑΣ ΤΟ ΣΤΑΥΡΟ"
Verse 1:
Arga kratontas to stafro
(Slowly holding the cross)
anevainei o Christos...
(Christ ascends...)
Stefani ponou kai ntropis
(Wreath of pain and shame)
forese o Yios!
(put on the Son!)
Gia mas kai oxos kai choli
(For us both oxo and gall)
epotisan to Lytroti,
(they watered the Redeemer,)
gia mas potiri empaigmoy
(for us a cup of reproach)
o Christos ipie ekei!
(Christ drank there!)
Verse 2:
De vrethike nero staxia
(No stagnant water was found)
na drosisei Afton
(to cool Him)
pou eplase tous potamoys,
(who made the rivers,)
limnes ki okeanoys!
(lakes and oceans!)
Ton afisan oi mathites
(The disciples left him)
me syntrofia tous dyo listes,
(accompanied by the two robbers,)
tin enochi mas ferontas
(bearing our guilt)
ekei, agonia...
(there, agony...)
Verse 3:
Sterna Ton egkateleipse
(He was left)
o Pateras Theos.
(the Father God.)
"Tetelestai" eip' o Christos
("It is done," said Christ)
ki egeire pia nekros!
(and now he lay dead!)
Pethainei o Athanatos
(The Immortal dies)
na dosei stous thnitoys zoi!
(to give mortals life!)
O, ti agapi thafmasti,
(Oh, what a wonderful love,)
theia, anekfrasti!
(godly, cannot be expressed!)
