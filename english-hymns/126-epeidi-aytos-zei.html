126 EPEIDI AYTOS ZEI

Greek Hymns - No. 126 | "ΕΠΕΙΔΗ ΑΥΤΟΣ ΖΕΙ"
Chorus:
Epeidi Aftos zei ki ego tha ziso!
(Because He lives and I will live!)
Epeidi Aftos zei, zo kai ego!
(Because He lives, I live too!)
Epeidi Aftos krata sta cheria Tou to mellon,
(Because He holds the future in His hands,)
gi' afto i zoi axizei, mono epeidi Aftos zei!
(therefore life is worth living, just because He lives!)
Verse 1:
O Theos esteile ton Gio Tou gia mena
(God sent His Son for me)
pano sto Stafro na karfothei!
(to be nailed to the Cross!)
Ton ethapsan, alla nikise ton tafo,
(They buried him, but he overcame the grave,)
thriamveftis anesti, aionia tora zei!
(risen triumphant, eternally now lives!)
Verse 2:
Afti i zoi einai toso ligi
(This life is so short)
kai oi chares pio ligostes!
(and joys less!)
Ki omos ego sto Christo elpizo,
(And yet I hope in Christ,)
giati anesti kai pantotina tha zei!
(because he rose and will live forever!)
