392 I TRIKYMIA VOYIZEI

Greek Hymns - No. 392 | "Η ΤΡΙΚΥΜΙΑ ΒΟΥΙΖΕΙ"
Verse 1:
I trikymia vouizei, ta kymata mas kykloyn,
(The storm is roaring, the waves are circling us,)
to pan pantachothen mafrizei ki oi fovoi mas apeiloyn.
(everything everywhere turns black and fears threaten us.)
«Exypnison! de Se mellei; Idoy, chanometha!»
("Wake up! isn't it coming for you? Behold, we perish!')
«Didaskale!», krafgazoume oloi, «O! sose, chanometha!»
(“Master!” we all shout, “Oh! save us, we are perishing!')
Chorus:
«To sympan se Me ypotassetai! Siopi!
("The universe submits to Me! Silence!)
Thalasses, anemoi, kymata,
(Seas, winds, waves,)
aorata ktismata kai orata.»
(invisible buildings and visible.")
To ploio imon den vythizetai,
(Our ship is not sinking,)
o Kyrios mesa efrisketai.
(the Lord is within.)
«To pan se Me ypotassetai! Siopi! Siopi!
("Everything submits to Me! Silence! Silence!)
To pan se Me ypotassetai! Isychase!».
(Everything submits to Me! Calm down!".)
Verse 2:
Stenochories kai thlipseis tacheos eperchontai,
(Troubles and sorrows quickly come,)
ta vathi ftochis mas kardias viaios tarassontai.
(the depths of our poor heart are violently stirred.)
Pathi frikta apeiloyne, pnigoune tin psychi.
(Horrible passions threaten, suffocate the soul.)
«Didaskale», deometha «Speyse, o, payse tin tarachi!».
("Master," we cried, "Hurry, oh, stop the commotion!")
Verse 3:
O tromos telos parilthe, ta panta gyro geloyn,
(The terror is finally over, everything around is laughing,)
pantoy vasileyei galini, oi aggeloi mas kykloyn!
(peace reigns everywhere, angels surround us!)
Sotira, meine plision, min apomakryntheis,
(Saviour, stay near, do not go away,)
eosoy se galinis limani makario mas dechtheis!
(Until in a peaceful harbor, may you receive us!)
