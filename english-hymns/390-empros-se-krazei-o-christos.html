390 EMPROS, SE KRAZEI O ChRISTOS

Greek Hymns - No. 390 | "ΕΜΠΡΟΣ, ΣΕ ΚΡΑΖΕΙ Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Empros! Se krazei o Christos,
(Front! Christ calls you,)
tis pisteos mas Archigos,
(of our faith Leader,)
na fereis panta to stafro!
(always carry the cross!)
Empros! piste, empros!
(Front! faithful, forward!)
Chorus:
Empros! Empros! Galini erchetai,
(Front! Front! peace comes)
i thyella parerchetai.
(the storm passes.)
Empros! Empros!
(Front! Front!)
Zei o Theos mas, panta zei, empros!
(Our God lives, always lives, forward!)
Verse 2:
An trikymies mainontai,
(If storms rage,)
to ploio de vythizetai,
(the ship is not sinking)
Sotiras mesa vrisketai,
(A savior within lies,)
Empros! loipon, empros!
(Front! so go ahead!)
Verse 3:
Mesa stis thlipseis tis zois
(In the sorrows of life)
me to Christo prochora sy,
(go with Christ,)
min apokameis stin odo,
(don't make money on the street)
Empros! m' Afton, empros!
(Front! with Him, forward!)
Verse 4:
O, eche tharros pantote,
(Oh, take courage always,)
o Iisoys enikise,
(jesus won)
de tha s' afisei oudepote,
(will never leave you)
Empros! Empros! Empros!
(Front! Front! Front!)
