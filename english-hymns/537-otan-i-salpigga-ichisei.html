537 OTAN I SALPIGGA IChISEI

Greek Hymns - No. 537 | "ΟΤΑΝ Η ΣΑΛΠΙΓΓΑ ΗΧΗΣΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Otan i salpigga ichisei me ischyri foni
(When the trumpet shall sound with a mighty voice)
kai to telos tha plisiasei ki i gi tha tarachthei,
(and the end will draw near and the earth will be shaken,)
tote o Christos me doxa tha elthei kai oi aggeloi mazi,
(then Christ will come in glory and the angels together,)
me foni alalagmoy stin ypodochi Aftoy!
(with a voice of rejoicing in His reception!)
O, «en ripi tou ofthalmoy!»
(Oh, "in the blink of an eye!")
Chorus:
O, «en ripi tou ofthalmoy!»
(Oh, "in the blink of an eye!")
Kai oi nekroi th' anastithoyn
(And the dead will rise)
me doxa lampra ki ego, ti chara,
(with glory bright and me, what joy,)
tha efretho ekei psila!
(I'll be up there!)
Verse 2:
Otan tha erthei stis nefeles tote oloi oi nekroi
(When he comes in the clouds then all the dead)
me chara sto Christo tha psalloun, giati th' anastithoyn!
(they will sing with joy to Christ, because they will be resurrected!)
To thnito tha lavei athanasia ki i zoi tha xanarthei,
(Mortal shall receive immortality and life shall rise again,)
me foni alalagmoy stin ypodochi Aftoy!
(with a voice of rejoicing in His reception!)
O, «en ripi tou ofthalmoy!»
(Oh, "in the blink of an eye!")
Verse 3:
O palios sporos pou esparthi pali th' anastithei
(The old seed that was sown will rise again)
kai zoi nea pali tha 'rthei, o fovos tha chathei!
(and new life will come again, fear will disappear!)
O! Ti endoxi stigmi otan oloi ekei tha sygkentrothoyn
(Oh! What a glorious moment when all there will gather)
me foni alalagmoy stin ypodochi Aftoy!
(with a voice of rejoicing in His reception!)
O, «en ripi tou ofthalmoy!»
(Oh, "in the blink of an eye!")
