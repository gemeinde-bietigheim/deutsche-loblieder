456 POY EISAI PAIDI MOY AGAPITO

Greek Hymns - No. 456 | "ΠΟΥ ΕΙΣΑΙ ΠΑΙΔΙ ΜΟΥ ΑΓΑΠΗΤΟ"
Verse 1:
Poy eisai, paidi mou agapito,
(where are you my dear child)
paidi ton prosefchon,
(child of prayers,)
sta xena eisai asfales
(abroad you are safe)
apo pollon deinon;
(from many afflictions?)
Chorus:
Poy eisai ti nychta afti;
(Where are you tonight?)
Poy eisai ti nychta afti;
(Where are you tonight?)
O! S' agapo! Poso, nai, s' agapo!
(Oh! I love you! How much, yes, I love you!)
Poy eisai ti nychta afti;
(Where are you tonight?)
Verse 2:
Isoun ki esy paidi agno
(You too were a pure child)
san drosos tis afgis,
(like morning dew)
athoo isoun kai semno,
(you were innocent and modest,)
tora asotos tis gis!
(now prodigal of the earth!)
Verse 3:
San prosefchosouna mikros
(As if you were praying as a child)
kai zitages to fos,
(and you asked for the light)
isouna toso katharos
(you were so clean)
san aggelos mikros!
(like a little angel!)
Verse 4:
O, na mporoysa, pos potho,
(Oh that I could, how I long)
kai pali katharo,
(clean again)
agno, athoo na se do,
(pure, innocent to see you,)
os isouna mikros!
(when you were little!)
Verse 5:
Zitisate mou ton yio
(Ask for my son)
mesa ston kosmo afto
(in this world)
kai anaggeilate s' afton
(and announce to him)
oti ton agapo!
(that I love him!)
