17 EIS TA VIMATA TOY

Greek Hymns - No. 17 | "ΕΙΣ ΤΑ ΒΗΜΑΤΑ ΤΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Eis ta vimata Tou me chara th' akoloutho
(In His footsteps I will gladly follow)
kai tis entoles Tou pantote tha ektelo!
(and I will always carry out His commands!)
Prochoro sto dromo tou Sotira mou Christoy,
(I proceed in the way of my Savior Christ,)
Aftos de m' afinei, einai panta filos mou!
(He doesn't leave me, he's always my friend!)
Chorus:
Doxa kai timi s' Afton, to Sotira mou Christo!
(Glory and honor to Him, my Savior Christ!)
Aftos de m' afinei, einai panta filos mou!
(He doesn't leave me, he's always my friend!)
Aftos de m' afinei, einai panta filos mou!
(He doesn't leave me, he's always my friend!)
Verse 2:
Ton Iisoy Christo mou den mporo n' arnitho,
(My Jesus Christ I cannot deny,)
afoy den m' afinei, opou kai an efretho,
(since he does not leave me, wherever I am,)
di' eme stafrothi kai m' Afton panta tha zo
(He was crucified for me and with Him I will always live)
kai tis entoles Tou me chara tha ektelo!
(and His commands I will gladly carry out!)
Verse 3:
Otan i zoi mou kai to ergon epi gis
(When my life and work on earth)
fthasoun eis to telos, tote tha 'mai asfalis,
(reach the end, then I'll be safe,)
giati o Christos mou tha me paralav' efthys!
(because my Christ will receive me immediately!)
Doxa st' onoma Tou! Poso eimai eftychis!
(Glory to His name! How happy I am!)
Verse 4:
Pantote tha psallo eis ton Iisoy Christo
(I will always sing to Jesus Christ)
m' olous tous aggelous kai to plithos ton piston!
(with all the angels and the multitude of believers!)
Tote ekei tha eimai eftychis dia pantos,
(Then there I will be happy forever,)
me aftoys tha meno eis ti doxa tou Patros!
(with them I will dwell in the glory of the Father!)
