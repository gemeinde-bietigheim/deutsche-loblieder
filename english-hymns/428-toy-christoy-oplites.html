428 TOY ChRISTOY OPLITES

Greek Hymns - No. 428 | "ΤΟΥ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΥ ΟΠΛΙΤΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Tou Christoy oplites, vainete empros
(Soldiers of Christ, go forth)
pantote mprosta sas einai o Stafros!
(always in front of you is the Cross!)
Monos archigos mas ein' o Iisoys,
(Our only leader is Jesus,)
me Afton nikame olous tous echthroys!
(with Him we defeat all enemies!)
Chorus:
Tou Christoy oplites vainete empros
(Soldiers of Christ go forward)
pantote mprosta sas einai o Stafros!
(always in front of you is the Cross!)
Verse 2:
San stratos vadizoun oloi oi pistoi,
(All the faithful march like an army,)
opos kai diilthan agioi polloi,
(as many saints have passed,)
me mia kardia kai me mia psychi
(with one heart and one soul)
pisti kai elpida eis tin prosefchi!
(faith and hope in prayer!)
Verse 3:
Thronous kai stefania pairnei o kairos,
(Time takes thrones and crowns,)
all' i ekklisia einai asaleftos!
(but the church is unsalable!)
Oyte pyles Adi den mporoyn pote
(Not even the gates of Hades ever can)
m' oli tous ti lyssa na nikisoune!
(with all their fury to win!)
Verse 4:
Psallete, o ethni, panta sto Christo,
(Sing, O nations, always to Christ,)
me doxologia kai me thriamvo!
(with praise and triumph!)
Doxa m' ena stoma pros ton Lytroti
(Glory with one mouth to the Redeemer)
tora as dothei kai pantote, Amin!
(now let it be given always, Amen!)
