410 OTAN ThLIPsEIS KAI DOKIMASIES

Greek Hymns - No. 410 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΘΛΙΨΕΙΣ ΚΑΙ ΔΟΚΙΜΑΣΙΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Otan thlipseis kai dokimasies
(When sorrows and trials)
se perivalloun kai s' apelpizoun,
(they surround you and make you despair,)
mi tarazesai kai mi fovasai,
(don't worry and don't be afraid)
trexe eis ton Iisoy!
(run to Jesus!)
Chorus:
Ela s' Ekeinon! Ela s' Ekeinon!
(Come to Him! Come to Him!)
pes kathe pono psychis.
(say every heartache.)
O Christos tha se parigorisei,
(Christ will comfort you,)
ean ton empisteftheis!
(if you trust him!)
Verse 2:
Eis ti lypi kai stenochoria
(In sadness and distress)
de tha s' afisei ki egkataleipsei,
(he will not leave you nor forsake you,)
tha sou dosei pliri eftychia,
(will give you complete happiness,)
trexe eis to Lytroti!
(run to the Redeemer!)
Verse 3:
An pote stereisai ki echeis chreia,
(If you're ever in need,)
einai o Monos pou tha frontisei,
(he's the only one who will take care)
stis elleipseis kai sti dyskolia
(in shortcomings and in difficulty)
trexe eis to Lytroti!
(run to the Redeemer!)
Verse 4:
Eche tharros kai min apokameis,
(Be brave and don't give up,)
pistepse tora eis to Sotira,
(believe now in the Savior,)
iasi sto soma sou tha laveis,
(healing in your body you will receive,)
ela eis to Golgotha!
(come to Calvary!)
