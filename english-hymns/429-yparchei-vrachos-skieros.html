429 YPARChEI VRAChOS SKIEROS

Greek Hymns - No. 429 | "ΥΠΑΡΧΕΙ ΒΡΑΧΟΣ ΣΚΙΕΡΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Yparchei vrachos skieros
(There is a shady rock)
s' afti tin erimi ti gi.
(in this desolate land.)
Sotiras, fylakas pistos,
(Saviour, faithful guardian,)
tou paschonta katafygi!
(the sufferer's refuge!)
Chorus:
Christos o Lytrotis, vrachos tis psychis,
(Christ the Redeemer, rock of the soul,)
prostatis kai dioikitis,
(patron and commander,)
Christos o Lytrotis, vrachos tis psychis,
(Christ the Redeemer, rock of the soul,)
stis thlipseis mas katafygi!
(in our sorrows refuge!)
Verse 2:
Sotiras ap' ton poniro,
(Savior from the wicked,)
asfaleia mes sti zoi,
(safety in life,)
prostatis apo ton echthro,
(protector from the enemy,)
stis thlipseis mas katafygi!
(in our sorrows refuge!)
Verse 3:
O anemos an mas ktypa,
(If the wind blows us,)
oi thyelles an apeiloyn,
(if storms threaten,)
tipote den tha mas nika,
(nothing will defeat us)
mprosta s' Afton ola siopoyn!
(before Him everything is silent!)
Verse 4:
O vrache theie tou pistoy,
(O grodly rock of the believer,)
s' afti tin erimi ti gi,
(in this desolate land,)
Sotira tou amartoloy,
(Savior of the sinner,)
tou paschonta katafygi!
(the sufferer's refuge!)
