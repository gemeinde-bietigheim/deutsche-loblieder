654 STON OYRANO ThA STRAFO

Greek Hymns - No. 654 | "ΣΤΟΝ ΟΥΡΑΝΟ ΘΑ ΣΤΡΑΦΩ"
Verse 1:
Ston ourano tha strafo
(To the sky I will turn)
kai th' anoixo tin kardia mou s' Afton,
(and I will open my heart to Him,)
san potami na ypercheilisei!
(like a river overflowing!)
Stin parousia Tou, ton eafto mou eleythero
(In His presence, myself free)
san pouli, nai, th' afiso gia na gemisei
(like a bird, yes, I'll leave it to fill)
apo aperanti chara, ap' agapi aionia,
(of boundless joy, of eternal love,)
pou vathia tin psychi mou stigmatizoun,
(which deeply mark my soul,)
stin parousia Tou san mpo,
(into His presence as I enter,)
kathe empodio prosperno
(I overcome every obstacle)
kai xechno ola afta pou me fovizoun!
(and I forget all that scares me!)
Verse 2:
Mes sti galini afti
(To this peace)
tis parousias Tou vriskomai
(of His presence I am)
ki etsi tipota den me noiazei!
(so I don't care about anything!)
Mono na vlepo, zito, to glyko Tou prosopo
(Only to see, I ask, His sweet face)
kai to eleos Tou panta na me skepazei!
(and may His mercy always cover me!)
Mia empeiria ourania, pou me logia anthropina
(A heavenly experience, that in human words)
ein' adynaton na exigiso!
(It's impossible to explain!)
Afto pou zo pano sti gi
(What I live on earth)
einai kati pou se ligo
(it's something that in a little while)
aionia ego tha ziso!
(I will live forever!)
