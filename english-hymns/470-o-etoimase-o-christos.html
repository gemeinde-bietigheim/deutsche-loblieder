470 O! ETOIMASE O ChRISTOS

Greek Hymns - No. 470 | "Ω! ΕΤΟΙΜΑΣΕ Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ"
Verse 1:
O! Etoimase o Christos trapeza ton agathon
(Oh! Christ prepared a bank of goods)
kai kalei ta tekna Tou, gia na gefthoyn!
(and calls His children to taste!)
Me to manna na trafoyn ki o,ti allo aftoi pothoyn,
(With the manna they may eat whatever else they desire,)
ti oraios deipnos me ton Iisoy!
(what a nice dinner with Jesus!)
Chorus:
Ela tora eis to deipno to lampro
(Come now to dinner the brilliant one)
na deipniseis me ton Iisoy Christo!
(to dine with Jesus Christ!)
Steketai kai se kalei, speyse tora parefthys,
(He stands and calls thee, haste now present,)
opos ek ton agathon kai sy geftheis!
(as of the goods and with tastes!)
Verse 2:
Tote oi apostoloi etrexan ston Iisoy,
(Then the apostles ran to Jesus,)
molis kalese aftoys, gia na gefthoyn!
(just invited them, to taste!)
Ki otan ilthan me chara vrikan arto kai fotia
(And when they came with joy they found bread and fire)
ki effranthikane m' Afton, o! ti lampra!
(and rejoiced with Him, oh! how brilliant!)
Verse 3:
Met' oligon o Christos tha xanarthei eis ti gi,
(Soon Christ will return to earth,)
gia na paralavei olous tous pistoys!
(to receive all believers!)
O! ti endoxi stigmi kai chara pantotini
(Oh! what a glorious moment and everlasting joy)
eis ekeinon pou tha efrethei ekei!
(to the one who will be found there!)
Verse 4:
Ama de ntytheis kai sy kathari lefki stoli,
(If you don't dress yourself in a pure white uniform,)
de tha dynitheis sto gamo na vretheis!
(you won't be able to be at the wedding!)
O! ti tromeri stigmi an i thyra sou kleistei
(Oh! what a terrible moment if your door closes)
ki ap' to deipno tou Christoy ekdiochtheis!
(and from the supper of Christ you are expelled!)
Verse 5:
Dia toyto min argeis, etoimasou tora efthys,
(Therefore do not delay, get ready now at once,)
gia na endytheis kai sy stoli lampri!
(to dress and dress brilliantly!)
Ki otan erthei o Christos, o Nymfios o kalos,
(And when Christ comes, the good Bridegroom,)
na eiseltheis stin aionia zoi!
(to enter into eternal life!)
