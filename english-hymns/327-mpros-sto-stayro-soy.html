327 MPROS STO STAYRO SOY

Greek Hymns - No. 327 | "ΜΠΡΟΣ ΣΤΟ ΣΤΑΥΡΟ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Mpros sto Stafro Sou gonatismenos
(Before Your Cross kneeling)
anazitao ti morfi Sou,
(I seek Your form,)
thelo n' akoyso ti foni Sou!
(I want to hear Your voice!)
Thelo n' akoyso ti foni Sou!
(I want to hear Your voice!)
Chorus:
Aftin tin ora Ese, Iisoy mou,
(At this time You, my Jesus,)
S' echei anagki i psychi mou!
(My soul needs you!)
Aftin tin ora gony klino,
(At this hour I kneel down,)
tin kardia mou s' Esena dino!
(I give you my heart!)
O! tin kardia mou s' Esena dino!
(Oh! I give you my heart!)
Verse 2:
Mpros sto Stafro Sou gonatismenos
(Before Your Cross kneeling)
zitaei i psychi mou ti thoria Sou,
(my soul seeks Your mercy,)
dipsaei na niosei to aggigma Sou!
(he thirsts to feel Your touch!)
Dipsaei na niosei to aggigma Sou!
(He thirsts to feel Your touch!)
Verse 3:
Christe mou, Esy otan m' aggizeis,
(My Christ, when you touch me,)
chanetai i gi apo mprosta mou!
(the earth is disappearing from before me!)
Zo ourano, eisai konta mou!
(I live sky, you are near me!)
Zo ourano, eisai konta mou!
(I live sky, you are near me!)
