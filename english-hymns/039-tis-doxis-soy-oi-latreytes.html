39 TIS DOXIS SOY OI LATREYTES

Greek Hymns - No. 39 | "ΤΗΣ ΔΟΞΗΣ ΣΟΥ ΟΙ ΛΑΤΡΕΥΤΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Tis doxis Sou oi latreftes,
(Of Thy glory the worshippers,)
o ypsiste Dimiourge,
(oh supreme Creator,)
foni ypsoyme tapeini,
(voice we raise humble,)
dexou aineseos odi!
(receive an ode of praise!)
Dexou aineseos odi!
(Receive praise ode!)
Patera, Pneyma kai Yio,
(Father, Spirit and Son,)
ston epouranio Theo,
(to the heavenly God,)
proskynisi, aino, timi,
(worship, praise, honor,)
prosferoume pantotini!
(we offer forever!)
Prosferoume pantotini!
(We offer forever!)
Anymneite, eflogeite, proskyneite, laoi, laoi,
(Sing, bless, worship, peoples, peoples,)
ton Patera kai to Logo kai to Pneyma,
(the Father and the Word and the Spirit,)
tin Triada, Theo ena!
(the Trinity, one God!)
Anymneite, eflogeite, proskyneite, laoi, Amin!
(Sing, bless, worship, people, Amen!)
Alliloyia! Amin! Alliloyia! Amin!
(Alleluia! Amen! Alleluia! Amen!)
