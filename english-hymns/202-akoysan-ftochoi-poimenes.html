202 AKOYSAN FTOChOI POIMENES

Greek Hymns - No. 202 | "ΑΚΟΥΣΑΝ ΦΤΩΧΟΙ ΠΟΙΜΕΝΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Akousan ftochoi poimenes tin ourania stratia,
(Poor shepherds heard the heavenly host,)
pou m' agapi melodoyse kai m' anekfrasti chara!
(which melodious with love and inexpressible joy!)
Ki antichoyse tin odi tous megaloprepis icho,
(And echoed their ode in majestic echo,)
«en anthropois efdokia», doxa, doxa sto Theo!
("prosperity among men", glory, glory to God!)
Verse 2:
Kai eno apo ton tromo, ekthamvoi, siopiloi,
(And while with terror, dazzled, silent,)
aporoysan ti simainei ton aggelon i odi.
(they wondered what the ode of angels meant.)
Xafnou, doxa perilampei kai aggelikos choros
(Awake, glory includes and angelic dance)
«Eis ti Vithleem», tous legei, «egennithike o Christos!».
("In Bethlehem," he tells them, "Christ was born!")
Verse 3:
Otan stou David tin poli efthasan perichareis,
(When David came to the city rejoicing,)
vrikan vrefos mes sti fatni anti ploytou kai chlidis!
(they found a baby in a manger instead of wealth and opulence!)
Edistasan, alla irthe eis t' aftia tous mia foni,
(They hesitated, but a voice came to their ears,)
«Doxa epi gis eirini, doxa eis to Lytroti!».
("Glory on earth, peace, glory to the Redeemer!")
Verse 4:
Mes sti fatni egennithi tou Ypsistou o Yios,
(In the manger the Son of the Most High was born,)
tous anthropous gia na ferei ap' tin plani eis to fos!
(people to bring from error to light!)
«Epi gis eirini, doxa eis ton Ypsisto Theo,
("On earth peace, glory to God the Most High,)
en anthropois efdokia», doxa, doxa sto Theo!
(prosperity among men", glory, glory to God!)
