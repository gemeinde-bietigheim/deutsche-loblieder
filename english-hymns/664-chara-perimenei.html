664 ChARA PERIMENEI

Greek Hymns - No. 664 | "ΧΑΡΑ ΠΕΡΙΜΕΝΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Chara perimenei afton pou prosmenei
(Joy awaits him who waits)
kai echei to vlemma psila!
(and he looks up!)
Chara perimenei afton
(Joy awaits him)
pou echei archigo to Christo!
(which has Christ as its leader!)
Chara perimenei afton
(Joy awaits him)
pou echei archigo to Christo!
(which has Christ as its leader!)
Verse 2:
Meta apo thlipseis, meta ap' agkathia
(After sorrows, after thorns)
kai apo karfia aichmira,
(and of sharp nails,)
afoy vgikes ap' ti fotia
(after you came out of the fire)
pou perases kai vasana,
(that you went through suffering,)
tha deis theio kallos Aftoy,
(you will see His divine beauty,)
tha feggeis mes sti doxa Tou!
(you will shine in His glory!)
Verse 3:
Kouragio akoma, gia na teleiosei
(Courage still, to finish)
i machi i toso skliri...
(the battle is so hard...)
Yposchetai doxa se mas,
(He promises glory to us,)
otan polemame mprosta!
(when we fight forward!)
Pan dakry tha leipsei ekei
(Every tear will be missed there)
oi ponoi kai oi stenagmoi!
(the pains and the groans!)
Verse 4:
Christos ein' o ilios tis dikaiosynis,
(Christ is the sun of justice,)
fotizei ton kosmo afton!
(lights up this world!)
Kathen' apo mas pou dipsa,
(Each of us who thirsts,)
pothei na tou dosei chara,
(longs to give him pleasure,)
pothei na ton ferei psila
(he longs to bring him up)
se ourania ki aionia agatha!
(to heavenly and eternal goods!)
