418 SAN PROChORO ME TO ChRISTO

Greek Hymns - No. 418 | "ΣΑΝ ΠΡΟΧΩΡΩ ΜΕ ΤΟ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ"
Verse 1:
San prochoro me to Christo
(As I walk with Christ)
sto dromo afto to skoteino,
(on this dark road,)
vadizo empros mes sti nychta,
(march forward into the night)
to fos krato to zontano!
(I keep the light alive!)
Verse 2:
San prochoro me to Christo
(As I walk with Christ)
sto monopati to steno,
(on the narrow path,)
stin agkalia me sikonei
(he lifts me up in his arms)
kai m' odigei ston Ourano!
(and leads me to Heaven!)
Verse 3:
Ki otan perno me to Christo
(And when I spend with Christ)
ton afrismeno okeano,
(the foaming ocean,)
Aftos krata to timoni
(He holds the steering wheel)
ki ego stin pisti xagrypno!
(I too am awake in faith!)
Verse 4:
San prochoro me to Christo
(As I walk with Christ)
sto Golgotha kai sto stafro,
(on Calvary and the cross,)
ap' ti skia tou thanatou
(from the shadow of death)
stin pleria ti zoi tha vgo!
(for the rest of my life I will go out!)
