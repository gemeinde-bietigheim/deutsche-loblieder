14 DOXA ANIKEI STON ChRISTON

Greek Hymns - No. 14 | "ΔΟΞΑ ΑΝΗΚΕΙ ΣΤΟΝ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΝ"
Verse 1:
Doxa anikei ston Christon,
(Glory belongs to Christ,)
ton monon Lytrotin!
(the only Redeemer!)
As psallomen oloi s' Afton
(Let us all sing to Him)
doxa sto nikitin!
(kudos to the winner!)
Chorus:
Eflogimenos kai doxasmenos,
(Blessed and glorified,)
o Kyrios mas kai Theos
(our Lord and God)
kai Lytrotis o ischyros!
(and Redeemer the mighty!)
Aftos gar esosen imas
(He saved us)
ek thlipseos pikras!
(bitter from grief!)
Verse 2:
Afovos pantes oi pistoi
(Of course all believers)
as psalloun pantachoy,
(let them sing everywhere,)
synetripsen o Iisoys
(crushed Jesus)
to kratos tou echthroy!
(the enemy state!)
Verse 3:
Doxasate loipon Afton
(So you glorified Him)
pantes tis gis laoi,
(all peoples of the earth,)
anapafsin tha lavete,
(rest you will receive,)
o, seis, amartoloi!
(oh, you sinners!)
