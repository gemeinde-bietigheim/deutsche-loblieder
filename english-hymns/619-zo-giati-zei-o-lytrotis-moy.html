619 ZO, GIATI ZEI O LYTROTIS MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 619 | "ΖΩ, ΓΙΑΤΙ ΖΕΙ Ο ΛΥΤΡΩΤΗΣ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Zo giati zei o Lytrotis mou.
(I live because my Redeemer lives.)
Fos o Christos mes ti zoi mou.
(Christ is the light of my life.)
To Pneyma Agio panta me odigei,
(The Holy Spirit always leads me,)
ti prepei na prosecho pano sti gi,
(what on earth should I watch out for,)
na termatiso nikitis.
(to finish victorious.)
Chorus:
Telikos proorismos einai o tritos ouranos
(Final destination is the third heaven)
stin agkalia Tou tha me parei o Christos.
(Christ will take me in His arms.)
Kalos irthes, tha mou pei, lytromeno mou paidi
(Welcome, he will tell me, my redeemed child)
kai pame na sou deixo ti nea gi.
(and let me show you the new land.)
Verse 2:
Zo ki agonizetai mazi mou
(I live and fight with me)
gia na sosei ti psychi mou.
(to save my soul)
Tha erthei kapoia mera kai tha mou pei,
(He will come one day and tell me,)
sti gi den echeis alli apostoli,
(on earth you have no other mission,)
feygeis gia ton ourano.
(you leave for heaven)
Verse 3:
Den mporo na perimeno,
(I can't wait,)
ton Christo mou ton prosmeno.
(I am waiting for my Christ.)
San to tyflo pou thelei na dei to fos
(Like the blind man who wants to see the light)
san ti steria pou vlepei o nafagos
(like the land seen by the castaway)
gia na vgei kai na sothei.
(to get out and be saved.)
