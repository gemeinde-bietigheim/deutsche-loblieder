660 TON ESTEILE O PATERAS

Greek Hymns - No. 660 | "ΤΟΝ ΕΣΤΕΙΛΕ Ο ΠΑΤΕΡΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Ton esteile o Pateras mia skoteini nychtia
(The Father sent him on a dark night)
kato sti gi mas,
(down to our earth,)
gia na skorpisei Aftos to valsamo glyko
(to scatter He the balm sweet)
mes stin psychi mas!
(into our soul!)
Chorus:
Tin pio glykia kardia pou t' ourania stolize
(The sweetest heart that adorned the heavens)
thysiase o Theos gi' amartoloys!
(God sacrificed for sinners!)
Ki opoios ti dipsa tis psychis
(And whoever thirsts for the soul)
ti svinei sto Christo,
(extinguishes it in Christ,)
tha zei stous ouranoys!
(he will live in heaven!)
Verse 2:
Ta logia Tou katharia, krystallina nera,
(His words pure, crystal waters,)
xedipsasan psyches.
(they quenched the thirst of souls.)
Me theia kalosyni ki agapi tryferi
(With divine goodness and tender love)
empike stis kardies!
(entered the hearts!)
Verse 3:
Konta mas vrike pikra, agnomones kardies,
(Near us he found bitterness, unforgiving hearts,)
pikres kai xenes!
(bitter and foreign!)
Ki Ekeinos stafromenos... «Patera» gyrepse,
(And He crucified... "Father" turned,)
«sygchorise tes!»
("forgive them!")
