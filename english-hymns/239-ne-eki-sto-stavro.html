239 Ne Eki Sto Stavro

Greek Hymns - No. 239 | "Yes, There at the Cross"
Verse 1:
Ne, eki sto stavro, o, pos me agapas!
(Yes, there at the cross, oh, how You love me!)
Me tin psychi mou ekei, Iisou, tha s' aineso!
(With my soul there, Jesus, I will praise You!)
Ke me tin chara an ke kitaso, sti patsi Sou,
(And with joy if I look upon Your suffering,)
theo tin agapi Sou gia to paidilein' o Theos!
(I see Your love for God's child!)
Chorus:
Keo sto stavro, sto stavro, eki anthropon mou ti chardin,
(And at the cross, at the cross, there my joy of man,)
ki an pethano, exo zoi eki apothano!
(and if I die, I have life, there I die!)
Verse 2:
Perisotero ap' ola, o Thee, tha s' agapiso,
(More than all, oh God, I will love You,)
kai gia tin agapi Sou horis telos tha tragoudi!
(and for Your love without end I will sing!)
Posi timoria pou egines, min ola kataperasi
(What punishment You became, yet overcame all)
kai pos min ksechaso tis efcharis me tin zoi mou!
(and how I won't forget to thank You with my life!)
