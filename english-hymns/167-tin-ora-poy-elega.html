167 TIN ORA POY ELEGA

Greek Hymns - No. 167 | "ΤΗΝ ΩΡΑ ΠΟΥ ΕΛΕΓΑ"
Verse 1:
Tin ora pou elega pos de th' antexo
(At the time when I was saying that I can't stand it)
na ziso allo mes sti zoi
(to live another life)
san thayma ilthes, gia na mou doseis
(like a miracle you came to give me)
kainoyrgia dynami, Iisoy mou, Esy!
(new strength, my Jesus, You!)
Chorus:
Itan pronomio na Se gnoriso
(It was a privilege to know you)
kai tin elpida mou na xanavro!
(and my hope to find again!)
Tora, Iisoy mou, thelo na ziso
(Now, my Jesus, I want to live)
mono kai mono na S' agapo!
(just to love you!)
Verse 2:
Nai, o Theos pou m' echei plasei,
(Yes, the God who made me,)
lypithike gia me pikra
(he pitied me bitterly)
ki esteile, Iisoy mou, Esena,
(and sent, my Jesus, You,)
na kaneis ton pono mou chara!
(make my pain joy!)
Verse 3:
Xafnou ximerose mia omorfi mera,
(Suddenly a beautiful day dawned,)
o ilios elampe kai ta poulia
(the sun was shining and the birds)
legan ti ekames Esy gia mena,
(they said what have you done for me,)
legan pos allaxa ego kardia!
(they said that I changed my heart!)
