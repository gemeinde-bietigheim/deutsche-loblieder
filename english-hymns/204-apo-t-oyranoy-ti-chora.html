204 APO T' OYRANOY TI ChORA

Greek Hymns - No. 204 | "ΑΠΟ Τ' ΟΥΡΑΝΟΥ ΤΗ ΧΩΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Apo t' ouranoy ti chora
(From the sky to the country)
ilthe minyma glyko
(message came sweetie)
pos sti gi mas mes sti fatni
(as in our land in the manger)
egennithi o Christos!
(Christ was born!)
Chorus:
Doxa, doxa, doxa,
(Glory, glory, glory,)
en ypsistois doxa!
(in the highest glory!)
Doxa, doxa, doxa
(Glory, glory, glory)
en ypsistois to Theo!
(in the highest to God!)
Verse 2:
Afise ouranies doxes
(He left heavenly glories)
ki' ilthe kato edo sti gi
(and he came down here to earth)
na zitisei kai na sosei
(to ask and to save)
kathe amartoli psychi!
(every sinful soul!)
Verse 3:
Psalloune laoi kai ethni,
(Peoples and nations sing,)
psallei i gi ki o ouranos,
(earth and sky sing,)
giati simera egennithi
(because today he was born)
o Sotiras mas Christos!
(our Savior Christ!)
