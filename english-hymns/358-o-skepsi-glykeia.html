358 O SKEPsI GLYKEIA

Greek Hymns - No. 358 | "Ω ΣΚΕΨΗ ΓΛΥΚΕΙΑ"
Verse 1:
O skepsi glykeia, diarkos me Se
(Oh sweet thought, always with You)
na meno Sotira kai Sy eis eme!
(May I live as a Savior and You are with me!)
S' Esena na vrisko pan o,ti potho
(In You I find everything I desire)
kai s' afton ton kosmo allo de zito!
(and I don't ask for anything else in this world!)
Verse 2:
Sy ontos na eisai ta panta gia me
(You really are everything to me)
ki' ego olokliros na zo dia Se!
(and I to live entirely through You!)
Sy n' aponekroseis, Kyrie, to «ego»
(By killing, Lord, the "I")
kai apo to Pneyma Sou na plirotho!
(and by Your Spirit to be paid!)
Verse 3:
Os dikaiosi mou Se na theoro
(As my justification I consider Thee)
kai theia sofia apo Se n' antlo!
(and divine wisdom from Thee to draw!)
Oles oi stigmes mou na agiasthoyn
(May all my moments be sanctified)
kai oles oi skepseis mou na agnisthoyn!
(and all my thoughts be purified!)
Verse 4:
Voitha, Sotira, se kathe stigmi
(Help, Saviour, at every moment)
na meno me pisti s' o,ti les Esy!
(to stay with faith in what You say!)
Kai panta na chairo m' olous tous pistoys
(And always rejoice with all the faithful)
na vretho sti doxa Sou stous ouranoys!
(to be in Your glory in the heavens!)
