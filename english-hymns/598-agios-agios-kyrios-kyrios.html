598 AGIOS, AGIOS! KYRIOS, KYRIOS!

Greek Hymns - No. 598 | "ΑΓΙΟΣ, ΑΓΙΟΣ! ΚΥΡΙΟΣ, ΚΥΡΙΟΣ!"
Chorus:
Agios, Agios! Kyrios, Kyrios!
(Holy, Holy! Lord, Lord!)
Sto Vasilea mas doxa, timi!
(To our King, glory, honor!)
Sikose pano Tou tis amarties mas!
(Take our sins upon Him!)
Doxa ston Archonta kai Lytroti!
(Glory to the Lord and Redeemer!)
