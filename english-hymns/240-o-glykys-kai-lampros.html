240 O GLYKYS KAI LAMPROS

Greek Hymns - No. 240 | "Ο ΓΛΥΚΥΣ ΚΑΙ ΛΑΜΠΡΟΣ"
Verse 1:
O glykys kai lampros Lytrotis mas
(Our sweet and bright Redeemer)
ostis ilthe gia me sti gi
(who came to earth for me)
kai sklira sto stafro ethysiasthei
(and die hard on the cross)
na mou dosei aionia zoi!
(give me eternal life!)
Chorus:
Stafrothik' o Christos gia me!
(Christ was crucified for me!)
Stafrothik' o Christos gia se!
(Christ was crucified for you!)
Sto stafro pethane, ypefere,
(On the cross he died, suffered,)
stafrothike Aftos gia me!
(He was crucified for me!)
Verse 2:
Den yparchei alloy sotiria,
(There is no salvation elsewhere,)
eimi mono sto aima Aftoy!
(I am only in His blood!)
Tha plytheis ap' tis amarties,
(You will be washed of sins,)
an pisteyeis sto Logo tou!
(if you believe in his Word!)
Verse 3:
Akoloythise sy to Sotira
(Follow the Savior)
na sotheis, o kairos eggys!
(be saved, the time is near!)
Tosa chronia o Sotiras se krazei,
(How many years has the Savior been crying to you,)
trexe, prin i porta kleisthei!
(run before the door closes!)
