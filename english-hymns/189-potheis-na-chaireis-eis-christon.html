189 POThEIS NA ChAIREIS EIS ChRISTON

Greek Hymns - No. 189 | "ΠΟΘΕΙΣ ΝΑ ΧΑΙΡΕΙΣ ΕΙΣ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΝ"
Verse 1:
Potheis na chaireis eis Christon; Arithmison kalos
(Do you desire to rejoice in Christ? Count well)
ta dora pou eis ton thniton charizei o Theos!
(the gifts that God bestows on mortals!)
Chorus:
Einai afesis, chara, eirini kai agiasmos,
(It is release, joy, peace and sanctification,)
ischys kai pisti stathera ta chorigei prothymos o Christos!
(strength and faith are constantly granted by Christ willingly!)
Verse 2:
Ta erga sou anofeli, pas kopos mataios,
(Your works are in vain, you labor in vain,)
tin afesi sou enteli charizei o Theos!
(God grants you complete forgiveness!)
Verse 3:
Kai met' aftis sou chorigei eirinin psychikin,
(And with this he grants you peace of mind,)
polytimon kai alithi charan pnefmatikin!
(precious and true spiritual joy!)
Verse 4:
Kai enischyei ton piston me dynamin Aftoy,
(And strengthens the believer with His power,)
dia na ferei ston Christon psychin amartoloy!
(to bring a sinner's soul to Christ!)
Verse 5:
Dia tis pisteos aftas lamvanei o pistos
(Through this faith the believer receives)
tas eflogias tas septas pou dinei o Theos!
(the blessings the septas that God gives!)
