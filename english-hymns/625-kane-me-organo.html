625 KANE ME ORGANO

Greek Hymns - No. 625 | "ΚΑΝΕ ΜΕ ΟΡΓΑΝΟ"
Chorus:
Kane me organo, organo agapis
(Make me an instrument, an instrument of love)
na se ypireto Christe!
(to serve you Christ!)
Kane me organo, organo diko Sou!
(Make me an instrument, Your instrument!)
Ypsono ta cheria mou pros Se!
(I raise my hands to You!)
Kane me organo, organo latreias
(Make me an instrument, an instrument of worship)
na se doxazo Christe!
(to glorify you Christ!)
Kane me organo, organo diko Sou!
(Make me an instrument, Your instrument!)
Ypsono ta cheria mou pros Se!
(I raise my hands to You!)
Verse 1:
Plasti mou, Dimiourge mou,
(My shaper, my creator,)
plase me Sy o Thee,
(deal with me O God,)
organo na 'mai diko Sou!
(instrument to be Yours!)
Ese na ypireto, Christe!
(Let me serve, Christ!)
Verse 2:
Pare ti gi ap' tin kardia mou,
(Take the earth from my heart)
gemise tin ourano,
(fill the sky)
organo na 'nai diko sou!
(instrument be yours!)
Christe mou, na se ypireto!
(My Christ, to serve you!)
