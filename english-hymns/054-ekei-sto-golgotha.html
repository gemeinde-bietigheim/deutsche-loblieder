54 EKEI STO GOLGOThA

Greek Hymns - No. 54 | "ΕΚΕΙ ΣΤΟ ΓΟΛΓΟΘΑ"
Verse 1:
Ekei sto Golgotha vlepo to Sotira
(There on Calvary I see the Savior)
ti thesi ti diki mou na pairnei sto stafro,
(take my place on the cross,)
vlepo tis pliges Tou, to aima Tou na trechei,
(I see His wounds, His blood running,)
na pethainei ekeinos, gia na ziso ego!
(let him die, that I may live!)
Chorus:
Toso o Theos ton kosmo ton agapise,
(God so loved the world,)
oste edose to gio Tou to monogeni,
(so that he gave his only begotten son,)
gia na min chathei kanenas pou s' Afton pisteyei,
(lest anyone who believes in Him perish,)
alla na 'chei aionia zoi!
(but may he have eternal life!)
Verse 2:
Thysiase o Theos to gio Tou gia emena,
(God sacrificed His son for me,)
ti thesi ti diki mou pire sto stafro
(he took my place on the cross)
plirose gia mena, gia kathe mou amartia,
(pay for me, for all my sin,)
plirose Ekeinos to chreos pou eicha ego!
(He paid the debt I owed!)
