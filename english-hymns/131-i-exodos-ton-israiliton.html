131 I EXODOS TON ISRAILITON

Greek Hymns - No. 131 | "Η ΕΞΟΔΟΣ ΤΩΝ ΙΣΡΑΗΛΙΤΩΝ"
Verse 1:
I exodos ton Israiliton
(The exodus of the Israelites)
stin istoria ton laon
(in the history of peoples)
einai i pio lampri, i pio alithini,
(she is the brightest, the truest,)
giati o archigos tous itane o Theos!
(because their leader was God!)
Verse 2:
Stin Aigypto ki ego edoylefa,
(In Egypt I also worked,)
edoylefa sto Farao
(I used to work in Pharaoh)
ki o ponos tis psychis itan poly varys
(and the pain of the soul was very heavy)
makria ap' to Theo, makria Tou sti skoteinia!
(away from God, away from Him in darkness!)
Verse 3:
Ma mesa mou i elpida foliaze
(But hope was nestling inside me)
pos fos tha lampsei ap' ouranoy,
(how light will shine from heaven,)
to fos tou erchomoy tou eleftherotoy
(the light of the coming of the liberator)
na dosei stin psychi ti doxa kai ti chara!
(to give the soul glory and joy!)
Verse 4:
Kai irthe i mera, i mera i lampri
(And the day came, the glorious day)
pou eskypse kato sti gi
(who bowed down to the earth)
o Megas Vasilias sti chora tis sklavias
(the Great King in the land of slavery)
kai tou vasanismoy pou eiche o laos Aftoy!
(and the torture that His people had!)
Verse 5:
Kai tote anoixe i thalassa
(And then the sea opened)
ta kymata san ta vouna
(the waves like the mountains)
kai peras' apo 'kei gia tin oraia gi,
(and pass from here to the fair land,)
ti chora tou Theoy, konta Tou, sti theia chara!
(God's country, close to Him, in divine joy!)
