388 EGERThIT' OI SESOSMENOI

Greek Hymns - No. 388 | "ΕΓΕΡΘΗΤ' ΟΙ ΣΕΣΩΣΜΕΝΟΙ"
Verse 1:
Egerthit' oi sesosmenoi,
(Rise up the inmates,)
osoi eis ton Archigon
(those to the Chief)
eisthe afosiomenoi,
(you are dedicated)
machesthe yper tis Sion!
(fight for Zion!)
Chorus:
Psallate megalofonos asma to thriamvikon.
(Sing aloud the triumphal hymn.)
Psallat' eseis charmosynos nikitiria stafroy!
(Sing ye joyous triumphs of the cross!)
Thriamveyomen imeis! Thriamveyomen imeis!
(We triumph! We triumph!)
Dia tou Estafromenou, thriamveyomen imeis!
(Through the Crucified, we triumph!)
Verse 2:
Plithos pro imon agion
(A multitude of saints before us)
allote epi tou aftoy
(sometimes on it)
ethriamvefsan pediou
(they triumphed in the field)
ki edoxasthisan pantoy!
(and they were glorified everywhere!)
Verse 3:
Meta tharrous as provomen
(After courage let's proceed)
pros ton tis psychis echthron,
(to the enemy of the soul,)
kat' aftoy as polemomen
(against him let us fight)
pantes ypo ton stafron!
(everyone under the cross!)
Verse 4:
Th' antameipsi me vraveion,
(I'll be rewarded with a prize,)
otan tha emfanisthi,
(when it will appear)
didon doxis aristeion
(glory of excellence)
o Sotir ston nikitin!
(Sotir to the winner!)
Verse 5:
Othen as entharrynthomen
(Wherefore let us be encouraged)
kai ton tis psychis echthron
(and the enemy of the soul)
krataios as polemomen
(let us fight fiercely)
pepoithotes eis Christon!
(believers in Christ!)
