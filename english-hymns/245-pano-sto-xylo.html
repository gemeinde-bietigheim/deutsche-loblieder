245 PANO STO XYLO

Greek Hymns - No. 245 | "ΠΑΝΩ ΣΤΟ ΞΥΛΟ"
Verse 1:
Pano sto xylo mes sto skotadi
(On the wood in the dark)
S' eida thlimmeno, choris omorfia...
(I saw you sad, without beauty...)
O Vasilia mou, glyke Sotira,
(O my King, sweet Saviour,)
mes stin odyni kai sta karfia,
(through the odium and the nails,)
vastaxes oles tis amarties,
(you bore all sins,)
kathe mas pono ekei ston stafro!
(all our pain there on the cross!)
Kathe mas pono ekei ston stafro!
(Our every pain there on the cross!)
Verse 2:
Pires ti thesi, nai, tin diki mas
(You took the place, yes, ours)
ki egin' o thanatos gia mas zoi!
(and death became our life!)
Anoix' o dromos gia ta ourania
(The way to heaven is open)
kai vriskei chari kathe psychi!
(and every soul finds grace!)
Kyrie, tora S' efcharistoyme gia tin
(Lord, now we thank You for her)
Agapi Sou ti thafmasti!
(Love you the fan!)
Agapi Sou ti thafmasti!
(Love you the fan!)
