408 O KYRIOS Th' ANOIXEI ODO GIA ME

Greek Hymns - No. 408 | "Ο ΚΥΡΙΟΣ Θ' ΑΝΟΙΞΕΙ ΟΔΟ ΓΙΑ ΜΕ"
Verse 1:
O Kyrios th' anoixei odo gia me!
(The Lord will open a way for me!)
O Kyrios th' anoixei odo gia me!
(The Lord will open a way for me!)
An sto fos peripato
(If in the light I walk)
kai Afton akoloutho,
(and Him I follow,)
o Kyrios th' anoixei odo gia me!
(the Lord will open a way for me!)
Verse 2:
Tha polemisei o Christos gia me!
(Christ will fight for me!)
Tha polemisei o Christos gia me!
(Christ will fight for me!)
Ston agona tis zois
(In the struggle of life)
tha 'mai panta nikitis,
(i will always be a winner)
tha polemisei o Christos gia me!
(Christ will fight for me!)
Verse 3:
Nai, tha nikisei o Christos gia me!
(Yes, Christ will win for me!)
Nai, tha nikisei o Christos gia me!
(Yes, Christ will win for me!)
An sto dromo prochoro
(If on the road I proceed)
kai sikono to Stafro,
(and I raise the Cross,)
nai, tha nikisei o Christos gia me!
(yes, Christ will win for me!)
