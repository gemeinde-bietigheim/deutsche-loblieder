368 NAI, ThA ERGASTO

Greek Hymns - No. 368 | "ΝΑΙ, ΘΑ ΕΡΓΑΣΤΩ"
Verse 1:
Nai, tha ergasto dia ton Iisoy
(Yes, I will work for Jesus)
kai pros doxa Tou kai pros timi
(and to His glory and honor)
chairomai panta pista n' akoloutho,
(always happy to faithfully follow,)
di Afton afino ti zoi!
(for Him I leave life!)
Chorus:
Nai, tha ergasto gi' Afton, tha ergasto
(Yes, I will work for Him, I will work)
gi' Afton se kathe ora tis zois!
(for Him in every hour of life!)
Nai, tha ergasto gi' Afton, tha ergasto
(Yes, I will work for Him, I will work)
gi' Afton mechris eschatis mou pnois!
(for Him until my last breath!)
Verse 2:
Nai, tha ergasto dia ton Iisoy,
(Yes, I will work for Jesus,)
eis Afton anikoume ki emeis,
(to Him we also belong,)
me ti chari Tou ki emeis sothikame
(by His grace we too are saved)
kai tha zoyme panta eftycheis!
(and we will live happily ever after!)
Verse 3:
Nai, tha ergasto dia ton Iisoy,
(Yes, I will work for Jesus,)
den anikoume ston kosmo afto,
(we do not belong to this world)
nai, kai tis psyches pou gyro mas dipsoyn
(yes, and the souls around us who are thirsty)
apaitei na feroume s' Afton!
(demands that we bring to Him!)
Verse 4:
Nai, tha ergasto dia ton Iisoy,
(Yes, I will work for Jesus,)
pantote, opou kai na vretho,
(always, wherever I am,)
apo tis proias eos tis nyktos
(from morning to night)
ennoo gi' Afton na ergasto!
(I mean for Him to work!)
