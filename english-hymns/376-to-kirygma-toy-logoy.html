376 TO KIRYGMA TOY LOGOY

Greek Hymns - No. 376 | "ΤΟ ΚΗΡΥΓΜΑ ΤΟΥ ΛΟΓΟΥ"
Verse 1:
To kirygma tou Logou pantachoy s' oli ti gi
(The preaching of the Word everywhere in all the earth)
charmosynos tous anthropous odigei s' odo zois!
(joyfully he leads men in the way of life!)
O Kyrios Theos toso agapise emas,
(The Lord God so loved us,)
oste to monogeni Tou esteile sti gi gia mas!
(so that His only begotten sent to earth for us!)
Chorus:
Ti chara antichei pantachoy epi tis gis!
(What joy resounds everywhere on earth!)
O Christos pethane ki anoixe odo zois!
(Christ died and opened a way of life!)
Verse 2:
To kirygma tou Logou proskalei kai sy na 'rtheis
(The preaching of the Word invites you to come too)
eis agian eflogia dorean, choris timi!
(holy blessing free, no price!)
«Ego eimai to fos kai o artos tis zois»,
("I am the light and the bread of life,")
ean eltheis sy me pisti, panta tha 'sai eftychis!
(if you come with faith, you will always be happy!)
Verse 3:
To kirygma tou Logou olous proeidopoiei
(The preaching of the Word warns everyone)
mega kindyno pou echoun apo tin anavoli!
(great danger they have from the postponement!)
Epistrepse s' Afton, mi statheis eis tin odo,
(Return to Him, do not stand in the way,)
oyte piso na koitaxeis, koitaxe eis to stafro!
(don't even look back, look at the cross!)
Verse 4:
To kirygma tou Logou dystycheis amartoloys
(The preaching of the Word wretched sinners)
proskalei eis sotiria kai megalous kai mikroys!
(invites both young and old to salvation!)
Pistos kai alithis ein' o Logos tou Stafroy,
(Faithful and true is the Word of the Cross,)
oloi na sothoyn kaloyntai me to aima tou Christoy!
(all are called to be saved by the blood of Christ!)
