124 EK TIS PLANIS SOThEIS

Greek Hymns - No. 124 | "ΕΚ ΤΗΣ ΠΛΑΝΗΣ ΣΩΘΕΙΣ"
Verse 1:
Ek tis planis sotheis me to aima tou Amnoy
(You are saved from error by the blood of the Lamb)
tha vadizo sta ichni Aftoy!
(I will walk in His footsteps!)
Stin psychi mou chara diarkis, o, plimmyrei!
(In my soul joy abiding, oh, it floods!)
ki' o Christos en emoi katoikei!
(and Christ dwells in me!)
Chorus:
Lytrotheis tha aino pantachoy, opou kai an efretho,
(You are redeemed I will praise everywhere, wherever I am,)
tha vadizo ekei, op' Aftos me odigei
(I will walk where He leads me)
kai tha meno pistos en Afto!
(and I will remain faithful to Him!)
Verse 2:
Lytrotheis ek pollon kai frikton amartion
(You are redeemed from many and horrible sins)
tha vadizo sto fos tou Theoy!
(I will walk in the light of God!)
Den fovoymai posos tis psychis mou ton echthron,
(I do not fear the enemy to the core of my soul,)
tha niko me tin charin Aftoy!
(I will overcome by His grace!)
Verse 3:
Lytrotheis, lytrotheis ek thanatou kai orgis
(Redeemed, redeemed from death and wrath)
diarkos pantachoy tha aino!
(always everywhere I will praise!)
Ti megali chara! O oiktirmon Lytrotis
(What a great joy! The gracious Redeemer)
m'esosen, ina Afton ergastho!
(He saved me, so that I may work for Him!)
Verse 4:
Stin psychin mou entos lampei tora neon fos
(A new light now shines within my soul)
ki eimai teknon Theoy alithos!
(and I am truly a child of God!)
Poson epithymo di' Afton na ergastho
(How I long to work for Him)
aseveis odigon ston Christon!
(ungodly guides to Christ!)
