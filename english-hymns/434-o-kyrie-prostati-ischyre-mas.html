434 O KYRIE, PROSTATI ISChYRE MAS

Greek Hymns - No. 434 | "Ω ΚΥΡΙΕ, ΠΡΟΣΤΑΤΗ ΙΣΧΥΡΕ ΜΑΣ"
Verse 1:
O Kyrie, prostati ischyre mas,
(O Lord, our mighty protector,)
mono me Se nikoyme ton echthro
(only with You do we defeat the enemy)
kai asfaleis to cheri Sou kratontas
(and secure Thy hand holding)
vadizoume to dromo to steno!
(we walk the narrow road!)
kai asfaleis to cheri Sou kratontas
(and secure Thy hand holding)
vadizoume to dromo to steno!
(we walk the narrow road!)
Verse 2:
O archige, kratoyme t' onoma Sou,
(O chief, we keep Thy name,)
to pio agapito mes sti zoi!
(the dearest thing in life!)
Esy dikaiosyni mas kai doxa
(You are our justice and glory)
kai tis agapis vasilias Esy!
(and You, king of love!)
Esy dikaiosyni mas kai doxa
(You are our justice and glory)
kai tis agapis vasilias Esy!
(and You, king of love!)
Verse 3:
Adynatoi kai omos prochoroyme,
(Weak and yet we move forward,)
to asma tou thriamvou mas metha!
(the song of triumph intoxicates us!)
Simaia mas monacha t' onoma Sou
(Only Your name is our flag)
pou 'nai grammeno mesa stin kardia!
(which is written in the heart!)
Simaia mas monacha t' onoma Sou
(Only Your name is our flag)
pou 'nai grammeno mesa stin kardia!
(which is written in the heart!)
Verse 4:
S' Esena anapafmenoi prochoroyme,
(Resting in You, we move forward,)
i machi ma kai i niki ein' apo Se!
(the battle and the victory are from You!)
Pernontas tis chryses ouranies pyles,
(Passing the golden gates of heaven,)
tha zoym' ekei aionia nikites!
(I will dwell there eternally victorious!)
Pernontas tis chryses ouranies pyles,
(Passing the golden gates of heaven,)
tha zoym' ekei aionia nikites!
(I will dwell there eternally victorious!)
