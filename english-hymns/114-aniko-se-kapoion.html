114 ANIKO SE KAPOION

Greek Hymns - No. 114 | "ΑΝΗΚΩ ΣΕ ΚΑΠΟΙΟΝ"
Verse 1:
Aniko se kapoion kai paradothika s' Afton,
(I belong to someone and surrendered to Him,)
stafrothike o Agios gia mena ton amartolo!
(the Saint was crucified for me the sinner!)
Prospathisa ston kosmo afton monachos na dikaiotho,
(I tried in this world alone to justify myself,)
ma i zoi pou ekana itan makria ap' to Christo!
(but the life I led was far from Christ!)
Chorus:
Iisoy Christe mou, Esy, megale Vasilia,
(Jesus Christ, You, great King,)
tis amarties den mou tis logariazeis pia,
(You don't impute my sins to me anymore,)
den tis thymasai, gia Sena einai parelthon,
(you don't remember them, for you they are past,)
gi' afto, Christe mou, aionia tha Se ymno!
(for this, my Christ, I will eternally praise You!)
Verse 2:
Sta chronia pou spatalisa sto diava toytis tis zois,
(In the years I've wasted on this life's journey,)
i kathe mou paravasi eiche to stigma tis ntropis.
(my every transgression had the stigma of shame.)
Ma ola afta xechastikan, giati aniko sto Christo!
(But all that was forgotten, because I belong to Christ!)
Kai den mporei na pei kaneis giati sygchorises afton.
(And no one can say why you forgave him.)
