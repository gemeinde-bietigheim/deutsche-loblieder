482 ERChETAI, ERChETAI

Greek Hymns - No. 482 | "ΕΡΧΕΤΑΙ, ΕΡΧΕΤΑΙ"
Chorus:
Erchetai, erchetai, o Iisoys mas erchetai!
(He is coming, he is coming, Jesus is coming to us!)
Erchetai, erchetai, o vasilias mas erchetai!
(He is coming, he is coming, our king is coming!)
Kai tha pao mazi Tou ekei psila
(And I will go with Him up there)
kai tha zo m' Afton aionia,
(and I will live with Him forever,)
stin ourania vasileia tis charas!
(in the heavenly kingdom of joy!)
Verse 1:
To xero kala pos eplisiase o kairos!
(I know well that the time has come!)
Gi' afto kai ego tha Ton prosmeno to Christo,
(That's why I too will wait for Christ,)
de tha apokamo kai tha agrypno gi' Afton!
(I will not rest and watch for Him!)
Verse 2:
I mera aftoy einai konta, poly konta!
(The day of this is near, very near!)
Agrypna kai sy, giati o Christos se kartera,
(Awake you too, because Christ cares for you,)
min apokameis, alla prosmene Afton!
(do not earn, but wait for Him!)
Verse 3:
Einai kalos o Kyrios mas o Theos!
(Our Lord God is good!)
Ein' vasilias ton vasiliadon o Christos!
(Christ is the king of kings!)
Gi' afto doxase kai prosmene Afton!
(Therefore glorify and wait upon Him!)
