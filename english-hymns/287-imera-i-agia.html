287 IMERA I AGIA

Greek Hymns - No. 287 | "ΗΜΕΡΑ Η ΑΓΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Imera i agia, imera tou Theoy,
(Holy day, God's day,)
imera ourania, eflogiti pantoy!
(heavenly day, blessed everywhere!)
Theias charas imera pnefmatikis tryfis,
(A day of divine joy and spiritual sorrow,)
iliou lamprotera, arritos eftychis!
(brighter than the sun, exceedingly happy!)
Verse 2:
O valsamon ton kopon, frontidon, merimnon,
(O balm of labors, cares, cares,)
anapafsis anthropon, tou ouranoy eikon!
(rest of men, of heaven of images!)
I ekklisia psallei ymnoysa ton Theon,
(The congregation sings praises to God,)
kardia pasa pallei latreyousa Afton!
(every heart beats adoring Him!)
Verse 3:
En tayti, nai, parichthi ek midenos to fos,
(In this, yes, light was produced from nothing,)
ek ton nekron anesti en tayti o Christos!
(Christ rose from the dead in this!)
En tayti katepemfthi to Pneyma ap' ouranoy,
(In him the Spirit from heaven was poured out,)
Fos dielampsen ex ypsous triployn apantachoy!
(Light shone from a triple height everywhere!)
Verse 4:
Eis erimon tou viou tin anydron aftin
(In the desert of life the arid her)
namaton einai theion i drosera pigi!
(The cool spring is divine!)
Ek taytis theoroymen me omma katharon
(Hence they are considered clean)
tin pothitin patrida ouranion Sion!
(the longed-for homeland of heavenly Zion!)
