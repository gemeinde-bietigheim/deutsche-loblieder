299 T' ARNOYMAI, O ChRISTE MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 299 | "Τ' ΑΡΝΟΥΜΑΙ, Ω ΧΡΙΣΤΕ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
T' arnoymai, o Christe mou, ola,
(I deny, O my Christ, all,)
thelo s' Esena na dotho!
(I want to give myself to You!)
Ta theoro ola zimia.
(I consider it all a loss.)
To thelima Sou na ektelo!
(Your will to do!)
Chorus:
To thelima Sou, o Christe mou,
(Thy will, O my Christ,)
na me krata konta se Se,
(keep me close to You,)
osotou eltheis pali me dynami megali
(until you come again with great power)
ki aionia s' Esena konta na zo!
(and to live eternally close to You!)
Verse 2:
Tora miso tin amartia
(Now I hate sin)
kai kathe astati odo,
(and every rough road,)
epipotho zoi agia,
(I seek life holy,)
to thelima Sou to agatho!
(Your will is good!)
Verse 3:
I thelisi Sou i glykeia
(Your will is sweetness)
zei stin kardia mou, o Thee!
(lives in my heart, O God!)
Eirini kai eleftheria
(Peace and freedom)
tha me frouroyne pantote!
(they will always guard me!)
Verse 4:
Se Se tha meino, Lytroti mou,
(In You I will abide, my Redeemer,)
pote mou na mi planitho!
(may I never go astray!)
Nai, den mporo choris Esena,
(Yes, I can't without you,)
to thelima Sou tha ektelo!
(Your will I will do!)
