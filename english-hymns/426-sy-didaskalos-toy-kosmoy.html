426 SY DIDASKALOS TOY KOSMOY

Greek Hymns - No. 426 | "ΣΥ ΔΙΔΑΣΚΑΛΟΣ ΤΟΥ ΚΟΣΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Sy didaskalos tou kosmou, ton anthropon Lytrotis,
(Thou teacher of the world, Redeemer of men,)
Sy to fos tou pneymatos mou, panta Sy me odigeis!
(You are the light of my spirit, you always lead me!)
De tha papso, o Thee mou, t' onoma Sou na ymno
(I will not cease, O my God, to praise Your name)
kai gia panta sti zoi mou Se, Christe, n' akoloutho!
(and forever in my life You, Christ, to follow!)
Chorus:
Eis ta kymata tou viou to timoni Sy krateis,
(In the waves of life You hold the rudder,)
eis tis thlipseis kai tous fovous asfalos me odigeis!
(you certainly lead me to sorrows and fears!)
Verse 2:
Sy tou sympantos o Plastis didaxe me tin odo,
(O Maker of the universe, teach me the way,)
na vadizo panta chairon kai na se doxologo,
(to always walk with joy and praise you,)
na ektelo tis entoles Sou, na eimai enas mathitis,
(to carry out Your commands, to be a disciple,)
me to Logo Sou gia fos mou kai pyxida tis zois!
(with Your Word for my light and compass of life!)
Verse 3:
Ti fthora tou kosmou afino, ployto, doxa kai timi,
(The corruption of the world I leave, wealth, glory and honor,)
sto Christo vrisko ta ployti, ti chara kai ti zoi!
(in Christ I find riches, joy and life!)
De zitao na ploutiso oyte kai na doxastho,
(I don't ask to get rich or to be glorified,)
alla ton kairo mou dino dia Afton na ergastho!
(but I give my time for Him to work!)
Verse 4:
Yposchethike o Christos mas oti ekei stous ouranoys
(Our Christ promised that there in the heavens)
oiko mega etoimazei gia agious kai pistoys!
(mega house prepares for saints and believers!)
Gi' afto tharros adelfoi mou, na 'maste pistoi s' Afton,
(Therefore, my brothers, take courage, let us be faithful to Him,)
tapeinoi kai me agapi, as douleyoume gi' Afton!
(humble and with love, let us work for Him!)
