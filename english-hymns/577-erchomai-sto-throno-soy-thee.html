577 ERChOMAI STO ThRONO SOY ThEE

Greek Hymns - No. 577 | "ΕΡΧΟΜΑΙ ΣΤΟ ΘΡΟΝΟ ΣΟΥ ΘΕΕ"
Verse 1:
Erchomai sto throno Sou, Thee,
(I come to Your throne, O God,)
sta agia ton agion, Kyrie!
(in the holy of holies, Lord!)
Ekei pou to ego mou chanetai,
(Where my ego is lost,)
ekei pou oi skepseis oles svinoune!
(where thoughts all fade away!)
Aggeloi pantoy trigyro Sou
(Angels all around You)
gonatizoun sti megalosyni Sou!
(they kneel to Your majesty!)
Se latreyoun kai Se proskynoyn
(They worship You and worship You)
kai me eflaveia ti doxa Sou ymnoyn!
(and with reverence Thy glory they praise!)
Chorus:
Me deos stekomai mprosta Sou
(In awe I stand before You)
kai liono mesa sti matia Sou!
(and I melt in Your gaze!)
Sta podia Sou mprosta lygizei i kardia,
(At Your feet the heart bends,)
kai chanomai sti fotia
(and I'm lost in the fire)
tis parousias Sou, tis agiosynis Sou!
(of Your presence, of Your holiness!)
Me deos stekomai mprosta Sou!
(I stand in awe before You!)
