224 SALEYOYN DAFNES

Greek Hymns - No. 224 | "ΣΑΛΕΥΟΥΝ ΔΑΦΝΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Saleyoun dafnes, dakryzoun matia sto diava tou Lytroti,
(They wave laurels, tearful eyes at the cross of the Redeemer,)
mikroi megaloi kalosorizoun to mega Thriamvefti!
(young and old welcome the great Triumphant!)
Stis amartias ti chora skorpa to fos Tou glyka
(In sin the country spreads His light sweetly)
ki ap' tis thlimmenes morfes mas feygei tou ponou i skia!
(and from our sad forms the shadow of pain leaves!)
Chorus:
Kapote sklavoi tis amartias,
(Once slaves to sin,)
tora eleytheroi pia,
(now free)
Sou yposchomaste pisti,
(We pledge you faith,)
Christe, aionia, vathia!
(Christ, eternal, deep!)
Verse 2:
Ki an potismenes apo ton pono ein' oi kardies mas, Christe,
(And if our hearts are watered with pain, Christ,)
san tis kithares glykosimainoun kai perimenoun Ese!
(like the guitars they sweetly mean and wait for You!)
O! na schizontan ta ourania kapoia imera glykia
(Oh! that the heavens would tear apart some sweet day)
kai na erchosoun me doxa, gia na mas pareis psila!
(and come with glory, to take us up!)
Verse 3:
Stenazei gyro oli i fysi, demeni mes sti fthora,
(All nature groans around, bound in decay,)
se mas akoma tous lytromenous einai o ponos konta!
(to us still the redeemed the pain is near!)
Ma na pou tora diavaineis, erchesai thriamveftis,
(But where are you crossing now, you are coming in triumph,)
gia n' apantiseis stous pothous kathe pistis sou psychis!
(to answer the desires of every faithful soul of yours!)
