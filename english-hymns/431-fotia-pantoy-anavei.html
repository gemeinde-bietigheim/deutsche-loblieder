431 FOTIA PANTOY ANAVEI

Greek Hymns - No. 431 | "ΦΩΤΙΑ ΠΑΝΤΟΥ ΑΝΑΒΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Fotia pantoy anavei kai polemos sti gi,
(Fire burns everywhere and war on earth,)
oryetai o echthros mou kai tin psychi mou zitei.
(my enemy is coming and he is seeking my soul.)
Akoyo foni tou Christoy mou na legei «prochora m' eme,
(I hear the voice of my Christ saying "go with me,)
pote ego de s' afino, de tha s' afiso pote!».
(I never leave you, I will never leave you!")
Chorus:
Oudepote, oudepote,
(Never, never)
o Kyrios de tha m' afisei monon oudepote!
(the Lord will never leave me alone!)
Oudepote, oudepote,
(Never, never)
mou legei mes sto Logo, de tha m' afisei pote!
(he tells me in the Word, he will never leave me!)
Verse 2:
Eis tis dokimasies kai sti steni odo
(In the trials and the narrow way)
me voithei o Christos mou na fero pantoy to stafro!
(my Christ helps me to carry the cross everywhere!)
Eis tis elleipseis kai chreies frontizei Aftos gia me
(He takes care of my shortcomings and needs)
kai sti Grafi mou to grafei de tha m' afisei pote!
(and in my Bible it is written, he will never leave me!)
Verse 3:
Apethane o Iisoys mou ekei sto Golgotha,
(My Jesus died there on Calvary,)
gia me echythei to aima, gia me ekentithei i plefra!
(for my blood pours out, for my side is stretched out!)
Ypesti to pan na me sosei kai mesiteyei gia me,
(He suffered all to save me and intercedes for me,)
mou legei panta o Iisoys mou de tha m' afisei pote!
(he always tells me my Jesus will never leave me!)
Verse 4:
Aftos mas yposchethike na 'rthei kai pali edo,
(He promised us to come here again,)
sti gi na vasilepsei ki ego tha eimai m' Afton!
(on earth to reign and I will be with Him!)
Mazi me tous agious tha parei kai eme,
(He will take me with the saints,)
na m' echei aionios, de tha m' afisei pote!
(may he have me forever, he will never leave me!)
