2 AINOYME OI PISTOI

Greek Hymns - No. 2 | "ΑΙΝΟΥΜΕ ΟΙ ΠΙΣΤΟΙ"
Verse 1:
Ainoyme oi pistoi ton Plasti mas Theo,
(We the faithful praise our Creator God,)
Despoti ouranoy kai gis ton Krataio!
(Lord of heaven and earth, Krataeus!)
Mprosta sto Throno sou to gony tapeina
(Before your Throne I humbly kneel)
tha klinoume se prosefchi eflavika!
(we will bow down in prayer reverently!)
Verse 2:
Aggelon straties, Se, Plasti, anymnoyn,
(Armies of angels, In, Plasti, sing,)
to fotovolo throno Sou perikykloyn!
(they surround Your shining throne!)
Me tharros sto Christo proserchomai ki ego,
(With courage I also come to Christ,)
to onoma Sou, Ypsiste, doxologo!
(Your name, Exalted, I praise!)
Verse 3:
Pateras oiktirmon edeichthikes, Thee,
(You have proved yourself, O God, to be a father of mercy.)
thysiases ton Iisoy dia eme!
(you sacrificed Jesus for me!)
Aftos eis to Stafro plirose tin poini,
(He on the Cross paid the penalty,)
exaleipse ptaismaton mou tin enochi!
(Take away my guilt!)
Verse 4:
Sto Pneyma t' Agio vaptizeis tin psychi,
(In the Holy Spirit you baptize the soul,)
pou ekzitaei tapeina stin prosefchi!
(who humbly seeks in prayer!)
Parecheis di' Aftoy fos, anesi, zoi,
(You provide by Him light, comfort, life,)
chara, agapi, dynami pnefmatiki!
(joy, love, spiritual strength!)
Verse 5:
O! Einai thafmasta, Thee, ta dora Sou!
(Oh! Wonderful, O God, are Your gifts!)
Anekfrasti i pros emas agapi Sou!
(Your love for us is inexpressible!)
Se Se prosferoume ti doxa kai timi!
(To You we offer glory and honor!)
Patera, Yio kai Pneyma Agio, Amin!
(Father, Son and Holy Spirit, Amen!)
