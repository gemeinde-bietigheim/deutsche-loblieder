650 STAChY MOY GERMENO

Greek Hymns - No. 650 | "ΣΤΑΧΥ ΜΟΥ ΓΕΡΜΕΝΟ"
Verse 1:
Stachy mou germeno, psachneis gia na eyreis
(My ear bent, you seek to find)
mia chrysi iliachtida gia na zestatheis.
(a golden ray of sunshine to warm you.)
Psachneis n' akoumpiseis to vary fortio,
(Looking to touch the heavy load,)
gyro sou agkathia kai tha trypitheis.
(thorns around you and you will be pierced.)
Gyro sou agkathia kai tha trypitheis.
(Thorns are around you and you will be pierced.)
Verse 2:
Akou ti foni mou, krazei ponemeni.
(Hear my voice, it cries out in pain.)
Thelo na se vlepo panta dynato!
(I want to see you always strong!)
Pira Ego ti thlipsi! Kai se sena menei
(I took the sadness! And it's up to you)
pano ap' to stafro mou na pareis lytromo!
(above my cross receive redemption!)
Pano ap' to stafro mou na pareis lytromo!
(On my cross be redeemed!)
Verse 3:
Sou edeixa to dromo gia na perpatiseis,
(I showed you the way to walk,)
dos' mou tin kardia sou, einai arketo.
(give me your heart, it's enough)
Kai tha echeis panta o,ti mou zitiseis,
(And you will always have whatever you ask of me,)
chari tha sou dino, giati s' agapo!
(I will give you grace, because I love you!)
Chari tha sou dino, giati s' agapo!
(I will give you grace, because I love you!)
Verse 4:
Stachy mou germeno, koita sta ourania!
(Leaning ear of corn, look to the heavens!)
Koitaxe s' Emena, pano sto stafro!
(Look to Me, on the cross!)
Afise s' Emena to vary fortio!
(Leave the heavy burden to Me!)
Pethana gia sena kai se agapo!
(I died for you and I love you!)
Pethana gia sena kai se agapo!
(I died for you and I love you!)
