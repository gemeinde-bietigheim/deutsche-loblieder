465 YPARChEI ODOS

Greek Hymns - No. 465 | "ΥΠΑΡΧΕΙ ΟΔΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Yparchei odos pou fantazei orthi
(There is a road that seems right)
ston anthropo ton epi gis,
(to man on earth,)
ma ta teli aftis odigoyn sto chamo,
(but its ends lead to destruction,)
makria apo to Sotira Christo!
(away from Christ the Savior!)
Chorus:
Makari na pareis ti moni odo,
(May you take the only road)
na vreis tis zois to skopo,
(to find the purpose of life,)
na vreis tin alitheia aftis tis zois
(to find the truth of this life)
konta sto Sotira Christo!
(near Christ the Savior!)
Verse 2:
Oi dromoi mas einai poly chamiloi,
(Our roads are very low,)
na fthasoun psila den mporoyn.
(they cannot reach high.)
Planiesai sti skepsi pos einai kaloi,
(You're fooled into thinking they're good,)
mprosta sto Theo de metroyn!
(before God they do not count!)
Verse 3:
Oi dromoi mas, oso ki an deichnoun sostoi,
(Our ways, however right they may seem,)
latheyoun, den ein' ikanoi
(they are wrong, they are not capable)
na dosoun afto pou zita i psychi,
(to give what the soul asks for,)
na vroun ti pigi ti sosti!
(to find the right source!)
