191 TI ChARA ALIThINI

Greek Hymns - No. 191 | "ΤΙ ΧΑΡΑ ΑΛΗΘΙΝΗ"
Verse 1:
Ti chara alithini kai teleia allagi!
(What a real joy and perfect change!)
Edechthika ton Iisoy.
(I accepted Jesus.)
Poso eim' eftychis ap' afti ti stigmi!
(How happy I am at this moment!)
Edechthika ton Iisoy!
(I accepted Jesus!)
Chorus:
Edechthika ton Iisoy
(I accepted Jesus)
kai tora zo mesa sto fos!
(and now I live in the light!)
Plimmyrizei i chara stin psychi mou entos,
(Joy floods my soul within,)
edechthika ton Iisoy!
(I accepted Jesus!)
Verse 2:
Afisa parefthys ti mataia zoi,
(I forsook vain life,)
san dechthika ton Iisoy!
(like I accepted Jesus!)
Kai tin plani aftis tis mataias zois,
(And the delusion of this vain life,)
san dechthika ton Iisoy!
(like I accepted Jesus!)
Verse 3:
Tha vadizo ekei pou zoi katoikei,
(I will walk where life dwells,)
edechthika ton Iisoy!
(I accepted Jesus!)
Kai tha psallo mazi eis tin alli zoi,
(And I'll sing along in the afterlife,)
edechthika ton Iisoy!
(I accepted Jesus!)
Verse 4:
De tha papso pote na ymno to Theo,
(I will never stop praising God,)
edechthika ton Iisoy!
(I accepted Jesus!)
Kai aionia tha zo na doxazo Afton,
(And forever I will live to glorify Him,)
edechthika ton Iisoy!
(I accepted Jesus!)
