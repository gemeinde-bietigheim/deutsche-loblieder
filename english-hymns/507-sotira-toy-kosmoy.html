507 SOTIRA TOY KOSMOY

Greek Hymns - No. 507 | "ΣΩΤΗΡΑ ΤΟΥ ΚΟΣΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Sotira tou kosmou, to cheri Sou dos' mou
(Savior of the world, give me Your hand)
na mpo stou ouranoy to palati,
(to enter heaven's palace,)
na noioso galini, aionia eirini
(to feel serenity, eternal peace)
kai fos stis kardias mou ta vathi!
(and light in the depths of my heart!)
Alliloyia, Alliloyia, Alliloyia... Kyrie!
(Hallelujah, Hallelujah, Hallelujah... Lord!)
Alliloyia, Alliloyia, Alliloyia... Kyrie!
(Hallelujah, Hallelujah, Hallelujah... Lord!)
Verse 2:
Sotira tou kosmou, to cheri Sou dos' mou
(Savior of the world, give me Your hand)
na mpo sti chrysi politeia,
(to enter the golden state,)
ekei pou ta panta m' agapis mpalanta
(where everything is a ballad of love)
ymnoyn tou Theoy ti sofia!
(praise the God of wisdom!)
Alliloyia, Alliloyia, Alliloyia... Kyrie!
(Hallelujah, Hallelujah, Hallelujah... Lord!)
Alliloyia, Alliloyia, Alliloyia... Kyrie!
(Hallelujah, Hallelujah, Hallelujah... Lord!)
Verse 3:
Sotira tou kosmou, to cheri Sou dos' mou
(Savior of the world, give me Your hand)
na mpo stin yperlampri pyli,
(to enter the shining gate,)
ekei pou de fthanei ton oplon i kani,
(where the barrel of weapons does not reach,)
ekei pou den erchetai deili!
(where no coward comes!)
Alliloyia, Alliloyia, Alliloyia... Kyrie!
(Hallelujah, Hallelujah, Hallelujah... Lord!)
Alliloyia, Alliloyia, Alliloyia... Kyrie!
(Hallelujah, Hallelujah, Hallelujah... Lord!)
