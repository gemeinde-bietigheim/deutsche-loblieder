533 ERChETAI O IISOYS ChRISTOS!

Greek Hymns - No. 533 | "ΕΡΧΕΤΑΙ Ο ΙΗΣΟΥΣ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ!"
Verse 1:
Erchetai o Iisoys Christos!
(Jesus Christ is coming!)
As kanei me spoudi
(Let him study)
kathe kardia eis Afton
(every heart to Him)
thermin ypodochi, thermin ypodochi
(warm welcome, warm welcome)
thermin, thermin ypodochi!
(warm, warm welcome!)
Verse 2:
Ston nikiti mas Iisoy,
(To our conqueror Jesus,)
as psalloun oi pistoi,
(let the faithful sing,)
apo ti gi kai ourano
(from earth and sky)
chara as antichei, chara as antichei,
(joy resound, joy resound,)
chara, chara as antichei!
(joy, let joy resound!)
Verse 3:
Dakrya, thlipseis, stenagmoi
(Tears, sorrows, sighs)
tha paysoun ola ekei,
(it will all stop there,)
o Kyrios tha ein' to fos
(the Lord will be the light)
kai tha mas eflogei kai tha mas eflogei
(and he will bless us and bless us)
kai tha, nai tha mas eflogei!
(and he will, yes he will bless us!)
Verse 4:
Me chari kai alitheia
(With grace and truth)
ton kosmo dioikei,
(the world rules,)
tin thafmasti agapi Tou
(His wonderful love)
kathenas as ymnei, kathenas as ymnei,
(let everyone praise, let everyone praise)
kathe... kathenas as ymnei!
(everyone... let everyone praise!)
Verse 5:
Erchet' o Iisoys Christos!
(Jesus Christ is coming!)
As kanei me spoudi
(Let him study)
kathe kardia eis Afton
(every heart to Him)
thermin ypodochi, thermin ypodochi
(warm welcome, warm welcome)
thermin, thermin ypodochi!
(warm, warm welcome!)
