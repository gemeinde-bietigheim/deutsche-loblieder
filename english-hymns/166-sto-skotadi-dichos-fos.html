166 STO SKOTADI DIChOS FOS

Greek Hymns - No. 166 | "ΣΤΟ ΣΚΟΤΑΔΙ ΔΙΧΩΣ ΦΩΣ"
Verse 1:
Sto skotadi dichos fos perpatoysa monachos,
(In the darkness without light I walked a monk,)
koitaza edo ki ekei thlipsi, ponos, fylaki.
(I looked here and there sadness, pain, prison.)
Rotoysa panta tin alitheia, gia na vro,
(I always asked the truth, to find,)
zoi zitoysa, to dromo ton alithino!
(I was asking for life, the true way!)
Iisoy, Iisoy, eisai Sy!
(Jesus, Jesus, it is You!)
Verse 2:
Ego eim' i odos, i alitheia kai to fos!
(I am the way, the truth and the light!)
Ego eim' i archi kai to telos ki i zoi!
(I am the beginning and the end and life!)
Ta panta eimai ego, o Kyrios!
(Everything is I, the Lord!)
Chara sou dino, eirini kai zoi ateleioti,
(I give you joy, peace and life without end,)
s' agapo, s' agapo!
(I love you, I love you!)
Verse 3:
Gios mou eisai kai s' agapo, koitaxe ston ourano,
(You are my son and I love you, look at the sky,)
palati echo Ego gia se, mi deiliaseis sti gi pote!
(I have a palace for you, never be afraid on earth!)
Tha echeis thlipsi, na skeftesai Eme!
(You will have sorrow, thinking of Me!)
Stafro tha echeis, ma fonaze se Me na se voitho!
(You will have a cross, but he was calling me to help you!)
S' agapo, s' agapo!
(I love you, I love you!)
Verse 4:
Otan eisai konta mou Esy, o ponos ginetai giorti,
(When You are near me, pain becomes a celebration,)
ki otan niotho na me koitas, feygoun ola monomias!
(and when I feel you looking at me, everything goes away at once!)
Antlo agapi ap' to vlemma Sou, Christe,
(I draw love from Your gaze, Christ,)
kouragio pairno na mi stamato pote to dromo Sou
(I take courage to never stop Your way)
n' akoloutho, S' agapo!
(to follow, I love you!)
