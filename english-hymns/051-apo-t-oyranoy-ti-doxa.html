51 APO T' OYRANOY TI DOXA

Greek Hymns - No. 51 | "ΑΠΟ Τ' ΟΥΡΑΝΟΥ ΤΗ ΔΟΞΑ"
Verse 1:
Apo t' ouranoy ti doxa irthe tou Theoy o Yios,
(From heaven came the glory of God the Son,)
apo ploysios egine ftochos!
(from rich to poor!)
Ap' to throno tou Patera irthe kato edo sti gi
(From the Father's throne he came down here to earth)
o Christos gia olous mas na stafrothei!
(may Christ be crucified for all of us!)
Chorus:
Agapi pou lytronei, vevaionei
(Love that redeems, affirms)
kai fernei elpida mesa stin kardia!΄
(and brings hope into the heart!')
Aionia ekei tha psalloume gia Sena,
(Forever there we will sing of You,)
pou mas echarises to Lytroti!
(for giving us the Redeemer!)
Verse 2:
Tin agkali tou Patera afise ston ourano
(He left the Father's embrace in heaven)
ki irthe na vrei ton amartolo!
(and he came to find the sinner!)
Plirose tin enochi mou m' aima Tou polytimo,
(He paid my guilt with His precious blood,)
kai mia mera tha Ton do ston ourano!
(and one day I will see Him in heaven!)
