304 ANISYChOS EINAI O KOSMOS

Greek Hymns - No. 304 | "ΑΝΗΣΥΧΟΣ ΕΙΝΑΙ Ο ΚΟΣΜΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Anisychos einai o kosmos,
(The world is restless,)
anisychi kai i psychi,
(restless soul too,)
ma ti galini vrika mono
(but I only found peace)
eis ti gonia tis prosefchis!
(in the prayer corner!)
Chorus:
Stin endoxi gonia ekei
(In the glorious corner there)
ta vari feygoun ki' i psychi
(the burdens leave and the soul)
vriskei galini diarki
(finds lasting peace)
se mia gonia, stin prosefchi!
(in a corner, in prayer!)
Verse 2:
Ekei lavaino dynami mou,
(That's where I get my strength,)
gia na nikiso ton echthro,
(to defeat the enemy,)
fovos kaneis eis tin psychi mou,
(fear no one in my soul,)
otan m' Ekeinon omilo!
(when I talk to Him!)
Verse 3:
Otan ta nefi me kyklonoun,
(When the clouds surround me,)
ki otan kaneis de m' ennoei,
(and when no one understands me,)
oi ouranoi xanachrysonoun
(the skies turn golden again)
sti thesi afti tis prosefchis!
(in this place of prayer!)
Verse 4:
O agia thesi, pou anoigei
(O holy place, that opens)
ton ourano me dynami
(the sky with power)
ki ola ta nefi dialyei,
(and all the clouds dissolve,)
eflogimeni prosefchi!
(blessed prayer!)
