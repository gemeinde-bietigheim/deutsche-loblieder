47 O, TI ChARA, NA EISAI

Greek Hymns - No. 47 | "Ω, ΤΙ ΧΑΡΑ, ΝΑ ΕΙΣΑΙ"
Verse 1:
O, ti chara, na eisai eis ti steni odo,
(Oh, what joy, to be in the narrow way,)
ton Iisoy na echeis pisto sou odigo!
(have Jesus as your faithful guide!)
Ti doxa aionia, ekei ston ourano,
(What glory forever, there in heaven,)
aionia na eisai mazi me to Christo!
(eternally be with Christ!)
Chorus:
Doxa kai timi s' Afton,
(Glory and honor to Him,)
to Sotira mou Christo!
(my Savior Christ!)
Me afton tha ziso kai tha eftychiso,
(With him I will live and be happy,)
an akoloutho Afton!
(if I follow Him!)
Verse 2:
O, pos potho na vlepo to prosopon Aftoy
(Oh, how I long to see His face)
kai ti foni n' akoyo tou Theiou Lytrotoy!
(and to hear the voice of the Divine Redeemer!)
Aftos tha m' odigisei ekei stous ouranoys
(He will lead me there to heaven)
aionia na eimai me olous tous pistoys!
(forever to be with all believers!)
Verse 3:
Ti eftychis tha eisai, an ton Christo dechteis
(How happy you will be if you accept Christ)
kai eis Aftoy ta podia proseltheis kai richteis!
(and come to His feet and throw yourself!)
Ela kai mi distazeis, speyse, gia na sotheis,
(Come and don't hesitate, hurry, to save yourself,)
pote min anavalleis, giati n' apolesteis;
(never procrastinate, why miss out?)
Verse 4:
Doxa sto Lytroti mou, Dotira tou pantos,
(Glory to my Redeemer, Giver of all,)
apo tin amartia m' elytrosen Aftos!
(He redeemed me from sin!)
Aftos me prostateyei kai me parigorei,
(He protects me and comforts me,)
pote den me afinei, pantote m' odigei!
(never leaves me, always leads me!)
