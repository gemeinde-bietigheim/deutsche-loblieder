659 T' ATSALI KI AN ChTYPA STIN PETRA

Greek Hymns - No. 659 | "Τ' ΑΤΣΑΛΙ ΚΙ ΑΝ ΧΤΥΠΑ ΣΤΗΝ ΠΕΤΡΑ"
Verse 1:
T' atsali ki an chtypa stin petra,
(Even if the steel hits the stone,)
afto tha spasei, tha to deis.
(this will break, you will see.)
Aftos pou eftiaxe t' atsali
(The one who made steel)
einai i petra tis zois!
(it is the stone of life!)
Einai o vrachos ton aionon,
(It is the rock of ages,)
i petra ein' o Iisoys!
(the stone is Jesus!)
T' atsali ein' oi enantioi,
(Steel is the opponents,)
i petra tsakise aftoys...
(the stone crushed them...)
Chorus:
Chtise to spiti sou epano
(Build your house up)
s' afti tin petra tis zois
(on this stone of life)
ki oses mpores kai na erthoun
(and as many as you can come)
pote, pote mi fovitheis!
(never, never fear!)
Chtise to spiti sou epano
(Build your house up)
s' afti tin petra tis zois
(on this stone of life)
ki oses mpores kai na erthoun
(and as many as you can come)
pote, pote mi fovitheis!
(never, never fear!)
Verse 2:
O! se doxazo, Kyrie mou,
(Oh! I praise you, my Lord,)
pou eis' o vrachos tis zois,
(that you are the rock of life,)
pou eis' i petra sotirias,
(where you are the stone of salvation,)
to katafygio tis psychis!
(the refuge of the soul!)
Ki ego to spiti mou echo chtisei
(I too have built my house)
s' afti tin petra tis zois
(on this stone of life)
ki eimai geros ki asfalismenos,
(and I am strong and secure,)
ela ki esy n' asfalisteis!
(come and get insured too!)
