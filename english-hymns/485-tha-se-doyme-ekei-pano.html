485 ThA SE DOYME EKEI PANO

Greek Hymns - No. 485 | "ΘΑ ΣΕ ΔΟΥΜΕ ΕΚΕΙ ΠΑΝΩ"
Verse 1:
Tha se doyme ekei pano
(We'll see you up there)
stin odo ton ouranon,
(on the way to heaven)
min argeis, all' etoimasou,
(don't be late, but get ready)
gia na doyme to Christo!
(to see Christ!)
Chorus:
Tha se doyme, tha se doyme eis tin poli
(We'll see you, we'll see you in town)
ti chrysi ton ouranon!
(the gold of the heavens!)
Tha se doyme, tha se doyme, tha se doyme
(We'll see you, we'll see you, we'll see you)
tin afgi ston Ourano!
(the dawn in Heaven!)
Verse 2:
Ean sy tha fthaseis protos
(If you will arrive first)
eis tin pyli tis Sion,
(at Zion's gate,)
met' oligo tha fanoyme
(I'll see you soon)
kai emeis ston ourano!
(and we in heaven!)
Verse 3:
Eche lampa anammeni,
(keep a light on)
prosmene sy to Christo,
(wait for Christ,)
O! i ora plisiazei
(Oh! the time is drawing near)
na se do ston ourano!
(see you in heaven!)
Verse 4:
O! ti endoxi imera
(Oh! what a glorious day)
synantiseos piston,
(meeting of believers,)
me ton Iisoy mas pleon
(with our Jesus now)
zontes eis ton ourano!
(alive in heaven!)
