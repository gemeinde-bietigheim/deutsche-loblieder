316 I ELPIDA STI ZOI MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 316 | "Η ΕΛΠΙΔΑ ΣΤΗ ΖΩΗ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
I elpida sti zoi mou
(The hope in my life)
kai i moni mou chara
(and my only joy)
einai na vadizo dipla
(is to walk by)
sto Christo pantotina!
(in Christ forever!)
Chorus:
O Amne tou Theoy,
(O Amne of God,)
Lytroti Iisoy,
(Jesus Redeemer,)
s' oli ti zoi mou thelo
(all my life I want)
na vadizo meta Sou!
(to walk after You!)
Verse 2:
Den potho gia mena doxa,
(I do not desire glory for myself,)
de zito chara sti gi,
(I seek no joy on earth,)
as mochtho ki as ypofero,
(let me toil and suffer)
mono na 'maste mazi!
(just be together!)
Verse 3:
Sy makria odigise me
(Take me away)
ap' tou ploytou ti fthora,
(from wealth to decay,)
ap' tin tarachi tou kosmou
(from the turmoil of the world)
stin aionia chara!
(to eternal joy!)
Verse 4:
Echo pisti kai elpida
(I have faith and hope)
san tha eimai meta Sou
(as if I will be after You)
oti tha vretho, sto telos,
(that I will find myself, in the end,)
meta tou Patros Theoy!
(after God the Father!)
