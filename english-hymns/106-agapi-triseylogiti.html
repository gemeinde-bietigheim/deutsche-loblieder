106 AGAPI TRISEYLOGITI

Greek Hymns - No. 106 | "ΑΓΑΠΗ ΤΡΙΣΕΥΛΟΓΗΤΗ"
Verse 1:
Agapi triseflogiti, pou emeines kleismeni
(Dear Triseuloger, that you remained closed)
ston kolpo tou Patera 'kei psila,
(in the gulf of the Father above,)
katevikes, o, toso chamila!
(you have descended, oh, so low!)
Ta varia mas krimata mas se trypisane vathia,
(Our heavy sorrows pierced you deep,)
ma i chari Sou megali kai glykia
(but Your grace is great and sweet)
mas syntripse ti dolia mas kardia!
(break our treacherous heart!)
Mas syntripse ti dolia mas kardia!
(He broke our treacherous heart!)
Chorus:
Mera lampri kai thafmasti
(A glorious and wonderful day)
egine gia mas i simerini!
(today was for us!)
Eftase pia i agapi Tou, anoixe kai i agkali Tou,
(His love has come, His embrace has opened,)
mas esmixe san adelfoys olous tous pistoys!
(unite us as brothers all believers!)
Verse 2:
Agapi pro katavolis tou kosmou matomeni,
(Love before the end of the world bloodied,)
Christe mas, pou ypsosas to stafro
(Our Christ, who lifted up the cross)
ton orismeno tou Theoy kairo,
(God's appointed time,)
meneis monos... O ti ponos! Egkataleipsi pikri!
(you are left alone... Oh what a pain! Abandonment bitter!)
Ki omos echeis eleos giatreftiko
(And yet you have healing mercy)
gia olous kai ton pio amartolo,
(for all and the most sinful,)
gia olous kai ton pio amartolo!
(for everyone and the most sinful!)
Verse 3:
Kai tora mas elytroses ap' ton egoismo mas,
(And now you have freed us from our selfishness,)
mas anoixes ta matia ta kleista
(you opened our closed eyes)
na vlepoume psila kai makria!
(to see high and far!)
Sto chameno, pligomeno kosmo mesa sto kako
(In the lost, wounded world in evil)
as tou dosoume elpida kai zoi
(let's give him hope and life)
na niosei ti diki Sou tin pnoi,
(to feel Your breath,)
na niosei ti diki Sou tin pnoi!
(to feel Your own breath!)
