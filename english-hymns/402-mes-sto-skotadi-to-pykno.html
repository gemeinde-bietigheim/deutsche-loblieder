402 MES STO SKOTADI TO PYKNO

Greek Hymns - No. 402 | "ΜΕΣ ΣΤΟ ΣΚΟΤΑΔΙ ΤΟ ΠΥΚΝΟ"
Verse 1:
Mes sto skotadi to pykno strifogyrizo ki agrypno
(In the thick darkness I twist and keep vigil)
kai mesa eis tin tarachi akoyo mia foni,
(and in the tumult I hear a voice,)
mia foni melodiki na mou milaei stin psychi
(a melodious voice to speak to my soul)
kai na mou dinei stin kardia thermi parigoria!
(and give my heart warm consolation!)
«Mi fovitheis, einai konta,
("Fear not, it is near,)
nai, se prostateyei o Christos,
(yes, Christ protects you,)
stratopedeyei gyro sou me tous aggelous Tou,
(encamps around you with His angels,)
o endoxos Theos!».
(the glorious God!')
Verse 2:
Polles fores mes sti zoi o Iisoys me voithei
(Many times in life Jesus helps me)
ki apo to cheri me krata, Aftos einai konta!
(and he holds me by the hand, He is near!)
Noiotho chara kai sigouria, psallei ki agallet' i kardia,
(I feel joy and confidence, my heart sings and rejoices,)
konta Tou eimai asfalis, o! nai, eim' eftychis!
(near Him I am safe, oh! yes, i'm happy!)
Me to Christo mes stin kardia,
(With Christ in the heart,)
nai, eimai st' alitheia asfalis,
(yes i'm really safe)
giati loipon na fovitho, einai konta mou Aftos,
(so why should I fear, He is near me,)
o Lytrotis Christos!
(Christ the Redeemer!)
Verse 3:
File, an noiotheis tarachi kai sou tarazet' i psychi,
(Friend, if you feel troubled and your soul is troubled,)
st' alitheia eisai dystychis, den eisai eftychis!
(you are really unhappy, you are not happy!)
Gyro sou einai skoteinia, fos den yparchei pouthena,
(All around you is darkness, there is no light anywhere,)
psachneis na vreis parigoria, ma vriskeis symfora!
(you seek to find comfort, but you find calamity!)
O, koitaxe ston ourano!
(Oh, look at the sky!)
ma giati koitazeis chamila;
(but why are you looking down?)
Ston Golgotha tha vreis chara, tha sou charisei fos
(In Calvary you will find joy, it will give you light)
o Iisoys Christos!
(Jesus Christ!)
