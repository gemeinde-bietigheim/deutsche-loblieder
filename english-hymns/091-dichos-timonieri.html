91 DIChOS TIMONIERI

Greek Hymns - No. 91 | "ΔΙΧΩΣ ΤΙΜΟΝΙΕΡΗ"
Verse 1:
Dichos timonieri sti zoi gia pou travas;
(Without a rudder in life where do you pull?)
Dichos odigo mes stin nychtia pou pas;
(Without a guide into the night where are you going?)
Laimarga ta kymata afrisane,
(Gluttonously the waves foamed,)
koita, ta pania sou skisane!
(look, your sails are torn!)
Chorus:
Mono o Christos, mono o stafros
(Only Christ, only the cross)
fernoun sigouria mes stin kardia!
(they bring confidence to the heart!)
Einai thafmastos kai filos dynatos,
(He is wonderful and a strong friend,)
Aftos pou sou chreiazetai ein' o Christos!
(The one you need is Christ!)
Verse 2:
Poios tha sou anoixei monopati na diaveis;
(Who will open a path for you to cross?)
Poios tha se frontisei, gia na min pnigeis;
(Who will take care of you, so you don't drown?)
Poios tha s' agkaliasei, gia na mi chatheis
(Who will embrace you, so that you do not perish?)
mes stin kataigida tis zois;
(into the storm of life?)
Verse 3:
Poios, otan i varka sou vouliazei st' anoichta,
(Who, when your boat sinks offshore,)
poios gia se tha pesei sta vathia nera;
(who for you will fall into the deep water?)
Poios tha sou prosferei mia zesti gonia,
(Who will offer you a warm corner,)
otan sou gkremisoun ti chara;
(when they destroy your joy?)
