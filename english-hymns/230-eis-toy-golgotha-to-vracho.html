230 EIS TOY GOLGOThA TO VRAChO

Greek Hymns - No. 230 | "ΕΙΣ ΤΟΥ ΓΟΛΓΟΘΑ ΤΟ ΒΡΑΧΟ"
Verse 1:
Eis tou Golgotha to vracho
(In Golgotha ​​the rock)
oloi gyro sto stafro,
(all around the cross,)
as ypsosoume to vlemma
(let's look up)
ston kremameno Christo.
(to the hanging Christ.)
Sto Christo, sto Christo,
(In Christ, in Christ,)
ston kremameno Christo!
(to the hanging Christ!)
Verse 2:
Ti mystirio agapis
(What a mystery of love)
xetyligetai mprosta!
(unfolds ahead!)
Olon mas tis amarties
(All our sins)
o Iisoys Christos vasta.
(Jesus Christ is enough.)
Tis vasta, tis vasta,
(The vastas, the vastas,)
o Iisoys Christos vasta!
(Jesus Christ is enough!)
Verse 3:
Me to aima pou stalazei
(With the blood dripping)
ap' ta cheria Tou sti gi,
(from His hands on earth,)
to antilytro metraei
(the change counts)
gia tin kathe mia psychi.
(for every single soul.)
Mia psychi, mia psychi,
(A soul, a soul,)
gia tin kathe mia psychi!
(for every single soul!)
Verse 4:
Oloi gyro Tou as stathoyme
(Let us all stand around Him)
kai me efgnomoni foni,
(and with a grateful voice,)
«o Christe, S' efcharistoyme!»,
("Oh Christ, we thank You!",)
as Tou pei kathe psychi.
(let every soul tell Him.)
As tou eipei, as tou eipei,
(Let him say, let him say,)
as tou eipei kathe psychi!
(let every soul tell him!)
