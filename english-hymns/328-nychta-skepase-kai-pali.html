328 NYChTA SKEPASE KAI PALI

Greek Hymns - No. 328 | "ΝΥΧΤΑ ΣΚΕΠΑΣΕ ΚΑΙ ΠΑΛΙ"
Verse 1:
Nychta skepase kai pali
(Night fell again)
politeies kai choria
(states and villages)
ki oi psyches mas perimenoun
(and our souls are waiting)
ki ein' i thlipsi mas varia!
(and our sorrow is heavy!)
Chorus:
Ela, Iisoy Christe,
(Come, Jesus Christ,)
oloi mas Se prosdokoyme,
(we all expect you,)
ti glykia Sou ti morfi
(Your sweet form)
n' atenisoume pothoyme!
(let's gaze lustfully!)
Verse 2:
Ein' i ora perasmeni
(It's past time)
den akoygetai foni
(no voice is heard)
ki i psychi vasanismeni
(and the soul tormented)
leionei mes' stin prosmoni!
(melts with anticipation!)
Verse 3:
Mas farmakose to psema
(He medicated us with a lie)
kai to misos to pikro,
(and hate the bitter,)
t' adika chymeno aima
(the unjustly spilled blood)
anarithmiton piston!
(countless believers!)
Verse 4:
Ela pali, Latrefte mas,
(Come again, worship us,)
«En to meso tis nychtos»,
("In the middle of the night",)
Iisoy, odigise mas
(Jesus, lead us)
stin patrida tou fotos!
(in the land of light!)
