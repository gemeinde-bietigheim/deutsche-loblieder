534 EPsALLETO PANTA

Greek Hymns - No. 534 | "ΕΨΑΛΛΕΤΟ ΠΑΝΤΑ"
Verse 1:
Epsalleto panta, proeipe to Pneyma
(Chant always, the Spirit foretold)
to telos plisiazei na 'rthei!
(the end is near!)
Skotadi ki aireseis tha afanisthoyne
(Darkness and heresies will disappear)
sto fos to lampro tis afgis!
(in the bright light of the dawn!)
Chorus:
O! Doxa s' Afton to Sotira Christo!
(Oh! Glory to this Savior Christ!)
To skotos parilthe kai lampei to fos
(The darkness is gone and the light is shining)
kai oloi kaloyntai na 'rthoun sti Sion!
(and all are invited to come to Zion!)
Verse 2:
Gematoi me Pneyma, Christoy panoplia,
(Spirit-filled, Christ's armor,)
ntythikame oloi Afton!
(we all clothed Him!)
Ypsonoume panta Christoy sotiria,
(We always exalt Christ's salvation,)
kiryttoum' Afton archigo!
(I preach this leader!)
Verse 3:
Eleytheroi tora, makria Vavylonas
(Free now, away from Babylon)
sti doxa pou dinei o Christos!
(to the glory that Christ gives!)
Chairomaste oloi, chairomaste panta
(We all rejoice, we always rejoice)
pou mas eleftheros' Aftos!
(that he set us free!)
Verse 4:
To orama tora profiti tha ginei,
(The vision now prophet will become,)
pou eimaste mes sti Sion!
(that we are in Zion!)
Eleytheroi tora doxazoume oloi!
(Free now we all glory!)
O, doxa, timi sto Theo!
(Oh, glory, honor to God!)
Verse 5:
Ta dendra vlastanoun, to theros eggizei,
(The trees are sprouting, summer is approaching,)
o Logos mas dinei to fos!
(the Word gives us light!)
Simeia tou kosmou mas deichnoun pos einai
(Signs of the world show us how it is)
plision na elthei o Christos!
(may Christ come near!)
