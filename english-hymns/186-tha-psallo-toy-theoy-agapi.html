186 ThA PsALLO TOY ThEOY AGAPI

Greek Hymns - No. 186 | "ΘΑ ΨΑΛΛΩ ΤΟΥ ΘΕΟΥ ΑΓΑΠΗ"
Verse 1:
Tha psallo tou Theoy agapi pou echo me to Christo,
(I will sing of God's love that I have with Christ,)
chara mou dinei anti lypis kai afesi amartion!
(he gives me joy instead of sorrow and forgiveness of sins!)
Ola ta nefi tha perasoun eis tin yposchesi afti,
(All the clouds will pass to this promise,)
chara mou dinei anti lypis kai doxa pantotini!
(He gives me joy instead of sorrow and eternal glory!)
Chorus:
Chara mou dinei anti lypis, mou dinei agapi perissi!
(He gives me joy instead of sadness, he gives me more love!)
Chara mou dinei anti lypis kai doxa pantotini!
(He gives me joy instead of sorrow and eternal glory!)
Verse 2:
Tha psallo eis to Lytroti mou pou epathe sto Stafro,
(I will sing to my Redeemer who suffered on the Cross,)
ton kosmo me mia thysia na lysei ap' ta desma!
(to free the world with one sacrifice!)
Irthe na sosei tin kardia tin poniri ki apatili,
(He came to save the wicked and deceitful heart,)
na dosei niki ki eftychia kai doxa pantotini!
(to give victory and happiness and glory forever!)
Verse 3:
Tha psallo eis to Lytroti mou pou epathe sto Stafro,
(I will sing to my Redeemer who suffered on the Cross,)
gia na me sosei aionios me chari ki agiasmo!
(to save me eternally with grace and sanctification!)
Eirini, nai, apolamvano, mou dinei pisti zontani,
(Peace, yes, I enjoy, it gives me faith alive,)
anti na echo lypi, chairo me doxa pantotini!
(instead of having sorrow, I rejoice with eternal glory!)
