528 O ThEOS AS EINAI METh IMON!

Greek Hymns - No. 528 | "Ο ΘΕΟΣ ΑΣ ΕΙΝΑΙ ΜΕΘ ΗΜΩΝ!"
Verse 1:
O Theos as einai meth' imon!
(May God be with us!)
Mechri na synantithoyme
(Until we meet)
pali kai syneffranthoyme!
(Congratulations again!)
O Theos as einai meth' imon!
(May God be with us!)
Chorus:
O Theos meth' imon!
(God with us!)
O prostatis ton piston!
(The protector of the faithful!)
O kalos igemon!
(The good ruler!)
O Theos as einai meth' imon!
(May God be with us!)
Verse 2:
O Theos as einai meth' imon!
(May God be with us!)
Pteryges Tou na 'nai skepi,
(His wings be a cover,)
me to manna na mas trefei.
(with the manna to feed us.)
O Theos as einai meth' imon!
(May God be with us!)
Verse 3:
O Theos as einai meth' imon!
(May God be with us!)
Otan mas tarattoun ponoi,
(When we are troubled by pains,)
tharros, den eimaste monoi.
(courage, we are not alone.)
O Theos as einai meth' imon.
(May God be with us.)
Verse 4:
O Theos as einai meth' imon!
(May God be with us!)
Eos otou doym' allilous,
(Until we see each other,)
pantas syggeneis kai filous.
(all relatives and friends.)
O Theos as einai meth' imon!
(May God be with us!)
Verse 5:
O Theos as einai meth' imon!
(May God be with us!)
Mechri ekeini tin imera,
(Until that day,)
pou i salpigx tou Patera
(where the trumpet of the Father)
tha kales' emas ston ourano!
(will call us to heaven!)
