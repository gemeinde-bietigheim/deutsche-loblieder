474 VAINOMEN PROS TIN PATRIDA

Greek Hymns - No. 474 | "ΒΑΙΝΟΜΕΝ ΠΡΟΣ ΤΗΝ ΠΑΤΡΙΔΑ"
Verse 1:
Vainomen pros tin patrida, tin ouranion Sion,
(We are going home, heavenly Zion,)
echontes efthy to vlemma ston Sotira mas Christon!
(looking directly at our Savior Christ!)
Oytos monos odigos mas, monos yperaspistis,
(This our only guide, only defender,)
kata panton ton echthron mas krataios polemistis!
(against all our enemies mighty warrior!)
Chorus:
Nai, tharroyntes, nai,
(Yes, brave, yes,)
tharroyntes vainomen pros tin agnin patrida!
(courageously we go to the pure homeland!)
Nai, tharroyntes, nai,
(Yes, brave, yes,)
tharroyntes pros tin ano Ierousalim!
(brave to the upper Jerusalem!)
Nai, tharroyntes, nai,
(Yes, brave, yes,)
tharroyntes enthermon stirizontes elpida!
(daring fervently supporting hope!)
Nai, tharroyntes, nai,
(Yes, brave, yes,)
tharroyntes eis ton Kyrion ton Cherouveim!
(daring in the Lord of the Cherubim!)
Verse 2:
Vainomen dia erimou, dia choras tracheias,
(We go through a desert, through a rough country,)
plin tou manna afthonia tin psychin pliroi charas!
(besides manna, abundance fills the soul with joy!)
Ek tou vrachou ton aionon ydor reei droseron,
(Cool water flows from the rock of ages,)
tis kardias katasvinon panta ponon thliveron!
(of the heart always quenching sorrowful pain!)
Verse 3:
Vainomen dia erimou pros tin gin tis Chanaan,
(We are going through the desert to the land of Canaan,)
dia mesou tou klafthmonos pros tin arriton charan!
(through tears to unspeakable joy!)
Ekei fthasantes aisios tha oikomen eftycheis,
(Arriving there, hopefully we will live happily,)
kai tha zomen exaisios eis aionas asfaleis!
(and we will live forever safe and secure!)
