551 ELEISE ME, O ThEE

Greek Hymns - No. 551 | "ΕΛΕΗΣΕ ΜΕ, Ω ΘΕΕ"
Chorus:
Eleise me, o Thee,
(have mercy on me oh god)
mes' ap' to mega eleos Sou!
(by Your great mercy!)
Mes' ap' to plithos tis agapis Sou
(Through the multitude of Your love)
me splachnisthikes! (2)
(you took pity on me! (2))
Verse 1:
Ela kai plyne tis amarties mou,
(Come and wash away my sins,)
katharise me ap' ta pathi mou!
(cleanse me of my passions!)
Ela kai plyne tis anomies mou,
(Come and wash away my iniquities,)
katharise me ap' ta lathi mou!
(cleanse me of my mistakes!)
