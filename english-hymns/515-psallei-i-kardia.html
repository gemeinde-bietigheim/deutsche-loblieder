515 PsALLEI I KARDIA

Greek Hymns - No. 515 | "ΨΑΛΛΕΙ Η ΚΑΡΔΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Psallei i kardia vadizontas sto dromo
(The heart sings while walking down the street)
tou ouranoy gia tin Ierousalim!
(of heaven for Jerusalem!)
Ki an ponoi tora thlivoun tin kardia mou,
(And if pains now grieve my heart,)
tha xechastoyn, san tha vretho ekei!
(they will be forgotten, as if I will be there!)
Chorus:
Stou ouranoy to dromo eimai tora,
(I'm on my way to heaven now,)
i kardia mou psallei apo chara!
(my heart sings for joy!)
Ta matia mou tha doyne to Christo mas,
(My eyes shall see our Christ,)
san me kalesei ekei psila!
(as if calling me up there!)
Verse 2:
Tis gis aftis oi axies oles svinoun
(The values ​​of this land are all extinguished)
agones, thlipseis kai ta vasana,
(struggles, sorrows and sufferings,)
ston ourano, ekei den echei thlipseis,
(in heaven, there is no sorrow,)
alla eirini, doxa kai chara!
(but peace, glory and joy!)
Verse 3:
Kosme ftoche, san ti mporeis na doseis;
(Poor world, like what can you give?)
Osa prosfereis einai efimera!
(What you offer is ephemeral!)
S' afino kai peto gia ta ourania,
(I leave you and fly to the skies,)
anoixan tis psychis mou ta ftera!
(they opened the wings of my soul!)
