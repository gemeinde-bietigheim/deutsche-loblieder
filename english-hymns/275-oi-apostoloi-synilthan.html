275 OI APOSTOLOI SYNILThAN

Greek Hymns - No. 275 | "ΟΙ ΑΠΟΣΤΟΛΟΙ ΣΥΝΗΛΘΑΝ"
Verse 1:
Oi apostoloi synilthan sto anogeio ekei,
(The apostles gathered in the upper room there,)
prosefchontousan me zilo dia tin Pentikosti!
(they prayed fervently for Pentecost!)
Chorus:
O Thee, steile pyr, theio pyr!
(O God, send fire, divine fire!)
O Thee, steile pyr, theio pyr!
(O God, send fire, divine fire!)
O Thee, steile pyr, theio pyr!
(O God, send fire, divine fire!)
kai vaptise ki emas!
(and baptize us too!)
Verse 2:
Tin Pentikostin imera ichos ilthe ex ouranoy,
(On the fiftieth day there came a sound from heaven,)
glosses pyrines kathisan pano s' olous tous pistoys!
(tongues of fire sat upon all believers!)
Verse 3:
San vaptistikan sto Pneyma, archisan na omiloyn
(As they were baptized in the Spirit, they began to speak)
kai megalynan me glosses to Sotira Iisoy!
(and they magnified with tongues the Savior Jesus!)
Verse 4:
Me to Pneyma plirose mas, vaptise kathe psychi,
(With the Spirit fill us, baptize every soul,)
dynami ex ypsous dos' mas opos tin Pentikosti!
(give us strength from on high as at Pentecost!)
Verse 5:
O Ouranie Patera, meine pantote mazi,
(O Heavenly Father, always be together,)
me agapi Sou zitoyme plirose kathe psychi!
(with love we ask You pay every soul!)
