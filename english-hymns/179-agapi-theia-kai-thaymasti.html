179 AGAPI ThEIA KAI ThAYMASTI

Greek Hymns - No. 179 | "ΑΓΑΠΗ ΘΕΙΑ ΚΑΙ ΘΑΥΜΑΣΤΗ"
Verse 1:
Agapi theia kai thafmasti
(Godly love and wonderful)
pou edeixe pote,
(who ever showed)
efanerothike kai se me
(it also appeared in me)
kai echo chara!
(and I'm happy!)
Chorus:
O, echo chara, chara!
(Oh, I have joy, joy!)
O, echo chara, chara!
(Oh, I have joy, joy!)
Esothin kai eplythin sto aima t' Amnoy!
(Enter and be washed in the blood of the Lamb!)
O, echo chara!
(Oh, I'm glad!)
Verse 2:
O Iisoys mou me odigei,
(My Jesus leads me,)
afotou m' esose
(after he saved me)
kai tin psychi mou parigorei
(and comforts my soul)
kai echo chara!
(and I'm happy!)
Verse 3:
To onoma mou egraftik' ekei,
(My name was written there,)
afotou m' esose,
(after he saved me)
ston ourano se selida lefki
(in the sky on a white page)
kai echo chara!
(and I'm happy!)
Verse 4:
Koita ki esy tora sto Christo,
(Look now to Christ too,)
perilypi psychi,
(sad soul,)
allos de sozei ektos ap' Afton
(no one saves except Him)
kai tha 'cheis chara!
(and you will have fun!)
