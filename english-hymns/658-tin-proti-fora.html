658 TIN PROTI FORA

Greek Hymns - No. 658 | "ΤΗΝ ΠΡΩΤΗ ΦΟΡΑ"
Chorus:
Tin proti fora pou antikrisa Esena,
(The first time I saw you,)
Christe mou, isoun s' enan xylino Stafro!
(My Christ, you were on a wooden Cross!)
M' anoichti agkalia kai ta cheria aplomena
(With open arms and outstretched arms)
m' ena Sou vlemma mou 'leges S' agapo!
(with one of your looks you said I love you!)
Verse 1:
Kathe fora pou prospatho
(Every time I try)
na do ti itane afto pou m' aggixe
(to see what it was that touched me)
toso vathia mes stin kardia,
(so deep in the heart)
pou olo to einai mou allaxe,
(that my whole being changed,)
thymamai xana!
(I remember again!)
Verse 2:
Polloi rotoyn, ma den mporoyn
(Many ask, but they cannot)
na katalavoun ti einai afto pou egine
(to understand what happened)
kai xafnika apo tyflos
(and suddenly from a blind man)
ta matia anoixan tis kardias
(the eyes of the heart were opened)
ki eida to fos!
(and I saw the light!)
Verse 3:
Kyrie, m' agapises, ston Adi katevikes,
(Lord, you loved me, you descended to Hades,)
ma to thanato nikises kai anastithikes!
(but you defeated death and rose again!)
Chorus:
Kai tora kathe fora pou antikrizo Esena,
(And now every time I face You,)
Christe, den eisai s' enan xylino Stafro!
(Christ, you are not on a wooden Cross!)
M' anoichti agkalia kai ta cheria aplomena
(With open arms and outstretched arms)
perimeno Esena, ton diko Sou erchomo!
(I await You, Your own coming!)
