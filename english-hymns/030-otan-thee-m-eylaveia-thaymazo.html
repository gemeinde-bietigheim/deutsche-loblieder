30 OTAN, ThEE, M' EYLAVEIA ThAYMAZO

Greek Hymns - No. 30 | "ΟΤΑΝ, ΘΕΕ, Μ' ΕΥΛΑΒΕΙΑ ΘΑΥΜΑΖΩ"
Verse 1:
Otan, Thee, m' eflaveia thafmazo
(When, God, I reverently admire)
pasan tin ktisin, ergon son cheiron,
(every building, the work of your hands,)
ki otan pote foni vrontis akoyo,
(and when ever I hear a voice of thunder,)
S' anagnorizo Megan ki Ypsiston!
(I recognize You Great and Most High!)
Chorus:
Poson megalos eisai, Kyrie!
(How great You are, Lord!)
O, i psychi mou S' eflogei!
(Oh, my soul blesses Thee!)
Eisai Megalos, nai, o Krataie,
(You are Great, yes, O Stronghold,)
as se ymnei pasa i gi!
(let all the earth praise you!)
Verse 2:
Ki' otan sta dasi ki ori anavaino,
(And when I climb the forests and mountains,)
akoyo ta poulia na se ymnoyn,
(I hear the birds singing your praises)
apo psila koilades ki anthi vlepo
(from high valleys and flowers I see)
kai potamoys me icho na kyloyn!
(and rivers with sound to flow!)
Verse 3:
Ki otan skefto oti Sy den efeistheis
(And when I think that You are not appealing)
Monogeni Yio sou sto stafro,
(Your only begotten Son on the cross,)
pos itan dynato na agapiseis
(how was it possible to love)
kai na dechtheis enan amartolo!
(and receive a sinner!)
Verse 4:
Pali tha elthei o Christos ex ypsous
(Christ will come again from on high)
na prosdechthei pistoys ston ourano,
(to welcome believers to heaven,)
tote tha chairo kai ego m' agious
(then I too will rejoice with saints)
doxa prosferon ston megan Theon!
(offerer glory to the great God!)
