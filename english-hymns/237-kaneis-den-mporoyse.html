237 KANEIS DEN MPOROYSE

Greek Hymns - No. 237 | "ΚΑΝΕΙΣ ΔΕΝ ΜΠΟΡΟΥΣΕ"
Verse 1:
Kaneis den mporoyse eme na lytrosei,
(No one could redeem me,)
ta ployti tou kosmou na sosoun psychi,
(may the riches of the world save a soul,)
to aima t' amnoy ein' i moni elpida,
(the blood of the lamb is the only hope,)
mou dinei chara kai aionia zoi!
(gives me joy and eternal life!)
Chorus:
Den elytrothika m' argyrio
(I was not redeemed with silver)
i me chrysafi to ftharto,
(or with perishable gold,)
alla me timion Tou aima,
(but with His holy blood,)
pou echythei sto stafro!
(which lies on the cross!)
Verse 2:
Kaneis den mporoyse eme na lytrosei,
(No one could redeem me,)
na dosei zoi sti nekri mou psychi,
(to give life to my dead soul,)
to aima t' Amnoy ein' i moni elpida,
(the blood of the Lamb is the only hope,)
apethan' Aftos, gia na lavo zoi!
(He died, that I might receive life!)
Verse 3:
Kaneis den mporoyse eme na lytrosei
(No one could redeem me)
kai oi entoles de mou edinan fos,
(and the orders gave me no light,)
chreiasthike mono t' Amnoy i thysia,
(only the Lamb was needed for the sacrifice,)
ekei sto Stafro egine ilasmos!
(there at Stavros there was a riot!)
Verse 4:
Kaneis den mporoyse eme na lytrosei
(No one could redeem me)
kaneis na mou deixei odo t' ouranoy,
(no one show me the way to heaven)
kaneis den mporoyse ti thyra n' anoixei,
(no one could open the door,)
tin anoixe tora to aima t' Amnoy!
(the blood of the Lamb opened it now!)
