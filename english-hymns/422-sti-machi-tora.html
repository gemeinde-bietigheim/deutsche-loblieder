422 STI MAChI TORA

Greek Hymns - No. 422 | "ΣΤΗ ΜΑΧΗ ΤΩΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Sti machi tora mas kalei o Stratigos,
(To battle now the General calls us,)
empros pistoi, Aftos ein' odigos!
(forward faithful, He is a guide!)
O Kyrios einai paron sto metopo,
(The Lord is present at the front,)
aftos tha polemisei ton echthro!
(he will fight the enemy!)
Chorus:
I machi einai lyssalea,
(The battle is fierce,)
o stratioti tou Christoy,
(oh soldier of Christ,)
analave sy tin panoplia,
(take up the armor,)
to theio Logo tou Theoy!
(the divine Word of God!)
Na 'sai gennaios ston agona,
(Be brave in the fight,)
prochora kata tou echthroy,
(advance against the enemy,)
giati echeis ena mega Sotira
(because you have a great Savior)
kai tha nikiseis pantachoy!
(and you will win everywhere!)
Verse 2:
Agrypnise loipon, opliti tou Christoy,
(Awake therefore, soldier of Christ,)
kai antistasou kata tou echthroy,
(and stand against the enemy,)
dikaiosyni, pisti kai alitheia
(justice, faith and truth)
einai ta opla kata tou kakoy!
(are the weapons against evil!)
Verse 3:
Na mi nikiesai, file, ap' ton peirasmo,
(Do not be overcome, friend, by temptation,)
tha echeis pali me ton poniro.
(you will have a fight with the wicked one.)
O, kratise ti machaira tou Pneymatos
(Oh, hold the sword of the Spirit)
kai o Christos tha einai voithos!
(and Christ will be a helper!)
