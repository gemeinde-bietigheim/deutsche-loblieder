296 OPOIOS ChASEI TI ZOI TOY

Greek Hymns - No. 296 | "ΟΠΟΙΟΣ ΧΑΣΕΙ ΤΗ ΖΩΗ ΤΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Opoios chasei ti zoi tou, zoi tha vrei!
(Whoever loses his life will find life!)
Kai to soma parastisei thysia zontani.
(And the body shall be sacrificed alive.)
Opoios dosei tin kardia tou tha anoichthei
(Whoever gives his heart will be opened)
se potamia eflogias kai aionias zois!
(in rivers of blessing and eternal life!)
Chorus:
Zei o Kyrios kai zo ego!
(The Lord lives and I live!)
Zei o Vasilias o Dimiourgos!
(Long live the King the Creator!)
Zei o Kyrios kai zei i psychi mou!
(The Lord lives and my soul lives!)
Zei o Vasilias mes sti zoi mou!
(The King lives in my life!)
Verse 2:
Opoios arnithei ta pathi tou kosmou aftoy
(Whoever denies the passions of this world)
kai to pneyma tou gemizei me to Pneyma tou Theoy,
(and his spirit is filled with the Spirit of God,)
se livadia eflogias tha peripatei
(in blessed meadows he will walk)
kai oi dromoi tou tha einai gematoi apo zoi!
(and its streets will be full of life!)
