243 O ChRISTOS MOY STO STAYRO

Greek Hymns - No. 243 | "Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ ΜΟΥ ΣΤΟ ΣΤΑΥΡΟ"
Verse 1:
O Christos mou sto stafro
(My Christ on the cross)
to potiri to pikro
(the bitter glass)
geythike gia mena to thnito,
(mortal has tasted for me,)
gia na zo ston ourano!
(to live in heaven!)
Chorus:
Kyrie mou, i agapi Sou
(Lord, Your love)
mou'dose ti lefteria!
(give me freedom!)
Kyrie mou, stin agkali Sou
(Lord, in Your arms)
vrisko elpida kai chara!
(I find hope and joy!)
Verse 2:
Monos dromos einai Aftos,
(He is the only way,)
i alitheia kai to fos!
(the truth and the light!)
Filos mou, poimenas, odigos,
(My friend, shepherd, guide,)
o Sotiras mou Christos!
(my Savior Christ!)
Verse 3:
Akou tora ti foni
(Now listen to the voice)
tou Theoy pou se kalei
(of God who calls you)
na dechtheis ton Iisoy Christo,
(to accept Jesus Christ,)
to monakrivo Tou Yio!
(His only Son!)
