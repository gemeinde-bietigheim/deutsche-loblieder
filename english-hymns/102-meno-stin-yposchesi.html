102 MENO STIN YPOSChESI

Greek Hymns - No. 102 | "ΜΕΝΩ ΣΤΗΝ ΥΠΟΣΧΕΣΗ"
Verse 1:
Meno stin yposchesi tou Iisoy,
(I abide in the promise of Jesus,)
perimeno na 'rthei ap' ton ourano.
(I expect it to come from heaven.)
Doxa, alliloyia, tora erchetai!
(Glory, hallelujah, now it comes!)
Meno eis to Logo tou Theoy!
(I live in the Word of God!)
Chorus:
Meno, meno,
(I stay, I stay)
meno stin yposchesi tou Iisoy mou!
(I abide in the promise of my Jesus!)
Meno, meno,
(I stay, I stay)
nai, meno eis to Logo tou Theoy!
(yes, I live in the Word of God!)
Verse 2:
Meno stin yposchesi de deilio,
(I keep my promise, I'm not a coward,)
ton kalo agona tha agonistho,
(I will fight the good fight)
Thlipseis otan erchontai tha prochoro.
(Tribulations when they come I will move on.)
Meno eis to Logo tou Theoy!
(I live in the Word of God!)
Verse 3:
Meno stin yposchesi, giati kai ego
(I keep the promise, because me too)
vasileia tou Theoy epipotho,
(kingdom of God I seek,)
m' esose kai eimai tora tou Christoy!
(He saved me and I am now Christ's!)
Meno eis to Logo tou Theoy!
(I live in the Word of God!)
Verse 4:
Meno stin yposchesi na ergastho,
(I keep my promise to work,)
ston agro tou Iisoy na ypireto
(in the field of Jesus to serve)
fernontas cheirovola meta charas!
(bringing handballs after joy!)
Meno eis to Logo tou Theoy!
(I live in the Word of God!)
