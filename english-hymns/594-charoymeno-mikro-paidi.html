594 ChAROYMENO MIKRO PAIDI

Greek Hymns - No. 594 | "ΧΑΡΟΥΜΕΝΟ ΜΙΚΡΟ ΠΑΙΔΙ"
Verse 1:
Charoymeno mikro paidi,
(Happy little boy,)
stis manas tin agkali
(to the mother the hug)
choros aggelon Se ymnei,
(a dance of angels praises You,)
Christoygenna kai pali!
(Christmas again!)
Chorus:
Na! mesa sti nychtia
(To! in the middle of the night)
ichoyn aggelon Osanna.
(angels sound Hosanna.)
Na! pali charopa
(To! happy again)
sti Vithleem as pame.
(Let's go to Bethlehem.)
Verse 2:
Mazi me zoa siopila
(Together with animals in silence)
koimatai o Christos mas.
(our Christ sleeps.)
Odes Tou psalloun tapeina
(Odes to Him they humbly sing)
ta cheili kathenos mas!
(the lips of each of us!)
Verse 3:
Gi' akomi fetos mia fora
(One more time this year)
sti gi tin ponemeni
(to the earth in pain)
giortazoume Christoygenna
(we celebrate christmas)
ki i fatni mas prosmenei!
(and the manger awaits us!)
