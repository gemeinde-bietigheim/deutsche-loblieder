187 NAI, ThA MPO STIS AYLES

Greek Hymns - No. 187 | "ΝΑΙ, ΘΑ ΜΠΩ ΣΤΙΣ ΑΥΛΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Nai, tha mpo stis afles tou Kyriou mou
(Yes, I will enter the courts of my Lord)
n' apolayso to kallos Tou!
(to enjoy His beauty!)
Nai, ekei eis ton Agio nao Aftoy
(Yes, there in His Holy temple)
gemizei i kardia mou me chara!
(my heart fills with joy!)

Mou edose chara, mou edose chara,

(jesus gave me joy!)
Mou edose chara, mou edose chara,

(jesus gave me joy!)

(Jesus made me happy!)
