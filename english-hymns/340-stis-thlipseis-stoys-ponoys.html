340 STIS ThLIPsEIS, STOYS PONOYS

Greek Hymns - No. 340 | "ΣΤΙΣ ΘΛΙΨΕΙΣ, ΣΤΟΥΣ ΠΟΝΟΥΣ"
Verse 1:
Stis thlipseis, stous ponous kai stis taraches,
(In sorrows, pains and troubles,)
o, ti eflogia ein' oi prosefches!
(oh, what a blessing prayers are!)
Ta ourania anoigoun kai erchesai Esy,
(The heavens open and You come,)
charizeis ti niki kai dineis zoi!
(you give victory and give life!)
Verse 2:
Zitoysa ston kosmo pantoy na sotho,
(I asked the world everywhere to be saved,)
ap' tin amartia mou na lytrotho!
(to be redeemed from my sin!)
Edo einai maches polles kai deines,
(Here are many battles and hardships,)
ekei ein' aionia eirini me Se!
(there is eternal peace with Thee!)
Verse 3:
Eno diaschizo tin erimi gi,
(As I traverse the desolate land,)
Sy dose agapi kai ypomoni!
(Give me love and patience!)
Se kathe mou kindyno trecho se Se,
(In all my perils I run to Thee,)
aporthitos pyrgos mou eisai, Christe!
(you are my impregnable tower, Christ!)
Verse 4:
O Kyrie, o,ti kai an m' arnitheis,
(O Lord, whatever you deny me,)
zito apo mena pote min kryftheis!
(please never hide from me!)
To Pneyma Sou charize, o Iisoy,
(Give Thy Spirit, O Jesus,)
chara na mou dinei, Christe mou, pantoy!
(may he give me joy, my Christ, everywhere!)
Verse 5:
Kai otan tha eltho mia mera ekei,
(And when I come one day there,)
glykia mou patrida, poly pothiti,
(my sweet country, much desired,)
se Sena aionia tha moiaso, Christe,
(I will forever look like You, Christ,)
timi kai latreia tha psallo se Se!
(honor and worship I will sing to Thee!)
