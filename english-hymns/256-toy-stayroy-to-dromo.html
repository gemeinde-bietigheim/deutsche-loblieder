256 TOY STAYROY TO DROMO

Greek Hymns - No. 256 | "ΤΟΥ ΣΤΑΥΡΟΥ ΤΟ ΔΡΟΜΟ"
Verse 1:
Tou stafroy to dromo pires Sy gia mena
(You took the way of the cross for me)
pros to Golgotha!
(to Calvary!)
Tous frichtoys tous ponous dechthikes gia mena
(You took their horrible pains for me)
pano kei psila!
(up and up!)
Chorus:
O glyke mou Lytroti,
(O sweet Redeemer,)
egines Esy thysia,
(You became a sacrifice,)
ti agapi theia!
(what a godly love!)
Verse 2:
Se afisan oloi sto pykno skotadi
(They all left you in the dark)
kai sto chlefasmo,
(and in mockery,)
t' agio Sou to aima kylise gia mena,
(Your holy blood flowed for me,)
gia na lytrotho!
(to redeem myself!)
