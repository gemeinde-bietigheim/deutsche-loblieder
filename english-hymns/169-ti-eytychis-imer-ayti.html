169 TI EYTYChIS IMER' AYTI

Greek Hymns - No. 169 | "ΤΙ ΕΥΤΥΧΗΣ ΗΜΕΡ' ΑΥΤΗ"
Verse 1:
Ti eftychis imer' afti
(What a happy day this is)
pou egine yposchesi,
(that was promised)
ego na eimai tou Theoy,
(I be of God,)
na ein' Aftos Theos emoy!
(may He be my God!)
Chorus:
Eftychis, eftychis einai i imera tis efchis!
(Happy, happy is the day of the wish!)
Me prosefchi na agrypno,
(With prayer to watch,)
me pisti kai chara na zo,
(with faith and joy to live,)
Eftychis, eftychis i epochi tis eklogis!
(Happy, happy election season!)
Verse 2:
Ti eftychis, christos desmos,
(What a happy, useful bond,)
syndethika o amartolos,
(I joined the sinner,)
ego me ton Panagatho
(me with Panagathos)
kai to Sotira mou Christo!
(and my Savior Christ!)
Verse 3:
Psalmoi kai ymnoi sti Sion
(Psalms and Hymns in Zion)
as antichoyn pros to Theo!
(let them echo to God!)
Ego vadizo me chara
(I walk with joy)
pros tin aionia trapeza!
(to the eternal bank!)
Verse 4:
Apefasisthi, egine,
(It was decided, it was done,)
theia foni m' ekalese!
(divine voice called me!)
Foni agapis kai ego
(Voice of love and me)
akoyo kai akoloutho!
(I listen and follow!)
