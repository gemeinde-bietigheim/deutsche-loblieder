491 LAMPROS O DROMOS T' OYRANOY!

Greek Hymns - No. 491 | "ΛΑΜΠΡΟΣ Ο ΔΡΟΜΟΣ Τ' ΟΥΡΑΝΟΥ!"
Verse 1:
Lampros o dromos t' ouranoy!
(Bright is the way of heaven!)
Mes' ap' ta matomena cheria
(Through bloody hands)
anoixe fernontas eirini
(opened bringing peace)
ki aionia stin kardia galini!
(and eternal peace in the heart!)
Chorus:
Nai, tha 'rthei, gia na charisei
(Yes, he will come, to give away)
stin psychi ti lefteria!
(freedom to the soul!)
Gia na parei tous dikoys Tou
(To get His own)
kai na zoun pantotina!
(and live forever!)
Verse 2:
To dromo afton edo sti gi
(This way here on earth)
kane na ton vadisoume,
(make us march him)
ospou na 'rthei charas stigmi,
(until a happy moment comes,)
Kyrie, na s' antikrisoume!
(Lord, let us face you!)
Verse 3:
Stin agia chora ti glykia,
(In the holy land the sweet,)
pou einai olo omorfia,
(which is all beauty,)
na 'rtho ki ego, charazei i afgi
(let me come too, the dawn is breaking)
sti chora t' ouranoy ekei!
(in the land of heaven there!)
Verse 4:
Rigmenoi mes stin agkalia
(Thrown into each other's arms)
me ti diki Tou ti chara,
(with His own joy,)
ton perimenoume na 'rthei,
(we are waiting for him to come,)
gia na mas parei ekei psila!
(to take us up there!)
