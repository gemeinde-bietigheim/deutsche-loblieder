576 ERChETAI O KYRIOS ME DOXA

Greek Hymns - No. 576 | "ΕΡΧΕΤΑΙ Ο ΚΥΡΙΟΣ ΜΕ ΔΟΞΑ"
Verse 1:
Erchetai o Kyrios me doxa
(The Lord is coming in glory)
plisiazei i pothiti stigmi!
(the coveted moment is approaching!)
I elpida stin kardia fountonei
(Hope in the heart kindles)
ki enas ymnos tora antichei!
(and a hymn now resounds!)
Chorus:
Mera lampri, ateleioti giorti!
(Brilliant day, endless celebration!)
Th' anoixoun oi ouranoi, o Kyrios tha 'rthei!
(The heavens will open, the Lord will come!)
Ti thafmasti aionia chara,
(What wonderful eternal joy,)
tha 'choume ekei psila!
(we'll be up there!)
Verse 2:
Tis zois aftis o ilios dyei,
(Of this life the sun is setting,)
plisiazei i ora lytromoy!
(the time of redemption is at hand!)
Xechna ponous vasana kai thlipseis,
(Forget pains sufferings and sorrows,)
ta provlimata tou kosmou aftoy.
(the problems of this world.)
Verse 3:
I salpigga tha ichisei ap' ta ourania,
(The trumpet will sound from the heavens,)
tha anastithoyne oi nekroi!
(the dead will rise!)
Ki osoi zoun tha metamorfothoyne,
(And those who live will be transformed,)
th' arpachthoyn mesa se mia stigmi!
(they will be snapped up in an instant!)
