175 O ERGO TIS ThEIAS AGAPIS

Greek Hymns - No. 175 | "Ω ΕΡΓΟ ΤΗΣ ΘΕΙΑΣ ΑΓΑΠΗΣ"
Verse 1:
O ergo tis theias agapis,
(O work of divine love,)
mystirio tou Golgotha!
(mystery of Calvary!)
Thafmazo to vathos tis charis
(I admire the depth of grace)
pou fernei zoi kai chara!
(that brings life and joy!)
Ekei tous theious palmoys,
(There the divine pulses,)
psychi mou, niotheis ki' akoys,
(my soul, you feel and hear)
ekei svisane ta palia
(there they erased the old ones)
ki ekei pira nea kardia!
(and there I got a new heart!)
Verse 2:
Desma me chontres alysides
(Bonds with thick chains)
ta pathi, vareia kai sklira,
(the passions, heavy and hard,)
me mataies, froydes elpides
(with vain, vain hopes)
me doylonan sta chamila!
(I was underpaid!)
Kai tora, ti thafmasto!
(And now, how wonderful!)
mathaino pos sto stafro
(I learn how on the cross)
i chari pou din' o Christos
(the grace that Christ gives)
mou fernei zoi kai to fos!
(brings me life and light!)
Verse 3:
Ta chronia kyloysan me agchos,
(The years went by anxiously,)
me pono kai me enochi,
(with pain and guilt,)
avastachto itan to varos,
(the weight was unbearable,)
i thlipsi kai i tarachi!
(the grief and the tumult!)
Ospou me vrik' o Christos,
(Until Christ found me)
ta vari sikos' Aftos
(He lifted the burdens)
kai m' efere mes sti chara,
(and brought me to joy,)
sti lytrosi, sti lefteria!
(to redemption, to freedom!)
