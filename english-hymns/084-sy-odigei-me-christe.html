84 SY ODIGEI ME, ChRISTE

Greek Hymns - No. 84 | "ΣΥ ΟΔΗΓΕΙ ΜΕ, ΧΡΙΣΤΕ"
Verse 1:
Sy odigei me, Christe, na min planitho pote,
(Lead me, O Christ, that I may never go astray,)
na S' akoloutho pantoy, na 'mai stin agapi Sou!
(to follow You everywhere, to be in Your love!)
Chorus:
Kyrie, Kyrie, Sy odigei m' asfalos,
(Lord, Lord, He leads me safely,)
os to terma tis zois odigei m' asfalos!
(to the end of life it leads me surely!)
Verse 2:
Sy eisai to froyrio se kairo ton peirasmon,
(You are the fortress in times of temptation,)
eimai asfalis me Se, de tha deilio pote!
(I'm safe with You, I'll never be afraid!)
Verse 3:
Sy odigei me sti gi mechri telous tis zois,
(He leads me to earth till the end of life,)
ki otan fthaso sti Sion, tha doxazo to Christo!
(and when I reach Zion, I will glorify Christ!)
