320 IISOY MOY, DIOXE TO FOVO

Greek Hymns - No. 320 | "ΙΗΣΟΥ ΜΟΥ, ΔΙΩΞΕ ΤΟ ΦΟΒΟ"
Verse 1:
Iisoy mou, dioxe to fovo ap' tin kardia mou,
(My Jesus, cast fear from my heart,)
Iisoy mou, dioxe to fovo ap' tin kardia!
(My Jesus, banish fear from the heart!)
Ta matia mou ta matia Sou mathe na vlepoun,
(My eyes Your eyes learn to see,)
dose mou pisti, dos' mou agapi, dos' mou chara!
(give me faith, give me love, give me joy!)
Ta matia mou ta matia Sou mathe na vlepoun,
(My eyes Your eyes learn to see,)
dose mou pisti, dos' mou agapi, dos' mou chara!
(give me faith, give me love, give me joy!)
Verse 2:
Mes sto mikro mou to myalo, mes sti ftochi kardia mou
(In my little mind, in my poor heart)
afto pou ekanes gia mena kane na mpei!
(what you did for me make it come in!)
Na zontanepsei mes stin kardia, mes stin psychi mou,
(To come alive in my heart, in my soul,)
meine mazi mou, meine mazi mou gia panta mazi!
(stay with me, stay with me forever together!)
Na zontanepsei mes stin kardia, mes stin psychi mou,
(To come alive in my heart, in my soul,)
meine mazi mou, meine mazi mou gia panta mazi!
(stay with me, stay with me forever together!)
Verse 3:
Dakrya charas, dakrya agapis ki efgnomosynis,
(Tears of joy, tears of love and gratitude,)
efgnomosynis dakrya na trechoun ap' tin kardia!
(tears of gratitude flow from the heart!)
Afto pou ekanes gia me, glyke Sotira,
(What you did for me, sweet Saviour,)
oyte metrietai ki oyte sto sympan mesa chora!
(neither measured nor contained in the universe!)
Afto pou ekanes gia me, glyke Sotira,
(What you did for me, sweet Saviour,)
oyte metrietai ki oyte sto sympan mesa chora!
(neither measured nor contained in the universe!)
Verse 4:
Steile fotia, steile fotia, mes stin kardia mou,
(Send fire, send fire, into my heart)
steile fotia, anapse Sy mia pyrkagia.
(send fire, kindle a fire.)
San floga agapis na ypsothei os ta ourania,
(Like a flame of love rising to the heavens,)
san floga agapis na ypsothei pros Se i kardia!
(like a flame of love rise to Your heart!)
San floga agapis na ypsothei os ta ourania,
(Like a flame of love rising to the heavens,)
san floga agapis na ypsothei pros Se i kardia!
(like a flame of love rise to Your heart!)
