176 O ThEE, ODIGISE ME

Greek Hymns - No. 176 | "Ω ΘΕΕ, ΟΔΗΓΗΣΕ ΜΕ"
Verse 1:
O Thee, odigise me
(Oh God, lead me)
sto taxidi aftis tis gis,
(on this earth's journey,)
ischyra Sy kratise me
(strong You hold me)
stis fourtoynes tis zois!
(in the storms of life!)
O Thee mou, Kyrie mou,
(Oh my God, my Lord,)
otan skoteinia xespa,
(when darkness breaks,)
deichne mou tin xasteria!
(show me the strip!)
Verse 2:
Stis piges odigise me,
(To the springs lead me,)
opou kathara nera,
(where clear waters,)
krystallenia, drosise me,
(crystal cool me)
Sy ischys mou kai chara!
(My strength and joy!)
Kai to pyr Sou ki i nefeli
(And Your fire and the cloud)
pros to theio Ourano
(to the divine Heaven)
na mou deichnoun tin odo!
(show me the way!)
Verse 3:
Ki otan fthaso mia imera
(And when I arrive one day)
stin ourania akti,
(on the heavenly shore,)
tha se do, theie Patera,
(i will see you godly father)
me agkali anoichti!
(with open arms!)
Tote ola ta paidia Sou,
(Then all Thy children,)
me chara ki alalagmo,
(with joy and rejoicing,)
tha Sou poyme: «Efcharisto!».
(we will say: "Thank you!".)
