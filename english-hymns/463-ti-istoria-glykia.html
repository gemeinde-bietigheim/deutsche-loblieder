463 TI ISTORIA GLYKIA!

Greek Hymns - No. 463 | "ΤΙ ΙΣΤΟΡΙΑ ΓΛΥΚΙΑ!"
Verse 1:
Ti istoria glykia! O! pes mou tin xana!
(What a sweet story! Oh! tell me again!)
Mia istoria agni, poly agapiti,
(A story pure, very dear,)
aggeloi tin anaggelloun, prepei pantoy n' akousthei!
(angels announce it, it must be heard everywhere!)
File mou, an tha pistepseis, tote kai sy tha sotheis!
(My friend, if you will believe, then you too will be saved!)
Chorus:
S' agapa o Christos,
(Christ loves you)
se prosmenei, gia na se sosei Aftos!
(He is waiting for you, so that He can save you!)
Verse 2:
Ti istoria glykia! ki omos poly palia!
(What a sweet story! but very old!)
Ilthe o Christos mas sti gi gia se na stafrothei!
(Our Christ came to earth to be crucified!)
Thelei Aftos na se sosei kai na se kanei eftychi,
(He wants to save you and make you happy,)
nea zoi na sou dosei, ela s' Afton, min argeis!
(new life to give you, come to Him, don't be late!)
Verse 3:
Ti istoria glykia! milaei stin kardia!
(What a sweet story! speaks to the heart!)
Charizei o Iisoys chara mes sti psychi!
(Jesus brings joy to the soul!)
Aftos tha se sygchorisei, dexou Ton mes stin kardia!
(He will forgive you, accept Him in your heart!)
Ela ki esy n' apolayseis zoi aionia glykia!
(Come and enjoy life forever sweet!)
