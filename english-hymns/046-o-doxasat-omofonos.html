46 O! DOXASAT' OMOFONOS

Greek Hymns - No. 46 | "Ω! ΔΟΞΑΣΑΤ' ΟΜΟΦΩΝΩΣ"
Verse 1:
O! Doxasat' omofonos ton pisto mas Lytroti!
(Oh! Praise our faithful Redeemer with one voice!)
O! Doxasat' oloi ton Amno!
(Oh! All praise the Lamb!)
Giati ilthe na mas ferei tin aionia zoi
(Because he came to bring us eternal life)
kai ti doxa ap' ton ourano!
(and glory from heaven!)
Chorus:
Einai doxa kai chara pantoy
(It is glory and joy everywhere)
na vadizeis me ton Iisoy!
(to walk with Jesus!)
Ein' elpida tis zois, einai terpsi tis psychis
(It is the hope of life, it is the indulgence of the soul)
na vadizeis me ton Iisoy!
(to walk with Jesus!)
Verse 2:
Ti oraia na gnorizeis, otan thlipseis se kykloyn,
(How nice to know, when sorrows surround you,)
oti echeis filo to Christo!
(that you have a friend in Christ!)
Ein' o vrachos ton aionon, otan ponoi s' apeiloyn,
(It is the rock of ages, when pains threaten you,)
einai katafygio terpno!
(it's shelter terpno!)
Verse 3:
O, ti doxa! otan pleon tha vretho ston ourano!
(Oh, what glory! when I will now be in heaven!)
Tha vadizo stis chryses odoys!
(I will walk the golden streets!)
Kai tha psallo aionios me aggelous kai pistoys
(And I will sing eternally with angels and believers)
ti lampri odi tou Iisoy!
(the glorious ode of Jesus!)
