216 STA OYRANIA AGGELOI

Greek Hymns - No. 216 | "ΣΤΑ ΟΥΡΑΝΙΑ ΑΓΓΕΛΟΙ"
Verse 1:
Sta ourania aggeloi
(Angels in heaven)
to Theanthropo Christo
(the God-man Christ)
anymnoyn me thafmasmo,
(sing with admiration,)
Vasilea tapeino!
(Humble King!)
Chorus:
Doxa, doxa sto Theo
(Glory, glory to God)
Endoxo kai Krataio!
(Glorious and Mighty!)
Kai eirini epi gis,
(And peace on earth,)
egennithi Lytrotis!
(a Redeemer was born!)
Kai eirini epi gis,
(And peace on earth,)
egennithi Lytrotis!
(a Redeemer was born!)
Verse 2:
Dexou vrefos tapeino
(Accept humble infant)
tin proskynisi piston.
(the worship of believers.)
Oloi mas gonatistoi
(All of us on our knees)
proskynoyme Se, Theon!
(we worship You, God!)
Verse 3:
Chaire, Vasilia eirinis!
(Hail, King of peace!)
Chaire, fos alithino!
(Hail, true light!)
Ilie Dikaiosynis,
(Sun of Justice,)
sotiria ton ethnon!
(salvation of the nations!)
