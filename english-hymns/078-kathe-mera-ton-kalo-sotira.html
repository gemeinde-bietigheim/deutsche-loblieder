78 KAThE MERA TON KALO SOTIRA

Greek Hymns - No. 78 | "ΚΑΘΕ ΜΕΡΑ ΤΟΝ ΚΑΛΟ ΣΩΤΗΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Kathe mera ton kalo Sotira tha akoloutho,
(Every day the good Savior I will follow,)
opoudipote me odigisei, tha akoloutho!
(wherever he leads me, I will follow!)
Chorus:
Nai, tha akoloutho Afton, efoson zo,
(Yea, I will follow Him as long as I live,)
meta charas pollis, mechri telous tis zois!
(after much joy, until the end of life!)
Verse 2:
Kathe mera thelo na mathaino peri tou Christoy,
(Every day I want to learn about Christ,)
pantote pnefmatika n' afxano eis ta tou Theoy!
(to always increase spiritually in the things of God!)
Verse 3:
Kathe mera thelo na gnorizo thelima Theoy
(Every day I want to know God's will)
kai me tharros thelo na vadizo me ton Iisoy!
(and with courage I want to walk with Jesus!)
Verse 4:
Kathe mera thelo na latreyo ton Iisoy Christo
(Every day I want to worship Jesus Christ)
kai aionia me Afton na chairo eis ton ourano!
(and eternally with Him to rejoice in heaven!)
