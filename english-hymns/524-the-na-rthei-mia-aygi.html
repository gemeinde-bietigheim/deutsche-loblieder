524 ThE NA 'RThEI MIA AYGI

Greek Hymns - No. 524 | "ΘΕ ΝΑ 'ΡΘΕΙ ΜΙΑ ΑΥΓΗ"
Verse 1:
The na 'rthei mia afgi,
(May a dawn come,)
pou tha ferei tin psychi
(which will bring the soul)
stin aionia chara,
(to eternal joy,)
sti galini ti glykia,
(in sweet peace,)
san tha 'rthei o Christos
(as if Christ will come)
na me ferei sto fos,
(bring me to the light)
na vretho stou ouranoy tin akti!
(to be in heaven the coast!)
Verse 2:
Ti stigmi thafmasti!
(What a wonderful moment!)
san tha do to Lytroti,
(as if I shall see the Redeemer,)
pou sti chari Tou chrosto
(which to His grace I owe)
to palati to lampro!
(the palace the brilliant!)
Ekei tha 'cho stoli
(I will have a uniform there)
ap' to chioni pio lefki
(whiter than snow)
kai t' Arniou tha psallo tin odi!
(and to the Lamb I will sing the ode!)
Verse 3:
Den yparchoun ekei
(They don't exist there)
thlipseis, ponos, tarachi,
(sorrows, pain, turmoil,)
oyte dakrya monaxias
(nor tears of loneliness)
oyte thanatou skia,
(no shadow of death,)
mono anespero fos
(only endless light)
ap' ton ilio pio lampro,
(brighter than the sun)
o Sion, thafmasti, pothiti!
(O Zion, admirer, desirer!)
