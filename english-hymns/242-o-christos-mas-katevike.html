242 O ChRISTOS MAS KATEVIKE

Greek Hymns - No. 242 | "Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ ΜΑΣ ΚΑΤΕΒΗΚΕ"
Verse 1:
O Christos mas katevike ap' ton ourano,
(Our Christ came down from heaven,)
gia na sosei tin kathe psychi!
(to save every soul!)
Kai gia mas ekremasthike eis to stafro
(And for us he hung on the cross)
na mas dosei aionia zoi!
(to give us eternal life!)
Chorus:
Sto Stafro, nai, ekei, pano sto Golgotha
(On the Cross, yes, there, on Calvary)
ekremasthik' o Theios Amnos!
(Theos Amnos was hanged!)
O! ti ponous friktoys kai tos' alla deina
(Oh! what horrible pains and so many other afflictions)
epathe o Sotiras Christos!
(Christ the Savior suffered!)
Verse 2:
Sto Stafro, sto Stafro, ekei eida to fos
(At the Cross, at the Cross, there I saw the light)
kai to varos mou efyg' efthys!
(and my weight went away immediately!)
Ti megali chara, m' esose o Christos
(What a great joy, Christ saved me)
ki eimai tor' alithos eftychis!
(and I am now truly happy!)
Verse 3:
Eis Afton ego panta tha meno pistos,
(To Him I will always remain faithful,)
ospou na 'rthei ap' ton ourano!
(until it comes from heaven!)
Pantote kai pantoy, opou ki an efretho,
(Always and everywhere, wherever I am,)
de tha papso Afton na ymno!
(I will not stop praising Him!)
