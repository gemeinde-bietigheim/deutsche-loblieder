236 «IDE O ANThROPOS!» IDE!

Greek Hymns - No. 236 | "«ΙΔΕ Ο ΑΝΘΡΩΠΟΣ!» ΙΔΕ!"
Verse 1:
«Ide o anthropos!» Ide!
("Behold the man!" See!)
En meso ponon kai echthron
(In the midst of pains and enemies)
oudolos einai skythropos,
(he is not sullen at all,)
all' echei vlemma ilaron!
(but he has a hilarious look!)
Verse 2:
«Ide o anthropos!» Ide!
("Behold the man!" See!)
Yios Theoy te kai Theos
(Son of God and God)
katilthen eis tin gin pote
(never came down to earth)
os anthropos tis tapeinos!
(as a humble person!)
Verse 3:
«Ide o anthropos!» Ide!
("Behold the man!" See!)
O mi poiisas poniron
(You have done no wrong)
os eis kakoyrgos misitos
(as a hated criminal)
katadikazetai eis stafron!
(condemned to the cross!)
Verse 4:
«Ide o anthropos!» Ide!
("Behold the man!" See!)
Anevi eis ton ouranon,
(ascended to heaven,)
o katafronitheis pote
(the most despised ever)
to pan en doxi kyvernon!
(all in glory rulers!)
Verse 5:
«Ide o anthropos!» Ide!
("Behold the man!" See!)
Pas aggelos Ton proskynei!
(You are an angel worshiping Him!)
S' Afton prosferontai odes,
(Odes are offered to Him,)
Afton to sympan anymnei!
(The universe celebrates him!)
