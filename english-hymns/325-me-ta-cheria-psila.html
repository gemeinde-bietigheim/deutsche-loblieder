325 ME TA ChERIA PsILA

Greek Hymns - No. 325 | "ΜΕ ΤΑ ΧΕΡΙΑ ΨΗΛΑ"
Verse 1:
Me ta cheria psila kai me dakrya sta matia mas
(With our hands up and tears in our eyes)
irthame, vasilia, na se niosoume xana!
(we have come, king, to feel you again!)
Taragmeni i psychi ap' ta tosa provlimata
(The soul is troubled by so many problems)
ena chadi pothei, ti galini tis na vrei!
(a caress she craves, to find her peace!)

Verse 2:
I kardia anazita ena logo, ena vlemma sou,
(The heart seeks a reason, a look of yours,)
tin ourania chara pou anavlyzei apo vathia!
(the heavenly joy that wells up from deep!)
Parousia Theoy plimmyrizei to einai mas
(God's presence floods our being)
kai mia geysi ouranoy reei mesa mas pantoy!
(and a taste of heaven flows through us everywhere!)
Parousia Theoy plimmyrizei to einai mas

Verse 3:
Malakonei i psychi, san akoyme ta logia Sou
(The soul softens, as we listen to Your words)
na skorpoyn dynami, gemise mas pio poly!
(to spread strength, fill us more!)
Mas allazeis Esy, nai, to niothoume mesa mas,
(You change us, yes, we feel it inside,)
i diki sou pnoi dinei noima sti zoi!
(your own breath gives meaning to life!)

Verse 4:
Mes sti thlipsi, Iisoy, mono Esena kalesame,
(In sorrow, Jesus, only You we called,)
pou na pame alloy, ti chara na vroyme pou;
(where else should we go, where should we find joy?)
T' onoma Sou, Thee, theloume na ypsosoume,
(Thy name, O God, we want to lift up,)
na doxasoume Se, o glykytate Amne!
(let us glorify Thee, O sweetness of Amne!)
Se latreyei i psychi, me mystiria i glossa mas
(The soul adores you, our tongue with mysteries)
pros Esena lalei, dynate mas Lytroti!
(to Thee he cries, our mighty Redeemer!)
