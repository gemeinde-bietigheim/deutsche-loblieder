105 AGAPI ThEIA, ThAYMASTI

Greek Hymns - No. 105 | "ΑΓΑΠΗ ΘΕΙΑ, ΘΑΥΜΑΣΤΗ"
Verse 1:
Agapi theia, thafmasti
(Godly love, wonderful)
pros ti sotiria pigi
(to the source of salvation)
eysplachna me odigise
(mercifully led me)
kai vrika ti zoi!
(and I found life!)
Chorus:
Ap' ti fthora me evgale,
(He brought me out of decay,)
ap' ta desma me lytrose,
(free me from bonds,)
sto theio fos m' odigise!
(to the divine light led me!)
O, ti chara, me esose!
(Oh, what joy, save me!)
Verse 2:
I theia chari tou Amnoy
(The divine grace of the Lamb)
m' elytrose ap' ta desma
(free me from my bonds)
kai ti kardia mou ti ftochi
(and my poor heart)
egemise chara!
(sigh of joy!)
Verse 3:
Desma thanatou espase
(Bond of death broken)
o Iisoys, o Lytrotis,
(Jesus, the Redeemer,)
zoi kainoyrgia charise
(new life he gave)
se kathe mia psychi!
(to every single soul!)
Verse 4:
Christe, diko Sou onoma
(Christ, Your name)
pantotina tha eflogo,
(forever I will bless,)
to thelima Sou de ego
(Your will, not mine)
panta tha ektelo!
(I will always perform!)
