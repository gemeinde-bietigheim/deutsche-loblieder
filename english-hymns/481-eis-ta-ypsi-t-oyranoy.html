481 EIS TA YPsI T' OYRANOY

Greek Hymns - No. 481 | "ΕΙΣ ΤΑ ΥΨΗ Τ' ΟΥΡΑΝΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Eis ta ypsi t' ouranoy, eis ti doxa ekei,
(In the heights of heaven, in glory there,)
opou i chari tou Theoy plimmyrei,
(where God's grace overflows,)
ekei pano sti zoi, stin anesper' afgi!
(up there in life, in the dawn!)
Ekei echoume patrida!
(We have a homeland there!)
Chorus:
O! Chara kai doxa imon, sotiria ton piston!
(Oh! Our joy and glory, salvation of the faithful!)
Ekei, opou o Christos, sto aprosito fos!
(There, where Christ, in the inaccessible light!)
Ekei echoume Patrida!
(There we have a Homeland!)
Verse 2:
Ekei opou i chara apastraptei pantoy,
(Where joy sparkles everywhere,)
opou asmata glyka antichoyn,
(where chants sweetly resound,)
ekei, opou o Christos, sto aprosito fos!
(there, where Christ, in the inaccessible light!)
Ekei echoume Patrida!
(There we have a Homeland!)
Verse 3:
Ston aggelon tous choroys, eis ta ypsi ekei,
(In the dances of the angels, in the heights there,)
opou psalloun Cherouveim, Serafeim,
(where Cherubim, Seraphim sing,)
opou reei o potamos tis zois katharos!
(where the river of life flows clear!)
Ekei echoume Patrida!
(There we have a Homeland!)
Verse 4:
Ekei, opou o Christos kathetai nikitis,
(There, where Christ sits victorious,)
me to aima tou stafroy Lytrotis,
(with the blood of the cross Redeemer,)
einai topos kai gia mas se endoxous monas!
(it is also a place for us in glorious monasteries!)
Ekei echoume Patrida!
(There we have a Homeland!)
