332 OTAN OI TRIKYMIES ELThOYN

Greek Hymns - No. 332 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΟΙ ΤΡΙΚΥΜΙΕΣ ΕΛΘΟΥΝ"
Verse 1:
Otan oi trikymies elthoun
(When the storms come)
kai lypes me perikykloyn
(and sorrows surround me)
kai filoi me egkataleipoun,
(and friends leave me,)
tha speyso pros ton Iisoy!
(I will rush to Jesus!)
Chorus:
O, pos potho na eimai monos
(Oh, how I long to be alone)
me to Theo stin prosefchi!
(with God in prayer!)
Makria ap' eme na ein' o kosmos,
(Far be the world from me,)
vrisko anapafsi ekei!
(I find rest there!)
Verse 2:
S' Afton efrisko eftychia,
(In Him I find happiness,)
nai, sotiria ki ilasmo,
(yes, salvation and joy,)
ekei vrisko chara agia,
(there I find joy saint,)
panta th' akoloutho Afton!
(I will always follow Him!)
Verse 3:
Ki an me profthasei o echthros mou,
(And if my enemy overtakes me,)
kathos vadizo tin odo,
(as I walk down the street,)
o Iisoys tha me voithisei
(Jesus will help me)
eis ton agona to skliro!
(in the fight the hard!)
