92 ZOYSA STI GI

Greek Hymns - No. 92 | "ΖΟΥΣΑ ΣΤΗ ΓΗ"
Verse 1:
Zoysa sti gi choris parigoria,
(I lived on earth without consolation,)
choris anapafsi kamia,
(without any rest,)
provlimata polla kai peirasmoi
(many problems and temptations)
ti thlipsi fernoun stin psychi!
(they bring sadness to the soul!)
Chorus:
Pou na statho, pou na stirichtho;
(Where should I stand, where should I lean?)
Galini psachno gia na vro!
(Peace I'm looking to find!)
Poy n' akoumpiso to vary stafro;
(Where shall I lay the heavy cross?)
Para monacha sto Christo!
(Except only in Christ!)
Verse 2:
Filous kaloys, adelfia, syggeneis
(Good friends, brothers, relatives)
echo, ma pou na stirichtho;
(I have, but where do I lean?)
Giati i kardia mou thelei kai zita
(Because my heart wants and asks)
trofi, nero apo psila;
(food, water from above?)
Verse 3:
Otan to telos fthasei kai gia me
(When the end comes for me too)
ki o thanatos sklira tha 'rthei,
(and death will come hard,)
gia na pagosei toyto to kormi,΄
(to freeze this body,')
poios tha zestanei tin psychi;
(who will warm the soul?)
