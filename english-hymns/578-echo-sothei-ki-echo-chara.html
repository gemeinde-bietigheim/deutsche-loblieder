578 EChO SOThEI KI EChO ChARA!

Greek Hymns - No. 578 | "ΕΧΩ ΣΩΘΕΙ ΚΙ ΕΧΩ ΧΑΡΑ!"
Verse 1:
Echo sothei ki echo chara!
(I am saved and I am happy!)
Echo sothei mesa ap' ti fthora.
(I am saved through corruption.)
Me pisti piga konta sto Stafro,
(With faith I went near the Cross,)
me pisti piga sto Christo!
(by faith I went to Christ!)
Me aima mou 'plyne tin kardia
(Wash my heart with blood)
ki echo sothei!
(and I am saved!)
Verse 2:
De me nika o kosmos pia!
(The world can't beat me anymore!)
De me nika dynami kamia!
(No power can defeat me!)
Konta mou echo Christo dynato,
(Near me I have strong Christ,)
to vracho echo n' akoumpo,
(the rock I have to lean on)
ki otan erchetai to kako,
(and when evil comes,)
de me nika!
(doesn't beat me!)
Verse 3:
Panta tha zo me to Christo!
(I will always live with Christ!)
Panta tha zo kai tha Ton ymno!
(I will always live and praise Him!)
Gemati tha 'cho tin kardia me chara
(I will have a heart full of joy)
kai me tis pistis ta ftera
(and with the wings of faith)
panta me to Christo konta,
(always with Christ near,)
panta tha zo!
(I will always live!)
Verse 4:
Ki otan vretho ston ourano
(And when I'm in heaven)
kai tha Ton do endoxo, lampro,
(and I will see Him glorious, bright,)
stefani tote lampro tha foro
(wreath then bright I will wear)
kai me aggelous tha ymno!
(and with angels I will sing!)
Panta me to Christo mou tha zo,
(Always with my Christ I will live,)
panta tha zo!
(I will always live!)
