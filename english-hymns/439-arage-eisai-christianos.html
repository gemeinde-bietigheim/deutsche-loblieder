439 ARAGE EISAI ChRISTIANOS

Greek Hymns - No. 439 | "ΑΡΑΓΕ ΕΙΣΑΙ ΧΡΙΣΤΙΑΝΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Arage eisai christianos, agios, dikaios, tapeinos,
(Are you a Christian, holy, just, humble,)
osios, praos kai efpeithis,
(faithful, meek and obedient,)
adolos, timios kai alithis;
(servile, honest and true?)
Chorus:
Ean den eisai, einai kairos, tha kanei toyto gia se o Christos!
(If you are not, it is time, Christ will do this for you!)
Aftos tha akoysei tin prosefchi
(He will hear the prayer)
kai tha exaleipsei tin enochi!
(and it will eliminate the guilt!)
Verse 2:
Legesai tora christianos, all' eisai pragmati kai agnos,
(You are now called a Christian, but you are indeed pure,)
agnos sto pneyma, stous logismoys,
(pure in spirit, in calculations,)
eis tin kardia kai ofthalmoys;
(in the heart and eyes?)
Verse 3:
Dia tou Pneymatos tou Theoy anagennitheis tou Krataioy,
(By the Spirit of God you are reborn of the Stronghold,)
kai i kardia sou i rypari
(and your heart the polluter)
egine tora pia kathari;
(is it clean now?)
Verse 4:
Fereis to onoma tou Christoy kai tin eikona fereis Aftoy,
(You bear the name of Christ and you bear His image,)
egines tora nea psychi,
(you have now become a new soul,)
egine nea oli i zoi;
(has all life become new?)
Verse 5:
O! Evaptistheis me to septo Pneyma to theio, zoopoio;
(Oh! Are you baptized with the holy Spirit, the divine, creator?)
Me dynami Toyto martyrei
(With power This testifies)
kai echei entos sou diamoni;
(and does he have a residence in you?)
Chorus:
Ean den echeis pneyma Theoy, zitise Toyto para Aftoy!
(If you don't have God's spirit, ask Him for this!)
Aftos tha akoysei tin prosefchi
(He will hear the prayer)
kai tha plirosei ese, psychi!
(and you will pay, soul!)
