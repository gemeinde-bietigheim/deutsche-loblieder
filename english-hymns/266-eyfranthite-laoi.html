266 EYFRANThITE, LAOI

Greek Hymns - No. 266 | "ΕΥΦΡΑΝΘΗΤΕ, ΛΑΟΙ"
Verse 1:
Effranthite, laoi, Christos anesti!
(Rejoice, people, Christ is risen!)
As Ton ymnisoume oloi mazi!
(Let's all praise Him together!)
Chorus:
Apo ton tafo ton nekron
(From the grave of the dead)
anastithike o Iisoys Christos!
(Jesus Christ is risen!)
Kai ypsothike yperano ton echthron,
(And rose above the enemies,)
pantote tha zei meth' olon ton piston,
(will always live with all believers,)
pantote, pantote, alliloyia, pantote!
(always, always, hallelujah, always!)
Verse 2:
Mataia fylagan Christoy ton tafo,
(They guarded Christ's tomb in vain,)
mataia fylagan soma Aftoy!
(they guarded His body in vain!)
Verse 3:
Endoxa espase desma tou Adi
(He gloriously broke the bonds of Hades)
kai anastithike, o, ti chara!
(and he rose again, oh, what joy!)
Verse 4:
Oloi as psalloume, «Christos anesti!»,
(Let us all sing, "Christ is risen!")
pantoy as kiryxoume «o Kyrios zei!».
(everywhere let us proclaim "the Lord lives!")
