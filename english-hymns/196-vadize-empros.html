196 VADIZE EMPROS

Greek Hymns - No. 196 | "ΒΑΔΙΖΕ ΕΜΠΡΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Vadize empros, my skyveis to kefali,
(March forward, bow your head,)
otan milas gia tou Theoy ti chari!
(when you talk about God's grace!)
Vadize mpros kai min apokameis,
(Go ahead and don't back down,)
rixe ta vari sto Sotira Christo!
(cast your burdens on Christ the Saviour!)
Chorus:
Koita psila, poly psila ston ourano!
(Look up, way up in the sky!)
Koita psila, poly psila pros ton Theo!
(Look up, far up to God!)
Verse 2:
Vadize empros, to mellon mi fovasai,
(Move forward, don't be afraid of the future,)
mila gi' Afton, zise gi' Afton!
(talk about Him, live for Him!)
Vadize empros ki ase to cheri Ekeinou
(Walk forward and let go of His hand)
na s' odigisei sto dromo pou tha pas!
(to lead you on the way you are going!)
Verse 3:
Vadize empros kai mi gyrizeis piso,
(Walk forward and don't turn back,)
afta pou petaxes min ta zitas!
(don't ask for what you threw away!)
Krata kala to Logo tou Theoy mas,
(Keep well the Word of our God,)
sou 'chei charaxei to dromo pou tha pas!
(He has marked the path you will take!)
