200 TON IISOY MOY Th' AKOLOYThISO

Greek Hymns - No. 200 | "ΤΟΝ ΙΗΣΟΥ ΜΟΥ Θ' ΑΚΟΛΟΥΘΗΣΩ"
Verse 1:
Ton Iisoy mou th' akolouthiso,
(I will follow my Jesus,)
ton Iisoy mou th' akolouthiso,
(my jesus i will follow)
nai, apofasisa m' Afton na ziso
(yes, I decided to live with Him)
kai de tha strepso pote mou piso!
(and I will never turn back!)
kai de tha strepso pote mou piso!
(and I will never turn back!)
Verse 2:
Mpros o stafros Tou ki o kosmos piso,
(His cross in front and the world behind,)
mpros o stafros Tou ki o kosmos piso,
(His cross ahead and the world behind,)
nai, apofasisa n' akolouthiso
(yes i decided to follow)
kai de tha strepso pote mou piso!
(and I will never turn back!)
kai de tha strepso pote mou piso!
(and I will never turn back!)
Verse 3:
Olo ton kosmo pare diko sou,
(Take the whole world for yourself)
ton Iisoy mou monacha dos' mou,
(my Jesus only give me)
ton Iisoy mou na ton kerdiso
(my Jesus to win him)
kai de tha strepso pote mou piso!
(and I will never turn back!)
kai de tha strepso pote mou piso!
(and I will never turn back!)
Verse 4:
Kai an den elthei kaneis mazi mou,
(And if no one comes with me,)
kai an den elthei kaneis mazi mou,
(and if no one comes with me,)
ego kai tote tha akolouthiso
(and then I will follow)
kai de tha strepso pote mou piso!
(and I will never turn back!)
kai de tha strepso pote mou piso!
(and I will never turn back!)
