273 ME TO ChRISTO MPROSTA

Greek Hymns - No. 273 | "ΜΕ ΤΟ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ ΜΠΡΟΣΤΑ"
Verse 1:
Me to Christo mprosta feygoun ta ori,
(With Christ in front, the mountains leave,)
me nikes pantote tha 'nai i zoi!
(with victories will always be life!)
Mpes sto karavi Tou kai vale plori,
(Get into His boat and set the bow,)
to Pneyma t' Agio tha s' odigei!
(the Holy Spirit will guide you!)
Chorus:
Pliroysthe pantote me Pneyma Agio!
(Always be filled with the Holy Spirit!)
Eis tous pisteyontas echei dothei!
(It has been given to those who believe!)
Pliroysthe pantote me Pneyma Agio!
(Always be filled with the Holy Spirit!)
Kante ti mera sas Pentikosti!
(Make your day Pentecost!)
Verse 2:
Me to Christo mprosta, pou mas antrionei,
(With Christ in front, who strengthens us,)
schizontai oi thalasses kai ta vouna!
(seas and mountains are torn apart!)
To Pneyma t' Agio, pou mas plironei,
(The Holy Spirit, who pays us,)
mas paei ston ourano mes sti chara!
(takes us to heaven to joy!)
Verse 3:
Me to Christo mprosta tha 'nai i zoi sou
(With Christ in front will be your life)
ena limani, mia sigouria!
(a port, a certainty!)
To Pneyma t' Agio tha 'nai mazi sou
(The Holy Spirit will be with you)
se kathe anagki sou parigoria!
(comfort in your every need!)
