513 ChARA MAS PROSMENEI

Greek Hymns - No. 513 | "ΧΑΡΑ ΜΑΣ ΠΡΟΣΜΕΝΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Chara mas prosmenei ekei,
(Joy awaits us there,)
opou o Christos me tous aggelous katoikei
(where Christ with the angels dwells)
kai perimenei tous agious ap' ti gi!
(and waits for the saints from the earth!)
I teleftaia i salpigga ichei
(The last trumpet sounds)
kai oi nekroi tou Kyriou afthartoi,
(and the dead of the Lord incorruptible,)
akoyo ti foni pou antichei,
(i hear the voice echoing)
kai me tous zontas adelfoys anerchontai
(and with the living brethren ascend)
sto mesouranima me doxa erchontai
(at midnight with glory they come)
na synantisoun ton Iisoy Christo,
(to meet Jesus Christ,)
to Lytroti tous kai panton archigo!
(their Redeemer and leader of all!)
Verse 2:
Kai eno eis ti gi ti skoteini
(And while in the dark earth)
stenochoria ton ethnon kai peirasmoi,
(distress of the nations and temptations,)
ichei i thalassa, tarassontai oi laoi!
(the sea sounds, the peoples are agitated!)
Ma ekei sta misa tou ouranoy
(But there in the middle of the sky)
o gamos tou nymfiou ekplironetai
(the bridegroom's marriage is fulfilled)
kai ton piston i doxa faneronetai!
(and the glory of the faithful is revealed!)
Eyge, makarioi oi doyloi alithinoi,
(Hail, blessed are the true servants,)
irthe i ora na zisoume mazi.
(it's time to live together.)
Irthe i ora na zisoume mazi,
(It's time to live together)
eyge, makarioi oi doyloi alithinoi!
(Hail, blessed are the true servants!)
