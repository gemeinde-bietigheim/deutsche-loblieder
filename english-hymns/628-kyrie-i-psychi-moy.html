628 KYRIE, I PsYChI MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 628 | "ΚΥΡΙΕ, Η ΨΥΧΗ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Kyrie, i psychi mou dipsaei gia Se!
(Lord, my soul thirsts for You!)
I kardia mou pothei Christe, ena aggigma Sou!
(My heart longs Christ, a touch of Yours!)
Erimos i zoi mes ston kosmo afto,
(Life is desolate in this world,)
agonizomai, prospatho, thelo tin agkalia Sou!
(I struggle, I try, I want Your embrace!)
Verse 2:
Se zito, ela, Kyrie na se do
(I am asking you, come, Lord, let me see you)
kai konta Sou n' anapaftho, pare ola ta vari!
(and close to You to rest, take all the burdens!)
Steile mou dynami gia na perpato
(Send me strength to walk)
mes sto dromo Sou to steno, ela, dose mou chari!
(into Thy way the strait, come, give me grace!)
Verse 3:
Kyrie, ti zoi Sou Sy proseferes
(Lord, You gave Your life)
kai to aima sou echyses, gia na me lytroseis!
(and you shed your blood to redeem me!)
Ta vari kratises sto stafro kai tis thlipseis mou!
(You kept the burdens on the cross and my sorrows!)
Agio Pneyma mou edoses, gia na me endynamosei!
(Holy Spirit you gave me, to strengthen me!)
Verse 4:
Stin kardia echo tora poly chara,
(I now have much joy in my heart,)
mesa mou i eirini Sou tora vasileyei!
(in me Your peace now reigns!)
Ki erchomai na mpo stin parousia Sou
(And I come to enter into Your presence)
kai sto oros Sou t' agio i psychi mou n' anevei!
(and to Your holy mountain may my soul ascend!)
