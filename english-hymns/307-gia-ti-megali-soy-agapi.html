307 GIA TI MEGALI SOY AGAPI

Greek Hymns - No. 307 | "ΓΙΑ ΤΗ ΜΕΓΑΛΗ ΣΟΥ ΑΓΑΠΗ"
Verse 1:
Gia ti megali sou agapi
(For your great love)
pou edeixes gia me, Christe,
(that you showed for me, Christ,)
s' efcharisto me tin kardia mou,
(thank you from my heart,)
amome tou Theoy Amne!
(for God's sake Amne!)
Chorus:
Gia me gennithikes sti fatni,
(For me you were born in a manger,)
gia mena vastaxes stafro,
(for me you bore the cross,)
gia me t' akanthino stefani,
(for with the crown of thorns,)
gia mena ton amartolo!
(for me the sinner!)
Verse 2:
Kaneis kai tipota ston kosmo
(Nobody and nothing in the world)
den mporese chara agni
(pure joy could not)
na dosei mesa sti kardia mou,
(to give into my heart,)
pou itan pantot' adeiani!
(which was always empty!)
Verse 3:
Ki ilthes Esy, o Lytroti mou,
(And You came, O my Redeemer,)
mes sti zoi mou ti ftochia
(into my life poverty)
na doseis ti glykia galini,
(to give sweet peace,)
chara, agapi ki omorfia!
(joy, love and beauty!)
Verse 4:
Esy thysiastikes gia mena
(You sacrificed yourself for me)
sto xylo pano tou stafroy
(on the wood on the cross)
kai ti diki mou amartia
(and my own sin)
sygchorises, o Iisoy!
(forgive, O Jesus!)
Verse 5:
S' efcharisto ap' ti kardia mou
(Thank you from the bottom of my heart)
pou m' esoses ap' to chamo,
(who saved me from destruction,)
esy pou ilthes, gia na soseis
(you who came to save)
kathe thnito amartolo!
(every mortal sinner!)
