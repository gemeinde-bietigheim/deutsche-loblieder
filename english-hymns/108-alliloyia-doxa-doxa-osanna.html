108 ALLILOYIA, DOXA, DOXA, OSANNA!

Greek Hymns - No. 108 | "ΑΛΛΗΛΟΥΙΑ, ΔΟΞΑ, ΔΟΞΑ, ΩΣΑΝΝΑ!"
Verse 1:
Alliloyia, doxa, doxa, Osanna!
(Hallelujah, glory, glory, Hosanna!)
To Christo mou tha ymno pantotina!
(I will praise my Christ forever!)
Ekeinon tha latreyo, Ekeinon th' agapo,
(Him I will adore, Him I will love,)
Ekeinon tha doxazo, Afton tha ypireto!
(Him will I glorify, Him will I serve!)
Verse 2:
Doxa, alliloyia, doxa, Osanna!
(Glory, hallelujah, glory, Hosanna!)
Den mporo na mi laliso fanera
(I can't help but cry out loud)
oti echo ena Sotira, enan kalo odigo,
(that I have a Savior, a good guide,)
pote de tha m' afisei na patho ti kako!
(he will never let me suffer any harm!)
Verse 3:
Doxa, doxa, alliloyia, Osanna!
(Glory, glory, hallelujah, Hosanna!)
Se protrepo na gnoriseis ti chara
(I urge you to know joy)
pou mon' Aftos prosferei, giati einai i pigi
(which only He offers, because He is the source)
tis kathe eftychias pano s' afti ti gi!
(of every happiness on this earth!)
Verse 4:
Alliloyia, alliloyia, Osanna!
(Hallelujah, hallelujah, Hosanna!)
Xefyg' ap' ti kolasi sta alithina!
(Escape from hell for real!)
To martyrei to Pneyma ki i pisti sti kardia,
(The Spirit and faith in the heart bear witness to it,)
den echo pleon varos, echo polli chara!
(I have no weight anymore, I have a lot of joy!)
Verse 5:
Doxa, doxa, alliloyia, Osanna!
(Glory, glory, hallelujah, Hosanna!)
Papse na peripateis sta skoteina!
(Stop walking in the dark!)
Ki otan loustheis sto aima, tote Pentikosti
(And when you are bathed in blood, then Pentecost)
tha kanei o Theos sou, gia na s' episkefthei!
(your God will do, to visit you!)
