476 GIGANTES ThA PESOYN

Greek Hymns - No. 476 | "ΓΙΓΑΝΤΕΣ ΘΑ ΠΕΣΟΥΝ"
Verse 1:
Gigantes tha pesoun,
(Giants will fall,)
nai, gigantes tha pesoun,
(yes, giants will fall,)
sta cheria mas tha einai i gi!
(in our hands will be the earth!)
I chora pou aiones
(The country that centuries)
oloi oi pistoi prosmenoun
(all the faithful are waiting)
na fthasoun s' afti!
(to reach her!)
Chorus:
Fos ekei ein' Aftos,
(Light there is He,)
o Iisoys mas Christos!
(our Jesus Christ!)
I chara kai i zoi, nai, i zoi!
(Joy and life, yes, life!)
Otan tha fexei i mera,
(When the day breaks,)
tha fthasoume sti chora
(we will reach the country)
ti chrysi!
(the gold!)
Verse 2:
Akou melodia!
(Hear a tune!)
Nai, akou melodia
(Yes, hear me tune)
pou erchetai ap' tin chora afti!
(who comes from this country!)
Oi aggeloi giortazoun,
(Angels celebrate,)
tin trapeza etoimazoun
(the bank is being prepared)
sti chora afti!
(in this country!)
