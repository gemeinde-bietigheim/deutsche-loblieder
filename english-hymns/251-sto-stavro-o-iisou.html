251 Sto Stavro O Iisou

Greek Hymns - No. 251 | "At the Cross, Oh Jesus"
Verse 1:
Sto Stavro, o Iisou,
(At the Cross, oh Jesus,)
kata me plision
(keep me near)
pathima na theoro
(to behold the suffering)
t' agio kai theio!
(the holy and divine!)
Chorus:
Sto stavro, sto stavro
(At the cross, at the cross)
ein' i kafchisi mou!
(is my boast!)
O Christos apethane
(Christ died)
kai gia tin psychi mou!
(and for my soul!)
Verse 2:
Sto stavro, deili psychi,
(At the cross, fearful soul,)
trexe, mi distazeis,
(run, don't hesitate,)
ela eis tin prosefchi
(come to prayer)
kai mi deiliazeis!
(and don't be afraid!)
Verse 3:
Sto stavro, Amne Theou,
(At the cross, Lamb of God,)
etrekse to aima
(the blood flowed)
na me sosei ap' to frikto
(to save me from the terrible)
amartias revma!
(river of sin!)
Verse 4:
Sto stavro tha agrypno
(At the cross I will watch)
kai tha ypomeno
(and I will endure)
kai ekei ston ourano
(and there in heaven)
me Ese tha meno!
(with You I will remain!)
