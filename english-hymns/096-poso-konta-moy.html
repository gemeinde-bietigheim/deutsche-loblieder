96 POSO KONTA MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 96 | "ΠΟΣΟ ΚΟΝΤΑ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Poso konta mou ein' o Christos mou,
(How close is my Christ,)
oso den einai allos kaneis,
(as long as no one else is,)
otan sto dromo vriskomai monos,
(when I'm alone on the road)
vadizei plai filos glykys!
(walks aside sweet friend!)
Chorus:
Otan den echo poy n' akoumpiso,
(When I have nowhere to lean,)
otan den echo pou na strafo,
(when I have nowhere to turn,)
Aftos mou einai to stirigma mou,
(He is my support,)
einai to cheri to patriko!
(it is the paternal hand!)
Verse 2:
Otan me zonoun mesa ston kosmo
(When they live me in the world)
oi dyskolies ki oi peirasmoi,
(difficulties and temptations,)
richno ena vlemma sto Stafromeno
(I glance at the Crucified)
ki erchetai niki aftostigmi!
(and victory is coming at once!)
Verse 3:
Kai otan thelo na Tou zitiso
(And when I want to ask Him)
kapoia voitheia mes sti zoi,
(some help in life)
den ein' anagki alloy na trexo,
(I don't need to run anywhere else,)
einai konta mou stin prosefchi!
(he is near me in prayer!)
