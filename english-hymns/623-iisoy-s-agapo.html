623 IISOY S' AGAPO

Greek Hymns - No. 623 | "ΙΗΣΟΥ Σ' ΑΓΑΠΩ"
Verse 1:
Iisoy S' agapo, giati m' echeis agapisei,
(Jesus I love you, because you have loved me,)
otan oloi gyro mou monacho m' eichan afisei!
(when everyone around me had left me alone!)
Ti stigmi pou ego eicha anagki gi' agapi,
(When I needed love,)
mono Esy, Iisoy, de mou gyrises tin plati!
(only You, Jesus, did not turn your back on me!)
Chorus:
S' agapo kai potho kapoia mera konta Sou
(I love you and long for you someday)
na vretho, Iisoy, sti zesti agkalia Sou!
(to find myself, Jesus, in Your warm embrace!)
Verse 2:
Oso ki an prospatho, den mporo na ekfraso,
(No matter how hard I try, I can't express,)
na Sou po gia ola afta pou m' echeis kanei na nioso!
(to tell You about everything you've made me feel!)
To onoma Sou to glyko oli mera psithyrizo,
(Your sweet name all day long I whisper,)
i agapi Sou i glykia tis kardias mou ein' o chtypos!
(Your love is the sweet beat of my heart!)
