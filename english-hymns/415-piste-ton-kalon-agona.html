415 PISTE, TON KALON AGONA

Greek Hymns - No. 415 | "ΠΙΣΤΕ, ΤΟΝ ΚΑΛΟΝ ΑΓΩΝΑ"
Verse 1:
Piste, ton kalon agona ofeileis n' agonistheis
(Believe me, you have to fight the good fight)
sto stadion toytou tou viou, an theleis telos na steftheis!
(at this stage of life, if you want to finally be crowned!)
Chorus:
Vraveion, nai, aeithales, amaranton sy eftychis
(Prizes, yes, evergreens, amaranth and happy)
tha laveis, an trexeis andreios ton dromo sou nyn epi gis!
(you will receive, if you bravely run your way now on earth!)
Verse 2:
Chrysoyn ekei s' anamenei vraveion en ourano,
(Gold there awaits you a prize in heaven,)
timis kai doxis aioniou ouchi fthartis kathos edo!
(honor and glory eternal not perishable as here!)
Verse 3:
Loipon tin osfyn sou zoson kai dromon nyn ton makron
(So gird up your loins and walk the long way)
sto stadion andreios trexon na laveis stefanon lampron!
(in the stadium bravely run to receive a brilliant crown!)
Verse 4:
Aporripson nyn pan varos kai pan to emplekon se,
(Cast off now all weight and all that involves you,)
en to stadio echon tharros andrizou, treche, o piste!
(in the stage of bravery, run, O believer!)
