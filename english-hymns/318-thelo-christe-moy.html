318 ThELO, ChRISTE MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 318 | "ΘΕΛΩ, ΧΡΙΣΤΕ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Thelo, Christe mou, pyxida mou na gineis,
(I want you, my Christ, to be my compass,)
na m' odigeis stin amomi odo.
(to lead me on the virgin road.)
Thelo noimona kardia na mou dineis,
(I want an intelligent heart for you to give me,)
ton Agio Logo Sou na krato!
(Your Holy Word to keep!)
Chorus:
Min epitrepseis na epistrepso
(Don't let me go back)
eis tin odo ton asevon.
(in the way of the ungodly.)
To vlemma mou xana, xana na strepso
(My gaze again, again to turn)
sto anomo mou parelthon.
(in my lawless past.)
To vlemma mou xana, xana na strepso
(My gaze again, again to turn)
sto anomo mou parelthon.
(in my lawless past.)
Verse 2:
Thelo ti mera na 'sai ilios kai skia mou,
(I want the day to be my sun and shadow,)
synodoiporos mou ta vari na kratas.
(my companion the burdens to hold.)
Thelo ti nychta na mou deichneis tin kardia mou,
(I want you to show me my heart at night)
na diorthonomai me chara!
(to be corrected with pleasure!)
Verse 3:
Thelo stis thlipseis na 'sai i parigoria mou,
(I want you to be my comfort in sorrows,)
na mou charizeis eirini stin kardia.
(grant me peace in my heart.)
Thelo, Christe mou, stis pliges mou Esy na gineis
(I want, my Christ, to become You in my wounds)
to valsamo kai i giatreia!
(the balm and the medicine!)
