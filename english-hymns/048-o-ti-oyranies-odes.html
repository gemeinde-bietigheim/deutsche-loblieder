48 O! TI OYRANIES ODES

Greek Hymns - No. 48 | "Ω! ΤΙ ΟΥΡΑΝΙΕΣ ΩΔΕΣ"
Verse 1:
O! ti ouranies odes
(Oh! what heavenly odes)
mesa eis ti Sion ichoyn!
(in Zion they resound!)
Ti melodia, ti fones,
(What melody, what voices,)
stous lofous tis pou antichoyn!
(on her echoing hills!)
Chorus:
Doxa, psallate laoi, doxa, ouranoi kai gi!
(Glory, sing people, glory, heavens and earth!)
Osanna! Osanna! Osanna! ston Yio David!
(Hosanna! Hosanna! Hosanna! to Son David!)
Doxa, psallate laoi, doxa, ouranoi kai gi!
(Glory, sing people, glory, heavens and earth!)
Osanna! Osanna! Osanna! ston Yio David!
(Hosanna! Hosanna! Hosanna! to Son David!)
Verse 2:
Paidia psalloun me chara,
(Children sing with joy,)
«doxa ston panton Vasilia!»
("glory to the King of all!")
«Na o Sotiras!» len' mazi,
("Behold the Saviour!" say together)
«olous mas sozei ki eflogei!».
("he saves and blesses us all!".)
Verse 3:
To onoma tou Iisoy
(The name of Jesus)
kanei psyches mas na charoyn
(it makes our souls happy)
kai Ioudaiou kai ethnikoy,
(both Jew and Gentile,)
gi' afto oloi to eflogoyn!
(therefore all bless it!)
Verse 4:
Ton Kyrio mas kai Theo,
(Our Lord and God,)
ton Iisoy, ymneite seis!
(praise Jesus!)
Doxastike ston ourano
(He was glorified in heaven)
kai tha 'rthei pali eis ti gi!
(and will come again to earth!)
