94 MEGALO FILO VRIKA EGO

Greek Hymns - No. 94 | "ΜΕΓΑΛΟ ΦΙΛΟ ΒΡΗΚΑ ΕΓΩ"
Verse 1:
Megalo filo vrika ego kai pos na Ton ymniso;
(I have found a great friend and how can I praise Him?)
M' agapise protoy ego katholou Ton gnoriso!
(He loved me before I even knew Him!)
Kaneis de tha mporesei pia ta vathi na metrisei
(No one will be able to measure the depths anymore)
kai apo tin agapi Tou, psychi mou, na chorisei!
(and from His love, my soul, to part!)
Verse 2:
Megalo filo vrika ego pou echyse to aima
(I found a great friend who shed the blood)
ki epano sto skliro stafro egin' Aftos to thyma!
(and on the hard cross He became the victim!)
O,ti ki an echo sto Christo prosfero gia thysia,
(Whatever I have in Christ I offer as a sacrifice,)
soma, psychi, zoi, ischy, oli mou tin kardia!
(body, soul, life, strength, all my heart!)
Verse 3:
Megalo filo vrika ego ki oli i exousia
(I found a great friend and all the power)
s' Afton edothike sti gi gia doxa aionia!
(to Him was given on earth for glory forever!)
Aftos eis tous agones mou frouros kai archigos mou,
(He in my struggles my guard and leader,)
i agapi Tou einai zoi, chara kai stefanos mou!
(His love is my life, joy and crown!)
Verse 4:
Megalo filo vrika ego, pisto, agapimeno,
(I found a great friend, faithful, beloved,)
sofo mou symvoulo, pisto, poly eflogimeno!
(my wise counselor, faithful, most blessed!)
Kaneis de tha mporesei pia eme na deleasei
(No one will be able to tempt me anymore)
oyte ap' ti agapi Tou pote na m' apospasei!
(never to distract me from His love!)
