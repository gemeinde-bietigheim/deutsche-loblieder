319 ThYMAMAI, ChRISTE MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 319 | "ΘΥΜΑΜΑΙ, ΧΡΙΣΤΕ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Thymamai, Christe mou, toses fores
(I remember, Christ, so many times)
pou irthes konta mou kai me aggixes
(that you came to me and touched me)
kai toses pliges mou giatrepses!
(and you healed so many wounds!)
Pos na xechaso toses stigmes
(How can I forget so many moments)
sta gonata pano pou me gemises,
(on your knees where you filled me)
me t' Agio to Pneyma me stirixes!
(with the Holy Spirit you supported me!)
Chorus:
S' efcharisto gia oles aftes tis stigmes,
(Thank you for all these moments,)
pou isoun mazi mou kai me zestanes.
(that you were with me and warmed me.)
Xero pos tha 'sai gia panta konta
(I know you'll be around forever)
kratontas to cheri mou mechri telous sfichta!
(holding my hand until the end tight!)
Kratontas to cheri mou mechri telous sfichta!
(Holding my hand all the way tight!)
Verse 2:
Thymamai mia nychta pou arrostisa,
(I remember one night when I got sick,)
ston pono mou mesa Ese fonaxa
(in my pain inside You I cried)
kai tote eniosa giatreia!
(and then I felt healing!)
Ki otan oi filoi de m' eniothan,
(And when my friends couldn't feel me,)
Esy me agapi kai cheria anoichta
(You with love and open arms)
erchosoun na doseis parigoria!
(you were coming to give comfort!)
