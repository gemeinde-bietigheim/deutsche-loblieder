15 DOXA KAI TIMI S' ESENA

Greek Hymns - No. 15 | "ΔΟΞΑ ΚΑΙ ΤΙΜΗ Σ' ΕΣΕΝΑ"
Verse 1:
Doxa kai timi s' Esena, o Patera kai Yie,
(Glory and honor to You, O Father and Son,)
kai se Se, o Pneyma Agio, o Triadike Thee!
(and to You, O Holy Spirit, O Triune God!)
Se ymnoyme, S' eflogoyme, proskynoyme, Krataie!
(We praise you, we bless you, we worship you, Krataie!)
Verse 2:
I latreia kai o ainos Sou anikoune, Christe!
(Worship and praise belong to You, Christ!)
Sou i doxa kai to kratos, o aionie Thee!
(Thine be the glory and the kingdom, O eternal God!)
Se ymnoyme, S' eflogoyme, proskynoyme, Krataie!
(We praise you, we bless you, we worship you, Krataie!)
Verse 3:
Lytromenoi kai plymenoi me to aima Sou, Christe,
(Redeemed and washed in Your blood, Christ,)
dexou to «efcharisto» mas, pantodyname Thee!
(accept our "thank you", almighty God!)
Alliloyia, alliloyia, alliloyia, pantote!
(Hallelujah, hallelujah, hallelujah, always!)
