62 TI APEIRI EIN' I AGAPI AYTOY

Greek Hymns - No. 62 | "ΤΙ ΑΠΕΙΡΗ ΕΙΝ' Η ΑΓΑΠΗ ΑΥΤΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Ti apeiri ein' i agapi Aftoy,
(How infinite is His love,)
san aperantos okeanos!
(like a vast ocean!)
Pleyse, adelfe, eis ta vathi tou Theoy,
(Sail, brother, into the depths of God,)
eosoy se agnisei o Christos!
(until Christ cleanses you!)
Chorus:
Pleyse, mi fovitheis,
(Sail on, don't be afraid)
sta vathi tou Theoy!
(in the depths of God!)
Pleyse ekei, pou agapi katoikei
(Sail there, where love dwells)
kai tha 'sai eftychis!
(and you will be happy!)
Verse 2:
Stin ochthi afti stekontai merikoi,
(On this bank stand some,)
den tolmoyn na richtoyn sta vathia,
(they dare not throw themselves into the deep,)
agnooyn oti, an pleysoun monon ekei,
(unaware that if they only sail there,)
th' apolaysoun aionia chara!
(they will enjoy eternal joy!)
Verse 3:
Kai alloi polloi stekoun apo makria
(And many others stand afar off)
kai den apofasizoun pote,
(and they never decide,)
tous kratoyne profaseis ki empodia polla,
(many pretexts and obstacles keep them,)
einai monon aploi theates!
(they are only mere spectators!)
Verse 4:
As pleysoume oloi mazi sta vathia,
(Let's all sail into the deep together,)
kata tin entoli tou Christoy,
(at the command of Christ,)
th' apolaysoume afthona ta agatha,
(we will enjoy the goods abundantly,)
thisafroys aionious Theoy!
(eternal treasures of God!)
