386 GIA TOY ChRISTOY TI ChARI

Greek Hymns - No. 386 | "ΓΙΑ ΤΟΥ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΥ ΤΗ ΧΑΡΗ"
Verse 1:
Gia tou Christoy ti chari trexte oloi,
(For Christ's sake run, all of you,)
gia tou Christoy ti doxa sti douleia,
(for Christ the glory at work,)
mes ston agro Tou oloi prepei na mpoyme,
(into His field we must all enter,)
gia na xefygei o kosmos ap' ti sklavia!
(for the world to escape from slavery!)
Chorus:
Sfixte ta cheria, oloi enotheite
(Shake hands, everyone unite)
s' enan agona, s' ena skopo,
(in a fight, in a cause,)
mia ekklisia pou deichnei s' olous
(a church that shows everyone)
Keinon pou einai o Lytromos!
(Who is the Redeemer!)
Verse 2:
Oloi mazi sti machi tis agapis
(All together in the battle of love)
gia tin eirini kai ti lefteria,
(for peace and freedom,)
sti theia toyti strata, sti doylepsi Tou,
(in this divine army, in His work,)
as prochorisoum' oloi me mia kardia!
(let us all move forward with one heart!)
Verse 3:
Gia tou Kyriou mas tin pisti oloi,
(For our Lord's faith all,)
gia tin apolafsi mias neas zois,
(to enjoy a new life,)
gia eftychia ourania kai ti galini,
(for heavenly happiness and peace,)
adelfia cheri-cheri me mia psychi!
(brothers hand in hand with one soul!)
Verse 4:
Gia ti giorti tis synaxis sta ourania,
(For the feast of the assembly in heaven,)
gia ti megali elpida ekei psila,
(for the great hope up there,)
stratefma tis agapis, me ton Christo sou
(army of love, with your Christ)
trava to dromo tora ap' ton Golgotha!
(make your way now from Calvary!)
