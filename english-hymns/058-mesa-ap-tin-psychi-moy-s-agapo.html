58 MESA AP' TIN PsYChI MOY S' AGAPO

Greek Hymns - No. 58 | "ΜΕΣΑ ΑΠ' ΤΗΝ ΨΥΧΗ ΜΟΥ Σ' ΑΓΑΠΩ"
Verse 1:
Mesa ap' tin psychi mou S' agapo
(From the bottom of my soul I love you)
kai gia panta tha S' efgnomono!
(and I will forever be grateful to You!)
Sy me lytroses ap' ta desma
(You freed me from my bonds)
tis amartias kai mou charises chara!
(of sin and you gave me joy!)
Chorus:
Nai, S' agapao!
(Yes, I love you!)
Me oli tin psychi mou S' agapao!
(With all my soul I love you!)
Me oli tin kardia mou S' agapao!
(With all my heart I love you!)
Dos' mou dynami na S' agapao kathe mera pio poly!
(Give me strength to love You more every day!)
Nai, S' agapao!
(Yes, I love you!)
Me oli tin psychi mou S' agapao!
(With all my soul I love you!)
Me oli tin kardia mou S' agapao!
(With all my heart I love you!)
Dos' mou dynami na S' agapao kathe mera pio poly!
(Give me strength to love You more every day!)
Verse 2:
Kyrie mou, poso S' agapo,
(my lord how i love you)
kai gia panta tha S' akoloutho!
(and forever I will follow You!)
Sy me eplynes ap' ti ntropi
(You washed me of shame)
tis amartias kai mou charises zoi!
(of sin and you gave me life!)
