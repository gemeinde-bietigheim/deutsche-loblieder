192 ChAIRETAI KAI PsALLEI I KARDIA MAS

Greek Hymns - No. 192 | "ΧΑΙΡΕΤΑΙ ΚΑΙ ΨΑΛΛΕΙ Η ΚΑΡΔΙΑ ΜΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Chairetai kai psallei i kardia mas
(Our hearts rejoice and sing)
mes sto diava toytis tis zois!
(in the way of this life!)
Proskairotis gyro kai konta mas,
(Opportunity around and near us,)
to gnorizoume afto emeis!
(we know this!)
Chairet' i kardia mas kai sta sothika mas
(Our heart rejoices and in our hearts)
echoume mia nea ki omorfi zoi,
(we have a new and beautiful life,)
mia zoi agia ki olo eflogia
(a life holy and full of blessing)
plimmyrizei mesa stin psychi!
(it floods into the soul!)
Verse 2:
Neoi, geroi oloi eftychismenoi
(Young, old all happy)
niothoume mia adoli chara!
(we feel an indescribable joy!)
Eimaste st' alitheia sesosmenoi.
(We are truly in love.)
Alliloyia psallei i kardia!
(Hallelujah the heart sings!)
O Christos charizei sti psychi galini,
(Christ gives peace to the soul,)
dinei sotiria, feygei i ara!
(it gives salvation, the plague goes away!)
File, an zitiseis na vreis tin eirini,
(Friend, if you ask to find peace,)
tha ti vreis ekei sto Golgotha!
(you will find her there at Calvary!)
Verse 3:
I zoi mas grigora diavainei,
(Our life is fast passing,)
ola gyro feygoun kai petoyn.
(everything around leaves and flies.)
Liges meres sti zoi mas menoun,
(We have a few days left in our lives,)
ma ki aftes se ligo tha chathoyn!
(but they too will soon disappear!)
Ki omos an theliseis aionia na ziseis,
(But if you want to live forever,)
prosexe ligaki, skepsou sovara!
(pay attention, think seriously!)
Na to mystiko mas, ela sto Christo mas,
(Here is our secret, come to our Christ,)
lave sotiria sti kardia!
(receive salvation in the heart!)
