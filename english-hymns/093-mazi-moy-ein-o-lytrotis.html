93 MAZI MOY EIN' O LYTROTIS

Greek Hymns - No. 93 | "ΜΑΖΙ ΜΟΥ ΕΙΝ' Ο ΛΥΤΡΩΤΗΣ"
Verse 1:
Mazi mou ein' o Lytrotis, afotou m' esose,
(The Redeemer is with me, after he saved me,)
stis dyskolies tis zois pote de m' afise!
(in the difficulties of life he never left me!)
Chorus:
Theia koinonia me ton Iisoy
(Divine communion with Jesus)
ein' eflogimeni schesi ki eftychia pantachoy!
(blessed relationship and happiness everywhere!)
Verse 2:
Apo to cheri me kratei eis ti steni odo,
(He holds me by the hand in the narrow street,)
stis thlipseis me parigorei, sikonei to stafro!
(in sorrows he comforts me, he lifts the cross!)
Verse 3:
An arrostiso ein' mazi o monos Iatros,
(If I fall ill, the only Physician is with me,)
einai Sotiras tis psychis kai filos mou pistos!
(he is Savior of the soul and my faithful friend!)
Verse 4:
Otan oi filoi m' arnithoyn ki aftoi oi syggeneis,
(When friends and relatives deny me,)
pistos tha menei o Christos sto dromo tis zois!
(Christ will remain faithful on the road of life!)
Verse 5:
Ki otan o thanatos tha 'rthei sto telos tis zois,
(And when death comes at the end of life,)
ekei tha einai o Christos na me ypodechthei!
(Christ will be there to welcome me!)
