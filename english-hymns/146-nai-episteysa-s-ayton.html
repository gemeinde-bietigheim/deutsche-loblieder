146 NAI, EPISTEYSA S' AYTON

Greek Hymns - No. 146 | "ΝΑΙ, ΕΠΙΣΤΕΥΣΑ Σ' ΑΥΤΟΝ"
Verse 1:
Nai, epistefsa s' Afton kai doxazo to Christo!
(Yes, I believed in Him and I glorify Christ!)
Nai, apolafsa to fos, o, doxa sto Theo!
(Yes, I enjoyed the light, oh, glory to God!)
Ti lampro kai thafmasto, tora zo me to Theo!
(How bright and wonderful, now I live with God!)
Afisa to parelthon, tora psallo sto Christo!
(I left the past, now I sing to Christ!)
Chorus:
Alliloyia! Alliloyia! Afisa to parelthon
(Alleluia! Alleluia! I let go of the past)
ki akoloythisa Afton! Alliloyia! Alliloyia!
(and I followed Him! Alleluia! Alleluia!)
Apofasisa na zo me to Christo!
(I decided to live with Christ!)
Verse 2:
Eimai tora pia pistos kai doxazo to Christo,
(I am now a believer and I glorify Christ,)
giati m' ekane yio! O, doxa sto Theo!
(because he made me a son! Oh, glory be to God!)
Kai to Pneyma tou fotos me mathainei diarkos
(And the Spirit of light teaches me constantly)
pos na prochoro pistos, tora psallo sto Christo!
(how to proceed faithfully, now I sing to Christ!)
Verse 3:
Me aggelous kai pistoys tha doxazo to Christo
(With angels and believers I will glorify Christ)
san vretho stous ouranoys!, O, doxa sto Theo!
(as if I were in the heavens!, Oh, glory to God!)
Perimeno ti stigmi pou tha efretho ekei
(I'm waiting for the moment to be there)
stin ourania zoi na doxazo to Christo!
(in the heavenly life to glorify Christ!)
