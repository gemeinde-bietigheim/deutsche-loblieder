116 VOIThEIA MOY S' AYTI TI ZOI

Greek Hymns - No. 116 | "ΒΟΗΘΕΙΑ ΜΟΥ Σ' ΑΥΤΗ ΤΗ ΖΩΗ"
Verse 1:
Voitheia mou s' afti ti zoi ein' o Dimiourgos,
(My help in this life is the Creator,)
Pateras tou sympantos kai Poiitis, o Pansofos Theos!
(Father of the universe and Poet, the All-Wise God!)
To onom' Aftoy ein' aspida pou svinei ta veli tou echthroy,
(His name is a shield that quenches the arrows of the enemy,)
propyrgio sti kataigida, o faros tou pistoy!
(bulwark in the storm, the beacon of the faithful!)
Verse 2:
San asotos gios epothoysa ki ego na noioso ti chara
(Like a prodigal son, I longed to feel the joy)
makria ap' tou Patros to zesto spitiko stou kosmou ti fthora!
(away from the Father the warm home in the world the decay!)
Rakendytos gyrisa piso sto spiti to patriko,
(Shaved, I returned to my father's house,)
sygchoresi gia na zitiso ap' ton Iisoy Christo!
(forgiveness to ask from Jesus Christ!)
Verse 3:
Me tremonta cheili kai yfos deilo Tou lego «Iisoy,
(With trembling lips and a timid tone I say to Him "Jesus,)
ego den axizo na m' echeis pia gio, kane me doylo Sou».
(I don't deserve you to have me as a son anymore, make me your slave".)
Ma Ekeinos katholou de stergei n' akoysei to ti tha Tou po,
(But He is not at all reluctant to listen to what I have to say to Him,)
ta cheria Tou anoigei kai legei «Paidi mou s' agapo!».
(He opens His hands and says "My child, I love you!".)
Verse 4:
Me sozei kai tora o Theios Amnos ap' tou echthroy tin orgi
(The Divine Lamb saves me even now from the wrath of the enemy)
kai t' Agio Tou Pneyma, o Paraklitos, ston ourano m' odigei!
(and His Holy Spirit, the Paraclete, leads me to heaven!)
Mou vazei kai ymno sta cheili na psallo gia t' onom' Aftoy
(He also puts a hymn on my lips to sing for His name)
kai logia agapis mou dinei gia tis psyches pou dipsoyn!
(and words of love he gives me for the thirsty souls!)
