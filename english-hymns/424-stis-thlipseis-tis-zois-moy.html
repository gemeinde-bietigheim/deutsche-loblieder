424 STIS ThLIPsEIS TIS ZOIS MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 424 | "ΣΤΙΣ ΘΛΙΨΕΙΣ ΤΗΣ ΖΩΗΣ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Stis thlipseis tis zois mou epikaloymai Afton
(In the sorrows of my life I call upon Him)
san agkyra psychis mou kai mono voitho,
(as an anchor of my soul and only helper,)
kai me diafylattei apo tou poniroy,
(and protects me from the wicked,)
i chari me skepazei tou Iisoy!
(the grace covers me of Jesus!)
Chorus:
Ego se limani agkyrovolisa,
(I anchored in a harbor,)
pote de fovoymai tou kosmou ta deina,
(I never fear the world's troubles,)
ego se limani agkyrovolisa,
(I anchored in a harbor,)
epano eis to vracho ton aionon!
(up on the rock of ages!)
Verse 2:
Kai i elpida einai san agkyra asfalis,
(And hope is like an anchor safe,)
epano eis tin petra, to Logo tis Zois,
(upon the stone, the Word of Life,)
otan koitazo gyro, de vlepo voitho
(when I look around, I don't see a helper)
kai tote atenizo eis to Christo!
(and then I look at Christ!)
Verse 3:
Einai pistos mou filos kai monos odigos,
(He is my faithful friend and only guide,)
pantote me fylaei kai m' odigei lampros,
(always guarding me and leading me brightly,)
kymata otan erthoun, Ekeinos me kratei,
(waves when they come, He holds me,)
einai o kyvernitis kai m' odigei!
(he is the captain and he leads me!)
