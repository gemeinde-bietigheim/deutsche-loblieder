655 TA MATIA SOY MIA MERA

Greek Hymns - No. 655 | "ΤΑ ΜΑΤΙΑ ΣΟΥ ΜΙΑ ΜΕΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Ta matia sou mia mera tha sfalisoune.
(Your eyes will close one day.)
Ta psemata pou zoyme tha t' afisoume.
(We will leave the lies we live.)
Tha prepei n' apantiseis stin agapi tou Theoy,
(You should respond to God's love,)
na doseis exigiseis mpros sto throno t' ouranoy!
(to give explanations before the throne of heaven!)
Chorus:
Petaxe sta vathi ola sou ta lathi,
(Throw away all your mistakes,)
thelei na se kanei eftychi,
(wants to make you happy)
An tou to zitiseis, tha to apoktiseis
(If you ask for it, you will get it)
sti stigmi!
(in the moment!)
Verse 2:
Mazi ki dyo listeyane, choris ntropi,
(Together they robbed, without shame,)
stafrothikan kai pethanan ki dyo mazi,
(they were crucified and both died together,)
o enas omos arpaxe to doro tou Theoy
(but one grabbed God's gift)
kai tora zei charoymenos sti chora t' ouranoy!
(and now he lives happily in the land of heaven!)
Verse 3:
Me misos kai ekdikisi gia to Christo,
(With hatred and vengeance for Christ,)
o Paylos porefotan pros ti Damasko,
(Paul was on his way to Damascus,)
tou milise o Kyrios zesta mes stin kardia
(the Lord spoke to him warmly in his heart)
ki o Paylos Ton agapise kai de chorisan pia!
(and Pavlos loved Him and they were no longer separated!)
Verse 4:
Klotsas, choris na xereis, pano se karfia
(You are kicking, without knowing, on nails)
kai paizeis me ta karvouna kai ti fotia,
(and you play with coals and fire,)
to aima Tou echythike gia sena sto stafro
(His blood was shed for you on the cross)
kai sy koitas adiaforos san na 'tan peritto!
(and you look indifferent as if it was unnecessary!)
