308 GLYKEIA ORA PROSEYChIS

Greek Hymns - No. 308 | "ΓΛΥΚΕΙΑ ΩΡΑ ΠΡΟΣΕΥΧΗΣ"
Verse 1:
Glykeia ora prosefchis, apo tis merimnes kaleis
(Sweet hour of prayer, from the cares you call)
na plisiaso tolmiros eos tou thronou tou Patros!
(to approach boldly to the Father's throne!)
Ekei niko ton peirasti kai apokto anapsychi
(There I defeat the tempter and obtain recreation)
se mia ourania epafi, glykeia ora prosefchis!
(in a heavenly touch, sweet hour of prayer!)
Verse 2:
Glykeia ora prosefchis, i tapeini mou deisi
(Sweet hour of prayer, my humble supplication)
pros to Theo as anevei, n' akoysei kai na m' eflogei!
(let him ascend to God, to hear and bless me!)
To prosopo Tou to septo, to eleos Aftoy zito,
(His holy face, His mercy I ask,)
echo galini stin psychi, glykeia ora prosefchis!
(I have peace in my soul, sweet hour of prayer!)
Verse 3:
Glykeia ora prosefchis, apo idees efteleis,
(Sweet hour of prayer, from base ideas,)
apo skopoys amartoloys kai apo peirasmoys polloys,
(from sinful purposes and from many temptations,)
apo apates vlaveres, apo frontides kosmikes,
(from harmful frauds, from worldly cares,)
sy eis ta ano mas kaleis, glykeia ora prosefchis!
(you call us above, sweet hour of prayer!)
Verse 4:
Glykeia ora prosefchis, eithe kathos o Moysis
(Sweet hour of prayer, may it be like Moses)
apo «Fasga» tin koryfi na doyme tin oraia gi!
(from "Fasga" the top to see the beautiful land!)
Kai tote otan anevo pros to Christo ston ourano,
(And then when I ascend to Christ in heaven,)
pleon de tha mou chreiastheis, glykeia ora prosefchis!
(I will no longer need you, sweet hour of prayer!)
