79 KALOS POIMENAS M' ODIGEI

Greek Hymns - No. 79 | "ΚΑΛΟΣ ΠΟΙΜΕΝΑΣ Μ' ΟΔΗΓΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Kalos Poimenas m' odigei,
(Good Shepherd leads me,)
pos toyto me parigorei,
(how this comforts me)
o,ti ki an kano pantachoy
(whatever i do everywhere)
einai to cheri tou Theoy!
(it is the hand of God!)
Chorus:
Me odigei, me odigei,
(He drives me, he drives me)
cheri Theoy me odigei!
(God's hand guides me!)
Aftos mou einai Archigos,
(He is my Chief,)
as eimai opados pistos!
(let me be a loyal fan!)
Verse 2:
Mes sti galini odigos
(Rest in peace guide)
kai mes sti thyella to fos
(and in the storm the light)
kai stin omichli panta Aftos
(and in the mist always He)
me katefthynei, o Christos!
(Christ directs me!)
Verse 3:
O Kyrie, Esy krateis
(O Lord, You hold)
to cheri mou kai m' odigeis
(my hand and you lead me)
mesa sto dromo tis zois,
(in the way of life,)
gia na 'mai panta asfalis!
(to always be safe!)
Verse 4:
Ki otan diavaino ta psychra
(And when I go through the cold)
tou Iordani reymata,
(jordan currents,)
noiotho galini kai ekei,
(I feel peace there too,)
afoy Esy me odigeis!
(since You lead me!)
