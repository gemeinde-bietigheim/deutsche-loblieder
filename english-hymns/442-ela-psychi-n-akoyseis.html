442 ELA, PsYChI, N' AKOYSEIS

Greek Hymns - No. 442 | "ΕΛΑ, ΨΥΧΗ, Ν' ΑΚΟΥΣΕΙΣ"
Verse 1:
Ela, psychi, n' akoyseis to theio Lytroti,
(Come, soul, to hear the divine Redeemer,)
ela, psychi, n' akoyseis Afton pou se kalei!
(come, soul, to listen to Him who calls you!)
Ela s' Afton, me pisti, ela, se karterei,
(Come to Him, in faith, come, He fastens you,)
me matomena cheria sou neyei, gia na 'rtheis!
(with bloody hands beckons you to come!)
Chorus:
O! Elthete pros Me pantes oi kopiontes,
(Oh! Come to With all the toilers,)
oloi oi fortomenoi se toyti ti zoi!
(all the burdened in this life!)
Fos, chara, eirini, anapafsi tha vreite,
(Light, joy, peace, rest you will find,)
Se Me elate oloi tis gis amartoloi!
(Come to Me, all sinners of the earth!)
Verse 2:
Psychi, min ameliseis na ertheis eis Afton,
(Soul, do not neglect to come to Him,)
se krazei o Kyrios sou epano ap' to stafro!
(your Lord is crying for you on the cross!)
Akouse ti foni Tou, amartoli psychi,
(Hear His voice, sinful soul,)
Aftos the na se kanei aionia eftychi!
(He will make you eternally happy!)
Verse 3:
O, pia min anavalleis na ertheis eis Afton!
(Oh, delay no longer to come to Him!)
Giati, psychi, na meneis makria ap' to Christo;
(Why, soul, stay away from Christ?)
Simera, ti foni Tou p' akoys na se kalei,
(Today, His voice you hear calling you,)
anoixe tin kardia sou sto theio Lytroti!
(open your heart to the redeemer!)
