49 OSANNA, OSANNA

Greek Hymns - No. 49 | "ΩΣΑΝΝΑ, ΩΣΑΝΝΑ"
Chorus:
Osanna, Osanna, alliloyia, doxa, doxa!
(Hosanna, Hosanna, hallelujah, glory, glory!)
Osanna, Osanna, alliloyia, doxa!
(Hosanna, Hosanna, hallelujah, glory!)
Verse 1:
Eis' Esy gia me Theos zontanos, alithinos,
(You are for me a living, true God,)
m' esoses, me lytroses, t' onoma Sou ymno!
(you saved me, you redeemed me, I praise your name!)
Verse 2:
Eis' Esy o Agios pou oi thalasses ymnoyn,
(You are the Holy One whom the seas praise,)
t' astra kai oi ouranoi Se doxologoyn!
(the stars and the heavens glorify You!)
Verse 3:
Ta vouna ki oi potamoi tremoune sto diava Sou,
(Mountains and rivers tremble at Your path,)
san polla ydata i foni Sou ichei!
(like many waters Thy voice resounds!)
