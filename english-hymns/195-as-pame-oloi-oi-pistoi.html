195 AS PAME OLOI OI PISTOI

Greek Hymns - No. 195 | "ΑΣ ΠΑΜΕ ΟΛΟΙ ΟΙ ΠΙΣΤΟΙ"
Verse 1:
As pame oloi oi pistoi pros to Sotira mas Christo,
(Let all the faithful go to our Savior Christ,)
gematoi pisti alithini na ergasthoym' Afton!
(full of true faith to work for Him!)
Chorus:
O! De tha salefto! Nai, de tha salefto!
(Oh! I will not shake! Yes, I will not shake!)
Ap' tin agapi tou Christoy, nai, de tha salefto!
(From the love of Christ, yes, I will not be shaken!)
Verse 2:
Akoyte oloi ti foni tou Lytroti tin kardion,
(Hear ye all the voice of the Redeemer of hearts,)
me pisti pia alithini as agapoym' Afton!
(with faith now true let's love Him!)
Verse 3:
Aftos panta mas omilei kai me to Logo Tou zitei
(He always speaks to us and asks with His Word)
na trexoume pou mas kalei dia ton ourano!
(let's run that calls us through the sky!)
Verse 4:
Tora yparchei o kairos tis energeias tou pistoy,
(Now is the time of the believer's energy,)
as prochorisoume empros pano sta ichni Tou!
(let's move forward in His footsteps!)
Verse 5:
Kathenas mas as enothei me to Christo stin prosefchi,
(Let each of us unite with Christ in prayer,)
stena m' Afton na syndethei kai tha 'nai eftychis!
(closely with Him to be connected and he will be happy!)
