512 TORA ARKOYMAI

Greek Hymns - No. 512 | "ΤΩΡΑ ΑΡΚΟΥΜΑΙ"
Verse 1:
Tora arkoymai sta liga pou echo,
(Now I make do with the little I have,)
sto ligo chrima, sti ligi trofi,
(in the little money, in the little food,)
giati gnorizo pos echo enan oiko
(because I know I have a house)
etoimasmeno kai aionia zoi!
(prepared and eternal life!)
Chorus:
Oikima echo sti gi tin oraia,
(I have a house in the beautiful earth,)
pou vasileyei aionia chara!
(who reigns eternal joy!)
Ekei tha paysei i lypi ki o ponos,
(There sorrow and pain will cease,)
tha chairo panta ekei ypsila!
(I will always rejoice there high!)
Verse 2:
Dokimasies kai thlipseis an echo,
(Trials and tribulations if I have,)
ponous, agona edo sti gi,
(pains, struggle here on earth,)
all' ekei pano th' allaxoune ola,
(but up there everything will change,)
tha niotho panta chara diarki!
(I will always feel lasting joy!)
Verse 3:
Edo eimai xenos, vadizo sto dromo
(Here I am a stranger, walking down the street)
me tin elpida mes stin kardia,
(with hope in the heart,)
potho na ftaso stin gi tin oraia
(I long to reach the beautiful land)
kai n' apolayso zoi ti glykia!
(and to enjoy sweet life!)
Verse 4:
O adelfe mou, prochora me pisti,
(O my brother, go forth in faith,)
koitaze panta eis to Christo!
(always look to Christ!)
Ilthe ston kosmo zoi na sou dosei,
(He came into the world to give you life,)
na sou charisei, nai, oiko lampro!
(to grant you, yes, a brilliant house!)
