509 TIN ELEYSI SOY, O ChRISTE

Greek Hymns - No. 509 | "ΤΗΝ ΕΛΕΥΣΗ ΣΟΥ, Ω ΧΡΙΣΤΕ"
Verse 1:
Tin elefsi Sou, o Christe,
(Thy coming, O Christ,)
prosmeno me elpida!
(I look forward to it!)
Potho pleon na zo gia Se,
(I long now to live for You,)
glykytate Sotira!
(sweet Saviour!)
Chorus:
Ela, Kyrie Iisoy,
(Come, Lord Jesus,)
nai, ela tacheos eis ti gi,
(yes, come quickly to earth,)
prosmenoume tin elefsi Sou,
(we await Your coming,)
makrothymo kai nostalgo
(I am longing and homesick)
gia ti stigmi ekeini!
(for that moment!)
O Iisoy, elthe! O! nai, elthe!
(O Jesus, come! Oh! yes, come on!)
Verse 2:
Pio agio kai dikaio
(More holy and righteous)
eithe, Christe, na m' eyreis,
(may Christ find me)
otan tha eltheis me mistho
(when you will come with a salary)
pistoys Sou na vraveyseis!
(Thy faithful ones to reward!)
Verse 3:
Mi genoito na steritho
(I was not born to be deprived)
apo to elaion Sou,
(from Your oil,)
svisti lampada na krato
(extinguished candle to hold)
eis ti synantisi Sou.
(to Your meeting.)
Verse 4:
To Pneyma tora mas kalei
(The Spirit now calls us)
eis to Christo na 'rthoyme,
(to Christ let us come,)
kai dorean mas chorigei
(and freely grants us)
nero zois na pioyme!
(water of life to drink!)
