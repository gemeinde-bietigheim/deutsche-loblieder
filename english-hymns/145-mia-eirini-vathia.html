145 MIA EIRINI VAThIA

Greek Hymns - No. 145 | "ΜΙΑ ΕΙΡΗΝΗ ΒΑΘΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Mia eirini vathia echo mes stin kardia,
(I have a peace deep in my heart,)
perissia, megali, plateia!
(too much, big, wide!)
O, kaneis den mporei na tin parei pote,
(Oh no one can ever take her)
aionios anikei se me!
(forever belongs to me!)
Chorus:
Einai o Christos mou, o Lytrotis,
(He is my Christ, the Redeemer,)
ein' o ouranos mou edo sti gi!
(is my heaven here on earth!)
Einai Aftos eirini mesa stin psychi.
(He is peace in the soul.)
O chara! dikos mou kathe stigmi!
(Oh joy! mine every moment!)
Verse 2:
Ein' o dromos stenos ma pote skoteinos,
(The road is narrow but never dark,)
efoson mou feggei o Christos!
(as long as Christ shines on me!)
Kai charadres vathies sti zoi an diavo,
(And ravines deep in life if I pass,)
to Christo tha 'cho gia odigo!
(Christ will be my guide!)
Verse 3:
Etsi panta mazi stin epigeia zoi
(So always together in earthly life)
vadizo me to Lytroti!
(I walk with the Redeemer!)
Tha vrethoyme mazi sta ourania ekei
(We will meet together in heaven there)
kapoia mera s' aionia zoi!
(someday in eternal life!)
