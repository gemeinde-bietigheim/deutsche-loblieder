417 SAN STRATIOTIS TOY ChRISTOY

Greek Hymns - No. 417 | "ΣΑΝ ΣΤΡΑΤΙΩΤΗΣ ΤΟΥ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΥ"
Verse 1:
San stratiotis tou Christoy ki akolouthos Aftoy
(As a soldier of Christ and His follower)
pantoy kai panta polemo ton aspondo echthro!
(everywhere and always I fight the spineless enemy!)
Chorus:
Kai otan teleiosei i machi i fovera,
(And when the battle or dread is over,)
tha lavo stefano, ti doxa, ti chara!
(I will receive a crown, what glory, what joy!)
Kai otan tha klitho, tha lavo stefano
(And when I am called, I will receive a crown)
eis tin endoxi Sion!
(to glorious Zion!)
Tha foro stefano, stefano zois lampro!
(I will wear a crown, a crown of life bright!)
Kai otan tha klitho, tha lavo stefano
(And when I am called, I will receive a crown)
eis tin endoxi Sion!
(to glorious Zion!)
Verse 2:
Pos perimeno n' arpachtho ekei stous ouranoys,
(How I expect to be raptured up there in the heavens,)
ap' to Christo na vraveftho mazi me tous pistoys!
(from Christ may I be rewarded together with the faithful!)
Verse 3:
Yparchoun, nai, polloi echthroi ston kosmo mas afto,
(There are, yes, many enemies in this world of ours,)
tha polemiso me chara mazi me to Christo!
(I will gladly fight with Christ!)
Verse 4:
Prepei ki ego synecheia edo na polemo,
(I too must always be here fighting,)
gia na vretho me tous pistoys ekei ston Ourano!
(to meet the faithful there in Heaven!)
