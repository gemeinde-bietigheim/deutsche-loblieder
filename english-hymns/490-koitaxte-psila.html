490 KOITAXTE PsILA

Greek Hymns - No. 490 | "ΚΟΙΤΑΞΤΕ ΨΗΛΑ"
Verse 1:
Koitaxte psila, erchetai o Christos!
(Look up, Christ is coming!)
Proskyniste Ton, latrepste Ton!
(Worship Him, worship Him!)
Psalte s' Afton me kathari kardia!
(Sing to Him with a pure heart!)
Oli i gi tha gonatisei mpros sto Vasilia!
(All the earth will bow to the King!)
Verse 2:
Koitaxte psila, erchetai o Christos!
(Look up, Christ is coming!)
Ta simeia martyroyn gi' Afton.
(The signs testify to Him.)
Tha katevei sto mesouranima,
(It will descend at midnight,)
gia na parei tous pistoys sto throno Tou psila!
(to take the faithful to His throne on high!)
Verse 3:
Koitaxte psila, erchetai o Christos!
(Look up, Christ is coming!)
Ton karterei kathe pistos.
(Every believer approves of him.)
Psalte ki eseis me kathari kardia!
(Sing with a pure heart too!)
Oli i gi tha gonatisei mpros sto Vasilia!
(All the earth will bow to the King!)
