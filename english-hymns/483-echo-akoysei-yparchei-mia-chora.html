483 EChO AKOYSEI YPARChEI MIA ChORA

Greek Hymns - No. 483 | "ΕΧΩ ΑΚΟΥΣΕΙ ΥΠΑΡΧΕΙ ΜΙΑ ΧΩΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Echo akoysei yparchei mia chora
(I have heard there is a country)
pio epano ap' ti sfaira tou kosmou,
(above the sphere of the world,)
Ouranos einai to onoma tis!
(Heaven is her name!)
O! Pos thelo ekei na vretho!
(Oh! How I want to be there!)
Alliloyia! pos chairet' i kardia mou!
(Alleluia! how my heart rejoices!)
Alliloyia! ti doxa gia mena!
(Alleluia! what glory to me!)
San vretho ekei pano m' aggelous
(Like I'm up there with angels)
kai m' agious aionia tha zo!
(and with saints I will live forever!)
Verse 2:
Echo akoysei stin endoxi chora
(I have heard in the glorious land)
den yparchei to dakry ki o ponos,
(there is no tear or pain,)
alla doxa, chara aionia
(but glory and joy forever)
stin patrida ekei t' ouranoy!
(to the homeland there in the sky!)
Alliloyia! Tha psallo m' agious!
(Alleluia! I will sing with saints!)
Alliloyia! Ki ego th' apolayso
(Alleluia! I will enjoy it too)
tin zoi tin aionia pou dinei
(the eternal life he gives)
o Sotiras mas, Iisoys Christos!
(our Savior, Jesus Christ!)
Verse 3:
Echo akoysei pos oloi tha einai
(I've heard they all will be)
me ourania stoli chrysafenia,
(in heavenly golden garb,)
tha vadizoun se endoxous dromous
(they will march in glorious ways)
kai tha chairontai oloi mazi!
(and everyone will be happy together!)
Alliloyia! Thelo tora na fygo!
(Alleluia! I want to leave now!)
Alliloyia! Ekei gia na ziso!
(Alleluia! There to live!)
O! Potho ki ego na eftychiso
(Oh! I want to be happy too)
st' ouranoy tin trisendoxi gi!
(in the sky the trisendous earth!)
