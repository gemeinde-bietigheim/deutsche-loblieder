529 OLI I ZOI MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 529 | "ΟΛΗ Η ΖΩΗ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Oli i zoi mou ki i anapnoi mou
(All my life and my breath)
einai dosmeni eis to Christo!
(she is given to Christ!)
Aftos gia mena ein' i chara mou,
(He is my joy to me,)
to oneiro mou pou agapo!
(my dream that I love!)
Aftos charizei tin eftychia
(He bestows happiness)
ki agapi theia mes stin kardia,
(and love deep in the heart,)
m' echei lytrosei, me echei sosei,
(he has redeemed me, he has saved me,)
eimai paidi Tou, o, ti chara!
(I am His child, oh, what joy!)
Verse 2:
O, ti oraia otan mia mera
(Oh, how nice when one day)
tha me kalesei ston ourano!
(he will call me to heaven!)
Mazi tha ziso, tha eftychiso,
(Together I will live, I will be happy,)
ekei konta Tou th' anapaftho!
(there near Him I will rest!)
Zoi aionia tha apolayso
(I will enjoy eternal life)
me to Christo mou ston ourano,
(with my Christ in heaven,)
ta dakrya mou tha ta stegnosei
(he will dry my tears)
kai me ta matia mou tha Ton do!
(and with my eyes I will see Him!)
Verse 3:
San i zoi mou afti tha svisei
(Like my life this will fade away)
kai eis ton tafo tha katevo,
(and to the grave I will go down,)
me allo soma tha m' anastisei
(in another body he will raise me up)
o Kyrios mou, de tha chatho!
(my Lord, I will not perish!)
Glykia elpida, eis tin patrida,
(Sweet hope, in the homeland,)
nai, tin ourania tha efretho!
(yes, I will find heaven!)
Echoume oloi ekei merida,
(We all have a share there,)
afoy pisteyoume sto Christo!
(since we believe in Christ!)
