174 PsYChI KAI SOMA MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 174 | "ΨΥΧΗ ΚΑΙ ΣΩΜΑ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Psychi kai soma mou, se Se,
(My soul and body, in Thee,)
me pisti dino, o Christe,
(in faith I give, O Christ,)
pista pantoy th' akoloutho, Sotira kai Thee!
(faithfully everywhere I will follow, Savior and God!)
Chorus:
Eim' eftychis, o Iisoy!
(I am happy, O Jesus!)
Tha zo gia Se, Amne Theoy!
(I will live for You, Amne God!)
Sy asfalos odigei me, Sotira kai Thee!
(You surely lead me, Savior and God!)
Verse 2:
Epi stafroy tou stygeroy
(On the Stygian cross)
anevikes, Amne Theoy,
(you have risen, Amne God,)
afierono eis Ese ta panta, o, Christe!
(I dedicate everything to You, O Christ!)
Verse 3:
Eis to stafro to soma Sou
(Your body on the cross)
paredoses Sy, Iisoy,
(You, Jesus, delivered)
dia na soseis kai eme, Sotira kai Thee!
(to save me too, Savior and God!)
Verse 4:
Ta dino ola eis Ese,
(I give it all to You,)
dexou tin prosfora, Christe,
(accept the offer, Christ,)
me Pneyma Agio Esy agiase psychi!
(with Holy Spirit You sanctify my soul!)
