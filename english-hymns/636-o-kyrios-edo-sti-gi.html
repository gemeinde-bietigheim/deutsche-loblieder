636 O KYRIOS EDO STI GI

Greek Hymns - No. 636 | "Ο ΚΥΡΙΟΣ ΕΔΩ ΣΤΗ ΓΗ"
Verse 1:
O Kyrios edo sti gi
(The Lord here on earth)
gi' agapi edidaske!
(he taught for love!)
Agkaliaze mikra paidia
(He was hugging small children)
kai toyta ta edeichne!
(and it showed this!)
Chorus:
Xero pos tha erthei o Christos sti gi,
(I know that Christ will come to earth,)
gia na mas prosferei afthoni zoi!
(to give us abundant life!)
Verse 2:
Oi lykoi kai ta provata
(The wolves and the sheep)
tha voskoune mazi,
(they will graze together)
mprosta tous tha ta odigei
(before them he will lead them)
ena mikro paidi!
(a small child!)
Verse 3:
Kai tote tha doxazoume
(And then we will glorify)
to onoma tou Christoy!
(the name of Christ!)
Tha psalloume gia to Christo
(We will sing for Christ)
pano stous ouranoys!
(above the heavens!)
