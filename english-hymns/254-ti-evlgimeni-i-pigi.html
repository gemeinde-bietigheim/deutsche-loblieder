254 Ti Evlogimeni I Pigi

Greek Hymns - No. 254 | "What a Blessed Fountain"
Verse 1:
Ti evlogimeni i pigi pou anevlyse sto stavro!
(What a blessed fountain that sprang forth on the cross!)
Ti evlogimeni i stigmi pou epestrepsa sto Christo!
(What a blessed moment when I returned to Christ!)
Imoun ap' ti poimni Tou makria ki eicha tin psychi mou rypari,
(I was far from His flock and my soul was defiled,)
m' eplynen o Amnos tou Theou ki echo tora kardia kathari!
(the Lamb of God washed me and now I have a pure heart!)
Chorus:
M' aima tou Christou, m' aima tou Amnou
(By the blood of Christ, by the blood of the Lamb)
eimai pio lefkos apo to chioni me to timio aima t' Amnou!
(I am whiter than snow by the precious blood of the Lamb!)
Verse 2:
To stefani to akanthino forese ekei sto Stavro!
(The crown of thorns He wore there on the Cross!)
Ti friktoi oi ponoi ton pligon pou gia me ypest' o Christos!
(How terrible the pains of the wounds that Christ suffered for me!)
Me to aima tou me eplynen, pio lefko ap' to chioni m' ekane!
(With His blood He washed me, made me whiter than snow!)
O, doxazo panta ton Amno pou elytrose kai eme!
(Oh, I always glorify the Lamb who redeemed me too!)
Verse 3:
Efyga apo Se, Patera mou, eplanihtika stin odo,
(I departed from You, my Father, I wandered on the path,)
erythres oi amarties mou, den ypirche to valsamo!
(my sins were red, there was no balm!)
Iltha sti pigi tis charitos, vrika Sotiria alithos,
(I came to the fountain of grace, I found Salvation truly,)
m' eplynen o Amnos tou Theou, gia na eimai panta katharos!
(the Lamb of God washed me, so that I may always be clean!)
