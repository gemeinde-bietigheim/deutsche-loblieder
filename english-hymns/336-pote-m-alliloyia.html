336 POTE M' «ALLILOYIA!»

Greek Hymns - No. 336 | "ΠΟΤΕ Μ' «ΑΛΛΗΛΟΥΙΑ!»"
Chorus:
Pote m' «Alliloyia!»,
(When with "Hallelujah!")
pote m' «Osanna!»,
(when "Hosanna!")
oi kardies mas smigoun
(our hearts melt)
sto Theo mprosta!
(before God!)
Verse 1:
Ypsoste tis kardies sas
(Lift up your hearts)
ki archiste to glyko tragoydi.
(and begin the sweet song.)
«Doxa ston Agio Lytroti!»
("Glory to the Holy Redeemer!")
as antichei pantoy.
(let it reverberate everywhere.)
Afiste ti chara sas
(Let go of your joy)
n' anthisei zontano louloydi
(to bloom a living flower)
ki aploste myro ki omorfia
(and spread myrrh and beauty)
tis charis tou Christoy!
(of the grace of Christ!)
Verse 2:
Gemiste me to Pneyma
(Be filled with the Spirit)
kai xecheiliste ap' tin agapi,
(and overflow with love,)
notes glykes mes stis kardies
(sweet notes in the hearts)
na peftoun san vrochi!
(to fall like rain!)
Fonaxte «Alliloyia!»
(Shout "Hallelujah!")
stou kosmou n' akoustei tin akri.
(for the world to hear the edge.)
«Glyke Sotira, s' agapo
("Sweet Savior, I love you)
m' oli mou tin psychi!»
(with all my soul!")
