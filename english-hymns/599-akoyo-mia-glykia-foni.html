599 AKOYO MIA GLYKIA FONI

Greek Hymns - No. 599 | "ΑΚΟΥΩ ΜΙΑ ΓΛΥΚΙΑ ΦΩΝΗ"
Verse 1:
Akoyo mia glykia foni:
(I hear a sweet voice:)
«S' agapo, se zito, se kalo!».
("I love you, I ask for you, I call you!")
Entos mou panta antichei:
(Inside me always echoes:)
«S' agapo, se zito, se kalo!».
("I love you, I ask for you, I call you!")
«Gia sena ponesa poly,
("I hurt a lot for you,)
gia sena ipia ti choli,
(for you i drank the gall)
stafrothika, gia na sotheis!
(I was crucified, that you might be saved!)
S' agapo, se zito, se kalo!».
(I love you, I ask for you, I call you!")
Verse 2:
«Oso ki an eis' amartolos,
("However sinful you are,)
s' agapo, se zito, se kalo!
(I love you, I ask you, I call you!)
Se dikaiono entelos,
(I completely justify you,)
s' agapo, se zito, se kalo!».
(I love you, I ask for you, I call you!")
«Mes sti zoi sou edo sti gi,
("Throughout your life here on earth,)
tha s' odigo kathe stigmi,
(I will lead you every moment)
ki aionia tha 'maste mazi!
(and we will be together forever!)
S' agapo, se zito, se kalo!»
(I love you, I ask you, I call you!")
Verse 3:
«Tis egnoies sou fere se Me,
("Bring your cares to Me,)
s' agapo, se zito, se kalo!
(I love you, I ask you, I call you!)
Ta vari sou sikono Ego,
(I bear your burdens,)
s' agapo, se zito, se kalo!».
(I love you, I ask for you, I call you!")
«Tous fovous sou dioxe makria,
("Drive your fears away,)
sto stithos mou gyre apala,
(on my chest it turned gently,)
tha ginei i lypi sou chara!
(your sorrow will become joy!)
S' agapo, se zito se kalo!».
(I love you, I'm asking you, I'm calling you!")
