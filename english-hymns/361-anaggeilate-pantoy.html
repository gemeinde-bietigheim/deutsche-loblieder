361 ANAGGEILATE PANTOY

Greek Hymns - No. 361 | "ΑΝΑΓΓΕΙΛΑΤΕ ΠΑΝΤΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Anaggeilate pantoy, tekna eseis tou Theoy,
(Announce everywhere, you children of God,)
efaggelia charas, o Christos sozei efthys!
(gospel of joy, Christ saves immediately!)
Deyte mathiteysate, pasa ktisin kai fylin,
(See ye disciples, of all castes and races,)
pantachoy kiryxate o Christos sozei efthys!
(everywhere you preached Christ saves directly!)
Verse 2:
Alitheias aggeloi speysate, speysate,
(Angels of truth hurry, hurry,)
sotirias salpigga s' olous ichisate!
(sound the trumpet of salvation to all!)
Peran ton okeanon anaggeilate efthys,
(Across the oceans you announced straight away,)
eis ta perata tis gis o Christos sozei efthys!
(to the ends of the earth Christ saves immediately!)
Verse 3:
Neo asma psallate, o pistoi tou Christoy,
(Sing a new song, O believers of Christ,)
martyreite di' Afton pantachoy, pantachoy!
(witness for Him everywhere, everywhere!)
Me agapi kai chara, afesi kiryxate,
(With love and joy, forgiveness you preached,)
pantachoy salpisate, o Christos sozei efthys!
(everywhere you blew the trumpet, Christ saves immediately!)
Verse 4:
Sotiria teleia chorigei o Christos,
(Christ grants perfect salvation,)
s' olous tous amartoloys dorean alithos!
(to all sinners free indeed!)
Kathe anthropo kalei kai oudena exairei,
(Every man calls and none excepted,)
Sozei olous entelos, o Christos sozei efthys!
(He saves all completely, Christ saves directly!)
