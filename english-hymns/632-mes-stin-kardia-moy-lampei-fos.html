632 MES STIN KARDIA MOY LAMPEI FOS

Greek Hymns - No. 632 | "ΜΕΣ ΣΤΗΝ ΚΑΡΔΙΑ ΜΟΥ ΛΑΜΠΕΙ ΦΩΣ"
Verse 1:
Mes stin kardia mou lampei fos
(A light shines in my heart)
pou mou edos' o Christos
(which Christ gave me)
kai perpato olos chara,
(and I walk happily,)
giati m' Esose Aftos!
(because He saved me!)
Chorus:
Nai, to fos pou lampei stin kardia mou
(Yes, the light that shines in my heart)
kai skorpizei gyro efodia,
(and spreads fragrance around,)
mou to 'dose o Kyrios
(the Lord gave it to me)
kai me gemise chara!
(and it filled me with joy!)
Verse 2:
Tyflos mes sto skotadi ego
(Blind in the dark me)
zoysa kapote skyftos,
(i used to live bent over)
ospou mia mera m' epsaxe
(until one day he looked for me)
kai me vrike o Christos!
(and Christ found me!)
Verse 3:
Kai sy an potheis mes sti zoi
(And you, if you are passionate about life)
na gnoriseis ti chara,
(to know joy,)
trexe kai ela sto Christo
(run and come to Christ)
pou m' agapi se zita!
(who asks for you with love!)
