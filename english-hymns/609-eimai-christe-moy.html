609 EIMAI, ChRISTE MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 609 | "ΕΙΜΑΙ, ΧΡΙΣΤΕ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Eimai, Christe mou, i agapi Sou,
(I am, my Christ, Your love,)
eimai to kerdos tis thysias Sou!
(I am the gain of Your sacrifice!)
Eimai kommati ap' to soma Sou,
(I am part of Your body,)
eimai plymenos me to aima Sou!
(I am washed in Your blood!)
Eimai kommati ap' to soma Sou,
(I am part of Your body,)

Verse 2:
Eisai o vrachos o aionios,
(You are the rock eternal,)
o Vasilias mou o ouranios!
(my King the heavenly!)
To froyrio mou, i katafygi,
(My fortress, my refuge,)
Sy 'sai, Christe mou, Theie lytroti!
(You are, my Christ, my redeemer!)
To froyrio mou, i katafygi,
(My fortress, my refuge,)
Sy 'sai, Christe mou, Theie lytroti!
(You are, my Christ, my redeemer!)
Verse 3:
Choris Esena adeia i zoi,
(Life is empty without you,)
adeio trapezi dichos trofi!
(empty table without food!)
Eisai to manna ap' ton ourano!
(You are manna from heaven!)
Eisai o vrachos, t' athanato nero!
(You are the rock, the immortal water!)
Eisai to manna ap' ton ourano!

Verse 4:
Mprosta Sou einai oli mou i zoi.
(Before You is my whole life.)
Dos' mou, Christe mou, Theia dynami!
(Give me, my Christ, divine strength!)
Ta dyo mou cheili otan tha ymnoyn,
(My two lips when they sing,)
Se na doxazoun, na Se eflogoyn!
(Let them glorify You, bless You!)
