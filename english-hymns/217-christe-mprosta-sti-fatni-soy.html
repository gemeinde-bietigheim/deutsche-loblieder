217 ChRISTE, MPROSTA STI FATNI SOY

Greek Hymns - No. 217 | "ΧΡΙΣΤΕ, ΜΠΡΟΣΤΑ ΣΤΗ ΦΑΤΝΗ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Christe, mprosta sti fatni Sou ki ego to gony klino,
(Christ, before Your manger I also bow my knee,)
prosferontas ta dora mou latreia apefthyno!
(offering my gifts worship I address!)
O, dexou soma kai psychi, kardia mou, pneyma kai zoi,
(Oh, receive body and soul, my heart, spirit and life,)
Sotira, i zoi mou!
(Savior, my life!)
Verse 2:
Sy ap' archis me kaleses na gino ego dikos Sou,
(You called me from the beginning to be Yours,)
ap' to skotadi m' eferes sto zoogono fos Sou!
(from the darkness you brought me to Your life-giving light!)
Me esose to aima Sou, m' efylaxe i chari Sou,
(Thy blood saved me, Thy grace preserved me,)
Sotira tis psychis mou!
(Savior of my soul!)
Verse 3:
Pigi elpidas kai fotos, o ilios tou kosmou,
(Source of hope and light, the sun of the world,)
to nou mou ela fotise, gennoy, Christe, to fos mou!
(come enlighten my mind, give birth, Christ, my light!)
Tin pisti mou enischyse, ta podia mou odigise,
(My faith strengthened, my feet led,)
Sotira, Ilie mou!
(Savior, my Sun!)
