283 TON PARAKLITO SOY STEILE

Greek Hymns - No. 283 | "ΤΟΝ ΠΑΡΑΚΛΗΤΟ ΣΟΥ ΣΤΕΙΛΕ"
Verse 1:
Ton Paraklito Sou steile,
(Send Your Advocate,)
eno deomai, Thee,
(while I pray, God,)
na didaxei theio Logo
(to teach divine Word)
kai na m' odigei pros Se!
(and lead me to You!)
Sto skotadi eplaniomoun
(I was wandering in the dark)
makria Sou eleeinos,
(away from You merciful,)
den ergastika katholou,
(I didn't work at all)
Sy odigei me sto fos!
(You lead me to the light!)
Verse 2:
Fos gia mena anatellei,
(Light for me shines,)
me eisakouses, Thee,
(you heard me, God,)
i chara mou plimmyrizei,
(my joy overflows,)
de tha ntropiastho pote!
(I will never be ashamed!)
I psychi mou th' anaggellei
(My soul will announce)
ergo Sou to thafmasto,
(your wonderful work,)
o kathenas na pistepsei
(everyone to believe)
eis to Lytroti Christo!
(to Christ the Redeemer!)
Verse 3:
O Sotira, Se zitoyne
(O Saviour, they seek You)
oi psyches pollon thniton,
(the souls of many mortals,)
rantise tes na sothoyne
(sprinkle them to save)
me to aima Sou t' agno!
(with Your pure blood!)
Oloi einai dipsasmenoi,
(Everyone is thirsty)
tous akoyo na zitoyn,
(I hear them ask,)
stin kardia tous gia na lavoun
(in their hearts to receive)
to Sotira Iisoy!
(the Savior Jesus!)
