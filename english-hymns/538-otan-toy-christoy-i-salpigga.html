538 OTAN TOY ChRISTOY I SALPIGGA

Greek Hymns - No. 538 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΤΟΥ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΥ Η ΣΑΛΠΙΓΓΑ"
Verse 1:
Otan tou Christoy i salpigga ichisei i tromeri
(When Christ's trumpet sounds the terrible)
kai imera th' anateilei i lampri,
(and day will dawn the bright one,)
otan oi pistoi th' arpazontai psila ston ourano,
(when believers will be caught up to heaven,)
tha 'mai tote kai ego me to Christo!
(I will then be with Christ too!)
Chorus:
Nai, ekei potho na eimai!
(Yes, that's where I want to be!)
Nai, ekei potho na eimai!
(Yes, that's where I want to be!)
Nai, ekei potho na eimai!
(Yes, that's where I want to be!)
Aionios chairon kai doxologon!
(Forever happy and blessed!)
Verse 2:
Me agious kai aggelous kai me salpiggas foni
(With saints and angels and with a trumpet voice)
o Sotiras tha fanei san astrapi!
(the Savior will appear like lightning!)
Tote, otan tha thrinisoun oles oi fyles tis gis,
(Then, when all the tribes of the earth shall mourn,)
mipos arage thriniso kai ego;
(shall I mourn too?)
Verse 3:
Osoi einai tou Kyriou tote tha anastithoyn
(Those who are the Lord's will then be resurrected)
kai mazi Tou stis nefeles th' arpachthoyn!
(and with Him in the clouds they will be caught up!)
Otan to Vivlio, tote, tis Zois tha anoichthei,
(When the Book, then, of Life shall be opened,)
ekei pano to onoma mou th' akousthei!
(up there my name will be heard!)
Verse 4:
To Christo ton prao tha akoloutho mes sti zoi,
(The meek Christ I will follow in life,)
tha milo gi' agapi Tou ti thafmasti!
(I will speak for His love the admirer!)
Otan de ton dromo mou tha teleioso eis ti gi,
(And when I finish my way on earth,)
sto Christo tha eimai tote asfalis!
(in Christ I shall then be safe!)
