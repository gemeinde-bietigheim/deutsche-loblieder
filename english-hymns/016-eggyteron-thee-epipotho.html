16 EGGYTERON, ThEE, EPIPOThO

Greek Hymns - No. 16 | "ΕΓΓΥΤΕΡΟΝ, ΘΕΕ, ΕΠΙΠΟΘΩ"
Verse 1:
Eggyteron, Thee, epipotho
(Nearer, God, I desire)
eggyteron pros Se na ypsotho!
(nearer to Thee may I rise!)
Esto kai epi stafroy thanatou stygeroy,
(Even on a stiff death cross,)
arkei na efretho eggys eis Se!
(as long as I am close to You!)
Verse 2:
An skotos zoferon en ti odo
(If darkness bleak on the road)
m' eyrei planomenon, pali potho
(He finds me wandering, I lust again)
to pneyma mou pros Se na ypsothei, Thee,
(let my spirit to Thee rise, O God,)
eggys na efretho, eggys pros Se!
(close to me, close to You!)
Verse 3:
Kathos o Israil, eithe ki ego
(As Israel, so may I)
oikodomon Vaithil na S' eflogo!
(building Bethel to bless You!)
Eithe pros Se eggys diagon epi gis
(May you be close to the devil on earth)
na se doxologo eggys eis Se!
(to glorify you close to You!)
Verse 4:
Ean apokamon kataklitho,
(If I'm overwhelmed by burnout,)
ton dromon mou dramon an koimitho,
(my way of drama if i sleep)
potho na mou fanei klimax aggeliki,
(I want my scale to seem angelic,)
di' is na ypsotho eggys eis Se!
(for me to rise close to You!)
