297 PARE OLI TI ZOI

Greek Hymns - No. 297 | "ΠΑΡΕ ΟΛΗ ΤΗ ΖΩΗ"
Verse 1:
Pare oli ti zoi, Sou afieron' afti,
(Take all of life, I dedicate it to you,)
Iisoy, kathe stigmi tha ymno ton Poiiti!
(Jesus, every moment I will praise the Poet!)
Verse 2:
Par' ta cheria mou, Christe, na kinoyntai apo Se
(Take my hands, Christ, to be moved by You)
kai ta podia mou sto fos, Se n' akolouthoyn pistos!
(and my feet in the light, They follow You faithfully!)
Verse 3:
Pare mou kai ti foni, gia na se doxologei,
(Take my voice too, so that it may glorify you,)
par' ta cheili mou, Christe, na omologoyn Ese!
(take my lips, Christ, to confess You!)
Verse 4:
Pare argyro, chryso, o,ti echo pothito,
(Take silver, gold, all that I desire,)
par' to nou mou, fotise, sti zoi odigise!
(for my mind, light up, lead to life!)
Verse 5:
Pare mou ti thelisi, Sou parachoro afti,
(Take my will, I give it to you,)
pare tin kardia mou na 'nai panta thronos Sou!
(take my heart that it may always be Your throne!)
Verse 6:
Pare tin agapi mou kai olous tous pothous mou,
(Take my love and all my desires,)
pare me olotelos na 'mai pantote pistos!
(take me wholly that I may always be faithful!)
