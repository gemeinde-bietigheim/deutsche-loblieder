629 LYTROMENOI TOY ChRISTOY

Greek Hymns - No. 629 | "ΛΥΤΡΩΜΕΝΟΙ ΤΟΥ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Lytromenoi tou Christoy, psallete mazi mas
(Redeemed of Christ, sing with us)
to tragoydi t' Ouranoy kai tis lefterias!
(the song of Heaven and freedom!)
I chara tis lytrosis pallei ti foni mas,
(The joy of redemption pulsates our voice,)
i glykia agapi Tou aggixe kai mas!
(His sweet love touched us too!)
I chara tis lytrosis pallei ti foni mas,
(The joy of redemption pulsates our voice,)
i glykia agapi Tou aggixe kai mas!
(His sweet love touched us too!)
Verse 2:
Lytromenoi tou Christoy, psallete mazi mas.
(Redeemed of Christ, sing with us.)
S' ena dromo omorfo mpros mas odigei,
(On a beautiful road ahead he leads us,)
tis zois o Archigos, o kalos Poimenas,
(of life the Chief, the good Shepherd,)
se livadia chloera, droseri pigi!
(in grassy meadows, cool spring!)
tis zois o Archigos, o kalos Poimenas,
(of life the Chief, the good Shepherd,)
se livadia chloera, droseri pigi!
(in grassy meadows, cool spring!)
Verse 3:
Lytromenoi tou Christoy, psallete mazi mas.
(Redeemed of Christ, sing with us.)
T' akrivo kamari mas ein' o Golgothas!
(Our precious pride is Calvary!)
Lafyro tou Iisoy einai i zoi mas,
(Jesus' booty is our life,)
doro mas i nea gi tis klironomias!
(gift to us the new land of heritage!)
Lafyro tou Iisoy einai i zoi mas,
(Jesus' booty is our life,)
doro mas i nea gi tis klironomias!
(gift to us the new land of heritage!)
Verse 4:
Lytromenoi tou Christoy, psallete mazi mas
(Redeemed of Christ, sing with us)
thoyria ston Nikiti pou erchetai gia mas!
(cheers to the Winner who is coming for us!)
Doxa stou Theoy to Gio, to thriamvefti mas,
(Glory be to God the Son, our triumphant,)
pou me aima sto Stafro lytrose ki emas!
(who with his blood on the Cross redeemed us too!)
Doxa stou Theoy to Gio, to thriamvefti mas,
(Glory be to God the Son, our triumphant,)
pou me aima sto Stafro lytrose ki emas!
(who with his blood on the Cross redeemed us too!)
