227 AP' TO AIMA TOY STAYROY SOY

Greek Hymns - No. 227 | "ΑΠ' ΤΟ ΑΙΜΑ ΤΟΥ ΣΤΑΥΡΟΥ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Ap' to aima tou Stafroy Sou,
(From the blood of Your Cross,)
o Christe mou, xekinoyn
(oh my christ, they're starting)
kymata theias agapis
(waves of divine love)
pou to sympan sygklonoyn!
(that shake the universe!)
Chorus:
O! plymenos me to aima
(Oh! washed with blood)
plisiazo to Theo!
(I'm getting closer to God!)
Kai tin apeiri Tou agapi
(And His infinite love)
tapeina efcharisto!
(humble thanks!)
Verse 2:
Tetoia agapi den mporoysa,
(Such love I could not,)
o! pote na fantastho,
(oh! never imagine)
na pethanei karfomenos
(to die nailed)
kai gia mena o Christos!
(and Christ for me!)
Verse 3:
Nai, karfothike sto xylo,
(Yes, nailed to the wood,)
gia na lytrotho ego
(that I may be redeemed)
ki apo tou stafroy to dromo
(and from the cross the way)
n' anevo ston ourano.
(to ascend to the sky)
Verse 4:
O Thee mou, se doxazo,
(O God, I praise you,)
pou mou edoses Sy fos
(that you gave me light)
na gnoriso to mystirio
(to know the mystery)
tis thysias sto stafro!
(of the sacrifice on the cross!)
