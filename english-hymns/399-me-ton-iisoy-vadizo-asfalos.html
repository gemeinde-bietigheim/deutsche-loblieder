399 ME TON IISOY VADIZO ASFALOS

Greek Hymns - No. 399 | "ΜΕ ΤΟΝ ΙΗΣΟΥ ΒΑΔΙΖΩ ΑΣΦΑΛΩΣ"
Verse 1:
Me ton Iisoy vadizo asfalos,
(With Jesus I walk surely,)
opou m' odigei Poimenas o kalos,
(where the good Shepherd leads me,)
terpseis feygoun kai chares choris Christo,
(pleasures are gone and joys without Christ,)
me Afton vadizo pros ton Ourano!
(with Him I march towards Heaven!)
Chorus:
Sto Christo Iisoy fovos pouthena!
(In Christ Jesus fear nowhere!)
Sto Christo asfaleia, pantoy chara!
(In Christ safety, joy everywhere!)
Verse 2:
Me ton Iisoy panta eim' asfalis,
(With Jesus I am always safe,)
ein' Ekeinos o prostatis tis zois.
(He is the protector of life.)
An vadizo di' akanthodon odon,
(If I walk through thorny paths,)
metavallei taytas eis Theoy naon!
(turn this into a temple of God!)
Verse 3:
Me ton Iisoy vadizo asfalos,
(With Jesus I walk surely,)
sto skotadi mesa Aftos einai to fos.
(in the darkness within He is the light.)
Pantote aino Afton me tin psychi,
(I always praise Him with my soul,)
sto Christo pantote eimai eftychis!
(in Christ I am always happy!)
Verse 4:
O Christos gemizei oli ti zoi
(Christ fills all life)
kai to kentro tou thanatou den ptoei.
(and the center of death does not flinch.)
Tora o tafos ein' i porta tis zois,
(Now the grave is the door of life,)
sto Christo ki o thanatos einai glykys!
(in Christ even death is sweet!)
