433 O VRAChE TON AIONON

Greek Hymns - No. 433 | "Ω ΒΡΑΧΕ ΤΩΝ ΑΙΩΝΩΝ"
Verse 1:
O vrache ton aionon, s' Esena tha kryftho,
(O rock of ages, in You I will hide,)
Sy moni mou elpida, eis Se tha stirichtho!
(You are my only hope, I will rely on You!)
Chorus:
O vrache ton aionon, eis Ese tha stirichtho!
(O rock of ages, I will lean on You!)
Esto kai kymat' an ktypoyn,
(Even if waves beat,)
arkei na efretho konta Sou panta, Iisoy mou,
(it is enough to be near You always, my Jesus,)
dos' mou o,ti potho, se oli ti zoi afti
(give me all that I desire, in all this life)
mesa Sou tha kryftho!
(I will hide in You!)
Verse 2:
Vrache, voithise me na Se akoloutho,
(Lord, help me to follow You,)
sto reyma tis zois mou eis Se tha stirichtho!
(in the current of my life in You I will lean!)
Verse 3:
Sy katafygio mou, Sy filos mou pistos,
(You are my refuge, You are my faithful friend,)
se oles mou tis thlipseis eis Se tha stirichtho!
(in all my sorrows in You I will rely!)
