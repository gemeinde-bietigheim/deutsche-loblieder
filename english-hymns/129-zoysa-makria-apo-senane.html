129 ZOYSA MAKRIA APO SENANE

Greek Hymns - No. 129 | "ΖΟΥΣΑ ΜΑΚΡΙΑ ΑΠΟ ΣΕΝΑΝΕ"
Verse 1:
Zoysa makria apo Senane,
(I lived far from Senane,)
makria ap' tin agapi Sou,
(away from your love)
makria ap' ti chara Sou!
(away from Your joy!)
Monos, xenos kai paroikos
(Alone, a stranger and a pariah)
se gi afiloxeni
(in an inhospitable land)
zitoysa patrida!
(I was asking for a homeland!)
Ma san irthes Esy,
(But when you came,)
irthe fos sti zoi,
(light came to life,)
irthe agapi, chara kai eirini!
(came love, joy and peace!)
Tora psallo se Se!
(Now I sing to You!)
S' agapo, Kyrie!
(I love you, Lord!)
S' agapo, Iisoy, Kyrie mou!
(I love you, Jesus, my Lord!)
Verse 2:
Eida to fos Sou to lampero,
(I saw Your light shining,)
i alitheia Sou elampse
(Your truth shone forth)
sti skoteinia mou!
(in my darkness!)
Me pires apo to thanato
(You took me from death)
ki esvises monomias
(and you disappeared all at once)
ta sfalmata mou!
(my mistakes!)
Tora trecho ki ego, panta S' akoloutho
(Now I run too, I always follow You)
me ourania klisi sto dromo!
(with heavenly calling on the way!)
Ein' makrys kai stenos,
(It's long and narrow,)
dyskolos kai omorfos!
(difficult and beautiful!)
Eisai Sy, Iisoy, odigos mou!
(You, Jesus, are my guide!)
