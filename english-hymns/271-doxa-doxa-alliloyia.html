271 DOXA, DOXA! ALLILOYIA!

Greek Hymns - No. 271 | "ΔΟΞΑ, ΔΟΞΑ! ΑΛΛΗΛΟΥΙΑ!"
Verse 1:
Doxa, doxa! Alliloyia! Psallei i kardia kai tragouda!
(Glory, glory! Alleluia! The heart sings and sings!)
O Christos mas esose, ti zoi mas allaxe
(Christ saved us, changed our lives)
kai to Pneyma Tou Theoy tora kyverna!
(and the Spirit of God now rules!)
O Christos mas esose, ti zoi mas allaxe

(and the Spirit of God now rules!)
Verse 2:
Ela tora, ela, Kyrie, kathe mera Sy na odigeis!
(Come now, come, Lord, every day You lead!)
Kratise mas katharoys, gine Sy o archigos
(Keep us clean, be the leader)
kai to Pneyma Sou, Christe, na mas kyverna!
(and let Your Spirit, Christ, govern us!)
Kratise mas katharoys, gine Sy o archigos
(Keep us clean, be the leader)
kai to Pneyma Sou, Christe, na mas kyverna!

Verse 3:
Dose tora mia Pentikosti sto lao Sou, gia na plirothei!
(Now give a Pentecost to Your people, to be paid!)
Dose mas charismata, kane nea thaymata,
(Give us gifts, do new miracles,)
i ekklisia Sou, Christe, na etoimastei!
(let Your church, Christ, be prepared!)
Dose mas charismata, kane nea thaymata,

Verse 4:
Alliloyia, doxa, Osanna! Erchetai o Kyrios xana!
(Hallelujah, glory, Hosanna! The Lord is coming again!)
Oi nekroi th' anastithoyn ki oi pistoi tha arpachthoyn
(The dead will rise and the faithful will be raptured)
kai sti doxa tou Theoy oloi tha vrethoyn!
(and in the glory of God all will be found!)
