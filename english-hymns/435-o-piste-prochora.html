435 O PISTE, PROChORA

Greek Hymns - No. 435 | "Ω ΠΙΣΤΕ, ΠΡΟΧΩΡΑ"
Verse 1:
O piste, prochora kai mi stamatas,
(O believer, go on and do not stop,)
tis zois o dromos einai Golgothas!
(of life the road is Calvary!)
Oso ki an se thlivei tora to kako,
(As much as evil now grieves thee,)
se prosmenei doxa 'kei, ston ourano!
(glory awaits you here in heaven!)
Chorus:
O, agallou, odoipore,
(Oh, dear, wayfarer,)
sto taxidi sou afto,
(on this trip of yours,)
echeis syntrofo ton prao,
(you have a meek companion,)
to glykytato Christo!
(the sweetest Christ!)
Verse 2:
Oso ki an i nychta einai skoteini,
(However dark the night may be,)
stin kardia sou mesa den mporei na mpei!
(it cannot enter your heart!)
Ki an san odoiporos tora kourasteis,
(And if as a hiker now you get tired,)
kapote gia panta tha anapaftheis!
(once and for all you will rest!)
Verse 3:
Echei akomi dromo gia ton ourano,
(He still has a way to heaven,)
dyskolo, thlimmeno kai poly steno!
(difficult, sad and very narrow!)
Ma kai tou Patera i asosti pigi
(But also the Father's inexhaustible source)
echei na zitiseis chari pio polli!
(you have to ask for more favors!)
Verse 4:
Erchet' o Sotiras ap' ton ourano
(The Savior from heaven is coming)
na se paralavei etoimo, agno!
(to receive you ready, pure!)
Eche panta tharros tora sti zoi,
(Always have courage now in life,)
opoios tha nikisei, pantote tha zei!
(whoever wins will always live!)
