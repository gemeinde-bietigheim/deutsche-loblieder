653 STO ONOMA TOY IISOY

Greek Hymns - No. 653 | "ΣΤΟ ΟΝΟΜΑ ΤΟΥ ΙΗΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Sto onoma tou Iisoy,
(In the name of Jesus,)
iltha ston agio throno Sou.
(I came to Your holy throne.)
Stekomai edo, sygchoresi zito.
(I'm standing here, I'm asking for forgiveness.)
Chorus:
Sto stafro gia mena
(On the cross for me)
ekane ergo thafmasto!
(he did a wonderful job!)
Pliros' o Christos me aima
(Christ paid with blood)
ki anoixe ton ourano!
(and open the sky!)
Verse 2:
Thelo tin eflogia Sou,
(I want Your blessing,)
ouranie Patera mou.
(my heavenly Father.)
Tora potho to Pneyma t' Agio!
(Now I crave the Holy Spirit!)
Verse 3:
Ti dynami Sou dose mou,
(Give me Your strength,)
na prato ego to logo Sou.
(let me do Your word.)
Mesa ston agro thelo na ergasto!
(I want to work in the field!)
Verse 4:
S' efcharisto, Patera mou,
(Thank you, my Father,)
gia ti megali agapi Sou!
(for Your great love!)
Krata me Esy mechri tin arpagi!
(Hold with You until the rapture!)
