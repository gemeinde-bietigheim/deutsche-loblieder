4 DOSTE DOXA

Greek Hymns - No. 4 | "ΔΩΣΤΕ ΔΟΞΑ"
Verse 1:
Doste doxa eis to megalo Theo mas
(Give glory to our great God)
kai Sotira gia tin agapi Tou!
(and Savior for His love!)
Archaggeloi, stepsat' Afton me ti doxa,
(Archangels, crown Him with glory,)
ypsosate t' onoma tou Christoy!
(you exalted the name of Christ!)
San poimenas, fylakas tis psychis mas,
(As a shepherd, guardian of our souls,)
mas sikonei eis tin agkali Tou!
(lifts us up in His arms!)
Chorus:
Ypsosate to apeiro Megaleio!
(You exalted the infinite Greatness!)
Doste doxa ston Ypsisto Theo!
(Give glory to the Most High God!)
Verse 2:
Doste doxa eis ton elthonta Sotira
(Give glory to the coming Savior)
kai pathonta epi skliroy stafroy!
(and suffered on a hard cross!)
Ein' elpida kai vrachos eis ton aiona,
(It is hope and a rock forever,)
doxasmeno to onoma Aftoy!
(glorified be His name!)
Kathe noso kai amartia pairnei.
(Every disease and sin takes.)
Ti agapi kai thafmasti storgi!
(What love and wonderful affection!)
Verse 3:
Doste doxa ston Iisoy mas ton zonta,
(Give glory to our living Jesus,)
tora mesiteyei stous ouranoys!
(now interceding in the heavens!)
S' Afton anikei oli i exousia
(To Him belongs all power)
kai tha vasileysei me tous pistoys!
(and will reign with the faithful!)
O thronos Tou menei eis ton aiona,
(His throne endures forever,)
Tha Ton doyme, erchet` o Lytrotis!
(We will see Him, the Redeemer is coming!)
