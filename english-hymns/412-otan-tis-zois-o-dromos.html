412 OTAN TIS ZOIS O DROMOS

Greek Hymns - No. 412 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΤΗΣ ΖΩΗΣ Ο ΔΡΟΜΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Otan tis zois o dromos fainetai adiavatos,
(When the road of life seems impassable,)
mi deilias, den eisai monos, syntrofos ein' o Christos!
(do not be afraid, you are not alone, Christ is your companion!)
Chorus:
Syntrofos pistos gia se ein' o Christos,
(Christ is your faithful companion,)
stis zois to dromo, sti chara, ston pono,
(in life the way, in joy, in pain,)
dose Tou to cheri ki Ekeinos tha se ferei
(give Him your hand and He will bring you)
eis to thafmasto Tou fos!
(in His wondrous light!)
Verse 2:
Otan gyro skoteiniazei o dikos sou ouranos,
(When your own sky darkens around,)
tharros kai mi se tromazei, syntrofos ein' o Christos!
(courage and don't be afraid, your partner is Christ!)
Verse 3:
Otan eis tin ochthi fthaseis, odigo tha 'cheis Afton
(When you reach the shore, you will have Him as your guide)
kai antikry tha peraseis kai tha zeis me to Christo!
(and instead you will pass and live with Christ!)
