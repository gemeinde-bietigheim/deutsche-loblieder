455 POSO GLYKA O ChRISTOS SE FONAZEI

Greek Hymns - No. 455 | "ΠΟΣΟ ΓΛΥΚΑ Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ ΣΕ ΦΩΝΑΖΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Poso glyka o Christos se fonazei,
(How sweetly Christ calls you,)
file, ese proskalei!
(dude, he's inviting you!)
Epithymei stin psychi sou na dosei
(Desires in your soul to give)
chara ki aionia zoi!
(joy and eternal life!)
Chorus:
Elthe, elthe, anthrope amartole!
(Come, come, sinful man!)
Poso glyka o Christos se fonazei!
(How sweetly Christ calls you!)
Elthe, o file, elthe!
(Come, oh friend, come!)
Verse 2:
Ti perimeneis ki argeis na proseltheis
(What are you waiting for and you are late to come?)
s' Afton pou se proskalei;
(to Him who invites you?)
Pistefse tora kai min amfivalleis,
(Believe now and doubt not,)
Aftos tha se eleei!
(He will have mercy on you!)
Verse 3:
Trechoun ta chronia kai o kairos feygei,
(Years go by and time goes away,)
ela s' Afton na sotheis!
(come to Him to be saved!)
Erchet' o thanatos, to telos fthanei,
(Death comes, the end comes,)
tote pou sy tha kryftheis;
(then where will you hide?)
Verse 4:
Skepsou ti theia agapi pou echei
(Think of the divine love he has)
o Iisoys kai gia se,
(Jesus and for you,)
chari kai afesi afthona dinei,
(grace and forgiveness abundantly gives,)
apethane kai gia se!
(died for you too!)
