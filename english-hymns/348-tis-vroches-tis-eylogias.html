348 TIS VROChES TIS EYLOGIAS

Greek Hymns - No. 348 | "ΤΙΣ ΒΡΟΧΕΣ ΤΗΣ ΕΥΛΟΓΙΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Tis vroches tis eflogias Sy charizeis, o Thee,
(The showers of blessing You bestow, O God,)
me chara tis sotirias Sy eflogise ki eme.
(with the joy of salvation, bless me too.)
Kai eme kai eme, Sy eflogise ki eme!
(And me and me, You blessed me too!)
Verse 2:
Kyrie, mi m' egkataleipseis, an kai esfala se Se,
(Lord, do not forsake me, though I have sinned against You,)
splachnika mi me afiseis, Sy eleise ki eme.
(Mercifully, don't leave me, have mercy on me too.)
Kai eme kai eme, Sy eleise ki eme!
(And me and me, You have mercy on me too!)
Verse 3:
Iisoy, min paravlepseis, eleimona Giatre,
(Jesus, do not overlook, merciful Physician,)
Sy mporeis na therapeyseis, Sy therapefse ki eme.
(You can heal, You healed me too.)
Kai eme kai eme, Sy therapefse ki eme!
(And me and me, You healed me too!)
Verse 4:
Se elypisa, Christe mou, kai to Pneyma Sou, Thee,
(I pitied Thee, my Christ, and Thy Spirit, O God,)
ma psyches Sy katharizeis, Sy katharise ki eme.
(But You cleanse souls, You cleansed me too.)
Kai eme kai eme, Sy katharise ki eme!
(And me and me, You cleanse me too!)
Verse 5:
Katafygio, elpida eisai, Amome Amne,
(Refuge, hope you are, Amome Amne,)
mone tis psychis Sotira, sose tora kai eme.
(only Savior of the soul, save me now too.)
Kai eme kai eme, sose tora ki eme!
(And me and me, save me now too!)
