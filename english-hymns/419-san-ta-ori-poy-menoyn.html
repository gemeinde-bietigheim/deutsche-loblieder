419 SAN TA ORI POY MENOYN

Greek Hymns - No. 419 | "ΣΑΝ ΤΑ ΟΡΗ ΠΟΥ ΜΕΝΟΥΝ"
Verse 1:
San ta ori pou menoun asalefta,
(Like the mountains that remain unscathed,)
i psychi mas ston Kyrio menei!
(our soul rests in the Lord!)
I agapi Tou einai asalefti,
(His love is unfathomable,)
i zoi mas konta Tou demeni!
(our life close to Him tied!)
Chorus:
Etsi eimaste emeis asaleftoi,
(That's how we are unscathed,)
laos tou Theoy, lytromenoi!
(people of God, redeemed!)
Etsi eimaste emeis asaleftoi,
(That's how we are unscathed,)
laos tou Theoy, lytromenoi!
(people of God, redeemed!)
Verse 2:
Einai vrachos kai menei asaleftos
(It is a rock and remains unsalted)
kai emeis pano s' Afton stirigmenoi!
(and we rest on Him!)
Einai Kyrios, vrachos asaleftos,
(He is Lord, rock unsalted,)
kai emeis chari s' Afton lytromenoi!
(and we, thanks to Him, redeemed!)
Verse 3:
Oi yposcheseis Tou menoun asaleftes
(His promises are unbreakable)
ki oi alitheies Tou zoun sti zoi mas!
(and His truths live in our lives!)
Einai Kyrios, vrachos asaleftos,
(He is Lord, rock unsalted,)
mas gemizei me fos ti psychi mas!
(fills our soul with light!)
Chorus:
Ki emeis menoume panta asaleftoi,
(And we always remain unsullied,)
laos tou Theoy, lytromenoi!
(people of God, redeemed!)
Ki emeis menoume panta asaleftoi,
(And we always remain unsullied,)
laos tou Theoy, lytromenoi!
(people of God, redeemed!)
