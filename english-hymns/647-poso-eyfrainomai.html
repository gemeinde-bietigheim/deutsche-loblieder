647 POSO EYFRAINOMAI

Greek Hymns - No. 647 | "ΠΟΣΟ ΕΥΦΡΑΙΝΟΜΑΙ"
Verse 1:
Poso effrainomai, Patera Ouranie,
(How I rejoice, Heavenly Father,)
mesa st' Agia ton Agion Sou na mpaino,
(into Your Holy of Holies to enter,)
ap' to aima t' agio tou Iisoy Christoy,
(from the holy blood of Jesus Christ,)
to aionio prosopo Sou na latreyo!
(Thy eternal face to adore!)
Chorus:
Psall' alliloyia! Psall' alliloyia!
(Sing hallelujah! Sing hallelujah!)
Psall' Alliloyia! Psall' alliloyia!
(Sing Hallelujah! Sing hallelujah!)
Verse 2:
I agapi Sou, Thee, monadiki,
(Your love, O God, unique,)
ein' i chari Sou asteirefti gia mena!
(Your grace is inexhaustible for me!)
Oi lofoi, ta vouna tha metakinithoyn,
(The hills, the mountains will move,)
to eleos Sou omos tha meinei ston aiona!
(but Your mercy will last forever!)
