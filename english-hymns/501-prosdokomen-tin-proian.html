501 PROSDOKOMEN TIN PROIAN

Greek Hymns - No. 501 | "ΠΡΟΣΔΟΚΩΜΕΝ ΤΗΝ ΠΡΩΙΑΝ"
Verse 1:
Prosdokomen tin proian,
(We look forward to the morning,)
O! yperlampros afgi,
(Oh! brilliant dawn,)
otan tis nyktos ta skoti
(when the night kills them)
thelousi dialythei!
(they will be disbanded!)
Chorus:
Prosdokoyme tin proia,
(We look forward to the morning,)
pothiti kai makaria,
(lust and bliss,)
tou Kyriou parousia,
(in the Lord's presence,)
tou endoxou Lytrotoy!
(of the glorious Redeemer!)
Verse 2:
Agrypnoyntes prosdokoyme
(Awake we expect)
to Sotira mas Christo,
(our Savior Christ,)
epi nefelon na elthei
(on clouds to come)
di' analipsin imon!
(for our ascension!)
Verse 3:
O, ti doxa! Prosdokoyme
(Oh, what glory! We expect)
Lytroti mas, nikiti,
(Our redeemer, conqueror,)
myriadon tin elpida
(myriads of hope)
fos, alitheia, zoi!
(light, truth, life!)
