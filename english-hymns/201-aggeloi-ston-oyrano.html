201 AGGELOI STON OYRANO

Greek Hymns - No. 201 | "ΑΓΓΕΛΟΙ ΣΤΟΝ ΟΥΡΑΝΟ"
Verse 1:
Aggeloi ston ourano psalloun: «Doxa sto Theo,
(Angels in heaven sing: "Glory to God,)
efdokia epi gis kai eirini tis psychis!».
(prosperity on earth and peace of the soul!".)
Alalaxate laoi, doste doxa kai timi!
(Change peoples, give glory and honor!)
Egennithike o Sotir eis tin poli tou David,
(Sotir was born in the city of David,)
egennithike o Christos, tou Ypsistou o Yios!
(Christ was born, the Son of the Most High!)
Verse 2:
O aionios Theos irthe vrefos tapeino
(The eternal God came as a humble infant)
di' Agiou Pneymatos ek parthenou gynaikos!
(by the Holy Spirit from a virgin woman!)
Itan tou Theoy Yios, tou Ypsistou Krataios,
(He was the Son of God, the Mighty One of the Most High,)
san ptochos pano sti gi fanike o Lytrotis,
(like a poor man on earth the Redeemer appeared,)
egennithike o Christos, tou Ypsistou o Yios!
(Christ was born, the Son of the Most High!)
Verse 3:
Kathe glossa kai fyli to Sotira exymnei!
(Every tongue and race the Savior glorifies!)
Me to sperma gynaikos epatachthike o echthros!
(The enemy was attacked with the sperm of a woman!)
Tin adamiki poini afairei o Lytrotis,
(Adam's punishment is removed by the Redeemer,)
«Deyteros Adam» Aftos, nikiforos kai pistos,
("Second Adam" He, victorious and faithful,)
pali erchetai sti gi krataios thriamveftis!
(again he comes to earth mighty triumphant!)
