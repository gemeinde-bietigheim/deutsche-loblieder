249 Stin Agapi Mu Epese

Greek Hymns - No. 249 | "Upon My Love Fell"
Verse 1:
Stin agapi mou epese i pagonia
(Upon my love fell the frost)
kai sta matia mou i skoteinia.
(and in my eyes the darkness.)
San gonatisa sto stavromeno mprosta,
(As I knelt before the crucified,)
agio fos elampse stin kardia!
(holy light shone in my heart!)
Chorus 1:
Sto Stavro, sto Stavro m' ena vlemma glyko
(At the Cross, at the Cross with a sweet gaze)
ki i kardia mou plimmyrise fos, agio fos!
(and my heart flooded with light, holy light!)
San gonatisa sto Stavromeno mprosta,
(As I knelt before the Crucified,)
agio fos elampse stin kardia!
(holy light shone in my heart!)
Verse 2:
O, ti vlemma glyko mbainei mes stin psychi,
(Oh, what sweet gaze enters the soul,)
echei elpida, agapi, storgi!
(it has hope, love, affection!)
Zontanevei nekrous, dinei fos stous tyflous,
(It revives the dead, gives light to the blind,)
anastainei kardia kai psychi!
(resurrects heart and soul!)
Chorus 2:
Sto Stavro sto Stavro m' ena vlemma glyko
(At the Cross at the Cross with a sweet gaze)
ki i kardia mou plimmyrise fos, agio fos!
(and my heart flooded with light, holy light!)
O! ti vlemma glyko mbainei mes stin psychi,
(Oh! what sweet gaze enters the soul,)
m' anastainei mou dinei zoi!
(it resurrects me, gives me life!)
Verse 3:
De tha papso pote na elpizo se Se,
(I will never stop hoping in You,)
o Christe mou, aionie Thee!
(oh my Christ, eternal God!)
Ki an pote kourasto ap' ta vari tis gis,
(And if I ever tire from the burdens of earth,)
Iisou, min argiseis na 'rtheis!
(Jesus, don't delay to come!)
