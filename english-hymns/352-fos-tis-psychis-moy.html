352 FOS TIS PsYChIS MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 352 | "ΦΩΣ ΤΗΣ ΨΥΧΗΣ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Fos tis psychis mou, o Christe,
(Light of my soul, O Christ,)
Sotira mou agapite,
(my dear savior,)
den einai nykta sti zoi,
(there is no night in life,)
otan konta mou eisai Sy!
(when you are near me!)
Verse 2:
Oli ti mera, Kyrie,
(All day, Lord,)
na 'sai mazi mou pantote,
(be with me always)
giati choris Esena ego
(because without You I)
den imporo oyte na zo!
(I can't even live!)
Verse 3:
Mes sto skotadi tis nyktos
(Into the darkness of the night)
na 'sai frouros mou ischyros,
(be my strong guard,)
giati choris esena ego
(because without you me)
kai na pethano den mporo!
(and I can't die!)
Verse 4:
Dia na ziso eftychis
(To live happily)
prepei esy na m' eflogeis,
(you must bless me)
mechri na 'rtho plision sou
(until I come near you)
eis tis mones tou Ouranoy!
(in the monasteries of Heaven!)
