645 PILINO SKEYOS

Greek Hymns - No. 645 | "ΠΗΛΙΝΟ ΣΚΕΥΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Pilino skeyos, ostrakino skeyos, eimai ego.
(Earthen vessel, shell vessel, it is I.)
Mesa mou omos echo ena thayma, echo ena thisafro!
(But inside me I have a miracle, I have a treasure!)
Verse 2:
Echo Esena, Patera!
(I have You, Father!)
Echo Esena, Christe mou!
(I have You, my Christ!)
Echo Esena, Agio Pneyma!
(I have You, Holy Spirit!)
Echo Esena, Patera!
(I have You, Father!)
Echo Esena, Christe mou!
(I have You, my Christ!)
Echo Esena, Thee mou!
(I have You, God!)
Verse 3:
Ti foni Sou akoyo, Esena niotho na mou milas!
(I hear Your voice, I feel You talking to me!)
Niotho na me koitazeis, niotho na m' agkaliazeis...
(I feel you looking at me, I feel you hugging me...)
ki eisai toso konta!
(and you are so close!)
Verse 4:
Plai mou eisai, Patera!
(You are by my side, Father!)
Plai mou eisai, Christe mou!
(You are by my side, my Christ!)
Plai mou eisai, Agio Pneyma!
(You are by my side, Holy Spirit!)
Mesa mou eisai, Patera!
(You are in me, Father!)
Mesa mou eisai, Christe mou!
(You are in me, my Christ!)
Mesa mou eisai, Thee mou!
(You are inside me, my God!)
Verse 5:
S' agapao, Patera!
(I love you, Father!)
S' agapao, Christe mou!
(I love you, my Christ!)
S' agapao, Agio Pneyma!
(I love you, Holy Spirit!)
S' agapao, Patera!
(I love you, Father!)
S' agapao, Christe mou!
(I love you, my Christ!)
S' agapao, Thee mou!
(I love you, my God!)
