588 TI FYSI SOY KOITAZO

Greek Hymns - No. 588 | "ΤΗ ΦΥΣΗ ΣΟΥ ΚΟΙΤΑΖΩ"
Verse 1:
Ti fysi Sou koitazo,
(Your nature I look,)
aporo, thafmazo ti sofia Sou tin trani!
(I wonder, I admire Your wisdom!)
Eisai Theos megalos!
(You are a great God!)
Den yparchei allos, etsi na megalourgei!
(There is no other, so let him grow!)
Ta erga Sou ta thafmasta mou miloyn gi' afti
(Thy wondrous works speak of her)
ti chari pou agathopoiei me dynami!
(the grace that benefices with power!)
Oi afges charazoun,
(The dawns break,)
t' astra antafgazoun ki ola psalloun me mia foni:
(the stars shine and all sing with one voice:)
Doxa, alliloyia, doxa, alliloyia, alliloyia, Amin!
(Glory, hallelujah, glory, hallelujah, hallelujah, Amen!)
O! alliloyia! O! alliloyia! O! alliloyia!
(Oh! Alleluia! Oh! Alleluia! Oh! Alleluia!)
O! alliloyia! O! alliloyia! O! alliloyia!
(Oh! Alleluia! Oh! Alleluia! Oh! Alleluia!)
O! alliloyia! Alliloyia!
(Oh! Alleluia! Alleluia!)
Verse 2:
Sto Golgotha koitazo,
(On Calvary I look,)
aporo, thafmazo ti sofia Sou tin trani!
(I wonder, I admire Your wisdom!)
Pos i dikaiosyni
(How is justice?)
ki i glykia eirini synantithikan ekei!
(and sweet peace met there!)
To ergo Sou to thafmasto mou mila gi' afti
(Your wonderful work speaks of her)
ti chari p' agathopoiei, dinei zoi!
(the grace that makes good, gives life!)
Sto Christo strammenoi,
(Facing Christ,)
me aima lytromenoi, oloi psalloun me mia foni:
(with blood redeemed, all sing with one voice:)
Doxa, alliloyia, doxa, alliloyia, alliloyia, Amin!
(Glory, hallelujah, glory, hallelujah, hallelujah, Amen!)
O! alliloyia! O! alliloyia! O! alliloyia!
(Oh! Alleluia! Oh! Alleluia! Oh! Alleluia!)
O! alliloyia! O! alliloyia! O! alliloyia!
(Oh! Alleluia! Oh! Alleluia! Oh! Alleluia!)
O! alliloyia! Alliloyia!
(Oh! Alleluia! Alleluia!)
