321 KAPOIA MERA

Greek Hymns - No. 321 | "ΚΑΠΟΙΑ ΜΕΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Kapoia mera o Theos esteile
(One day God sent)
Ton Yio Tou na pethanei gia me.
(His Son to die for me.)
O! ti megalo doro itan afto!
(Oh! what a great gift that was!)
Pio polytimo apo kathe thisafro!
(More precious than any treasure!)
Verse 2:
Patera mou ouranie, tin prosefchi mou stelno,
(My heavenly father, I send my prayer,)
ela kai lytrose me, Christe mou, se prosmeno!
(come and redeem me, my Christ, I await you!)
Sygchorese me, Kyrie, amartisa s' Esena.
(Forgive me, Lord, I have sinned against You.)
Pos ezisa makria Sou choris tin parousia Sou!
(How did I live far from You without Your presence!)
Verse 3:
Mou charises ta dora t' ouranoy,
(You gave me the gifts of heaven,)
mou 'doses aionia zoi,
(you gave me eternal life,)
ma pos mporesa na S' arnitho,
(but how could I refuse You,)
tin plati mou na strepso sto Stafro!
(to turn my back to the Cross!)
Verse 4:
Patera mou ouranie, tin prosefchi mou stelno,
(My heavenly father, I send my prayer,)
ela kai lytrose me, Christe mou, se prosmeno!
(come and redeem me, my Christ, I await you!)
Sygchorese me, Kyrie, amartisa s' Esena.
(Forgive me, Lord, I have sinned against You.)
Pos ezisa makria Sou choris tin parousia Sou!
(How did I live far from You without Your presence!)
Verse 5:
Esy, Kyrie me perimenes
(You, Lord, waited for me)
tosa chronia na epistrepso se Se!
(so many years to return to You!)
Ti zesti Sou agkalia anoixes,
(You opened Your warm embrace,)
ti zoi mou pali gemises!
(you filled my life again!)
Verse 6:
S' efcharisto Patera sto throno Sou pigaino,
(Thank You Father to Your throne I go,)
proseychomai s' Esena ki anoigei i kardia mou!
(I pray to You and my heart opens!)
O,ti Sou 'cho zitisei, Esy mou to 'cheis dosei,
(All that I have asked of Thee, Thou hast given me,)
aionia efgnomosyni tha echo mesa mou, Christe!
(I will have eternal gratitude in me, Christ!)
