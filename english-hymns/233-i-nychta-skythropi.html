233 I NYChTA SKYThROPI

Greek Hymns - No. 233 | "Η ΝΥΧΤΑ ΣΚΥΘΡΩΠΗ"
Verse 1:
I nychta skythropi, m' oligous opadoys
(The night is gloomy, with few followers)
pros ti Gethsimani paei o Iisoys,
(to Gethsemane Jesus goes,)
kai tin psychi Aftoy xifos diaperna,
(and his soul his sword pierces,)
kosmou tin enochi ferei ki agonia...
(world brings guilt and anguish...)
Verse 2:
Se toyto to frikto agona prin na mpei,
(In this horrible struggle before he entered,)
pairnei sta cheria Tou ton arto ki eflogei:
(takes the bread in His hands and blesses:)
«Lavete, fagete, einai to soma mou,
("Take, eat, it is my body,)
toyto na kamete chari sti mnimi mou».
(do this as a favor to my memory".)
Verse 3:
Dinei stous mathites ap' to potiri Tou:
(He gives to the disciples from His cup:)
«Einai to aima mou gia sas pou tha chythei
(“It is my blood for you that will be shed)
tis Diathikis mou tis pros esas Kainis»,
(of my New Testament to you,")
toyto einai Theoy simeio tis zois!
(this is God's sign of life!)
Verse 4:
Sotira Iisoy, omoios kai emeis,
(Savior Jesus, so are we,)
apo ton ourano mechri na xanartheis,
(from heaven until you come again,)
toyti tin teleti fylame oloi emeis,
(we all keep this ceremony,)
gnorizontas kala oti aionia zeis!
(knowing full well that you live forever!)
