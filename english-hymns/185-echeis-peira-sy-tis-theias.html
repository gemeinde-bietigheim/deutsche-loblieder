185 EChEIS PEIRA SY TIS ThEIAS

Greek Hymns - No. 185 | "ΕΧΕΙΣ ΠΕΙΡΑ ΣΥ ΤΗΣ ΘΕΙΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Echeis peira sy tis theias, o piste, charas,
(You have experience of the divine, O faithful one of joy,)
nai, tis endoxis, ouranias kai tis katharas;
(yea, of the glorious, heavenly, and pure?)
Chorus:
Chaire, giati eisai mathitis Christoy!
(Rejoice, for you are a disciple of Christ!)
Chaire, giati t' onoma sou einai sti Vivlo tou ouranoy!
(Rejoice, for your name is in the Bible of heaven!)
Chaire, giati t' onoma sou einai sti Vivlo tou ouranoy!
(Rejoice, for your name is in the Bible of heaven!)
Verse 2:
Na mi chairesai sta ployti oyte stis times,
(Do not rejoice in riches or honors,)
isos meta liga chronia steritheis aftes.
(maybe after a few years you will be deprived of them.)
Verse 3:
Na mi chaireis, giati ygeia echeis tora esy,
(Don't be happy, because you have health now,)
tosoi se stigmes oliges echasan aftin!
(so many in moments few missed her!)
Verse 4:
Mi zileyeis tous epainous tous timitikoys,
(Do not envy the honorable praises,)
chaire, pou tha vasileyeis eis tous ouranoys!
(Rejoice, who will reign in the heavens!)
Verse 5:
An sy pascheis, mi lypasai, tharros, o piste,
(If you suffer, do not be sad, courage, O believer,)
kai an ola ta stereisai, ano elpize!
(and if you lack everything, hope for more!)
