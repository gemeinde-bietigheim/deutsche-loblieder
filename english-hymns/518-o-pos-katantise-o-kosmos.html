518 O! POS KATANTISE O KOSMOS

Greek Hymns - No. 518 | "Ω! ΠΩΣ ΚΑΤΑΝΤΗΣΕ Ο ΚΟΣΜΟΣ"
Verse 1:
O! pos katantise o kosmos pano sti gi
(Oh! how the world came to be on earth)
choris Christo matomeni psychi!
(without Christ bloody soul!)
Ki ego pou vlepo me pono krazo pros Se!
(And I, who see with pain, cry out to You!)
Ela, Kyrie Iisoy Christe!
(Come, Lord Jesus Christ!)
Chorus:
Iisoy, Iisoy mou, ela, min argeis!
(Jesus, my Jesus, come, don't be late!)
Iisoy, Iisoy mou, grigora na 'rtheis.
(Jesus, my Jesus, come quickly.)
Verse 2:
Makria ap' to fos Sou, Christe, den yparchei zoi
(Far from Your light, Christ, there is no life)
skotadia zonoun t' anthropou tin psychi,
(darkness lives man's soul,)
Ki ego pou vlepo me pono krazo pros Se!
(And I, who see with pain, cry out to You!)
Ela, Kyrie Iisoy Christe!
(Come, Lord Jesus Christ!)
Verse 3:
Erimoi dromoi, nekroi perpatane sti gi,
(Deserted streets, the dead walk the earth,)
choris Christo, matomeni psychi!
(without Christ, bloody soul!)
Ki ego pou vlepo me pono krazo pros Se!
(And I, who see with pain, cry out to You!)
Ela, Kyrie Iisoy Christe!
(Come, Lord Jesus Christ!)
