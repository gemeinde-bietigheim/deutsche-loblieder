450 MIN AMELEIS, EINAI KAIROS

Greek Hymns - No. 450 | "ΜΗΝ ΑΜΕΛΕΙΣ, ΕΙΝΑΙ ΚΑΙΡΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Min ameleis, einai kairos na ertheis tora eis to fos,
(Do not neglect, it is time for you to come into the light now,)
amartole, gia na sotheis! O! Speyse efthys!
(sinner, to be saved! Oh! Hurry now!)
Chorus:
O! Speyse efthys! O! Speyse efthys!
(Oh! Hurry now! Oh! Hurry now!)
Nai, tha sotheis, an speyseis efthys!
(Yes, you will be saved if you hurry straight away!)
Verse 2:
Poios gnorizei an tha deis to fos tis allis charafgis;
(Who knows if you will see the light of another dawn?)
Tora ein' ora na sotheis! O! Speyse efthys!
(Now it's time to save yourself! Oh! Hurry now!)
Verse 3:
Olo ton kosmo ki an chareis kai tin psychi zimiotheis,
(The whole world and if you rejoice and the soul is damaged,)
panta tha eisai dystychis! O! Speyse efthys!
(you will always be unhappy! Oh! Hurry now!)
Verse 4:
O Iisoys se proskalei, gia na se kanei eftychi!
(Jesus invites you to make you happy!)
O! Akouse theia foni kai speyse efthys!
(Oh! Hear a divine voice and hurry at once!)
Verse 5:
Den aporriptei o Christos proserchomenous tapeinos.
(Christ does not reject those who come humbly.)
Nai, pistefse kai tha sotheis, o, speyse efthys!
(Yea, believe and thou shalt be saved, oh, make haste at once!)
