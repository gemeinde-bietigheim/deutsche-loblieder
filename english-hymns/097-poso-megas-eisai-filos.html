97 POSO MEGAS EISAI FILOS

Greek Hymns - No. 97 | "ΠΟΣΟ ΜΕΓΑΣ ΕΙΣΑΙ ΦΙΛΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Poso megas eisai filos,
(How great you are friend,)
latrefte mas Iisoy!
(adore us Jesus!)
Posi chari kai agapi
(How much grace and love)
dineis eis ton vio mou!
(you give my life!)
Posi tis psychis galini
(How much peace of soul)
mas prosfereis diarkos
(you offer us constantly)
kai me Pneyma mas plironeis,
(and with Spirit you pay us,)
gia na feroume karpo.
(to bear fruit.)
Verse 2:
Mas afinoune oi filoi,
(Friends leave us,)
katafeygoume se Se,
(we take refuge in You,)
otan peirasmoi poikiloi,
(when temptations varied,)
otan thlipseis perisses,
(when you grieve too much,)
otan lypes apeiloyne,
(when sorrows threaten,)
katafeygoume se Se,
(we take refuge in You,)
kai chares otan tha 'rthoyne
(and joys when they come)
pali speydoume pros Se.
(again we hasten to Se.)
Verse 3:
Otan vari eis Esena
(When burdens to You)
fernoume stin prosefchi,
(we bring to prayer,)
tote eis to prosopo Sou
(then in Your face)
charis aktinovolei!
(grace shines!)
Oles mas tis amarties,
(All our sins,)
exaleifeis Sy, Christe,
(you eliminate, Christ,)
oles mas tis anomies
(all our iniquities)
emeis fernoume se Se.
(we bring to Se.)
