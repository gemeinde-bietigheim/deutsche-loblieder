587 STRATIOTES TOY MEGALOY VASILIA

Greek Hymns - No. 587 | "ΣΤΡΑΤΙΩΤΕΣ ΤΟΥ ΜΕΓΑΛΟΥ ΒΑΣΙΛΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Stratiotes tou megalou Vasilia,
(Soldiers of the great King,)
prochoroyme panta empros!
(we are always moving forward!)
Me simaia tou ti Vivlo polema
(With his banner the Bible he fights)
tou Kyriou o stratos!
(the Lord's army!)
Chorus:
Oloi empros! Oloi empros!
(Everyone forward! Everyone forward!)
Ap' ola pano o Christos!
(Above all, Christ!)
Osoi tha sothoyn, as vrethoyn
(Those who will be saved, let them be found)
stou Christoy ti stratia!
(to Christ the army!)
Verse 2:
To tholo potami tis sarkos pantoy
(The murky river of flesh everywhere)
mes ston kosmo plimmyra!
(into the world flood!)
Ma ta niata de fovoyntai tou Christoy,
(But the youth are not afraid of Christ,)
vriskontai sto Golgotha!
(they are at Calvary!)
Verse 3:
O agonas ein' epimonos, skliros
(The fight is persistent, hard)
ki o echthros ein' ischyros!
(and the enemy is strong!)
Ma ti niki mas yposchetai lampri
(But he promises us a brilliant victory)
na mas steilei o Theos!
(may God send us!)
Verse 4:
Stratiotes oloi, martyres pistoi,
(Soldiers all, faithful martyrs,)
dialaloyme to Christo!
(we proclaim Christ!)
M' aichmalotous ap' tis echthrikes grammes
(With prisoners from the enemy lines)
pame gia ton ourano!
(let's go to heaven!)
