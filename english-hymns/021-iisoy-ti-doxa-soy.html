21 IISOY, TI DOXA SOY

Greek Hymns - No. 21 | "ΙΗΣΟΥ, ΤΗ ΔΟΞΑ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Iisoy, ti doxa Sou tha psalloume,
(Jesus, Thy glory we will sing,)
tha poyme s' olous giati stafrothikes.
(we will tell everyone why you were crucified.)
To chreos pliroses me to aima Sou
(You paid the debt with Your blood)
kai pali piges psila stous ouranoys!
(again you went high in the heavens!)
Chorus:
Pote den epapses gia me na mesiteyeis,
(You never stopped interceding for me,)
poso, alitheia, m' agapas!
(how, indeed, you love me!)
Pote den epapses gia me na mesiteyeis,
(You never stopped interceding for me,)
poso, alitheia, m' agapas!
(how, indeed, you love me!)
Verse 2:
Iisoy, ti doxa Sou tha psalloume,
(Jesus, Thy glory we will sing,)
giati mas gennises epano sto stafro.
(because you gave birth to us on the cross.)
Poly i agapi Sou mas elkyse,
(Much did Thy love draw us,)
gi' afto i kardia mas zei stous ouranoys!
(therefore our hearts live in the heavens!)
Verse 3:
Iisoy, ti doxa sou tha psalloume,
(Jesus, your glory we will sing,)
kai se prosmenoume me doxa na faneis
(and we await you with glory to appear)
na paralaveis tin ekklisia Sou
(to receive Your church)
kai na vrethoyme mazi stous ouranoys!
(and let's meet together in heaven!)
