613 ELA, MIN ARGEIS

Greek Hymns - No. 613 | "ΕΛΑ, ΜΗΝ ΑΡΓΕΙΣ"
Chorus:
Ela, min argeis sto Christo na ertheis!
(Come, don't be late to Christ to come!)
Opou ki an vretheis, opou kai na pas
(Wherever you are, wherever you go)
na to thymitheis pos se perimenei,
(remember that he is waiting for you,)
ela kai tha deis poso s' agapa!
(come and see how much he loves you!)
Verse 1:
Kathe dakry pou echeis chysei ap' ta vathi tis psychis,
(Every tear you have shed from the depths of your soul,)
mathe pos tha to skoupisei to 'chei yposchethei!
(find out how he will wipe it, he has promised!)
Ki ola afta pou se pligosan kai sou 'skisan tin kardia,
(And all the things that hurt you and tore your heart,)
tha ta parei mia gia panta toso makria!
(will take them once and for all so far!)
Verse 2:
Olo les pos den axizeis o Theos na s' agapa,
(You keep saying you don't deserve God's love,)
san koitas pos echeis pesei toso chamila.
(as you see how you have fallen so low.)
Ma Aftos se perimenei m' anoichti tin agkalia
(But He is waiting for you with open arms)
kai pothei na s' anevasei ston ourano psila!
(and longs to raise you to the sky high!)
