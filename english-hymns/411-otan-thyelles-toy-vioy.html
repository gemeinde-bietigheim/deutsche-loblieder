411 OTAN ThYELLES TOY VIOY

Greek Hymns - No. 411 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΘΥΕΛΛΕΣ ΤΟΥ ΒΙΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Otan thyelles tou viou m' apeiloyne pantachoy,
(When storms of life threaten me everywhere,)
katafygio mou echo to Theo tou ouranoy!
(I have the God of heaven as my refuge!)
Chorus:
Tha me krypsei, tha me krypsei asfali kai me chara,
(She'll hide me, she'll hide me safe and happy,)
Panagathos tha me krypsei stou cherioy Tou ti skia!
(Panagathos will hide me in His hand the shadow!)
Verse 2:
Otan echo ponous, thlipseis, apovlepo eis Afton
(When I have pains, sorrows, I look to Him)
kai Ekeinos omalynei kathe dyskoli odo!
(and He makes smooth every rough way!)
Verse 3:
Ta deina tou kosmou olou den tromazoun ton pisto,
(The troubles of the whole world do not frighten the believer,)
dynatotera den einai ap' ton endoxo Theo!
(it is not stronger than the glorious God!)
Verse 4:
Tin psychi mou de desmeyei giinos desmos kaneis,
(No earthly bond binds my soul,)
o Theos tin prostateyei ki einai panta asfalis!
(God protect her and she is always safe!)
