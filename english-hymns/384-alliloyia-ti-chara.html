384 ALLILOYIA! TI ChARA!

Greek Hymns - No. 384 | "ΑΛΛΗΛΟΥΙΑ! ΤΙ ΧΑΡΑ!"
Verse 1:
Alliloyia! Ti chara! I kardia mou pos skirta,
(Alleluia! What a joy! My heart is pounding)
m' esose o Christos!
(Christ saved me!)
Nai, me esose entelos, gia na zo dia pantos
(Yes, he saved me completely, to live forever)
me Afton nikitis!
(with Him victorious!)
Chorus:
Nikitis, nai, nikitis!
(Winner, yes, winner!)
Alliloyia, nikitis!
(Hallelujah, winner!)
Tora eimai eftychis!
(Now I'm happy!)
Doxa! Doxa! Alliloyia!
(Glory! Glory! Alleluia!)
Sto Christo eim' asfalis!
(In Christ I am safe!)
Verse 2:
Alliloyia! Ti chara! I kardia mou pos skirta
(Alleluia! What a joy! My heart is pounding)
diarkos sti zoi,
(forever in life)
ap' agapi alithini ki effrosyni diarki
(from true love and lasting joy)
pou 'cho mes stin psychi!
(that I have in my soul!)
Verse 3:
I psychi mou asfalis aionios ki epi gis,
(My soul safe forever and on earth,)
sto Christo eftychis!
(happy in Christ!)
Amartias dynami mou exaleipse efthys,
(My power of sin obliterated right away,)
o Christos nikitis!
(Christ is victorious!)
Verse 4:
Me aggelous kai pistoys tha 'mai eis tous ouranoys
(I will be in heaven with angels and believers)
pantote eftychis!
(always happy!)
Prosopo Tou to septo tha koito ston ourano
(His face the sept I will look in the sky)
pantote nikitis!
(always a winner!)
