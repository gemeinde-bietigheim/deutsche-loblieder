278 PNEYMA ThEIO, MEIN' ENTOS MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 278 | "ΠΝΕΥΜΑ ΘΕΙΟ, ΜΕΙΝ' ΕΝΤΟΣ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Pneyma theio, mein' entos mou,
(Holy Spirit, abide within me,)
eis eme ergasou Esy,
(you work for me,)
ola Sou ta anatheto,
(I entrust everything to You,)
Sy kyverna ti zoi!
(You ruled life!)
Chorus:
Pneyma theio, Pneyma theio,
(Spirit divine, Spirit divine,)
Sy eis' o monos odigos,
(You are the only guide,)
Sy odigise ki ergasou
(Drive and work)
eis eme pantotinos!
(to me forever!)
Verse 2:
Ti glykia ein' i foni sou,
(How sweet is your voice,)
ki i agapi Sou thermi,
(and Your love is warm,)
episkiaze psychi mou
(it overshadowed my soul)
kai odigise me Sy!
(and lead me Sy!)
Verse 3:
Me katharises pros doxa
(You have cleansed me to glory)
kai pros katoikia Sou,
(and to Your abode,)
ti zoi mou n' analaveis
(take over my life)
eis tin prostasia Sou!
(in Your protection!)
Verse 4:
To karpo sou charise mou,
(Give me your fruit,)
dose mou ti dynami,
(give me the strength)
fylaxe me, gia na ziso
(save me, that I may live)
katharos eis ti zoi!
(pure in life!)
