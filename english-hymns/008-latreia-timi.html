8 LATREIA, TIMI

Greek Hymns - No. 8 | "ΛΑΤΡΕΙΑ, ΤΙΜΗ"
Verse 1:
Latreia, timi eis ton Plasti Theo,
(Worship, honor to the Maker God,)
san Pateras splachnisthike ton kosm' afto!
(like a Father he had compassion on this world!)
Chorus:
O Theos einai oiktirmon, Alliloyia, Amin!
(God is gracious, Hallelujah, Amen!)
O Theos ein' eleimon, chara eis ti gi!
(God is merciful, joy on earth!)
Verse 2:
Latreia, timi sto Christo Iisoy,
(Worship, honor to Christ Jesus,)
to Sotira tou kosmou, Amno tou Theoy!
(the Savior of the world, Lamb of God!)
Verse 3:
Latreia, timi eis Se Pneyma Septo,
(Worship, honor to In Holy Spirit,)
odigeis tous anthropous sto fos, to Christo!
(you lead people to the light, Christ!)
Verse 4:
Thee, fotise ofthalmoys ton piston
(God, enlighten the eyes of the believers)
kai na mas etoimaseis gia ton ourano!
(and prepare us for heaven!)
Verse 5:
Xypnis' olous Esy tous ftochoys dystycheis,
(Wake up all You poor wretches,)
dia na lytrothoyn tis melloysis orgis!
(to escape the wrath to come!)
