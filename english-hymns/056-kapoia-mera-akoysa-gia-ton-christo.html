56 KAPOIA MERA AKOYSA GIA TON ChRISTO

Greek Hymns - No. 56 | "ΚΑΠΟΙΑ ΜΕΡΑ ΑΚΟΥΣΑ ΓΙΑ ΤΟΝ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ"
Verse 1:
Kapoia mera akousa gia to Christo,
(One day I heard about Christ,)
pistepsa pos pethane gia mena!
(I thought he died for me!)
I kardia mou tote gemise chara
(My heart was then filled with joy)
ki i zoi mou allaxe vathia!
(and my life changed profoundly!)
Chorus:
Ki apo ton ilio pio zesti ein' i agapi tou Theoy!
(And God's love is warmer than the sun!)
kai pio vathia apo tis thalasses kai tous okeanoys!
(and deeper than the seas and oceans!)
Einai asygkriti, aperanti kai toso dynati,
(She is incomparable, vast and so powerful,)
ein' i agapi tou Theoy monadiki!
(God's love is unique!)
Verse 2:
Einai o Christos megalo mystiko,
(Is Christ a great secret,)
toso foteino ki alithino!
(so bright and true!)
Svinei ta palia, syntrivei ta desma,
(Erases the old, shatters the bonds,)
fernei tin eirini stin kardia!
(brings peace to the heart!)
Verse 3:
Ti chara kai sy na ertheis ston Christo!
(What a joy for you to come to Christ!)
Plirose ti lytrosi me aima,
(He paid the ransom in blood,)
gia na echeis tora mesa stin kardia
(to have now in the heart)
tin alitheia kai ti lefteria!
(truth and freedom!)
