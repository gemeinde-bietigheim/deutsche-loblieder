288 SKOTADI PYKNO

Greek Hymns - No. 288 | "ΣΚΟΤΑΔΙ ΠΥΚΝΟ"
Verse 1:
Skotadi pykno stin Aigypto,
(Darkness thick in Egypt,)
thlipsi kai stenagmos.
(sorrow and sighing.)
Skorpaei ton pono, to sparagmo
(It spreads the pain, the heartbreak)
o voyrdoulas tou Farao.
(Pharaoh's dungeon.)
Skorpaei ton pono, to sparagmo
(It spreads the pain, the heartbreak)
o voyrdoulas tou Farao.
(Pharaoh's dungeon.)
Chorus:
Yparchei mia gi pou o Theos ti fotizei,
(There is a land that God lights up,)
einai i gi Gesen.
(it is the land of Goshen.)
Ti gi Gesen thanatos den aggizei,
(The land of Geshen death does not touch,)
zei o laos tou Theoy.
(God's people live.)
Sti gi Gesen i chara plimmyrizei,
(In the land of Goshen joy overflows,)
katoikei to Pneyma tou Theoy!
(dwells the Spirit of God!)
Verse 2:
Varia i sklavia stin Aigypto,
(Heavy slavery in Egypt,)
ponos kai marasmos.
(pain and wilting.)
Chtypaei me lyssa, me thymo,
(He strikes with rage, with anger,)
o voyrdoulas tou Farao.
(Pharaoh's dungeon.)
Chtypaei me lyssa, me thymo,
(He strikes with rage, with anger,)
o voyrdoulas tou Farao.
(Pharaoh's dungeon.)
