445 ThELEI NA S' AGGIXEI

Greek Hymns - No. 445 | "ΘΕΛΕΙ ΝΑ Σ' ΑΓΓΙΞΕΙ"
Chorus:
Thelei na s' aggixei
(He wants to touch you)
me to chadi tis agapis Tou,
(with the caress of His love,)
thelei na gemisei
(wants to fill up)
tin adeia sou kardia
(your empty heart)
m' agapi, eirini, chara, eftychia,
(with love, peace, joy, happiness,)
thelei na sou dosei zoi aionia!
(He wants to give you eternal life!)
Verse 1:
Ki an tou kosmou xethoriase i agapi,
(And if love faded from the world,)
ki an oi filoi se pikranan poly,
(and if your friends made you very bitter,)
Ekeinos stin porta tis kardias sou
(He at the door of your heart)
perimenei, mesa gia na mpei!
(waiting, inside to enter!)
Verse 2:
Ki an i chara echei fygei apo konta sou,
(And if the joy is gone from you,)
ki an s' egkateleipse oli i gi,
(and if all the earth forsake thee,)
Ekeinos sti zesti agkalia Tou
(Him in His warm embrace)
perimenei esena na dechthei!
(waiting for you to accept!)
