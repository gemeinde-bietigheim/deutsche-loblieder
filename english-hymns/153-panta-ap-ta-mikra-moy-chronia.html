153 PANTA AP' TA MIKRA MOY ChRONIA

Greek Hymns - No. 153 | "ΠΑΝΤΑ ΑΠ' ΤΑ ΜΙΚΡΑ ΜΟΥ ΧΡΟΝΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Panta ap' ta mikra mou chronia
(Ever since my childhood)
rotoysa gia symponia, zitoysa ti chara!
(I was asking for compassion, I was asking for joy!)
Panta tous gyro mou rotoysa
(I always asked those around me)
an tacha tha mporoysa na'cho ki ego ftera!
(if so i could have wings too!)
Mia istoria ein' i zoi mou pou itan grammeni me stenagmoys,
(A story is my life that was written with sighs,)
o ponos mazi ki i thlipsi mou eichan krypsei kai tous ouranoys!
(the pain together with the sadness had hidden the heavens too!)
o ponos mazi ki i thlipsi mou eichan krypsei kai tous ouranoys!
(the pain together with the sadness had hidden the heavens too!)
Verse 2:
Tote pou epsachna sta skoti
(When I was searching in the dark)
kai xodepsa ti nioti kai emeina ftochos!
(and I spent my youth and was left poor!)
Tote p' animporos sernomoun
(Then I crawled helplessly)
ki olo apelpizomoun pos tha vro ligo fos!
(and I still despaired of finding some light!)
Mia istoria ein' i zoi mou pou itan grammeni me stenagmoys!
(A story is my life that was written with sighs!)
o ponos mazi ki i thlipsi mou eichan krypsei kai tous ouranoys!
(the pain together with the sadness had hidden the heavens too!)
o ponos mazi ki i thlipsi mou eichan krypsei kai tous ouranoys!
(the pain together with the sadness had hidden the heavens too!)
Verse 3:
Xafnou, ouranio asteri vgainei
(Suddenly, heavenly star comes out)
kai stin kardia anevainei elpida kai chara!
(and hope and joy rises in the heart!)
Logia olodrosa, stalmena
(Full words, sent)
ap' to Theo gia mena, m' aggizoun tryfera!
(by God for me, they touch me tenderly!)
Mia istoria ein' i zoi mou pou itan grammeni me stenagmoys!
(A story is my life that was written with sighs!)
Ma xafnou lampro asteri elpida fernei ap' tous ouranoys!
(But a sudden bright star brings hope from the heavens!)
Ma xafnou lampro asteri elpida fernei ap' tous ouranoys!
(But a sudden bright star brings hope from the heavens!)
Verse 4:
Mathe, mou legei, pos agapi
(Learn, he tells me, how love is)
tha vreis sto monopati pou paei sto Golgotha!
(you will find on the path that goes to Calvary!)
Mathe sta podia tou Kyriou
(Learn at the feet of the Lord)
tin pikra tou fortiou n' afiseis apala!
(leave the bitterness of the burden gently!)
Mia istoria ein' i zoi mou pou'chei ti lampsi tou lytromoy!
(My life is a story that has the glow of redemption!)
Christe mou, glyke Sotira, apo Sena pira zoi t' ouranoy!
(My Christ, sweet Saviour, from You I took life of heaven!)
Christe mou, glyke Sotira, apo Sena pira zoi t' ouranoy!
(My Christ, sweet Saviour, from You I took life of heaven!)
