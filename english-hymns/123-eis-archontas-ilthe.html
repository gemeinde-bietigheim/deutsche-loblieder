123 EIS ARChONTAS ILThE

Greek Hymns - No. 123 | "ΕΙΣ ΑΡΧΟΝΤΑΣ ΗΛΘΕ"
Verse 1:
Eis archontas ilthe eis ton Christon,
(In lordship he came to Christ,)
dia na sothei egkardios ziton,
(to be saved heartily asks,)
me dipsa ton Kyrio Ton erota
(he seeks the Lord with thirst)
ki Ekeinos tou apanta:
(and He answers him:)
Chorus:
«Prepei na gennitheis, na anagennitheis,
("You must be born, be reborn,)
me Pneyma kai ydor n' anagennitheis,
(with Spirit and water to be reborn,)
an theleis na sotheis!»
(if you want to be saved!”)
Verse 2:
Paidia ton thniton, milaei o Christos,
(Children of mortals, Christ speaks,)
kyrittei kai legei se olous ritos:
(decrees and tells everyone expressly:)
«O anthropos prepei n' anagennithei,
("Man must be reborn,)
dia na lytrothei!».
(to be redeemed!".)
Verse 3:
Esy pou na zeis me agious potheis,
(You who live with holy desires,)
polla tora kaneis, dia na sotheis,
(now you do a lot to save yourself)
ton Logon Aftoy pote mi lismoneis:
(never forget His Word:)
«Prepei na gennitheis!».
("You must be born!")
Verse 4:
Mataios elpizeis sy, gia na sotheis,
(In vain you hope to be saved,)
ean ek tou Pneymatos den gennitheis,
(if you are not born of the Spirit,)
oiktra apatasai kai th' apolestheis,
(you are bitterly deceived and you will be lost,)
ean den gennitheis!
(if you are not born!)
Verse 5:
Sy anothen zita n' anagennitheis!
(Ask from above to be reborn!)
Kat' allon de tropon, o, min prospatheis,
(In another way, oh, don't try,)
me typous kai erga de tha lytrotheis,
(by formulas and works you shall not be redeemed,)
Prepei na gennitheis!
(You must be born!)
