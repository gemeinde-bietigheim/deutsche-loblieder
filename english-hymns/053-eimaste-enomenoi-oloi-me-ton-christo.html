53 EIMASTE ENOMENOI OLOI ME TON ChRISTO

Greek Hymns - No. 53 | "ΕΙΜΑΣΤΕ ΕΝΩΜΕΝΟΙ ΟΛΟΙ ΜΕ ΤΟΝ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ"
Verse 1:
Eimaste enomenoi oloi me to Christo,
(We are all united with Christ,)
eimaste ena soma endoxo kai lampro,
(we are a glorious and shining body,)
oi pistoi mia poimni oloi kato edo sti gi!
(the faithful one flock all down here on earth!)
Chorus:
Gia na doun pos mas kanei adelfoys o Christos!
(To see how Christ makes us brothers!)
I agapi pos enonei tous pistoys!
(How love unites believers!)
Verse 2:
Oloi mas mes ston idio dromo pame mprosta,
(We are all on the same road moving forward,)
prochoroyme piasmenoi ap' ta cheria sfichta
(we move forward holding hands tightly)
kai skorpoyme ta nea tou Christoy s' olous pantoy!
(and we spread the news of Christ to everyone everywhere!)
Verse 3:
Sti douleia enomenoi oloi me mia kardia,
(At work all united with one heart,)
o katheis plai - plai me ton allon konta,
(each side by side with the other close,)
mes sto idio chorafi oloi ymnoyme me chara!
(in the same field we all sing with joy!)
Verse 4:
Oli i doxa anikei ston Patera Theo,
(All glory belongs to God the Father,)
i timi sto megalo Lytroti mas Christo,
(the honor to our great Redeemer Christ,)
kai sto Pneyma to Agio, ton triadiko Theo!
(and in the Holy Spirit, the triune God!)
