203 ANO LAMPEI O ASTIR

Greek Hymns - No. 203 | "ΑΝΩ ΛΑΜΠΕΙ Ο ΑΣΤΗΡ"
Verse 1:
Ano lampei o astir, egennithi o Sotir,
(Above the star shines, Sotir was born,)
ton ethnon o fotismos, o aionios Theos!
(of the nations the illumination, the eternal God!)
Chorus:
As doxazoume pantoy ton Theon tou ouranoy!
(Let us praise the God of heaven everywhere!)
Egennithike o Christos, ein' mazi mas o Theos!
(Christ is born, may God be with us!)
Egennithike o Christos, ein' mazi mas o Theos!
(Christ is born, may God be with us!)
Verse 2:
Oi poimenes eflogoyn kai to vrefos theoroyn,
(The shepherds bless and the infant consider,)
einai o Emmanouil, i elpis tou Israil!
(he is Emmanuel, the hope of Israel!)
Verse 3:
Kai oi magoi proskynoyn ton Sotira Iisoy
(And the magi worship the Savior Jesus)
kai aggelon straties anymnoyn kai eflogoyn!
(and armies of angels sing and bless!)
Verse 4:
Ton ymnoyn ta Serafeim, doylou elave morfi,
(The Seraphim praise him, he took the form of a servant,)
etapeinos' Eafton, Osanna eis ton Theon!
(humble Himself, Hosanna to God!)
