225 AGAPI TRYFERI

Greek Hymns - No. 225 | "ΑΓΑΠΗ ΤΡΥΦΕΡΗ"
Verse 1:
Agapi tryferi einai tou Lytroti
(Tender love is the Redeemer's)
na elthei eis ti gi na stafrothei,
(to come to earth to be crucified,)
m' esose o Christos plironontas Aftos
(Christ saved me by paying)
ta lytra tis poinis anti gia me!
(the ransom of punishment instead of me!)
Chorus:
Ti thafmasto gegonos!
(What a wonderful event!)
Nai, tou Theoy o Amnos
(Yes, the Lamb of God)
apethane sto Stafro
(died on the Cross)
anti gia me!
(instead of me!)
Verse 2:
Epano sto Stafro vlepo ton Iisoy
(On the Cross I see Jesus)
na ypoferei Aftos anti gia me!
(let Him suffer instead of me!)
Pire epano Tou tou kosmou tin ara
(He took upon Himself the weight of the world)
kai m' eleftherose ap' ta desma!
(and set me free from the bonds!)
Verse 3:
Dikaiosyni Tou kai i agapi Tou
(His justice and His love)
synantithikane sto Golgotha!
(they met at Calvary!)
Ekei san vrethika ki ego Ton dechthika
(As I found myself there, I accepted Him)
ki amesos sothika, Alliloyia!
(and immediately I was saved, Hallelujah!)
