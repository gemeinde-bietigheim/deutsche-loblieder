69 POSO S' AGAPO, O IISOY

Greek Hymns - No. 69 | "ΠΟΣΟ Σ' ΑΓΑΠΩ, Ω ΙΗΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Poso s' agapo, o Iisoy,
(How I love you, oh Jesus,)
Sy eisai i zoi gia me!
(You are the life for me!)
Onoma glykytero, Christe,
(Sweetest name, Christ,)
den efrethike pote!
(never found!)
Chorus:
Iisoy, Iisoy mou,
(Jesus, my Jesus,)
onoma glyky!
(sweet name!)
Effrainei tin kardia mou,
(gladdens my heart,)
dinei ymno stin psychi!
(gives hymn to the soul!)
Verse 2:
Sy eisai tis psychis mou thisafros,
(You are the treasure of my soul,)
Sy eisai di' eme to fos!
(You are the light for me!)
Pos potho na psallo pantote
(How I long to sing always)
gia ti doxa sou, Christe!
(for your glory, Christ!)
Verse 3:
Eis ta ichni Sou th' akoloutho
(I will follow in your footsteps)
kai pista panta tha zo,
(and faithfully I will always live,)
na thysiastho pros doxa Sou,
(to sacrifice to Your glory,)
o Christe mou Iisoy!
(oh my christ jesus!)
Verse 4:
An oi filoi me afisoune,
(If my friends left me,)
eimai asfalis me Se!
(I'm safe with You!)
Sy 'sai di' eme to pan, Christe,
(You are everything to me, Christ,)
tis psychis mou agapite!
(of my soul dear!)
