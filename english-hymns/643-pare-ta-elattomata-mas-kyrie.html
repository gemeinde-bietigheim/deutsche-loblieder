643 PARE TA ELATTOMATA MAS, KYRIE

Greek Hymns - No. 643 | "ΠΑΡΕ ΤΑ ΕΛΑΤΤΩΜΑΤΑ ΜΑΣ, ΚΥΡΙΕ"
Verse 1:
Pare ta elattomata mas, Kyrie,
(Take our faults, Lord,)
vale agapi stin kardia mas, Ypsiste! Thafmaste!
(put love in our hearts, Rise up! Fans!)
Kane glykia foni n' akoyme
(Make a sweet voice for us to hear)
diki Sou pantotina!
(yours forever!)
Kane glykia foni n' akoyme
(Make a sweet voice for us to hear)
diki Sou pantotina!
(yours forever!)
Verse 2:
Meres kakes tha elthoun pali kai ponires,
(Bad days will come again and wicked,)
ma emeis tha prochoroyme panta me prosefches!
(but we will always proceed with prayers!)
Na fthasoume psila ston ourano,
(To reach high in the sky,)
na zoyme me to Theo!
(to live with God!)
Na fthasoume psila ston ourano,
(To reach high in the sky,)
na zoyme me to Theo!
(to live with God!)
Verse 3:
N' apallachtoyme ap' to dynasti, ton poniro,
(To free ourselves from the tyrant, the wicked,)
ekei tha zoyme panta mono me to Christo!
(there we will always live only with Christ!)
Dikaiosyni tha yparchei ekei,
(Justice will be there,)
agapi pantotini!
(love forever!)
Dikaiosyni tha yparchei ekei,
(Justice will be there,)
agapi pantotini!
(love forever!)
