365 EIS TO ERGO TO ThEIO

Greek Hymns - No. 365 | "ΕΙΣ ΤΟ ΕΡΓΟ ΤΟ ΘΕΙΟ"
Verse 1:
Eis to ergo to theio os doylos Christoy,
(In the divine work as a servant of Christ,)
dose cheri voitheias ki esy, o piste!
(lend a helping hand too, O believer!)
Eis ta ichni tou theiou mas tou Lytrotoy
(In the footsteps of our godly Redeemer)
vadize kai ergasou pros doxan Aftoy!
(walk and work for His glory!)
Chorus:
Ston agona ki ego me chara prochoro,
(In the fight I too go forward with joy,)
prosdoko, agrypno, mechri na elthei o Christos!
(I expect, I watch, until Christ comes!)
Verse 2:
Stous anthropous tou skotous na eis' odigos,
(To the people of darkness be a guide,)
sti zoi sou me erga na lampeis san fos!
(in your life with works to shine like a light!)
To paradeigma na 'nai gia se o Christos,
(Let Christ be your example,)
pou 'nai panta ston kosmo aionio fos!
(which is always in the world eternal light!)
Verse 3:
Tous peinontes sy trefe me arto zois,
(You feed the hungry with bread of life,)
tous dipsontes me Logo Theoy zontano!
(those who thirst for the living Word of God!)
Na odigiseis aftoys sti pigi tis zois
(To lead them to the source of life)
kai tha laveis mia mera ki esy to mistho!
(and one day you too will receive the salary!)
Verse 4:
Tharralea ergasou kai min entrapeis,
(Work bravely and don't give in,)
i exousia tou skotous tha katastrafei!
(the power of darkness will be destroyed!)
Vasileia aionia ouranias zois
(Kingdom of eternal heavenly life)
tha idrysei o Kyrios, o Lytrotis!
(shall the Lord, the Redeemer, establish!)
Verse 5:
Me ti chari Tou panta ergasou pista
(By His grace always work faithfully)
kai gia olous tous kopous sou san amoivi
(and for all your labors as a reward)
chryso stefano tha sou charis' o Christos
(Christ will give you a golden crown)
eis tin poli Sion, otan fthaseis ekei!
(to the city of Zion, when you get there!)
