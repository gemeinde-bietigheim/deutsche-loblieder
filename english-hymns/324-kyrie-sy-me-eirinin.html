324 KYRIE, SY ME EIRININ

Greek Hymns - No. 324 | "ΚΥΡΙΕ, ΣΥ ΜΕ ΕΙΡΗΝΗΝ"
Verse 1:
Kyrie, Sy me eirinin nyn apolyson imas
(Lord, in peace now dismiss us)
kai pnefmatiki galini dorise eis tas psychas
(and gave spiritual peace to the souls)
ton paronton, ton paronton pantas emplison charas!
(of the present, of the present, be filled with joy!)
Verse 2:
Me eilikrini kardian Se doxazomen imeis
(With a sincere heart we glorify You)
dia tin didaskalian ek tis Ieras Grafis
(through teaching from the Holy Scriptures)
odigoysan, odigoysan eis ton dromon tis zois!
(they were leading, they were leading on the road of life!)
Verse 3:
I Si theia parousia esto imin voithos
(Let the divine presence be our helper)
kai i charis i agia neou viou odigos!
(and the holy grace of new life guide!)
Kai to Pneyma kai to Pneyma syntrofos pantotinos!
(And Spirit and Spirit eternal companion!)
Verse 4:
Nyn edo eflogison mas ki eis ton oikon palin Sy
(Now here bless us and in the house again You)
efmenos synodefson mas, oste pasa tis psychi
(auspiciously accompany us, so that all souls)
sotirias, sotirias effrosynos na dechthi!
(Salvation, Salvation gladly accept!)
