294 ZOI GIA ChARI MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 294 | "ΖΩΗ ΓΙΑ ΧΑΡΗ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Zoi gia chari mou proseferes, Christe,
(Life for my sake you offered me, Christ,)
kai me to aima Sou elytroses eme!
(and with Your blood you redeemed me!)
Plin ti prosefera pote ego se Se, Christe;
(But what did I ever offer You, Christ?)
Plin ti prosefera pote ego se Se, Christe;
(But what did I ever offer You, Christ?)
Verse 2:
Ti doxa tou ouranoy kai oles tis times
(The glory of heaven and all honors)
afises kai sti gi katevikes, Christe!
(you left and came down to earth, Christ!)
Plin ti arnithika gia Se, o Iisoy Christe;
(But what have I denied for Thee, O Jesus Christ?)
Plin ti arnithika gia Se, o Iisoy Christe;
(But what have I denied for Thee, O Jesus Christ?)
Verse 3:
Anekfrasta, pikra ta pathi tou Stafroy,
(Inexpressible, bitter passions of the Cross,)
dia eme polla epathes, Iisoy!
(for me you suffered a lot, Jesus!)
Ego ti epatha gia Se, o Iisoy Christe;
(What have I suffered for You, O Jesus Christ?)
Ego ti epatha gia Se, o Iisoy Christe;
(What have I suffered for You, O Jesus Christ?)
Verse 4:
Dora tou Pneymatos mou charises Esy
(You gave me gifts of the Spirit)
chari kai eleos kai apolytrosi!
(grace and mercy and redemption!)
Plin ti prosfero ego se Se, o Iisoy Christe;
(But what do I offer You, O Jesus Christ?)
Plin ti prosfero ego se Se, o Iisoy Christe;
(But what do I offer You, O Jesus Christ?)
Verse 5:
Oli mou ti zoi Sy pare, Iisoy,
(All my life Take, Jesus,)
ta chronia mou sti gi ta dino charin Sou!
(I give my years on earth for Your sake!)
Ta panta dino eis Ese, o Iisoy Christe!
(I give everything to You, O Jesus Christ!)
Ta panta dino eis Ese, o Iisoy Christe!
(I give everything to You, O Jesus Christ!)
