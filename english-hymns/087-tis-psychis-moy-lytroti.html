87 TIS PsYChIS MOY LYTROTI

Greek Hymns - No. 87 | "ΤΗΣ ΨΥΧΗΣ ΜΟΥ ΛΥΤΡΩΤΗ"
Verse 1:
Tis psychis mou Lytroti, endoxe mou Poiiti,
(Redeemer of my soul, my glorious Poet,)
eno gyro mou vronta ki eno kymata frikta
(while around me thunders and while horrible waves)
gyro gyro me ktypoyn, synechos me apeiloyn,
(they beat me all around, they constantly threaten me,)
stin agapi Sou ego trecho gia na mi chatho.
(in Your love I run so I don't get lost.)
Verse 2:
Katafygio kalo den efrisketai alloy,
(Good shelter is not found elsewhere,)
Sy 'sai to propyrgio kai prostatis mou pantoy,
(You are my fortress and protector everywhere,)
Sy 'sai achtida tis zois, i elpida tis psychis,
(You are the ray of life, the hope of the soul,)
exartamai apo Se, eysplachne Iisoy Christe.
(I depend on You, merciful Jesus Christ.)
Verse 3:
Ola osa chreiastho, ola osa ki an potho,
(All that I need, all that I desire,)
eis Ese mporo na vro, apo Sena ta zito.
(in You I can find, from You I ask.)
Tora de pou astheno, therapeia Sou zito,
(And now that I am sick, I ask You for healing,)
ki an proskopto san tyflos, Sy odigei m' asfalos.
(and if I scout like a blind man, You lead me safely.)
Verse 4:
Agio kai iero t' onoma Sou to septo,
(Holy and holy is Your sept name,)
oloi Se latreyoume, Se ton Agio Theo!
(we all adore You, Holy God!)
Omos eimai ego deilos, asthenis ki amartolos,
(But I am a coward, sick and sinful,)
tora, Se parakalo, voitha me gia Se na zo!
(now, Please, help me to live for You!)
