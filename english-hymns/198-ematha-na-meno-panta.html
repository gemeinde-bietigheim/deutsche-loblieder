198 EMAThA NA MENO PANTA

Greek Hymns - No. 198 | "ΕΜΑΘΑ ΝΑ ΜΕΝΩ ΠΑΝΤΑ"
Verse 1:
Ematha na meno panta eis ta podia tou Christoy
(I learned to always stay at the feet of Christ)
kai m' ypomoni megali na mathaino ex Aftoy!
(and I have great patience to learn from Him!)
Aftos pantote mou legei: «Mi distazeis, mi fovoy,
(He always tells me: "Do not hesitate, do not be afraid,)
akoloytha Me sy panta kai ego eimai meta sou!».
(follow Me always and I will follow you!".)
Chorus:
Nai, tha meno sto Christo kai th' akoloutho pantoy,
(Yes, I will abide in Christ and follow everywhere,)
thelo pantote na meno eis ti chari tou Christoy!
(I always want to stay in the grace of Christ!)
Verse 2:
Epapsa praxeis anomous, otan iltha sto Christo,
(I stopped doing lawless things when I came to Christ,)
tin apati kai to misos, kathe pseydos kai kako!
(deceit and hatred, every lie and evil!)
«Meta tou Christoy estafrothin», zei Aftos anti emoy,
("After Christ was crucified", He lives instead of me,)
neo ktisma eimai tora me ti chari tou Theoy!
(new building I am now by the grace of God!)
Verse 3:
Oles mou tis dyskolies anatheto sto Christo
(I entrust all my difficulties to Christ)
kai Aftos panta me sozei ek ton stenochorion!
(and He always saves me from troubles!)
Theia dynami mou dinei ki o,ti allo chreiastho
(Divine power gives me everything else I need)
kai pote de tha distaso, gia na Ton empisteftho!
(and I will never hesitate to trust Him!)
Verse 4:
Panta ap' Afton lavaino empnefsi na omilo,
(I am always inspired to speak from Him,)
tharros, pisti kai elpida ap' to Logo Tou antlo!
(I draw courage, faith and hope from His Word!)
Ploytos pou me perimenei diarkos ston ourano...
(Riches always waiting for me in heaven...)
Doxa eis to Lytroti mou, pantote tha Ton ymno!
(Glory to my Redeemer, I will always praise Him!)
