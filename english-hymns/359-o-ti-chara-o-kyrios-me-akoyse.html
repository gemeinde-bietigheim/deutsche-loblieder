359 O TI ChARA! O KYRIOS ME AKOYSE

Greek Hymns - No. 359 | "Ω ΤΙ ΧΑΡΑ! Ο ΚΥΡΙΟΣ ΜΕ ΑΚΟΥΣΕ"
Verse 1:
O ti chara! O Kyrios me akouse,
(Oh what joy! The Lord heard me,)
san ti foni mou ypsosa s' Afton!
(like my voice I lifted up to Him!)
San prosefchithika, eskypse ki akouse,
(As I prayed, he bent down and listened,)
gi' afto, oso zo, tha epikaloymai Afton!
(therefore, as long as I live, I will call upon Him!)
Verse 2:
Ponoi thanatou san me perikyklosan,
(Pains of death seemed to surround me,)
kai stenochoria tou Adi san me vrike,
(and distress of Hades as if he found me,)
thlipsi kai ponos otan me synantisan,
(sorrow and pain when they met me,)
ap' ola afta me lytrose o Theos!
(God redeemed me from all this!)
Verse 3:
Gematos eleos kai dikaiosyni
(Full of mercy and justice)
kai me agapi einai o Theos!
(and with love is God!)
Talaiporithika, ma Aftos me esose,
(I suffered, but He saved me,)
psychi mou, isychase, dioti o Kyrios
(my soul, be still, for the Lord)
se akouse kai s' efergetise!
(he heard you and benefited you!)
