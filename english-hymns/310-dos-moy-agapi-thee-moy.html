310 DOS' MOY AGAPI, ThEE MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 310 | "ΔΩΣ' ΜΟΥ ΑΓΑΠΗ, ΘΕΕ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Dos' mou agapi, Thee mou,
(give me love oh god)
na Se ymno, na Se ymno, na Se ymno!
(to praise You, to praise You, to praise You!)
Dos' mou agapi, Thee mou,
(give me love oh god)
na Se ymno, na Se ymno, na Se ymno, Christe!
(to praise You, to praise You, to praise You, Christ!)
Se Se anikei o ainos,
(To You belongs the glory,)
i dynami, i doxa, Thee!
(the power, the glory, God!)
Dos' mou agapi, Thee mou,
(give me love oh god)
na Se ymno, na Se ymno, na Se ymno, Christe!
(to praise You, to praise You, to praise You, Christ!)
Verse 2:
Dos' mou ti dynami Sou,
(Give me Your strength,)
na perpato, na perpato, na perpato!
(to walk, to walk, to walk!)
Dos' mou ti dynami Sou,
(Give me Your strength,)
na perpato, na perpato, na perpato me Se!
(to walk, to walk, to walk with You!)
Mono i chari Sou, Thee mou,
(Only Your grace, O God,)
dinei ftera, psila na peto!
(gives wings, high to fly!)
Dos' mou ti dynami Sou,
(Give me Your strength,)
na perpato, na perpato, na perpato, Christe!
(to walk, to walk, to walk, Christ!)
Verse 3:
Chtise to throno Sou entos mou
(Build Your throne within me)
na katoikeis, na katoikeis, na katoikeis!
(dwell, dwell, dwell!)
Chtise to throno Sou entos mou
(Build Your throne within me)
na katoikeis, na katoikeis, na katoikeis, Christe!
(dwell, dwell, dwell, Christ!)
Ki ego tha dino se Se doxa,
(And I will give You glory,)
aino, proskynisi, latreia, timi!
(praise, worship, worship, honor!)
Chtise to throno Sou entos mou
(Build Your throne within me)
na katoikeis, na katoikeis, na katoikeis, Christe!
(dwell, dwell, dwell, Christ!)
