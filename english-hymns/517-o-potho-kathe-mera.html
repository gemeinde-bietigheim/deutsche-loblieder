517 O, POThO KAThE MERA

Greek Hymns - No. 517 | "Ω, ΠΟΘΩ ΚΑΘΕ ΜΕΡΑ"
Verse 1:
O, potho kathe mera gia Se, o Christe,
(Oh, I long for You every day, O Christ,)
neo ymno na psallo se Se!
(new hymn to sing to You!)
Giati tote doxazo Ese monacha
(For then I glorify You alone)
ki i psychi mou konta Sou peta!
(and my soul flies near You!)
Chorus:
Otan tha 'rtho konta Sou mia mera glykia,
(When I come to You one day sweet,)
tha mou doseis kithara chrysi!
(will you give me a golden guitar!)
Kai tha psallo aionia pros Se, Lytrota,
(And I will sing eternally to Thee, Redeemed,)
gia ti chari Sou tin perissi!
(for Your grace the excess!)
Verse 2:
Ekei pano, Sotira, me theia chara
(Up there, Saviour, with divine joy)
th' atenizo aionia pros Se!
(I will look eternally to You!)
Kai tha psallo m' agni chorodia, lampra
(And I will sing with a pure choir, brilliantly)
tin agapi Sou, o Thafmaste!
(Thy love, O Admirer!)
Verse 3:
Me stefani chryso sto kefali lampro
(With a crown of gold on the head brilliant)
stou Patera to throno mprosta
(to the Father the throne before)
to Theo mou kai to Lytroti tha ymno
(I will praise my God and my Redeemer)
ki i kithara tha paizei glyka!
(and the guitar will play sweetly!)
Verse 4:
O! Esy mou empneeis sto vio mou afto
(Oh! You inspire this in my life)
tous psalmoys tis agias Sion!
(the psalms of holy Zion!)
Ki i elpida fountonei ki o pothos xespa
(And hope flares up and lust breaks out)
na 'rtho grigora ston ourano!
(to come quickly to heaven!)
