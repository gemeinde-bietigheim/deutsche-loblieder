420 SAN TON IISOY DEN EChO

Greek Hymns - No. 420 | "ΣΑΝ ΤΟΝ ΙΗΣΟΥ ΔΕΝ ΕΧΩ"
Verse 1:
San ton Iisoy den echo
(Like Jesus I don't have)
allo filo pio pisto,
(another friend more faithful,)
pou eplirose gia mena
(who paid for me)
pano sto skliro stafro!
(on the hard cross!)
Chorus:
Empisteyomai s' Ekeinon
(I trust in Him)
kathe vima tis zois
(every step of life)
kai m' Afton tha thriamveyo
(and with Him I will triumph)
mechri telous nikitis!
(all the way winner!)
Verse 2:
O Iisoys gia mena einai
(Jesus for me is)
vrachos theios t' ouranoy,
(heavenly rock,)
gia na me yperaspizei
(to defend me)
ap' ta veli tou echthroy!
(from the arrows of the enemy!)
Verse 3:
Ston Iisoy mou thelo psallei
(To my Jesus I want to sing)
gia to doro Tou afto,
(for this His gift,)
pou me ti diki Tou chari
(which by His own grace)
th' anevo ston Ourano!
(I will ascend to Heaven!)
