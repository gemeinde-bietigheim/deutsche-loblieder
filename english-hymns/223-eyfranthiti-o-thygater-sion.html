223 EYFRANThITI, O ThYGATER SION!

Greek Hymns - No. 223 | "ΕΥΦΡΑΝΘΗΤΙ, Ω ΘΥΓΑΤΕΡ ΣΙΩΝ!"
Verse 1:
Effranthiti, o thygater Sion!
(Exult, O daughter of Zion!)
Chaire kai agallou, Ierousalim!
(Hail, O Jerusalem!)
Nyn o Vasileys sou erchetai pros se,
(Now your King is coming to you,)
feron soi eirinin, doxan kai ischyn!
(bringing you peace, glory and power!)
Chorus:
Effranthiti, o thygater Sion!
(Exult, O daughter of Zion!)
Chaire kai agallou, Ierousalim!
(Hail, O Jerusalem!)
Verse 2:
Archon eirinis, tou Dafid Yie,
(Prince of Peace, of David Son,)
aionia est' i Vasileia Sou!
(forever be Your Kingdom!)
En dikaiosyni ki en efthytiti
(In justice and uprightness)
theleis ton laon Sou pantot' odigei!
(you want your people always lead!)
Verse 3:
En tois ypsistois doxa to Theo!
(In the highest glory to God!)
Triseflogimenos o erchomenos
(Blessed is the coming one)
en onomati Kyriou tou Theoy!
(in the name of the Lord God!)
Vasileys, Christos tou oikou Israil!
(King, Christ of the house of Israel!)
