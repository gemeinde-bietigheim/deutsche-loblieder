539 PERIMENO, OSPOY NA 'RThEI

Greek Hymns - No. 539 | "ΠΕΡΙΜΕΝΩ, ΩΣΠΟΥ ΝΑ 'ΡΘΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Perimeno, ospou na 'rthei i megali i stigmi,
(I wait until the big moment comes,)
xafnika o Iisoys mas th' anateilei eis ti gi.
(suddenly our Jesus will rise on earth.)
To skotadi tha perasei kai i imera tha fanei,
(The darkness will pass and the day will appear,)
th' anoichthei o ouranos kai tha erthei o Christos!
(heaven will open and Christ will come!)
Verse 2:
Otan o Christos tha erthei kai i salpigga akoustei,
(When Christ shall come and the trumpet shall sound,)
oi pistoi pou koimithikan oloi tha anastithoyn,
(the faithful who have fallen asleep will all rise,)
ki osoi zoyne me to Pneyma, o! tha metamorfothoyn,
(and those who live by the Spirit, oh! they will transform)
gia na paroun neo soma kai stin poli tous na zoun!
(to get a new body and live in their city!)
Verse 3:
Metaxy pollon aggelon tha ton doyme ton Amno.
(Among many angels we will see the Lamb.)
Se nefeles pano tha 'rthei me megali dynami!
(In clouds above it will come with great power!)
Aggelos tote tha kraxei, tha akoustei s' oli ti gi,
(Angel then shall cry, heard through all the earth,)
o Theos mas tha salpisei kai tha ginei i arpagi!
(our God will sound the trumpet and the rapture will take place!)
Verse 4:
Oi pistoi tote tha lampoun kai stefania tha foroyn!
(The faithful will then shine and wear crowns!)
O ti doxa, tha fanei! Ti chara tha ein' ekei!
(Oh what a glory, it will be seen! What a joy it will be there!)
Ton Patera tha thoroyme prosopo pros prosopo,
(We will meet the Father face to face,)
me aggelous tha ymnoyme ton ouranio Amno!
(with angels we will praise the heavenly Lamb!)
Verse 5:
Erchetai tora o Kyrios stin yposchesi pistos,
(Now comes the Lord faithful to the promise,)
th' anapaysei tin psychi mas ap' ton kosmo to ftharto!
(will rest our soul from the perishable world!)
Erchetai Aftos tacheos! As charei kathe pistos!
(He is coming quickly! Let every believer rejoice!)
Perimenoume na doyme prosopo Tou to glyko!
(We are waiting to see His sweet face!)
