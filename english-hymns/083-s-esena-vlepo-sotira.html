83 S' ESENA VLEPO SOTIRA

Greek Hymns - No. 83 | "Σ' ΕΣΕΝΑ ΒΛΕΠΩ ΣΩΤΗΡΑ"
Verse 1:
S' Esena vlepo, Sotira, na eisai Sy odigos,
(In You I see, Saviour, be You guide,)
eis tin odo pou vadizo na 'sai gia mena to fos!
(be the light for me on the road I walk!)
Chorus:
Konta mou ela! Mi me afiseis pote
(Come near me! don't ever leave me)
na planitho kai na peso ston peirasmo, o Christe!
(to err and fall into temptation, O Christ!)
Verse 2:
Opos to fos tis imeras skoti skorpizei nyktos,
(As the light of day scatters darkness in the night,)
Sy tis psychis to skotadi skorpize m' Agio fos!
(The darkness of the soul was scattered with Holy light!)
Verse 3:
Tis pisteos to spinthira mes stin kardia, Iisoy,
(The spark of faith in the heart, Jesus,)
pyrini kan' ton aspida kata pantos peirasmoy!
(fiery shield against all temptation!)
Verse 4:
I se chara tha provaino i kai se thlipsi deini,
(Either in joy I will proceed, or in grievous sorrow,)
dose, Christe mou, n' akoyo tin idiki Sou foni!
(grant, my Christ, that I may hear Your own voice!)
