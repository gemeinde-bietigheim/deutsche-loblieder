641 OTAN O ILIOS PAYEI NA LAMPEI

Greek Hymns - No. 641 | "ΟΤΑΝ Ο ΗΛΙΟΣ ΠΑΥΕΙ ΝΑ ΛΑΜΠΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Otan o ilios payei na lampei
(When the sun stops shining)
ki otan fovasai ti skoteinia,
(and when you're afraid of the dark,)
otan den echeis pou n' akoumpiseis
(when you have nowhere to lean on)
kai otan i mera paei strava...
(and when the day goes wrong...)
Chorus:
Trexe s' Ekeinon pou eipe: «Elate oi kourasmenoi
(Run to Him who said, "Come ye who are weary)
kai oi fortomenoi me vari stin psychi,
(and those burdened with burdens in the soul,)
tha sas xekouraso, ta vari sas tha paro,
(I will give you rest, I will take your burdens,)
de tha sas afiso oyte stigmi.»
(I will not spare you even a moment.")
Verse 2:
Otan vretheis se dyskolies
(When you're in trouble)
ki otan mprosta vriskeis vouna,
(and when you see mountains ahead,)
otan paleyeis stis trikymies
(when you fight the storms)
kai i kardia chtypaei trela...
(and the heart is beating madly...)
