642 PANTES OI LAOI

Greek Hymns - No. 642 | "ΠΑΝΤΕΣ ΟΙ ΛΑΟΙ"
Verse 1:
Pantes oi laoi krotisate cheiras,
(All the peoples clap your hands,)
kamete endoxon ton ymnon Aftoy!
(make His hymn glorious!)
Eis ta dexia tis megalosynis
(To the right of majesty)
einai o thronos tou aioniou vasilia!
(it is the throne of the eternal king!)
Verse 2:
Aftos kat' archas tin gin ethemeliose,
(He first founded the earth,)
erga ton cheiron Tou einai oi ouranoi!
(the works of His hands are the heavens!)
Aftos diamenei, aftoi apoloyntai
(He abides, they perish)
kai os imation palaiothisontai!
(and as a garment they grow old!)
Verse 3:
Aineite Afton en kymvalois efichois,
(Praise Him with loud cymbals,)
pasa pnoi as ymnei Ton Kyrion!
(let every breath praise the Lord!)
Aineite Afton en chordais kai organois,
(Praise Him with strings and organs,)
epeffimisate ton thriamvon Aftoy!
(cheer His triumph!)
Verse 4:
Idoy erchetai endedymenos doxan,
(Behold, he comes clothed in glory,)
opsontai Afton pasai ai fylai tis gis!
(All the tribes of the earth look at Him!)
To Pneyma ki i nymfi legousin erchou,
(The Spirit and the nymph say come,)
nai, erchou Kyrie, dikaie kriti!
(yes, come Lord, righteous judge!)
Verse 5:
To Pneyma ki i nymfi legousin erchou,
(The Spirit and the nymph say come,)
nai, erchou Kyrie, Eleftheroti!
(yes, come Lord, Liberator!)
To Pneyma ki i nymfi legousin erchou,
(The Spirit and the nymph say come,)
nai, erchou Kyrie, Eleftheroti!
(yes, come Lord, Liberator!)
