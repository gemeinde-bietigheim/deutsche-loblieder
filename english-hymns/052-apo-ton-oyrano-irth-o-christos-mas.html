52 APO TON OYRANO IRTh' O ChRISTOS MAS

Greek Hymns - No. 52 | "ΑΠΟ ΤΟΝ ΟΥΡΑΝΟ ΗΡΘ' Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ ΜΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Apo ton ourano irth' o Christos mas
(Our Christ came from heaven)
to aima Tou na chysei sto stafro
(His blood to be shed on the cross)
kai pethane sto xylo tis aischynis
(and died on the stake)
na vroyme ti zoi esy ki ego.
(to find life you and me)
Chorus:
Ti platia pou einai i agapi
(How wide is love)
pou edeixe gia mena o Christos!
(that Christ showed for me!)
Oso pio mikros eimai konta Tou,
(The smaller I am to Him,)
o, toso pio megalos ein' Aftos!
(oh, so much greater is He!)
Verse 2:
Adeiase olokliro ton Eafto Tou,
(He emptied His whole Self,)
to stemma tou afise to endoxo
(his crown left the glorious)
kai dechthike sto agio Tou kefali
(and accepted in His holy head)
t' agkathino stefani to skliro!
(the crown of thorns the hard!)
Verse 3:
Anevike xana pano sto throno
(He ascended the throne again)
kai tora echei doxa pio trani,
(and now he has more strange glory,)
giati den einai monos sta ourania,
(for he is not alone in heaven,)
einai ki oi lytromenoi Tou mazi!
(are also His redeemed together!)
