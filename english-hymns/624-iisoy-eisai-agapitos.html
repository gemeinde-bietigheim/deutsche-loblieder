624 IISOY, EISAI AGAPITOS

Greek Hymns - No. 624 | "ΙΗΣΟΥ, ΕΙΣΑΙ ΑΓΑΠΗΤΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Iisoy, eisai agapitos,
(jesus you are dear)
eisai o Nymfios o lampros!
(you are the Bridegroom the bright one!)
Eisai Sy «to rodo tou Saron»,
(You are "the rose of Sharon",)
o Vasilias tis doxas tis Sion!
(the King of Zion's glory!)
Verse 2:
I nymfi perimenei ekstatiki
(The nymph waits ecstatically)
gia 'keini ti thafmasia stigmi,
(for that wonderful moment,)
pou oi salpigges tha ichisoune mazi,
(where the trumpets shall sound together,)
tha antilalisoune pantoy sti gi!
(they will protest everywhere on earth!)
Verse 3:
Ti glykia Sou optasia perimenei i ekklisia,
(The church awaits Your sweet apparition,)
mechri tote mas gemizeis me prosdokia kai chara!
(until then you fill us with anticipation and joy!)
I thermi Sou parousia mas krataei syntrofia,
(Your warm presence keeps us company,)
Pneyma kai nymfi lene: «Ela, Vasilia!».
(Spirit and nymph say: "Come, King!")
