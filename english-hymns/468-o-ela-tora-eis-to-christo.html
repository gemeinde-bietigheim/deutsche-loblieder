468 O! ELA TORA EIS TO ChRISTO

Greek Hymns - No. 468 | "Ω! ΕΛΑ ΤΩΡΑ ΕΙΣ ΤΟ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ"
Verse 1:
O! Ela tora eis to Christo,
(Oh! Come now to Christ,)
amartole, nai, ela s' Afton!
(sinner, yes, come to Him!)
O, mi distazeis, o, min argeis,
(Oh don't hesitate, oh don't be late)
ela kai tha sotheis!
(come and you will be saved!)
Chorus:
Nai, tha se sosei o Christos,
(Yes, Christ will save you,)
gia sena pethane Aftos!
(for you He died!)
Tha se lytrosei, amartole,
(He will redeem you, sinner,)
tora efthys elthe!
(now come right away!)
Verse 2:
I amartia mi se plana,
(Don't let sin fool you,)
einai plektani tou Satana,
(it's satan's braid,)
gyrise ki ela eis to Christo,
(turn and come to Christ,)
«sose me» pes s' Afton!
("save me" say to Him!)
Verse 3:
Mes stin kardia sou tha ferei efthys
(It will bring straight into your heart)
chara ki eirini o Lytrotis!
(joy and peace the Redeemer!)
Tha fygoun fovoi, pikres, kaimoi,
(Fears, bitterness, sorrows will leave,)
th' anoixoun ouranoi!
(the heavens will open!)
Verse 4:
Tha erthei pali o Iisoys
(Jesus will come again)
na arpaxei olous tous christianoys
(to seize all Christians)
pou perimenoun, gia na Ton doun,
(who wait, to see Him,)
na Ton ypodechthoyn!
(to welcome Him!)
