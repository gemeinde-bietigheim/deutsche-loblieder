132 IMOYN AMARTOLOS, DEN EIChA POTE ChARA

Greek Hymns - No. 132 | "ΗΜΟΥΝ ΑΜΑΡΤΩΛΟΣ, ΔΕΝ ΕΙΧΑ ΠΟΤΕ ΧΑΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Imoun amartolos, den eicha pote chara
(I was a sinner, I never had joy)
kai i zoi mou esvine me marasmo.
(and my life withered away.)
Irthe o Iisoys kai me eleftherose,
(Jesus came and set me free,)
zoi aionia tora mou echarise!
(eternal life now you have given me!)
Chorus:
Me esose kai me eleftherose
(He saved me and set me free)
kai egin' o Christos Sotiras mou pistos, ti chara!
(and Christ became my faithful Savior, what a joy!)
Me esose kai me eleftherose,
(Save me and set me free,)
kainoyrgio ktisma o Christos me ekane!
(Christ made me a new building!)
Verse 2:
Mesa sti symfora kai mesa sti thlipsi mou
(In my calamity and in my sorrow)
irthe kai m' esose o Iisoys Christos!
(Jesus Christ came and saved me!)
Aftos mou milise kai me parigorise,
(He spoke to me and comforted me,)
mou edose chara mesa sti symfora!
(it gave me joy in the midst of calamity!)
Verse 3:
O, noiotho stin kardia chara pou de legetai,
(Oh, I feel in my heart joy unspeakable,)
kainoyrio anthropo me ekan' o Christos!
(Christ made me a new man!)
Doxazo to Theo, giati me agapise
(I praise God, because he loved me)
kai ti zoi mou entelos tin allaxe!
(and it completely changed my life!)
