281 TIN PENTIKOSTI IMERA

Greek Hymns - No. 281 | "ΤΗΝ ΠΕΝΤΗΚΟΣΤΗ ΗΜΕΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Tin Pentikosti imera meta tin Anastasi
(On the Pentecost day after the Resurrection)
epese to Agio Pneyma me megali dynami!
(the Holy Spirit fell with great power!)
Chorus:
Anastithike o Sotiras Iisoys kai Kyrios mas
(Our Savior Jesus and Lord is risen)
kai mas esteile to Pneyma! Doxa eis to onoma Aftoy!
(and sent us the Spirit! Glory to His name!)
Verse 2:
Eplirothikan me Pneyma tou Christoy oi apostoloi
(The apostles were filled with the Spirit of Christ)
kai elalisan se glosses, pou tous thaymazan polloi!
(and they spoke in tongues, which many admired!)
Verse 3:
Tis Pentikostis to Pneyma dinetai kai eis emas!
(On Pentecost the Spirit is given to us too!)
Ein' Theoy epaggelia dia tous pisteyontas!
(It is God's promise for those who believe!)
Verse 4:
Gony klinoume me thermi gia to Pneyma t' Agio!
(We bow down with fervor to the Holy Spirit!)
Perimenoume me pisti dia ton Paraklito!
(We wait with faith through the Advocate!)
Verse 5:
Kyrie, Se efcharistoyme gia ton Iisoy Christo!
(Lord, we thank You for Jesus Christ!)
Tora, Se parakaloyme, dos' to Pneyma t' Agio!
(Now, we beg You, give the Holy Spirit!)
