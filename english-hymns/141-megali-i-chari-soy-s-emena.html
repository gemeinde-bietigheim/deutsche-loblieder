141 MEGALI I ChARI SOY S' EMENA

Greek Hymns - No. 141 | "ΜΕΓΑΛΗ Η ΧΑΡΗ ΣΟΥ Σ' ΕΜΕΝΑ"
Verse 1:
Megali i chari Sou s' emena
(Great is Your grace to me)
pou de mporo na ti metro,
(which I cannot measure,)
gi' afto ta panta eis Esena,
(therefore everything to You,)
Christe mou, ta parachoro!
(Christ, I give it up!)
Chorus:
Afto pou ekanes, Thee mou,
(What you did, my God,)
Plasti mou kai Dimiourge,
(My Shaper and Maker,)
kanena ap' ta plasmata Sou
(none of Thy creatures)
de fantazotane pote!
(never imagined!)
Verse 2:
Gia tin vevaiosi pou niotho,
(For the confirmation I feel,)
valmeni stin kardia apo Se,
(put in the heart by You,)
de vrisko pos na efcharistiso
(I don't know how to thank you)
tin chari Sou, Iisoy Christe!
(Your grace, Jesus Christ!)
Verse 3:
Me tin aionia agapi
(With eternal love)
pou echeis, Iisoy, gia me,
(what do you have for me Jesus)
ela kai dioxe to skotadi
(come and banish the darkness)
ap' ti zoi mou, Thafmaste!
(of my life, Admirers!)
Verse 4:
Gi' afto ego klaio kai leo
(That's why I cry and say)
me syntrimmeni tin kardia,
(with a broken heart,)
Christe mou, ela, perimeno
(Christ, come, I'm waiting)
n' anapaftho mazi Sou pia.
(to rest with You now.)
Verse 5:
O! Doxa, doxa, alliloyia!
(Oh! Glory, glory, hallelujah!)
Oloi ymneite to Theo!
(All praise God!)
giati einai dikaio na lavei
(for it is fair to receive)
kathe proskynisi Aftos!
(every worship Him!)
