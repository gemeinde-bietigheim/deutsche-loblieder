542 PROS SE AGAPIS

Greek Hymns - No. 542 | "ΠΡΟΣ ΣΕ ΑΓΑΠΗΣ"
Verse 1:
Pros Se agapis theia pigi,
(To You divine source of love,)
pros Se pou eisai i storgi,
(to You whose affection is,)
pros Se ypsoyme prosefchi
(to You we raise prayer)
me tin kardia mas tapeini!
(with our humble hearts!)
Esy aftoys glyka kai storgika
(You them sweetly and lovingly)
na agkaliaseis, o Thee,
(embrace oh god)
kai o desmos
(and the bond)
tou gamou tis zois afton
(of the marriage of their lives)
as martyrei pantoy gia Se!
(let it testify everywhere about You!)
Verse 2:
Esy na eisai odigos s' aftoys,
(You be their guide,)
fos kai agapi sti zoi!
(light and love in life!)
Kai apo ta dora t' ouranoy
(And from the gifts of heaven)
s' aftoys Esy na chorigeis!
(You grant them!)
Esy ap' ton voria, otan fysa,
(You from the north, when it blows,)
na prostateyeis, o Thee,
(to protect, O God,)
kai stou spitioy
(and at home)
ti fatni na katoikeis
(the manger to dwell)
me tin agapi Sou, Christe!
(with Your love, Christ!)
