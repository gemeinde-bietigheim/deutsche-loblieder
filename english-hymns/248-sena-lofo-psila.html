248 S' Ena Lofo Psila

Greek Hymns - No. 248 | "On a Hill High"
Verse 1:
S' ena lofo psila stithike o stavros,
(On a hill high stood the cross,)
simeio aischynis kai aras.
(a sign of shame and curse.)
Kai epano s' afton ekremasth' o Christos,
(And upon it hung Christ,)
ne, dia na lytrosei ki emas!
(yes, to redeem us too!)
Chorus:
Th' agapo to stavro tou Christou,
(I will love the cross of Christ,)
tha vadizo kratontas Afton,
(I will walk holding it,)
kai sto telos anti gia stavro,
(and in the end instead of a cross,)
o, tha lavo stefani chryso!
(oh, I will receive a golden crown!)
Verse 2:
Eis afton to stavro vlepo aima Christou,
(On this cross I see Christ's blood,)
posi dynami kai ilasmos!
(such power and atonement!)
Dorean kai gia mas sotiria lampri
(Free and bright salvation for us)
proetoimase, ne, o Amnos!
(the Lamb prepared, yes!)
Verse 3:
O Stavros tou Christou pou o kosmos misei
(The Cross of Christ that the world hates)
gia eme einai toso glykys!
(for me is so sweet!)
Giati xero kala oti echo sothei
(Because I know well that I have been saved)
ki ein' elpida tis kathe psychis!
(and it is the hope of every soul!)
Verse 4:
Kline gony kai sy sto stavro tapeina
(Bow your knee humbly at the cross)
kai tha laveis afto pou potheis!
(and you will receive what you desire!)
O Christos yposchethike ki eipe rita,
(Christ promised and said clearly,)
otan ertheis s' Afton, tha sotheis!
(when you come to Him, you will be saved!)
