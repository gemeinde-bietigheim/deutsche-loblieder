234 IRThES ARAGE

Greek Hymns - No. 234 | "ΗΡΘΕΣ ΑΡΑΓΕ"
Verse 1:
Irthes arage kai sy eis to Christo,
(Have you also come to Christ,)
erantisthikes m' aima t' Amnoy;
(have you sprinkled yourself with the blood of the Lamb?)
Tin psychi sou edoses sto Lytroti
(You gave your soul to the Redeemer)
na sou dosei aionia zoi;
(give you eternal life?)
Chorus:
Erantistheis pote
(Are you ever satisfied?)
me to aima tou theiou Amnoy;
(with the blood of the divine Lamb?)
I psychi sou ara egine lefki
(Your soul therefore became white)
eis to aima Amnoy tou Theoy;
(in the blood of the Lamb of God?)
Verse 2:
Meneis ara pantote eis to Christo,
(You therefore always remain in Christ,)
erantisthikes m' aima t' Amnoy;
(have you sprinkled yourself with the blood of the Lamb?)
Vrikes tin eirini sy eis to Stafro,
(You found peace in the Cross,)
eisai eis tin odo t' ouranoy;
(are you on the way to heaven?)
Verse 3:
Otan o Nymfios erth' ex ouranoy,
(When the Bridegroom came from heaven,)
gia na parei ti nymfi Aftoy,
(to take His bride,)
i psychi sou arage tha efrethei
(will your soul be found)
kathari me to aima t' Amnoy;
(pure with the blood of the Lamb?)
Verse 4:
Fer' amesos ti ftochi sou tin kardia
(Bring your poor heart at once)
na plythei eis to aima t' Amnoy!
(to be washed in the blood of the Lamb!)
O Christos sikonei oli tin ara
(Christ raises the whole ara)
kai sou dinei ti niki pantoy!
(and gives you victory everywhere!)
