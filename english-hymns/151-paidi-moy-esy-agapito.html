151 PAIDI MOY, ESY, AGAPITO

Greek Hymns - No. 151 | "ΠΑΙΔΙ ΜΟΥ, ΕΣΥ, ΑΓΑΠΗΤΟ"
Verse 1:
Paidi mou, esy, agapito,
(My child, you, dear,)
pos efyges makria,
(how did you get away)
pos afises adiafora
(how did you leave indifferent)
ti storgiki mou agkalia;
(my loving embrace?)
Chorus:
Des tin kardia mou pos pona,
(See my heart ache)
ta cheria mou einai anoichta!
(my hands are open!)
O, se zito, se kartero
(Oh, I want you, I want you)
na 'rtheis, paidi mou agapito!
(come, my dear child!)
Verse 2:
Se exegelase o echthros
(The enemy has tricked you)
kai sou 'taxe polla,
(and he told you a lot,)
sou eipe pos tha s' ekane
(he told you what he would do to you)
s' olo ton kosmo vasilia!
(to all the world king!)
Verse 3:
Afoy ton akoloythises,
(After you followed him,)
sou pire ti chara
(it took away your joy)
kai fthonera sou karfose
(and jealously nailed you)
tou ponou ta krya karfia!
(of pain the cold nails!)
Verse 4:
Ta roycha sou exeskise,
(He tore your clothes,)
se pligose vathia,
(hurt you deeply)
kathe elpida sou esvise
(all your hope is gone)
ki o Adis pia se kartera!
(and Hades is now in your cards!)
