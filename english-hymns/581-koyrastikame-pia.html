581 KOYRASTIKAME PIA

Greek Hymns - No. 581 | "ΚΟΥΡΑΣΤΗΚΑΜΕ ΠΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Kourastikame pia na koimomaste, ftanei!
(We are tired of sleeping, that's enough!)
Mas epligos' o ponos vathia.
(The pain hit us deeply.)
Kapoios thriamvos tora san ilios provallei,
(Some triumph now like the sun projects,)
ta skotadia tou ypnou nika!
(the darkness of sleep wins!)
Chorus:
Me ta matia psila prochoroyme,
(With our eyes up we move forward,)
tou Christoy lytromeni genia,
(Christ's redeemed generation,)
pou sta stithia mas tora chtypaei
(which in our breasts now beats)
mia kainoyrgia, kainoyrgia kardia!
(a new, new heart!)
Verse 2:
Den yparchei allos tropos na zisoume, adelfia,
(There is no other way to live, brothers,)
apomenei o Christos monacha,
(Christ alone remains,)
pou mas fernei konta ston Ouranio Patera
(which brings us closer to the Heavenly Father)
kai mas kanei paidia Tou pista!
(and makes us His faithful children!)
Verse 3:
O, na mi mas gelaei to kivdilo gelio,
(Oh, don't let mock laughter laugh at us,)
pou mas dinei i pseytra idoni,
(that false pleasure gives us,)
mas to pairnei me toko poly kai me pono,
(he takes it from us with great interest and with pain,)
ki i psychi gelasmeni thrinei!
(and the laughing soul mourns!)
Verse 4:
O Christos mas perpatise mesa ston pono,
(Christ walked us through pain,)
mes stin adeia mas mpike kardia
(a heart entered our leave)
kai tin eplyne me to diko Tou to aima,
(and washed her with His own blood,)
gia na lampei ntymeni lefka!
(to shine dressed in white!)
