334 PANTA STO PLEYRO KONTA MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 334 | "ΠΑΝΤΑ ΣΤΟ ΠΛΕΥΡΟ ΚΟΝΤΑ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Panta sto plefro konta mou
(Always by my side)
thelo Esena, Iisoy,
(i want you jesus)
thelo mesa stin kardia mou
(i want in my heart)
na se noiotho, Iisoy!
(to feel you, Jesus!)
Chorus:
Otan eisai Sy konta mou,
(When you are near me,)
de fovamai, Iisoy,
(I'm not afraid, Jesus)
tipote ego de thelo
(I don't want anything)
sti zoi mou ektos Sou!
(in my life without You!)
Verse 2:
Sto krevati otan eimai
(In bed when I am)
ki ypofero asthenis,
(and I suffer sick,)
tote Sy konta mou eisai,
(then you are close to me,)
m' entharryneis, mou mileis!
(you encourage me, you talk to me!)
Verse 3:
Poso thelo, Kyrie mou,
(How I want, my Lord,)
eis to terma tis zois
(at the end of life)
prosopo Sou n' antikriso,
(to face your face,)
gia na eimai eftychis!
(to be happy!)
Verse 4:
Ki otan eltho ekei konta Sou
(And when I come there to You)
kai mazi Sou efretho,
(and with You I find myself,)
klinontas stin agkalia Sou,
(leaning in Your arms,)
de tha S' apochoristho!
(I will not part with you!)
