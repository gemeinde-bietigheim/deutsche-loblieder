269 O LYTROTIS ANESTI

Greek Hymns - No. 269 | "Ο ΛΥΤΡΩΤΗΣ ΑΝΕΣΤΗ"
Verse 1:
O Lytrotis anesti! Gnorizo oti zei!
(The Redeemer is risen! I know he lives!)
O,ti ki an pei o kosmos, s' afto echo peistei.
(Whatever people say, I am convinced of this.)
To cheri Tou to vlepo, akoyo ti foni,
(I see His hand, I hear His voice,)
Aftos se kath' anagki me voithei!
(He helps me when needed!)
Chorus:
Nai, zei, nai, zei o Iisoys Christos!
(Yes, Jesus Christ lives, yes, Jesus Christ lives!)
Peripatei kai omilei
(He walks and talks)
mazi mou sti zoi!
(with me in life!)
Nai, zei, nai, zei kai menei sti psychi,
(Yes, he lives, yes, he lives and lives in the soul,)
to Pneyma Tou to martyrei,
(His Spirit testifies to it,)
nai, zei o Lytrotis!
(yes, the Redeemer lives!)
Verse 2:
Gia men' Aftos frontizei me tin agapi Tou,
(For me He cares with His love,)
me tharros me gemizei, gia na mi ptoitho!
(it fills me with courage, so that I will not be dismayed!)
Mou dinei odigies se ora peirasmoy,
(He instructs me in time of temptation,)
m' Aftoy tin prostasia de deilio!
(I am not afraid of His protection!)
Verse 3:
Christiane, na chaireis, dose doxa s' Afton!
(Christian, rejoice, give glory to Him!)
Kai psalle «alliloyia» ston Iisoy Christo!
(And sing "hallelujah" to Jesus Christ!)
Dinei Aftos elpida, nika kathe echthro,
(He gives hope, defeats every enemy,)
mas ekane metochous ston ourano!
(made us shareholders in heaven!)
