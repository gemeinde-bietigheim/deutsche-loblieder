421 STA VOYNA PsILA

Greek Hymns - No. 421 | "ΣΤΑ ΒΟΥΝΑ ΨΗΛΑ"
Verse 1:
Sta vouna psila
(In the mountains high up)
agnanteyei i matia mou,
(my gaze gazes,)
psachno gia na do
(looking to see)
poios tha me lytrosei.
(who will redeem me)
Chorus:
O Thee, o Thee!
(Oh God, Oh God!)
I lytrosi apo sena tha 'rthei,
(Redemption from you will come,)
pou 'cheis plasei ta ourania kai ti gi!
(who made the heavens and the earth!)
Verse 2:
O Sotiras mou
(My Savior)
pote de tha nystaxei,
(never sleepy)
kai to podi mou
(and my leg)
de tha vrei na skontapsei!
(he will not stumble!)
Verse 3:
Stin psychri nychtia
(In the cold night)
astro de tha me vlapsei
(star won't hurt me)
kai sto feggisma
(and in the flashing)
ilios de tha me kapsei!
(the sun won't burn me!)
Verse 4:
Kathe diava mou
(Go ahead)
stis zois ton agona
(in life the struggle)
Sy, Thee, fylas
(You, God, protect)
tora kai ston aiona!
(now and forever!)
