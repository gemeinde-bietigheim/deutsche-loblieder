258 O, AN ISOYN EKEI STO GOLGOThA

Greek Hymns - No. 258 | "Ω, ΑΝ ΗΣΟΥΝ ΕΚΕΙ ΣΤΟ ΓΟΛΓΟΘΑ"
Verse 1:
O! An isoun ekei sto Golgotha,
(Oh! If you were there at Calvary,)
na 'vlepes to Christo pou stayrosan...
(to see the crucified Christ...)
O! Mono na to skefteis, tha tremeis... tremeis... tremeis!
(Oh! Just thinking about it will make you shudder... shudder... shudder!)
O! An isoun ekei sto Golgotha!
(Oh! If you were there at Calvary!)
Verse 2:
O! Ekei na 'soun, pou Ton karfosan...
(Oh! There you were, where they nailed Him...)
O! nai, 'kei na 'soun, pou Ton trypisan...
(Oh! yes, there you are, who pierced Him...)
O! An to skefteis vathia, tha tremeis... tremeis... tremeis!
(Oh! If you think about it deeply, you will tremble... tremble... tremble!)
O! Ekei na soun, pou Ton karfosan!
(Oh! There you were, where they nailed Him!)
Verse 3:
O! Ekei na 'soun, pou Ton logchisan...
(Oh! Be there, where they stabbed Him...)
O! nai, 'kei na 'soun, pou Ton logchisan...
(Oh! yes, there you are, who stabbed Him...)
O! San to skefteis vathia, pos tremeis... tremeis... tremeis!
(Oh! When you think about it deeply, how you tremble... you tremble... you tremble!)
O! ekei na 'soun, pou Ton logchisan!
(Oh! there you were, who stabbed Him!)
Verse 4:
O! Ekei ki an den eides to Christo,
(Oh! Even if you didn't see Christ there,)
stafromenos gia se ein' edo!
(crucified for you is here!)
O! Tosa ypefere gia sena! Ela! Ela!
(Oh! He suffered so much for you! Come! Come!)
Tora edo karterei gia sena!
(Now here it is for you!)
