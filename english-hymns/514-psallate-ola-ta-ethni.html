514 PsALLATE OLA TA EThNI

Greek Hymns - No. 514 | "ΨΑΛΛΑΤΕ ΟΛΑ ΤΑ ΕΘΝΗ"
Verse 1:
Psallate ola ta ethni
(Sing all nations)
tin agapi tou Christoy,
(the love of Christ,)
gia ti synaxi mas olon
(for our gathering of all)
ston paradeiso Aftoy!
(in His heaven!)
Chorus:
Nai, ekei tha anevoyme
(Yes, that's where we'll go up)
me chara ston oraio ourano!
(with joy in the beautiful sky!)
Kai ekei tha zoyme
(And there we will live)
me to Sotira mas Christo!
(with our Savior Christ!)
Verse 2:
Oloi tha peripatoyme
(We will all walk)
eis ta ployti t' ouranoy,
(to the riches of heaven,)
sti chara tou paradeisou
(in the joy of heaven)
me ton stefanon Aftoy!
(with His crown!)
Verse 3:
Oloi sto Christo kaleisthe
(All are called to Christ)
stin odo tou ouranoy,
(on the way to heaven)
pou anoichthike se olous
(which was opened to all)
me to aima tou Christoy!
(with the blood of Christ!)
Verse 4:
Echete elpida oloi
(Have hope everyone)
tou Christoy oi zilotes,
(of Christ the zealous,)
mia pisti kai elpida
(a faith and hope)
kai tha eisthe nikites!
(and you will be winners!)
