453 O ChRISTOS AMARTOLOYS

Greek Hymns - No. 453 | "Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ ΑΜΑΡΤΩΛΟΥΣ"
Verse 1:
O Christos amartoloys se metanoia kalei,
(Christ calls sinners to repentance,)
oti esose polloys pantachoy as kirychthei.
(that he saved many let it be proclaimed everywhere.)
Chorus:
Psalle, gi kai ourane, o oiktirmon Iisoys
(Sing, earth and heaven, gracious Jesus)
diakiryxe pantoy dechetai amartoloys!
(proclaim everywhere accept sinners!)
Verse 2:
O Christos se proskalei, empisteysou eis Afton.
(Christ invites you, trust in Him.)
Dinei eirini stin psychi, ela tora eis Afton!
(He gives peace to the soul, come now to Him!)
Verse 3:
O Christos to parelthon olo sou to sygchorei,
(Christ forgives all your past,)
karfose eis to stafro oli sou tin enochi!
(nail all your guilt to the cross!)
Verse 4:
Ase kathe distagmo tora pou se proskalei,
(Leave all hesitation now that he invites you,)
nikise ton peirasmo, ela kai se sygchorei!
(overcome temptation, come and he forgives you!)
