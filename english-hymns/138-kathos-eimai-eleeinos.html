138 KAThOS EIMAI ELEEINOS

Greek Hymns - No. 138 | "ΚΑΘΩΣ ΕΙΜΑΙ ΕΛΕΕΙΝΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Kathos eimai eleeinos, athlios kai amartolos,
(As I am pitiful, wretched, and sinful,)
se Se elpizo tapeina kai erchomai, Amne Theoy!
(to You I humbly hope and come, Amne God!)
Verse 2:
Choris alli anavoli i enochi n' apaleifthei
(Without further adjournment let the guilt be removed)
psychis mou tis amartolis proserchomai, Amne Theoy!
(my sinful soul I come, Amne God!)
Verse 3:
Afoy ypemeines stafro, gia na me soseis ton deinon,
(After you endured the cross, to save me from suffering,)
ton athlio kai ryparo, proserchomai, Amne Theoy!
(the wretched and filthy, I come, Amne God!)
Verse 4:
Eflogimeno ki eftychi eme ton toso dystychi
(Blessed and happy with him so unhappy)
gia na me kaneis tora Sy, proserchomai, Amne Theoy!
(to make me now You, I come, Amne God!)
