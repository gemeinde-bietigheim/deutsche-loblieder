241 O ChRISTOS ILThE GIA ME

Greek Hymns - No. 241 | "Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ ΗΛΘΕ ΓΙΑ ΜΕ"
Verse 1:
O Christos ilthe gia me ston kosmo afton edo,
(Christ came for me in this world here,)
pethane gia mena epano sto skliro stafro!
(He died for me on the cruel cross!)
Edose to soma Tou, echyse to aima Tou
(He gave His body, shed His blood)
kai Aftos ypefere na sotho ego!
(and He suffered that I might be saved!)
Edose to soma Tou, echyse to aima Tou
(He gave His body, shed His blood)
kai Aftos ypefere na sotho ego!
(and He suffered that I might be saved!)
Verse 2:
Stala stala etrexe to aima Tou sti gi
(Drop by drop His blood ran on the earth)
kai afto itan to timima gia se psychi!
(and that was the price for your soul!)
Plirose o Iisoys ti diki sou tin poini,
(Jesus paid your penalty,)
na sotheis ap' to chamo, na sotheis esy!
(save yourself from destruction, save yourself!)
Plirose o Iisoys ti diki sou tin poini,
(Jesus paid your penalty,)
na sotheis ap' to chamo, na sotheis esy!
(save yourself from destruction, save yourself!)
Verse 3:
Ti potami einai afto pou trechei dynata,
(What river is that which runs fast,)
parasernei stin ormi tou ola ta sapra.
(it drags away all the sapras in its momentum.)
Katharizei ti zoi, sou agiazei tin psychi.
(It purifies life, sanctifies your soul.)
Nai, to aima tou Christoy einai dynato!
(Yes, the blood of Christ is powerful!)
Katharizei ti zoi, sou agiazei tin psychi.
(It purifies life, sanctifies your soul.)
Nai, to aima tou Christoy einai dynato!
(Yes, the blood of Christ is powerful!)
Verse 4:
To chrysafi olou tou kosmou den arkei, Thee,
(All the gold in the world is not enough, God,)
na plirosei ti thysia Sou afti gia me!
(to pay Thy sacrifice for me!)
Sou prosfero tin kardia, sou prosfero tin psychi,
(I offer you my heart, I offer you my soul,)
o,ti pio polytimo echo sti zoi!
(the most precious thing I have in life!)
Sou prosfero tin kardia, sou prosfero tin psychi,
(I offer you my heart, I offer you my soul,)
o,ti pio polytimo echo sti zoi!
(the most precious thing I have in life!)
