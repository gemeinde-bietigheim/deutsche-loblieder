603 AN ESY DE M' AGAPOYSES

Greek Hymns - No. 603 | "ΑΝ ΕΣΥ ΔΕ Μ' ΑΓΑΠΟΥΣΕΣ"
Verse 1:
An Esy de m' agapoyses,
(If you didn't love me,)
poion mporoysa n' agapo;
(who could i love)
Kyrie mou, an den isoun,
(My lord, if you were not)
poy tha imouna ego;
(where would i be)
Chorus:
S' efcharisto, Iisoy Christe,
(Thank you, Jesus Christ,)
S' efcharisto, S' efcharisto!
(Thank you, thank you!)
S' efcharisto, Iisoy Christe,
(Thank you, Jesus Christ,)
S' efcharisto, S' efcharisto!
(Thank you, thank you!)
Verse 2:
An Esy de mou 'ches dosei,
(If You have not given me,)
poso tha 'mouna ftochos!
(how poor I would be!)
Kyrie, an den erchosoun,
(Sir, if you didn't come,)
tha 'moun panta monachos!
(I will always be a monk!)
Verse 3:
An Esena den akoyso,
(If I don't hear You,)
echo thlipsi stin kardia!
(I have sorrow in my heart!)
An s' Esena den koitaxo,
(If I don't look to You,)
svinei oli i chara!
(all the joy fades away!)
Verse 4:
An Esy de mas gemizeis,
(If You do not fill us,)
poso tha 'maste adeianoi!
(how empty we will be!)
An Esy de mas kratiseis,
(If You don't keep us,)
Kyrie mas, poia i zoi;
(Our Lord, what is life?)
