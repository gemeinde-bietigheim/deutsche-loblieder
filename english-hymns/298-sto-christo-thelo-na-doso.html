298 STO ChRISTO ThELO NA DOSO

Greek Hymns - No. 298 | "ΣΤΟ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ ΘΕΛΩ ΝΑ ΔΩΣΩ"
Verse 1:
Sto Christo thelo na doso olo mou ton eafto.
(I want to give my whole self to Christ.)
Sy voithise me, Thee mou, na 'mai pistos!
(Help me, my God, to be faithful!)
Sy voithise me, Thee mou, na 'mai pistos!
(Help me, my God, to be faithful!)
Verse 2:
Na adeiaseis tin kardia mou apo kathe kosmiko
(Empty my heart of all worldliness)
kai agapi na mou doseis gia to Christo!
(and love to give me for Christ's sake!)
kai agapi na mou doseis gia to Christo!
(and love to give me for Christ's sake!)
Verse 3:
Pare olo to ego mou, Kyrie mou Iisoy,
(Take all my ego, my Lord Jesus,)
na gemizei tin kardia mou i Chari Sou!
(may Your Grace fill my heart!)
na gemizei tin kardia mou i Chari Sou!
(may Your Grace fill my heart!)
Verse 4:
Doxa, doxa, alliloyia, niki dinei o Christos,
(Glory, glory, hallelujah, victory to Christ,)
Sotiria ston kathena charizei Aftos!
(He gives salvation to everyone!)
Sotiria ston kathena charizei Aftos!
(He gives salvation to everyone!)
