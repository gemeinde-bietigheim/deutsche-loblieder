59 O ThEOS MAS EIN' AGAPI

Greek Hymns - No. 59 | "Ο ΘΕΟΣ ΜΑΣ ΕΙΝ' ΑΓΑΠΗ"
Verse 1:
O Theos mas ein' agapi, akoue, psychi,
(Our God is love, listen, soul,)
ki opoios sti kardia tin echei, einai eftychis!
(and whoever has it in his heart is happy!)
Ela tora ki exetasou, an echeis aftin,
(Come now and examine, if you have her,)
zitise tin meta zilou kai tha eflogitheis!
(ask for it after zeal and you will be blessed!)
Verse 2:
O! mi ameleis katholou, min argoporeis,
(Oh! do not neglect at all, do not be late,)
einai pragma anagkaio, gia na afxitheis!
(it is a necessary thing, in order to grow!)
O! agapi thafmasia, pou 'soses kai emas!
(Oh! love wonder, who saved us too!)
O karpos sou ein' thysia eis to Golgotha.
(Your fruit is a sacrifice on Calvary.)
Verse 3:
I agapi ein' i vasi kathenos pistoy,
(Love is the basis of every believer,)
me afti tha prochorisei sto taxidi tou.
(with her he will proceed on his journey.)
I agapi mas gemizei ti zoi karpo,
(Our love fills our life with fruit,)
tin agnizei, tin ypsonei, eis ton ourano.
(he purifies her, raises her to heaven.)
Verse 4:
Opou vasileyei agapi, ein' ola kala
(Where love reigns, all is well)
kai ta erga mas ta kanei panta kathara.
(and he always makes our projects clean.)
I psychi mas zontaneyei, martyrei kala
(Our soul comes to life, it testifies well)
kai doxazei kai latreyei to Sotira mas.
(and glorifies and adores our Savior.)
