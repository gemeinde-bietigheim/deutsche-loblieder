414 PANTA FERO TON STAYRO MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 414 | "ΠΑΝΤΑ ΦΕΡΩ ΤΟΝ ΣΤΑΥΡΟ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Panta fero ton stafro mou
(I always carry my cross)
me chara, o Iisoy,
(with joy, O Jesus,)
eythyne Sy tin odo mou,
(direct my way,)
krata me plision Sou!
(keep me close to You!)
Chari Sou ola t' afino,
(Thanks to You I leave everything,)
ginomai ftochos, gymnos,
(I become poor, naked,)
chari Sou ton kosmo krino
(thanks to You I judge the world)
mataiotitas atmo!
(futility steam!)
Verse 2:
Ton anthropon i kardia
(People's hearts)
kai ta cheili apatoyn,
(and lips are deceiving,)
plin elpida echo mia,
(but hope I have one,)
ton pisto mou Iisoy!
(my faithful Jesus!)
An o kosmos me afisei,
(If the world leaves me,)
ton Sotira afise,
(he left the savior,)
an o kosmos me misisei
(if people hate me)
kai Afton emisise!
(and he hated Him!)
Verse 3:
Eno Sy, Thee ischyos,
(While You, God of power,)
epivlepeis eis eme,
(you watch over me)
den tarazomai teleios,
(I'm completely unfazed,)
empisteyomai eis Se!
(I trust in You!)
Ki otan eimai se kindynous,
(And when I'm in danger,)
echo pantote chara,
(I'm always happy)
strefeis prosopo aktinon
(you turn ray face)
kai fotizeis tin kardia!
(and you light up the heart!)
