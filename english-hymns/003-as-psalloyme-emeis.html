3 AS PsALLOYME EMEIS

Greek Hymns - No. 3 | "ΑΣ ΨΑΛΛΟΥΜΕ ΕΜΕΙΣ"
Verse 1:
As psalloume emeis ston Plasti Theo,
(Let us sing to the Maker God,)
latreia, timi anikoun s' Afton!
(worship, honor belong to Him!)
Me ymnous kai ainous, psychis mas karpo,
(With hymns and praises, fruit of our soul,)
prosferoume oloi ti doxa s' Afton!
(we all give Him the glory!)
Verse 2:
Gia panta se Se anikei timi,
(Forever to You belongs honor,)
agapi thermi kai pantotini!
(warm and everlasting love!)
Ti doxa Sou psalloun stereoma, gi
(They sing your glory firmament, earth)
kai oli i ktisi Ese anymnei!
(and all creation sings to You!)
Verse 3:
Gia kathe psychi Theos merimna,
(For every soul God cares,)
ta krina kosmei kai trefei ptina!
(he adorns the lilies and feeds the birds!)
Pistoys kai echthroys Tou frontizei sti gi,
(He takes care of His faithful and enemies on earth,)
O, vathos sofias! O, theia storgi!
(Oh, depth of wisdom! Oh, divine affection!)
Verse 4:
Sy dexou apo mas thysia agni
(Accept from us a pure sacrifice)
ki apantise Sy kathe prosefchi!
(and You answered every prayer!)
Christe, Lytroti mas, Thee ton piston,
(Christ, our Redeemer, God of the faithful,)
Sy mas prostateyeis apo ton echthro!
(You protect us from the enemy!)
