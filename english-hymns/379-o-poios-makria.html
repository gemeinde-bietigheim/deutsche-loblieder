379 O! POIOS MAKRIA

Greek Hymns - No. 379 | "Ω! ΠΟΙΟΣ ΜΑΚΡΙΑ"
Verse 1:
O! Poios makria, sta vathi tis agnoias,
(Oh! Who far, in the depths of ignorance,)
opou psyches vythizontai frikta,
(where souls sink horribly,)
san aggelos tha trexei sotirias,
(like an angel he will run to salvation,)
na ergasthei gia to Christo pista;
(to work for Christ faithfully?)
Chorus:
Tin exousia o Christos
(Christ is the authority)
sou dinei legontas rita:
(gives you by saying explicitly:)
«Porefthentes mathiteysate ta ethni
("You went and taught the nations)
kai ego tha eimai mazi sas».
(and I will be with you.")
Verse 2:
Apeira plithi tora mas fonazoun,
(Infinite multitudes now call to us,)
oplites tou Christoy as trexoume,
(soldiers of Christ let us run,)
amesos o Sotiras tha voithisei
(immediately the Savior will help)
tis planis ta desma na lysoume.
(of delusion the bonds to untie.)
Verse 3:
Giati amartoloi n' apolesthoyne,
(Why should sinners perish,)
afou o Lytrotis aftoys kalei;
(since the Redeemer calls them?)
Nai, o Christos plironontas ta lytra
(Yes, Christ paying the ransom)
parechei sotiria stin psychi!
(provides salvation to the soul!)
Verse 4:
Tachyne, Kyrie, Sy tin imera,
(Haste, O Lord, the day,)
otan oi pantes «Doxa sto Theo!»
(when everyone "Glory to God!")
tha psalloune, dechthentes sotiria
(they will sing, having received salvation)
dia tis pisteos eis to Christo!
(through faith in Christ!)
