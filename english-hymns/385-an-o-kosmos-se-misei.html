385 AN O KOSMOS SE MISEI

Greek Hymns - No. 385 | "ΑΝ Ο ΚΟΣΜΟΣ ΣΕ ΜΙΣΕΙ"
Verse 1:
An o kosmos se misei, stefanos, chara, timi
(If the world hates you, crown, joy, honor)
se anamenei, mi fovoy meine pistos!
(he is waiting for you, don't be afraid stay faithful!)
Na thymasai o Christos pos yposchetai pistos
(Remember that Christ promises faithfully)
na 'nai pantote konta sou diarkos!
(may he always be near you all the time!)
Chorus:
Nai, ekei... Nai, ekei...
(Yes, there... Yes, there...)
Fere panta sto stafro, nai, ekei!
(Always bring to the cross, yes, there!)
Me ti pisti sou, Aftos, tha se vgalei nikiti,
(By your faith, He will make you victorious,)
n' anatheseis ola eis to Lytroti!
(entrust everything to the Redeemer!)
Verse 2:
Eis tin ora ton machon, mi fovasai ton echthro,
(In the time of battle, do not fear the enemy,)
na thymasai to Christo eis to stafro,
(remember Christ on the cross,)
ton echthro enikise, s' elyse ap' ta desma,
(defeated the enemy, freed you from the bonds,)
gia na eisai sti zoi pantotina!
(to be alive forever!)
Verse 3:
Otan thlipseis eis ese elthoun kapote, piste,
(When sorrows come to you sometime, believe me,)
o Christos tha kamei ekvasi efthys,
(Christ will come forth immediately,)
dia toyto min argeis, trexe na prosefchitheis,
(therefore do not delay, run to pray,)
eis Afton ta panta na empisteftheis!
(entrust everything to Him!)
Verse 4:
Otan erthoun peirasmoi, thlipseis kai oneidismoi
(When temptations, sorrows and insults come)
ki o stafros sou fainetai poly varys,
(and your cross seems very heavy,)
tote eis ton Iisoy trexe, file, parefthys,
(then to Jesus run, friend, at once,)
ti chara kai tin eirini sou tha vreis!
(you will find your joy and peace!)
