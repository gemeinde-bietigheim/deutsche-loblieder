646 POIOS EIN' AYTOS

Greek Hymns - No. 646 | "ΠΟΙΟΣ ΕΙΝ' ΑΥΤΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Poios ein' Aftos pou stekei epano
(Who is He that stands above)
s' afto to lofo to gymno;
(on this hill the naked?)
Ta cheria tou einai karfomena
(His hands are nailed)
pano s' ena skliro stafro!
(on a hard cross!)
To prosopo Tou matomeno,
(His face bloody,)
ma kai to soma Tou gymno!
(but also His body naked!)
Kai ap' tin plefra ti logchismeni
(And from the speared side)
to aima trechei kai nero!
(the blood flows and water!)
Verse 2:
Ein' i thysia tou Patera!
(It is the Father's sacrifice!)
Einai o amomos Amnos!
(He is the innocent Lamb!)
Ein' i alitheia tis agapis!
(It's the truth of love!)
Einai o Iisoys Christos!
(It is Jesus Christ!)
Verse 3:
Poios einai Aftos pou ein' ston tafo,
(Who is He that is in the grave,)
ki exo stratiotes Ton frouroyn;
(and outside are soldiers guarding Him?)
Ki oi myrofores oi gynaikes,
(And the myrrh-bearing women,)
prin na charaxei xekinoyn.
(before they start to carve.)
Vriskoun ton tafo anoigmeno
(They find the tomb open)
ki o Iisoys den einai ekei!
(and Jesus is not there!)
Ta roycha Tou einai diplomena,
(His clothes are folded,)
ki aggeloi stekontai ekei!
(and angels stand there!)
Verse 4:
Ma ti zitate gia na vreite,
(But what do you ask to find,)
to zonta mesa stous nekroys;
(the living among the dead?)
Den ein' edo all' anestithi!
(He is not here, but he is risen!)
Tora tha zei stous ouranoys!
(Now he will live in heaven!)
