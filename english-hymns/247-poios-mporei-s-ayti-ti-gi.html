247 POIOS MPOREI S' AYTI TI GI

Greek Hymns - No. 247 | "ΠΟΙΟΣ ΜΠΟΡΕΙ Σ' ΑΥΤΗ ΤΗ ΓΗ"
Verse 1:
Poios mporei s' aftin ti gi
(Who on this earth can)
to tis amartias stigma
(the mark of sin)
na exaleipsei entelos;
(to eliminate completely?)
Mono tou Christoy to aima!
(Only Christ's blood!)
Chorus:
O, poso eftychis,
(oh how happy)
opoios echei sothei!
(who is saved!)
De sozei allos kaneis,
(No one else saves,)
mono tou Christoy to aima!
(only Christ's blood!)
Verse 2:
Poios mporei ston kosm' afto
(Who in the world can)
to dieftharmeno pneyma
(the corrupt spirit)
na allaxei entelos;
(to change completely?)
Mono tou Christoy to aima!
(Only Christ's blood!)
Verse 3:
Poios mporei ston ourano
(Who can in heaven)
to tis katadikis gramma
(the letter of condemnation)
na to svisei entelos;
(delete it completely?)
Mono tou Christoy to aima!
(Only Christ's blood!)
Verse 4:
Poios mporei eis tin psychi
(Who can in the soul)
to tis sotirias thayma
(the miracle of salvation)
na to kanei entelos;
(do it completely?)
Mono tou Christoy to aima!
(Only Christ's blood!)
