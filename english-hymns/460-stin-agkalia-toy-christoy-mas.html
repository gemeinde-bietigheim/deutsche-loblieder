460 STIN AGKALIA TOY ChRISTOY MAS

Greek Hymns - No. 460 | "ΣΤΗΝ ΑΓΚΑΛΙΑ ΤΟΥ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΥ ΜΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Stin agkalia tou Christoy mas vrika parigoria!
(In the arms of our Christ I found consolation!)
Ki otan Aftos m' agkaliazei, o, noiotho sigouria!
(And when He embraces me, oh, I feel confident!)
Eimai paidi Tou, to xero, aniko eis Afton!
(I am His child, I know it, I belong to Him!)
M' echei lytrosei o Christos mou kai eimai
(My Christ has redeemed me and I am)
paidi Tou, doxa eis Afton!
(His child, glory to Him!)
Chorus:
O! Ti oraia! O! Ti lampra ekei!
(Oh! How beautiful! Oh! How brilliant there!)
Oloi parea ston ourano mazi!
(All hang out in the sky together!)
Chara aionia pou mas prosmenei psila,
(Joy eternal that awaits us above,)
ela ki esy, file, tora diki sou
(come too, friend, now yours)
i ora ki esy na sotheis!
(time for you to be saved too!)
Verse 2:
Chara kai aionia elpida charizei o Christos!
(Christ gives joy and eternal hope!)
Ap' to skotadi se vgazei kai sou charizei fos!
(He takes you out of the darkness and gives you light!)
Pairnei ta vari Ekeinos kai mesa stin psychi
(He takes the burdens in the soul as well)
eleftheria sou dinei, se kanei
(it gives you freedom, it makes you)
na eisai st' alitheia eftychis!
(be truly happy!)
Verse 3:
O! Ti oraia na eisai paidi tou Iisoy!
(Oh! How good it is to be a child of Jesus!)
Tote mazi Tou tha chaireis ekei stous ouranoys!
(Then with Him you will rejoice there in the heavens!)
Otan ekei oi sosmenoi mazi Tou sti Sion
(When there the saved with Him in Zion)
tha Ton doxazoun kai oloi tous tha 'choun
(they will glorify Him and all of them will have it)
stefani aionio, lampro!
(wreath eternal, brilliant!)
