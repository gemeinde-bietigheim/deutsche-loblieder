499 PERA APO TON ILIO

Greek Hymns - No. 499 | "ΠΕΡΑ ΑΠΟ ΤΟΝ ΗΛΙΟ"
Verse 1:
Pera apo ton ilio, stin aionia chora,
(Beyond the sun, in the eternal land,)
stou ouranoy ti doxa tha vretho ekei!
(in heaven the glory will be there!)
Doxa aionia tha einai gia mena,
(Glory forever be to me,)
san vretho m' agious sti chrysi akti!
(as if I were with saints on the golden coast!)
Chorus:
Sti chrysi akti, t' ouranoy ekei,
(On the golden shore, the sky there,)
echo ki ego ena spiti lampro!
(I have a brilliant house too!)
Sti chrysi akti!
(On the gold coast!)
Sti chrysi akti, t' ouranoy ekei,
(On the golden shore, the sky there,)
echo ki ego ena spiti lampro,
(I have a bright house too,)
sti chrysi akti!
(on the gold coast!)
Verse 2:
Noiotho kourasmenos ki apokamomenos
(I feel tired and worn out)
ki o Iisoys mou tora me kalei ekei!
(and my Jesus now calls me there!)
Tha me anapaysei mes stin agkalia Tou
(He will rest me in His arms)
kai tha m' odigisei sti chrysi akti!
(and it will lead me to the golden coast!)
Verse 3:
To skotadi feygei, i afgi charazei,
(The darkness leaves, the dawn breaks,)
vlepo to Christo mou na me proskalei!
(I see my Christ inviting me!)
Se kalei kai sena, akou ti foni Tou
(He is calling you too, listen to His voice)
ki ela eis ti doxa t' ouranoy ekei!
(and come to the glory of heaven there!)
