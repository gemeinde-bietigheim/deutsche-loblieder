178 O, TI AGGELMA GLYKO!

Greek Hymns - No. 178 | "Ω, ΤΙ ΑΓΓΕΛΜΑ ΓΛΥΚΟ!"
Verse 1:
O, ti aggelma glyko! Alliloyia!
(Oh, what a sweet message! Alleluia!)
Irtha ston Iisoy Christo, Alliloyia!
(I have come to Jesus Christ, Hallelujah!)
Mou allaxe oli ti zoi
(It changed my whole life)
me ti chari Tou ki ischy
(with His grace and power)
kai me ekan' eftychi, Alliloyia!
(and it made me happy, Hallelujah!)
Chorus:
Alliloyia, m' esose, doxa, doxa, m' esose!
(Hallelujah, save me, glory, glory, save me!)
Prochoro eis tin odo, chairomai nychthimeron,
(I go on the road, I rejoice day and night,)
eimai tekno Tou pisto, Alliloyia!
(I am His faithful child, Hallelujah!)
Verse 2:
Me to aima Tou o Christos, Alliloyia!
(With His blood Christ, Hallelujah!)
Me katharis' entelos, Alliloyia!
(Cleanse me completely, Hallelujah!)
Ekentithi stin plefra
(Stretched to the side)
na me sosei ap' ta desma.
(to save me from the shackles.)
O, poso me agapa! Alliloyia!
(Oh, how he loves me! Alleluia!)
Verse 3:
Vrika filo to Christo, Alliloyia!
(I found a friend in Christ, Hallelujah!)
Nai, Afton tha empisteftho, Alliloyia!
(Yes, in Him I will trust, Hallelujah!)
Thelo na prosefchitho,
(I want to pray)
me chara panta na zo,
(with joy always to live,)
me tharros na prochoro, Alliloyia!
(with courage to go forth, Hallelujah!)
