370 PIRAME STA ChERIA

Greek Hymns - No. 370 | "ΠΗΡΑΜΕ ΣΤΑ ΧΕΡΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Pirame sta cheria tou Theoy to Logo,
(We took the Word into God's hands,)
sto megalo agro Tou pame gia spora!
(to His great field we are going to sow!)
Mera, nychta pame, richnoume to sporo,
(Day and night we go, we cast the seed,)
me glykia elpida kai kryfi chara!
(with sweet hope and secret joy!)
Chorus:
Tha 'rthei epochi gia to therismo
(It will be harvest time)
kai tha doyme stachya m' orimo karpo!
(and we will see ears of corn with ripe fruit!)
Me cheirovola ton psychon polla,
(With lots of soul sleights of hand,)
chairontes tha 'rthoyme me cheirovola!
(rejoicing we will come with handguns!)
Verse 2:
Spernoume se ayra, spernoume se mpora,
(We sow in the breeze, we sow in the rain,)
de mas foverizoun mayres synnefies!
(we are not intimidated by dark clouds!)
Ki otan i douleia mas kapote teleiosei,
(And when our work is once done,)
tha gyrisoum' oloi me cheirovola!
(we will all return with handguns!)
Verse 3:
Klaime kai poname gia psyches chamenes
(We cry and grieve for lost souls)
kai me varos panta pame sti spora!
(and we always go to sowing with weight!)
Ma ta dakrya mas kapote tha paysoun,
(But our tears will one day stop,)
san tha 'rthoyme piso me cheirovola!
(like we'll come back with handguns!)
