222 POSO SYNTOMOS EINAI O KAIROS

Greek Hymns - No. 222 | "ΠΟΣΟ ΣΥΝΤΟΜΟΣ ΕΙΝΑΙ Ο ΚΑΙΡΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Poso syntomos einai o kairos
(How short is the time)
eis tin proskairi oikia,
(in the temporary home,)
prepei o pistos na zitei thermos
(the believer must fervently ask)
tou Christoy tin eflogia!
(of Christ the blessing!)
Chorus:
Th' antallaxo, nai, tote to stafro
(I'll exchange, yes, then the cross)
me to stefano tis doxis!
(with the crown of glory!)
Tha me thelgei, nai, kath' ypervolin
(It will attract me, yes, in excess)
tou Sotira mou i opsi!
(the face of my Savior!)
Verse 2:
Lypes, dakrya, thlipseis, peirasmoi
(Sorrows, tears, sorrows, temptations)
ki anarithmit' enochliseis
(and innumerable annoyances)
tha perasoun, nai, kai oi diogmoi
(yes, the persecutions will pass)
kai oi toses anamniseis!
(and so many memories!)
Verse 3:
Einai dyskolos o agonas mas
(Our fight is difficult)
kai o dromos akanthodis,
(and the road thorny,)
alla omorfi i zoi ekei
(but life there is beautiful)
endoxi kai ideodis!
(glorious and ideal!)
Verse 4:
Endoxi chara pou tha aisthantho,
(Glorious joy that I shall feel,)
otan 'keini tin imera
(when that day)
ton Iisoy Christo tha axiotho
(Jesus Christ I will claim)
n' apantiso ston aera!
(to answer in the air!)
