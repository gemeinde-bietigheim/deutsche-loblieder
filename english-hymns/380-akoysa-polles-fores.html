380 AKOYSA POLLES FORES

Greek Hymns - No. 380 | "ΑΚΟΥΣΑ ΠΟΛΛΕΣ ΦΟΡΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Akousa polles fores ton kosmo na rota:
(I have often heard people ask:)
«Pou einai pia ta thaymata pou ginontan palia;
("Where are the miracles that used to be done?)
I dynami ti egine, emikryne o Theos;
(What happened to the power, did God shrink?)
M' akoyei san proseychomai i allaxe kai pos;»
(Does he hear me as if I am praying or has he changed and how?')
Chorus:
Tha ton do ischyro to Theo mou xana,
(I will see him strong my God again,)
Dynatos ein' Aftos tora, opos kai chthes,
(Mighty is He now, as yesterday,)
ki an argei, den xechna, apanta prosefches,
(and if he is late, he does not forget, he answers prayers,)
kathe thayma, tou chthes, tha to doyme xana!
(every miracle, of yesterday, we will see again!)
Verse 2:
Xero pos den allaxe to cheri tou Theoy,
(I know God's hand has not changed,)
i dynami Tou anallachti gia panta kai pantoy!
(His power unchangeable forever and everywhere!)
Pistos aionia steketai o Kyrios kai na!
(The Lord stands faithful forever and behold!)
dokimase to thayma Tou pou kanei stin kardia!
(taste His miracle He works in the heart!)
