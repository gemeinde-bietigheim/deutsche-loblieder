206 DOXA, DOXA ThEO EN YPsISTOIS!

Greek Hymns - No. 206 | "ΔΟΞΑ, ΔΟΞΑ ΘΕΩ ΕΝ ΥΨΙΣΤΟΙΣ!"
Verse 1:
Doxa, doxa Theo en ypsistois!
(Glory, glory to God in the highest!)
Psalloun oi aggeloi stous ouranoys.
(The angels are singing in the heavens.)
Doxa, doxa Theo en ypsistois!
(Glory, glory to God in the highest!)
As antichisei sti gi kai pantoy.
(Let it reverberate on earth and everywhere.)
Kai tis dikes mas fones me latreia
(And our own voices in worship)
oloi ypsonoume tora s' Afton.
(we all lift up to Him now.)
Doxa Theo en ypsistois!
(Praise God in the highest!)
Pantote akoyei me prothymia,
(He always listens willingly,)
dechetai aino cheileon thniton.
(receives praise from mortal lips.)
Doxa Theo en ypsistois!
(Praise God in the highest!)
Verse 2:
Doxa, doxa Theo en ypsistois!
(Glory, glory to God in the highest!)
Stous ouranoys antichei i foni.
(The voice echoes in the heavens.)
Doxa, doxa Theo en ypsistois!
(Glory, glory to God in the highest!)
Oli i ktisi ton plasti ainei.
(All creation praises the molder.)
Theias agapis polytimo doro,
(precious gift of divine love,)
oli i gi as dechthei to Christo!
(let the whole earth receive Christ!)
Doxa, Theo en ypsistois!
(Glory, God in the highest!)
Ton tapeino tis kardias mas to foro
(Our humble heart's tribute)
oloi prosferoume tora s' Afton.
(we all now offer to Him.)
Doxa Theo en ypsistois!
(Praise God in the highest!)
