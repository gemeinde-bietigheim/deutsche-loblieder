140 ME TO ChRISTO EChO PIA STAYROThEI

Greek Hymns - No. 140 | "ΜΕ ΤΟ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ ΕΧΩ ΠΙΑ ΣΤΑΥΡΩΘΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Me to Christo echo pia stafrothei
(I am already crucified with Christ)
kai zo mazi tou mia nea zoi!
(and live a new life with him!)
Noiotho me Keinon mia doxa lampri,
(I feel with Him a glory bright,)
Christe, dikos Sou, nai, kathe stigmi!
(Christ, Yours, yes, every moment!)
Chorus:
Mes stin agapi Sou kathe stigmi
(Into Your love every moment)
zo t' ouranoy tin oraia zoi!
(I'm living the good life in heaven!)
Lampei trigyro mou doxa lampri,
(Shines around me glory bright,)
Christe, dikos Sou, nai, kathe stigmi!
(Christ, Yours, yes, every moment!)
Verse 2:
Se kathe thlipsi mou einai paron,
(In my every sorrow he is present,)
ki ola ta vari mou peftoun s' Afton!
(and all my burdens fall on Him!)
Se kathe pono mou ki Aftos ponei,
(In my every pain He also hurts,)
vrisket' entos mou, nai, kathe stigmi!
(you are within me, yes, every moment!)
Verse 3:
Ki otan akoma pono stin kardia,
(And when my heart still aches,)
ki otan ta dakrya trechoun pikra,
(and when the tears run bitterly,)
kai mes ston kindyno vrisket' ekei,
(and in danger you are there,)
einai konta mou, nai, kathe stigmi!
(he is near me, yes, every moment!)
Verse 4:
Kai se arrostia i pikra varia,
(And in sickness or bitterness heavy,)
mou dinei pantote ti giatreia!
(always gives me the medicine!)
Mes sti zoi mou edo kai pantoy
(In my life here and everywhere)
o Iisoys mou einai plai mou!
(my Jesus is by my side!)
