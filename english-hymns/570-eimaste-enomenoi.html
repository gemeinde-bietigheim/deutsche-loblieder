570 EIMASTE ENOMENOI

Greek Hymns - No. 570 | "ΕΙΜΑΣΤΕ ΕΝΩΜΕΝΟΙ"
Verse 1:
Eimaste enomenoi oloi me to Christo,
(We are all united with Christ,)
eimaste ena soma endoxo kai lampro,
(we are a glorious and shining body,)
oi pistoi mia poimni oloi kato edo sti gi!
(the faithful one flock all down here on earth!)
Chorus:
Gia na doun pos mas kanei adelfoys o Christos,
(To see how Christ makes us brothers,)
i agapi pos enonei tous pistoys!
(how love unites believers!)
Verse 2:
Oloi mesa ston idio dromo pame mprosta,
(We're all on the same road moving forward,)
prochoroyme piasmenoi ap' ta cheria sfichta
(we move forward holding hands tightly)
kai skorpame ta nea tou Christoy s' olous pantoy!
(and we spread the news of Christ to everyone everywhere!)
Verse 3:
Oli i doxa anikei ston Patera Theo,
(All glory belongs to God the Father,)
i timi sto Megalo Lytroti, to Christo
(the honor to the Great Redeemer, Christ)
kai sto Pneyma to Agio, ton Triadiko Theo!
(and in the Holy Spirit, the Triune God!)
Verse 4:
Minyma ouranoy, minyma Theoy,
(Message from heaven, message from God,)
minyma agapis stalmeno gia sas!
(love message sent for you!)
Verse 5:
Sas anaggelloume tin agapi,
(We announce love to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin agapi,
(we announce love to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin agapi,
(we announce love to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin agapi, tin agapi,
(we announce to you love, love,)
tin agapi tou Christoy!
(the love of Christ!)
Verse 6:
Sas anaggelloume ti chara,
(We announce to you the joy,)
sas anaggelloume ti chara,
(we announce joy to you,)
sas anaggelloume ti chara,
(we announce joy to you,)
sas anaggelloume ti chara, ti chara,
(we announce to you joy, joy,)
ti chara tou Christoy!
(the joy of Christ!)
Verse 7:
Sas anaggelloume tin eirini,
(We announce peace to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin eirini,
(we announce peace to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin eirini,
(we announce peace to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin eirini, tin eirini,
(we announce to you peace, peace,)
tin eirini tou Christoy!
(the peace of Christ!)
Chorus:
Gyro sto throno Sou tha doxazo,
(Around Your throne I will glory,)
gyro sto throno Sou th' alalazo,
(around Your throne I will sing,)
gyro sto throno Sou tha Sou psallo
(around Your throne I will sing to You)
to «Agios, Agios o Theos, Agios einai o Theos!»
(the "Holy, Holy God, Holy is God!")
Verse 8:
To Onoma sou, nai, tha ypsothei ki oloi tha ymnoyn Esena!
(Your Name, yes, will be exalted and all will praise You!)
Mia mera 'kei sto throno Sou psila,
(One day here on Your throne above,)
tha lampeis apo doxa! Tha lampeis apo doxa!
(you will shine with glory! You will shine with glory!)
Verse 9:
Eisai o Protos kai o Eschatos, eisai archi kai telos!
(You are the First and the Last, you are the beginning and the end!)
Eisai o Zon pou egines nekros!
(You are Zon who became dead!)
Kai tora zeis eis tous aiones, kai tora zeis stous aiones!
(And now you live forever, and now you live forever!)
Verse 10:
Zei o Kyrios, eflogimeno t' onoma Tou!
(The Lord lives, blessed be His name!)
Nai, zei o Kyrios, o vrachos tis sotirias mou!
(Yes, the Lord lives, the rock of my salvation!)
Zei o Kyrios kai doste doxa st' onoma Tou!
(The Lord lives and give glory to His name!)
Nai, zei o Kyrios, eflogimeno t' onoma Tou!
(Yes, the Lord lives, blessed be His name!)
Verse 11:
Stin parousia Sou yparchei chara,
(In Your presence there is joy,)
pliris chara, pliris chara!
(full joy, full joy!)
Stin parousia Sou ola allazoun, gemizoun me nea zoi!
(In Your presence everything changes, is filled with new life!)
Stin parousia Sou ola allazoun, gemizoun me nea zoi!
(In Your presence everything changes, is filled with new life!)
Verse 12:
Esy eisai konta mou, de tha steritho,
(You are near me, I will not be deprived,)
sta cheria Sou ti zoi mou afino. O, poso Se agapo!
(I leave my life in Your hands. Oh, how I love You!)
Sta cheria Sou ti zoi mou afino. O, poso Se agapo!
(In Your hands I leave my life. Oh, how I love You!)
Verse 13:
As trexoume oloi emeis ti doxa Tou na ymnisoume!
(Let us all run to praise His glory!)
Einai o Agios, o Aionios Theos!
(He is the Holy, Eternal God!)
Dimiourgos kai olon mas Pateras!
(Creator and Father of us all!)
O Vasilias sti doxa t' ouranoy
(The King in the glory of heaven)
ki olis tis gis!
(and of the whole earth!)
Chorus:
Alliloyia! Alliloyia! Alliloyia! Alliloyia!
(Alleluia! Alleluia! Alleluia! Alleluia!)
Alliloyia! Alliloyia! I doxa anikei s' Afton! (2)
(Alleluia! Alleluia! The glory belongs to Him! (2))
Verse 14:
Einai o Iisoys to Alfa kai to Omega!
(Is Jesus the Alpha and the Omega!)
To proino to astro to lampro!
(The morning star the bright one!)
O Lytrotis kai o kalos poimenas!
(The Redeemer and the Good Shepherd!)
Ein' i zoi, tou kosmou olou to fos!
(It is life, the light of the whole world!)
Ein' o Theos!
(It's God!)
