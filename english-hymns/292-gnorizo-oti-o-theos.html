292 GNORIZO OTI O ThEOS

Greek Hymns - No. 292 | "ΓΝΩΡΙΖΩ ΟΤΙ Ο ΘΕΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Gnorizo oti o Theos sta cheria Tou
(I know that God is in His hands)
krata to nima tis zois,
(holds the thread of life,)
kai tote eimai asfalis
(and then I am safe)
sta cheria Tou! sta cheria Tou!
(in His hands! in His hands!)
Verse 2:
An kyvernoys' allos kaneis plin tou Theoy
(If you are ruled by someone other than God)
to vio mou epi tis gis,
(my life on earth)
poso tha imoun dystychis,
(how unhappy I would be)
o Iisoy! o Iisoy!
(oh jesus! oh jesus!)
Verse 3:
Ta schedia Sou t' agnosta pou Sy potheis,
(Your plans the unknown that You love,)
th' akoloutho amerimnos,
(I will follow carelessly)
gnorizontas oti sofos
(knowing that wisely)
me odigeis! me odigeis!
(you drive me! you drive me!)
Verse 4:
An echo thlipseis i chara, proseychomai,
(If I have sorrows or joys, I pray,)
kai tote eis tin prosefchi
(and then to prayer)
to prosopo Sou to glyky
(Your sweet face)
strefeis se me! strefeis se me!
(you turn to me! you turn to me!)
Verse 5:
Nai, sy frontizeis, Pater mou, dia eme.
(Yes, you care, my Father, for me.)
Ti merimna loipon se Se
(So I took care of you)
oli ti richno, o Thee,
(I throw it all, oh God)
mono se Se! mono se Se!
(only in You! only in You!)
