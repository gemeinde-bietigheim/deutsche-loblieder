394 ThELO NA PISTEYO

Greek Hymns - No. 394 | "ΘΕΛΩ ΝΑ ΠΙΣΤΕΥΩ"
Verse 1:
Thelo na pisteyo san ton Avraam,
(I want to believe like Abraham,)
tetoia pisti mono xerei na nika!
(only such faith knows how to win!)
Ton yio tou fernei sto Moria,
(He brings his son to Moria,)
thysia sto Theo pou agapa!
(sacrifice to the God who loves!)
Thelo na pisteyo san ton Avraam!
(I want to believe like Abraham!)
Verse 2:
Thelo na pisteyo san to Daniil,
(I want to believe like Daniel,)
tetoia pisti mono einai ikani!
(such faith alone is capable!)
Ton leonton stoma frazei afti,
(She shuts the mouths of lions,)
eleftheronei ap' ti fotia ti dynati!
(frees the strong from the fire!)
Thelo na pisteyo san to Daniil!
(I want to believe like Daniel!)
Verse 3:
Thelo na pisteyo san to Symeon,
(I want to believe like Simeon,)
tetoia pisti mono doxazei to Theo!
(such faith alone glorifies God!)
Vrike chari ki eide to Christo,
(He found grace and saw Christ,)
eftychismenos efyge ston ourano!
(happy he went to heaven!)
Thelo na pisteyo san to Symeon!
(I want to believe like Simeon!)
Verse 4:
Thelo na pisteyo san ki aftoys, Christe,
(I want to believe like them, Christ,)
tetoia pisti mono s' efcharistei, Thee!
(such faith only pleases you, God!)
Skeptomai ton Avel, ton Enoch,
(I think of Abel, Enoch,)
to Noe, ton Ilia kai to Stefano!
(Noah, Elijah and Stephen!)
Thelo na pisteyo san ki aftoys, Christe!
(I want to believe like them, Christ!)
