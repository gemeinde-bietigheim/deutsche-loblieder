454 PISTEPsE EIS TO SOTIRA

Greek Hymns - No. 454 | "ΠΙΣΤΕΨΕ ΕΙΣ ΤΟ ΣΩΤΗΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Pistepse eis to Sotira pou epano sto Stafro
(Believe in the Savior on the Cross)
dia kathe amartia ekane ton ilasmo!
(for every sin he made the miasma!)
Chorus:
Pistepse eis to Sotira,
(Believe in the Savior,)
sotiria stin psychi
(salvation to the soul)
dorean sou tin prosferei,
(offers it to you for free,)
me agapi se kalei!
(he calls you with love!)
Verse 2:
Oles sou tis amarties, to fortio to vary,
(All your sins, the heavy burden,)
dia se Aftos sikonei, entelos to afairei!
(through you He lifts up, completely removes it!)
Verse 3:
Eftychia kai eirini, dynami ki agiasmo
(Happiness and peace, strength and sanctification)
kai tin kathe sou anagki tha vreis mono sto Christo!
(and you will find your every need only in Christ!)
Verse 4:
Tora erchomai, Sotira, sou prosfero tapeina
(Now I come, Saviour, I offer you humbly)
ti psychi mou kai to soma me agapi kai chara!
(my soul and body with love and joy!)
Chorus:
Nai, pisteyo sto Sotira,
(Yes, I believe in the Savior,)
dechomai tin prosfora!
(I accept the offer!)
Nai, Afton th' akolouthiso
(Yes, I will follow Him)
me agapi kai chara!
(with love and joy!)
