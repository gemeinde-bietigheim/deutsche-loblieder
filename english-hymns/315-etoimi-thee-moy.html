315 ETOIMI, ThEE MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 315 | "ΕΤΟΙΜΗ, ΘΕΕ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Etoimi, Thee mou, einai i kardia mou
(Ready, O God, is my heart)
kai s' Esena tha 'rtho ymno na Sou po!
(and I will sing a hymn to You!)
To eleos Sou ftanei mechri ta ourania,
(Your mercy reaches to the heavens,)
synnefa aggizei i alitheia Sou!
(Your truth touches the clouds!)
Chorus:
S' agapo, Thee mou, tis zois pigi mou,
(I love you, my God, my source of life,)
ti platia i kardia sou na me ypomeneis!
(how big your heart is to bear with me!)
Ki otan gonatizo stis siopis tis ores,
(And when I kneel in silence during the hours,)
i kardia mou psallei «poso eisai kalos!»
(my heart sings "how good you are!")
Verse 2:
Tin psychi mou ypsono, Kyrie, s' Esena,
(I lift up my soul, Lord, to You,)
petra, froyrio mou, vrache dynate!
(stone, my fortress, rain hard!)
«Agios, agios, agios» tapeina psellizo,
("Holy, holy, holy" I humbly chant,)
st' Onoma Sou elpizo, gia na prochoro!
(in Your Name I hope, to move forward!)
