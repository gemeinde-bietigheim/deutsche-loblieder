26 NAI, ThA PsALLO KAThE MERA

Greek Hymns - No. 26 | "ΝΑΙ, ΘΑ ΨΑΛΛΩ ΚΑΘΕ ΜΕΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Nai, tha psallo kathe mera, alliloyia!
(Yes, I will sing every day, hallelujah!)
Nai, tha psallo, tha doxazo diarkos
(Yes, I will sing, I will praise forever)
to Theo gia ti megali sotiria
(God for the great salvation)
kai pou m' efer' ap' to skotos eis to fos!
(and who brought me from darkness to light!)
Chorus:
Nai, tha psallo, tha doxazo to Theo mou diarkos,
(Yes, I will sing, I will praise my God forever,)
pou me evgale ap' to skotos kai tin plani
(who brought me out of darkness and error)
kai me efere sto endoxo Tou fos!
(and brought me into His glorious light!)
Verse 2:
Nai, tha psallo gia tin tosi eflogia
(Yes, I will sing for so much blessing)
pou mou dinei o Christos mes sti zoi!
(that Christ gives me in life!)
Tora vrika ti chara, tin eftychia
(Now I found joy, happiness)
kai me kanei kathe mera eftychi!
(and makes me happy every day!)
Verse 3:
O Christos mas einai fos kai sotiria,
(Our Christ is light and salvation,)
Aftos einai o Poimenas o kalos!
(This is the Good Shepherd!)
Sti zoi mou dinei panta eflogia
(He always gives blessing in my life)
kai pista panta tha Ton akoloutho!
(and faithfully I will always follow Him!)
Verse 4:
De tha payso na ymno to Lytroti mou,
(I will not cease to praise my Redeemer,)
panta prothyma na Ton ypireto,
(always willing to serve Him,)
tha kirytto tin agapi Tou me tharros,
(I will preach His love with courage,)
eos otou fthaso eis ton ourano!
(until I reach heaven!)
