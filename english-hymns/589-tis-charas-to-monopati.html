589 TIS ChARAS TO MONOPATI

Greek Hymns - No. 589 | "ΤΗΣ ΧΑΡΑΣ ΤΟ ΜΟΝΟΠΑΤΙ"
Verse 1:
Tis charas to monopati
(The path of joy)
t' ouranoy to skalopati
(the stairway to heaven)
vrika sto Christo!
(I found in Christ!)
M' emathe na tragoudao
(He taught me to sing)
m' emathe na agapao
(she taught me to love)
m' emathe na zo!
(he taught me to live!)
Chorus:
Den allazo, de tha kano piso,
(I'm not changing, I'm not going back,)
Den mporo choris Afton na ziso!
(I can't live without Him!)
Ta pania mou diaplata th' anoixo
(I will open my sails wide)
To Christo na synantiso!
(Christ to meet!)
Verse 2:
Eskypse sto provlima mou,
(Lean into my problem,)
skoypise ta dakrya mou,
(wipe my tears)
o Iisoys Christos!
(Jesus Christ!)
Steriose ta vimata mou,
(Sterilize my steps,)
tou chrostao ti chara mou,
(I owe him my happiness,)
m' efere sto fos!
(brought me to light!)
