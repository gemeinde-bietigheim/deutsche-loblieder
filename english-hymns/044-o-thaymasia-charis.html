44 O ThAYMASIA ChARIS

Greek Hymns - No. 44 | "Ω ΘΑΥΜΑΣΙΑ ΧΑΡΙΣ"
Verse 1:
O thafmasia charis, theia kai thafmasti!
(O admiring grace, godly and wonderful!)
Pos na tin perigrapso, glossa mou adynatei!
(How can I describe it, my language fails me!)
Afti m' eleftheronei, varos mou afairei.
(She frees me, removes a burden from me.)
O! ti endoxos charis, triseflogiti!
(Oh! what a glorious grace, triseulogite!)
Chorus:
Aparamillos i theia charis,
(Incomparable divine grace,)
vathytera ton okeanon,
(deeper than the oceans,)
toson yperechei, toson, nai, exechei,
(so excels, so, yes, excels,)
olos eparkis kai di' eme, nai, di' eme!
(all sufficient for me too, yes, for me!)
Efrytera olon ton skopon mou,
(Broader than all my purposes,)
anotera ton amartion.
(above sins.)
O! megalynate t' onoma Tou esaei!
(Oh! magnify His name!)
Verse 2:
O thafmasia charis, sozei t' apololos,
(O wondrous grace, saveth the desolate,)
kai di' aftis ki ego, nyn, esothin pantelos!
(and because of her I too, now, am completely hidden!)
Ethrafse tas alyseis, thanatou ta desma!
(Break the chains, the bonds of death!)
Poson eimai efgnomon, Theie Lytrota!
(How grateful I am, O Redeemer!)
Verse 3:
O thafmasia charis, sozous' amartolon,
(O wondrous grace, saving sinner,)
ton pleon molysmenon, ton pleon ryparon,
(the most polluted, the most polluting,)
ton kamnei klironomon, metochon t' Ouranoy!
(makes him an heir, a sharer of Heaven!)
O, ti endoxos charis tou Patros Theoy!
(Oh, what a glorious grace of God the Father!)
