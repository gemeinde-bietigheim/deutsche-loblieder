98 PREPEI NA LEO KAThE MOY PONO

Greek Hymns - No. 98 | "ΠΡΕΠΕΙ ΝΑ ΛΕΩ ΚΑΘΕ ΜΟΥ ΠΟΝΟ"
Verse 1:
Prepei na leo kathe mou pono,
(I must tell my every pain,)
na anatheto stin prosefchi
(to entrust to prayer)
eis to Christo, ton pio pisto filo,
(in Christ, the most faithful friend,)
gia na 'cho panta anapsychi!
(to always have leisure!)
Chorus:
Eis to Christo mou prepei na leo
(To my Christ I must say)
gia to fortio mou to vary
(for my burden the heavy)
pos den mporo pia na to sikoso,
(that I can't take it anymore)
choris Aftos na me voithei!
(without Him helping me!)
Verse 2:
Se kathe thlipsi kai dyskolia
(In every sorrow and difficulty)
prepei na trecho ston Iisoy,
(i must run to jesus)
se eftychia i dystychia
(in happiness or unhappiness)
Aftoy oi logoi me empsychoyn!
(These reasons cheer me up!)
Verse 3:
Otan i ftocheia me dokimazei
(When poverty tries me)
ki oloi oi filoi m' apostrafoyn,
(and all my friends turn away,)
otan o kosmos eme peirazei,
(when the world bothers me,)
vlepo mprosta mou ton Iisoy!
(I see Jesus in front of me!)
Verse 4:
O kosmos stinei gia me pagida
(The world is setting a trap for me)
kai sto kako panta m' odigei,
(and always leads me to evil,)
konta mou omos echo Sotira,
(but I have a Savior near me,)
panta ti niki mou chorigei!
(He always grants me victory!)
