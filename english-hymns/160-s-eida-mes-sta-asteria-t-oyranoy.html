160 S' EIDA MES STA ASTERIA T' OYRANOY

Greek Hymns - No. 160 | "Σ' ΕΙΔΑ ΜΕΣ ΣΤΑ ΑΣΤΕΡΙΑ Τ' ΟΥΡΑΝΟΥ"
Verse 1:
S' eida mes sta asteria t' ouranoy,
(I saw you in the stars of the sky,)
isoun sti foni tou kerafnoy!
(you were in the voice of thunder!)
Mes sta pelaga kai stis steries S' evlepa, Christe!
(Through the seas and the lands I saw You, Christ!)
Poso thafmasta ta erga Sou,
(How wonderful are Your works,)
posi dynami sta cheria Sou!
(how much power in Your hands!)
Kai skeftomoun ki elega, Christe, ti eisai Sy gia me!
(And I was thinking and saying, Christ, what are You to me!)
Ospou kapoia mera sto stafro,
(Until someday on the cross,)
o glykie Christe, S' efcharisto,
(oh sweet christ, thank you,)
vrika pleria tin agapi Sou,
(I found Your love full,)
me pisti stin kardia akoympisa ta vari!
(with faith in the heart I supported the burdens!)
Chorus:
Tora eisai o ouranos gia me,
(Now you are the sky for me)
tora leyteros peto, Christe!
(now freer fly, Christ!)
Krata pantote to cheri mou,
(Always hold my hand,)
eisai to pan gia me, eisai to pan gia me!
(you are everything to me, you are everything to me!)
Verse 2:
S' epsachna sta ori na Se vro,
(I was looking for you in the mountains to find you,)
san tyflos pou psachnei ligo fos,
(like a blind man looking for some light,)
ma otan anoixa ta matia mou, isoun plai mou!
(but when I opened my eyes, you were by my side!)
Opou ki an gyriso, Se koito,
(Everywhere I turn, I see you)
Sy plireis ti gi, ton ourano!
(You fill the earth, the sky!)
Posi dynami i agapi Sou, meine plai mou!
(How strong is Your love, stay by my side!)
Didaxe me tin diki Sou tin odo,
(Teach me your way,)
didaxe me akrivos na perpato,
(teach me exactly how to walk)
didaxe me tin agapi Sou
(teach me Your love)
kai vale mou chara n' afiso ola ta vari!
(and give me joy to let go of all burdens!)
