137 KAThE MERA EIS TOYS KOLPOYS

Greek Hymns - No. 137 | "ΚΑΘΕ ΜΕΡΑ ΕΙΣ ΤΟΥΣ ΚΟΛΠΟΥΣ"
Verse 1:
Kathe mera eis tous kolpous tou Sotira mou Christoy
(Every day in the bosom of my Savior Christ)
anapayomai kai eimai asfalis!
(rest and be safe!)
Poso toyto m' entharrynei kai o Logos tou Theoy
(How much the Word of God encourages me in this)
zoi dinei, giati einai alithis!
(gives life, because it is true!)
Chorus:
Me chara ton Iisoy mou pantote th' akoloutho
(I will always follow my Jesus with joy)
kai me zilo sti psychi mou di' Afton tha martyro!
(and with zeal in my soul for Him I will testify!)
Verse 2:
Tosa chronia eplaniomoun se dromakia skoteina
(For so many years I wandered in dark alleys)
kai de vrika isychia pouthena.
(and I found no peace anywhere.)
Epsachna tin eftychia, all' apetycha oiktros,
(I was looking for happiness, but I failed miserably,)
mechris otou m' episkefthik' o Christos!
(until Christ visited me!)
Verse 3:
Me poikiles yposcheseis tis psychis mou o echthros
(With various promises of my soul the enemy)
me kratoyse sto skotadi diarkos.
(kept me in the dark all the time.)
Tora omos thriamveyo me to Logo tou Theoy
(But now I triumph with the Word of God)
kai ta erga katastrefo tou echthroy!
(and I destroy the works of the enemy!)
Verse 4:
Metochos tis vasileias ton agion tou Theoy,
(Partaker of the kingdom of God's saints,)
klironomos tou afthartou thisafroy!
(heir to the imperishable treasure!)
Doxa eis to Lytroti mou, tora eimai eftychis
(Glory to my Redeemer, now I am happy)
kai m' afton tha efretho stous ouranoys!
(and with him I will be in heaven!)
