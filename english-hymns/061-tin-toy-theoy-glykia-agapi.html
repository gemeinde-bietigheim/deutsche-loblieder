61 TIN TOY ThEOY GLYKIA AGAPI

Greek Hymns - No. 61 | "ΤΗΝ ΤΟΥ ΘΕΟΥ ΓΛΥΚΙΑ ΑΓΑΠΗ"
Verse 1:
Tin tou Theoy glykia agapi, anekfrasti kai thafmasti,
(God's sweet love, inexpressible and wonderful,)
arkoyntos na tin perigrapsei anthropou gloss' adynatei!
(sufficient to describe it human language fails!)
Ton enocho ton dikaionei, ki afton ton asevi
(He justifies the guilty, and the ungodly)
ton athoonei, ton lytronei kai ton yiothetei!
(acquits him, redeems him and adopts him!)
Chorus:
O thafmasti glykia agapi,
(O wondrous sweet love,)
i theia kai agni!
(the aunt and pure!)
Sy diarkos tha diameneis
(You will always stay)
pantote i afti!
(always her!)
Verse 2:
Toson agapise ton kosmo o agathos imon Patir,
(Our good Father so loved the world,)
ost' o Monogenis Yios Tou mas apestalei os Sotir!
(until His Only Begotten Son is sent to us as a Savior!)
Ina o pisteyon eis Afton na min apolesthei,
(May those who believe in Him not perish,)
zoi aionia na echei kai telos doxasthei!
(eternal life may he have and end be glorified!)
Verse 3:
I thafmasti afti agapi vathytata tha sygkinei!
(This wondrous love will deeply move!)
Kathe efgnomoni kardia oudepote tha lismonei
(Every grateful heart will never forget)
tin endoxi Aftoy thysia ekei, eis to stafro,
(His glorious sacrifice there, on the cross,)
tin apeiri Tou efsplachnia kai ton exilasmo!
(His infinite mercy and forgiveness!)
