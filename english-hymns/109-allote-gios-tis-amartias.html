109 ALLOTE GIOS TIS AMARTIAS

Greek Hymns - No. 109 | "ΑΛΛΟΤΕ ΓΙΟΣ ΤΗΣ ΑΜΑΡΤΙΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Allote gios tis amartias
(Sometimes a son of sin)
zoysa makria ap' to Christo!
(I was living far from Christ!)
Tora pia s' Esena, Patera,
(Now to You, Father,)
vrika aionio lytromo!
(I have found eternal redemption!)
Chorus:
Nai, Sou dino oli ti zoi mou,
(Yes, I give you all my life,)
par' tin kardia mou kai tin psychi mou
(take my heart and my soul)
na me kratiseis agno konta Sou
(keep me pure near You)
kai nikitis na Se akoloutho!
(and victorious to follow You!)
Verse 2:
Sto stafro pano ekei gia mena
(On the cross up there for me)
pethanes san enas listis!
(you died like a bandit!)
Egines Sy gia me katara,
(You became S to curse me,)
gia na vro ego ti lytrosi!
(for me to find redemption!)
Verse 3:
Anastithikes ap' ton tafo,
(You rose from the grave,)
piges pali ston ourano,
(you went to heaven again)
mesiteyeis ekei gia mena
(you intercede there for me)
agrypnos gia ton amartolo!
(watchful for the sinner!)
Verse 4:
Nai, ta panta s' Esena dino,
(Yes, I give everything to You,)
par' ta Sy, Iisoy Christe,
(despite You, Jesus Christ,)
kane ta Sy mia eflogia
(make them a blessing)
me to Pneyma Sou, Thafmaste!
(with Your Spirit, Admirers!)
