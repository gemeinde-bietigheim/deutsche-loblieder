600 ALALAXTE STON KYRIO

Greek Hymns - No. 600 | "ΑΛΑΛΑΞΤΕ ΣΤΟΝ ΚΥΡΙΟ"
Verse 1:
Alalaxte ston Kyrio oli i gi!
(Rejoice in the Lord all the earth!)
Doulepste ston Kyrio me vathia chara!
(Work for the Lord with deep joy!)
Elate mprosta Tou m' agalliasi
(Come before Him with joy)
kai mathete oti o Kyrios einai o Theos!
(and know that the Lord is God!)
Elate mprosta Tou m' agalliasi
(Come before Him with joy)
kai mathete oti o Kyrios einai o Theos!
(and know that the Lord is God!)
Chorus:
Aftos ekane emas.
(He made us.)
Aftos ekane emas kai ochi emeis.
(He made us and not us.)
Emeis eimaste laos Tou
(We are His people)
kai provata tis voskis Tou.
(and sheep of His pasture.)
Emeis eimaste laos Tou
(We are His people)
kai provata tis voskis Tou.
(and sheep of His pasture.)
Verse 2:
Eiselthete stis pyles Tou me doxologia,
(Enter His gates with praise,)
mpeste stis afles Tou me ymnodia,
(enter His courts with hymns,)
eflogeite to onoma Tou giati einai agathos,
(bless his name for he is good,)
to eleos Tou menei eis ton aiona
(His mercy endures forever)
kai i alitheia Tou menei se oles tis genies.
(and His truth abides in all generations.)
