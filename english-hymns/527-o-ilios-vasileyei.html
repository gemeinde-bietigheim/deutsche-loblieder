527 O ILIOS VASILEYEI

Greek Hymns - No. 527 | "Ο ΗΛΙΟΣ ΒΑΣΙΛΕΥΕΙ"
Verse 1:
O ilios vasileyei, o ilios vasileyei,
(The sun reigns, the sun reigns,)
psychi mou, kane ftera!
(my soul, grow wings!)
Me oplo sou tin agapi
(With love as your weapon)
mi fovitheis skoteinia, mi fovitheis skoteinia,
(don't be afraid of the dark don't be afraid of the dark)
nai, skoteinia!
(yes, darkness!)
Verse 2:
Dioxe makria ton pono, dioxe makria ton pono,
(Drive away the pain, drive away the pain,)
protimise ti chara
(prefer joy)
pou o Christos mas charizei
(which Christ gives us)
sta dialekta Tou paidia, sta dialekta Tou paidia,
(in His dialects children, in His dialects children,)
pista paidia!
(faithful children!)
Verse 3:
O ilios vasileyei, o ilios vasileyei,
(The sun reigns, the sun reigns,)
kai i psychi mou zita
(and my soul asks)
san to pouli na petaxei,
(like a bird flies)
Christe, s' Esena konta, Christe, S' Esena konta,
(Christ, close to You, Christ, close to You,)
nai, pio konta!
(yes, closer!)
Verse 4:
Konta sto Lytroti mas, konta sto Lytroti mas,
(Near our Redeemer, near our Redeemer,)
ein' ola efodiasta!
(everything is fragrant!)
Xechnioyntai kakies kai ponoi
(Evils and pains are forgotten)
kai svinoun ola makria, kai svinoun ola makria,
(and they all fade away, and they all fade away,)
nai, makria!
(yes, away!)
