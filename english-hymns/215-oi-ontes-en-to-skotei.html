215 OI ONTES EN TO SKOTEI

Greek Hymns - No. 215 | "ΟΙ ΟΝΤΕΣ ΕΝ ΤΩ ΣΚΟΤΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Oi ontes en to skotei nyn eidon mega fos
(The beings in the darkness now saw a great light)
kai chairousi dioti efani o Christos!
(and they rejoice because Christ has appeared!)
Katilthe na syntripsei tis planis ton desmon
(He came down to crush the delusion of the desmon)
kai na apokalypsei sotirion odon!
(and reveal a way of salvation!)
Verse 2:
Thnitoi, nyn enikithi o daimon tis fthoras!
(Mortals, now the demon of decay is atoned for!)
Chareite! Egennithi paidion eis imas!
(Rejoice! A child was born to us!)
Aftoy i exousia estai pantotini!
(His authority is forever!)
Aftoy i vasileia den thelei salefthei!
(His kingdom will not be shaken!)
Verse 3:
En gi skias thanatou fos lampei tou loipoy,
(In a land of shadow of death light shines of the rest,)
to mega onoma Tou kiryssetai pantoy!
(His great name is proclaimed everywhere!)
O Symvoulos kai monos Theos o ischyros,
(The Counselor and only God the mighty,)
tou mellontos aionos Patir kai archigos!
(of the age to come Father and leader!)
Verse 4:
O Archon tis eirinis, elpidos o Dotir,
(Lord of peace, Giver of hope,)
pigi dikaiosynis, amartolon Sotir!
(source of justice, Savior of sinners!)
As lavi nyn ton topon tis lypis i chara,
(Let joy now take the place of sorrow,)
pasa fyli anthropon as psallei «Osanna!».
(let every race of people sing "Hosanna!")
