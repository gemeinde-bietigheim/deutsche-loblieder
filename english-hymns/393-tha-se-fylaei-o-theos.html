393 ThA SE FYLAEI O ThEOS

Greek Hymns - No. 393 | "ΘΑ ΣΕ ΦΥΛΑΕΙ Ο ΘΕΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Tha se fylaei o Theos, na min apelpistheis,
(God will protect you, do not despair,)
nai, proporeyetai Aftos sto dromo tis zois!
(yes, He leads the way of life!)
Chorus:
Tha einai meta sou, provlepei pantote gia se,
(He will be after you, always provide for you,)
tha einai meta sou, frontizei dia se!
(he will be after you, he takes care of you!)
Verse 2:
Otan tha erthoun peirasmoi, pote min ptoitheis,
(When temptations come, never flinch,)
o Kyrios se voithei, otan dokimastheis!
(the Lord helps you when you are tested!)
Verse 3:
Kai tis polles elleipseis sou na Tou empisteftheis,
(And your many shortcomings to entrust to Him,)
tha s' odigei Aftos pantoy, pote mi fovitheis!
(He will lead you everywhere, never fear!)
Verse 4:
Stis dyskolies tis zois tha pronoei gia se,
(In the difficulties of life he will provide for you,)
s' Afton, nai, na empistefteis, panta tha 'nai me se!
(in Him, yes, trust, He will always be with you!)
