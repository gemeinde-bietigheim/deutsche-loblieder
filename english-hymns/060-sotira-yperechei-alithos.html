60 SOTIRA, YPEREChEI ALIThOS

Greek Hymns - No. 60 | "ΣΩΤΗΡΑ, ΥΠΕΡΕΧΕΙ ΑΛΗΘΩΣ"
Verse 1:
Sotira, yperechei alithos,
(Saviour, he excels indeed,)
i agapi Sou ton kathe logismo!
(Your love is beyond measure!)
Ap' tin psychi ego zito thermos
(From the soul I ask warmly)
tin ektasi aftis kai to vathmo n' antiliftho!
(the extent of it and the degree to perceive!)
Verse 2:
I anthropini glossa den mporei
(Human language cannot)
gia ti thysia tis na diigithei,
(for her sacrifice to be told,)
all' omos i tou Pneymatos ormi
(but the impulse of the Spirit)
gia tin agapi Sou kinei ta cheili na milo!
(for Your love moves my lips to speak!)
Verse 3:
Pote foni anthropou den mporei
(Never a human voice can)
axios tin agapi Sou na ymnei.
(worthy to praise Your love.)
Sotira, i kardia mou me othei
(Savior, my heart impels me)
m' oli ti dynami kai ti foni na exymno!
(with all my strength and voice to praise!)
Verse 4:
Sy gemise kardia mou ti ftochi
(You filled my poor heart)
me tin agapi Sou ti thafmasti,
(with Your loving love,)
se Se ti ferno tora adeiani
(in I bring it to you now empty)
kai sou zito me tin agapi Sou na plirothei!
(and I ask you with Your love to be paid!)
Verse 5:
Kai otan anevo plision Sou
(And when I ascend near You)
kai Se ido prosopo pros prosopo,
(and I see You face to face,)
th' archiso molis tote, Iisoy,
(I'll start only then, Jesus)
to vathos tis t' ametrito ki ego na ennoo!
(its depth is immeasurable and I mean it!)
