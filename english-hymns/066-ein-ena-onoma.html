66 EIN' ENA ONOMA

Greek Hymns - No. 66 | "ΕΙΝ' ΕΝΑ ΟΝΟΜΑ"
Verse 1:
Ein' ena onoma mes stin kardia mou
(It's a name in my heart)
ki einai gia mena tos' agapito,
(and it is so dear to me,)
me sygkinei kai me enthousiazei
(it moves and excites me)
kai den yparchei pio glyko ap' afto!
(and there is nothing sweeter than that!)
Chorus:
Ein' endoxo to onoma tou Iisoy,
(Blessed be the name of Jesus,)
mes stin kardia ichei melodika,
(in the heart it sounds melodically,)
mou charise aionia sotiria
(grant me eternal salvation)
kai m' eleftherose ap' ta desma!
(and set me free from the bonds!)
Verse 2:
Me anapayei mesa eis tis thlipseis,
(He comforts me in sorrows,)
otan trigyro einai skoteina,
(when it's dark around,)
otan xefeygoun ki ola gyro svinoun,
(when they escape and everything around fades,)
mou dinei elpida kai parigoria!
(gives me hope and comfort!)
Verse 3:
Zei t' onoma Tou kai tha zei aionia,
(His name lives and will live forever,)
eno ta alla svinoun kai pernoyn
(while the others fade and pass)
ki otan o kosmos toytos tha parelthei,
(and when this world shall pass away,)
m' afto tha ziso eis tous ouranoys!
(with this I will live in the heavens!)
