371 POS MPORO NA SYNANTISO

Greek Hymns - No. 371 | "ΠΩΣ ΜΠΟΡΩ ΝΑ ΣΥΝΑΝΤΗΣΩ"
Verse 1:
Pos mporo na synantiso
(How can I meet?)
m' adeia cheria to Christo,
(Christ with empty hands,)
oyte mia mera na ziso
(not a day to live)
mi douleyontas gi' Afton;
(not working for Him?)
Chorus:
Otan prosklitho plision
(When I am invited near)
tou Christoy ston ourano,
(of Christ in heaven,)
o, pos tha Ton synantiso,
(oh, how shall I meet Him,)
m' adeia cheria na vretho;
(to find myself empty-handed?)
Verse 2:
Na pethano de fovoymai,
(I'm not afraid to die)
eimai etoimos gi' afto,
(i'm ready for it)
alla pos stenochoroymai
(but how upset I am)
m' adeia cheria na Ton do!
(to see Him with empty hands!)
Verse 3:
Ithela na anakaleso
(I wanted to retract)
olo mou to parelthon,
(all my past)
olo na t' afieroso
(all to dedicate)
kai na zo gia to Christo!
(and to live for Christ!)
Verse 4:
O, seis, agioi, agrypneite
(O ye saints, keep vigil)
eis to ergo to iero,
(in the holy work,)
gia psyches na ergastheite,
(for souls to work,)
oso echete kairo!
(while you have time!)
