119 DEN EPAISChYNOMAI, ChRISTE

Greek Hymns - No. 119 | "ΔΕΝ ΕΠΑΙΣΧΥΝΟΜΑΙ, ΧΡΙΣΤΕ"
Verse 1:
Den epaischynomai, Christe, na Se omologo,
(I am not ashamed, Christ, to confess You,)
oyte to ergo Sou pote tha payso na aino!
(nor Thy work shall I ever cease to praise!)
Chorus:
Sto Stafro tou Christoy vrika ti lytrosi
(In the Cross of Christ I found redemption)
ap' tin piesi tis enochis
(from the pressure of guilt)
kai ekei vrika eirini psychis
(and there I found peace of soul)
kai tora eim' olo eftychis!
(and now I'm all happy!)
Verse 2:
To onoma Sou, o Christe, elpida tis psychis
(Thy name, O Christ, hope of the soul)
kai Sy stin ora ton machon eisai o Lytrotis!
(and You in the time of battles are the Redeemer!)
Verse 3:
Oi yposcheseis Sou, Thee, ein' oles alitheis
(Your promises, God, are all true)
kai o elpizon epi Se einai triseftychis!
(and he who hopes in You is happy!)
Verse 4:
O,ti ki an Sou empisteftho fylas Sy asfalos,
(Whatever I entrust to You You surely keep,)
osotou eis ton ourano tha ziso eftychos!
(until in heaven I will live happily!)
