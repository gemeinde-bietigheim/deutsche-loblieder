355 O KYRIE MOY, SY, KAI ThEE MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 355 | "Ω ΚΥΡΙΕ ΜΟΥ, ΣΥ, ΚΑΙ ΘΕΕ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
O Kyrie mou, Sy, kai Thee mou,
(O my Lord, You, and my God,)
mesa ston kosmo afto,
(in this world)
mono Esena kai ti morfi Sou
(only You and Your form)
na vlepo, afto potho!
(to see, that's what I crave!)
Chorus:
Mi me afiseis apo konta Sou
(Don't leave me from You)
na fygo makria,
(to go away)
mi me afiseis, giati fovamai
(don't leave me, because I'm afraid)
mesa sti skoteinia!
(in the dark!)
Verse 2:
O Kyrie mou, otan mazi Sou
(O my Lord, when with Thee)
sti strata prochoro,
(I'm going to the army)
apo to cheri mi me afiseis,
(don't let go of my hand)
giati tha planitho!
(why will I go astray!)
Verse 3:
O Kyrie mou, sti theia strata
(O my Lord, in the divine army)
thelo na m' odigeis,
(I want you to drive me)
kai gia tin doxa, gia ton Patera
(and for the glory, for the Father)
thelo na mou mileis!
(I want you to talk to me!)
Verse 4:
O Kyrie mou, an to theliseis
(Oh my Lord, if You will)
gia Sena na pono,
(for you to hurt)
voithise me mesa ston pono
(help me through the pain)
panta na Se ymno!
(always to praise You!)
