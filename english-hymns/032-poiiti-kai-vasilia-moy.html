32 POIITI KAI VASILIA MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 32 | "ΠΟΙΗΤΗ ΚΑΙ ΒΑΣΙΛΙΑ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Poiiti kai Vasilia mou, thelo mesa ap' tin kardia mou,
(My poet and King, I want from my heart,)
na sou po me chilious ymnous «S' agapo».
(to tell you with a thousand hymns "I love you".)
Eisai Kyrios, Theos mou, eisai o dimiourgos mou,
(You are Lord, my God, you are my creator,)
eisai o Iisoys Christos, eisai tou Theoy o gios!
(you are Jesus Christ, you are God's son!)
Chorus:
Se doxazo, Kyrie mou!
(I praise you, my Lord!)
ap' ta vathi tis kardias, ap' ta vathi tis psychis mou,
(from the depths of my heart, from the depths of my soul,)
Se latreyo, Kyrie mou!
(I adore you, my Lord!)
ap' ta vathi tis kardias, ap' ta vathi tis psychis mou
(from the depths of my heart, from the depths of my soul)
Se doxazo!
(I praise you!)
Verse 2:
T' onoma Sou einai pio pano apo gi kai ourano,
(Thy name is above earth and heaven,)
einai pio lampro akoma ki ap' ton ilio!
(it is brighter than even the sun!)
T' onoma Sou antilalei pantachoy s' oli ti gi
(Thy name is resounding everywhere throughout the earth)
ki echei dynami na sosei ti diki mou tin psychi!
(and has power to save my own soul!)
Verse 3:
Ela tora, Kyrie mas, ela tora anamesa mas,
(Come now, our Lord, come now among us,)
aggixe mas, milise mas, Iisoy!
(touch us, speak to us, Jesus!)
Eimaste paidia dika Sou, theloume ti syntrofia Sou,
(We are Your children, we want Your company,)
gemise mas parousia, gemise mas ourano!
(fill us with presence, fill us with heaven!)
