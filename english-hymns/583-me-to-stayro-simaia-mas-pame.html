583 ME TO STAYRO SIMAIA MAS PAME

Greek Hymns - No. 583 | "ΜΕ ΤΟ ΣΤΑΥΡΟ ΣΗΜΑΙΑ ΜΑΣ ΠΑΜΕ"
Verse 1:
Me to stafro simaia mas pame
(With the cross flag we go)
mes' apo mia nychta varia,
(through a heavy night,)
ki olo vadizoume tharralea,
(and still we march bravely,)
giati ein' o Christos stin kardia!
(because Christ is in the heart!)
Chorus:
Se Se, Christe, anikoun
(To You, Christ, they belong)
ta niata kai i zoi!
(youth and life!)
Se Se pistoi
(To You faithful)
os tin teleftaia pnoi!
(until the last breath!)
Verse 2:
An o echthros chyma na mas vlapsei,
(If the enemy seeks to harm us,)
tin niki na parei zita!
(he wants to get the victory!)
Mi strefeis piso, panta mprosta sou,
(Don't turn back, always ahead,)
sfichta o Christos se krata!
(Christ holds you tight!)
Verse 3:
Einai simaia, einai aspida
(It's a flag, it's a shield)
gia mas o Christos sti zoi!
(Christ in life for us!)
Oloi me tharros as prochoroyme
(Let us all move forward with courage)
ki i niki tha 'rthei na mas vrei!
(and victory will come to find us!)
