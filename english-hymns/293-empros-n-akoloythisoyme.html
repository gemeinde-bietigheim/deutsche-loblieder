293 EMPROS N' AKOLOYThISOYME

Greek Hymns - No. 293 | "ΕΜΠΡΟΣ Ν' ΑΚΟΛΟΥΘΗΣΟΥΜΕ"
Verse 1:
Empros n' akolouthisoume
(Let's follow)
to mega Lytroti mas
(our great Redeemer)
pou afise ton ourano
(who left the sky)
kai irthe edo sti gi mas!
(and came here to our land!)
Pou afise ton ourano
(Who left the sky)
kai irthe edo sti gi mas!
(and came here to our land!)
Verse 2:
Ilthe na ferei ti chara,
(He came to bring joy,)
amartoloys na sosei
(sinners to save)
ki ap' tin aionia kolasi
(and from eternal hell)
na mas eleftherosei!
(to set us free!)
Ki ap' tin aionia kolasi
(And from eternal hell)
na mas eleftherosei!
(to set us free!)
Verse 3:
Ilthe ki anevi sto stafro
(He came and ascended the cross)
kai plirose me aima
(and paid in blood)
ta tosa amartimata
(so many sins)
pou eicha kamomena!
(that I was tired of!)
Ta tosa amartimata
(So many sins)
pou eicha kamomena!
(that I was tired of!)
Verse 4:
Gi' afto loipon, psychi, s' Afton
(Therefore, soul, to Him)
doxa, timi, latreia
(glory, honor, worship)
na doseis, pou sou charise
(to give, who gave you)
zoi tin aionia!
(life forever!)
Na doseis, pou sou charise
(To give, what was given to you)
zoi tin aionia!
(life forever!)
