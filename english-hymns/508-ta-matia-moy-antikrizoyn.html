508 TA MATIA MOY ANTIKRIZOYN

Greek Hymns - No. 508 | "ΤΑ ΜΑΤΙΑ ΜΟΥ ΑΝΤΙΚΡΙΖΟΥΝ"
Verse 1:
Ta matia mou antikrizoun tou Christoy ton erchomo,
(My eyes behold the coming of Christ,)
me chara Ton vlepo na 'rchetai xana ap' ton ourano!
(with joy I see Him coming again from heaven!)
Amarantina stefania sto ena cheri Tou kratei,
(Amaranth wreaths in one hand He holds,)
kai i nymfi th' arpachthei!
(and the nymph shall be raptured!)
Chorus:
Doxa, doxa sto Sotira!
(Glory, glory to the Savior!)
Einai tis zois i thyra,
(It is the door of life,)
einai tis charas i peira,
(experience is a joy,)
i alitheia kai to fos!
(the truth and the light!)
Verse 2:
Tora akoyo salpisma psila ap' ton ourano
(Now I hear a trumpet blast from heaven)
psalmodia ton aggelon kai fones charas piston!
(chanting of angels and shouts of joy of believers!)
Ta patimata Tou tora antichoyn pio dynata,
(His footsteps now echo louder,)
Erchetai, mou len' ki afta!
(It's coming, I'm told that too!)
Verse 3:
Etoimi i kardia sto Lytroti gia ypodochi
(The heart of the Redeemer is ready to receive)
i thysia tou stafroy Tou, mou 'svise tin enochi!
(the sacrifice of His cross, erased my guilt!)
Tora st' ouranoy ti doxa lytromeno me kalei
(Now in heaven the glory redeemed calls me)
sti chara kai sti zoi!
(to joy and to life!)
