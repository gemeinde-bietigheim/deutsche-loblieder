135 IMOYN I MERA DIChOS TON ILIO

Greek Hymns - No. 135 | "ΗΜΟΥΝ Η ΜΕΡΑ ΔΙΧΩΣ ΤΟΝ ΗΛΙΟ"
Verse 1:
Imoun i mera dichos ton ilio,
(I was the day without the sun,)
imoun i mera dichos to fos,
(I was the day without light,)
imoun i anoixi dichos louloydia,
(I was the spring without flowers,)
imoun to synnefo dichos vrochi.
(I was the cloud without rain.)
Chorus:
Mia thlimmeni skia itan i zoi mou
(A sad shadow was my life)
pnigmeni sto dakry, sto stenagmo.
(drowned in tears, in sighs.)
Zitoysa agapi mesa sta syntrimmia
(I was asking for love in the wreckage)
pou ekryv' o ponos ki i monaxia.
(where pain and loneliness hid.)
Verse 2:
Imoun kardia dichos agapi,
(I was a heart without love,)
imoun pouli dichos ftera,
(I was a bird without wings)
imoun karavi dichos pyxida
(I was a ship without a compass)
mesa sta kymata dichos pania.
(in the waves without sails.)
Verse 3:
Ki omos mia mera kapoios me vrike
(And yet one day someone found me)
kai mou psithyrise pos m' agapa,
(and he whispered that he loves me)
mou eipe ela konta s' Emena,
(He said come to Me,)
gia sena plirosa sto Golgotha!
(for you I paid at Calvary!)
Chorus:
Tora echo chara pou eimai paidi Tou!
(Now I am happy to be His child!)
Aftos me esose ap' to chamo!
(He saved me from destruction!)
Kai tora mporo me olo to tharros
(And now I can with all courage)
na leo, Patera, S' efcharisto!
(to say, Father, I thank You!)
