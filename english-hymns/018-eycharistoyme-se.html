18 EYChARISTOYME SE

Greek Hymns - No. 18 | "ΕΥΧΑΡΙΣΤΟΥΜΕ ΣΕ"
Verse 1:
Efcharistoyme Se,
(Thank You,)
Patera tou Christoy,
(Father of Christ,)
Patera mas piste,
(our faithful father,)
efcharistoyme Se!
(thank You!)
Verse 2:
Proseferes se mas
(You offered us)
to mono Sou Yio,
(Your only Son,)
sto xylo tou stafroy
(on the wood of the cross)
anevases Afton!
(you uploaded Him!)
Verse 3:
Proorises emas
(You destined us)
me ton Iisoy Christo
(with Jesus Christ)
na zisoume mazi
(to live together)
os isoi adelfoi!
(as equal brothers!)
Verse 4:
Synenoses emas
(You brought us together)
se soma tou Christoy,
(in the body of Christ,)
tis eflogies Sou
(Your blessings)
afthonos mas skorpas!
(you scatter us abundantly!)
Verse 5:
Asteirefti chara
(Immense joy)
skorpizeis stin kardia
(you scatter in the heart)
ki agapi kathari
(and pure love)
mas denei olous sfichta!
(binds us all tightly!)
Verse 6:
As anevoyn se Se
(Let them rise to Se)
oi ymnoi mas aftoi,
(these our hymns,)
os aroma Christoy
(as the fragrance of Christ)
as ginoune dektoi!
(let them be accepted!)
