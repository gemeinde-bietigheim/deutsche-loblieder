605 ATENISE EKEI STO STAYRO

Greek Hymns - No. 605 | "ΑΤΕΝΙΣΕ ΕΚΕΙ ΣΤΟ ΣΤΑΥΡΟ"
Verse 1:
Atenise ekei sto Stafro,
(Stare there at the Cross,)
kai des to Sotira, ton Iisoy Christo!
(and see the Savior, Jesus Christ!)
Anevike pano sto skliro Stafro
(He ascended upon the cruel Cross)
na sotheis esy ki ego!
(may you and I be saved!)
Chorus:
Doxa s' Afton, s' Afton pou m' agapise!
(Glory to Him, to Him who loved me!)
Doxa s' Afton, s' Afton pou me lytrose!
(Glory to Him, to Him who redeemed me!)
Doxa, timi sto mono Sotira, Amin!
(Glory, honor to the only Savior, Amen!)
Verse 2:
Atenise sto Golgotha
(Stare at Calvary)
kai des Poion kremasan sta sklira karfia!
(and see Whom they hung on the hard nails!)
I agapi Ton esteile pano sto Stafro
(Love sent Him to the Cross)
na sotheis esy ki ego!
(may you and I be saved!)
Verse 3:
O! psalte kai seis sto Theo!
(Oh! sing to God too!)
Psalte, doxaste to Dimiourgo!
(Sing, praise the Creator!)
I agapi Ton esteile pano sto Stafro
(Love sent Him to the Cross)
na sotheis esy ki ego!
(may you and I be saved!)
