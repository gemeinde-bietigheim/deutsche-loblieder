314 EIN' I KARDIA MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 314 | "ΕΙΝ' Η ΚΑΡΔΙΑ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Ein' i kardia mou mprosta Sou ftochi,
(My heart is poor before You,)
ma ploytise tin Esy, nai, Esy
(but make her rich You, yes, You)
me tis agapis, Christe, t' agatha,
(with love, Christ, the goods,)
pou 'doses sto Golgotha!
(that you gave me at Calvary!)
Chorus:
Dos' mou ti chari na Se doxazo,
(Give me the grace to glorify You,)
o Kyrie, pantotina!
(oh Lord, forever!)
Kai i psychi mou gemati chara
(And my soul full of joy)
eis tou stafroy Sou na zei ti skia!
(may the shadow of Your cross live!)
Verse 2:
Eisai gia mena i moni odos,
(You are the only way for me)
o Kyrios kai Theos, o Theos,
(the Lord and God, God,)
pou m' odigeis ap' to cheri Esy
(that you lead me by the hand)
se mia kainoyria zoi!
(to a new life!)
Verse 3:
Otan mazi Sou ego prochoro
(When with You I advance)
sto dromo afto to steno, to steno,
(on the road this narrow, narrow,)
tha 'cho eirini vathia stin kardia
(I will have peace deep in my heart)
kai mes sti mayri nychtia!
(and into the dark night!)
