353 ChRISTE MOY, SAN SYLLOGISTO

Greek Hymns - No. 353 | "ΧΡΙΣΤΕ ΜΟΥ, ΣΑΝ ΣΥΛΛΟΓΙΣΤΩ"
Verse 1:
Christe mou, san syllogisto
(Christ, I think)
to ergo Sou to thafmasto,
(Your work is wonderful,)
m' agapi skyvo na sou po S' efcharisto!
(With love, I bow down to say thank you!)
Christe, gia mena sto stafro
(Christ, for me on the cross)
agona palepses skliro,
(fight you fought hard)
me lytroses ap' ton echthro, S' efcharisto!
(You saved me from the enemy, I thank You!)
Chorus:
Gia ti zoi S' efcharisto!
(Thank you for life!)
Gia ti chara S' efcharisto!
(Thank you for the pleasure!)
Gia tin agapi Sou, Christe, S' efcharisto!
(For Your love, Christ, I thank You!)
Gia ti glykia Sou syntrofia,
(For Your sweet companionship,)
gia tin platia Sou agkalia,
(for Your wide embrace,)
gia ti megali Sou kardia S' efcharisto!
(Thank you for your big heart!)
Verse 2:
Konta mou irthes me storgi,
(You came close to me with affection,)
mou giatrepses kathe pligi,
(you healed my every wound,)
mou 'doses fos ap' tin pigi, S' efcharisto!
(You gave me light from the source, thank you!)
Christe, dikos Sou tora ego
(Christ, I am Yours now)
pantotina tha S' eflogo,
(forever I will bless you,)
aionia tha S' efgnomono, S' efcharisto!
(I will be eternally grateful to you, thank you!)
Verse 3:
Christe, mazi Sou perpato,
(Christ, with You I walk,)
Christe, to cheri Sou krato,
(Christ, I hold Your hand,)
kai mou milas kai Sou milo, S' efcharisto!
(and you talk to me and I talk to you, thank you!)
Gia kathe «nai» S' efcharisto!
(For every "yes" I thank you!)
Gia kathe «ochi» S' agapo!
(For every "no" I love you!)
Gia kathe logo Sou zesto S' efcharisto!
(For all your warm words, thank you!)
