615 ENAN ILIO EChO STIN KARDIA

Greek Hymns - No. 615 | "ΕΝΑΝ ΗΛΙΟ ΕΧΩ ΣΤΗΝ ΚΑΡΔΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Enan ilio echo stin kardia
(I have a sun in my heart)
pou me metha me fos!
(that intoxicates me with light!)
Tetoion ilio den echei kaneis
(No one has such a sun)
oyte ki o ouranos!
(not even the sky!)
Chorus:
Theio fos, chara, zoi,
(Divine light, joy, life,)
Sy, Christe mou, ilie tis psychis!
(You, my Christ, sun of the soul!)
Ti chari Sou vrady proi
(Your grace night and morning)
panta psallo eftychis!
(always singing happy!)
Verse 2:
De fovoymai to skotadi pia,
(I'm not afraid of the dark anymore)
mou ftanei o Christos!
(Christ is enough for me!)
Ola lampoun, lampo ki ego
(Everything shines, I shine too)
m' ena ouranio fos!
(with a heavenly light!)
Verse 3:
Ston paradeiso apo tora zo,
(In heaven from now on I live,)
m' Afton san perpato!
(with Him as I walk!)
Eftychia noiotho diarkos,
(I feel happiness all the time,)
sta ypsi, nai, peto!
(soaring, yes, I fly!)
