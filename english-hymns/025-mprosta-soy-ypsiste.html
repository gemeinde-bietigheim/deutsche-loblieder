25 MPROSTA SOY, YPsISTE

Greek Hymns - No. 25 | "ΜΠΡΟΣΤΑ ΣΟΥ, ΥΨΙΣΤΕ"
Verse 1:
Mprosta sou, Ypsiste, stekomaste thnitoi,
(Before you, Rise up, we mortals stand,)
Pros Se ypsonoume ton nou kai tin psychi!
(To You we raise our mind and soul!)
Pros Se, Thee,
(To You, God,)
tin doxan oli, tin timi prosferoume!
(all the glory, we offer the honor!)
Verse 2:
Ton mono Sou Yio ton esteiles, Thee,
(You sent Your only Son, God,)
na ginei ilasmos sto Golgotha gia me!
(to be glorified at Calvary for me!)
To «efcharisto»
(the "thank you")
Sy dex' ap' tin kardia thermo kai tapeino!
(Accept from the heart warmly and humbly!)
Verse 3:
Kai me to Pneyma Sou mas dineis ti zoi
(And with Your Spirit you give us life)
kai niki, dynami, chara pantotini!
(and victory, strength, joy everlasting!)
Pros Se, Thee,
(To You, God,)
ti doxa oli kai timi prosferoume!
(all glory and honor we offer!)
Verse 4:
Aggelon straties to onoma Sou ymnoyn
(Hosts of angels praise Your name)
ki oi tholoi t' ouranoy donoyntai ki antichoyn!
(and the domes of heaven vibrate and resound!)
O! dexou Sy
(Oh! accept Sy)
ton ymno tis latreias mas kai tin timi!
(the hymn of our worship and honor!)
