277 PERIMENEIS DIA TIN EPAGGELIA

Greek Hymns - No. 277 | "ΠΕΡΙΜΕΝΕΙΣ ΔΙΑ ΤΗΝ ΕΠΑΓΓΕΛΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Perimeneis dia tin epaggelia tou Patros
(You wait for the promise of the Father)
kai s' esena na dothei;
(and to you be given?)
Dynasai na vaptistheis kai sy dia tou Pneymatos,
(You can also be baptized by the Spirit,)
opos oloi oi pistoi!
(like all believers!)
Chorus:
Tha plirosei ti zoi sou m' eflogia,
(He will repay your life with blessing,)
tha sou dosei anapafsi, tha sou dosei dynami,
(will give you rest, will give you strength,)
to potirion sou tha ypercheilizei
(your glass will overflow)
me to Pneyma t' Agio!
(with the Holy Spirit!)
Verse 2:
Katharisate ta skeyi sas me aima tou Amnoy,
(You have cleansed your vessels with the blood of the Lamb,)
elthet' oloi sto vomo,
(come all to the altar,)
perimenete me pisti eis ta podia tou Theoy
(wait with faith at the feet of God)
dia ton Paraklito!
(through the Advocate!)
Verse 3:
Ateleioto, nai, einai tou Theoy to eleos
(Endless, yes, is God's mercy)
ki i agapi Tou polli,
(and His love is great,)
kata tin yposchesi tis doreas tou Pneymatos
(upon the promise of the gift of the Spirit)
tha plironei tin psychi!
(will pay the soul!)
Verse 4:
«Potamoi ydaton zonton» tha pigazoun apo se
("Rivers of living water" will flow from you)
se psyches amartoles!
(to sinful souls!)
O Christos tha doxasthei kai tha eisai pantote
(Christ will be glorified and you will always be)
se ouranies chares!
(to heavenly joys!)
