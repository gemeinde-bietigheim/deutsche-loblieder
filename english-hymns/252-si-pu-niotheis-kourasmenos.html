252 Si Pu Niotheis Kourasmenos

Greek Hymns - No. 252 | "You Who Feel Weary"
Verse 1:
Sy pou niotheis kourasmenos,
(You who feel weary,)
dystychis ki amartolos,
(unhappy and sinful,)
ela tora na se sosei
(come now to save you)
me to aima Tou o Christos!
(with His blood, Christ!)
Chorus:
Mono to aima, mono to aima,
(Only the blood, only the blood,)
to aima pou etrekse epano sto stavro!
(the blood that flowed upon the cross!)
Ela tora, ela tora,
(Come now, come now,)
to aima plenei kai ton pio amartolo!
(the blood washes even the most sinful!)
Verse 2:
Tis pliges mas tis giatrevei,
(Our wounds He heals,)
richnei valsamo glyko
(pours sweet balm)
kai gemizei tis kardies mas
(and fills our hearts)
me ouranio thisavro!
(with heavenly treasure!)
Verse 3:
Pare tou stavrou to dromo,
(Take the way of the cross,)
o Christos se proskalei
(Christ invites you)
na se plynei, na se peisei
(to wash you, to convince you)
pos kai sy echeis sothei!
(that you too have been saved!)
