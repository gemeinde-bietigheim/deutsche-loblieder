479 EIMAST' EMEIS LAOS ThEOY

Greek Hymns - No. 479 | "ΕΙΜΑΣΤ' ΕΜΕΙΣ ΛΑΟΣ ΘΕΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Eimast' emeis laos Theoy, laos tou ouranoy!
(We are the people of God, the people of heaven!)
To kaychima mas o Christos ki i agapi tou Theoy!
(Our boast is Christ and the love of God!)
Pisteyoume eis to Christo, prochoroyme me chara
(We believe in Christ, we move forward with joy)
gia ti patrida mas psila, ekei ston ourano!
(for our country up there in the sky!)
Verse 2:
Laos tou ouranoy sti gi ki ap' oles tis fyles,
(People of heaven on earth and of all races,)
ypsonoume sta cheria mas tis agies Grafes!
(we raise the Holy Scriptures in our hands!)
Adelfomena trechoume me koino proorismo,
(As brothers we run with a common destination,)
na fthasoume oloi mazi ekei ston ourano!
(let's all get there together in heaven!)
Verse 3:
Anikoume ki emeis s' afto ton endoxo lao
(We also belong to this glorious people)
kai me ti chari tou Theoy vadizoume empros!
(and with the grace of God we march forward!)
To vlemma mas ypsonoume sto Theo ekei psila,
(We look up to God up there,)
mazi Tou gia na zisoume me doxa kai chara!
(with Him to live with glory and joy!)
Verse 4:
Gia tin patrida mas afti agonizomaste
(We are fighting for this country)
kai na sothoyn polles psyches, nai, prosefchomaste
(and many souls be saved, yes we pray)
na vroun thnitoi ton Iisoy, na gnorisoun to Theo
(to find Jesus as mortals, to know God)
ki oloi mazi na zisoume ekei ston Ourano!
(and let us all live together there in Heaven!)
