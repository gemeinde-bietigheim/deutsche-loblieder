291 AYXISI PISTEOS

Greek Hymns - No. 291 | "ΑΥΞΗΣΗ ΠΙΣΤΕΩΣ"
Verse 1:
Ayxisi pisteos n' apoktiseis potheis
(Increase faith to acquire desires)
ki i efchi sou na einai afto.
(and let this be your wish.)
De tha anapaftheis oyte tha eflogitheis,
(You will not rest nor be blessed,)
an de doseis to pan sto Christo!
(if you don't give everything to Christ!)
Chorus:
Ta thysiases ola dia to Christo;
(Did you sacrifice everything for Christ?)
I kardia sou pistos martyrei;
(Does your heart testify faithfully?)
Nai, tha anapaftheis kai eirini tha vreis,
(Yes, you will rest and find peace,)
an ta panta gi' afton arnitheis!
(if you deny everything about him!)
Verse 2:
Theleis n' akoloutheis eis to fos tis Grafis,
(You want to follow in the light of Scripture,)
alla eis' ellipis sti zoi;
(but are you lacking in life?)
Eis to thelima Aftoy tote tha efretheis,
(In His will then you will be found,)
otan agiastheis stin psychi!
(when you are sanctified in the soul!)
Verse 3:
Dia tis prosefchis kai agias zois
(Through prayer and a holy life)
sy mporeis na areseis s' Afton!
(you can please Him!)
Kathe ti kosmiko prepei na arnitheis
(Everything worldly you must deny)
kai prothymos na fereis stafro!
(and willingly to bear a cross!)
Verse 4:
Ployto, doxa, chara, tha 'cheis mes stin psychi
(Wealth, glory, joy, you will have in your soul)
kai gia panta tha eisai eftychis!
(and forever you will be happy!)
Koinonia agni tha 'cheis me to Theo,
(You will have pure communion with God,)
an sta podia Tou tora richteis!
(if you fall at His feet now!)
