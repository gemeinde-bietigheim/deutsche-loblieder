469 O! ESY P' AKOYS TA LOGIA

Greek Hymns - No. 469 | "Ω! ΕΣΥ Π' ΑΚΟΥΣ ΤΑ ΛΟΓΙΑ"
Verse 1:
O! Esy p' akoys ta logia tou Christoy toso kairo
(Oh! You who have been listening to Christ's words for so long)
kai akoma tin kardia sou den tin edoses s' Afton!
(and you still haven't given your heart to Him!)
Nai, to vlepo pos s' aresoun, prosexe mi gelasteis
(Yes, I can see that you like them, be careful not to laugh)
kai nomiseis pos me toyto tha mporeseis na sotheis!
(and you think that with this you will be able to be saved!)
Chorus:
O! An den apofasiseis na dotheis eis to Christo,
(Oh! If you do not decide to give yourself to Christ,)
mataios o erchomos sou, eisai xenos eis Afton!
(your coming is in vain, you are a stranger to Him!)
Ki omos, nai, kai gia se echyse aima, amartole,
(And yet, yes, blood was shed for you too, sinner,)
kai ese karterei, min argeis, deili psychi!
(and you care, don't be late, you cowardly soul!)
Verse 2:
O! Polles fores tha echeis ap' to stoma tou Christoy,
(Oh! Many times you will have from the mouth of Christ,)
nai, akoysei pos monacha me to aima tou stafroy
(yes, hear how only with the blood of the cross)
tha mporeseis na xefygeis apo ta frikta desma
(you will be able to escape from the horrible bonds)
tis dikis sou amartias, opou sklavo se krata!
(of your own sin, where it holds you a slave!)
