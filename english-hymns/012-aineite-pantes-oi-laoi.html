12 AINEITE PANTES OI LAOI

Greek Hymns - No. 12 | "ΑΙΝΕΙΤΕ ΠΑΝΤΕΣ ΟΙ ΛΑΟΙ"
Verse 1:
Aineite pantes oi laoi
(Praise all peoples)
ton Ypsiston Theon!
(the Most High God!)
Ymnoyn Afton oi ouranoi!
(The heavens praise Him!)
Doxasate Afton!
(Praise Him!)
Chorus:
Doxa, doxa, ymneite ton Theon, Theon
(Glory, glory, praise God, God)
epi ti gis kai eis ton Ouranon!
(on earth and in Heaven!)
Doxasate Afton, Anarchon, Krataion,
(Praise Him, Anarch, Crateon,)
doxa, doxa, aineite ton Theon, Theon!
(glory, glory, praise God, God!)
Verse 2:
Ein' anarchos kai ischyros,
(He is anarchic and powerful,)
i cheir Tou krataia,
(His hand is strong,)
en tois ypsistois o Theos
(God in the highest)
to sympan kyverna!
(the universe rules!)
Verse 3:
Dikaiosyni met' Aftoy
(Justice with Him)
ki eirini katoikoyn,
(and peace dwell,)
laoi kai ethni epi gis
(peoples and nations on earth)
Afton as proskynoyn!
(Let them worship him!)
