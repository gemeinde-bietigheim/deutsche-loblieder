120 DOXASMENO T' ONOMA SOY

Greek Hymns - No. 120 | "ΔΟΞΑΣΜΕΝΟ Τ' ΟΝΟΜΑ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Doxasmeno t' onoma Sou to agio,
(Blessed be Your holy name,)
megale efergeti!
(great benefactor!)
Doxasmeno t' onoma Sou to agio,
(Blessed be Your holy name,)
megale efergeti!
(great benefactor!)
Chorus:
Eprepe ego na eicha chathei,
(I should have been lost)
na gino fotia, na gino spondi,
(to be a fire, to be a libation,)
ki irthes Esy kai pethanes gia mena,
(and You came and died for me,)
kai pethanes gia mena
(and you died for me)
kai mou 'doses zoi, kai mou 'doses zoi!
(and you gave me life, and you gave me life!)
Verse 2:
Zoysa mes stin amartia prota
(I was living in sin first)
kai m' eftheire o chronos
(and time spoiled me)
ki o misthos tis amartias itan
(and the wages of sin was)
thanatos kai ponos!
(death and pain!)
Verse 3:
Tora niotho tis kardias to dakry
(Now I feel the tear of the heart)
na kylaei gia Sena
(to flow for You)
kai tharro pos anavlyzei pali
(and I dare say it springs up again)
t' Agio Sou aima!
(Your Holy Blood!)
Verse 4:
O karpos tou Pneymatos Sou einai
(The fruit of Your Spirit is)
i zoi gia mena
(life for me)
kai zito na me gemizei panta
(and I ask that it fill me always)
t' Agio Sou Pneyma!
(Your Holy Spirit!)
