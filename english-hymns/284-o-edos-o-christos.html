284 O, EDOS' O ChRISTOS

Greek Hymns - No. 284 | "Ω, ΕΔΩΣ' Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ"
Verse 1:
O, edos' o Christos to Pneyma kai s' emas,
(Oh, Christ gave the Spirit to us too,)
aionio frouro eirinis kai charas!
(eternal guardian of peace and joy!)
Afotou ilth' entos to Pneyma t' Agio,
(After the Holy Spirit came in,)
agapi statheri echo pros to Theo!
(constant love I have for God!)
Chorus:
To Pneyma t' Agio, to Pneyma t' Agio
(The Holy Spirit, the Holy Spirit)
flogizei ti kardia mou! Doxa eis to Theo!
(it sets my heart on fire! Glory be to God!)
Thelo na deitho, thelo na martyro,
(I want to witness, I want to testify)
afotou ilth' entos to Pneyma t' Agio!
(after the Holy Spirit came inside!)
Verse 2:
Sta podia tou Christoy prosfero eafton,
(At the feet of Christ I offer myself,)
thysia tapeini sto mono mou Theo,
(humble sacrifice to my only God,)
pou edose gia me to mono Tou Yio,
(who gave for His only Son,)
gia na plistho charas kai Theiou Pneymatos!
(to be filled with joy and the Holy Spirit!)
Verse 3:
Plisiase, thnite, ki esy, amartole,
(Draw near, mortal, and you, sinner,)
ki o Iisoys Christos tha sosei kai ese!
(and Jesus Christ will save you too!)
Mi meneis adranis, mi chaneis ton kairo,
(Don't be idle, don't waste time,)
trexe na laveis sy to Pneyma t' Agio!
(run to receive the Holy Spirit!)
Verse 4:
As psalloume mazi sto mega Lytroti
(Let us sing together to the great Redeemer)
na dosoume s' Afton latreia kai timi!
(to give Him worship and honor!)
O! Alliloyia! Amin! As krazoume s' Afton!
(Oh! Alleluia! Amen! Let us cling to Him!)
Edothei kai se mas to Pneyma t' Agio!
(The Holy Spirit is given to us too!)
