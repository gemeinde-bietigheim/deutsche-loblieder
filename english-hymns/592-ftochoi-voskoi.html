592 FTOChOI VOSKOI

Greek Hymns - No. 592 | "ΦΤΩΧΟΙ ΒΟΣΚΟΙ"
Verse 1:
Ftochoi voskoi pou ta provata
(Poor shepherds tending the sheep)
mes stin krya ti nychta efylagan,
(in the cold night they kept,)
aggelon foni apo psila
(angel's voice from above)
theio minyma tou Ouranoy akousan!
(divine message of Heaven they heard!)
Chorus:
Chara! Chara! Christoygenna!
(Joy! Joy! Christmas!)
Simera egennithi Sotir!
(Sotir was born today!)
Verse 2:
Magoi sofoi me dora polla
(Wise magicians with many gifts)
m' odigo tous t' asteri evadizan!
(they walked with the star as their guide!)
Gnorizan aftoi pos irthe sti gi
(They knew how he came to earth)
o Sotiras tou kosmou se fatni ftochi!
(the Savior of the world in a poor manger!)
