496 OTAN PERA APO T' ASTERIA

Greek Hymns - No. 496 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΠΕΡΑ ΑΠΟ Τ' ΑΣΤΕΡΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Otan pera apo t' asteria,
(When beyond the stars,)
pano eis ton ourano
(up in the sky)
fthaso, tha Se do, Sotira,
(come, I will see Thee, Saviour,)
prosopo pros prosopo!
(face to face!)
Chorus:
Tou Sotira mou ti doxa
(To my Savior be the glory)
kai to kallos to lampro
(and the beautiful the brilliant)
th' apolayso ekei aionia,
(I will enjoy there forever,)
prosopo pros prosopo!
(face to face!)
Verse 2:
Tora opos se kathrefti
(Now like in a mirror)
vlepo ainigmatodos,
(i see puzzlingly)
ma se ligo tha Se vlepo
(but I will see you soon)
prosopo pros prosopo!
(face to face!)
Verse 3:
Tote lypes, ponoi, thlipseis
(Then sorrows, pains, sorrows)
de tha ein' ston ourano,
(they won't be in heaven,)
tote eftychis tha eimai
(then I will be happy)
pantote me to Christo!
(always with Christ!)
Verse 4:
O! ti doxa aionia
(Oh! what glory eternity)
oi pistoi tha aisthanthoyn,
(believers will feel,)
otan tha Se synantisoun
(when they will meet You)
kai ti doxa Sou tha doyn!
(and Your glory they will see!)
