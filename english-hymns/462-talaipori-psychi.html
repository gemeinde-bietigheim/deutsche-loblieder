462 TALAIPORI PsYChI

Greek Hymns - No. 462 | "ΤΑΛΑΙΠΩΡΗ ΨΥΧΗ"
Verse 1:
Talaipori psychi, anapafsi ziteis
(Suffering soul, you ask for rest)
thlimmeni kai anisychi, vlepe, min planitheis!
(sad and anxious, see, do not go astray!)
Verse 2:
Ouranogenniti, athanati psychi,
(Heaven-born, immortal soul,)
o kosmos olos pia gia se chara de chorigei!
(the whole world no longer grants you joy!)
Verse 3:
O, na tis kivotoy i thyra anoikti,
(Oh, the door of the ark is open,)
psychi mou, speyse gia na mpeis amesos, prin kleisthei!
(my soul, hurry to enter now, before it closes!)
Verse 4:
Ekei asfaleia, ekei anapafsi,
(There safety, there rest,)
ekei aionia chara kai agalliasi!
(there eternal joy and rejoicing!)
Verse 5:
Isychase loipon talaipori psychi,
(So rest in peace, suffering soul,)
o kosmos olos, pia gia se, chara de chorigei!
(the whole world, for you, no longer grants joy!)
