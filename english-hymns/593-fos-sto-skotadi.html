593 FOS STO SKOTADI

Greek Hymns - No. 593 | "ΦΩΣ ΣΤΟ ΣΚΟΤΑΔΙ"
Verse 1:
Fos sto skotadi, niata Christoy,
(Light in the darkness, youth of Christ,)
niki skorpate pantoy!
(spread victory everywhere!)
Neo tragoydi, ymnos charas,
(New song, hymn of joy,)
anthos kainoyrias genias!
(new generation flower!)
Chorus:
Niata Christoy, me tharros mprosta,
(Youth of Christ, with courage ahead,)
eimast' emeis tou Theoy ta paidia!
(we are God's children!)
De mas fovizei i skoteinia,
(We are not afraid of the dark,)
echoume fos stin kardia!
(we have light in our hearts!)
Verse 2:
Stis dyskolies, niata Christoy,
(In difficulties, youth of Christ,)
nikes kratoyn tou stafroy!
(victories keep of the cross!)
Stis ponemenes kai amartoles
(To the sick and sinners)
fernoun eirini psyches!
(bring peace to souls!)
Verse 3:
Mesa sti lypi, ston peirasmo,
(In sorrow, in temptation,)
koita psila sto Stafro!
(look up at the Cross!)
Eimaste emeis oi thriamveftes,
(We are the triumphant ones,)
oloi Christoy machites!
(all Christ fighters!)
