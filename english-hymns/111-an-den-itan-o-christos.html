111 AN DEN ITAN O ChRISTOS

Greek Hymns - No. 111 | "ΑΝ ΔΕΝ ΗΤΑΝ Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ"
Verse 1:
An den itan o Christos, otan planiomouna,
(Had it not been for Christ, when I was wandering,)
an den itan o Christos, pou tha vriskomouna;
(if it wasn't for Christ, where would I be?)
An den itan o Christos, dichos storgi
(If it weren't for Christ, without affection)
san kalami sto voria tha eicha tsakistei!
(like a reed in the north I would have broken!)
Chorus:
O,ti ki an echo s' Afton to chreosto
(Whatever I have to Him I owe)
ki oso antecho gi' Afton tha milo!
(and as long as I endure I will speak of Him!)
Ston Kyrio mou na moiaso lachtaro
(I long to appear to my Lord)
kai sta ourania na mi ntropiasto!
(and in the heavens not to be ashamed!)
Verse 2:
An den epsachn' o Christos me pono na me vrei
(If Christ did not search with pain to find me)
sta sokakia tis zois pou 'cha pagideftei,
(in the alleys of life I'm trapped,)
an den epsachne o Christos, choris to fos
(if Christ did not seek, without the light)
stis charadres tis zois tha eicha gkremistei!
(in the ravines of life I would have fallen!)
Verse 3:
An den eicha to Christo, san me pligosane
(If I didn't have Christ, as if they hurt me)
ki ap' tis portes tous sklira otan me dioxane,
(and from their doors harshly when they drove me out,)
an den eicha to Christo na me dechthei,
(if I did not have Christ to receive me,)
san dentraki sto chionia tha eicha marathei!
(I would have withered like a tree in the snow!)
