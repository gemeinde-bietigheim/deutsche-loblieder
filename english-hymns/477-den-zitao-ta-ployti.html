477 DEN ZITAO TA PLOYTI

Greek Hymns - No. 477 | "ΔΕΝ ΖΗΤΑΩ ΤΑ ΠΛΟΥΤΗ"
Verse 1:
Den zitao ta ployti kai ta alla tis gis,
(I do not seek the riches and other things of the earth,)
perimeno m' elpida ta «en tois ouranois».
(I await with hope "in the heavens".)
Se ouranio vivlio, se selida lefki,
(In a heavenly book, on a blank page,)
Iisoy, Lytroti mou, katagrafika ekei!
(Jesus, my Redeemer, I was recorded there!)
Chorus:
Nai, egraftika ekei se selida lefki!
(Yes, I signed up there on page white!)
Se ouranio vivlio katagrafika ekei!
(In a heavenly book I was recorded there!)
Verse 2:
Kokkines amarties san malli to lefko
(Red sins like white wool)
i porfyrines einai tha ginoun chion!
(or porphyrins are they will become chion!)
San tin ammo thalassis kai an einai aftes,
(Like the sand of the sea, even though they are)
omos t' Agio Sou aima katharizei kardies!
(but Your Holy blood cleanses hearts!)
Verse 3:
Opou leipoun oi thlipseis ponoi kai ta deina,
(Where sorrows pains and sufferings are absent,)
opou agioi oloi ntymenoi lefka!
(where saints all dressed in white!)
Ti oraia i poli opou fos katoikei!
(How beautiful is the city where light dwells!)
Kai ego me aggelous kategrafika ekei!
(And I with angels was registered there!)
