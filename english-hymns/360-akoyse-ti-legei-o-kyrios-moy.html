360 AKOYSE TI LEGEI O KYRIOS MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 360 | "ΑΚΟΥΣΕ ΤΙ ΛΕΓΕΙ Ο ΚΥΡΙΟΣ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Akouse ti legei o Kyrios mou:
(Hear what my Lord says:)
«Poios tha ypagei di' eme;
("Who shall fall for me?")
Poios tha ypagei ston agro mou,
(Who shall fall into my field,)
gia na ergasthei gia tis psyches;».
(to work for souls?')
Chorus:
Mila mou, Iisoy, mila mou kai etoimos ego,
(Speak to me, Jesus, speak to me and I am ready,)
prothymos, Kyrie, thelima Sou gia na ektelo!
(willing, Lord, Your will to perform!)
Verse 2:
Otan anthrakas pyros agiou
(When coal fire saint)
tou profiti cheili aggixe,
(the prophet's lips touched,)
akouse efthys foni Kyriou
(He immediately heard the voice of the Lord)
ki anafonise: «Idoy ego!».
(and he exclaimed: "Here I am!")
Verse 3:
Plithos apeiro stin amartia,
(Multitudes infinite in sin,)
oloi chanontai choris Christo.
(all are lost without Christ.)
Trexe simera me prothymia,
(Run today with eagerness,)
gia na peis: «Eim' etoimos ego!».
(to say: "I'm ready!".)
Verse 4:
Ystera to theros tha perasei,
(Then the summer will pass,)
tora ein' kairos tou therismoy,
(now is harvest time,)
o cheimonas grigora tha elthei,
(winter will soon come)
trexe ston agro tou Iisoy!
(run to the field of Jesus!)
