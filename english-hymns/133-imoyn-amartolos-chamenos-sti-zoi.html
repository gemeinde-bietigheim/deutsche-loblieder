133 IMOYN AMARTOLOS, ChAMENOS STI ZOI

Greek Hymns - No. 133 | "ΗΜΟΥΝ ΑΜΑΡΤΩΛΟΣ, ΧΑΜΕΝΟΣ ΣΤΗ ΖΩΗ"
Verse 1:
Imoun amartolos, chamenos sti zoi
(I was a sinner, lost in life)
kai epsachna na vro ligi chara,
(and I was looking to find some joy,)
mia mera xafnika me vrike o Christos
(one day suddenly Christ found me)
kai allax' i zoi mou entelos!
(and my life changed completely!)
Chorus:
Kyrie, odigise, tin kardia mou gemise
(Lord, lead, fill my heart)
me agapi perissia, agni!
(with love too much, pure!)
Dos' mou chari ston agro gia psyches na ergasto
(Give me grace in the field for souls to work)
kai konta sou mia mera na 'rtho!
(and to come to you one day!)
Verse 2:
Pothoysa sti zoi na vro ligi chara
(I longed to find some joy in life)
kai ena filo pou na m' agapa.
(and a friend to love me.)
Efrika to Christo, filo pragmatiko,
(I found Christ, a real friend,)
kai ena syntrofo pantotino!
(and a companion forever!)
Verse 3:
Tora o Iisoys me odigei pista
(Now Jesus leads me faithfully)
kai to timoni tis zois kratei!
(and the steering wheel of life holds!)
Noiotho na mou mila, to cheri mou vasta,
(I feel it talking to me, my hand reaches out,)
se droseres piges me odigei!
(to cool springs it leads me!)
Verse 4:
Aftos ein' o Christos, Sotiras mou pistos,
(This is Christ, my faithful Saviour,)
ki esena, file mou, se agapa!
(he loves you too, my friend!)
An erthei sti zoi, tha vreis parigoria,
(If it comes to life, you will find comfort,)
tha sou gemisei tin kardia chara!
(it will fill your heart with joy!)
