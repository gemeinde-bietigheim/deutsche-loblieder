618 ZITAS ChARA

Greek Hymns - No. 618 | "ΖΗΤΑΣ ΧΑΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Zitas chara sta vouna ta psila,
(You seek joy in the mountains high,)
se galano pelago ki ourano,
(in blue sea and sky,)
omos cheimonia kai varia katachnia
(but winter and heavy gloom)
sou kryvei afti ti chara!
(hides this joy from you!)
Verse 2:
Zitas chara se chrysi syntrofia
(You ask for joy in golden company)
me filikes anoichtes agkalies,
(with friendly open arms,)
omos oi stygnes tis alitheias stigmes
(but the harsh moments of truth)
se vriskoun sti monaxia!
(they find you lonely!)
Verse 3:
Zitas chara mes stin kathe kardia,
(You seek joy in every heart,)
na deis lampri kathenos ti matia,
(to see everyone's look bright,)
omos stin pikri, ti skliri ti zoi
(but in the bitter, hard life)
foliazei i pagonia!
(the frost is nesting!)
Verse 4:
Tragoudistis tis kainoyrias genias,
(Singer of the new generation,)
tha po pantoy enan ymno charas!
(I will sing everywhere a song of joy!)
Tou Christoy i zoi kai diki sou an genei,
(Christ's life and yours if born,)
tha echeis tote chara!
(you will be happy then!)
Verse 5:
Tha vreis chara stou Christoy ti matia
(You will find joy in Christ the look)
stin anoichti matomeni agkalia!
(in the open bloody embrace!)
Opou ki an vretheis o Christos Lytrotis
(Wherever you are, Christ the Redeemer)
tha se gemizei chara!
(it will fill you with joy!)
