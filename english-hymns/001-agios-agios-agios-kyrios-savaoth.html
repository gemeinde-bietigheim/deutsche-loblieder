1 AGIOS, AGIOS, AGIOS, KYRIOS SAVAOTh!

Greek Hymns - No. 1 | "ΑΓΙΟΣ, ΑΓΙΟΣ, ΑΓΙΟΣ, ΚΥΡΙΟΣ ΣΑΒΑΩΘ!"
Verse 1:
Agios, Agios, Agios, Kyrios Savaoth!
(Holy, Holy, Holy, Lord of hosts!)
Pliris tis doxis Sou o ouranos ki i gi!
(Heaven and earth are full of Your glory!)
Kyrie Pantokrator, Poiiti ton olon,
(Lord Pantokrator, Poet of all,)
s' Esena prepei doxa kai timi!
(You are due glory and honor!)
Verse 2:
Agios, Agios, Agios! Archaggelon plithi
(Holy, Holy, Holy! Archangel multitudes)
me foni alalagmoy, Thee mou, Se ymnoyn!
(with a loud voice, O God, they praise You!)
Cherouveim kai Serafeim pantotina ymnoyne
(Cherubim and Seraphim forever sing)
Se ap' aionon «nyn kai esaei»!
(For centuries "now and forever"!)
Verse 3:
Agios, Agios, Agios! Se doxologoyme!
(Holy, Holy, Holy! We praise you!)
Sy eisai fos aprosito, ta panta dioikeis!
(You are unreachable light, you control everything!)
Monos Sy eisai Agios, agies oi voules Sou,
(You alone are Holy, Your counsels are holy,)
dexou ton aino, Sotira tis psychis!
(receive the praise, Savior of the soul!)
Verse 4:
Agios, Agios, Agios! Se ymnei i ktisi!
(Holy, Holy, Holy! The building praises you!)
Se ainoyn ta erga Sou se ourano kai gi!
(They praise You for Your works in heaven and earth!)
Eis ton Kyrio mas kai Dimiourgo mas
(To our Lord and Creator)
anikei mono doxa kai timi!
(belongs only glory and honor!)
