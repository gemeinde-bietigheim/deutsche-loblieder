649 REEI ZOI APO SENA, IISOY

Greek Hymns - No. 649 | "ΡΕΕΙ ΖΩΗ ΑΠΟ ΣΕΝΑ, ΙΗΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Reei zoi apo Sena, Iisoy,
(Life flows from You, Jesus,)
potamos tora entos mou anavlyzei!
(a river now flows within me!)
Ametri chari pigazei apo Se,
(Immeasurable grace flows from Thee,)
mechri akomi ki emena aggizei!
(until it even touches me!)
Chorus:
I zontani elpida eisai Sy, Kyrie!
(The living hope is You, Lord!)
Ti doxa oli dinoume s' Esena!
(We give all the glory to You!)
To thanato mas nikises Esy, o Amne,
(Thou hast conquered our death, O Amne,)
Vasilia, Lytroti kai Thee mas!
(Our King, Redeemer and God!)
Verse 2:
Kathe agapi tou kosmou echei chathei
(All the love of the world is lost)
ki eisai Esy, Lytroti, i chara mas!
(and you, Redeemer, are our joy!)
Eisai «ta panta en pasi» tora Esy
(You are "all in all" now You)
kai o thronos Sou ein' i kardia mas!
(and Your throne is our heart!)
Verse 3:
Tora zitoyme n' anoixoun oi ouranoi
(Now we ask for the heavens to open)
kai to Pneyma, Christe, na ekchyseis!
(and the Spirit, Christ, pour out!)
Akouse, Kyrie, ta tekna Sou vooyn
(Hear, Lord, Your children cry)
me fotia, Iisoy, n' apantiseis!
(with fire, Jesus, answer!)
