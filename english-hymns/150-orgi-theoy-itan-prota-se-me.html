150 ORGI ThEOY ITAN PROTA SE ME

Greek Hymns - No. 150 | "ΟΡΓΗ ΘΕΟΥ ΗΤΑΝ ΠΡΩΤΑ ΣΕ ΜΕ"
Verse 1:
Orgi Theoy itan prota se me,
(God's wrath was first on me,)
se lakko vathy m' esyran enoches,
(guilt dragged me into a deep pit,)
alla fonaxa ston Iisoy stin prosefchi
(but I cried out to Jesus in prayer)
pou me esose kai m' ekane eftychi!
(who saved me and made me happy!)
Chorus:
Ap' ton pilo me anevase,
(He lifted me up from the clay,)
pano stin petra m' estise
(on the stone he set me)
kai eis to stoma mou evale
(and put it in my mouth)
ymno neo, Alliloyia!
(new hymn, Hallelujah!)
Verse 2:
Se vracho aionon me anypsose,
(On a rock of ages he raised me,)
ta podia mou ekei estereose,
(my feet there solidified,)
asfaleia echo otan eimai ekei,
(I have security when I'm there,)
me ti chari Tou panta me voithei!
(by His grace He always helps me!)
Verse 3:
O, posi chara echo eis tin psychi!
(Oh, what joy I have in my soul!)
Ymno diarkos me glykeia odi,
(I sing continually with a sweet ode,)
echo martyria mesa stin kardia,
(I have testimony in my heart,)
afotou esothika apo ta desma!
(after I escaped from the bonds!)
Verse 4:
Tin chari Tou panta ego tha ymno,
(His grace I will always praise,)
gia tin kalosyni Tou tha omilo,
(of His goodness I will speak,)
eis ton ampelona Tou tha ergastho
(in His vineyard I will work)
kai to onoma Tou tha to martyro!
(and His name I will testify!)
Verse 5:
Apo vorvorodi pilo o Christos
(Christ is made of turbid clay)
elytrose kathe psychi thafmastos
(redeem every soul wonderfully)
kai tora fonazoun oloi sti zoi:
(and now everyone in life shouts:)
«Mas esose ki eimaste oloi eftycheis!».
("He saved us and we are all happy!")
