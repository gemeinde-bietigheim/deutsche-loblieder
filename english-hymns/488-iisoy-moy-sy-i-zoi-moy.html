488 IISOY MOY, SY I ZOI MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 488 | "ΙΗΣΟΥ ΜΟΥ, ΣΥ Η ΖΩΗ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Iisoy mou, Sy i zoi mou, Sy eftychia mou!
(My Jesus, You are my life, You are my happiness!)
Alithina m' agapises!
(You really loved me!)
Chara eis ti zoi mou, mou charises, Christe!
(You gave me joy in my life, Christ!)
Ki eim' eftychis panta me Se!
(And I am always happy with You!)
Chorus:
Potho na fthaso ston Christo mou,
(I long to reach my Christ,)
potho na ziso di' Afton,
(I long to live for Him,)
mechri na fthaso stin patrida,
(until i reach home)
ekei psila ston ourano!
(up there in the sky!)
Verse 2:
Noiotho oti vadizo eis tis chryses odoys
(I feel like I'm walking the golden streets)
tou ouranoy m' agious Tou!
(of heaven with His saints!)
Noiotho na plimmyrizei... ti endoxi chara,
(I feel it overflowing... what glorious joy,)
m' aggelous Tou na psalmodo!
(with His angels to chant!)
Verse 3:
Trechoun ta dakrya mou ap' ti lachtara mou,
(My tears flow from my longing,)
gia na Se do ston ourano!
(to see you in heaven!)
Dos' mou ti dynami Sou, Christe mou, pantote
(Give me Your strength, my Christ, always)
na prochoro gia ti Sion!
(to advance to Zion!)
