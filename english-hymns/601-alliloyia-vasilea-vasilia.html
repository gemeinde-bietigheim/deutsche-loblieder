601 ALLILOYIA! VASILEA, VASILIA!

Greek Hymns - No. 601 | "ΑΛΛΗΛΟΥΙΑ! ΒΑΣΙΛΕΑ, ΒΑΣΙΛΙΑ!"
Chorus:
Alliloyia! Vasilea, Vasilia!
(Alleluia! King, King!)
Alliloyia! Vasilea, Vasilia!
(Alleluia! King, King!)
Se doxazei oli i gi! Se ymnoyn oi ouranoi!
(The whole earth praises you! The heavens praise you!)
Alliloyia! Vasilea, Vasilia!
(Alleluia! King, King!)
Verse 1:
Alliloyia! Vasilea, Vasilia!
(Alleluia! King, King!)
Alliloyia! Vasilea, Vasilia!
(Alleluia! King, King!)
Eisai fos kai zoi, eisai agapi alithini!
(You are light and life, you are true love!)
Alliloyia! Vasilea, Vasilia!
(Alleluia! King, King!)
Verse 2:
Zei o Theos, o ischyros, o krataios!
(God lives, the mighty, the mighty!)
Zei o Theos, o pantodynamos Theos!
(God lives, God almighty!)
Nai, zei aftos, pou ein' pistos ki alithinos,
(Yes, he lives who is faithful and true,)
o pantokratoras, o Agios Theos!
(the Almighty, the Holy God!)
Verse 3:
Psalle s' Afton Ton pansofo Dimiourgo!
(Sing to Him the all-wise Creator!)
Psalle s' Afton Ton eleimona Theo!
(Sing to this merciful God!)
Psalle s' Afton Ton endoxo, Ton fovero!
(Sing to Him the glorious, the awesome!)
Psalle s' Afton Ton aionio Theo!
(Sing to Him the eternal God!)
