500 POSO ChAIRETAI I KARDIA MAS

Greek Hymns - No. 500 | "ΠΟΣΟ ΧΑΙΡΕΤΑΙ Η ΚΑΡΔΙΑ ΜΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Poso chairetai i kardia mas edo sti gi,
(How our hearts rejoice here on earth,)
giati echei ena Sotira mes stin psychi!
(because he has a Savior in his soul!)
Echei Ekeinon pou estafrothei sto Golgotha
(It has Him crucified on Calvary)
kai mas edose theia chara!
(and gave us divine joy!)
Chorus:
O, nai, ki ego ekei tha zo!
(Oh yes, I will live there too!)
Me lampri stoli, alliloyia,
(In glorious garb, hallelujah,)
tha efretho!
(I will be found!)
Verse 2:
Tha 'rthei mera pou ta panta tha xechastoyn,
(There will come a day when everything will be forgotten,)
opos ta louloydia svinoun, san marathoyn,
(as flowers fade, as wither,)
ma mia doxa mas prosmenei ekei psila,
(but a glory awaits us up there,)
st' ouranoy ti gi, chora lampri!
(in heaven the earth, glorious country!)
Verse 3:
Nai, afti i chora einai gia tous pistoys
(Yes, this country is for believers)
pou tha lavoun sti kardia tous ton Iisoy!
(who will receive Jesus in their hearts!)
Kai sto terma tha tous dosei stemma lampro,
(And at the end he will give them a brilliant crown,)
otan tha vrethoyn ston ourano!
(when they will be in heaven!)
Verse 4:
O! Ti endoxi imera, otan ki ego
(Oh! What a glorious day, when I)
me agious eis to throno Tou tha vretho!
(with saints in His throne I will be found!)
Kai tha psallo me aggelous pantotina
(And I will sing with angels forever)
stin oraia gi tin pothiti!
(in the beautiful land the lustful!)
