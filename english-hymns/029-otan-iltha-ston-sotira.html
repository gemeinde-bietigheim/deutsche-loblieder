29 OTAN ILThA STON SOTIRA

Greek Hymns - No. 29 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΗΛΘΑ ΣΤΟΝ ΣΩΤΗΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Otan iltha sto Sotira
(When I came to Sotira)
pou stafrothike gia me,
(who was crucified for me)
aplose s' eme to cheri
(reach out to me)
kai efthys me lytrose!
(and redeem me at once!)
Chorus:
Tha doxazo, tha doxazo
(I will glorify, I will glorify)
ton Amno tou Golgotha!
(the Lamb of Calvary!)
Pantote tha ton doxazo,
(I will always praise him,)
giati m' esose ap' ta desma!
(because he saved me from the shackles!)
Verse 2:
Aima sto stafro echythei,
(Blood is shed on the cross,)
ilasmos tis enochis,
(guilty pleasure,)
kai esothik' i psychi mou
(and my soul was hidden)
ap' to fovo tis orgis!
(for fear of wrath!)
Verse 3:
San thysia tha prosfero
(As a sacrifice I will offer)
ti zoi mou sto Christo,
(my life in Christ,)
kathe mera tha doxazo
(every day I will glorify)
t' onoma Tou to septo!
(His name is the seventh!)
Verse 4:
Otan i odos steneyei,
(When the road narrows,)
tote vlepo sto Christo,
(then I see in Christ,)
fero ola mou ta vari
(I carry all my burdens)
kai t' afino eis Afton!
(and I leave it to Him!)
