68 PES MOY N' AKOYSO TO LOGO

Greek Hymns - No. 68 | "ΠΕΣ ΜΟΥ Ν' ΑΚΟΥΣΩ ΤΟ ΛΟΓΟ"
Verse 1:
Pes mou n' akoyso to Logo tis istorias tou Christoy,
(Tell me to hear the Word of Christ's story,)
pos irthe s' afto ton kosmo ap' to lampro ourano!
(how he came into this world from the bright sky!)
Otan kirychthike tote apo aggelous Theoy,
(When it was then preached by the angels of God,)
irth' o Sotiras sto kosmo, chara as ginei pantoy!
(the Savior has come to the world, let there be joy everywhere!)
Chorus:
Pes mou glykia istoria tou Iisoy mas Christoy!
(Tell me sweet story of our Jesus Christ!)
Dinei Aftos sotiria, as akoustei pantachoy!
(He gives salvation, let it be heard everywhere!)
Verse 2:
Stin erimo me nisteia kai synechi prosefchi
(In the desert with fasting and constant prayer)
itan saranta imeres choris kamia trofi!
(it was forty days without any food!)
Irthe s' Afton o peirazon, all' o Iisoys nikitis!
(The tempter came to Him, but Jesus is victorious!)
Kiryxe alitheia ston kosmo kai giatrepse astheneis!
(Preach truth to the world and heal the sick!)
Verse 3:
Pes pos kremasthik' Ekeinos ki apethane sto stafro
(Say how He hanged himself and died on the cross)
ki apo ton tafo anestithi kai zei eis ton ourano!
(and he rose from the grave and lives in heaven!)
Ein' tryferi i agapi tou Iisoy mou gia me,
(Is my Jesus' love for me tender,)
antilytro paredothi gia tis ftoches mas psyches!
(a ransom was given for our poor souls!)
