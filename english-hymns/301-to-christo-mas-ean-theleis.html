301 TO ChRISTO MAS EAN ThELEIS

Greek Hymns - No. 301 | "ΤΟ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ ΜΑΣ ΕΑΝ ΘΕΛΕΙΣ"
Verse 1:
To Christo mas ean theleis na akolouthiseis pistos,
(Our Christ if you will follow faithfully,)
egkateleipse ta panta di' Afton.
(gave up everything for Him.)
Na 'sai panta sti psychi sou agios kai katharos,
(May you always be holy and pure in your soul,)
gia na fthaseis asfalos ston ourano!
(to reach heaven safely!)
Chorus:
Ton Sotira sou an ton agapas,
(Your Savior if you love him,)
alithos sy fylage tis entoles!
(indeed you kept the commandments!)
Typos ton piston na 'sai pantote
(Always be a type of believers)
kai m' agapi akoloytha to Christo!
(and follow Christ with love!)
Verse 2:
Min planiesai me tous mythous ton anthropon tous plastoys.
(Don't be fooled by the myths of fake people.)
Eche pantote to Logo tou Theoy.
(Always have the Word of God.)
Einai tis zois o artos, synetizei tous aploys,
(It is the bread of life, it makes the simple wise,)
vadize panta m' Afton stous ouranoys!
(always walk with Him in the heavens!)
Verse 3:
Na thymasai oti o kosmos pantote paratirei,
(Remember the world is always watching,)
gia na mathei ean zoyme alithina
(to find out if we really live)
san Christianoi ton ergon amomoi kai katharoi!
(as Christians of the works unblemished and clean!)
As mi zoyme pleon ypokritika!
(Let's not live pretending anymore!)
Verse 4:
Dia toyto se protrepo na vadizeis diarkos,
(Therefore I exhort you to walk continually,)
gia na ftaseis asfalis ston ourano!
(to reach the sky safely!)
Eche pisti eis Ekeinon, elpize pantotinos,
(Have faith in Him, hope forever,)
mechris otou deis mia mera to Christo!
(until one day you see Christ!)
