505 SE PROSMENOYME NA 'RThEIS

Greek Hymns - No. 505 | "ΣΕ ΠΡΟΣΜΕΝΟΥΜΕ ΝΑ 'ΡΘΕΙΣ"
Verse 1:
Se prosmenoume na 'rtheis, Christe, Vasilia,
(We await your coming, Christ, King,)
mes stin tosi edo skoteinia!
(in so much darkness here!)
Ti matia mas ypsonoume panta psila
(We always look up)
na se doyme na 'rtheis, Vasilia!
(to see you come, King!)
Ti matia mas ypsonoume panta psila
(We always look up)
na se doyme na 'rtheis, Vasilia!
(to see you come, King!)
Verse 2:
Mes ston kosmo mas ponos kai thlipsi polli
(There is a lot of pain and sorrow in our world)
ki agonia i fysi perna!
(and anguish nature passes!)
Stenagmoi kai krafges antichoyne sti gi.
(Groans and cries echo through the land.)
Pote tha 'rtheis, Christe, Lytroti;
(When will you come, Christ, Redeemer?)
Stenagmoi kai krafges antichoyne sti gi.
(Groans and cries echo through the land.)
Pote tha 'rtheis, Christe, Lytroti;
(When will you come, Christ, Redeemer?)
Verse 3:
Ela, Kyrie, krazoun se Se oi pistoi
(Come, Lord, the faithful cry to You)
me to vlemma tous panta psila,
(with their gaze always up,)
na Se doun stis nefeles me doxa na 'rtheis!
(to see You in the clouds with glory to come!)
Min argeis, Vasilia, na faneis!
(Don't be late, King, to appear!)
na Se doun stis nefeles me doxa na 'rtheis!
(to see You in the clouds with glory to come!)
Min argeis, Vasilia, na faneis!
(Don't be late, King, to appear!)
