11 AGIOS, AGIOS, AGIOS O ThEOS

Greek Hymns - No. 11 | "ΑΓΙΟΣ, ΑΓΙΟΣ, ΑΓΙΟΣ Ο ΘΕΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Agios, Agios, Agios, Agios o Theos!
(Holy, Holy, Holy, Holy God!)
Agios, Agios, Agios, Agios, Ischyros!
(Holy, Holy, Holy, Holy, Mighty!)
Anarchos, Aionios, Megas, Krataios!
(Anarch, Eternal, Great, Mighty!)
Kyverna tin Plasi, stylos tou pantos!
(He rules Plasis, pillar of everything!)
Verse 2:
Doxa, doxa, doxa, ainos sto Theo!
(Glory, glory, glory, praise be to God!)
Ymno kai latreia as dosoume s' Afton!
(Hymn and worship let us give to Him!)
Edose ston kosmo doro ton Yio,
(He gave the world a gift of the Son,)
me Afton na sosei kath' amartolo!
(with Him to save every sinner!)
Verse 3:
Oloi as ymnoyme, ethni kai laoi,
(Let us all sing, nations and peoples,)
as doxologisei sympasa i gi!
(let the earth sing together!)
Lytrosi aionia ekam' o Christos!
(Redemption eternally made by Christ!)
«Doxa!» as fonaxei kath' amartolos.
("Glory!" let every sinner cry out.)
