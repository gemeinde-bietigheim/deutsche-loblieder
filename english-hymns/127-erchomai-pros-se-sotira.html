127 ERChOMAI PROS SE, SOTIRA

Greek Hymns - No. 127 | "ΕΡΧΟΜΑΙ ΠΡΟΣ ΣΕ, ΣΩΤΗΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Erchomai pros Se, Sotira, klaio kai thrino,
(I come to You, Savior, I cry and mourn,)
eis to throno tou eleous chari Sou zito!
(to the throne of mercy I ask for Your grace!)
Chorus:
O Sotira, erchomai pros Se,
(O Savior, I come to You,)
tin kardia mou Sou dino, sose kai eme!
(I give you my heart, save me too!)
Verse 2:
Mpros se Sena ego klino gony tapeino,
(Before You I bow my humble knee,)
tin kardia mou Sou dino, kathe pothito!
(I give you my heart, every desire!)
Verse 3:
Allaxe mou tin kardia, o, afto potho,
(Change my heart, oh that's what I crave)
kane m' ena neo ktisma, Se na ypireto!
(make me a new building, To serve you!)
Verse 4:
Opos esoses kai allous, sose kai eme,
(As you saved others, save me too,)
ap' ti plani kai to skotos fere me pros Se!
(from error and darkness bring me to You!)
Verse 5:
Oles mou tis amarties Sy tis sygchoreis
(You forgive all my sins)
kai aionia sotiria Sy mou chorigeis!
(and eternal salvation You grant me!)
