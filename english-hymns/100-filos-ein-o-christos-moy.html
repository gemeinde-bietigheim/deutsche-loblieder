100 FILOS EIN' O ChRISTOS MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 100 | "ΦΙΛΟΣ ΕΙΝ' Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Filos ein' o Christos mou, psychis mou Lytrotis,
(Christ is my friend, Redeemer of my soul,)
ein' o monos eis to kosmo di' eme,
(he is the only one in the world for me)
«to anthos tis koiladas», elpida tis psychis,
("the flower of the valley", hope of the soul,)
einai katafygio mou pantote!
(it is always my refuge!)
Aftos me prostateyei pantote kai pantoy,
(He protects me always and everywhere,)
mou legei «tharros tekno mi fovoy», Alliloyia!
(He says to me "Courage child do not be afraid", Hallelujah!)
Ein «to krino tis koiladas», «o Proinos Astir»,
(It is "the lily of the valley", "the Morning Star",)
ein' o monos tis psychis mou Lytrotis!
(He is the only Redeemer of my soul!)
Verse 2:
Tis amarties oles exaleipse Aftos,
(All sins blotted out by Him,)
kai mou dinei ekvasi stous peirasmoys.
(and he gives me an outcome to temptations.)
Gia oles mou tis thlipseis kai tis astheneies
(For all my sorrows and sicknesses)
plirose eis to stafro o Iisoys!
(Jesus paid on the cross!)
Mesa stis trikymies Ekeinos me kratei,
(In the storms He holds me,)
ston ourano Aftos me odigei, Alliloyia!
(to heaven He leads me, Hallelujah!)
Ein «to krino tis koiladas», «o Proinos Astir»,
(It is "the lily of the valley", "the Morning Star",)
ein' o monos tis psychis mou Lytrotis!
(He is the only Redeemer of my soul!)
Verse 3:
Pote de tha m' afisei se toyti ti zoi,
(He will never leave me in this life,)
oso ego to thelima Tou tha ektelo,
(as long as I do His will,)
mesa eis tous kindynous pisteyo sto Christo,
(in the midst of dangers I believe in Christ,)
me to Logo Tou tha parigoritho!
(by His Word I will be comforted!)
Mou dinei Agio Pneyma na me kratei pisto,
(He gives me the Holy Spirit to keep me faithful,)
pantote na mai martyras gi' Afton, Alliloyia!
(may I always be a witness for Him, Hallelujah!)
Ein «to krino tis koiladas», «o Proinos Astir»,
(It is "the lily of the valley", "the Morning Star",)
ein' o monos tis psychis mou Lytrotis!
(He is the only Redeemer of my soul!)
Verse 4:
Ton Iisoy an theleis, file, n' akoloutheis,
(Jesus if you want, friend, to follow,)
egkataleipse ta ola di' Afton!
(forsake all for Him!)
Me synesi na ziseis, na eisai alithis
(Live wisely, be true)
prochorontas pantote me to Christo!
(always moving forward with Christ!)
Aftos tha se fylaei, tha einai meta sou,
(He will guard you, he will be after you,)
panta tha s' odigei sto vio sou, Alliloyia!
(he will always lead you in your life, Hallelujah!)
Ein «to krino tis koiladas», «o Proinos Astir»,
(It is "the lily of the valley", "the Morning Star",)
ein' o monos tis psychis mou Lytrotis!
(He is the only Redeemer of my soul!)
