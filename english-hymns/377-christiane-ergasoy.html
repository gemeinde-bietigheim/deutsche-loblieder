377 ChRISTIANE, ERGASOY

Greek Hymns - No. 377 | "ΧΡΙΣΤΙΑΝΕ, ΕΡΓΑΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Christiane, ergasou, oso einai proi,
(Christian, work while it's morning,)
oso i drosia yparchei kai anthizei i gi.
(as long as there is dew and the earth blooms.)
Ergasou me agapi kai me epimoni,
(Work with love and perseverance,)
i nychta plisiazei, o Christos tha 'rthei!
(the night is drawing near, Christ will come!)
Verse 2:
Akomi mesimeri na eisai epimelis,
(Even at noon be diligent,)
erchetai, nai, se ligo i anapafsi.
(rest is coming, yes, soon.)
Kathe stigmi ergasou me pisti kai chara
(Every moment work with faith and joy)
i nychta plisiazei, tote tha 'nai arga.
(the night is drawing near, then it will be late.)
Verse 3:
Mechri na dysei o ilios, ergasou eis to fos,
(Till the sun goes down, work in the light,)
prin erthei to skotadi kai o thanatos,
(before darkness and death come,)
eche agapi s' olous enthermi kathari,
(have love for all ardent pure,)
i nychta plisiazei, payei i zoi.
(night draws near, life ceases.)
Verse 4:
Kiryxe eis ta ethni to Logo tou Theoy,
(Preach the Word of God to the nations,)
ypsose pan' ap' ola t' onoma tou Christoy!
(exalt above all the name of Christ!)
Kane omologia ti ekane se se,
(Confess what he did to you)
pes tous gia to Sotira oti erchetai!
(tell them about the Savior who is coming!)
