448 MES STI ThERMI TOY AGKALI

Greek Hymns - No. 448 | "ΜΕΣ ΣΤΗ ΘΕΡΜΗ ΤΟΥ ΑΓΚΑΛΗ"
Verse 1:
Mes sti thermi Tou agkali gyre,
(Into His warm embrace turn,)
gyre s' Afton, gyre s' Afton!
(turn to Him, turn to Him!)
Glyka o Christos tha s' agkaliasei,
(Sweetly Christ will embrace you,)
ela loipon s' Afton!
(so come to Him!)
Chorus:
Ela s' Afton kai mi fovitheis!
(Come to Him and do not be afraid!)
Ela s' Afton pou se proskalei!
(Come to Him who invites you!)
Ela s' Afton kai tha 'sai asfalis
(Come to Him and you will be safe)
mes stin agkali Tou!
(into His arms!)
Verse 2:
I chari Tou einai megali!
(His grace is great!)
Aftos sygchorei! Aftos sygchorei!
(He forgives! He forgives!)
To eleos Aftoy skepazei
(His mercy covers)
kathe sou enochi!
(all your guilt!)
Verse 3:
Tin kathe pikra sou i lypi
(Your every bitterness or sorrow)
pes tin s' Afton, pes tin s' Afton!
(tell it to Him, tell it to Him!)
Ki Ekeinos tote sou charizei
(And He then gives it to you)
chara kai thriamvo!
(joy and triumph!)
Verse 4:
Kai etsi kathe mera tha 'nai
(And so every day will be)
gia se o Christos, gia se o Christos!
(Christ for you, Christ for you!)
Kai panta i kardia tha leei
(And always the heart will say)
s' Afton efcharisto!
(thank Him!)
