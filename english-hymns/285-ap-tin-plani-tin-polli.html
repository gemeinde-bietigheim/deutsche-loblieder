285 AP' TIN PLANI TIN POLLI

Greek Hymns - No. 285 | "ΑΠ' ΤΗΝ ΠΛΑΝΗ ΤΗΝ ΠΟΛΛΗ"
Verse 1:
Ap' ti plani tin polli trechoun tora parefthys
(They are now running away from the great error)
eis ti mia ekklisia tou Theoy!
(in the one church of God!)
Pselnoun omothymadon glykeis ymnous tis Sion
(They sing sweet hymns of Zion in unison)
ki einai tora eis tin poimni tou Christoy!
(and is now in the flock of Christ!)
Chorus:
O ekklisia tou Theoy,
(O church of God,)
opou einai fos, alitheia, zoi!
(where is light, truth, life!)
O ekklisia tou Theoy,
(O church of God,)
opou agapi kai enotis katoikei!
(where love and unity dwells!)
Verse 2:
Choris Vivlo kai Christo eplaniomoun pantachoy,
(Without Bible and Christ I wandered everywhere,)
se poikiles ton anthropon didaches,
(in various doctrines of men,)
tora omos sti Sion vrika fos alithino
(but now in Zion I have found true light)
ki eimai olo eftychia kai chara!
(and I am all happiness and joy!)
Verse 3:
Doxa, doxa ston Amno, to Sotira kai Theo,
(Glory, glory to the Lamb, the Savior and God,)
pou mas efer' ap' to skotos eis to fos!
(who brought us from darkness to light!)
As doxazoume mazi ki as ymnei oli i gi,
(Let us praise together and let all the earth praise,)
Aftos einai o Poimenas o kalos!
(This is the Good Shepherd!)
Verse 4:
Nea Ierousalim, doxa tou Emmanouil,
(New Jerusalem, glory of Emmanuel,)
protogonos ekklisia tou Theoy!
(primitive church of God!)
Thelo se na theoro, diarkos efoson zo,
(I want to consider you, always as long as I live,)
eosoy ston ourano anapaftho!
(until I rest in heaven!)
