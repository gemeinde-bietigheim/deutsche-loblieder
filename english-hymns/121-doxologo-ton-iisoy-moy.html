121 DOXOLOGO TON IISOY MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 121 | "ΔΟΞΟΛΟΓΩ ΤΟΝ ΙΗΣΟΥ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Doxologo ton Iisoy mou, pou me agapise,
(I praise my Jesus, who loved me,)
eno imoun stin amartia, irthe kai me esose!
(while I was in sin, he came and saved me!)
Chorus:
Mou esygchoris' o Christos tis amarties mou,
(Christ forgive me my sins,)
zei tora Aftos anti gia me, doxa sto onoma Tou!
(now He lives instead of me, glory to His name!)
Verse 2:
I amartia de me elkyei, afotou o Christos
(Sin does not attract me, after Christ)
me ekane kainoyrgio ktisma na zo pia katharos!
(it made me a new building to live clean now!)
Verse 3:
Tou kosmou ta polla deina de me tromazoun pia,
(The world's many troubles no longer frighten me,)
giati o Kyrios mou dinei agapi sti kardia!
(because the Lord gives love to my heart!)
Verse 4:
Mes stis zois tis trikymies pantote tha tharro,
(Through life's storms I will always brave,)
giati echo san kyverniti ton Iisoy Christo!
(because I have Jesus Christ as my ruler!)
Verse 5:
Kai poios mporei na me chorisei ap' ton Iisoy Christo;
(And who can separate me from Jesus Christ?)
Tipote de tha logariaso oyte to thanato!
(I will count nothing, not even death!)
