634 O ThEOS MAS EINAI DYNATOS

Greek Hymns - No. 634 | "Ο ΘΕΟΣ ΜΑΣ ΕΙΝΑΙ ΔΥΝΑΤΟΣ"
Verse 1:
O Theos mas einai dynatos,
(Our God is mighty,)
nikitis mas, archigos tis zois mas,
(our conqueror, leader of our life,)
katoikei ston paradeiso!
(dwells in heaven!)
