396 KAI AN ORYETAI O EChThROS

Greek Hymns - No. 396 | "ΚΑΙ ΑΝ ΩΡΥΕΤΑΙ Ο ΕΧΘΡΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Kai an oryetai o echthros me kathe apeili,
(And if the enemy with every threat grows,)
omos me sozei o Christos kai de tha m' arnithei!
(but Christ saves me and will not deny me!)
Chorus:
O, de tha m' arnithei, nai, de tha m' arnithei!
(Oh, he won't deny me, yes, he won't deny me!)
O Iisoys mou m' esose kai de tha m' arnithei!
(My Jesus saved me and He will not deny me!)
Verse 2:
O Iakov dynamose me to Theo mazi
(Jacob became strong with God together)
ki ego s' Afton tha stirichtho kai de tha m' arnithei!
(and I will rely on Him and He will not deny me!)
Verse 3:
Mou psithyrizei o diavolos, thelei na me ptoei,
(The devil whispers to me, he wants to frighten me,)
alla o Christos mou einai pistos kai de tha m' arnithei!
(but my Christ is faithful and will not deny me!)
Verse 4:
Ki an echo thlipseis sti zoi, tha eimai nikitis,
(And if I have sorrows in life, I will be victorious,)
oudepote tha m' arnithei o Megas Lytrotis!
(the Great Redeemer will never deny me!)
