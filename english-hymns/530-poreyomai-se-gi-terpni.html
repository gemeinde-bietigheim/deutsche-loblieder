530 POREYOMAI SE GI TERPNI

Greek Hymns - No. 530 | "ΠΟΡΕΥΟΜΑΙ ΣΕ ΓΗ ΤΕΡΠΝΗ"
Verse 1:
Poreyomai se gi terpni,
(I'm marching on a dry land,)
ploysia kai eflogiti,
(rich and blessed,)
me perimenei o Christos
(Christ is waiting for me)
pou einai to aionio fos!
(which is the eternal light!)
Chorus:
Oraia chora pothiti,
(Nice land of lust,)
Tora ti vlepo ap' ti gi,
(Now I see her from earth,)
poly makria plin kathara,
(far but clear,)
spitia ekei poly lampra
(houses there very brilliant)
etoimasmena di' eme,
(prepared by me)
na katoiko me Se, Christe!
(to dwell with Thee, Christ!)
Verse 2:
Me to Christo peripato
(With Christ I walk)
kai odigia Tou zito,
(and I ask Him for guidance,)
apo to cheri me kratei
(he's holding me by the hand)
kai pros ta ano m' odigei!
(and leads me upwards!)
Verse 3:
Synecheia stin prosefchi
(Continue to pray)
o Iisoys mou omilei,
(jesus speaks to me)
mou legei «eisai tekno mou,
(says to me "you are my child,)
elpize sy sto Logo mou».
(you hoped in my Word".)
Verse 4:
Zo ena vio eftychi,
(I live a happy life,)
amerimno kai asfali,
(carefree and safe,)
tha eftychiso entelos,
(I will be completely happy,)
otan me parei o Christos!
(when Christ takes me!)
