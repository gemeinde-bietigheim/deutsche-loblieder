107 AKOYO TI FONI

Greek Hymns - No. 107 | "ΑΚΟΥΩ ΤΗ ΦΩΝΗ"
Verse 1:
Akoyo ti foni pou me kaleis, Christe,
(I hear your voice calling me, Christ,)
me theio aima n' agnistho pou chythike apo Se!
(with divine blood to be cleansed that was shed by You!)
Chorus:
Erchomai pros Se, eysplachne Christe,
(I come to You, merciful Christ,)
dexou kai katharise kardia mou, Kyrie!
(accept and cleanse my heart, Lord!)
Verse 2:
Sy eisai agathos, amartolos ego,
(You are good, I am a sinner,)
se Se empistefomenos, vevaios, tha sotho!
(in Trusting in You, I will surely be saved!)
Verse 3:
Protrepei o Christos se pisti diarki,
(Christ exhorts to lasting faith,)
elpida epourania ki agapi alithini!
(heavenly hope and true love!)
Verse 4:
Se olous martyrei kai olous proskalei,
(To all he testifies and invites all,)
to ergo eteleiose, i pisti tora arkei!
(the work is finished, faith is now sufficient!)
Verse 5:
Oloi as eflogoyn to Lytroti Christo,
(Let all bless Christ the Redeemer,)
sofia kai dikaiosi, thysia, ilasmo!
(wisdom and justification, sacrifice, joy!)
