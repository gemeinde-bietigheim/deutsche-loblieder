82 O IISOYS ChRISTOS SE KRAZEI

Greek Hymns - No. 82 | "Ο ΙΗΣΟΥΣ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ ΣΕ ΚΡΑΖΕΙ"
Verse 1:
O Iisoys Christos se krazei,
(Jesus Christ cries to you,)
mi distazeis, mi fovoy!
(don't hesitate, don't be afraid!)
«Lave dynami», fonazei,
("Get strength," he cries,)
«Ego eimai, eimai meta sou».
("I am, I am after you.")
Chorus:
Opou Aftos me odigisei,
(Where He leads me,)
tha ypago asfalos!
(I will fall for sure!)
Ola tha ta egkataleipso,
(I will give it all up,)
gia na ziso me Afton pistos!
(to live with Him faithfully!)
Verse 2:
An kai dyskolos o dromos,
(Although the road is difficult,)
ki o agonas synechis,
(and the struggle is continuous,)
nai, tha prochoro afovos,
(yes i will go ahead without fear)
mechri telous, telous tis zois!
(until the end, the end of life!)
Verse 3:
Tipota de me chorizei
(Nothing separates me)
ap' ton Iisoy Christo
(from Jesus Christ)
kai kaneis de m' empodizei,
(and no one stops me,)
gia na eimai, eimai me Afton!
(to be, I am with Him!)
Verse 4:
Me ton Iisoy tha pao
(With Jesus I will go)
eis tin endoxi Sion,
(in glorious Zion,)
kai aionia tha psallo,
(and forever I will sing,)
Alliloyia! Doxa eis Afton!
(Alleluia! Glory to Him!)
