397 KAPOIA MERA EKEI STIN IERIChO

Greek Hymns - No. 397 | "ΚΑΠΟΙΑ ΜΕΡΑ ΕΚΕΙ ΣΤΗΝ ΙΕΡΙΧΩ"
Chorus:
Kapoia mera ekei stin Iericho,
(Someday there in Jericho,)
Iericho, Iericho,
(Jericho, Jericho,)
pesane ta teichi ta psila,
(the high walls fell,)
otan fonaxe o laos!
(when the people shouted!)
Verse 1:
Mprosta sta teichi ta psila
(In front of the high walls)
eiche mazeftei laos
(people had gathered)
pou 'che pisti mesa stin kardia
(who has faith in his heart)
s' o,ti tou 'lege o Theos!
(to what God told him!)
Verse 2:
Xafnika alalaxe o laos
(Suddenly the people roared)
kai oi salpigges mazi,
(and the trumpets together,)
etsi diataxe o archigos,
(so the chief ordered,)
o Iisoys gios tou Nafi.
(Joshua son of Noah.)
Verse 3:
Peftoune ta teichi ta psila
(The high walls are falling)
me tin idia entoli,
(with the same command,)
opoudipote kai na vrethei
(wherever it may be found)
dyskolia sti zoi!
(difficulty in life!)
