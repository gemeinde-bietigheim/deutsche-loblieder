302 O KYRIE MOY, POSO LAChTARO

Greek Hymns - No. 302 | "Ω ΚΥΡΙΕ ΜΟΥ, ΠΟΣΟ ΛΑΧΤΑΡΩ"
Verse 1:
O Kyrie mou, poso lachtaro
(Oh Lord, how I long)
panta sta ichni sou na perpato,
(always in your footsteps to walk,)
glykys kai praos, agnos kai tapeinos,
(sweet and meek, pure and humble,)
ki akoma na 'mai sto zilo pio thermos!
(and even to be more fervent in zeal!)
Chorus:
Pare, Christe, oli mou tin psychi,
(Take, Christ, my whole soul,)
mono me Se axizei i zoi!
(only with You is life worth living!)
Krata me Sy epano sto stafro
(Keep me with You on the cross)
na zeis entos mou ki ego pia na mi zo!
(live in me and I no longer live!)
Verse 2:
Mesa stin pali tis zois aftis
(In the struggle of this life)
thelo na eimai panta nikitis!
(i want to always be a winner!)
Se kathe vima na Se akoloutho,
(In every step to follow You,)
na se doxazo kai na se martyro!
(to glorify you and testify to you!)
Verse 3:
Thelo sto telos, otan tha statho
(I want at the end, when I stand)
mprosta sto vima Sou, n' antameiftho,
(before Your step, may I be rewarded,)
na S' ateniso choris na ntropiasto,
(to gaze upon You without being ashamed,)
na 'nai i zoi mou gemati me karpo!
(may my life be full of fruit!)
