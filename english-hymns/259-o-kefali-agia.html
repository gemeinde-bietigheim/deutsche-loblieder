259 O KEFALI AGIA

Greek Hymns - No. 259 | "Ω ΚΕΦΑΛΗ ΑΓΙΑ"
Verse 1:
O Kefali agia, apeiros thlivera,
(O Holy Head, infinitely sad,)
ypo cheiron anomon epligis fovera!
(at the hands of the lawless you have suffered terribly!)
Akanthais estemmeni, ochra, aimostagis,
(Thorn-crowned, pale, bleeding,)
o stochos iso misous kai chlefasmon tis gis!
(the object of both hate and scorn of the earth!)
Verse 2:
Vevarymeni lypais kai ponois, fef! deinois,
(Burdened with sorrow and pain, phew! sufferers,)
i doxa estemmeni pote en ouranois,
(the glory ever crowned in heaven,)
adynatos nyn klineis en meso empaigmon,
(lean now you lie amid taunts,)
en agonia fthineis thanaton vdelykton!
(in agony you shrink to death abominable!)
Verse 3:
Pos oytos illoiothis, o panseptos morfi;
(How art thou thus desecrated, O pansept form?)
Odyni, pos etrothis i igemoniki;
(Sorrow, how do you eat the hegemonic?)
Fey! I orgi i Theia epilthen epi Se,
(Phew! The wrath of the Divine came upon Thee,)
epepese vareia, tin gin esalefse!
(heaviness fell, he saved the earth!)
Verse 4:
O! Apeiros agapi einai i Si, Christe!
(Oh! Infinite love is the Lord, Christ!)
Tis dynatai na eipi oti aftin pote
(He may have told them that he never)
thnitos tha ennoisi edo epi tis gis
(mortal will mean here on earth)
i tha katametrisi to megethos aftis;
(or will you count the size of it?)
