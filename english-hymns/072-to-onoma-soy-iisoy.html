72 TO ONOMA SOY, IISOY

Greek Hymns - No. 72 | "ΤΟ ΟΝΟΜΑ ΣΟΥ, ΙΗΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
To onoma Sou, Iisoy, glyky ein' stin kardia mou,
(Your name, Jesus, is sweet to my heart,)
chara mou dinei kai zoi mesa eis tin psychi mou!
(it gives me joy and life in my soul!)
Chorus:
Iisoys, onoma glyky, Iisoys, theia mousiki
(Jesus, sweet name, Jesus, divine music)
einai stin kardia mou to onoma Sou, Iisoy!
(in my heart is Your name, Jesus!)
Verse 2:
Esy Mesitis kai Theos, Sy endoxos kai Krataios,
(Thou Mediator and God, Thou Glorious and Mighty,)
ypsoneis ton amartolo, dineis zoi, Sotira!
(you raise the sinner, you give life, Savior!)
Verse 3:
Den imporo na ekfrastho. To onoma sou thafmasto!
(I can't express myself. Your name is wonderful!)
Den echo logous arketoys gia t' onoma sou, Iisoy!
(I have no reasons enough for your name, Jesus!)
Verse 4:
To onoma Sou to septo eis tous pistoys ein' endoxo,
(Your name, revered by the faithful, is glorious,)
polytimo kai thafmasto to onoma Sou, Iisoy!
(precious and wonderful is Your name, Jesus!)
