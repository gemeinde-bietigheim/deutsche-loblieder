231 EKEI PsILA STON GOLGOThA

Greek Hymns - No. 231 | "ΕΚΕΙ ΨΗΛΑ ΣΤΟΝ ΓΟΛΓΟΘΑ"
Verse 1:
Ekei psila sto Golgotha
(Up there on Calvary)
gia mas ypefere frikta
(for us he suffered terribly)
kai echyse to aima Tou,
(and shed His blood,)
gia na mas sosei ap' ta desma!
(to save us from the shackles!)
Odyni kai chlefasmoys
(Pain and taunts)
ypefere toso friktoys,
(suffered so terribly,)
gia na mas dosei ti chara!
(to give us joy!)
O, Alliloyia!
(Oh, Hallelujah!)
Verse 2:
O Kyrie, S' efcharisto,
(oh lord thank you)
pou m' esoses ap' to chamo
(who saved me from destruction)
kai anoixes ton ourano
(and you opened the sky)
gia me, aionio thafmasto!
(to me, eternal wonder!)
Aionia s' efgnomono,
(I am eternally grateful to you,)
pou me elytroses, Christe,
(who redeemed me, Christ,)
o amome Theoy Yie!
(O Holy Son of God!)
O, Alliloyia!
(Oh, Hallelujah!)
