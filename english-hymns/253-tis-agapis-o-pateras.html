253 Tis Agapis O Pateras

Greek Hymns - No. 253 | "The Father of Love"
Verse 1:
Tis agapis o Pateras
(The Father of love)
esteile edo sti gi
(sent here to the earth)
ton monakrivo Yio Tou
(His only begotten Son)
kai gia se na stavrothei!
(and for you to be crucified!)
Chorus:
Me thanato skliro,
(With a harsh death,)
o, epano sto Stavro
(oh, upon the Cross)
esvise tis amarties!
(He erased the sins!)
Se prosmenei o Christos.
(Christ awaits you.)
Empros, as fygei kathe distagmos!
(Forward, let every hesitation depart!)
Verse 2:
Apo tou Stavrou ton pono
(From the pain of the Cross)
pou ypefer' o Christos
(that Christ suffered)
eklonistike i gi mas
(our earth was shaken)
ki eskotisthi o ouranos!
(and the heavens were darkened!)
Verse 3:
Tora echei teleiosei
(Now it has been completed)
i thysia tou Stavrou,
(the sacrifice of the Cross,)
ki einai pano ston Patera,
(and He is with the Father,)
mpros sto throno t' ouranou!
(before the throne of heaven!)
