610 EIN' ENAS ILIOS

Greek Hymns - No. 610 | "ΕΙΝ' ΕΝΑΣ ΗΛΙΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Ein' enas ilios 'kei ston ourano,
(There is a sun in the sky,)
pou 'nai dikos mou kai ton agapo!
(that he is mine and I love him!)
Me chari anastainei ti nekri psychi,
(With grace he raises the dead soul,)
me dikaiosyni omorfainei ti zoi!
(with justice beautifies life!)
Me kanei na niotho ta ourania
(It makes me feel heavenly)
kontina ki agapita!
(near and dear!)
Chorus:
Einai ilios o Christos zestos!
(Christ is a warm sun!)
Me fos ki alitheia mou gemizei tin psychi!
(It fills my soul with light and truth!)
Otan dipsasmeni gia chara
(When thirsty for joy)
Ton psachnei i matia, psila Ton synanta!
(The eye is looking for Him, high up it meets Him!)
Verse 2:
Akomi ki otan erthei i synnefia,
(Even when the clouds come,)
ki otan xespaei i mpora xafnika,
(and when the rain breaks out suddenly,)
astraftoun ola gyro, peftoun kerafnoi,
(everything sparkles around, lightning falls,)
kleistoi moiazoun na 'nai kai thlimmenoi
(they seem to be closed and sad)
oi ouranoi, ton ilio to lampro
(the skies, the sun the bright)
den payo na thoro astraftero!
(I don't stop shining bright!)
