407 O KYRIOS EINAI FOS MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 407 | "Ο ΚΥΡΙΟΣ ΕΙΝΑΙ ΦΩΣ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
O Kyrios einai fos mou kai o Sotir tis psychis,
(The Lord is my light and the Savior of my soul,)
petra mou kai froyrio mou kai dynami tis zois!
(my stone and my fortress and strength of life!)
Tipota de me fovizei, ki an polemos sikothei,
(Nothing scares me, even if war breaks out,)
kai tote thelo elpizei, stratefma an paratachthei!
(and then I hope, troop if lined up!)
Chorus:
Mi tromaze, o deile, mi fovoy!
(Fear not, O coward, fear not!)
Pantote eimai Ego meta sou!
(It's always Me after you!)
Verse 2:
Mia kai moni imera mes stis «afles» me Ese,
(A single day in the "yards" with you,)
panta ein' protimotera para polles dichos Se!
(is always better than many without You!)
Thelo, nai, panta na eimai sto «oikon» Sou «thyroros»,
(I want, yes, always to be in Your "house" "gatekeeper",)
para na zo se palatia kai na planiemai oiktros!
(than to live in palaces and wander miserably!)
Verse 3:
Makarios opoios echei ton Kyrio arogo,
(Blessed is he who has the Lord as his helper,)
prostati kai voitho tou kai tis zois odigo!
(protector and helper and guide of life!)
Ena zito, o Thee mou, na eimai plision Sou,
(One thing I ask, O my God, to be near You,)
na katoiko aionios eis to lampro Oiko sou!
(to dwell eternally in your glorious House!)
