322 KARDIA KAThARI

Greek Hymns - No. 322 | "ΚΑΡΔΙΑ ΚΑΘΑΡΗ"
Verse 1:
Kardia kathari chtise mesa mou, Thee,
(A pure heart build in me, O God,)
kai Pneyma efthes pali dos' mou!
(and give me straight Spirit again!)
Kardia kathari chtise mesa mou, Thee,
(A pure heart build in me, O God,)
kai Pneyma efthes pali dos' mou!
(and give me straight Spirit again!)
Mi me aporripseis apo tou prosopou Sou
(Cast me not from Thy face)
kai to Pneyma Sou min afaireseis, Thee!
(and do not take away Your Spirit, God!)
Dos' mou ti chara tis sotirias Sou kai pali,
(Give me the joy of Your salvation again,)
me Pneyma igemoniko stirixe me!
(with a sovereign Spirit support me!)
Verse 2:
Kardia statheri chtise mesa mou, Thee
(Build a firm heart in me, God)
kai exocho Pneyma dos' mou!
(and excellent Spirit give me!)
Kardia statheri chtise mesa mou, Thee
(Build a firm heart in me, God)
kai exocho Pneyma dos' mou!
(and excellent Spirit give me!)
Gia na ektelo ta diatagmata Sou mono
(To execute Your decrees only)
kai pote mou na mi Se lypiso, Thee!
(and never to pity You, God!)
Me to aima tou Christoy mou Sy katharise me,
(With the blood of my Christ You cleansed me,)
me Pneyma igemoniko stirixe me!
(with a sovereign Spirit support me!)
