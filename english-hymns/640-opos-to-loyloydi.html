640 OPOS TO LOYLOYDI

Greek Hymns - No. 640 | "ΟΠΩΣ ΤΟ ΛΟΥΛΟΥΔΙ"
Verse 1:
Opos to louloydi anthizei to proi,
(As the flower blooms in the morning,)
opos vgainei o ilios ap' tin anatoli,
(as the sun rises in the east,)
opos feggoun t' astra mesa sti nychtia,
(as the stars shine in the night,)
etsi o Christos mas xerei n' agapa!
(this is how Christ knows how to love us!)
Etsi o Christos mas xerei n' agapa!
(This is how Christ knows how to love us!)
Verse 2:
Tou Christoy i agapi einai i pio glykia!
(Christ's love is the sweetest!)
Mono Ekeinos xerei pos na agapa!
(Only He knows how to love!)
Mono Ekeinos xerei pos na agapa!
(Only He knows how to love!)
Verse 3:
Opos ta karavia pleoun stis thalasses,
(As ships sail the seas,)
me proorismo tous chores makrines,
(bound for distant lands,)
zo kai perimeno na 'rthei i stigmi,
(I live and wait for the moment to come,)
pou ki ego tha ftaso sti diki mou gi!
(that I too will reach my own land!)
Pou ki ego tha ftaso sti diki mou gi!
(Where I too will reach my own land!)
Verse 4:
Echo ego pyxida ton Iisoy Christo,
(I have Jesus Christ as my compass,)
pou me odigei pros ton ourano!
(that leads me to heaven!)
Pou me odigei pros ton ourano!
(Which leads me to the sky!)
Verse 5:
Otan tha salpisei Kyrios o Theos,
(When the Lord God blows his trumpet,)
thelo na petaxo psila ston ourano!
(i want to fly high in the sky!)
Thelo na petaxo opos to pouli
(I want to fly like a bird)
pou to eleftherosan mesa ap' to klouvi!
(who freed it from the cage!)
Pou to eleftherosan mesa ap' to klouvi!
(That they released it from the cage!)
Verse 6:
Kai tha Ton doxazo pia pantotina!
(And I will praise Him forever!)
Tou Christoy i agapi ein' i pio glykia!
(Christ's love is the sweetest!)
Tou Christoy i agapi ein' i pio glykia!
(Christ's love is the sweetest!)
