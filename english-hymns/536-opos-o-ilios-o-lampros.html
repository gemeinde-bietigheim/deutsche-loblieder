536 OPOS O ILIOS O LAMPROS

Greek Hymns - No. 536 | "ΟΠΩΣ Ο ΗΛΙΟΣ Ο ΛΑΜΠΡΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Opos o ilios o lampros
(Like the sun the bright one)
fotagogei tous ouranoys,
(illuminates the heavens,)
etsi tha elthei krataios
(so it will come with force)
na vasilepsei o Iisoys!
(let Jesus reign!)
Verse 2:
Kai pros Afton pantotina
(And to Him forever)
tha stelnetai i prosefchi,
(prayer will be sent,)
to onoma Tou tapeina
(His name humbly)
kath' ethnos tha to eflogei!
(every nation will bless it!)
Verse 3:
Ymnoi glykeis pollon laon
(Sweet hymns of many peoples)
tha antichoyne me chara!
(they will resonate with joy!)
Fones nipion pros Afton
(Infant voices to Him)
tha krazoun Alliloyia!
(they will shout Hallelujah!)
Verse 4:
O dikaios panta tha zei,
(The righteous will always live,)
oi desmioi, ap' ta desma
(the captives, from the bonds)
eleytheroi, tha echoune
(free, they will have)
anapafsi aionia!
(rest eternal!)
Verse 5:
Pros to Sotira oi laoi
(To the Saviour, the peoples)
as psalloun endoxi odi,
(let them sing a glorious ode,)
as Ton ymnoyn oi aggeloi
(let the angels praise Him)
ki as antichei oli i gi!
(let all the earth resound!)
