128 EChO TO ChRISTO MAZI MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 128 | "ΕΧΩ ΤΟ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ ΜΑΖΙ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Echo to Christo mazi mou,
(I have Christ with me,)
opou kai an metavo,
(wherever I go)
tha fylaei tin psychi mou
(he will guard my soul)
ki eftychis panta tha zo!
(and I will always live happily!)
Chorus:
Ti chara alithis, o pistos ein' eftychis!
(What true joy, the believer is happy!)
O Christos chorigei sotiria kai zoi!
(Christ grants salvation and life!)
Verse 2:
Me chara to Lytroti mou
(With joy my Redeemer)
panta tha akoloutho
(I will always follow)
kai me tolmi stin psychi mou
(and boldly in my soul)
di' Afton tha martyro!
(for Him I will testify!)
Verse 3:
Me ti vivlo ana cheiras
(With the Bible in hand)
kai me pisti sto Christo,
(and by faith in Christ,)
stin odo tis alitheias
(on the path of truth)
pantote tha prochoro!
(I will always move forward!)
Verse 4:
Ean tora ypofero
(If now I suffer)
diogmoys kai peirasmoys,
(persecutions and temptations,)
'kei aionia tha doxazo
(I will glory here forever)
to Christo stous ouranoys!
(Christ in heaven!)
