458 ST' AYTIA MOY ANTIChOYN

Greek Hymns - No. 458 | "ΣΤ' ΑΥΤΙΑ ΜΟΥ ΑΝΤΗΧΟΥΝ"
Verse 1:
St' aftia mou antichoyn afta pou ekane o Christos,
(In my ears echo what Christ did,)
to idio kai gia se mporei na kanei Aftos!
(He can do the same for you!)
An Tou zitiseis dynami, tha erthei voithos,
(If you ask Him for strength, a helper will come,)
yparchei kai gia sena o Ypsistos Theos!
(there is the Most High God for you too!)
Chorus:
Den einai mystiko ti o Theos
(It is no secret that God)
mporei na kanei gia sena Aftos!
(He can do for you!)
Tha sygchorisei tin enochi
(He will forgive the guilt)
kai tha apantisei stin prosefchi!
(and he will answer prayer!)
Verse 2:
Mes sti zoi min ptoitheis, panta na 'sai pistos,
(In life do not be afraid, always be faithful,)
gi' afto mi fovitheis na Ton empistefteis!
(so don't be afraid to trust Him!)
Chara aionia tha geftheis kai ti zoi sou Aftos
(Eternal joy you will taste and your life He)
me doxa tha kalypsei, o endoxos Christos!
(with glory will cover, the glorious Christ!)
Verse 3:
Tora den einai mystiko san ti mporei Aftos
(Now it's no secret what He can do)
na kanei kai gia se, o dynatos Theos!
(may the mighty God do for you too!)
An doseis tin kardia sou kai to einai sou s' Afton,
(If you give your heart and your being to Him,)
zoi aionia dinei o Iisoys Christos!
(Jesus Christ gives eternal life!)
