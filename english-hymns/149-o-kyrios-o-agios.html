149 O KYRIOS, O AGIOS

Greek Hymns - No. 149 | "Ο ΚΥΡΙΟΣ, Ο ΑΓΙΟΣ"
Verse 1:
O Kyrios, o Agios,
(The Lord, the Holy One,)
ston kosmo irthe tapeinos,
(he came into the world humble,)
perpatise s' afti ti gi
(walk this earth)
me teleia ypakoi!
(with perfect obedience!)
Stafrothike sto Golgotha,
(He was crucified on Calvary,)
gemis' odyni i kardia,
(heart full of pain,)
loidorisan to Lytroti,
(they cursed the Redeemer,)
to aima chythike sti gi!
(the blood was shed on the earth!)
Chorus:
Glyke Amne, glyke Amne,
(Sweet Amne, sweet Amne,)
i agapi Sou me lytrose!
(Your love redeemed me!)
Aionia tha S' efcharisto,
(I will forever thank you,)
Iisoy Christe, Theoy Amne!
(Jesus Christ, God Amne!)
Verse 2:
Imoun chamenos sti zoi,
(I was lost in life,)
gemati dakrya i psychi,
(the soul full of tears,)
ma san Se eida sto Stafro,
(but as I saw You on the Cross,)
gnorisa Ese, ton Kyrio!
(I met You, the Lord!)
