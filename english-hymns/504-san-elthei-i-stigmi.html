504 SAN ELThEI I STIGMI

Greek Hymns - No. 504 | "ΣΑΝ ΕΛΘΕΙ Η ΣΤΙΓΜΗ"
Verse 1:
San elthei i stigmi megali lampri,
(When the great moment comes,)
to salpisma pou th' akoustei,
(the blowing of the trumpet that shall be heard,)
tha elthei o Christos mas me doxa polli
(our Christ will come with much glory)
m' agious aggelous mazi!
(with holy angels together!)
Chorus:
Ekei prosdoko
(That's where I expect)
ki ego na vretho,
(to be found too)
san ichisei i salpigx i aggeliki,
(as the trumpet sounds the angelica,)
tha do tou Christoy ti morfi!
(I will see the form of Christ!)
Verse 2:
Ston kosmo edo mafrila, kako,
(In the world here blackness, evil,)
skotadi pantoy fovero.
(darkness everywhere terrible.)
Se ligo tha fexei, rodizei i afgi,
(Soon it will break, the dawn is rosy,)
tha zisoume sti nea gi!
(we will live in the new earth!)
Verse 3:
S' aftin ti zoi pikri, pageri
(In this bitter, cold life)
pantoy monaxia foveri.
(terrible loneliness everywhere.)
San elth' o Christos mas chara stin psychi,
(As our Christ brings joy to the soul,)
tha meinoun oi ponoi sti gi!
(pains will remain on earth!)
