255 To Diko Mou Stavro

Greek Hymns - No. 255 | "My Cross You Carried"
Verse 1:
To diko mou stavro kouvalouses!
(You carried my cross!)
Ta dika mou karfia Sy forouses!
(You wore my nails!)
Egines gia mena amartia,
(You became sin for me,)
sta matia tou Patera mia thysia!
(a sacrifice in the Father's eyes!)
Chorus:
Logia tis agapis mou den vrisko
(I cannot find words of my love)
tin kardia mou na S' anoixo!
(to open my heart to You!)
Poneses poly, Christe mou!
(You suffered so much, my Christ!)
Pethanes gia mena, Kyrie mou!
(You died for me, my Lord!)
Poneses poly, Christe mou!
(You suffered so much, my Christ!)
Pethanes gia mena, Kyrie mou!
(You died for me, my Lord!)
Verse 2:
Sa listi se karfosan sto xylo
(Like a thief they nailed You to the wood)
kai s' enepazan oloi trigyro.
(and everyone around mocked You.)
Nero zitises, Christe mou...
(You asked for water, my Christ...)
choli sou 'dosan, Thee mou!
(they gave You gall, my God!)
Verse 3:
Otan eida to aima Sou na trechei
(When I saw Your blood flowing)
kai to vlemma Sou emena na prosechei,
(and Your gaze watching over me,)
apofasi pira stin zoi mou
(I made a decision in my life)
na zo gia Sena, Lytroti mou!
(to live for You, my Redeemer!)
