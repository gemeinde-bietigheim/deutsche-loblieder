177 O, POS AGAPO

Greek Hymns - No. 177 | "Ω, ΠΩΣ ΑΓΑΠΩ"
Verse 1:
O, pos agapo to Sotira Christo!
(Oh, how I love the Savior Christ!)
Ti logia na vro, gia na po
(What words can I find, to say)
o,ti echei kamei ekei sto stafro
(what he has done there on the cross)
gia mena ton amartolo!
(for me the sinner!)
Chorus:
Epliros' Aftos ti diki mou poini
(He paid my penalty)
me to aima Tou t' akrivo!
(with His precious blood!)
Me pire apo Adi frikto, skoteino
(He took me from a horrible, dark Hades)
na zo se lampro Ourano,
(to live in bright Heaven,)
na zo se lampro Ourano!
(to live in bright Heaven!)
Verse 2:
O, pos agapo to Christo mou ego!
(Oh, how I love my Christ!)
Mazi Tou pantoy prochoro.
(With Him everywhere I go.)
Mazi Tou o dromos ki an einai trachys,
(With Him the road is rough,)
gia me einai toso glykys!
(he is so sweet to me!)
Verse 3:
O, pos agapo ton Sotira Christo!
(Oh, how I love Christ the Savior!)
Gi' afton kathe ti paraito.
(For him I give up everything.)
Ki analafros mes sti zoi perpato
(And I walk lightly through life)
me varos glyky to stafro!
(with sweet weight the cross!)
