502 PROChORO GIA TON OYRANO

Greek Hymns - No. 502 | "ΠΡΟΧΩΡΩ ΓΙΑ ΤΟΝ ΟΥΡΑΝΟ"
Verse 1:
Prochoro gia ton ourano,
(I'm heading for the sky,)
prochoro, o! nai, prochoro
(onward, oh! yes, i'm moving on)
eis ta ichni tou Lytrotoy,
(in the footsteps of the Redeemer,)
panta tha prochoro!
(I will always move forward!)
Chorus:
Lefki stoli, stemma chryso,
(White uniform, gold crown,)
kithara kai spiti lampro,
(guitar and house bright,)
niki, doxa, chara diarki
(victory, glory, joy lasting)
tha 'cho otan fthaso ekei!
(I will when I get there!)
Verse 2:
Prochoro me ton Iisoy,
(I go with Jesus,)
prochoro, o! nai, prochoro,
(onward, oh! yes i'm moving)
kai an thlipseis pantoy kykloyn,
(and if sorrows circulate everywhere,)
panta tha prochoro!
(I will always move forward!)
Verse 3:
Prochoro kai niko pantoy,
(I advance and conquer everywhere,)
Prochoro, o! nai, prochoro
(I'm moving on, oh! yes, i'm moving on)
kai kirytto to Logo Aftoy,
(and I preach His Word,)
panta tha prochoro!
(I will always move forward!)
