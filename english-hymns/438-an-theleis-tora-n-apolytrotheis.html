438 AN ThELEIS TORA N' APOLYTROThEIS

Greek Hymns - No. 438 | "ΑΝ ΘΕΛΕΙΣ ΤΩΡΑ Ν' ΑΠΟΛΥΤΡΩΘΕΙΣ"
Verse 1:
An theleis tora n' apolytrotheis,
(If you want to break free now,)
vlepse eis ton Amno!
(look at Amnos!)
Gia na se sosei ilthe eis ti gi,
(He came to earth to save you,)
vlepse eis ton Amno!
(look at Amnos!)
Chorus:
Vlepse eis ton Amno!
(Look at Amnos!)
Vlepse eis ton Amno!
(Look at Amnos!)
Tha se lytrosei kai tha se sosei,
(He will redeem you and save you,)
vlepse eis ton Amno!
(look at Amnos!)
Verse 2:
An thlipseis, fovoi tora s' apeiloyn,
(If you grieve, fears now threaten you,)
vlepse eis ton Amno!
(look at Amnos!)
Mi deiliaseis, de tha se nikoyn,
(Do not be afraid, they will not defeat you,)
vlepse eis ton Amno!
(look at Amnos!)
Verse 3:
An sti zoi sou dyskoleyesai,
(If you are struggling in your life,)
vlepse eis ton Amno!
(look at Amnos!)
Ki an astheniseis pali elpize,
(And if you get sick again, he hoped,)
vlepse eis ton Amno!
(look at Amnos!)
Verse 4:
Mi fovitheis ti lyssa tou echthroy,
(Fear not the fury of the enemy,)
vlepse eis ton Amno!
(look at Amnos!)
Chara i lypi o Christos pantoy,
(Joy or sorrow Christ everywhere,)
vlepse eis ton Amno!
(look at Amnos!)
