520 O, TI LAMPRI STOLI!

Greek Hymns - No. 520 | "Ω, ΤΙ ΛΑΜΠΡΗ ΣΤΟΛΗ!"
Verse 1:
O, ti lampri stoli! O, ti oraia gi!
(Oh, what a brilliant outfit! Oh, what a beautiful land!)
pou vasileyei ekei chara pantotini!
(that reigns there joy everlasting!)
Ti stefano chryso gia panta tha foro,
(What a crown of gold forever I'll wear,)
otan eis ti Sion tha afichtho!
(when I arrive at Zion!)
Chorus:
Lefki stoli, oraia gi,
(White uniform, fine land,)
oikos lampros, doxa ekei!
(glorious house, glory there!)
O, ti lampros o stefanos
(Oh, what a brilliant crown)
pou tha foroyn ekei ol' oi pistoi!
(which all the faithful will wear there!)
Verse 2:
Patrida agapiti, ti pothiti stigmi!
(Dear country, what a lustful moment!)
Makarios ekei opoios tha katoikei!
(Blessed is he who dwells there!)
Prothymos kai ego ta panta apsifo,
(Willingly I also defy everything,)
me tharros stin kardia tha prochoro.
(with courage in my heart I will go forward.)
Verse 3:
Aionia zoi mas perimenei ekei,
(Eternal life awaits us there,)
ponos kai lypi pia de tha xanavrethei!
(pain and sorrow will never be found again!)
Ola ein' anthira eis tin oraia gi,
(Everything is blooming in the beautiful land,)
aggelon i odi tha antichei!
(angels the ode will resound!)
Verse 4:
O nous mou aporei, ti doxa ein' ekei!
(My mind wonders, what glory is there!)
O, poso tha charei i kathe mia psychi!
(Oh, how each soul will rejoice!)
Otan oloi mazi stous tholous t' ouranoy
(When all together in the vaults of heaven)
krazoume: «Adi, poy i niki sou;»
(we cry: "Hades, where is your victory?")
