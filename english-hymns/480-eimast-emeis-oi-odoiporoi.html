480 EIMAST' EMEIS OI ODOIPOROI

Greek Hymns - No. 480 | "ΕΙΜΑΣΤ' ΕΜΕΙΣ ΟΙ ΟΔΟΙΠΟΡΟΙ"
Verse 1:
Eimast' emeis oi odoiporoi,
(We are the trekkers,)
oi diavates tis zois,
(the passersby of life,)
pame gia kapoia alli chora
(let's go to another country)
pou den vrisketai sti gi!
(which is not found on earth!)
Chorus:
Einai i panendoxi Sion
(She is the all-glorious Zion)
ton neon ouranon,
(of the new heavens,)
i chora ton piston!
(the land of the faithful!)
Lytromenoi afinoume ti gi
(Redeemed we leave the earth)
kai ftanoum' oi pistoi ekei!
(and the believers get there!)
Verse 2:
Den echoume ravdi sto cheri,
(We don't have a stick in our hand,)
de mas gernei to kormi,
(our body does not lean,)
tis amartias to fortio mas
(of sin our burden)
ferame sto Lytroti!
(we brought to the Redeemer!)
Verse 3:
Panta empros tha prochoroyme,
(Always forward we will move,)
den yparchei edo stathmos,
(there is no station here)
gia mas anapafsi tha einai
(it will be rest for us)
monacha o ouranos!
(only the sky!)
