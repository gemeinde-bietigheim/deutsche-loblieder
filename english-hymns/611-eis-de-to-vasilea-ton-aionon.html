611 EIS DE TO VASILEA TON AIONON

Greek Hymns - No. 611 | "ΕΙΣ ΔΕ ΤΟ ΒΑΣΙΛΕΑ ΤΩΝ ΑΙΩΝΩΝ"
Verse 1:
Eis de to Vasilea ton aionon,
(And to the King of ages,)
ton aftharto, ton aorato, to mono sofo Theo,
(the imperishable, the invisible, the only wise God,)
Eis de to Vasilea ton aionon,
(And to the King of ages,)
ton aftharto, ton aorato, to mono sofo Theo,
(the imperishable, the invisible, the only wise God,)
timi kai doxa, timi kai doxa,
(honor and glory, honor and glory,)
timi kai doxa s' Afton!
(honor and glory to Him!)
Stous aiones ton aionon,
(In the ages of ages,)
Stous aiones ton aionon, amin!
(To the ages of ages, amen!)
Verse 2:
Eis de to Vasilea ton aionon,
(And to the King of ages,)
ton endoxo, ton Agio, to mono Sotira Christo,
(the glorious, the Holy, the only Savior Christ,)
Eis de to Vasilea ton aionon,
(And to the King of ages,)
ton endoxo, ton Agio, to mono Sotira Christo,
(the glorious, the Holy, the only Savior Christ,)
timi kai doxa, timi kai doxa,
(honor and glory, honor and glory,)
timi kai doxa s' Afton!
(honor and glory to Him!)
Stous aiones ton aionon,
(In the ages of ages,)
Stous aiones ton aionon, amin!
(To the ages of ages, amen!)
Verse 3:
Aineite ton Kyrio panta ta ethni!
(Praise the Lord all nations!)
Doxologeite Afton pantes oi laoi,
(Praise Him all ye peoples,)
dioti to eleos Tou einai megalo se mas!
(because His mercy is great for us!)
Kai i alitheia Tou Kyriou
(And the truth of the Lord)
menei ston aiona, menei ston aiona!
(it lives forever, it lives forever!)
Alliloyia!
(Alleluia!)
