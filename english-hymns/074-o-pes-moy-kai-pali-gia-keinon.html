74 O, PES MOY KAI PALI GIA KEINON

Greek Hymns - No. 74 | "Ω, ΠΕΣ ΜΟΥ ΚΑΙ ΠΑΛΙ ΓΙΑ ΚΕΙΝΟΝ"
Verse 1:
O, pes mou kai pali gia Keinon pou onoma echei glyky,
(Oh, tell me again of One whose name is sweet,)
pou i efodia Tou san krino skorpizetai mes stin psychi!
(that His fragrance like a lily spreads in the soul!)
Gia Keinon pou agapis' emena kai afise tous ouranoys
(For the One who loved me and left the heavens)
kai epathe tosa gia mena, o, den to choraei o nous!
(and he suffered so much for me, oh, the mind cannot contain it!)
Chorus:
O! Ti onoma glyky Christos!
(Oh! What a name sweet Christ!)
Gia kathe pistoy psychi Aftos!
(For every faithful soul He!)
Me posi chara to psalloun pantoy
(How joyfully they sing it everywhere)
to onoma tou Christoy!
(the name of Christ!)
Grammeno mes stin kardia mou
(Written in my heart)
tha menei pantotina,
(will stay forever)
kai ston ourano m' aggelon choro
(and in heaven with angels dancing)
aionia tha Ton ymno!
(forever I will praise Him!)
Verse 2:
O, pos na xechaso Ekeinon pou eiche stefani lampro,
(Oh, how can I forget Him who was crowned bright,)
me dafnes aionias doxas amaranto ston ourano!
(with laurels of eternal glory amaranth in the sky!)
Kai t' afise gia na foresei, ston kosmo afton to skliro,
(And he left it to wear, in this harsh world,)
stefani ap' agkathia tou ponou gia mena ton amartolo!
(crown of thorns of pain for me the sinner!)
Verse 3:
Pos thelo gia t' onoma Ekeino n' akoyo to vasiliko,
(How I want for that name to hear the basilisk,)
pou egine doro se mena ton athlio ki amartolo!
(who became a gift to me the wretched and sinful!)
Ki an pethan' Ekeinos gia mena, ego tora zo di' Afton,
(And if He died for me, I now live for Him,)
pistos os to telos tha meino ki aionia tha Ton ymno!
(faithful to the end I will remain and eternally I will praise Him!)
