389 EIN' AYTOS NIKITIS!

Greek Hymns - No. 389 | "ΕΙΝ' ΑΥΤΟΣ ΝΙΚΗΤΗΣ!"
Verse 1:
Ein' Aftos nikitis!
(He is the winner!)
Tou thanatou nikitis ein' o Christos!
(Christ is the conqueror of death!)
Kai zois pigi antichei sti gi
(And a source of life reverberates in the earth)
tis nikis i krafgi!
(the cry of victory!)
Verse 2:
Ein' Aftos dynatos!
(Is He strong!)
Dynatos kai thafmastos ein' o Christos!
(Mighty and wonderful is Christ!)
Mia glykia afgi irthe me storgi
(A sweet dawn came with affection)
kai mou 'dose zoi!
(and gave me life!)
Verse 3:
Ein' Aftos zontanos!
(Is He alive!)
Alliloyia, zontanos ein' o Christos!
(Hallelujah, Christ is alive!)
Stin kardia mou zei ki ouranoy zoi
(Heavenly life also lives in my heart)
chairomaste mazi!
(we are happy together!)
