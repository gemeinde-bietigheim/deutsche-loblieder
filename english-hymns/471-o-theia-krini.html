471 O ThEIA KRINI

Greek Hymns - No. 471 | "Ω ΘΕΙΑ ΚΡΗΝΗ"
Verse 1:
O theia krini, pou chorigeis
(O divine fountain, who bestows)
zoi se olous tous dystycheis,
(life to all the unfortunate,)
dineis na pioun zontano nero!
(you give them living water to drink!)
Doxa eis Afton!
(Glory to Him!)
Chorus:
Doxa eis Afton! Doxa eis Afton!
(Glory to Him! Glory to Him!)
To Lytroti ton psychon ymon.
(The Redeemer of your souls.)
Doxa eis Afton!
(Glory to Him!)
Verse 2:
Trexe sy, file, gia na plytheis
(Run, friend, to wash)
eis tin agia toyti pigi,
(to this holy spring,)
tha vreis aionio thisafro!
(you will find eternal treasure!)
Doxa eis Afton!
(Glory to Him!)
Verse 3:
Min anavalleis kai min argeis,
(Don't delay and don't be late,)
simera trexe, gia na sotheis,
(today run to save yourself)
dos' tin kardia sou eis to Christo!
(give your heart to Christ!)
Doxa eis Afton!
(Glory to Him!)
Verse 4:
Poios xerei, file, ti tha symvei,
(Who knows, man, what will happen,)
tha eisai ayrio sti zoi;
(will you be alive tomorrow)
Ela amesos eis to Christo!
(Come immediately to Christ!)
Doxa eis Afton!
(Glory to Him!)
