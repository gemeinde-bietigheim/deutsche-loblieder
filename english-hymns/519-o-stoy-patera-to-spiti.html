519 O! STOY PATERA TO SPITI

Greek Hymns - No. 519 | "Ω! ΣΤΟΥ ΠΑΤΕΡΑ ΤΟ ΣΠΙΤΙ"
Verse 1:
O! Stou Patera to spiti epano,
(Oh! To the Father the house above,)
per' apo t' astra kai ton ourano,
(beyond the stars and the sky,)
echei gia me o Christos fylagmeni
(Christ has in store for me)
thesi se throno lampro foteino!
(throne bright bright!)
Chorus:
O! Pote tha 'rthei i ora ekeini
(Oh! When will that time come?)
pou tha me ferei konta sto Christo!
(that will bring me closer to Christ!)
Grigora tha 'rthei i ora ekeini
(That time will soon come)
pou tha me ferei konta sto Christo!
(that will bring me closer to Christ!)
Verse 2:
Me perimenoune oi adelfoi mou,
(My brothers are waiting for me,)
me perimenei kai o Lytrotis,
(and the Redeemer awaits me,)
ti syntrofia tous pos tin perimeno
(their companionship as I expect it)
mesa sti doxa na zo eftychis!
(in the glory to live happily!)
Verse 3:
Lefkoi aggeloi tha mas perivalloun,
(White angels will surround us,)
lefki stoli tha foreso ki ego,
(I will also wear a white uniform,)
to Lytroti mou kai ton Kyrio mou
(my Redeemer and my Lord)
eis tous aiones tha ymnologo!
(forever I will sing!)
Verse 4:
O! I zoi tha kylaei me chari,
(Oh! Life will flow gracefully,)
ponos kai lypi pia tha xechastoyn!
(pain and sorrow will be forgotten!)
I amartia kai o peirasmos tis
(Sin and its temptation)
de tha yparchoun na mas tyrannoyn!
(there won't be any to tyrannize us!)
