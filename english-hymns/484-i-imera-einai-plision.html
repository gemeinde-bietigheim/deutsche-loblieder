484 I IMERA EINAI PLISION

Greek Hymns - No. 484 | "Η ΗΜΕΡΑ ΕΙΝΑΙ ΠΛΗΣΙΟΝ"
Verse 1:
I imera einai plision, erchetai o Christos
(The day is near, Christ is coming)
ntymenos olos me doxa, me plires lampon fos!
(clothed all with glory, with full shining light!)
Tote i thlipsi tha papsei, to dakry to pikro,
(Then sorrow will cease, the bitter tear,)
kai gyro sto mega throno tha psallo sto Theo!
(and around the great throne I will sing to God!)
Chorus:
Doxa, doxa s' Afton, to Lytroti Christo!
(Glory, glory to Him, Christ the Redeemer!)
Doxa, doxa s' Afton, to mega Archigo!
(Glory, glory to Him, the great Chief!)
Doxa! Alliloyia! Tha psallo diarkos.
(Glory! Alleluia! I will sing all the time.)
Perimenoume tin imera na erthei Aftos!
(We await the day He will come!)
Verse 2:
Stin ano mas katoikia thanatos de chorei,
(In our upper abode death cannot fit,)
alla aionia tha zoyme psallontas tin odi!
(but eternally we shall live singing the ode!)
Kathe kardia lypimeni th' anapafthei ekei,
(Every sad heart will rest there,)
tote i lypi tha leipsei kai i chara tha zei!
(then sorrow will disappear and joy will live!)
Verse 3:
O, eis ti gi tin oraia fovos den ein' ekei!
(Oh, in the beautiful land fear is not there!)
Anti gia pali kai mochtho eirini katoikei!
(Instead of struggle and toil peace dwells!)
To neo ymno t' Arniou tha leme me chara,
(We will sing the new hymn of the Lamb with joy,)
ntymenoi me tis stoles mas, vadizontas lampra!
(dressed in our uniforms, marching brightly!)
Verse 4:
O! Ti lampri ein' i poli! Nykta den ein' ekei!
(Oh! How brilliant is the city! Night is not there!)
To fos tha einai o Christos mas, ta panta tha pliroi!
(The light will be our Christ, he will fulfill everything!)
Pos lachtaro na eiseltho ki ego ston ourano,
(How I long to enter heaven too,)
gia na foreso me doxa stefani to chryso!
(to wear with glory a wreath of gold!)
