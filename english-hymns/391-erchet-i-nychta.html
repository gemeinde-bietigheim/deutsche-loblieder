391 ERChET' I NYChTA

Greek Hymns - No. 391 | "ΕΡΧΕΤ' Η ΝΥΧΤΑ"
Verse 1:
Erchet' i nychta, skotos prochorei,
(The night is coming, darkness is advancing,)
ela, Sotira, meine Sy mazi.
(come, Saviour, stay with You.)
Parigoria mou echo mono Se,
(I have only You as my comfort,)
Sy, Kyrie mou, meine me eme!
(You, my Lord, stay with me!)
Verse 2:
Svinoun ta oneira eis ti zoi,
(They extinguish dreams in life,)
chara tou kosmou, thriamvoi sti gi,
(joy to the world, triumphs on earth,)
fthora thanatou vlepo pantachoy,
(death wear I see everywhere,)
o Afthartos Sy, meine plai mou!
(O Immortal One, stay by my side!)
Verse 3:
Kathe stigmi na se echo odigo,
(Every moment to have you as my guide,)
enischye me mes ston peirasmo.
(strengthen me in temptation.)
Fos odigos mou na 'sai Sy frouros
(Be my guiding light, be your guard)
imera, nychta, na 'mai ki ego fos!
(day, night, let me be light too!)
Verse 4:
Echthro den tremo, an Sy voithos,
(Enemy I do not tremble, if You are a helper,)
dakrya, ponous, pairnei o Christos.
(tears, pains, Christ takes.)
Thanatou kentro, Adi niki pou;
(Death center, Hades victory where?)
Na thriamveyo meno plai Sou!
(To triumph, I stay by Your side!)
