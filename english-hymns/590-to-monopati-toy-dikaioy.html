590 TO MONOPATI TOY DIKAIOY

Greek Hymns - No. 590 | "ΤΟ ΜΟΝΟΠΑΤΙ ΤΟΥ ΔΙΚΑΙΟΥ"
Verse 1:
To monopati tou dikaiou einai san tin afgi lampro.
(The path of the righteous is as bright as the dawn.)
Olo pio poly,
(more and more,)
to monopati tou dikaiou einai san tin afgi lampro.
(the path of the righteous is as bright as the dawn.)
Lampro san imera, lampro san imera,
(Bright as day Bright as day)
lampro san imera!
(bright as day!)
Verse 2:
Me aggixe mia mera o Iisoys mou kai ti zoi mou allaxe.
(I was touched one day by my Jesus and my life changed.)
Pote den eimai pia san prota, giati tora me esose.
(I am never the same again, because now he saved me.)
Ezoysa mesa sta skotadia, ezoysa mesa stin ntropi,
(I lived in darkness, I lived in shame,)
ma tora irthe o Lytrotis mou
(but now my Redeemer has come)
kai mou 'dose aionia zoi!
(and gave me eternal life!)
Verse 3:
Eisai ta logia pou psallo, eisai o ymnos aftos,
(You are the words I sing, you are that hymn,)
eisai i nea odi, gia Se thelo psalmodei,
(you are the new ode, I want to sing about you,)
ti doxa Sou thelo ymnei!
(I want to praise Your glory!)
Eisai o Thafmastos, eisai o Symvoulos, eisai Theos Ischyros!
(You are the Admirer, you are the Counselor, you are the Mighty God!)
Kathe pnoi Ese tha ainei, enoso konta Sou tha zei!
(Every breath will praise You, while near You it will live!)
Verse 4:
Eisai o Sotiras Christos, m' eleftherose Aftos!
(You are the Savior Christ, he set me free!)
Elyse ta desma mou, gi' afto fonazo doxa s' Afton!
(He loosed my bonds, so I shout glory to Him!)
Einai megali allagi, megali allagi, echo chara, echo sothei.
(It is a great change, a great change, I have joy, I am saved.)
Ap' ti sklavia evgika stin eleftheria!
(I came out of slavery to freedom!)
O! o! o! o! ti allagi! O! o! o! o! ti allagi!
(Oh! oh! oh! oh! what a change! Oh! oh! oh! oh! what a change!)
