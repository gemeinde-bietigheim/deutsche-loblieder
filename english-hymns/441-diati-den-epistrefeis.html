441 DIATI DEN EPISTREFEIS

Greek Hymns - No. 441 | "ΔΙΑΤΙ ΔΕΝ ΕΠΙΣΤΡΕΦΕΙΣ"
Verse 1:
Diati den epistrefeis eis to dromo tou Theoy;
(Why don't you return to the way of God?)
Epimonos sy kofeyeis eis tin prosklisi Aftoy!
(You stubbornly turn a deaf ear to His invitation!)
Chorus:
Proselthe, ti anameneis, den akoys sy ti foni;
(Come, what are you waiting for, don't you hear the voice?)
O elthe, dioti mellei n' apoleseis tin psychi!
(O come, for you are about to lose your soul!)
Verse 2:
O kairos pou tha eklexeis tou Christoy mas tin odo
(The time when you will choose our Christ the way)
einai tora na proseltheis eis Afton metanoon!
(it is now to come to Him in repentance!)
Verse 3:
Min afiseis na parelthei i sotiria stigmi,
(Don't let the saving moment pass,)
pou to Pneyma iketeyei na se ferei sti zoi!
(that the Spirit begs to bring you to life!)
Verse 4:
Skepsou prin apofasiseis, an to Pneyma arnitheis,
(Think before you decide, if you deny the Spirit,)
fovera sy tha thriniseis kai tha eisai dystychis!
(terribly you will mourn and be miserable!)
