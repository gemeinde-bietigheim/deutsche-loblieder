86 SOTIRAS PISTOS

Greek Hymns - No. 86 | "ΣΩΤΗΡΑΣ ΠΙΣΤΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Sotiras pistos ein' gia me o Christos,
(Christ is a faithful savior for me,)
Sotiras apo tin orgi,
(Savior from wrath,)
epano sto vracho me echei Aftos,
(on the rock He has me,)
s' aionia pigi m' odigei!
(leads me to an eternal source!)
Chorus:
Se vracho aionio me kryv' o Christos,
(Christ hides me in an eternal rock,)
sta cheria Tou eim' asfalis!
(in His hands I am safe!)
Sfichta me krata stin agapi pisto,
(Holds me tight in love faithful,)
ekei me fylaei asfalos
(there he is surely guarding me)
dia tis cheiros Tou Aftos!
(by His hand He!)
Verse 2:
O, ti thafmastos ein' gia me o Christos!
(Oh, how wonderful Christ is to me!)
Mou afairese to varos mou.
(It took my weight off me.)
Mou dinei zoi kai eirini Aftos,
(He gives me life and peace,)
einai di' eme i odos!
(the street is mine!)
Verse 3:
Megali eflogia mou din' o Christos,
(Christ gives me a great blessing,)
me Pneyma Theoy me kratei!
(by the Spirit of God he keeps me!)
Me kanei na psallo, nai, panta s' Afton,
(Makes me sing, yes, always to Him,)
anikei s' Afton i timi!
(to Him belongs the honor!)
Verse 4:
Otan eis ta ypsi ki ego arpachtho,
(When in the heights I too am raptured,)
gia na synantiso Afton,
(to meet Him,)
m' aggelous ki agious ekei tha ymno
(with angels and saints there I will sing)
to ergo Tou eis to Stafro!
(His work on the Cross!)
