229 GNORIZO MIA FORA

Greek Hymns - No. 229 | "ΓΝΩΡΙΖΩ ΜΙΑ ΦΟΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Gnorizo mia fora ekei ston ourano
(I know once there in heaven)
ypirche kapoio chreos, chreos amartion,
(there was some debt, debt of sins,)
to chreos de afto me pieze pantoy,
(and this debt pressed me everywhere,)
all' iltha sto Sotira kai esothin pro polloy!
(but I came to Sotira and was hidden a long time ago!)
Chorus:
Pro polloy, pro polloy,
(Long ago, long ago)
nai, to chreos eplirothei pro polloy!
(yes, the debt is paid off long ago!)
Alliloyia!
(Alleluia!)
Me to aima Tou o Christos m' ekatharis' entelos
(With His blood, Christ cleansed me completely)
kai to chreos eplirothei pro polloy!
(and the debt is paid off long ago!)
Verse 2:
O, itan tromero to chreos sto Theo,
(Oh, the debt to God was terrible,)
dioti kathe mera amartana s' Afton,
(for every day I sin unto Him,)
all' otan pistepsa thysia tou Stafroy,
(but when I believed in the sacrifice of the Cross,)
katalava amesos m' echei sosei pro polloy!
(I knew right away he saved me a long time ago!)
Verse 3:
Kai otan o Christos tha elthei eis ti gi
(And when Christ will come to earth)
ki anoixei to vivlio, tha eimai eftychis!
(and open the book, I will be happy!)
Pote de tha vrethei to chreos tou kakoy,
(The debt of evil will never be found,)
plirothike teleios ki esvisthei pro polloy!
(fully paid and long gone!)
Verse 4:
Kai sy amartole, o, ela sto Christo,
(And you sinner, oh, come to Christ,)
plirothike to chreos, tha vreis ton ilasmo!
(the debt is paid, you will find the joy!)
Kai otan tha vretheis mprosta ston Iisoy,
(And when you stand before Jesus,)
den tha vrethei to chreos, eplirothei pro polloy!
(the debt will not be found, it will be paid long ago!)
