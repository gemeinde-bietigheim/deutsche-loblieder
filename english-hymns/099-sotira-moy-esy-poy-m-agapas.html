99 SOTIRA MOY, ESY POY M' AGAPAS

Greek Hymns - No. 99 | "ΣΩΤΗΡΑ ΜΟΥ, ΕΣΥ ΠΟΥ Μ' ΑΓΑΠΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Sotira mou, Esy pou m' agapas
(My Savior, You who love me)
ki apo to cheri panta me kratas,
(and you always hold my hand,)
mes sta pykna skotadia san diavo,
(into the thick darkness like a devil,)
to xero, echo Esena odigo!
(I know, I have You as my guide!)
Chorus:
Esy mou dineis dynami, mou dineis zoi,
(You give me strength, you give me life,)
kai tora echo agapi kai chara alithini!
(and now I have true love and joy!)
Eirini Esy mou charises vathia stin psychi
(Peace You gave me deep in my soul)
kai edoses to Pneyma Sou na m' odigei!
(and you gave me your Spirit to guide me!)
Verse 2:
Sotira mou, Esy pou m' odigeis
(My Savior, You who lead me)
kai to timoni tis zois krateis,
(and the helm of life you hold,)
se dyskolies ki an tha poreftho,
(in difficulties even if I walk,)
Esena panta tha akoloutho!
(I will always follow you!)
Verse 3:
Sotira mou, Esy pou m' agapas,
(My savior, You who love me,)
ston ourano mia mera tha me pas
(to heaven one day you will take me)
na zisoume aionia mazi,
(to live forever together)
na chairomai tin endoxi zoi!
(to enjoy the glorious life!)
