317 I PISTI MOY SE SE

Greek Hymns - No. 317 | "Η ΠΙΣΤΗ ΜΟΥ ΣΕ ΣΕ"
Verse 1:
I pisti mou se Se stirizetai, Christe,
(My faith rests in You, Christ,)
Amne Theoy!
(Oh God!)
Dexou mou tin efchi, sose mou tin psychi,
(Grant my wish, save my soul,)
kane me mathiti kai doylo Sou!
(make me Your disciple and servant!)
Verse 2:
Anapse eis eme zilo eilikrini
(Ignite me with sincere zeal)
kai pio thermo.
(and warmer.)
Dos' pisti zontani, agapi kathari,
(Give faith alive, love pure,)
thermi kai statheri na Se zito!
(warm and firm to ask You!)
Verse 3:
Eno adimono, se kosmo poniro
(While I crave, in a wicked world)
peripato.
(promenade.)
Na eisai odigos, skotadi to pykno
(Be a guide, darkness is thick)
metatrepse se fos, min planitho!
(turn into light, do not go astray!)
Verse 4:
Sy eisai archigos, Sotiras kai Theos,
(You are leader, Savior and God,)
Iisoy Christe!
(Jesus Christ!)
Kathe mou dakry Sy pikro exaleipse
(My every tear You bitter wiped away)
kai eis ton ourano dexou ki eme!
(and in heaven accept me too!)
