639 O KYRIOS MOY, ChARA KAI FOS MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 639 | "Ο ΚΥΡΙΟΣ ΜΟΥ, ΧΑΡΑ ΚΑΙ ΦΩΣ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
O Kyrios mou, chara kai fos mou, o Kyrios mou me agapa!
(My Lord, my joy and light, my Lord loves me!)
Chorus:
Tha leo panta afto pou niotho entos mou,
(I will always say what I feel inside,)
o Kyrios mou me agapa!
(my Lord loves me!)
Verse 2:
Imoun chamenos, apodiogmenos, imoun chamenos pantotina!
(I was lost, cast out, I was lost forever!)
Verse 3:
Stin amartia, sti dystychia, stin amartia me vrike ekei!
(In sin, in misery, in sin found me there!)
Verse 4:
I katadiki, o theia friki, i katadiki the na me vrei!
(Damnation, oh divine horror, damnation will find me!)
Verse 5:
I enochi mou, oli i poini mou, i enochi mou varainei Afton!
(My guilt, all my punishment, my guilt weighs upon Him!)
Verse 6:
Paratimenos kai stafromenos, paratimenos ap' ton Theo!
(Forsaken and crucified, forsaken by God!)
Verse 7:
I sotiria me mia thysia, i sotiria einai gia mas!
(Salvation with a sacrifice, salvation is for us!)
Verse 8:
Dikaiosyni pou olous ntynei, dikaiosyni, agni Theoy!
(Justice that clothes all, justice, pure of God!)
Verse 9:
Me dikaionei kai me enonei, me dikaionei me to Theo!
(It justifies me and unites me, it justifies me with God!)
Verse 10:
I doxa anikei kai oli i niki, s' Esen' anikei, Amne Theoy!
(The glory belongs and all the victory belongs to You, Amne God!)
