238 ME DEOS STEKOMAI

Greek Hymns - No. 238 | "ΜΕ ΔΕΟΣ ΣΤΕΚΟΜΑΙ"
Verse 1:
Me deos stekomai mpros sto stafro Sou!
(I stand in awe before Your cross!)
Asylliptos einai i prosfora
(The offer is unbelievable)
t' agiou aimatos, Christe Sotira,
(of the holy blood, Christ the Saviour,)
pou mas lytronei ap' ti fthora!
(that redeems us from decay!)
T' agiou aimatos, Christe Sotira,
(Of the holy blood, Christ the Saviour,)
pou mas lytronei ap' ti fthora!
(that redeems us from decay!)
Verse 2:
Gia na mas doseis chara ki elpida,
(To give us joy and hope,)
esy stafrothikes sto Golgotha
(you were crucified on Calvary)
kai me to aima Sou, Christe Sotira,
(and with Your blood, Christ the Savior,)
esy mas elytroses alithina!
(you have truly redeemed us!)
Kai me to aima Sou, Christe Sotira,
(And with Your blood, Christ the Savior,)
esy mas elytroses alithina!
(you have truly redeemed us!)
Verse 3:
Mpros sti thysia Sou erchomai pali,
(To Thy sacrifice I come again,)
Christe Sotira mou, na deitho,
(Christ my Saviour, let me be bound,)
to aima p' etrexe ap' tis pliges Sou
(the blood that flowed from Your wounds)
zito, Sotira mou, na giatrefto!
(I ask, my Savior, to be healed!)
To aima p' etrexe ap' tis pliges Sou
(The blood that flowed from Your wounds)
zito, Sotira mou, na giatrefto!
(I ask, my Savior, to be healed!)
Verse 4:
Poly s' efcharisto, glyke Iisoy mou,
(Thank you so much, my sweet Jesus,)
agapi apeiri, alithini,
(love infinite, true,)
se Se, Patera mou, kai ston Yio Sou
(to You, my Father, and to Your Son)
anikei doxa pantotini!
(belongs glory forever!)
Se Se, Patera mou, kai ston Yio Sou
(To You, my Father, and to Your Son)
anikei doxa pantotini!
(belongs glory forever!)
