526 MYSTIRIA I ZOI MAS KLEINEI

Greek Hymns - No. 526 | "ΜΥΣΤΗΡΙΑ Η ΖΩΗ ΜΑΣ ΚΛΕΙΝΕΙ"
Verse 1:
Mystiria i zoi mas kleinei,
(Mysteries our life closes,)
liges chares kai fos mas dinei,
(little joys and light it gives us,)
elpides, oneira ta svinei,
(hopes, dreams extinguishes,)
mon' o Theos xerei "giati".
(only God knows "why".)
Chorus:
Prosmeno ekeini tin imera,
(I look forward to that day,)
pou i agapi tou Patera,
(where the love of the Father,)
tha fanerosei kathe ti,
(will reveal everything)
tote tha matho to "giati".
(then I will know "why".)
Verse 2:
Choris apantisi pernane
(They pass without an answer)
oi prosefches, feygoun kai pane,
(prayers come and go)
kainoyrioi peirasmoi kylane,
(new temptations roll in,)
mon' o Theos xerei "giati".
(only God knows "why".)
Verse 3:
Mia pachni ola ta skepazei,
(A dew covers everything,)
o anthos pethainei, molis skazei,
(the flower dies, as soon as it bursts,)
o thanatos kapoion arpazei,
(death seizes one,)
mon' o Theos xerei "giati".
(only God knows "why".)
Verse 4:
Ki an i nychtia gyro vastaei,
(And if the night is around,)
o Kyrios ta kleidia krataei,
(the Lord holds the keys,)
i mera t' ouranoy fotaei,
(the day of the sky brightens,)
pou ola tha matho ta "giati".
(that I will learn the "why" of everything.)
