101 EIN' O LOGOS SOY

Greek Hymns - No. 101 | "ΕΙΝ' Ο ΛΟΓΟΣ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Ein' o Logos sou sta vimata mou fos,
(May your Word be a light to my steps,)
einai lychnos stis zois ta monopatia.
(the paths are a light in life.)
Tis kardias ein' o ilios
(The heart is the sun)
pou fotizei lampros,
(shining bright,)
ein' o Logos sou sta vimata mou fos!
(your Word is a light to my steps!)
Verse 2:
Ein' o Logos sou psomi tou ouranoy,
(Is your Word the bread of heaven,)
to nero to zontano pou xedipsaei.
(the living water that quenches thirst.)
Tin psychi mou drosizei,
(It cools my soul,)
mou chortainei to nou,
(it fills my mind)
ein' o Logos sou psomi tou ouranoy!
(is your Word the bread of heaven!)
Verse 3:
Ein' o Logos sou machairi koftero,
(If your Word is a sharp knife,)
ein' o Logos sou spathi ki einai kontari.
(your Word is a sword and it is a staff.)
Ton krato mes sti machi
(I keep him in the fight)
kai niko ton echthro,
(and defeat the enemy,)
ein' o Logos sou machairi koftero!
(is your Word a sharp knife!)
