264 DOXA, DOXA, DOXA EIS TON IISOY MAS!

Greek Hymns - No. 264 | "ΔΟΞΑ, ΔΟΞΑ, ΔΟΞΑ ΕΙΣ ΤΟΝ ΙΗΣΟΥ ΜΑΣ!"
Verse 1:
Doxa, doxa, doxa eis ton Iisoy mas!
(Glory, glory, glory to our Jesus!)
giati irthe kato edo sti gi,
(why did he come down here on earth,)
gia na ferei kathe chara kai eftychia,
(to bring all joy and happiness,)
doxa, doxa sto mono Lytroti!
(glory, glory to the only Redeemer!)
Alliloyia! Poso mas agapaei
(Alleluia! How much he loves us)
kai apethan' o Iisoys gia mas!
(and Jesus died for us!)
Alla endoxa anastithike pali,
(But gloriously rose again,)
gia na ferei eirini kai chara!
(to bring peace and joy!)
Verse 2:
Doxa, doxa, doxa sto mono Sotira!
(Glory, glory, glory to the only Savior!)
pou itan panta praos kai tapeinos
(who was always meek and humble)
kai ta panta epathe, gia na mas ferei
(and he suffered everything to bring us)
ap' to skotos sto endoxo Tou fos!
(from darkness to His glorious light!)
O! ymneite to mega onoma Tou
(Oh! praise His great name)
kathe ethnos, kathe fyli tis gis!
(every nation, every tribe of the earth!)
Psallate, nai, psallate ol' omofonos,
(Sing, yes, sing in unison,)
anastithik' o megas Lytrotis!
(the great Redeemer is risen!)
Verse 3:
Doxa, doxa psalloun oi aggeloi epano
(Glory, glory sing the angels above)
tou Christoy anastasi ti lampri!
(Christ's glorious resurrection!)
Anastithik' o krataios Vasilias mas
(Our mighty King is risen)
na mas ferei eirini kai zoi!
(to bring us peace and life!)
Doxa, doxa, doxa eis ton Iisoy mas
(Glory, glory, glory to our Jesus)
pou evgike tou tafou nikitis!
(who came out of the grave victorious!)
Psallate, nai, psallate oloi omofonos
(Sing, yes, all sing in unison)
anastithik' o megas Lytrotis!
(the great Redeemer is risen!)
