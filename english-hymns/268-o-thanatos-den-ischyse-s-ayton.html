268 O ThANATOS DEN ISChYSE S' AYTON

Greek Hymns - No. 268 | "Ο ΘΑΝΑΤΟΣ ΔΕΝ ΙΣΧΥΣΕ Σ' ΑΥΤΟΝ"
Verse 1:
O thanatos den ischyse s' Afton
(Death did not apply to Him)
O! doxa, alliloyia, ston Amno!
(Oh! glory, hallelujah, to the Lamb!)
Enikise to fovero echthro.
(Defeat the formidable enemy.)
O! doxa,alliloyia, ston Amno!
(Oh! glory, hallelujah, to the Lamb!)
Chorus:
Nai, anesti o Christos
(Yes, Christ is risen)
ap' ton tafo dynatos!
(strong from the grave!)
Ethriamvefse, piste,
(Triumph, believer,)
gia na sosei tis psyches!
(to save souls!)
O, doxa,alliloyia, ston Amno!
(Oh, glory, hallelujah, to the Lamb!)
Verse 2:
O Iisoys apethane gia mas.
(Jesus died for us.)
O! doxa,alliloyia, ston Amno!
(Oh! glory, hallelujah, to the Lamb!)
Zoi mas edose kai ti chara.
(He gave us life and joy.)
O! doxa,alliloyia, ston Amno!
(Oh! glory, hallelujah, to the Lamb!)
Verse 3:
Kathos Ekeinos etsi ki emeis.
(As He so we.)
O! doxa,alliloyia, ston Amno!
(Oh! glory, hallelujah, to the Lamb!)
Tha lavoume aionia timi!
(We will receive eternal honor!)
O! doxa,alliloyia, ston Amno!
(Oh! glory, hallelujah, to the Lamb!)
