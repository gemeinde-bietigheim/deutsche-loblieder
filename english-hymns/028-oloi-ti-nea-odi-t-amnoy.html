28 OLOI TI NEA ODI T' AMNOY

Greek Hymns - No. 28 | "ΟΛΟΙ ΤΗ ΝΕΑ ΩΔΗ Τ' ΑΜΝΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Oloi ti nea odi t' Amnoy as psalloume.
(Let us all sing the new ode of the Lamb.)
Doxa s' Afton! Alliloyia!
(Glory to Him! Alleluia!)
Kai me pisti alithini na Ton doxasoume.
(And with true faith to glorify Him.)
Doxa s' Afton! Alliloyia!
(Glory to Him! Alleluia!)
Chorus:
O, ta tekna tou Theoy prepei na doxologoyn,
(Oh, God's children must praise,)
giati elytrothikan me to aima tou Amnoy!
(because they were redeemed by the blood of the Lamb!)
Psallontas eis tin odo prochoroyn gia ti Sion!
(Singing in the street they go to Zion!)
Doxa s' Afton! Alliloyia!
(Glory to Him! Alleluia!)
Verse 2:
Me to aima tou Amnoy loustikame ki emeis.
(We too were bathed in the blood of the Lamb.)
Doxa s' Afton! Alliloyia!
(Glory to Him! Alleluia!)
O Christos zei stin kardia mas ki eimast' eftycheis.
(Christ lives in our heart and we are happy.)
Doxa s' Afton! Alliloyia!
(Glory to Him! Alleluia!)
Verse 3:
Nai, tha fthasoume sto spiti tou Patera mas.
(Yes, we will arrive at our Father's house.)
Doxa s' Afton! Alliloyia!
(Glory to Him! Alleluia!)
To Christo tote tha doyme, to Sotira mas.
(Then we will see Christ, our Savior.)
Doxa s' Afton! Alliloyia!
(Glory to Him! Alleluia!)
Verse 4:
O, tis doxas Tou to throno tha kyklosoume.
(Oh, of His glory we'll circle the throne.)
Doxa s' Afton! Alliloyia!
(Glory to Him! Alleluia!)
Me to plithos ton agion tha ymnisoume.
(With the multitude of the saints we will sing.)
Doxa s' Afton! Alliloyia!
(Glory to Him! Alleluia!)
