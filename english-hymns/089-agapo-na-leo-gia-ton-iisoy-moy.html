89 AGAPO NA LEO GIA TON IISOY MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 89 | "ΑΓΑΠΩ ΝΑ ΛΕΩ ΓΙΑ ΤΟΝ ΙΗΣΟΥ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Agapo na leo gia ton Iisoy mou,
(I love to say about my Jesus,)
giati einai enas filos mou pistos
(for he is a faithful friend of mine)
kai na martyro pos allax' i zoi mou,
(and to testify how my life has changed,)
allos filos den yparchei san Afton!
(there is no other friend like Him!)
Chorus:
San ton Iisoy den einai filos
(Like Jesus he is not a friend)
na frontizei kai na merimna!
(to take care and take care of!)
M' esose Aftos ap' ta desma teleios,
(He saved me from the chains completely,)
mou 'dose zoi charas!
(give me life of joy!)
Verse 2:
I zoi mou itan olo amartia,
(My life was all sin,)
ki i kardia mou itan olo tarachi,
(and my heart was all turmoil,)
me edechthik' o Christos stin agkalia Tou,
(Christ accepted me in His arms,)
m' anagennise kai eimai eftychis!
(He reborn me and I am happy!)
Verse 3:
Pire kathe enochi kai amartia
(He took away all guilt and sin)
kai to varos tis psychis mou parefthys,
(and the weight of my soul near,)
m' odigei eis tis voskes Tou kathe mera,
(He leads me to His pastures every day,)
me potizei ap' ti zontani pigi!
(he waters me from the living fountain!)
Verse 4:
Synechos m' endynamonei eis tin pisti,
(He continually strengthens me in faith,)
ennoo ton Logo Tou kai agapo!
(I mean His Word and love!)
Stin kardia mou dinei diarkos eirini,
(In my heart he gives peace always,)
thelo pantote na Ton ypireto!
(I always want to serve Him!)
