667 O, PATERA MAS GLYKE

Greek Hymns - No. 667 | "Ω, ΠΑΤΕΡΑ ΜΑΣ ΓΛΥΚΕ"
Verse 1:
O, Patera mas glyke se efcharistoyme,
(Oh, our sweet Father, we thank you,)
giati kathe dakry ginetai chara
(because every tear becomes joy)
san skeftomaste ti gi pou mas prosmenei,
(as we think of the land that awaits us,)
opou den yparchei thlipsi, monaxia.
(where there is no sadness, no loneliness.)
Chorus:
Kai tha psalloume ti doxa Sou gia panta
(And we will sing Your glory forever)
stin ourania tin poli ti chrysi,
(in the heavenly city of gold,)
giati Esy mas echeis plynei me to aima.
(for You have washed us with the blood.)
Sou anikei oli i doxa kai i timi.
(To You belongs all the glory and honor.)
Verse 2:
Alliloyia, alliloyia, Kyrie mas,
(Hallelujah, hallelujah, our Lord,)
oi kardies mas plimmyrizoun me chara
(our hearts overflow with joy)
i aionia zoi einai diki mas.
(eternal life is ours.)
S' agapoyme latrefte mas vasilia.
(We love you our worshipful king.)
Verse 3:
Oli i gi theloume Kyrie na noiosei
(We want the whole earth to feel Lord)
tin agapi Sou pou noiosame kai emeis,
(Your love that we felt too,)
giati xeroume pos erchetai i mera
(because we know the day is coming)
pou i salpigga tou telous th' akoustei.
(when the trumpet of the end will be heard.)
