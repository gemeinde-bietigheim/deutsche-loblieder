644 PATERA MOY, SE PARAKALO

Greek Hymns - No. 644 | "ΠΑΤΕΡΑ ΜΟΥ, ΣΕ ΠΑΡΑΚΑΛΩ"
Verse 1:
Patera mou, se parakalo,
(father please)
opou ein' o Iisoys thelo na 'mai kai 'go,
(where Jesus is I want to be,)
gia na vlepo Afton kai ti doxa Tou!
(to see Him and His glory!)
Afto zito, ston ourano na 'rtho!
(This is what I ask, to heaven to come!)
Verse 2:
Ta matia mou tha antikrisoune
(My eyes will see)
tin oraiotita tou Vasilia,
(the beauty of the King,)
to throno to lefko kai ti doxa Tou
(the white throne and His glory)
th' antikrisoune ti gi ti lampra!
(they will face the earth the bright!)
Verse 3:
Pos perimeno afti ti stigmi,
(How am I waiting for this moment,)
pou ap' to throno Sou tha sikotheis
(that from Your throne you will rise)
kai tha kateveis sto mesouranima!
(and you will descend in the middle of the sky!)
Pos perimeno tin arpagi!
(How I await the rapture!)
