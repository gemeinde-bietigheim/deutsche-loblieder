181 VRIKA ChARI, DOXA KAI TIMI

Greek Hymns - No. 181 | "ΒΡΗΚΑ ΧΑΡΗ, ΔΟΞΑ ΚΑΙ ΤΙΜΗ"
Verse 1:
Vrika chari, doxa kai timi
(I have found grace, glory and honor)
kai anekfrasti chara,
(and inexpressible joy,)
ployto aftharto kai ti zoi
(wealth imperishable and life)
kai kardia kathari!
(and a pure heart!)
Chorus:
Ein' chara anekfrasti kai pliris,
(A joy unexpressed and complete,)
doxis pliris, doxis pliris!
(full of glory, full of glory!)
Einai i chara anekfrasti kai pliris,
(It is joy inexpressible and complete,)
chairomai kai psallo niki tis psychis!
(I rejoice and sing victory of the soul!)
Verse 2:
Tin elpida vrika sto Christo
(I found hope in Christ)
pou pothoysa pro polloy,
(that I longed for,)
tha doxaz' Afton, enoso zo,
(I will glorify Him while I live,)
kai tha martyro pantoy!
(and I will testify everywhere!)
Verse 3:
Tin enotita vrika ekei
(I found the section there)
eis tin endoxi Sion,
(in glorious Zion,)
tin agapi tin eilikrini
(the sincere love)
kai to fos t' alithino!
(and the real light!)
Verse 4:
Sto Christo san irtha kai ego,
(To Christ as I came too,)
vrika fos alithino,
(I found true light)
me Afton panta tha prochoro,
(with Him I will always go forward,)
tha Ton do ston ourano!
(I will see Him in heaven!)
