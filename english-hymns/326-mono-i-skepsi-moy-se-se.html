326 MONO I SKEPsI MOY SE SE

Greek Hymns - No. 326 | "ΜΟΝΟ Η ΣΚΕΨΗ ΜΟΥ ΣΕ ΣΕ"
Verse 1:
Mono i skepsi mou se Se chara gemizei eme,
(Only my thought of You fills me with joy,)
ma poia tha einai i chara, otan se do, Christe!
(but what will be the joy when I see you, Christ!)
Verse 2:
De dynatai i glossa mou na vrei, o Iisoy,
(My tongue cannot find, O Jesus,)
pio omorfo kai endoxo onomatos tou Sou!
(most beautiful and glorious of Your name!)
Verse 3:
O, Sy tou paschonta elpis, chara tou tapeinoy
(Oh, You are the hope of the afflicted, joy of the humble)
kai tou thlimmenou i ischys, i doxa tou pistoy!
(and the power of the afflicted, the glory of the believer!)
Verse 4:
Otan Se vrika, o Christe, vrika to thafmasto,
(When I found You, O Christ, I found the wondrous,)
polytimo, parigoro, prostati ischyro!
(precious, comforting, protector powerful!)
Verse 5:
Chara mas eisai, Iisoy, pano s' afti ti gi
(You are our joy, Jesus, on this earth)
kai stous aiones pantote o endoxos Poimin!
(and forever the glorious Shepherd!)
