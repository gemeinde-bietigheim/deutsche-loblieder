357 O! TIN KARDIA MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 357 | "Ω! ΤΗΝ ΚΑΡΔΙΑ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
O! tin kardia mou pare, Christe,
(Oh! take my heart, Christ,)
kai tin fotia Sou vale se me!
(and put your fire on me!)
To pan entos mou na zisei gia Se,
(Let all within me live for You,)
na 'sai Kyrios mou, Sotira thafmaste!
(be my Lord, Saviour, admirers!)
Chorus:
Pigi agapis, thafmasti zoi,
(Source of love, wonderful life,)
s' Esena krazo kathe stigmi!
(I cry to You every moment!)
Akou ti foni mou, na 'sai odigos,
(Hear my voice, be a guide,)
parigorise me Sy, o thafmastos!
(comfort me You, the wonder!)
Akou ti foni mou, na 'sai odigos,
(Hear my voice, be a guide,)
parigorise me Sy, o thafmastos!
(comfort me You, the wonder!)
Verse 2:
Profylaxe me apo to kako,
(Protect me from harm,)
na 'sai odigos mou, tis pistis mou archigos!
(be my guide, the leader of my faith!)
Giati i nychta peftei sta matia mou empros,
(For the night falls before my eyes,)
Sy na lampseis ilios stin kardia mou entos!
(May the sun shine in my heart within!)
Verse 3:
Zygonei mera, mera thafmasti,
(It weighs day, day wondrous,)
ston ourano rodizei glyko fos tis afgis!
(in the sky the sweet light of the dawn is rosy!)
O Iisoys plisiazei, giati ton lismoneis;
(Jesus is coming, why do you forget him?)
Sikose to vlemma, tha 'rthei, den argei!
(Look up, it's coming, it's not long!)
