260 O! TI ThAYMASTI DYNAMI ThEOY!

Greek Hymns - No. 260 | "Ω! ΤΙ ΘΑΥΜΑΣΤΗ ΔΥΝΑΜΗ ΘΕΟΥ!"
Verse 1:
O! ti thafmasti dynami Theoy!
(Oh! what a wonderful power of God!)
Sozei kathe deili psychi!
(Saves every cowardly soul!)
Na empisteftheis eis ton Iisoy
(Trust in Jesus)
kai tha sou dosei tin afesi!
(and he will give you absolution!)
Chorus:
Me tis pliges tou Amnoy,
(With the wounds of the Lamb,)
tou thanonta Christoy,
(of the dead Christ,)
esothikame,nai,sothikame
(we were saved, yes, we were saved)
me tis pliges tou Amnoy!
(with the wounds of the Lamb!)
Verse 2:
Einai dynami eis to aima Tou
(It is power in His blood)
n' afairesei tin enochi,
(to remove the guilt,)
na sou dosei ti therapeia sou
(to give you your cure)
eis to soma sou kai stin psychi!
(in your body and soul!)
Verse 3:
Vlepse eis ton estafromeno Christo,
(Look at the crucified Christ,)
gia na apallachtheis efthys!
(to get rid of it right away!)
Eis to agion ergo pistepse
(He believed in the holy work)
kai amesos esy tha sotheis!
(and immediately you will be saved!)
Verse 4:
Pairnei oles sou tis astheneies,
(He takes away all your diseases,)
ponous, thlipseis kai enochi!
(pains, sorrows and guilt!)
Paradexou Ton os Sotira sou
(Accept Him as your Savior)
na sou dosei aionia zoi!
(to give you eternal life!)
