37 SE YMNO, SE DOXAZO, PATERA

Greek Hymns - No. 37 | "ΣΕ ΥΜΝΩ, ΣΕ ΔΟΞΑΖΩ, ΠΑΤΕΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Se ymno, Se doxazo, Patera,
(I praise you, I praise you, Father,)
gia tin tosi Sou agapi gia me!
(for Your so much love for me!)
Esteiles to monakrivo Yio Sou
(You sent Your only Son)
sto stafro na pethanei gia me!
(on the cross to die for me!)
Chorus:
O Thee, Se doxazei i psychi mou,
(O God, my soul praises You,)
gemises tin kardia mou chara!
(you filled my heart with joy!)
Mou 'doses ti glykia Sou eirini
(You gave me Your sweet peace)
ki anoixes neo dromo gia me!
(and you opened a new way for me!)
Verse 2:
Eis Ese paradino, Christe mou,
(I surrender to You, my Christ,)
tin psychi kai to soma mou ego!
(my soul and my body!)
Giati Esy edioxes kathe thlipsi,
(For Thou hast banished all sorrow,)
gemises tin kardia mou chara!
(you filled my heart with joy!)
Verse 3:
Ki an vretho se fourtoynes kai mpores,
(And if I find myself in storms and rains,)
xero pos eisai dipla mou Esy!
(I know you are by my side!)
O stafros Sou mou deichnei to dromo,
(Your cross shows me the way,)
m' odigei sti glykia nea gi!
(lead me to the sweet new land!)
