168 TI ORAIA GINET' I ZOI MAS

Greek Hymns - No. 168 | "ΤΙ ΩΡΑΙΑ ΓΙΝΕΤ' Η ΖΩΗ ΜΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Ti oraia ginet' i zoi mas,
(How beautiful our life has become,)
otan tin empneei o Theos!
(when God inspires her!)
Ti chara sti synanastrofi mas,
(What joy in our fellowship,)
otan ein' en to meso o Christos!
(when Christ is in the middle!)
Monon i pnoi tou Kyriou
(Only the breath of the Lord)
dinei ti glykia ti chara!
(gives sweet joy!)
Monon i ourania achtida
(Only the heavenly ray)
mas metamorfonei vathia!
(it transforms us profoundly!)
Chorus:
Nai, to mystiko
(Yes, the secret)
einai o Christos!
(it is Christ!)
Einai o Christos i chara mas
(Christ is our joy)
kai i lytrosi ein' o Christos!
(and redemption is Christ!)
Verse 2:
Oloi me to aima lytromenoi,
(All redeemed by blood,)
adelfoi en Christo Iisoy,
(brothers in Christ Jesus,)
me agni agapi enomenoi,
(with pure love united,)
oikogeneia tou Ouranoy!
(family of Heaven!)
Me to mystiko tis kardias mas,
(With the secret of our hearts,)
ti glykia elpida t' ouranoy,
(the sweet hope of heaven,)
as charoyme tora edo ston kosmo
(let us rejoice now here in the world)
me chares kai agapi Christoy!
(with joy and love of Christ!)
