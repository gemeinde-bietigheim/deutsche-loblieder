157 POLY PALIA TA NEA

Greek Hymns - No. 157 | "ΠΟΛΥ ΠΑΛΙΑ ΤΑ ΝΕΑ"
Verse 1:
Poly palia ta nea ki omos pantote spoudaia
(Very old news but still great)
oti pethane sto Golgotha Sotiras, o Christos!
(that the Savior, Christ, died on Calvary!)
To aima Tou lytronei, sygchorei kai dikaionei,
(His blood redeems, forgives and justifies,)
gi' afto ki ego Ton dechthika kai zo tora sto fos!
(that's why I also accepted Him and now I live in the light!)
Chorus:
O! ti chara pou niotho! Ton echo Sotira!
(Oh! what joy I feel! I have a Savior!)
Me vrike, me edechthike i agkali Tou i thermi!
(He found me, His warm embrace accepted me!)
Me agapise, pos chairo, choris ego na xero!
(He loved me, how I rejoice, without my knowing!)
Sti niki tora m' odigei se oli ti zoi!
(To victory now leads me throughout life!)
Verse 2:
Mou eipan pos gia mena echei endoxi mia poli,
(They told me that for me there is a glorious city,)
pou chrysoi einai oloi oi dromoi tis, krystallinoi, lamproi!
(that all her roads are golden, crystal clear, brilliant!)
Kai aggeloi pou psalloun tou Stafroy tin istoria,
(And angels singing the story of the Cross,)
ekei ki ego tha psallo tou Arniou tin odi!
(there I too will sing the ode of the Lamb!)
Verse 3:
Mou eipan pos giatreyei, kathe pono therapeyei!
(They told me that it heals, every pain heals!)
Oi choloi vrikan ti giatreia kai oi tyfloi to fos!
(The lame found the medicine and the blind the light!)
Kai fonaxa: «Christe mou, ela giatrepse ki emena!»
(And I cried out: "My Christ, come and heal me too!")
kai irthe kai me giatrepse o Lytrotis Christos!
(and Christ the Redeemer came and healed me!)
