7 ThEE, TA ChEROYVEIM

Greek Hymns - No. 7 | "ΘΕΕ, ΤΑ ΧΕΡΟΥΒΕΙΜ"
Verse 1:
Thee, ta Cherouveim meta ton Serafeim
(God, the Cherubim after the Seraphim)
me mia foni ainoyn ston ourano Ese ton Agio!
(with one voice they praise in heaven You the Holy One!)
To sympan tapeina Se proskynei!
(The universe humbly worships You!)
Verse 2:
S' Esena, Kyrie, empistefomaste,
(In You, Lord, we trust,)
me mia psychi oloi erchomaste kai prosefchomaste,
(with one soul we all come and pray,)
o,ti deomaste dose, Christe!
(what we owe give, Christ!)
Verse 3:
O! Sy odigise, olous eflogise
(Oh! Guide you, bless everyone)
tous doylous Sou na deixoun tin odo pou einai o Christos.
(Your servants to show the way which is Christ.)
Epitychia dos' sto Logo Sou!
(Give success to Your Word!)
Verse 4:
Me Pneyma plirose, pista ekplirose
(With Spirit he paid, faithfully fulfilled)
kathe efchi, olous mas ploytise, karpo Sou charise
(every wish, enrich us all, grant You fruit)
Agiou Pneymatos afthona Sy!
(Holy Spirit abundantly!)
