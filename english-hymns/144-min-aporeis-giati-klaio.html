144 MIN APOREIS GIATI KLAIO

Greek Hymns - No. 144 | "ΜΗΝ ΑΠΟΡΕΙΣ ΓΙΑΤΙ ΚΛΑΙΩ"
Verse 1:
Min aporeis giati klaio, min aporeis giati meno
(Don't wonder why I cry, don't wonder why I stay)
se mian akri monachos, me kefali skymmeno...
(at one end alone, with bowed head...)
me kefali skymmeno...
(with head down...)
Verse 2:
Einai ora metanoias, pou i kardia gonatizei
(It is a time of repentance, when the heart is on its knees)
sto Stafro Sou, Christe mou, efcharisto, psithyrizei,
(to Your Cross, my Christ, thank you, he whispers,)
efcharisto, psithyrizei.
(thank you, she whispers.)
Verse 3:
Gia ta tosa mou lathi, metaniono lypamai
(For my many mistakes, I regret I am sorry)
kai zito mia chari enas doylos Sou na 'mai,
(and I ask a favor to be Your servant,)
enas doylos Sou na 'mai!
(I am Your servant!)
Verse 4:
Tora svise gia panta me to Agio Sou aima
(Now extinguish forever with Your Holy blood)
tis ntropis mou to misos tin kakia, to psema,
(of my shame, hatred, malice, lies,)
tin kakia, to psema.
(the wickedness, the lie.)
Verse 5:
Kratise me konta Sou mes stou kosmou ti zali
(Keep me close to You in the world of dizziness)
allaxe ti zoi mou, ela ftiaxe me pali,
(change my life come make me again)
ela, ftiaxe me pali!
(come make me again!)
Verse 6:
San ta matia sikono, synanto ta dika Sou
(As the eyes I raise, I meet Yours)
kai me dakrya tote gonatizo mprosta Sou,
(and with tears then I kneel before You,)
gonatizo mprosta Sou!
(I kneel before You!)
Verse 7:
Kai akoyo ti foni Sou na mou leei: «Mi fovasai,
(And I hear Your voice saying to me: "Fear not,)
panta tha 'mai mazi sou kai sy filos mou tha 'sai,
(I will always be with you and you will be my friend,)
kai sy filos mou tha 'sai!»
(and you will be my friend!")
