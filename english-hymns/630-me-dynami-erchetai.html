630 ME DYNAMI ERChETAI

Greek Hymns - No. 630 | "ΜΕ ΔΥΝΑΜΗ ΕΡΧΕΤΑΙ"
Verse 1:
Me dynami erchetai o Kyrios,
(The Lord comes with power,)
megaloprepeia kai timi,
(majesty and honor,)
alitheia kai dikaiosyni,
(truth and justice,)
gemizoun doxa oi ouranoi!
(the heavens are filled with glory!)
Chorus:
Doste doxa sto Theo, einai Agios!
(Give glory to God, He is Holy!)
Doste doxa sto Theo, einai dikaios!
(Give glory to God, he is just!)
Doste doxa sto Theo, einai Kyrios!
(Give glory to God, he is Lord!)
Gia panta, gia panta!
(Forever, forever!)
Verse 2:
Stratos ouranios kalpazei,
(Heavenly army gallops,)
piso ap' Afton akolouthei,
(behind Him follows,)
peftei o echthros ekei sti machi,
(falls the enemy there in battle,)
ap' to spathi tou nikiti!
(by the sword of the victor!)
