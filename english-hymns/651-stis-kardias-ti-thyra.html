651 STIS KARDIAS TI ThYRA

Greek Hymns - No. 651 | "ΣΤΗΣ ΚΑΡΔΙΑΣ ΤΗ ΘΥΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Stis kardias ti thyra exo karterei,
(In the heart the door outside is banging,)
thelei na mpei mesa, akou pos milei:
(he wants to come in, listen to him speak:)
"Na 'xeres, alitheia, poso s' agapo,
("If you knew, really, how much I love you,)
epatha gia sena pano sto stafro!".
(I suffered for you on the cross!".)
Chorus:
Akou... stis kardias ti thyra
(Listen... in the heart the door)
stekei... sena karterei!
(he stands... he's staring at you!)
Trexe na Tou anoixeis mesa sou na mpei!
(Run to open Him inside you to enter!)
Orthios akomi exo karterei...
(Standing still outside he's pounding...)
Verse 2:
Antichei ap' exo mia foni glykia:
(A sweet voice echoes from outside:)
«Erchomai gia sena mes sti skoteinia!»
("I'm coming for you in the dark!")
Trexe anoixe Tou, tora sti stigmi,
(Run open to Him, now in the moment,)
prin apo ti thyra apomakrynthei!
(before the door is removed!)
Verse 3:
Tis kardias ti thyra ki alloi ti chtypoyn,
(Others are knocking at the heart's door,)
o, min tous anoixeis mesa sou na mpoun!
(oh, do not open them in you to enter!)
O Christos sou ftanei, anoixe efthys!
(Christ is coming to you, open right away!)
Ploysio se prosmenei deipno tis zois!
(A rich dinner of life awaits you!)
