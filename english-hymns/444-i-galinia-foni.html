444 I GALINIA FONI

Greek Hymns - No. 444 | "Η ΓΑΛΗΝΙΑ ΦΩΝΗ"
Verse 1:
I galinia foni tou Christoy se kalei
(The peaceful voice of Christ is calling you)
na sou dosei eirini psychis,
(to give you peace of mind,)
thelei tora Aftos na se sosei entelos!
(now He wants to save you completely!)
O! Elthe, se kalei o Christos!
(Oh! Come, Christ is calling you!)
Chorus:
O! Giati amfivalleis sti theia foni;
(Oh! Why do you doubt the divine voice?)
O Theos se prosmenei, psychi.
(God awaits you, soul.)
Ela tora s' Afton, se kalei o Christos,
(Come now to Him, Christ calls you,)
tha sou dosei eirini Aftos!
(He will give you peace!)
Verse 2:
Me to aima efthys tou Christoy na plytheis
(Be washed with the blood of Christ)
kai to varos ton amartion
(and the burden of sins)
th' afairesei o Christos, tou Theoy o Amnos!
(Christ, the Lamb of God, will take away!)
O! Elthe, se kalei o Theos!
(Oh! Come, God is calling you!)
Verse 3:
I zoi mas perna, mipos tha vreis xana
(Our life is passing, maybe you will find again)
efkairies pou echeis, psychi;
(opportunities you have, soul?)
Stin kardia sou empros kroyei tora o Christos,
(In your heart Christ now beats forward,)
anoixe Tou na elthei entos!
(open for Him to come in!)
