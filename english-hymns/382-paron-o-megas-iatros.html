382 PARON O MEGAS IATROS

Greek Hymns - No. 382 | "ΠΑΡΩΝ Ο ΜΕΓΑΣ ΙΑΤΡΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Paron o Megas Iatros einai na therapeysei,
(The Great Physician is present to heal,)
Christos mas o efsplachnikos arrosties n' afairesei!
(May our merciful Christ take away diseases!)
Chorus:
Aftos tis amarties mou eis to stafro evastaxe
(He bore my sins on the cross)
arrosties kai astheneies kai mou 'dose eirini!
(sicknesses and diseases and give me peace!)
Verse 2:
Paron o Megas Lytrotis einai, gia na mas sosei,
(The Great Redeemer is present, to save us,)
o tou Thanatou Nikitis psychi sou na lytrosei!
(the Conqueror of Death may your soul be redeemed!)
Verse 3:
Tis Galilaias o Christos einai paron kai pali,
(Of Galilee Christ is present again,)
pantote einai o aftos me dynami megali!
(he is always the one with great power!)
Verse 4:
Ta panta dynata s' Afton, to Mega Archigo mas,
(All things are possible to Him, our Great Leader,)
to zontano mas Iisoy, Kyrio kai Theo mas!
(our living Jesus, our Lord and God!)
