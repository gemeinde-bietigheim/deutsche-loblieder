85 SOTIRA IISOY

Greek Hymns - No. 85 | "ΣΩΤΗΡΑ ΙΗΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Sotira Iisoy, itan enas kairos,
(Savior Jesus, there was a time,)
otan makria san provato imoun apololos!
(when far away like a sheep I was desolate!)
Makria Sou, o Christe, makria ap' ti poimni Sou,
(Far from Thee, O Christ, far from Thy flock,)
den agapoysa, o Thee, oyte to Logo Sou!
(I loved not, O God, even Thy Word!)
Verse 2:
San agathos voskos me vrikes Sy, Christe,
(You found me like a good shepherd, Christ,)
m' ezitises me epimoni, Sotira agathe!
(You sought me with persistence, dear Saviour!)
Sto cheilos tou gkremoy imoun choris zoi,
(At the edge of the cliff I was lifeless,)
me pires eis ta cheria Sou kai m' esoses Esy!
(You took me in Your hands and You saved me!)
Verse 3:
Christos o Lytrotis, poimenas o kalos,
(Christ the Redeemer, the good shepherd,)
gia ti diki mou tin psychi egine o Amnos!
(for my own soul became the Lamb!)
Me aima Tou Aftos me ekatharise
(With His blood He cleansed me)
kai ti kardia mou allaxe kai m' eleftherose!
(and my heart changed and set me free!)
Verse 4:
Nai, tora agapo tin poimni Sou, Christe,
(Yes, now I love Your flock, Christ,)
ki anagnorizo ti foni pou me kalei ki eme!
(and I recognize the voice that calls me too!)
Se thelo, Iisoy, san mono Odigo,
(I want you, Jesus, as only Guide,)
mechri na ertho kai ego ekei ston ourano!
(until I too come there in heaven!)
