523 AFOTOY EIRINI

Greek Hymns - No. 523 | "ΑΦΟΤΟΥ ΕΙΡΗΝΗ"
Verse 1:
Afotou eirini, chara kai zoi,
(After peace, joy and life,)
mou edose edo eis ti gi,
(gave me here on earth,)
an thlipseis epelthoun polles kai deines,
(if sorrows come many and afflictions,)
i psychi omos ein' asfalis!
(but the soul is safe!)
Chorus:
Asfalis, asfalis, i psychi omos ein' asfalis!
(Safe, safe, but the soul is safe!)
Verse 2:
Kai an me prosvalei tora o poniros,
(And if the wicked offend me now,)
i pisti mou oti o Christos
(my faith that Christ)
thysia, nai, egine, einai frouros
(sacrifice, yes, it was done, he is a guard)
di' eme ischyros kai pistos!
(by me strong and faithful!)
Verse 3:
Afoy oli tin amartia, Christe,
(After all sin, Christ,)
ekarfoses eis to stafro,
(you ascended the cross)
to varos aftis efyge ap' eme
(the weight of it left me)
kai doxazo Ese, Iisoy!
(and I praise You, Jesus!)
Verse 4:
Gi' afto eis eme to na zo ein' o Christos!
(That's why Christ is mine to live!)
Katholou de tha deilio,
(I will not be shy at all,)
dioti eirini tha dosei Aftos
(for He will give peace)
eite zo eite thnisko ego!
(whether I live or die!)
Verse 5:
Sotira, tin elefsi Sou prosdoko,
(Saviour, I await Your coming,)
o tafos den ein' o psychros
(the grave is not the cold one)
tou viou to terma den einai aftos
(he is not the end of life)
alla o ouranos o lampros!
(but the sky the bright!)
Chorus:
Ouranos o lampros, alla o ouranos o lampros!
(Uranus the brilliant, but the sky the brilliant!)
