652 STO LYChNARI MOY SY VALE LADI

Greek Hymns - No. 652 | "ΣΤΟ ΛΥΧΝΑΡΙ ΜΟΥ ΣΥ ΒΑΛΕ ΛΑΔΙ"
Verse 1:
Sto lychnari mou Sy vale ladi,
(Put oil in my lamp,)
gia na lampei, ospou na faneis,
(to shine, until you are seen,)
na fotizei me floga megali,
(to illuminate with a great flame,)
na fotizei, ospou Sy na 'rtheis
(to illuminate, until You come)
na me pareis, Alliloyia! eis to spiti Sou, ston Ourano,
(take me, Hallelujah! to Your home, in Heaven,)
na me pareis, Alliloyia! kai na m' echeis foteino!
(take me, Hallelujah! and keep me bright!)
Verse 2:
Stin kardia mou Esy vale pisti
(In my heart You put faith)
na kinei, opos les, vouna,
(to move, as you say, mountains,)
na vadizo me Sena sto kyma,
(to walk with You on the wave,)
na vadizo me sigouria!
(to walk with confidence!)
Dos' mou chari, Alliloyia! gia na eimai dynatos sti gi,
(Give me grace, Hallelujah! to be strong on earth,)
na prosmeno pote tha 'rtheis Doxasmenos mian afgi!
(to wait when you will come Glorious one dawn!)
Verse 3:
Dos' mou agapi, Christe, dos' mou agapi,
(Give me love, Christ, give me love,)
dos' mou agapi alithini,
(give me true love)
n' agkaliazo echthroys kai adelfia;
(to embrace enemies and brothers?)
n' agapo san mikro paidi!
(to love like a little child!)
Dos' mou agapi, Alliloyia! gia na Se ypireto pantoy,
(Give me love, Hallelujah! to serve You everywhere,)
gia na ziso san ki Esena, mes ston kosmo tou kakoy!
(to live like You, in the world of evil!)
Verse 4:
Dos' mou floga, Christe, dos' mou floga,
(Give me flame, Christ, give me flame,)
na fountosei mes stin psychi,
(to flare up in the soul,)
gia na pesoun oi pagoi tou misous
(for the icicles of hatred to fall)
kai ta chionia liosoun sti gi.
(and the snows melt to the earth.)
Dos' mou floga, Alliloyia! Na milo gia Se me zestasia,
(Give me flame, Hallelujah! To speak of You with warmth,)
tis agapis Sou ti floga stis zois tin pagonia!
(of Your love the flame to life the frost!)
