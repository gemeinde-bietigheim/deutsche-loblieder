35 S' ESENA ThA PsALLO

Greek Hymns - No. 35 | "Σ' ΕΣΕΝΑ ΘΑ ΨΑΛΛΩ"
Verse 1:
S' Esena tha psallo ton ymno mou, Christe,
(To You I will sing my hymn, Christ,)
giati anevikes ston Golgotha gia me!
(why did you climb Calvary for me!)
Megale Kyrie, Sotira mou piste,
(Great Lord, my faithful Savior,)
Esena doxazo, Thafmaste!
(I praise you, Admirer!)
Verse 2:
Ton ymno mou stelno ston ourano, Christe,
(I send my hymn to heaven, Christ,)
giati ta dora Sou ein' apeira se me!
(because Your gifts are infinite to me!)
Gemati i psychi Sou stelnei prosefchi,
(Your soul is full of prayer,)
eleytheri psallei kai ymnei!
(free she sings and praises!)
Verse 3:
Anoigo to stoma, afino tin kardia
(I open my mouth, let out my heart)
na pei “Efcharisto” s' Esena, Vasilia,
(to say "Thank you" to You, King,)
giati m' agapises kai dromo m' anoixes
(because you loved me and opened a way for me)
n' anevo sti doxa Sou psila!
(to ascend to Your glory on high!)
