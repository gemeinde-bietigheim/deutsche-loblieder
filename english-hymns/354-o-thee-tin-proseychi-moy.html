354 O ThEE, TIN PROSEYChI MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 354 | "Ω ΘΕΕ, ΤΗΝ ΠΡΟΣΕΥΧΗ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
O Thee, tin prosefchi mou
(Oh God, my prayer)
thelo tora na Sou po,
(I want to tell you now,)
na ypsoso ti foni mou,
(to raise my voice)
tis kardias mou ton palmo!
(of my heart the pulse!)
Chorus:
Pos potho na 'sai konta mou,
(How I long for you to be near me,)
pantote kai pantachoy,
(always and everywhere,)
na 'cho mesa stin kardia mou
(to have in my heart)
tin eirini tou Christoy,
(the peace of Christ,)
tin eirini tou Christoy!
(the peace of Christ!)
Verse 2:
O, eflogei me, Thee mou,
(oh bless me god)
kai sto dromo tis zois,
(and on the road of life,)
Esy protos, Kyrie mou,
(You first, my Lord,)
archigos kai nikitis!
(leader and winner!)
