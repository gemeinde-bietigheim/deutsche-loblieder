531 SAN TON ILIO LAMPEI I GI

Greek Hymns - No. 531 | "ΣΑΝ ΤΟΝ ΗΛΙΟ ΛΑΜΠΕΙ Η ΓΗ"
Verse 1:
San ton ilio lampei i gi
(The earth shines like the sun)
pou koitame me pisti apli,
(that we look upon with simple faith,)
o Pateras prosmenei ekei,
(the Father waits there,)
etoimazei gia mas diamoni!
(prepares accommodation for us!)
Chorus:
Met' oligon chara!
(With a little joy!)
Tha synapantithoyme ekei!
(We will meet there!)
Met' oligon chara!
(With a little joy!)
Tha synapantithoyme ekei!
(We will meet there!)
Verse 2:
Eis tis ochthes tis gis tis lampras
(On the shores of the land of brilliance)
ti glykeia odi tou Amnoy
(the sweet ode of the Lamb)
tha ymnoyme gematoi chara
(we will sing with joy)
me agious eis tous ouranoys!
(with saints in heaven!)
Verse 3:
Ekei thlipsi kai ponos kaneis,
(There sorrow and pain no one,)
oyte dakry kai fovos psychis,
(nor tear nor fear of soul,)
alla mono fones tis odis
(but only voices of the ode)
tha antichoyne se oli ti gi!
(they will resound throughout the earth!)
Verse 4:
Eis ton Plasti to mono Theo
(To the Maker only God)
doxa, aino, timi, epaino
(glory, praise, honor, praise)
tha prosferoume oloi agno
(we will all offer pure)
eis aionas aionon pollon!
(for centuries of centuries of many!)
