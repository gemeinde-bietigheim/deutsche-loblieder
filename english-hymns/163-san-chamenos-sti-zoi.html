163 SAN ChAMENOS STI ZOI

Greek Hymns - No. 163 | "ΣΑΝ ΧΑΜΕΝΟΣ ΣΤΗ ΖΩΗ"
Verse 1:
San chamenos sti zoi trigyrnoysa
(I wandered around like a loser in life)
zitontas na vro ti chara!
(asking to find joy!)
Den mporesa na xedipsaso
(I couldn't quench my thirst)
mes stou kosmou ta thola nera!
(in the world the cloudy waters!)
Chorus:
Ma san vrika ton Lytroti mou,
(But as I found my Redeemer,)
tin kardia mou edosa s' Afton!
(I gave my heart to Him!)
Ki apo tote allax' i zoi mou,
(And since then my life has changed,)
ein' Aftos to nero to zontano!
(He is the living water!)
Verse 2:
San pouli ti lefteria kynigoysa
(Like a bird I chased freedom)
stis niotis ton trelo kairo,
(in youth the crazy time,)
ma mesa stin kardia ponoysa
(but inside my heart I was hurting)
ki eicha varos, pikra kai kaimo!
(and I had weight, bitterness and woe!)
Verse 3:
An dipsas gia mia zoi pio gemati,
(If you thirst for a fuller life,)
an thes chara pantotini,
(if you want eternal happiness,)
to vlemma rixe sto Sotira,
(cast your eyes on the Saviour,)
mon' Aftos chortainei tin psychi!
(only He satisfies the soul!)
Chorus:
Pote tha 'rtheis ston Lytroti sou;
(When will you come to your Redeemer?)
Tin kardia sou anoixe s' Afton!
(Open your heart to Him!)
Kai tha psalleis mes' ap' tin psychi sou,
(And you will sing from your heart,)
ein' Aftos to nero to zontano!
(He is the living water!)
