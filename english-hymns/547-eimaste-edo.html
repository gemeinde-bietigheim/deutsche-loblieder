547 EIMASTE EDO

Greek Hymns - No. 547 | "ΕΙΜΑΣΤΕ ΕΔΩ"
Verse 1:
Eimaste edo na Se latreysoume!
(We are here to worship You!)
Ki olo mas to einai na Sou prosferoume!
(And our everything is to offer you!)
I agapi stin kardia pou evales Esy,
(The love in the heart that You put,)
fonazei dynata s' Ese: «Avva, Patera».
(he cries aloud to You: "Abba, Father.")
Theloume, Thee, na dosoume se Se
(We want, God, to give You)
psychi, kardia kai nou pou leei: «Se agapo!».
(soul, heart and mind that says: "I love you!".)
