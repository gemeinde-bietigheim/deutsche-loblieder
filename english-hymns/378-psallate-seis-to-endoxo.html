378 PsALLATE SEIS TO ENDOXO

Greek Hymns - No. 378 | "ΨΑΛΛΑΤΕ ΣΕΙΣ ΤΟ ΕΝΔΟΞΟ"
Verse 1:
Psallate seis to endoxo
(Sing the glorious)
aggelma tis zois,
(message of life,)
ti chari anymnisate
(the grace you celebrated)
sta perata tis gis!
(to the ends of the earth!)
Chorus:
O! Zoi aionia, pantote makaria,
(Oh! Life forever, bliss forever,)
dinei o Christos eis ton pisto,
(Christ gives to the believer,)
svinei tin amartia!
(blots out sin!)
Dinei o Christos eis ton pisto,
(Christ gives to the believer,)
svinei tin amartia!
(blots out sin!)
Verse 2:
Olo ton kosmo proskalei
(The whole world is invited)
Christos o Lytrotis,
(Christ the Redeemer,)
amartole, ypakouse
(sinner, obey)
kai tha apolytrotheis!
(and you will be redeemed!)
Verse 3:
Tin klisi anaggeilate,
(You announced the call,)
kiryxate pantoy,
(you preached everywhere,)
afesi kai eirinefsi,
(release and peace,)
ti chari tou Christoy!
(the grace of Christ!)
Verse 4:
O Iisoys mas erchetai
(Jesus is coming to us)
na parei tous pistoys!
(to get the faithful!)
Tora olous tous dechetai
(Now he accepts them all)
ftochoys, amartoloys!
(poor sinners!)
