510 TO ChRISTO MOY PERIMENO

Greek Hymns - No. 510 | "ΤΟ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ ΜΟΥ ΠΕΡΙΜΕΝΩ"
Verse 1:
To Christo mou perimeno pou anevike ekei pano
(I wait for my Christ who ascended up there)
ki yposchethike na elthei pali, nai!
(and promised to come again, yes!)
I psychi mou poso chairei mono toyto na exeyrei,
(How my soul rejoices only to discover this,)
oti o Christos mou erchetai gia me!
(that my Christ is coming for me!)
Chorus:
O, erchetai gia me, nai, erchetai gia me!
(Oh, he's coming for me, yes, he's coming for me!)
O, erchetai o Christos na parei nymfi eklekti!
(Oh, Christ is coming to take a chosen bride!)
O, poso tora chairo mono toyto pou exeyro,
(Oh, how now I rejoice only that I find,)
oti o Christos mou erchetai gia me!
(that my Christ is coming for me!)
Verse 2:
O agapite, gnorizeis, i chara mou plimmyrizei,
(Oh dear, you know, my joy overflows,)
otan m' esose o Christos kai ektote
(when Christ saved me and since then)
m' exagnizei me to aima, me gemizei me to Pneyma
(he purifies me with the blood, he fills me with the Spirit)
ki erchetai kai pali, gia na parei eme!
(and he comes again, to take me!)
Verse 3:
Eis ton kosmo einai thlipseis kai ta panta mataiotis
(In the world there are sorrows and all things are vanity)
kai gia me o kosmos einai ena miden!
(and for me the world is a zero!)
Tora pliris me to Pneyma perimeno tin imera
(Now full of the Spirit I await the day)
pou tha elthei o Christos na parei eme!
(where Christ will come to take me!)
Verse 4:
Ela, file, sto Sotira pou se perimenei tora
(Come, friend, to the Savior who now awaits you)
na se ferei ap' ton Adi stin Edem,
(to bring you from Hades to Eden,)
na sou dosei eftychia kai teleia sotiria,
(to give you happiness and perfect salvation,)
gia na parei, otan elthei, kai ese!
(to take, when he comes, you too!)
