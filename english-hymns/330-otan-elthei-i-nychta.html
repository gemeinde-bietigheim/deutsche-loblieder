330 OTAN ELThEI I NYChTA

Greek Hymns - No. 330 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΕΛΘΕΙ Η ΝΥΧΤΑ"
Verse 1:
Otan elthei i nychta, de fovamai!
(When the night comes, I'm not afraid!)
Otan erthei skotadi vathy,
(When deep darkness comes,)
echo Esena, Christe mou, pou mou pianeis to cheri,
(I have You, my Christ, holding my hand,)
eisai dipla mou kathe stigmi!
(you are by my side every moment!)
Echo Esena, Christe mou, pou mou pianeis to cheri,
(I have You, my Christ, holding my hand,)
eisai dipla mou kathe stigmi!
(you are by my side every moment!)
Verse 2:
Otan echo diogmo, de fovamai!
(When I am persecuted, I am not afraid!)
Otan oloi m' afinoun monacho,
(When everyone leaves me alone,)
echo Esena, Christe mou, mou skoupizeis ta dakrya,
(I have You, my Christ, you wipe away my tears,)
i skia Sou me parigorei!
(Your shadow comforts me!)
Echo Esena, Christe mou, mou skoupizeis ta dakrya,
(I have You, my Christ, you wipe away my tears,)
i skia Sou me parigorei!
(Your shadow comforts me!)
Verse 3:
Otan vlepo vouna, de fovamai!
(When I see mountains, I'm not afraid!)
Otan dysvati einai i zoi,
(When life is difficult,)
echo Esena, Christe mou, pou mou deichneis to dromo
(I have You, my Christ, who shows me the way)
kai s' afton perpatame mazi!
(and in him we walk together!)
Echo Esena, Christe mou, pou mou deichneis to dromo
(I have You, my Christ, who shows me the way)
kai s' afton perpatame mazi!
(and in him we walk together!)
Verse 4:
Otan oi alloi geloyn, de fovamai!
(When others laugh, I'm not afraid!)
Otan me theoroyn fanatiko,
(When they think I'm a fanatic,)
echo Esena, Christe mou, pou glyka m' agkaliazeis,
(I have You, my Christ, who sweetly embraces me,)
mou gemizeis me fos tin psychi!
(you fill my soul with light!)
Echo Esena, Christe mou, pou glyka m' agkaliazeis,
(I have You, my Christ, who sweetly embraces me,)
mou gemizeis me fos tin psychi!
(you fill my soul with light!)
