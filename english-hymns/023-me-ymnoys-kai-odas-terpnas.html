23 ME YMNOYS KAI ODAS TERPNAS

Greek Hymns - No. 23 | "ΜΕ ΥΜΝΟΥΣ ΚΑΙ ΩΔΑΣ ΤΕΡΠΝΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Me ymnous kai odas terpnas ton anarchon Theon
(With hymns and odes you praise the anarchic God)
ainesomen meta charas meth' olon ton piston!
(praise after joy with all the faithful!)
Aftos ton panton poiitis, despotis, archigos,
(He of all things poet, despot, leader,)
Aftos imon syntiritis monarchis tou pantos!
(This our preserver monarch of all!)
Verse 2:
Apo tou midenos Aftos epoiise tin gin,
(From nothing He made the earth,)
ton ouranon, t' astra, to fos, pasan zoin, pnoin!
(the sky, the stars, the light, all life, breath!)
Me to meidiama Aftoy skorpizei tin charan
(With His sneer He spreads joy)
kai me to fysima Aftoy marainetai to pan!
(and with His breath everything withers!)
Verse 3:
Ekplittomai otan Aftoy ta erga theoro,
(I am amazed when I consider His works,)
tin taxin kai tous nomous Tou osakis fantastho!
(the class and His laws whenever I imagine!)
Poy i archi, to terma poy, ton ktiseon Aftoy;
(Where is the beginning, where is the end, of His creations?)
Ta astra posa t' ouranoy, afton to terma poy;
(How many stars in the sky, where do they end?)
Verse 4:
Os thafmasta ta erga Sou, Thee, sti plasin Sou!
(How wonderful are Thy works, O God, in Thy face!)
Ymnoyn aei ola omoy tin pansofian Sou!
(May they all sing praises to Your wisdom!)
Kai nyn me ymnous kai odas S' ainoymen kai imeis
(And now with hymns and odes we also praise You)
me ainous, doxan kai timas, ex olis tis psychis!
(with praise, glory and honor, from the whole soul!)
