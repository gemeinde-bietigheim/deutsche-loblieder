543 O DIMIOYRGE TON PANTON

Greek Hymns - No. 543 | "Ω ΔΗΜΙΟΥΡΓΕ ΤΩΝ ΠΑΝΤΩΝ"
Verse 1:
O Dimiourge ton panton agathon, Sy, chorige,
(O Creator of all good things, Thou granter,)
poiiti ton protoplaston, Se doxazoume, Thee!
(poet of the first, We praise you, God!)
Chorus:
Sy, Thee, dorise ploysia tis efches
(You, God, richly grant wishes)
stous nymfious kai gia panta tis psyches tous enose!
(to the newlyweds and forever united their souls!)
Verse 2:
Sy pou thespises to gamo stin Edem ti thalera
(You who instituted marriage in Eden the thalera)
kai eflogises katopin me to thayma stin Kana!
(and blessed afterwards with the miracle at Cana!)
Verse 3:
S' iketeyoume, Patera, me to Pneyma Sou elthe
(We beseech Thee, Father, by Thy Spirit come)
kai tous neonymfous toytous Sy eflogise, Thee!
(and bless these newlyweds, God!)
Verse 4:
Dos' agapi kai eirini, gia na einai eftycheis
(Give them love and peace, that they may be happy)
kai mes stin psychi galini, oso zoyne epi gis!
(and peace in the soul, as long as they live on earth!)
Verse 5:
Dose na 'nai pistoi doyloi tou Christoy Sy, Dynate,
(Grant that they may be faithful servants of Christ. You, Mighty One,)
kai mes sti zoi tous oli na timoyn Ese, Thee!
(and throughout their lives to honor You, God!)
