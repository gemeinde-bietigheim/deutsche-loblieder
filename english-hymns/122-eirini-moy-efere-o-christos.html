122 EIRINI MOY EFERE O ChRISTOS

Greek Hymns - No. 122 | "ΕΙΡΗΝΗ ΜΟΥ ΕΦΕΡΕ Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Eirini mou efere o Christos,
(Christ brought me peace,)
eirini mes stin psychi.
(peace in the soul.)
Kai oli mou Tou 'dosa ti zoi
(And I gave Him my whole life)
Aftos na ti dioikei! Aftos na ti dioikei!
(Let him run it! Let him run it!)
Chorus:
Thafmasto! Thafmasto!
(Amazing! Amazing!)
Endoxo kai thafmasto
(Glorious and wonderful)
o,t' echei kamei gia me,
(what he has done for me)
de vrisko logia na po!
(I can't find words to say!)
O, einai thafmasto!
(Oh, it's amazing!)
Einai endoxo kai thafmasto
(It is glorious and wonderful)
afto pou 'chei kanei o Christos gia me,
(what Christ has done for me,)
den xero pos na to po!
(I don't know how to say it!)
Verse 2:
Mou gemise Aftos tin psychi me fos,
(He filled my soul with light,)
me mia elpida lampri!
(with a bright hope!)
Kai tora me ymnous mes stin kardia
(And now with hymns in my heart)
poreyomai sti zoi, poreyomai sti zoi!
(marching on life, marching on life!)
Verse 3:
Egeythika ligi ap'ti chara
(I got a bit of joy)
pou me prosmenei psila,
(that awaits me above,)
ekei opou to Lytroti mou pia
(where my Redeemer is now)
tha vlepo aionia, tha vlepo aionia!
(I will see forever, I will see forever!)
