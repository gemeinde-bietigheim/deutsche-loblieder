596 PsALLE S' AYTON YMNOYS ChARAS

Greek Hymns - No. 596 | "ΨΑΛΛΕ Σ' ΑΥΤΟΝ ΥΜΝΟΥΣ ΧΑΡΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Psalle s' Afton ymnous charas
(Sing to Him hymns of joy)
apo To Pneyma ki apo tin kardia!
(from the Spirit and from the heart!)
Doxas' Afton kai ypsose!
(Praise Him and lift Him up!)
Dose ti thesi pou anikei
(Give it where it belongs)
s' ena Vasilia, Kyrio, Dimiourgo,
(to a King, Lord, Creator,)
dose to throno tis kardias!
(give the throne of the heart!)
Giati o Kyrios echei ti dynami
(Because the Lord has the power)
na eleftheronei kai na lynei ta desma! (2)
(to free and loose the bonds! (2))
Aftos sou dinei niki, thriamvo, chara!
(He gives you victory, triumph, joy!)
Ela mazi mas, psalle dynata!
(Come with us, sing out loud!)
Verse 2:
Echo foresei imatio doxis!
(I have put on a robe of glory!)
Echo foresei imatio doxis!
(I have put on a robe of glory!)
Kai to pneyma tou skotous echei fygei ap' eme,
(And the spirit of darkness is gone from me,)
echo foresei imatio doxis!
(I have put on a robe of glory!)
Zei o Theos mou, nai zei,
(My God lives, yes he lives)
zei kai vasileyei, zei kai tha fanei!
(he lives and reigns, lives and will be seen!)
Nai zei! Nai zei! Nai zei!
(Yes, he lives! Yes, he lives! Yes, he lives!)
Verse 3:
Doxa, doxa st' Agio onoma Tou,
(Glory, glory to His Holy Name,)
doxa, doxa stin agapi Tou!
(glory, glory to His love!)
Doxa, doxa eis ti dynami Tou,
(Glory, glory to His power,)
doxa sti megali chari Tou! (2)
(glory to His great grace! (2))
