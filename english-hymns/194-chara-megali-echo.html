194 ChARA MEGALI EChO

Greek Hymns - No. 194 | "ΧΑΡΑ ΜΕΓΑΛΗ ΕΧΩ"
Verse 1:
Chara megali echo mes stin kardia,
(I have great joy in my heart,)
mes stin kardia, mes stin kardia!
(into the heart, into the heart!)
Chara megali echo mes stin kardia
(I have great joy in my heart)
kai psallo ston Christo!
(and I sing to Christ!)
Verse 2:
Eirini mou 'dose Ekeinos mes stin kardia,
(He gave me peace in my heart,)
mes stin kardia, mes stin kardia!
(into the heart, into the heart!)
Eirini mou 'dose Ekeinos ploysia mes stin kardia
(He gave me peace abundantly in my heart)
kai Ton efcharisto!
(and I thank Him!)
Verse 3:
Agapi echo tora mes stin kardia,
(I have love in my heart now,)
mes stin kardia, mes stin kardia!
(into the heart, into the heart!)
Agapi echo tora mes stin kardia
(I have love in my heart now)
kai olous agapo!
(and I love everyone!)
Verse 4:
Chara, eirini, agapi tha 'cho panta mes stin kardia,
(Joy, peace, love I will always have in my heart,)
mes stin kardia, mes stin kardia!
(into the heart, into the heart!)
Chara, eirini, agapi tha 'cho panta mes stin kardia,
(Joy, peace, love I will always have in my heart,)
kai throno sto Christo!
(and throne to Christ!)
