303 ANAGKI EChO SOY

Greek Hymns - No. 303 | "ΑΝΑΓΚΗ ΕΧΩ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Anagki echo Sou, o Iisoy,
(I need You, O Jesus,)
S' Esena erchomai kai deomai!
(To You I come and obey!)
Dia tou Pneymatos Sou Sy katharise mou
(Through Your Spirit, cleanse me)
psychi, nou kai kardia ti rypari!
(soul, mind and heart the polluter!)
Verse 2:
Anagki echo Sou, o Iisoy,
(I need You, O Jesus,)
kai pantote, Christe agapite,
(and always, dear Christ,)
se kathe peirasmo mou dos' ti dynami Sou!
(in every temptation give me Your strength!)
O! Dos' mou tin agapi ti thafmasti!
(Oh! Give me love the fan!)
Verse 3:
Anagki echo Sou, o Iisoy,
(I need You, O Jesus,)
to thelima Sou ego thelo n' akoloutho!
(I want to follow Your will!)
Se olo mou to vio krata me plision Sou,
(Throughout my life keep me close to You,)
o estafromene Theoy Amne!
(O crucified God Amne!)
