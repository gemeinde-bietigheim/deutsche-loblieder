193 ChAIROMAI POY O ChRISTOS M' AGAPA

Greek Hymns - No. 193 | "ΧΑΙΡΟΜΑΙ ΠΟΥ Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ Μ' ΑΓΑΠΑ"
Verse 1:
Chairomai pou o Christos m' agapa,
(I am glad that Christ loves me,)
toyto mou legei i Agia Grafi.
(this is what the Bible tells me.)
O thafmasia agapi gia me,
(O admiration love for me)
pou 'nai polytimi mes stin psychi!
(which is precious in the soul!)
Chorus:
Chairomai pou ki emena agapa,
(I'm glad he loves me too)
me agapa, me agapa!
(he loves me, he loves me!)
O Iisoys mou me agapa
(My Jesus loves me)
ki echo gi' afto chara!
(and I'm glad for that!)
Verse 2:
Kai an tha xefygo apo tin odo,
(And if I run off the street,)
o Iisoys panta m' anazita,
(jesus is always looking for me)
tin agkalia Tou efthys ekzito,
(I immediately seek His embrace,)
otan skeftho poso me agapa!
(when I think how much he loves me!)
Verse 3:
Chairomai, psallo gi' Afton pou agapo,
(I rejoice, I sing of Him whom I love,)
to vasilia mou ton Iisoy Christo!
(my king Jesus Christ!)
Ti melodia aionia, glykia
(What an eternal melody, sweet)
pou plimmyrizei oli mou tin kardia!
(that floods my whole heart!)
Verse 4:
Ton perimeno ap' ton ourano
(I am waiting for him from heaven)
na 'rthei na fygo mazi tou ki ego!
(let me go with him too!)
Ti eflogia, aionia chara
(What a blessing, eternal joy)
tha 'cho konta Tou mesa stin kardia!
(I will have near Him in my heart!)
