478 DYSKOLOI KAIROI

Greek Hymns - No. 478 | "ΔΥΣΚΟΛΟΙ ΚΑΙΡΟΙ"
Verse 1:
Dyskoloi kairoi kai kakes imeres irthan,
(Hard times and bad days have come,)
oi anthropoi skliroi gia to Theo adiaforisan.
(the people cruel to God did not care.)
Katastrofes pantoy, polemoi kai anisychies,
(Disasters everywhere, wars and worries,)
peines kai seismoi, arrosties kai talaipories.
(famines and earthquakes, diseases and sufferings.)
Chorus:
Kai oi meres plisiasan, min argeis, glyke Iisoy!
(And the days are near, don't be late, sweet Jesus!)
Se fonazei i agapi Sou na 'rtheis!
(Your love is calling you to come!)
Kai oi meres plisiasan, min argeis, glyke Iisoy!
(And the days are near, don't be late, sweet Jesus!)
Se fonazei i agapi Sou na 'rtheis!
(Your love is calling you to come!)
Verse 2:
Pantoy paidia Theoy kiryttoune ti vasileia,
(Everywhere God's children preach the kingdom,)
ourania prosklisi gia tin aionia sotiria!
(heavenly invitation to eternal salvation!)
O Kyrios erchetai, Aftos i moni mas elpida,
(The Lord is coming, He is our only hope,)
etoimase gia mas chrysi ourania politeia!
(prepare for us a golden heavenly state!)
