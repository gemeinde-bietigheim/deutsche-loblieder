250 Sto Dipno Su Ton Telefteo

Greek Hymns - No. 250 | "At Your Last Supper"
Verse 1:
Sto deipno Sou ton teleftaio,
(At Your last supper,)
o Lytroti mou Iisou,
(oh my Redeemer Jesus,)
tous mathites Sou antikrizo
(I behold Your disciples)
stin parousia tou Theou!
(in the presence of God!)
Soma kai aima pou tous dineis
(Body and blood that You give them)
kai tryfera tous exigeis
(and tenderly You explain to them)
kai tous ziteis na Se thymoyntai
(and You ask them to remember You)
sta monopatia tis zois!
(on the paths of life!)
Verse 2:
Ypotagmenoi sti foni Sou,
(Submitted to Your voice,)
tin ora afti tin ieri,
(at this holy hour,)
o Lytroti mas, ti thani Sou
(oh our Redeemer, Your death)
thymomaste ti stavriki!
(we remember on the cross!)
Tou ponou, Nikiti megale,
(Of pain, great Victor,)
se mas ti theia Sou thoria
(give us Your divine vision)
charize panta choris metro
(always grant without measure)
kai theiki parigoria!
(and divine comfort!)
