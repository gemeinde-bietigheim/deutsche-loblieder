620 ThA IThELA NA IMOYNA STI GI

Greek Hymns - No. 620 | "ΘΑ ΗΘΕΛΑ ΝΑ ΗΜΟΥΝΑ ΣΤΗ ΓΗ"
Verse 1:
Tha ithela na imouna sti gi,
(I wish I was on earth)
otan, Iisoy mou, ezises Esy!
(when, my Jesus, You lived!)
Tha ithela san ti Magdalini
(I would like to be like Magdalene)
na S' evlepa esto kai mia stigmi!
(to see you even for a moment!)
Ach! na 'mouna na evlepa ki ego
(Ah! I wish I could see it too)
ta thaymata pou ekanes edo,
(the miracles you did here,)
na S' akouga na les: «Se sygchoro»,
(to hear you say: "I forgive you",)
na imouna konta Sou sto Stafro!
(to be close to You on the Cross!)
Chorus:
Kapoia mera san ftaso ekei ston ourano,
(Someday when I get there in heaven,)
to dromo mou otan teleioso kato edo
(my way when I'm done down here)
ki ekei pera to prosopo Sou san tha do,
(and over there Your face as if I will see,)
to oneiro mou pia tha ein' alithino!
(my dream will now come true!)
Verse 2:
Tha ithela na imouna sti gi,
(I wish I was on earth)
otan, Iisoy mou, ezises Esy!
(when, my Jesus, You lived!)
Na S' akouga na les gia ourano,
(To hear you speak of heaven,)
gia enan topo toso alithino!
(for a place so real!)
Na S' evlepa ta matia ton tyflon
(May the eyes of the blind see You)
na anoigeis m' ena logo sou glyko!
(to open with a word of yours sweet!)
Na chortaina me psari kai psomi,
(To be satisfied with fish and bread,)
mia mera pou ta eflogises Esy!
(a day blessed by You!)
