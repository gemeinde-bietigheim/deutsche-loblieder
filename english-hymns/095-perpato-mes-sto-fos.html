95 PERPATO MES STO FOS

Greek Hymns - No. 95 | "ΠΕΡΠΑΤΩ ΜΕΣ ΣΤΟ ΦΩΣ"
Verse 1:
Perpato mes sto fos pou mou feggei i Grafi
(I walk in the light that the Bible shines on me)
ki echo filo pisto to Christo!
(and I have a faithful friend in Christ!)
M' odigei sti zoi ki ena mono zitei
(He leads me through life and asks only one thing)
na ypakoyo kai na empisteftho!
(to obey and trust!)
Chorus:
Vrika filo pisto, vrika dromo sosto
(I found a faithful friend, I found a right path)
kai vadizo me to Christo!
(and I walk with Christ!)
Ki an mou dosei stafro, ki an se machi vretho,
(And if he gives me a cross, and if I find myself in battle,)
tha ypakoyso kai tha empisteftho!
(I will obey and trust!)
Verse 2:
San mou leipei i chara ki echo thlipsi varia,
(As if I lack joy and have heavy sorrow,)
pou na psaxo na vro ti giatreia;
(where should I look to find the cure?)
Xero to mystiko, tha strafo sto Christo,
(I know the secret, I'll turn to Christ,)
tha ypakoyso kai tha empisteftho!
(I will obey and trust!)
Verse 3:
San to telos tha 'rthei tis poreias sti gi
(As the end will come of the course on earth)
kai thanatou skotadi me vrei,
(and darkness of death found me,)
de fovamai gi' afto, to Christo mou krato,
(I'm not afraid of that, I keep my Christ,)
tha ypakoyso kai tha empisteftho!
(I will obey and trust!)
