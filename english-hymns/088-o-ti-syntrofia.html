88 O, TI SYNTROFIA

Greek Hymns - No. 88 | "Ω, ΤΙ ΣΥΝΤΡΟΦΙΑ"
Verse 1:
O, ti syntrofia kai chara agni
(Oh, what companionship and pure joy)
mesa stin agapi tou Christoy!
(in the love of Christ!)
O, ti pothiti kai lampri zoi,
(Oh, what a lustful and glorious life,)
vrisko stin agapi tou Christoy!
(I find in the love of Christ!)
Chorus:
Klino, klino ki eim' asfalis sto kolpo Tou,
(I lean, I lean and I'm safe in His bosom,)
klino, klino, sto vrachiona tou Iisoy!
(I lean, I lean, on the arm of Jesus!)
Verse 2:
Ti terpni odo pou perpato,
(What a wretched road I walk,)
mesa stin agapi tou Christoy!
(in the love of Christ!)
Kathimerina, nai, tha prochoro
(Daily, yes, I will move on)
kai tha m' odigei to cheri Tou!
(and His hand will lead me!)
Verse 3:
Pos na deilio kai na fovitho
(How can I be timid and afraid?)
mesa stin agapi tou Christoy;
(in the love of Christ?)
Eimai asfalis kai m' Afton tha zo,΄
(I am safe and with Him I will live)
gia na fthaso eis ton ourano!
(to reach the sky!)
