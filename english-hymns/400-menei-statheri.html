400 MENEI STAThERI

Greek Hymns - No. 400 | "ΜΕΝΕΙ ΣΤΑΘΕΡΗ"
Verse 1:
Menei statheri panta i agkyra,
(The anchor always remains firm,)
otan thyella ferei i tarachi,
(when tumult brings a storm,)
tha kratei kala kai de tha kopei
(it will hold well and won't cut)
i agkyra eis ti zoi!
(the anchor in life!)
Chorus:
Echoume agkyra ischyri,
(We have a strong anchor,)
kymata an mas ktypoyn pantoy,
(waves if they hit us everywhere,)
menei stin petra statheri,
(remains in the stone fixed,)
mesa stin agapi tou Iisoy!
(in the love of Jesus!)
Verse 2:
Stou Christoy ton ormo prosfeygoume,
(To Christ the bay we resort,)
eosoy perasoun oi thyelles,
(until the storms pass,)
m' odigia Tou taxideyoume,
(by His direction we travel,)
echontas elpida pantote!
(always having hope!)
Verse 3:
Statheri i agkyra tis psychis
(The anchor of the soul is stable)
mesa eis ta kymata tis zois.
(in the waves of life.)
An oi anemoi ischyra fysoyn,
(If the winds blow hard,)
to karavi mas de tha chathei!
(our ship will not be lost!)
Verse 4:
Otan pleon fthasoume sti Sion,
(Now when we come to Zion,)
sto limani tis chrysis poleos,
(in the harbor of the golden city,)
tha eiselthoume kai tha zisoume
(we will enter and live)
me galini kai dia pantos!
(with peace and forever!)
