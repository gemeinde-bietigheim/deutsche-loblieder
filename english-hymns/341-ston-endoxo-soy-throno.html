341 STON ENDOXO SOY ThRONO

Greek Hymns - No. 341 | "ΣΤΟΝ ΕΝΔΟΞΟ ΣΟΥ ΘΡΟΝΟ"
Verse 1:
Ston endoxo sou throno, ypsono prosefchi,
(To your glorious throne I raise a prayer,)
zito apo Esena pnefmatiki zoi!
(I ask You for spiritual life!)
O, plirose me, Thee mou, me pyr Pentikostis
(Oh, pay me, my God, with fire Pentecost)
kai dos' charismata Sou, kosmimata psychis!
(and give me Your gifts, jewels of the soul!)
Verse 2:
Afairese Christe mou, psychis to nystagmo
(Take away, my Christ, the nystagmus of my soul)
kai stin kardia mou vale pono yper psychon!
(and in my heart put pain for souls!)
O Logos Sou na trechei, san potamos zois,
(Let Your Word flow, like a river of life,)
kai oi pistoi Sou oloi, na ein' osmi zois!
(and all Your faithful, may they be the fragrance of life!)
Verse 3:
Aionia agapi, na S' agapo zito,
(Eternal love, to love You I ask,)
m' oli mou tin kardia ese n' akoloutho!
(I follow you with all my heart!)
Ki afoy gia me thysia Sy egines, Christe,
(And since You became a sacrifice for me, Christ,)
na mi krato kakia se anthropo pote!
(to never hold a grudge against a person!)
Verse 4:
Sotira, na 'rtheis pali, epipotho sfodros,
(Saviour, come again, I long for,)
m' agious kai aggelous ki ego na psalmodo!
(with saints and angels, let me chant too!)
To neo Sou to asma na psallo kai ego,
(Your new song may I sing too,)
na chairet' i psychi mou ekei, ston ourano!
(may my soul be happy there, in heaven!)
