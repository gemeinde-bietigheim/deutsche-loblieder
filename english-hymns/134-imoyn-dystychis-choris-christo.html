134 IMOYN DYSTYChIS ChORIS ChRISTO

Greek Hymns - No. 134 | "ΗΜΟΥΝ ΔΥΣΤΥΧΗΣ ΧΩΡΙΣ ΧΡΙΣΤΟ"
Verse 1:
Imoun dystychis choris Christo,
(I was miserable without Christ,)
den egnoriza gia to Theo,΄
(I did not know about God)
oti o Christos mas pethane
(that our Christ died)
sto Golgotha!
(at Calvary!)
Chorus:
Eleos kai chari sti zoi
(Mercy and grace in life)
elava ki ego pantotini,
(I also received forever)
vrika afesi amartion
(I found forgiveness of sins)
sto Golgotha!
(at Calvary!)
Verse 2:
Imoun kapote kai ego tyflos,
(I was once blind too,)
enas athlios amartolos,
(a miserable sinner,)
ki omos m' esose o Iisoys
(and yet Jesus saved me)
sto Golgotha!
(at Calvary!)
Verse 3:
To Christo mou tora agapo
(My Christ now I love)
kai gi' Afton thelo na martyro,
(and for Him I want to testify,)
pethane gia tous amartoloys
(he died for sinners)
sto Golgotha!
(at Calvary!)
Verse 4:
Ela, file, simera kai sy,
(Come, friend, today too,)
dexou to Christo mes stin psychi,
(receive Christ into your soul,)
nai, gia sen' Aftos stafrothike
(yes, for you He was crucified)
sto Golgotha!
(at Calvary!)
