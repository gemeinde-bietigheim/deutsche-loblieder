6 I TIMI KAI LATREIA

Greek Hymns - No. 6 | "Η ΤΙΜΗ ΚΑΙ ΛΑΤΡΕΙΑ"
Verse 1:
I timi kai latreia ston Plasti Theo,
(Honor and worship to the Maker God,)
eflogia kai doxa anikoun s' Afton!
(blessing and glory belong to Him!)
Tis agapis to thayma, to mono Yio,
(The miracle of love, the only Son,)
gia emas Ton paredose eis to stafro!
(He delivered Him to the cross for us!)
Sto Theo, sto Theo, doxa, doxa s' Afton!
(To God, to God, glory, glory to Him!)
Ymnos, ainos, timi, proskynisi pantotini!
(Hymn, praise, honor, worship forever!)
Verse 2:
Doxa,ainos, timi sto Sotira Christo,
(Glory, praise, honor to Christ the Savior,)
eflogia kai ymnos anikoun s' Afton!
(blessing and praise belong to Him!)
As prosferoume ymno ek ton kardion
(Let us offer hymns from the hearts)
mpros sto throno tou monou Sotira psychon!
(to the throne of the only Savior of souls!)
Ston Yio, ston Yio, dox' anikei s' Afton,
(To the Son, to the Son, glory belongs to Him,)
ymnos, ainos, timi, proskynisi pantotini!
(hymn, praise, honor, worship forever!)
Verse 3:
Eis to Pneyma to Agio prepei timi,
(To the Holy Spirit we must honor,)
doxa, ainos, proskynisi «nyn kai aei»!
(glory, praise, worship "now and forever"!)
O Paraklitos menei se kathe pisto
(The Paraclete abides in every believer)
ki' odigei kai stirizei emas sto Christo!
(and leads and supports us in Christ!)
Eis to Pneyma timi prepei pantotini,
(To the Spirit honor must be everlasting,)
eis to mono Theo, Patera, Pneyma kai Yio!
(in the one God, Father, Spirit and Son!)
