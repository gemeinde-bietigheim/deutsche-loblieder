395 ThORYVOI KAI TARAChES

Greek Hymns - No. 395 | "ΘΟΡΥΒΟΙ ΚΑΙ ΤΑΡΑΧΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Thoryvoi kai taraches trigyro mou provalloun,
(Noises and tumults around me project,)
thlipseis, ponoi, dokimes erchontai synechos,
(sorrows, pains, trials come continually,)
apeiles kai kindynoi pote de me tromazoun
(threats and dangers never scare me)
oyte me chorizoun ap' to Christo!
(nor do they separate me from Christ!)
Chorus:
Eis to oros tou Theoy mou katoiko diapantos,
(In the mountain of my God I dwell forever,)
xedipsao ap' to vracho kai aionia tha zo!
(I quench my thirst from the rock and I will live forever!)
O, nai, kathe mera trogo manna
(Oh yes, every day I eat manna)
epouranio glyko kai vadizo pros ti Sion!
(heavenly sweet and marching to Zion!)
Verse 2:
Polemoi kai taraches i gi mas ypoferei,
(Wars and riots our land suffers,)
oi laoi odyrontai, fovoyntai ton echthro,
(the peoples lament, they fear the enemy,)
ma gia me pantoy chara, eirini vasileyei
(but for me everywhere joy, peace reigns)
kai peto sta ypsi pros to Christo!
(and soar to Christ!)
Verse 3:
Trikymies, thyelles pote de me deiliazoun,
(Tempests, storms never frighten me,)
asfalis tha kathomai se vracho ischyro,
(safe I will sit on a strong rock,)
oi dynameis tou Theoy ekei me anapayoun,
(the powers of God there rest me,)
pantote tha meno eis to Christo!
(I will always stay in Christ!)
