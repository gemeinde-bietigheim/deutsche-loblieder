329 XERO KYRIE EIS' ELEIMON

Greek Hymns - No. 329 | "ΞΕΡΩ ΚΥΡΙΕ ΕΙΣ' ΕΛΕΗΜΩΝ"
Verse 1:
Xero, Kyrie, eis' eleimon
(I know, Lord, you are merciful)
ki einai i chari Sou megali!
(and your grace is great!)
Eisai agathos ki oiktirmon
(You are good and kind)
kai mou edeixes agapi!
(and you showed me love!)
Mechri tora isoun mazi mou
(Until now you were with me)
kai tha eisai mechri telous!
(and you will be until the end!)
Iisoy mou, axiose me
(Jesus, claim me)
n' anevo ston ourano!
(to ascend to the sky!)
Chorus:
Thelo na do to prosopo Sou,
(I want to see your face)
Iisoy mou, Kyrie mou,
(my Jesus, my Lord,)
kai na zisoume mazi
(and live together)
ston ourano psila ekei!
(in the sky up there!)
Thelo na ftasei ekeini i ora
(I want that time to come)
pou tha ertheis na me pareis
(where will you come to get me)
kai aionia tha doxazo, tha latreyo,
(and eternally I will praise, I will adore,)
tha S' efcharisto, Thee!
(I will thank You, God!)
Verse 2:
Xero echo adynamies,
(I know I have weaknesses,)
elattomata kai lathi,
(defects and errors,)
monos den ta kataferno,
(I can't do it alone)
choris Esena den mporo!
(I can't without you!)
Kyrie, eisai i dynami mou
(Lord, you are my strength)
kai to mono stirigma mou,
(and my only support,)
eisai o vrachos o aionios,
(you are the rock eternal,)
o monadikos Theos!
(the only God!)
