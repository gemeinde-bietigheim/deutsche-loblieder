413 OTAN TON ILIO SKEPAZOYN

Greek Hymns - No. 413 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΤΟΝ ΗΛΙΟ ΣΚΕΠΑΖΟΥΝ"
Verse 1:
Otan ton ilio skepazoun synnefa mayra, pykna,
(When the sun is covered by black, thick clouds,)
otan trigyro yparchei mono vathia skoteinia,
(when all around is deep darkness,)
otan vrochi perimenei to choma pou toso dipsa,
(when rain awaits the soil that is so thirsty,)
otan esy den antecheis ki archizeis siga na lygas.
(when you can't stand it and you slowly start to bend.)
Chorus:
Strepse ta matia sou psila, psila ston ourano,
(Turn your eyes high, high to the sky,)
ekei pou voitheia tha vreis!
(where you will find help!)
Oi dyskolies pou pernas tha sou vgoyne se kalo,
(The difficulties you are going through will be good for you,)
an mono agapas to Theo!
(if only you love God!)
Verse 2:
An gia Patera sou echeis to thafmasto mas Theo,
(If you have our wonderful God as your Father,)
ki an tis elpides sou oles echeis stirixei s' Afton,
(and if you have placed all your hopes in Him,)
tote ap' ta synnefa piso tha deis to galazio ourano
(then behind the clouds you will see the blue sky)
kai sta pykna sou skotadia tha lampsei to aionio fos!
(and in your thick darkness the eternal light will shine!)
