516 PsALLO ENO PROChORO

Greek Hymns - No. 516 | "ΨΑΛΛΩ ΕΝΩ ΠΡΟΧΩΡΩ"
Verse 1:
Psallo eno prochoro gia ton ourano!
(I'm singing while I'm on my way to heaven!)
Panta deichno stis psyches tou Christoy odo!
(I always show the souls of Christ a way!)
An kai thlipseis synanto, ponous, peirasmoys,
(Though sorrows I meet, pains, temptations,)
me Afton tha niko olous tous echthroys!
(with Him I will defeat all enemies!)
Chorus:
Pos potho na ido prosopo Christoy
(How I long to see the face of Christ)
kai na psallo ymno sotirias Tou!
(and to sing His hymn of salvation!)
Eis to throno gyro na ymno Afton!
(On the throne around to praise Him!)
O! ti doxa, chara eis ton ourano!
(Oh! what glory, joy in heaven!)
Verse 2:
Otan einai skoteina kai apelpistho,
(When it's dark and I'm desperate,)
anatellei fos gia me, gia na fotistho!
(shine forth light for me, that I may be enlightened!)
Otan thlivei o echthros mesa tin psychi,
(When the enemy inside grieves the soul,)
katafeygo se Ese, eis tin prosefchi!
(I take refuge in You, in prayer!)
Verse 3:
To megalo mati Tou panta m' odigei,
(His great eye always guides me,)
sto skotadi tis nychtias me paratirei,
(in the dark of the night he watches me,)
de m' afinei na chatho, panta me kratei,
(he doesn't let me get lost, he always holds me)
me fylaei pistos, ein' toso kalos!
(he faithfully guards me, he is so good!)
Verse 4:
Otan erthei o kairos gia ton ourano
(When it's time for heaven)
kai sto telos tis zois ponous aisthantho,
(and at the end of life I feel pains,)
eis to Logo tou Christoy tha statho ego
(I will stand in the Word of Christ)
kai tha ftaso sigoura eis ton ourano!
(and I will surely reach heaven!)
