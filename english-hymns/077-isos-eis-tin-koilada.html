77 ISOS EIS TIN KOILADA

Greek Hymns - No. 77 | "ΙΣΩΣ ΕΙΣ ΤΗΝ ΚΟΙΛΑΔΑ"
Verse 1:
Isos eis tin koilada skias na efretho
(Perhaps in the valley of shadow I will be found)
i isos eis to oros pou ein' chara kai fos,
(or perhaps on the mountain where there is joy and light,)
gia me einai chara, opou ki an efretho,
(it is a joy to me wherever I am,)
Christon otan echo, pantoy prochoro!
(When I have Christ, everywhere I go!)
Chorus:
An einai mazi o Christos, prochoro,
(If Christ is with me, I go on,)
ki an nefi polla, gia me ein' chara, de deilio!
(even if it clouds a lot, it's fine for me, I'm not afraid!)
Pronomio einai na fero to stafro.
(It is a privilege to carry the cross.)
Ki opou o Christos m' odigisei, tha prochoro!
(And wherever Christ leads me, I will go!)
Verse 2:
O Kyrios prostazei gi' Afton na ergastho,
(The Lord commands for Him that I work,)
opos Aftos me thelei kai ochi opos ego!
(as He wants me and not as I!)
Tote tha chreiasthei na fero to stafro,
(Then I will need to carry the cross,)
na po, nai, Christe mou, tora prochoro!
(to say, yes, my Christ, now I go forward!)
Verse 3:
Ean Aftos thelisei ta panta n' arnitho,
(If He wants me to deny everything,)
glyka tha mou milisei «ese tha chreiastho».
(he will sweetly talk to me "I will need you".)
De tha skeftho poly, tha trexo pros Afton,
(I won't think too much, I'll run to Him,)
tha po «nai, Christe mou, pantoy prochoro».
(I will say "yes, my Christ, everywhere I go.")
Verse 4:
Den imporo n' allaxo ti skepsi tou Theoy
(I cannot change God's mind)
oyte na amfivalo sti thelisi Aftoy!
(nor to doubt His will!)
To mono pou mporo einai na ypotachtho
(All I can do is submit)
kai opou me thelei, ekei prochoro!
(and wherever he wants me, there I go!)
