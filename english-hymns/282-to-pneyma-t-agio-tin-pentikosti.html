282 TO PNEYMA T' AGIO TIN PENTIKOSTI

Greek Hymns - No. 282 | "ΤΟ ΠΝΕΥΜΑ Τ' ΑΓΙΟ ΤΗΝ ΠΕΝΤΗΚΟΣΤΗ"
Verse 1:
To Pneyma t' Agio tin Pentikosti irthe me dynami megali
(The Holy Spirit came with great power at Pentecost)
ki i martyria Tou dia ton piston efere plithi sto Christo!
(and His testimony through the believers brought multitudes to Christ!)
Ki otan tou Iisoy oi akolouthoi skorpistikan s' oles tis chores,
(And when the followers of Jesus were scattered into all countries,)
eferan me chara to minyma, minyma agapis kai zois!
(they brought the message with joy, a message of love and life!)
Chorus:
O Agio Pneyma, nai, ela tora!
(O Holy Spirit, yes, come now!)
Sou to zitame me oli mas tin psychi!
(We ask you with all our souls!)
O, plirose mas ki agiase mas,
(Oh, reward us and sanctify us,)
na mi vradyneis, anazopyrose mas!
(don't be late, revive us!)
Verse 2:
Otan i ekklisia tou Theoy mpike stous skoteinoys aiones,
(When God's church entered the dark ages,)
to Pneyma t' Agio den esvise, emeine lychnos eis ti gi!
(the Holy Spirit did not go out, it remained a light on earth!)
Ki otan stis «eschates imeres» mas pistoi sta gonata ekzitoysan,
(And when in our "last days" believers on their knees sought,)
ilthan oi floges tis Pentikostis mechri ta perata tis gis!
(the flames of Pentecost have come to the ends of the earth!)
Verse 3:
Ean potheis anazopyrosi na 'rthei, piste, mes stin kardia sou,
(If you desire revival to come, believe, into your heart,)
pistepse stou Theoy to thelima me mia tyfli ypakoi!
(believe God's will with a blind obedience!)
Zitise tora ton Paraklito, na throniastei mes stin kardia sou,
(Ask now the Paraclete, to enthrone himself in your heart,)
meine s' Ekeinon me ypotagi, kai tha 'sai mes stin arpagi!
(abide in Him in submission, and you will be in the rapture!)
