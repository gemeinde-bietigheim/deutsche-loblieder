602 ALLILOYIA! EChO ChARA POLLI!

Greek Hymns - No. 602 | "ΑΛΛΗΛΟΥΙΑ! ΕΧΩ ΧΑΡΑ ΠΟΛΛΗ!"
Chorus:
Alliloyia! Echo chara polli!
(Alleluia! I am very happy!)
Echo to Christo mes stin kardia ki echo chara polli!
(I have Christ in my heart and I have a lot of joy!)
Alliloyia! Echo chara polli!
(Alleluia! I am very happy!)
Echo to Christo mes stin kardia ki echo chara polli!
(I have Christ in my heart and I have a lot of joy!)
Verse 1:
Perpatoysa mesa sti zoi
(I was walking through life)
dichos kamia elpida
(without any hope)
kai zitoysa na vro edo ki ekei
(and I asked to find here and there)
chara ki eftychia!
(joy and happiness!)
Verse 2:
Mia mera omos o Christos
(But one day Christ)
mou eipe s' agapo
(he told me i love you)
kai thelo stin kardia sou Ego
(and I want in your heart Me)
pantotina na zo!
(forever to live!)
Verse 3:
Vrika filo mesa sti zoi
(I found a friend in life)
pou panta m' odigei,
(who always leads me)
to cheri mou sfichta kratei
(my hand holds tight)
kath' ora kai stigmi!
(every hour and moment!)
Verse 4:
Iisoy mou, Iisoy mou,
(my jesus my jesus)
Esena agapo!
(I love you!)
Kai thelo sti zoi mou
(And I want in my life)
Ese n' akoloutho!
(Follow me!)
