161 SAN EKEINI TI GYNAIKA

Greek Hymns - No. 161 | "ΣΑΝ ΕΚΕΙΝΗ ΤΗ ΓΥΝΑΙΚΑ"
Verse 1:
San ekeini ti gynaika ap' ti Samareia,
(Like that woman from Samaria,)
sto voyrko psachname kai meis,
(we were also looking in the mud,)
ma xafnou ilthe o Lytrotis mas,
(but suddenly our Redeemer came,)
mas edose to ydor tis zois!
(He gave us the water of life!)
Chorus:
Gemise mas, nai gemise mas,
(Fill us up, yes fill us up)
dos' mas ap' ti zontani pigi!
(give it to us from the living source!)
To potiri mas Kyrie xecheilise,
(Our cup Lord is overflowing,)
svise mas ti dipsa ap' ti psychi!
(quench our thirst from the soul!)
Verse 2:
Tosoi anthropoi ston kosmo afto zitoyne,
(So many people in this world are asking,)
agapi, eirini kai chara.
(love, peace and joy.)
M' afta ta dinei mono Enas,
(With these only One gives,)
Aftos pou anevike sto Golgotha!
(He who ascended Golgotha!)
Verse 3:
Adelfe mas, an afta pou o kosmos dinei,
(Our brother, if what the world gives,)
den sou gemizoun tin psychi,
(they don't fill your soul,)
gonatise kai prosefchisou
(kneel down and pray)
ki o Kyrios mas tha s' episkeftei!
(and our Lord will visit you!)
