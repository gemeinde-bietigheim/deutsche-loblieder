110 AMARTIAS KYMATA

Greek Hymns - No. 110 | "ΑΜΑΡΤΙΑΣ ΚΥΜΑΤΑ"
Verse 1:
Amartias kymata emena pantelos
(Sin waves at me completely)
olo me eskepazan ki imoun apololos,
(I was always surrounded and I was lonely,)
all' o Archon tis zois foni mou akouse
(but the Lord of life heard my voice)
ki ap' ti plani ti frikti me esose!
(and save me from the horrible error!)
Chorus:
Nai, sozei efthys! Nai, sozei efthys!
(Yes, it saves straight away! Yes, it saves straight away!)
To aima tou Christoy, nai, sozei efthys!
(The blood of Christ, yes, saves immediately!)
Nai, sozei efthys! Nai, sozei efthys!
(Yes, it saves straight away! Yes, it saves straight away!)
To aima tou Christoy, nai, sozei efthys!
(The blood of Christ, yes, saves immediately!)
Verse 2:
Enas ein' o Archigos tis neas mas zois,
(One is the Leader of our new life,)
enas einai o Christos, psychis mou Lytrotis!
(one is Christ, Redeemer of my soul!)
Mesa eis to Logo tou panta eim' asfalis,
(In His Word I am always safe,)
me agapi kai chara eim' eftychis!
(I am happy with love and joy!)
Verse 3:
Pos potho pantotina na meno sto Christo,
(How I desire forever to abide in Christ,)
me tin parousia Tou tharros poly n' antlo!
(with His presence I must draw a lot of courage!)
Na koitazo tou Christoy prosopo to glyky,
(To look at Christ's sweet face,)
toyto panta Tou zito stin prosefchi!
(this is what I always ask of Him in prayer!)
Verse 4:
Tha ymnoyme ti storgi tou Iisoy Christoy
(We will praise the love of Jesus Christ)
pou eplirose gia mas epi skliroy stafroy!
(who paid for us on a hard cross!)
Mi fovasai tora esy, agapiti psychi,
(Fear not now, dear soul,)
Trexe, plysou sto stafro, gia na sotheis!
(Run, wash at the cross, to be saved!)
