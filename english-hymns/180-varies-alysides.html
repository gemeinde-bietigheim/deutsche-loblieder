180 VARIES ALYSIDES

Greek Hymns - No. 180 | "ΒΑΡΙΕΣ ΑΛΥΣΙΔΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Varies alysides demeni i psychi,
(Heavy chains bind the soul,)
zoysame se mia fylaki.
(we lived in a prison.)
Kapoios mas edose nea ftera,
(Someone gave us new wings,)
gia na petaxoume psila!
(to fly high!)
Chorus:
Tora petame gia ti nea gi
(Now we fly to the new land)
kai o Christos mas odigei!
(and Christ leads us!)
Evale mesa mas nea kardia
(He put a new heart in us)
kai t' Agio Pneyma kyverna!
(and the Holy Spirit rules!)
Verse 2:
Ta pathi mas ola kryvan to fos
(Our passions all hid the light)
pou 'nai o Iisoys Christos!
(where is Jesus Christ!)
Aftos mas elytrose ap' ti sklavia,
(He redeemed us from slavery,)
mas charise ti lefteria!
(grant us freedom!)
Verse 3:
Sta cheria kratame to fos t' ouranoy
(We hold the light of heaven in our hands)
pou 'nai o Logos tou Theoy!
(where is the Word of God!)
Aftos me to Pneyma Tou mas odigei,
(He by His Spirit leads us,)
mas odigei sti nea gi!
(leads us to the new earth!)
