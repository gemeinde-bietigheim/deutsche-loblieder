362 APERANTOS KAMPOS

Greek Hymns - No. 362 | "ΑΠΕΡΑΝΤΟΣ ΚΑΜΠΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Aperantos kampos, chorafia polla,
(Endless plain, many fields,)
me stachya pou gernoun varia,
(with heavy drooping ears,)
provalloun empros mou kai kapoia foni
(they also project some voice in front of me)
akoyo na me proskalei!
(I hear him inviting me!)
Chorus:
Me kalei, gia na trexo ki ego
(He calls me, so I can run too)
kai mes sti douleia na vretho,
(and to get to work,)
na kano megali ki ego thimonia,
(to make a big haystack too,)
ap' osa theriso sparta!
(from what I reap seeds!)
Verse 2:
O Kyrios stekei olorthos mprosta
(The Lord stands straight ahead)
me tin ponemeni matia,
(with the pained look,)
koitazei ton kampo ki emena kalei
(he looks at the plain and calls me)
na trexo gia kapoia psychi!
(to run for some soul!)
Verse 3:
Anavoun ta stachya, vogkoyn oi psyches,
(The ears of corn light up, the souls moan,)
douleyoun sklira oi theristes,
(the reapers work hard,)
ma tha 'nai o idrotas ki o kopos glykos,
(but the sweat and the toil will be sweet,)
afoy mazeftei o karpos!
(after the fruit is picked!)
Verse 4:
Megalos o kampos, mou leei o Christos,
(The plain is great, Christ tells me,)
ma pou 'nai o dikos sou agros;
(but where is your field?)
Apo to chorafi pou sou elache edo,
(From the field that came to you here,)
ego perimeno karpo!
(I am waiting for fruit!)
