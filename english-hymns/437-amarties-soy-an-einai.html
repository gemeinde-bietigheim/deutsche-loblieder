437 AMARTIES SOY AN EINAI

Greek Hymns - No. 437 | "ΑΜΑΡΤΙΕΣ ΣΟΥ ΑΝ ΕΙΝΑΙ"
Verse 1:
Amarties sou an einai san to aima erythres,
(If your sins are as red as blood,)
amarties sou an einai san to aima erythres,
(if your sins are as red as blood,)
oles tha katharisthoyne, tha genoyn lefkes!
(they will all be cleansed, they will become white!)
Amarties sou an einai erythres os porfyroyn,
(If your sins are red as purple,)
san to chioni tha genoyn, oles tha exaleifthoyn!
(like snow they will be born, they will all be wiped out!)
Verse 2:
O Pateras se prosmenei eis to eleos Aftoy,
(The Father awaits you in His mercy,)
o Pateras se prosmenei eis to eleos Aftoy,
(the Father awaits you in His mercy,)
thelei na se katharisei m' aima tou Christoy!
(He wants to cleanse you with the blood of Christ!)
O Pateras se prosmenei, amarties sou na plyneis
(The Father is waiting for you, your sins to wash away)
eis to aima tou Christoy pou echyth' epi stafroy!
(in the blood of Christ on the cross!)
Verse 3:
Akouse ti psithyrizei tou Christoy foni pros se,
(Hear what Christ's voice whispers to you,)
akouse ti psithyrizei tou Christoy foni pros se
(hear what Christ's voice whispers to you)
«Strepse kai des estafrothik' o Christos gia se!»
("Turn and see Christ crucified for you!")
Akouse ti psithyrizei me agapi o Sotiras,
(Hear what the Savior whispers with love,)
«estafrothika gia se, ela tora, amartole!»
("I am crucified for you, come now, sinner!")
