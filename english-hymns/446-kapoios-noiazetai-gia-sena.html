446 KAPOIOS NOIAZETAI GIA SENA

Greek Hymns - No. 446 | "ΚΑΠΟΙΟΣ ΝΟΙΑΖΕΤΑΙ ΓΙΑ ΣΕΝΑ"
Verse 1:
Kapoios noiazetai gia sena pou 'cheis pono stin kardia,
(Someone cares about you heartache,)
kapoios thelei na sou dosei eftychia kai chara!
(someone wants to give you happiness and joy!)
Kapoios noiazetai gia sena pou 'cheis pono stin kardia,
(Someone cares about you heartache,)
kapoios thelei na sou dosei eftychia kai chara!
(someone wants to give you happiness and joy!)
Kapoios pou ton xechases stis pio omorfes stigmes,
(Someone you forgot in the most beautiful moments,)
kapoios pou se perimenei me agkales anoichtes!
(someone who is waiting for you with open arms!)
Chorus:
O Iisoys Christos, o Iisoys Christos
(Jesus Christ, Jesus Christ)
einai Aftos pou noiazetai gia sena!
(it is He who cares for you!)
O Iisoys Christos, o Iisoys Christos
(Jesus Christ, Jesus Christ)
einai Aftos pou pethane gia sena!
(it is He who died for you!)
O Iisoys, o Iisoys
(Jesus, Jesus)
einai Aftos pou noiazetai gia sena!
(it is He who cares for you!)
O Iisoys, o Iisoys
(Jesus, Jesus)
einai Aftos pou pethane gia sena!
(it is He who died for you!)
Verse 2:
Kapoios noiazetai gia sena, ekei pou esy de noiazesai,
(Someone cares for you, where you don't,)
kapoios prospathei gia sena, oso de fantazesai!
(someone is trying for you, more than you can imagine!)
Kapoios noiazetai gia sena, ekei pou esy de noiazesai,
(Someone cares for you, where you don't,)
kapoios prospathei gia sena, oso de fantazesai!
(someone is trying for you, more than you can imagine!)
Kapoios noiazetai gia sena pou 'cheis thlipsi stin psychi,
(Someone cares for you who have sorrow in the soul,)
kapoios pou giatreyei aionia, tha Ton vreis stin prosefchi!
(one who heals eternally, you will find Him in prayer!)
