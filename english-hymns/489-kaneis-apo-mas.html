489 KANEIS APO MAS

Greek Hymns - No. 489 | "ΚΑΝΕΙΣ ΑΠΟ ΜΑΣ"
Verse 1:
Kaneis apo mas de tha xerei tin ora,
(None of us will know the time,)
otan o Christos tou Patera ti chora
(when Christ the Father the country)
th' afisei kai tha 'rthei kai pali endoxos
(he will leave and come again in glory)
na dechthei tous pistoys Aftoy!
(to receive His faithful!)
Chorus:
Pote, telos, Christe, tha psallo ti nea odi;
(When, finally, Christ, shall I sing the new ode?)
O Christos mas erchetai!
(Christ is coming to us!)
Alliloyia! Amin!
(Alleluia! Amen!)
Alliloyia! Amin!
(Alleluia! Amen!)
Verse 2:
Proi i arga i sto fos tis imeras
(Morning or late or daylight)
i isos mia nychta me skotos vathy,
(or perhaps a night of deep darkness,)
Christos o Sotiras tha elthei endoxos
(Christ the Savior will come in glory)
na dechthei tous pistoys Aftoy!
(to receive His faithful!)
Verse 3:
Eno straties ton aggelon ymnoyne
(While hosts of angels sing)
kai pantachoy nikis odes antichoyne,
(and everywhere odes of victory resound,)
Christos o Sotiras tha elthei endoxos
(Christ the Savior will come in glory)
na dechthei tous pistoys Aftoy!
(to receive His faithful!)
Verse 4:
O! Ti eftychis i imera ekeini!
(Oh! What a happy day!)
Ean, prin me plixei thanatou odyni,
(If, before death pangs strike me,)
o megas Sotiras mas elthei endoxos
(may our great Savior come in glory)
na dechthei tous pistoys Aftoy!
(to receive His faithful!)
