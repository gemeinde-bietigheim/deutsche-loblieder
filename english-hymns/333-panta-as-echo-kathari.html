333 PANTA AS EChO KAThARI

Greek Hymns - No. 333 | "ΠΑΝΤΑ ΑΣ ΕΧΩ ΚΑΘΑΡΗ"
Verse 1:
Panta as echo kathari kardia, o Thee,
(May I always have a pure heart, O God,)
na psallo panta me chara ki enthermos, Agathe!
(may I always sing with joy and fervor, Agatha!)
Verse 2:
Pantote adoli, agni, praeia, efpeithi
(Always docile, pure, meek, docile)
pou na akoyei Se, Christe, kai na S' akolouthei!
(who listens to You, Christ, and follows You!)
Verse 3:
Kardia agni kai kathari me aima tou Christoy,
(Heart pure and clean with blood of Christ,)
eilikrini kai tapeini, omoioma Aftoy!
(honest and humble, His image!)
Verse 4:
Kardia nea entelos, keni amartion,
(Heart new entirely, void of sin,)
apallagmeni entelos apo ton poniro!
(completely free from the wicked!)
Verse 5:
As einai pantote afti naos tou Lytroti
(May this always be the temple of the Redeemer)
kai thronos tou Theoy Patros, ton panton Poiiti!
(and throne of God the Father, Poet of all!)
