5 EYLOGISE, PsYChI

Greek Hymns - No. 5 | "ΕΥΛΟΓΗΣΕ, ΨΥΧΗ"
Verse 1:
Eflogise, psychi, ton Ypsisto Theo,
(Bless, soul, the Most High God,)
pou s' efere edo sti gi me endoxo skopo!
(who brought you here on earth with a glorious purpose!)
Verse 2:
San tin eikona Tou thelei na kamei se,
(Like His image He wants to make you,)
s' egennis' i agapi Tou, eflogei Ton, piste!
(His love gave birth to you, bless Him, believer!)
Verse 3:
Gia odigo sti gi, gia tis psychis sou fos
(For a guide on earth, for the light of your soul)
sou charise to Logo tou, megalo thisafro!
(he gave you his Word, great treasure!)
Verse 4:
Ki ektos ton agathon pou sou 'dose sti gi,
(And besides the goods he gave you on earth,)
sou charise me to Christo aionia zoi!
(grant you eternal life with Christ!)
Verse 5:
Pote loipon, psychi, mi lismoneis Afton,
(So never, soul, forget Him,)
alla eflogei diarkos, eflogei to Theo!
(but bless continually, bless God!)
