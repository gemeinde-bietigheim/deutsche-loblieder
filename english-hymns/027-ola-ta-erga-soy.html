27 OLA TA ERGA SOY

Greek Hymns - No. 27 | "ΟΛΑ ΤΑ ΕΡΓΑ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Ola ta erga Sou gia Se miloyne,
(All Thy works speak of Thee,)
Patera endoxe kai krataie.
(Glorious and Mighty Father.)
Megaloprepeia eisai ntymenos,
(Magnificence thou art clothed,)
tou kosmou Ypsiste Dimiourge!
(of the world Supreme Creator!)
Ola ta erga Sou gia se miloyne,
(All Thy works speak of Thee,)
themelia evales Esy sti gi.
(You laid foundations on the earth.)
Potamia anoixes mes sta faraggia
(You opened rivers in the gorges)
kai stelneis ti vrochi s' oli ti gi!
(and you send the rain to all the earth!)
Verse 2:
Ola ta erga Sou gia Se miloyne,
(All Thy works speak of Thee,)
gia ti sofia Sou ti thafmasti
(for Your admirable wisdom)
ki ola me mia foni doxologoyne
(and all praise with one voice)
tin ploysia agapi Sou kai ti storgi!
(Your rich love and affection!)
Ola ta erga Sou gia Se miloyne
(All Your works speak of You)
kai prosdokoyne m' elpida polli
(and they expect with great hope)
to cheri Sou to storgiko n' aploseis,
(your loving hand to stretch out,)
gia na chortasoune apo trofi!
(to get their fill of food!)
Verse 3:
Ola ta erga Sou gia Se miloyne,
(All Thy works speak of Thee,)
potamia thalasses ki okeanoi,
(rivers seas and oceans,)
me ti sofia Sou einai plasmena
(by Thy wisdom they are wrought)
asteria, ilios kai ouranoi!
(stars, sun and skies!)
Ola ta erga Sou gia Se miloyne,
(All Thy works speak of Thee,)
psalmoi kai ymnoi anikoun se Se
(psalms and hymns belong to Se)
gia tin agapi Sou kai pronoia Sou
(for Your love and Your providence)
s' oli tin ktisi Sou, Dimiourge!
(in all Thy creation, Creator!)
