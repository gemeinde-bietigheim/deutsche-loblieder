573 ELATE NIATA STO LYTROTI

Greek Hymns - No. 573 | "ΕΛΑΤΕ ΝΙΑΤΑ ΣΤΟ ΛΥΤΡΩΤΗ"
Verse 1:
Elate niata sto Lytroti,
(Come youths to the Redeemer,)
to stafromeno thriamvefti,
(the crucified triumphant,)
pou mas charizei zoi agni,
(who gives us pure life,)
aionia ki omorfi!
(eternal and beautiful!)
Chorus:
Ti chara ta niata na vrethoyn
(What a joy for youth to find)
sto Christo, gi' Afton na ergasthoyn!
(in Christ, for Him to work!)
Me ti simaia tou Logou Tou
(With the banner of His Word)
empros na prochoroyn!
(forward to advance!)
Verse 2:
Elate niata ston Archigo,
(Come youths to the Chief,)
san stratiotes Tou sto plefro,
(as soldiers at His side,)
mas perimenei doxa lampri,
(glorious glory awaits us,)
niki pantotini!
(eternal victory!)
Verse 3:
Oloi mas gelasan fovera,
(We all had a great laugh,)
mas spasan tis psychis ta ftera!
(the wings broke our souls!)
Aftos monacha mas menei pia
(He's the only one left)
elpida kai chara!
(hope and joy!)
