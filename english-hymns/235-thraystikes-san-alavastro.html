235 ThRAYSTIKES SAN ALAVASTRO

Greek Hymns - No. 235 | "ΘΡΑΥΣΤΗΚΕΣ ΣΑΝ ΑΛΑΒΑΣΤΡΟ"
Verse 1:
Thraystikes san alavastro
(You broke like alabaster)
stou Golgotha ton akmona,
(on Golgotha ​​the anvil,)
myro zois varytimo agapis ki omorfias!
(life-giving myrrh of love and beauty!)
Ki olous emas afypnises
(And you woke us all up)
se mian afgi anoixiatiki,
(on a spring dawn,)
kathos mas eprosferthikes ourania efodia!
(as you offered us heavenly fragrance!)
Chorus:
Ela, Christe, i elpida mas,
(Come, Christ, our hope,)
kai fotise to dromo mas
(and light our way)
me ti lampri Sou anastasi
(with Your glorious resurrection)
sti skoteini nychtia!
(in the dark night!)
Verse 2:
Efyges, ma i thymisi,
(You're gone, but the memory)
t' oneiro omos zontano,
(but the dream is alive)
mesa mas gigantonetai xana o erchomos Sou!
(within us Your coming is magnified again!)
Chronia zesti i eikona Sou
(Long live Your image)
anantikry sta matia mas,
(before our eyes,)
indalma tis latreias mas, na se thoroyn Patera mas!
(symbol of our worship, may you be called our Father!)
