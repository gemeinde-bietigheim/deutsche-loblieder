585 MIKRI, SEMNI KAI TAPEINI

Greek Hymns - No. 585 | "ΜΙΚΡΗ, ΣΕΜΝΗ ΚΑΙ ΤΑΠΕΙΝΗ"
Verse 1:
Mikri, semni kai tapeini,
(Small, modest and humble,)
o polis Vithleem,
(O city of Bethlehem,)
mes sti vathia ti nychta sou
(deep into your night)
to sympan siopa
(the universe is silent)
kai xafnou sto lampro ourano
(and suddenly in the bright sky)
efanik' ena fos,
(a light appeared)
to fos pou dinei stin kardia
(the light it gives to the heart)
elpida kai chara!
(hope and joy!)
Verse 2:
Eno koimoyntai oi anthropoi
(While people sleep)
ki oi magoi agrypnoyn,
(and wizards keep watch,)
oi aggeloi emfanizontai
(angels appear)
kai to Theo ymnoyn!
(and they praise God!)
Doxa Theo kai epi gis
(Praise God and on earth)
eirini tois laois!
(peace to the people!)
O Iisoys gennithike,
(Jesus was born,)
amartolon Sotir!
(Savior of sinners!)
Verse 3:
Mikri, semni kai tapeini,
(Small, modest and humble,)
o polis Vithleem,
(O city of Bethlehem,)
sti ftochiki ti fatni sou
(in your poor manger)
efdokis' o Theos
(God bless you)
na gennithei, ensarkotheis
(to be born, incarnate)
o Iisoys Christos,
(jesus christ,)
gia na vrei fos kathe psychi
(for every soul to find light)
sto theio Lytroti!
(to Uncle Lytrotis!)
