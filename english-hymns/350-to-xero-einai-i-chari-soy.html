350 TO XERO, EINAI I ChARI SOY

Greek Hymns - No. 350 | "ΤΟ ΞΕΡΩ, ΕΙΝΑΙ Η ΧΑΡΗ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1:
To xero, einai i chari Sou pou m' esose,
(I know, it's Your grace that saved me,)
den axiza, ma Esy gia mena pethanes.
(I didn't deserve it, but You died for me.)
Esy poso eponeses gia mena!
(How much you suffered for me!)
Esy poso agapises ki emena!
(How you loved me too!)
Chorus:
Gi' afto plase me, plase me sta cheria Sou,
(Therefore mold me, mold me in Thy hands,)
vale me, vale me sto dromo Sou,
(put me, put me in Your way,)
Kyrie, Kyrie Iisoy Christe!
(Lord, Lord Jesus Christ!)
Verse 2:
Se kathe mou lepto S' echo anagki,
(In my every minute I need you,)
se kathe mou stigmi thelo Esena!
(in my every moment I want You!)
Na m' odigeis sta chnaria ta dika Sou,
(Lead me in Your footsteps,)
gia na 'mai kato apo to thelima Sou!
(to be under Your will!)
Verse 3:
Stenos kai dyskolos einai o dromos Sou,
(Narrow and difficult is Your way,)
to xero thlipseis, mpores tha peraso!
(I know sorrows, rains I will pass!)
Ma xero pos Esy pote de lygises
(But I know that You never bent)
kai thelo oyt' ego na mi lygiso!
(and I don't want to bend either!)
