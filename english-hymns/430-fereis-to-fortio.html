430 FEREIS TO FORTIO

Greek Hymns - No. 430 | "ΦΕΡΕΙΣ ΤΟ ΦΟΡΤΙΟ"
Verse 1:
Fereis to fortio monos sou pantoy,
(You carry the load yourself everywhere,)
echeis lypi, pono eis to vio sou;
(do you have sorrow, pain in your life?)
Sy den eisai monos, einai meta sou
(You are not alone, they are after you)
kai se entharrynei o Christos pantoy!
(and Christ encourages you everywhere!)
Chorus:
Nai, einai pantoy, panta eflogei,
(Yes, he is everywhere, always blessing,)
akoue Afton tora sou 'milei!
(listen to Him now He is speaking to you!)
Afise to mellon eis ta cheria Tou,
(Leave the future in His hands,)
sti chara, sti lypi, o Christos pantoy!
(in joy, in sorrow, Christ everywhere!)
Verse 2:
Eis to varos pou 'cheis, tha se voithei,
(At your weight, it will help you,)
ki an stenochoriesai, se parigorei!
(and if you are upset, he comforts you!)
Xerei Aftos to varos, kathe thlipsi sou,
(He knows your weight, your every sorrow,)
min klais diavati, o Christos pantoy!
(don't cry wayfarer, Christ is everywhere!)
Verse 3:
An oi peirasmoi pote se apeiloyn,
(If temptations ever threaten you,)
gnorize pos echeis mega Iisoy!
(know that you have a great Jesus!)
Sy mono ergasou to thelima Aftoy,
(You alone do His will,)
kai mi deiliaseis, o Christos pantoy!
(and do not be afraid, Christ is everywhere!)
Verse 4:
Dose oli ti zoi sou sto Christo
(Give your whole life to Christ)
ki oi dokimasies ein' pros to kalo!
(and trials are for good!)
Afise to mellon eis ta cheria Tou,
(Leave the future in His hands,)
sti chara, sti lypi, o Christos pantoy!
(in joy, in sorrow, Christ everywhere!)
