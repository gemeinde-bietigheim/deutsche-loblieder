148 O KOSMOS DEN ME IKANOPOIEI

Greek Hymns - No. 148 | "Ο ΚΟΣΜΟΣ ΔΕΝ ΜΕ ΙΚΑΝΟΠΟΙΕΙ"
Verse 1:
O kosmos de me ikanopoiei,
(The world does not satisfy me,)
o Iisoys mou dinei ti zoi!
(Jesus gives me life!)
Iltha ki ego me pisti sto Christo
(I also came with faith in Christ)
kai katharisthika me aima sto stafro!
(and I was cleansed with blood on the cross!)
Chorus:
M' agapa o Christos kai pethane gia me.
(Christ loves me and died for me.)
Ton agapo ki ego, giati me esose!
(I love him too, because he saved me!)
Verse 2:
Zoysa palia san asotos yios,
(I used to live like a prodigal son,)
makria ap' tin poimni tou Theoy Patros!
(away from the flock of God the Father!)
Ma kapote irtha eis eafton
(But once I came to myself)
ki epestrepsa metaniomenos sto Christo!
(and I returned repentant to Christ!)
Verse 3:
Imouna doylos, tora eleytheros,
(I was a slave, now I'm free)
imoun tyflos, kai tora vlepo fos,
(I was blind, and now I see light)
imoun nekros, tora zo sto Christo,
(I was dead, now I live in Christ,)
eirini kai chara evrika sto Christo!
(I found peace and joy in Christ!)
