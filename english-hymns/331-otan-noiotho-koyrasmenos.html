331 OTAN NOIOThO KOYRASMENOS

Greek Hymns - No. 331 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΝΟΙΩΘΩ ΚΟΥΡΑΣΜΕΝΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Otan noiotho kourasmenos
(When I feel tired)
ki echo thlipsi mes stin psychi.
(and I have sorrow in my soul.)
Chorus:
Trecho sto Christo pou me karterei,
(I run to Christ who fastens me,)
einai ora gia prosefchi!
(it's time to pray!)
Trecho sto Christo pou me karterei,
(I run to Christ who fastens me,)
einai ora gia prosefchi!
(it's time to pray!)
Verse 2:
Otan koyrasi i ponos
(When fatigue or pain)
vasanizoun to kormi.
(they torture the body.)
Verse 3:
Otan noiotho na foliazei
(When I feel it nestling)
kapoios fovos mes stin psychi.
(some fear in the soul.)
Verse 4:
Otan vlepo ta oneira mou
(When I see my dreams)
san syntrimmia mes sti zoi.
(like debris in life.)
Verse 5:
Otan mesa stin kardia mou
(When inside my heart)
kapoia elpida fyllorroei.
(some hope flickers.)
