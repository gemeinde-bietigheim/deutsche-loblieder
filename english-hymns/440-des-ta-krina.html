440 DES TA KRINA

Greek Hymns - No. 440 | "ΔΕΣ ΤΑ ΚΡΙΝΑ"
Verse 1:
Des ta krina pera sto livadi
(Look at the lilies over in the meadow)
pos anthizoun kai moschovoloyn.
(how they bloom and blossom.)
Moiazei i zoi mas m' ena krino,
(Our life is like a lily,)
pou to proi anthei gia ligo
(which in the morning blooms for a while)
kai to vrady echei marathei,
(and the night is withered,)
poso syntomi einai i zoi!
(how short life is!)
Verse 2:
Des to kyma pera st' akrogiali
(Look at the wave on the shore)
pos afrizei 'kei stin ammoudia.
(how it foams here on the beach.)
Moiazei i zoi mas m' ena kyma,
(Our life is like a wave,)
pou kryvei mesa tou mystiria
(which hides mysteries within)
kai fourtoyna fernei sti stigmi,
(and tempest he brings in the moment,)
poso astati einai i zoi!
(how fickle is life!)
Verse 3:
Des ton Iisoy Christo pou krazei,
(See Jesus Christ crying,)
thelei na se kanei eftychi!
(he wants to make you happy!)
Na 'nai i zoi sou mia eflogia
(May your life be a blessing)
gemati chari ki eftychia!
(full of grace and happiness!)
Me ton Iisoy Christo mazi
(With Jesus Christ together)
poso omorfi einai i zoi!
(how beautiful life is!)
