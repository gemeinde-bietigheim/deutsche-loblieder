351 YPsOSE ME YPERANO

Greek Hymns - No. 351 | "ΥΨΩΣΕ ΜΕ ΥΠΕΡΑΝΩ"
Verse 1:
Ypsose me yperano ton nefon tou peirasmoy,
(Lift me above the clouds of temptation,)
ypsose me eis ta ypsi tis agapis tou Christoy!
(raise me to the heights of Christ's love!)
Ypsose me eis ti chari, eis tin pisti tou Theoy,΄
(Raise me to the grace, to the faith of God,')
ypsose me na vadizo stin odo tou Iisoy!
(lift me up to walk in the way of Jesus!)
Chorus:
Ypsose me yperano ton merimnon tis zois,
(Lift me above the cares of life,)
ypsose me eis to oros, o Christe, tis prosefchis!
(raise me to the mountain, O Christ, of prayer!)
Verse 2:
Ypsose me yperano ton echthron mou, o Christe,
(Lift me above my enemies, O Christ,)
eis to agio thelima Sou na peripato me Se!
(in Your holy will to walk with You!)
Ypsose me, otan elthei trikymia sti zoi,
(Lift me up when life's stormy,)
didaxe me n' atenizo eis Ese, to Lytroti!
(teach me to gaze upon You, the Redeemer!)
Verse 3:
Ypsose me, otan elthei o echthros o poniros,
([Translation unavailable])
ypsose me, otan filoi me afisoun entelos!
([Translation unavailable])
Ypsose me eis ti thlipsi, fere me eis ti chara,
([Translation unavailable])
ypsose me gia na fthaso asfalos sti Chanaan!
(lift me up that I may come safely to Canaan!)
Verse 4:
Ypsose me, otan eltheis, gia na Se ypodechtho
(Lift me up, when you come, that I may receive you)
kai me olous tous agious kai ego na arpachtho!
(and with all the saints I too shall be raptured!)
Ypsose me na se ido eis ti vasileia Sou,
(Raise me up to see you in your kingdom,)
gia na do to prosopo Sou kai tin parousia Sou!
(to see Your face and Your presence!)
