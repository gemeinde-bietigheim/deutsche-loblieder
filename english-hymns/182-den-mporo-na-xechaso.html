182 DEN MPORO NA XEChASO

Greek Hymns - No. 182 | "ΔΕΝ ΜΠΟΡΩ ΝΑ ΞΕΧΑΣΩ"
Verse 1:
Den mporo na xechaso pos irth' o Christos
(I cannot forget how Christ came)
kapoia mera m' agapi polli!
(someday with lots of love!)
Imoun tote st' alitheia tyflos, monachos
(I was really blind then, a monk)
kai mou charise nea zoi!
(and gave me new life!)
To diko Tou to aima me plenei,
(His blood washes me,)
katharizei tin enochi!
(clears the guilt!)
Sotiria Ekeinos prosferei elpida,
(Salvation He offers hope,)
chara tharros mes stin psychi!
(joy courage in the soul!)
Verse 2:
Sto ypsilo korfovoyni tou Golgotha
(On the high hill of Calvary)
o Sotiras ypefer' ekei!
(the Savior suffered there!)
Ki itan toso sklira 'keina ta karfia
(And those nails were so hard)
ki i odyni ki i thlipsi pikri!
(and pain and sorrow bitter!)
Plirose o Sotiras gia mena,
(The Savior paid for me,)
ponese pano sto Stafro!
(hurt on the Cross!)
Echyse to diko Tou to aima, o nai,
(He shed His blood, oh yes)
ki anoixe to lampro ourano!
(and opened the bright sky!)
Verse 3:
Kai an ta chronia pernoyn ki an oi meres kyloyn,
(And if the years pass and if the days go by,)
stin kardia mou tha zei o Christos!
(Christ will live in my heart!)
Ki an fourtoynes kai mpores polles me ktypoyn,
(And if many storms and rains beat me,)
ein' Aftos thafmastos, zontanos!
(is He wonderful, alive!)
Ti chara me gemiz' o Sotiras!
(What joy the Savior fills me with!)
Ein' Aftos filos mou pistos!
(Is He my faithful friend!)
Kai tha einai gia panta dikos mou, o! nai,
(And he'll be mine forever, oh! Yes,)
Kyrios, Lytrotis, Dynatos!
(Lord, Redeemer, Mighty!)
