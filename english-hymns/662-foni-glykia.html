662 FONI GLYKIA

Greek Hymns - No. 662 | "ΦΩΝΗ ΓΛΥΚΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Foni glykia apo ton ourano
(Sweet voice from heaven)
mas gemise fos ki elpida!
(fill us with light and hope!)
Limani Theio prao kai zesto,
(Port Divine gentle and warm,)
galini stin kataigida!
(calm in the storm!)
Chorus:
Stou Christoy to limani eimai asfalis,
(In Christ the harbor I am safe,)
me gemizei i agapi Tou ki eimai eftychis!
(I am filled with His love and I am happy!)
Tin agapi pou dinei, den ti dinei allos kaneis,
(The love he gives, no one else gives,)
oso eimai konta Tou, eimai asfalis!
(as long as I'm near Him, I'm safe!)
Verse 2:
Ta logia Tou agapi kai zoi,
(His words love and life,)
sta erga Tou i sofia!
(in His works wisdom!)
Oi lexeis Tou aggizoun tin psychi,
(His words touch the soul,)
i agapi Tou einai theia!
(His love is divine!)
