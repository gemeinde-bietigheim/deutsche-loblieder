172 ChRISTOS I AMPELOS

Greek Hymns - No. 172 | "ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ Η ΑΜΠΕΛΟΣ"
Verse 1:
Christos i ampelos, ego to klima!
(Christ the vine, I the vine!)
ego odoiporos, Aftos i odos!
(I am a traveler, This is the road!)
Aftos einai to elaion kai ego to aggeion Aftoy!
(He is the oil and I am His vessel!)
Aftos einai o plastis ki ego eim' o pilos!
(He is the molder and I am the clay!)
Ego louloydi, Aftos to aroma!
(I flower, He the perfume!)
Eim' i nymfi me to onoma Aftoy!
(I am the nymph with His name!)
Chorus:
Tha Ton do, tha Ton do, tha Ton do!
(I will see Him, I will see Him, I will see Him!)
Nai, tha do to Sotira pou agapo!
(Yes, I will see the Savior I love!)
M' aima Tou me eplyne,
(He washed me with His blood,)
M' elouse, katharise, Alliloyia!
(Wash me, cleanse me, Hallelujah!)
Tha Ton do, tha Ton do, tha Ton do!
(I will see Him, I will see Him, I will see Him!)
Nai, tha do to Christo mou pou agapo!
(Yes, I will see my beloved Christ!)
Verse 2:
O Iisoys mou einai o Sotiras,
(Jesus is my Savior,)
ego sosmenos kai paneftychis!
(me saved and very happy!)
Aftos epire thanato ki ego tin aionia zoi!
(He received death and I received eternal life!)
Aftos pige ston Adi ki ego th' anastitho!
(He went to Hades and I will be resurrected!)
Aftos Yios tou Theoy aionios
(He Son of God eternal)
kai ego me Afton ston Ourano!
(and I with Him in Heaven!)
