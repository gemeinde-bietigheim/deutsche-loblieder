586 SAS ANAGGELOYME TIN AGAPI

Greek Hymns - No. 586 | "ΣΑΣ ΑΝΑΓΓΕΛΟΥΜΕ ΤΗΝ ΑΓΑΠΗ"
Verse 1:
Sas anaggelloume tin agapi,
(We announce love to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin agapi,
(we announce love to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin agapi,
(we announce love to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin agapi, tin agapi,
(we announce to you love, love,)
tin agapi tou Christoy!
(the love of Christ!)
Verse 2:
Sas anaggelloume ti chara,
(We announce to you the joy,)
sas anaggelloume ti chara,
(we announce joy to you,)
sas anaggelloume ti chara,
(we announce joy to you,)
sas anaggelloume ti chara, ti chara,
(we announce to you joy, joy,)
ti chara tou Christoy!
(the joy of Christ!)
Verse 3:
Sas anaggelloume tin eirini,
(We announce peace to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin eirini,
(we announce peace to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin eirini,
(we announce peace to you,)
sas anaggelloume tin eirini, tin eirini,
(we announce to you peace, peace,)
tin eirini tou Christoy!
(the peace of Christ!)
Verse 4:
Sas anaggelloume tin agapi!
(We announce love to you!)
Sas anaggelloume ti chara!
(We announce the joy!)
Sas anaggelloume tin eirini!
(We announce peace to you!)
Sas anaggelloume tin agapi, ti chara,
(We announce to you love, joy,)
tin eirini tou Christoy!
(the peace of Christ!)
