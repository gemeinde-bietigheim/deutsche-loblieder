154 PANTA ThA MENEI STO NOY ZOIRI

Greek Hymns - No. 154 | "ΠΑΝΤΑ ΘΑ ΜΕΝΕΙ ΣΤΟ ΝΟΥ ΖΩΗΡΗ"
Verse 1:
Panta tha menei sto nou zoiri, omorfi mera glykia!
(Lively, beautiful day sweetie will always be remembered!)
Lampei axechasti kai thafmasti pantote mes stin kardia!
(She shines unforgettable and wonderful always in the heart!)
Tote pou vadiza dichos skopo pros tou chamoy to gkremo,
(When I was walking aimlessly towards the cliff of perdition,)
tote pou vrik' o Sotiras ki emena ton athlio ki amartolo.
(when the Savior found me, the wretched and sinful one.)
Chorus:
Axechasti mera xechoristi,
(Unforgettable special day,)
o ouranos irthe kato sti gi!
(heaven has come down to earth!)
Mou egin' o Christos Sotiras mou pistos!
(Christ my Savior became faithful to me!)
Ti eftychis! Doxa, eim' asfalis!
(How happy! Glory, I am safe!)
Verse 2:
Mera pou archisa tote na zo sto Lytroti mou Christo!
(Day when I then began to live in Christ my Redeemer!)
Mera pou archisa na perpato mes stis alitheias to fos!
(The day I began to walk in the truth and the light!)
Pos na xechaso san irthe o Christos sto skoteino mou keli
(How can I forget how Christ came to my dark cell)
ki eipe pos eimai eleytheros na vgo na ziso mia nea zoi!
(and said I am free to go out and live a new life!)
Verse 3:
Mera pou fernei glykia thalpori mes sti ftochi mou kardia
(Day that brings sweet warmth to my poor heart)
kai me to fos tis skorpizei edo kathe mou thlipsi varia!
(and with her light scatters here all my sorrow heavy!)
Mera pou yfainei sto mellon gia me kapoia elpida glykia,
(Day that weaves into the future for with some hope sweet,)
otan o Kyrios m' oli ti doxa tha 'rthei na me parei psila!
(when the Lord in all glory will come to take me up!)
