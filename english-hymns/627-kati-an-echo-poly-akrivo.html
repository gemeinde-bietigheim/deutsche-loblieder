627 KATI AN EChO POLY AKRIVO

Greek Hymns - No. 627 | "ΚΑΤΙ ΑΝ ΕΧΩ ΠΟΛΥ ΑΚΡΙΒΟ"
Verse 1:
Kati an echo poly akrivo,
(Something if I have too expensive,)
ein' ena doro ki afto theiko!
(it's a divine gift too!)
Imoun chamenos, amartolos,
(I was lost, a sinner,)
tora lytromenos kai pistos!
(now redeemed and faithful!)
Chorus:
Imoun chamenos, dystychis,
(I was lost, unhappy,)
tora sosmenos ki eftychis!
(now safe and happy!)
To mystiko mou, o, na to diko mou,
(My secret, oh my,)
yparchei gia mena ouranos!
(there is heaven for me!)
Verse 2:
O,ti ki an echo apo tous goneis,
(Whatever I have from parents,)
mesa ston kosmo to echei katheis.
(everyone in the world has it.)
Einai mia fysi amartoli,
(It is a sinful nature,)
skliri, agria, chamili.
(hard, wild, low.)
Verse 3:
T' alla pou echo einai ta ftharta.
(The others I have are the perishable ones.)
O, kapoia mera tha leipsoun ki' afta!
(Oh, one day those will be gone too!)
Ena tha menei pantotina,
(One will remain forever,)
ein' i sotiria i glykia!
(is the salvation the sweet!)
Verse 4:
Kati an echo p' axizei edo,
(If I have anything worth here,)
ein' ena doro apo to Theo!
(it's a gift from God!)
Athlios eimai, midaminos,
(Wretched am I, worthless,)
moni kaychisi mou o Christos!
(my only boast is Christ!)
