265 EYSEVEIS GYNAIKES

Greek Hymns - No. 265 | "ΕΥΣΕΒΕΙΣ ΓΥΝΑΙΚΕΣ"
Verse 1:
Efseveis gynaikes ilthan klaigontas
(Pious women came weeping)
stou Christoy to mnima, myra ferontas,
(to Christ's grave, bearing myrrh,)
dia ton thanonta Iisoy Christo,
(through the dead Jesus Christ,)
alla na! ton tafo vlepoun adeiano!
(but yes! they see the tomb empty!)
Chorus:
«O Christos anesti!» psalloun ouranoi,
("Christ is risen!" singing skies)
«Alithos anesti!» antichei i gi.
("Truly he is risen!" the earth echoes.)
Verse 2:
Aggelos katevei, ginontai seismoi,
(Angel descends, there are earthquakes,)
fylakes Romaioi peftoun san nekroi.
(Roman guards fall as dead.)
Tharros myrofores, mi foveisth' eseis,
(Courage honey bearers, do not be afraid,)
o Christos anesti, einai nikitis!
(Christ is risen, he is victorious!)
Verse 3:
Pros tous mathites Tou speydoun me chara,
(To His disciples they hasten with joy,)
na tous anaggeiloun nea ta kala,
(to announce good news to them,)
«O Christos anesti!», alla stin odo
("Christ is risen!", but on the street)
eno evadizan, eidan to Christo!
(while they were praying, they saw Christ!)
Verse 4:
«O Christos anesti!», nai, as kyrichthei,
("Christ is risen!", yes, let him be proclaimed,)
ton anastimenon einai aparchi!
(of the resurrected is the beginning!)
Asfalis i vasi tis elpidas mas,
(Safe is the basis of our hope,)
zei o Lytrotis mas, o Sotiras mas!
(our Redeemer lives, our Savior!)
