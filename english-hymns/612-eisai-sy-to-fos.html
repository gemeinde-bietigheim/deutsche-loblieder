612 EISAI SY TO FOS

Greek Hymns - No. 612 | "ΕΙΣΑΙ ΣΥ ΤΟ ΦΩΣ"
Verse 1:
Eisai Sy to fos, o Dimiourgos,
(You are the light, the Creator,)
eisai Sy, Christe mou, fylakas pistos!
(You, my Christ, are a faithful guardian!)
Eisai o kalos, storgikos Theos,
(You are the good, loving God,)
eisai o poimenas mou kai odigos!
(you are my shepherd and guide!)

Chorus:
Eisai i elpis tis sotirias mou,

Verse 2:
Mou 'doses kardia, mou 'doses ftera,
(You gave me a heart, you gave me wings,)
mou 'doses to Pneyma na peto psila!
(you gave me the Spirit to fly high!)
Na 'rchomai s' Esena, Agie Thee,
(To come to You, Holy God,)
ston paradeiso pou 'kanes gia me!
(to the heaven you made for me!)

Verse 3:
Agalliasi niotho stin psychi
(I feel joy in my soul)
pou mou dineis Sy, mega Lytroti!
(that you give me, great Redeemer!)
Sy mou etoimases spiti alithino,
(You prepared a real home for me,)
ston paradeiso aionia na zo!
(in heaven forever to live!)
