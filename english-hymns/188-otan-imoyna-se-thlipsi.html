188 OTAN IMOYNA SE ThLIPsI

Greek Hymns - No. 188 | "ΌΤΑΝ ΗΜΟΥΝΑ ΣΕ ΘΛΙΨΗ"
Verse 1:
Otan imouna se thlipsi,
(When I was in sorrow,)
se skotadia zofera,
(in gloomy darkness,)
ekraxa pros to Sotira
(I cried out to the Savior)
na me sosei ap' ta desma.
(to save me from the shackles.)
Chorus:
Ti chara! ti chara!
(What a joy! what a joy!)
M' esose o Lytrotis, otan ilth' epi tis gis!
(I was saved by the Redeemer, when I came to earth!)
O, ti doxa aionia ki eflogia tis psychis!
(Oh, what eternal glory and blessing of the soul!)
Doxa, doxa sto Sotira, tora eimai eftychis!
(Glory, glory to the Savior, now I am happy!)
Verse 2:
Nai, thymamai tin imera
(Yes, I remember the day)
pou m' ekales' o Christos,
(that Christ called me,)
m' efere ap' to skotadi
(brought me from the darkness)
eis to thafmasto Tou fos!
(in His wondrous light!)
Verse 3:
Nai, thymamai pos to varos
(Yes, I remember the weight)
afairethike efthys,
(removed straight away)
otan eis ton Iisoy mou
(when you see my Jesus)
etrexa gonyklinis!
(I ran on my knees!)
Verse 4:
Nai, thymamai tin imera,
(yes i remember the day)
o oiktirmon Lytrotis
(the gracious Redeemer)
egrapse to onoma mou
(he wrote my name)
sto vivlio tis zois!
(in the book of life!)
Verse 5:
Ti chara, Ti eftychia,
(What joy, what happiness,)
Eimai pia paidi Theoy,
(I am now a child of God,)
metochos tis vasileias
(sharer of the kingdom)
tou aioniou thisafroy!
(of eternal treasure!)
