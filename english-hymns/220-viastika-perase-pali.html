220 VIASTIKA PERASE PALI

Greek Hymns - No. 220 | "ΒΙΑΣΤΙΚΑ ΠΕΡΑΣΕ ΠΑΛΙ"
Verse 1:
Viastika perase pali ki allos chronos, adelfoi,
(Hastily passed again another time, brothers,)
kai mas ilthe o kainoyrgios kai meis chairoume. Giati;
(and the new one has arrived and we are happy. Why?)
Giati ilthame sto telos me chara kai ygieis,
(Because we came to the end happy and healthy,)
poios omos ston perasmeno ezise os efsevis;
(but who in the past lived as pious?)
Verse 2:
An kai perase teleios, den xechastike aftos,
(Although he is completely gone, he is not forgotten,)
alla st' ouranoy egrafi ta vivlia epimelos.
(but in heaven the books were diligently written.)
Pali omos kapoia mera sy tha ton enthymitheis,
(But again one day you will remember him,)
tin imera ti megali, otan meleis na kritheis.
(on the great day, when you are about to be judged.)
Verse 3:
O Kritis tote tha ferei osa epraxes sto fos
(The Judge will then bring what you have done to light)
kai to chrono kai ta meri tha sta deixei akrivos.
(and the time and places will show you exactly.)
Ola tha st' apokalypsei, san diavasei ta grapta,
(He will reveal everything to you, as if he reads the writings,)
ta vivlia de tha krypsei, erga sou t' amartola.
(books will not hide, your works the sinners.)
Verse 4:
Etsi, eis to neo chrono pano se charti lefko
(So, in the new year on white paper)
as eggrafoume kai mono o,ti to christianiko!
(let's register only what is Christian!)
Me metanoia kai pisti kai agapi alithini
(With repentance and faith and true love)
kai elpida eis ton Plasti na diagoume agnoi!
(and hope in Plastis that we may speak pure!)
Verse 5:
As archisoume to chrono toyto choris distagmoys,
(Let us begin this year without hesitation,)
me aisthimata kainoyrgia kai me agious skopoys!
(with new feelings and with holy purposes!)
Me kardia teleios nea na latreyoume Afton
(With a completely new heart to worship Him)
kai na dinoume ti doxa eis ton Pansofo Theo!
(and give glory to the All-Wise God!)
