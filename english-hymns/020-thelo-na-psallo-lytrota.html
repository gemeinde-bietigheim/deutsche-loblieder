20 ThELO NA PsALLO, LYTROTA

Greek Hymns - No. 20 | "ΘΕΛΩ ΝΑ ΨΑΛΛΩ, ΛΥΤΡΩΤΑ"
Verse 1:
Thelo na psallo, Lytrota,
(I want to sing, Redeemed,)
gia tin agapi ti vathia
(for deep love)
pou edeixes Esy gia me
(which You showed for me)
ki' ilthes sti gi na stafrotheis!
(and you came to earth to be crucified!)
Gia mena ton amartolo
(For me the sinner)
anevikes eis to stafro,
(you went up to the cross)
pires stous omous Sou Esy
(You took on Your shoulders)
tin idiki mou enochi!
(my own fault!)
Chorus:
Thelo na psallo, Lytrota,
(I want to sing, Redeemed,)
gia tin agapi ti vathia,
(for deep love)
pou edeixes Esy gia me,
(that you showed for me)
Amome tou Theoy Amne!
(For God's sake Amne!)
O! Den mporo na kratitho,
(Oh! I can't hold on)
amesos trecho sto Stafro
(immediately I run to the Cross)
me dakrya, gia na sou po
(with tears, to tell you)
“Sotira mou, S' efcharisto”.
("My Savior, I thank You".)
Verse 2:
Poso m' agapises, Christe,
(How you loved me, Christ,)
kai pethanes Esy gia me!
(and You died for me!)
Esy, to akako Arni,
(You, the poor Lamb,)
pires ti thesi tou listi!
(you took the bandit's place!)
Gia na sotho ap' tin orgi,
(To save myself from wrath,)
katara egines Esy!
(you became a curse!)
Epano sto skliro Stafro
(On the hard Cross)
pethaineis san amartolos!
(you die like a sinner!)
