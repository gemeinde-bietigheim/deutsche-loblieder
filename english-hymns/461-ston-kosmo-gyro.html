461 STON KOSMO GYRO

Greek Hymns - No. 461 | "ΣΤΟΝ ΚΟΣΜΟ ΓΥΡΩ"
Verse 1:
Ston kosmo gyro ti zitas na vreis
(In the world around you, what are you looking to find?)
kai psachneis tosa chronia dystychis;
(and looking for so many years of misery?)
Den enoioses akoma pos makria
(You didn't feel that far yet)
ap' to Christo mas de tha vreis chara;
(will you not find joy in our Christ?)
Chorus:
Ela, se perimenei o Iisoys Christos!
(Come, Jesus Christ is waiting for you!)
Eis to stafro Aftos
(In the cross He)
anevike gia se, ela, amartole!
(rose for thee, come, sinner!)
Verse 2:
Oi doxes kai ta ployti ki oi chares
(The glories and the riches and the joys)
ki oi anomes tou kosmou idones
(and the lawless pleasures of the world)
me thlipsi sou gemizoun tin kardia
(they fill your heart with sadness)
kai i zoi sou achari kyla!
(and your life goes without joy!)
Verse 3:
Eis to stafro epano o Christos,
(Christ on the cross,)
gia na se sosei anevike Aftos!
(He rose to save you!)
Stagones aima echyse gia se,
(Drops of blood shed for you,)
na se lytrosei, o amartole!
(redeem thee, O sinner!)
Verse 4:
O! Mi distazeis, ela eis Afton,
(Oh! Do not hesitate, come to Him,)
min anavaleis, ela sto Christo!
(do not delay, come to Christ!)
Eirini, fos, agapi kai chara
(Peace, light, love and joy)
dinei o Christos se kathe mia kardia!
(Christ gives to every heart!)
