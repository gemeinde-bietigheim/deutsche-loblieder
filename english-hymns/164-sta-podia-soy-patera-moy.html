164 STA PODIA SOY, PATERA MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 164 | "ΣΤΑ ΠΟΔΙΑ ΣΟΥ, ΠΑΤΕΡΑ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Sta podia Sou, Patera mou, perilypos gyrizo
(At Your feet, my Father, I turn around)
kai tremo kai dakryzo, sygchoresi zito...
(and I tremble and cry, I ask for forgiveness...)
sygchoresi zito!
(I apologize!)
Verse 2:
S' adikisa, Se lypisa, eskorpisa to vios Sou.
(I wronged You, I pitied You, I scattered Your life.)
O, na kaloymai gios Sou, den prepei, den tolmo...
(Oh, to be called Your son, I must not, I dare not...)
den prepei, den tolmo!
(I must not, I dare not!)
Verse 3:
Eis tous agroys katantisa tous choirous na fylao
(I left the pigs in the fields to herd)
ki ap' ti voski na fao mou 'rchotan pethymia...
(and from the pasture to eat I had a desire...)
mou 'rchotan pethymia!
(I wanted to!)
Verse 4:
Sta podia Sou, Patera mou, gyrizo, endyse me,
(At Your feet, my Father, I turn, clothe me,)
pos eimai koitaxe me, xypolytos, gymnos...
(how am I look at me, barefoot, naked...)
xypolitos, gymnos!
(barefoot, naked!)
Verse 5:
O! dexou me, Patera mou, san doylo stin afli Sou,
(Oh! receive me, my Father, as a servant in Your court,)
ta pathi mou lypisou, an eisai efsplachnikos,
(pity my passions, if you are merciful,)
an eisai efsplachnikos!
(if you are merciful!)
Verse 6:
«Siko, paidi mou, olos chara, se dechet' i kardia mou
("Get up, my child, all joy, my heart receives you)
kai mes stin agkalia mou, ein' ora na chareis,
(and in my arms, it's time to be happy,)
ein' ora na chareis!»
(it's time to be happy!”)
Verse 7:
«Ki as sfaxoun to kalytero galaktero moschari,
("Even if they slaughter the best milch calf,)
gia ti megali chari pou mou 'dose o Theos,
(for the great grace that God gave me,)
pou mou 'dose o Theos!».
(that God gave me!".)
