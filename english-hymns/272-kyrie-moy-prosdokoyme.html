272 KYRIE MOY PROSDOKOYME

Greek Hymns - No. 272 | "ΚΥΡΙΕ ΜΟΥ ΠΡΟΣΔΟΚΟΥΜΕ"
Verse 1:
Kyrie mou, prosdokoyme oloi emeis stin prosefchi
(Lord, we all hope in prayer)
kai to Pneyma Sou zitoyme! Dose mas Pentikosti!
(and we ask for Your Spirit! Give us Pentecost!)
Chorus:
Dos' to Pneyma! Dos' to Pneyma!
(Give me the Spirit! Give me the Spirit!)
Plirose kathe psychi,
(Pay every soul,)
kai mes stis kardies mas dose
(and into our hearts give)
t' ouranoy anapsychi!
(heavenly recreation!)
Verse 2:
Kathe ti agiase to, na mi zoum' emeis, Christe,
(Sanctify everything, let us not, Christ,)
to «ego» mas nekrose to na doxazoume Ese!
(kill our "I" so that we may glorify You!)
Verse 3:
Sy katharise kardies mas me to aima Sou, Amne,
(Cleanse our hearts with Your blood, Amne,)
ki entelos agiase mas, tou Theoy Paraklite!
(and completely sanctify us, God's Prayer!)
Verse 4:
Ti sfragida Sou zitoyme kai ti dynami mazi,
(We ask for Your seal and strength together,)
eimaste laos dikos Sou, ouranos edo sti gi!
(we are Your people, heaven here on earth!)
Verse 5:
Ta charismata Sou dose eis emas, Paraklite!
(Give us Your gifts, Pray!)
Ekteine tin krataia Sou Sy, Patera Agie!
(Extend Your might, Holy Father!)
Chorus:
Kyrie, S' efcharistoyme
(Lord, we thank You)
me kardia eilikrini
(with an honest heart)
ki ola afta Sou ta zitoyme
(and all this we ask of You)
chari tou Christoy. Amin!
(grace of Christ. Amen!)
