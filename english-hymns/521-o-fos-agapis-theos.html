521 O FOS! AGAPIS ThEOS

Greek Hymns - No. 521 | "Ω ΦΩΣ! ΑΓΑΠΗΣ ΘΕΟΣ"
Chorus:
O fos! Agapis Theos!
(O light! Dear God!)
O fos! Agapis Pateras!
(O light! Dear Father!)
O fos! Eirinis Yios, Iisoys, Iisoys!
(O light! Son of Peace, Jesus, Jesus!)
Verse 1:
San aetos ischyros, pou 'chei ftera stin kardia tou,
(Like an eagle mighty, that hath wings in his heart,)
egina ego o ftochos kai pao na ftaso konta Tou!
(I became the poor and I go to reach Him!)
S' ena taxidi zois, ena stafro n' antallaxo
(In a journey of life, a cross to exchange)
sto synnefo to lampro, Vasilia!
(in the cloud the bright one, King!)
Verse 2:
An kapoia mera skliri tha katevaso ta matia,
(If some day is hard I'll look down,)
an i kardia prodothei apo tis gis ta palatia,
(if the heart is betrayed by the palaces of the earth,)
thelo na 'rtheis viastika kai m' ena logo glyko Sou
(I want you to come quickly and with a word, sweet of you)
na m' anastiseis xana, Vasilia!
(raise me up again, King!)
