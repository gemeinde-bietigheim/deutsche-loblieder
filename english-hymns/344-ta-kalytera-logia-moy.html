344 TA KALYTERA LOGIA MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 344 | "ΤΑ ΚΑΛΥΤΕΡΑ ΛΟΓΙΑ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Ta kalytera logia mou den ta echo grammena,
(I haven't written my best words,)
den yparchoune symvola, den yparchoun fones.
(there are no symbols, no voices.)
Me mia glossa agnoristi i kardia mou s' Esena
(With a language unrecognizable my heart to You)
psithyrizei, o Kyrie, myries, myries fores!
(he whispers, O Lord, a thousand, a thousand times!)
Me mia glossa agnoristi i kardia mou s' Esena
(With a language unrecognizable my heart to You)
psithyrizei, o Kyrie, myries, myries fores!
(he whispers, O Lord, a thousand, a thousand times!)
Verse 2:
Einai ores pou stekomai kai koitazo t' asteria,
(There are hours I stand and look at the stars,)
ti galinia ti thalassa, to vathy ourano.
(the calm sea, the deep sky.)
Kai aplono s' Esena, nai, ta ftocha mou ta cheria,
(And I stretch out to Thee, yes, my poor hands,)
me ta matia dakryvrechta, me to stoma kleisto!
(with tearful eyes, with a closed mouth!)
Kai aplono s' Esena, nai, ta ftocha mou ta cheria,
(And I stretch out to Thee, yes, my poor hands,)
me ta matia dakryvrechta, me to stoma kleisto!
(with tearful eyes, with a closed mouth!)
Verse 3:
Ki einai ores pou vlepontas to diko Sou to cheri
(And there are hours when seeing Your own hand)
na skoupizei ta dakrya, n' akoumpa stin pligi,
(to wipe the tears, to touch the wound,)
noiotho mesa mou, gyro mou, tis pnois Sou t' ageri
(I feel in me, around me, the air of Your breath)
kai ta logia t' aneipota ta akoys sti siopi!
(and you hear the unspoken words in the silence!)
Noiotho mesa mou, gyro mou, tis pnois Sou t' ageri
(I feel in me, around me, the air of Your breath)
kai ta logia t' aneipota ta akoys sti siopi!
(and you hear the unspoken words in the silence!)
