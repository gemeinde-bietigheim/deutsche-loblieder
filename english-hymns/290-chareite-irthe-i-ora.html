290 ChAREITE IRThE I ORA

Greek Hymns - No. 290 | "ΧΑΡΕΙΤΕ ΗΡΘΕ Η ΩΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Chareite, irthe i ora pou tha pame
(Rejoice, it is time for us to go)
ston oiko tou Kyriou, opou Aftos
(in the house of the Lord, where He)
tha vrisketai sti thyra, san pername,
(will be at the door, as we pass,)
ekei mas perimenei o Christos!
(Christ is waiting for us there!)
Ekei mas perimenei o Christos!
(Christ is waiting for us there!)
Chorus:
Ton oiko tou Kyriou as ton stolisoume
(Let us decorate the Lord's house)
me agies parousies, deiseis prosefches!
(with holy presences, prayers!)
Me ta charismata Tou as ton kosmisoume,
(With His gifts let us adorn him,)
me agio theio Logo, pnefmatikes odes!
(with holy divine Word, spiritual odes!)
Verse 2:
Parte mazi sas merimnes kai vari,
(Take with you cares and burdens,)
ta pire ola Aftos, o Dynatos!
(He, the Mighty One, took it all!)
Stous omous Tou o Kyrios tha ta parei,
(On His shoulders the Lord will take them,)
o Lytrotis mas, o Iisoys Christos!
(our Redeemer, Jesus Christ!)
O Lytrotis mas, o Iisoys Christos!
(Our Redeemer, Jesus Christ!)
Verse 3:
Doxaste to Mesiti kai Sotira,
(Praise the Mediator and Savior,)
ti sotiria dinei mono Aftos!
(only He gives salvation!)
Kalos poimenas, ton provaton thyra,
(Good shepherd, door of the sheep,)
to onoma Tou Iisoys Christos!
(the name of Jesus Christ!)
To onoma Tou Iisoys Christos!
(The name of Jesus Christ!)
