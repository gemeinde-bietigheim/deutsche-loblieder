10 SOTIRAS, ONOMA SEPTO

Greek Hymns - No. 10 | "ΣΩΤΗΡΑΣ, ΟΝΟΜΑ ΣΕΠΤΟ"
Verse 1:
Sotiras, onoma septo, aggelon o choros,
(Saviour, sept name, angel's dance,)
as proskynisoume Afton, Despoti tou pantos!
(let us worship Him, Lord of all!)
As proskynisoume Afton, Despoti tou pantos!
(Let us worship Him, Lord of all!)
Verse 2:
Eimaste martyres Theoy, mas esos' o Christos,
(We are God's witnesses, Christ saved us,)
doxologoyme oloi Afton, Despoti tou pantos!
(we all praise Him, Lord of all!)
Doxologoyme oloi Afton, Despoti tou pantos!
(We all praise Him, Lord of all!)
Verse 3:
Kai seis, i nymfi tou Christoy, o eklektos laos,
(And you, the bride of Christ, the chosen people,)
prosmenete ton Iisoy, Despoti tou pantos!
(wait for Jesus, Lord of all!)
Prosmenete ton Iisoy, Despoti tou pantos!
(Wait for Jesus, Lord of all!)
Verse 4:
Seis ap' ta ethni amartoloi, sas sozei o Christos,
(You sinners of the nations, Christ saves you,)
anakiryxate Afton Despoti tou pantos!
(you proclaimed Him Lord of all!)
Anakiryxate Afton Despoti tou pantos!
(You proclaimed Him Lord of all!)
Verse 5:
Kai oles oi fyles tis gis, kath' ethnos kai laos,
(And all the tribes of the earth, every nation and people,)
stepste Afton meta timis Despoti tou pantos!
(crown Him with honor Lord of all!)
Stepste Afton meta timis Despoti tou pantos!
(Crown Him with honor Lord of all!)
