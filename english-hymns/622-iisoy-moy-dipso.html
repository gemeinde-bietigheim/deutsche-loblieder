622 IISOY MOY, DIPsO

Greek Hymns - No. 622 | "ΙΗΣΟΥ ΜΟΥ, ΔΙΨΩ"
Verse 1:
Iisoy mou, dipso, dose mou nero!
(Jesus, I'm thirsty, give me water!)
Iisoy mou, dipso, dose mou na pio
(Jesus, I'm thirsty, give me drink)
apo tin asteirefti pigi Sou,
(from Your inexhaustible source,)
dose mou na pio, na xedipsaso,
(give me drink, quench my thirst)
dose mou nero!
(give me water)
Mes sta cheria Sou na xapostaso,
(In Your hands I rest,)
dose mou na pio, na xedipsaso,
(give me drink, quench my thirst)
dose mou nero, Iisoy mou, dipso!
(give me water, my Jesus, I'm thirsty!)
Verse 2:
Iisoy mou, peino, dose mou trofi!
(Jesus, I am hungry, give me food!)
Iisoy mou, peino, dose mou psomi
(Jesus, I am hungry, give me bread)
ap' to logo Sou, gia na chortaso,
(from Your word, that I may be satisfied,)
kai sta podia Sou na xapostaso,
(and at Your feet to rest,)
dose mou trofi!
(give me food!)
Dose m' apo t' Agio Sou Soma,
(Give me of Your Holy Body,)
meine edo, Se chreiazomai akoma,
(stay here i still need you)
nai, mporeis Esy, Iisoy mou, Esy!
(yes, You can, my Jesus, You!)
Verse 3:
Iisoy mou, zito Pneyma Agio!
(My Jesus, I ask Holy Spirit!)
Iisoy mou zito to Pneyma t' Agio,
(Jesus I ask for the Holy Spirit,)
na mporeso na S' akolouthiso,
(to be able to follow You,)
ta chnaria Sou na min afiso,
(I will not leave Your footsteps,)
Iisoy, zito
(Jesus, I ask)
tin aionia zoi na ziso,
(eternal life to live,)
kai mazi Sou gia na katoikiso!
(and with You to dwell!)
Iisoy, zito, Iisoy, zito, pantote mazi!
(Jesus, I ask, Jesus, I ask, always together!)
