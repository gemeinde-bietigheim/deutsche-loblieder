356 O PATER IMON

Greek Hymns - No. 356 | "Ω ΠΑΤΕΡ ΗΜΩΝ"
Verse 1:
O Pater imon, o en tois ouranois,
(O our Father who art in heaven,)
deometha Sou pantachoy epi gis.
(we see You everywhere on earth.)
To mega Sou onoma n' agiasthei
(Hallowed be Your great name)
ypo ton anthropon kai na doxasthei!
(under men and be glorified!)
Verse 2:
I Si vasileia na krataiothei,
(May His kingdom be established,)
pantos i kardia na ypotachthei!
(let the heart be subdued!)
To thelima tou Vasileos imon
(The will of our King)
na gin' epi gis os eis ton ouranon!
(may it be on earth as it is in heaven!)
Verse 3:
Sy dos epioysion arton imin,
(This is our daily bread,)
sygchorison pasan mas praxin kakin,
(forgive all our bad deeds,)
kathos sygchoroymen eis panta echthron,
(as they forgive every enemy,)
as sygchorithei kathe ptaisma imon!
(let all our sins be forgiven!)
Verse 4:
Mi fereis imas pote eis peirasmon,
(Never lead us into temptation,)
plin rysai imas apo ton poniron!
(save us from the wicked!)
Ischys vasileia kai doxa kai nyn
(Power reign and glory and now)
kai eis tous aionas Soy einai, Amin!
(and in Your age it is, Amen!)
