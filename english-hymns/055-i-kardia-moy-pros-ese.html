55 I KARDIA MOY PROS ESE

Greek Hymns - No. 55 | "Η ΚΑΡΔΙΑ ΜΟΥ ΠΡΟΣ ΕΣΕ"
Verse 1:
I kardia mou pros Ese, o, ypsonetai, Christe!
(My heart to Thee, oh, rises, Christ!)
Iisoy Christe, Se doxazo, Kyrie!
(Jesus Christ, I praise You, Lord!)
Se latreyo, vasilia, se ypsono, Iisoy!
(I adore you, king, I exalt you, Jesus!)
Plirose me me to Pneyma Sou, Thee!
(Reward me with Your Spirit, God!)
Chorus:
S' agapo, S' agapo, S' agapo, Iisoy!
(I love you, I love you, I love you, Jesus!)
Iisoy, Iisoy, Iisoy, S' agapo!
(Jesus, Jesus, Jesus, I love You!)
Verse 2:
Agio Pneyma tou Theoy, o Paraklite piste,
(Holy Spirit of God, O Pray faithful,)
na ekcheeis tin agapi Sou se me!
(to pour out Your love on me!)
Agio Pneyma tou Theoy, o Paraklite septe,
(Holy Spirit of God, O Pray Sept,)
ela tora en dynamei, Thafmaste!
(come now in might, Admirer!)
