405 O DROMOS SOY ERIMOS

Greek Hymns - No. 405 | "Ο ΔΡΟΜΟΣ ΣΟΥ ΕΡΗΜΟΣ"
Verse 1:
O dromos sou erimos kai skoteinos,
(Your way is desolate and dark,)
mi stekesai, vadize empros!
(don't stand still, march forward!)
Stous ymnous edo de zei o echthros,
(In the hymns, the enemy does not live here,)
all' eftychis ein' o pistos!
(but happy is the believer!)
Chorus:
Me ymno pros ton ourano!
(With a hymn to the sky!)
Me ymno pros ton ourano!
(With a hymn to the sky!)
Kai mes stin kardia tou Christoy i chara,
(And in the heart of Christ the joy,)
nai, vadize, vadize empros!
(yes, march, march forward!)
Verse 2:
Kai an chreiastei na dokimastheis,
(And if you need to be tested,)
mi stekesai, vadize empros!
(don't stand still, march forward!)
o Paylos ki o Silas mes sti fylaki
(Pavlos and Silas in prison)
ymnoysan ekei to Theo!
(they praised God there!)
Verse 3:
Se lakko leonton o Daniil
(Daniel in a den of lions)
me pisti ymnoyse Afton!
(with faith he praised Him!)
Thymisou loipon tin pisti afti
(So remember this faith)
kai vadize, vadize empros!
(and marched, marched forward!)
Verse 4:
O! nai, tha doxazo to Lytroti!
(Oh! yes, I will praise the Redeemer!)
Afton tha ymno pantachoy!
(I will praise him everywhere!)
Me lytrose kai m' odigei, nai, Aftos
(He redeemed me and leads me, yes, He)
kai vadizo panta empros!
(and I'm always moving forward!)
