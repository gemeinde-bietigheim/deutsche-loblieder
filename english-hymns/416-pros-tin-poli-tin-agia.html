416 PROS TIN POLI TIN AGIA

Greek Hymns - No. 416 | "ΠΡΟΣ ΤΗΝ ΠΟΛΗ ΤΗΝ ΑΓΙΑ"
Verse 1:
Pros tin poli tin agia vadizeis, xefyges ap' ti diafthora,
(Towards the holy city you march, you escaped from corruption,)
to Christo stin kardia sou katecheis, s' echei sosei apo ta desma!
(you possess Christ in your heart, he has saved you from the shackles!)
Merikoi pou itan panta mazi sou s' egkateleipsan pia monacho.
(Some who have always been with you have now abandoned you as a monk.)
Ech' elpida, piste, sto Christo pantote!
(Have hope, believer, in Christ always!)
Zei o Theos imon!
(Our God lives!)
Chorus:
Zei o Theos imon kai sto throno Tou kathetai!
(Our God lives and sits on His throne!)
An thlipseis epelthoun kai vari piezoun,
(If sorrows come and burdens press,)
de tha mas afisei pote!
(he will never leave us!)
Zei o Theos imon kai mas voithei pantote!
(Our God lives and always helps us!)
Einai dynatos na fylaxei pistos.
(He is able to guard faithfully.)
Zei o Theos imon!
(Our God lives!)
Verse 2:
Apokameis psychi ston agona, dyskoleyesai na prochoreis;
(Earning soul in the fight, finding it difficult to advance?)
Mi fovasai, giati echeis Sotira eis ti machi afti tis zois!
(Do not be afraid, because you have a Savior in this battle of life!)
Prosefchomenos kline to gony, mi distazeis oyte mia stigmi,
(Bend the knee in prayer, do not hesitate for a moment,)
dynata th' akousthei i diki sou efchi!
(your wish will be heard loudly!)
Zei o Theos imon!
(Our God lives!)
Verse 3:
Isos zeis se skini i kalyva, agnostos ston aiona afto,
(Perhaps you live in a tent or a hut, unknown in this age,)
katoikia gia se etoimazei o Christos mas eis ton ourano!
(our Christ is preparing a home for you in heaven!)
Ti lampri kai oraia i poli! Den sygkrinetai m' aftin edo.
(What a bright and beautiful city! It doesn't compare to her here.)
O Christos ypeschethi na ziseis ekei!
(Christ promised to live there!)
Zei o Theos imon!
(Our God lives!)
