165 STI ZOI PERIPATOYSA DYSTYChIS

Greek Hymns - No. 165 | "ΣΤΗ ΖΩΗ ΠΕΡΙΠΑΤΟΥΣΑ ΔΥΣΤΥΧΗΣ"
Verse 1:
Sti zoi peripatoysa dystychis,
(In life I walked unhappy,)
mesa sti thlipsi, ston pono choris Christo!
(in sorrow, in pain without Christ!)
Koitoysa na vro eirini, fos, chara,
(I looked to find peace, light, joy,)
zitoysa agapi na nioso mes stin kardia!
(I was asking for love to feel in my heart!)
Chorus:
Ela, Christe, plyne mou tin kardia Esy,
(Come, Christ, wash my heart You,)
dose mou agapi stin psychi na Se ymno!
(give me love in the soul to praise You!)
Pare, Christe, oli mou ti zoi Esy,
(Take, Christ, all my life You,)
katharise ti sto stafro, S' efcharisto!
(cleanse her on the cross, thank you!)
Verse 2:
Tora zo me ton Iisoy mou ton kalo,
(Now I live with my good Jesus,)
m' agapi anoigo ti Vivlo kai meleto!
(I open the Bible with love and study!)
File mou, dokimase ton Iisoy,
(My friend, try Jesus,)
eplirose kai gia sena sto Golgotha!
(pay for you too at Calvary!)
