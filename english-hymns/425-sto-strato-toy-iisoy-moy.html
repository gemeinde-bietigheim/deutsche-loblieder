425 STO STRATO TOY IISOY MOY

Greek Hymns - No. 425 | "ΣΤΟ ΣΤΡΑΤΟ ΤΟΥ ΙΗΣΟΥ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1:
Sto strato tou Iisoy mou polemo,
(In the army of my Jesus I fight,)
thlipseis, fovous kai kindynous apsifo!
(sorrows, fears and dangers I defy!)
De fovoymai ton echthro, o Sotiras mou Christos
(I am not afraid of the enemy, my Savior Christ)
m' entharrynei, gia na prochoro empros!
(it encourages me to move forward!)
Chorus:
Sto strato tou Iisoy diarkos tha polemo,
(In the army of Jesus I will always fight,)
eos otou ap' ti gi afti klitho!
(until from this earth I am called!)
Me ti dynami Aftoy vgaino panta nikitis,
(By His power I always come out victorious,)
katatachthika sto Logo tis Zois!
(I ranked in the Word of Life!)
Verse 2:
Kathe mera tis psychis mou o echthros
(Every day of my soul the enemy)
richnei veli enantion diarkos,
(shoot arrows at constantly,)
plin i pisti dynati svinei toyta parefthys
(except strong faith is extinguished immediately)
kai m' afti tha eimai panta nikitis!
(and with her I will always be a winner!)
Verse 3:
Tora o kosmos epapse na m' agapa
(Now people stopped loving me)
ki ap' tous filous mou kaneis de me tima,
(and none of my friends honor me,)
alla o anastitheis doxasmenos Iisoys
(but the resurrected glorified Jesus)
m' agapa kai tha me odigei pantoy!
(he loves me and will take me everywhere!)
Verse 4:
De tha papso diarkos na polemo,
(I will never stop fighting,)
eosoy ston ourano anapaftho!
(until I rest in heaven!)
Tote meta ton loipon teknon tou Theoy piston
(Then after the other faithful children of God)
tha doxazo kai tha psallo ston Amno!
(I will praise and sing to the Lamb!)
