487 ThELO NA SE YMNO

Greek Hymns - No. 487 | "ΘΕΛΩ ΝΑ ΣΕ ΥΜΝΩ"
Verse 1:
Thelo na Se ymno, thelo na Se latreyo!
(I want to praise You, I want to worship You!)
To onoma Sou na aino, to Agio, to doxasmeno!
(Thy name I praise, the Holy, the glorified!)
Gia tin agapi na milo
(To talk about love)
pou karfose ta dyo Sou cheria,
(who nailed Your two hands,)
ta dyo Sou podia sto stafro,
(Your two feet on the cross,)
gia na peto ego st' asteria!
(so that I can fly to the stars!)
Chorus:
Iisoy, ela, Iisoy! Endoxe, Lampre Thee mou!
(Jesus, come, Jesus! Glory, Lampre God!)
Lytroti mou, Kyrie mou!
(My Redeemer, my Lord!)
Iisoy, ela, Iisoy! Se kalei to Pneyma na 'rtheis
(Jesus, come, Jesus! The Spirit calls you to come)
kai i nymfi pali na 'rtheis, Iisoy!
(and the nymph come again, Jesus!)
Verse 2:
O ponos Sou gia mas chara egine tora kai ymnoyme
(Your pain for us has become joy now and we praise)
kai psalloume to «Osanna» s' Esena, nai, pou agapoyme!
(and we sing "Hosanna" to You, yes, whom we love!)
Se perimenoume na 'rtheis
(We are waiting for you to come)
kai me lachtara karteroyme,
(and we yearn with longing,)
to prosopo Sou to glyko
(Your face is sweet)
sto mesouranima na doyme!
(see you at midnight!)
