262 ANASTASEOS IMERA

Greek Hymns - No. 262 | "ΑΝΑΣΤΑΣΕΩΣ ΗΜΕΡΑ"
Verse 1:
Anastaseos imera, i megali kai trani,
(Resurrection day, the great and strange,)
anatellei lamprotera, as charoyme eis aftin!
(she rises brighter, let us rejoice in her!)
Chaire, Pascha iero, agio Pascha kai septo!
(Hail, holy Easter, holy Easter and sept!)
Chaire, Pascha, o Christos, o ensarkotheis Theos!
(Hail, Easter, Christ, God incarnate!)
Verse 2:
Pyles Adi elyses, tous desmious lytroses
(You opened the gates of Hades, you redeemed the bonds)
kai to thanato patisas Sy ti niki edoses!
(and death you trampled You gave the victory!)
Chaire, zoifore, Sy ton piston Sou i zoi!
(Rejoice, life-giving One, the life of Your faithful!)
Thriamveyeis krataios, o ensarkotheis Theos!
(You triumph mighty, God incarnate!)
Verse 3:
Foro tapeinis kardias Sou prosferoume emeis,
(A humble heart's tribute we offer Thee,)
tin pnefmatiki latreia san thysia zontani!
(spiritual worship as a living sacrifice!)
Ton piston Sou tis kardies lytromenes kai agnes
(The hearts of Your faithful redeemed and pure)
dexou Sy, tou kosmou fos, o ensarkotheis Theos!
(accept You, light of the world, God incarnate!)
