71 TI ONOMA GLYKO

Greek Hymns - No. 71 | "ΤΙ ΟΝΟΜΑ ΓΛΥΚΟ"
Verse 1:
Ti onoma glyko, ti onoma glyko,
(What a sweet name, what a sweet name,)
ti onoma glyko Iisoys Christos!
(what a sweet name jesus christ!)
Ein' o sotiras mou, einai o filos mou,
(He is my savior, he is my friend,)
ein' o Iisoys Christos o Thafmastos!
(it is Jesus Christ the Wonderful!)
Aftos me frontizei, Aftos me stirizei,
(He takes care of me, He sustains me,)
prostatis mou pistos!
(my faithful protector!)
Mou dinei tharros kai kathe varos
(It gives me courage and every burden)
krata o dynatos, Iisoys Christos!
(the mighty, Jesus Christ holds!)
Verse 2:
Ti onoma glyko, ti onoma glyko
(What a sweet name, what a sweet name)
ti onoma glyko Iisoys Christos!
(what a sweet name jesus christ!)
Protos m' agapise ki irthe m' agkaliase
(He loved me first and came and hugged me)
kai mou 'dose o Christos zoi kai fos.
(and Christ gave me life and light.)
Aftos me chortainei, Aftos me poimainei,
(He feeds me, He shepherds me,)
einai o kalos voskos!
(he is the good shepherd!)
Kai kapoio deili th' anoixei i pyli,
(And some coward will open the gate,)
tha m' odigis' Aftos ston ourano!
(He will lead me to heaven!)
