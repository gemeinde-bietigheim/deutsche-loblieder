280 TIN EMPISTOSYNI

Greek Hymns - No. 280 | "ΤΗΝ ΕΜΠΙΣΤΟΣΥΝΗ"
Verse 1:
Tin empistosyni echeis sto Christo;
(Do you trust in Christ?)
Agapas Ekeinon perissotero;
(Do you love Him more?)
Eisai mathitis Tou; Vlepe sti Grafi
(Are you His disciple? See in Scripture)
ki o,ti sou chreiazetai tha sou dothei!
(and everything you need will be given to you!)
Chorus:
Afthoni zoi, nai, tha sou dothei,
(Abundant life, yes, it will be given to you,)
zitise me pisti kai tha sou dothei!
(ask in faith and it will be given to you!)
Pneyma Agio, nai, tha sou dothei,
(Holy Spirit, yes, it will be given to you,)
zitise me pisti kai tha sou dothei!
(ask in faith and it will be given to you!)
Verse 2:
Doxase to onoma tou Iisoy,
(Praise the name of Jesus,)
pistepse se osa leei o Logos Tou!
(believe what His Word says!)
Zitise to Pneyma me oli tin psychi
(Seek the Spirit with all your soul)
kai tha laveis ap' Afton o,ti potheis!
(and you will receive from Him whatever you desire!)
Verse 3:
Tin yposchesi Tou den arneit' Aftos
(He does not deny His promise)
s' opoion an zitisei ap' Afton pistos!
(to anyone who asks Him faithfully!)
Thelei na sou dosei o,ti tou ziteis,
(He wants to give you whatever you ask of him,)
tin eirini kai tin niki tis psychis!
(peace and victory of the soul!)
Verse 4:
Akouse ti legei o Christos se se,
(Listen to what Christ says to you,)
afise ta ola kai s' Afton elthe!
(leave everything to Him and come!)
De tha s' aporripsei, trexe parefthys,
(He won't reject you, run right along)
tha se prosdechthei kai tha eflogitheis!
(he will welcome you and you will be blessed!)
