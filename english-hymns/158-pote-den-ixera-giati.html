158 POTE DEN IXERA GIATI

Greek Hymns - No. 158 | "ΠΟΤΕ ΔΕΝ ΗΞΕΡΑ ΓΙΑΤΙ"
Verse 1:
Pote den ixera giati i chari tou Theoy
(I never knew why the grace of God)
egine kai se me gnosti pou imoun echthros Aftoy!
(it became known to you that I was His enemy!)
Chorus:
Omos xero se poion pisteyo!
(But I know who I believe in!)
Ki oti mporei, nai, na me fylaxei
(And that he can, yes, protect me)
os to telos eis ti zoi mou,
(till the end of my life)
pou anethesa s' Afton!
(which I entrusted to Him!)
Verse 2:
Pote den ixera giati s' emena o Christos
(I never knew why Christ to me)
edose pisti zontani, vevaiosi kai fos!
(gave living faith, confirmation and light!)
Verse 3:
Pote den ixera giati o Logos tou Theoy
(I never knew why the Word of God)
allazei tin kardia mas, tin kanei tou Christoy!
(it changes our heart, makes it Christ's!)
Verse 4:
Pote den ixera to pos elegchet' o thnitos
(I never knew how a mortal is controlled)
apo to Pneyma tou Theoy, gia na sothei ki aftos!
(by the Spirit of God, that he too may be saved!)
Verse 5:
Den xero akomi sti zoi ti allo tha symvei,
(I don't know yet in life what else will happen,)
chares, imeres dyskoles, synteleia sti gi!
(joys, hard days, end on earth!)
