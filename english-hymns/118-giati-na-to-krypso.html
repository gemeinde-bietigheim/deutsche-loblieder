118 GIATI NA TO KRYPsO

Greek Hymns - No. 118 | "ΓΙΑΤΙ ΝΑ ΤΟ ΚΡΥΨΩ"
Verse 1:
Giati na to krypso, giati na ntrapo;
(Why should I hide it, why should I be ashamed?)
Vathia stin kardia mou Afton agapo!
(Deep in my heart I love Him!)
Afton kai latreyo, poios allos sti gi
(I adore him, who else on earth)
gia mena mporoyse Stafro na dechthei;
(for me could Stavros accept?)
Chorus:
Sto Christo, nai, gi' Afton i kardia mou chtypa!
(To Christ, yes, for Him my heart beats!)
Sto Christo, nai, gi' Afton i zoi mou mila!
(In Christ, yes, my life speaks of Him!)
Afton kai latreyo, poios allos sti gi
(I adore him, who else on earth)
gia mena mporoyse Stafro na dechthei;
(for me could Stavros accept?)
Verse 2:
Giati na to krypso; Den kryvetai afto,
(Why hide it? There is no hiding this,)
pos echei i zoi mou allaxei entelos!
(how my life has completely changed!)
To vlepoune oloi, to vlepo ki ego,
(Everyone can see it, I can see it too,)
o ponos echathei kai zei o Christos!
(the pain disappears and Christ lives!)
Verse 3:
Giati na to krypso; Pantoy n' akoustei
(Why hide it? Everywhere to be heard)
kai oloi na mathoun pos echo sothei!
(and let everyone know that I am saved!)
I mayri zoi mou egemise fos,
(My dark life filled with light,)
chara kai eirini mou dinei o Christos!
(Christ gives me joy and peace!)
