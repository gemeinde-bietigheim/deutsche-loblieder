76 ThEOS TO FROYRION IMON

Greek Hymns - No. 76 | "ΘΕΟΣ ΤΟ ΦΡΟΥΡΙΟΝ ΗΜΩΝ"
Verse 1:
Theos to froyrion imon, aspis en tois kindynois
(God our fortress, shield in danger)
kai voithos pantos pistoy en ora tis odynis!
(and helper of every believer in time of suffering!)
Kata tou poniroy, tou ton psychon echthroy,
(Against the wicked, the enemy of souls,)
propyrgion imon o panta kyvernon,
(our bulwark the ever-ruling,)
o Archon tis eirinis!
(the Prince of Peace!)
Verse 2:
Ean imeis eis eaftoys echomen tin elpida,
(If we ourselves have hope,)
mataios thelomen zitei ouranion patrida!
(in vain we want to ask for a heavenly homeland!)
All' einai meth' imon o Megas Igemon,
(But the Great Ruler is with us,)
Christos o Lytrotis, echthroy tou tis psychis
(Christ the Redeemer, enemy of the soul)
syntripsas tin pagida!
(you smashed the trap!)
Verse 3:
O kosmos ean egerthei imas na afanisei,
(If the world rises to destroy us,)
thelomen ara fovithei mi ontos katischysei;
(do we therefore want to be afraid of actually being oppressed?)
Tacheos o Theos tha elthi arogos,
(Quickly God will come to help,)
en ti dynamei Tou tis alitheias Tou
(in His power of His truth)
to kratos tha idrysei!
(the state will establish!)
