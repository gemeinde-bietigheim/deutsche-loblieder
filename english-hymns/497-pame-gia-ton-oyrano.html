497 PAME GIA TON OYRANO

Greek Hymns - No. 497 | "ΠΑΜΕ ΓΙΑ ΤΟΝ ΟΥΡΑΝΟ"
Verse 1:
Pame gia ton ourano, ti chara!
(Let's go to heaven, what a joy!)
Ela, file mas, kai sy mes sti syntrofia.
(Come, our friend, and you in company.)
Ein' o kosmos pia gia mas xeniteia,
(The world is now a stranger to us,)
i patrida ton piston ein' ekei psila!
(the homeland of the faithful is up there!)
Chorus:
O, ti lampra, ti omorfa
(Oh, how brilliant, how beautiful)
ston ourano ekei psila!
(in the sky up there!)
Se proskalei o Lytrotis,
(The Redeemer invites you,)
o Vasilias, sti chora Tou kai sy na 'rtheis!
(the King, to His country you too may come!)
Verse 2:
Planithikam' arketa oloi emeis,
(We were all quite misled,)
os na vroyme to sosto dromo tis zois.
(how to find the right path in life.)
Ein' o monos o Christos Lytrotis
(Christ the Redeemer is the only one)
ki ein' o dromos asfalis, ela kai tha deis!
(and the road is safe, come and see!)
Verse 3:
Mia poreia endoxi kai lampri,
(A course glorious and brilliant,)
neoi, geroi kai paidia oloi sti grammi.
(young, old and children all in line.)
Xeroume pou prochoroyn oloi aftoi,
(We know where they all go,)
stin aionia, sti lampri ki endoxi zoi!
(to eternal, bright and glorious life!)
