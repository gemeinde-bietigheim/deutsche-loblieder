193 OTAN VLEPO TIN AGIOTITA SOY

Asma Neon - No. 193 | "ΟΤΑΝ ΒΛΕΠΩ ΤΗΝ ΑΓΙΟΤΗΤΑ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1
Otan vlepo tin agiotita Sou,otan vlepo tin agapi Sou,ola gyro mou thamponounmprosta sto diko Sou fos.Otan vrisko ti chara tis parousias Soukai sto thelima Sou paradinomai,ola gyro mou thamponoun, mprosta sto diko Sou fos.Latreyo Ese, latreyo Ese,na o logos pou zo: na latreyo Ese. (2)
(When I see Your holiness, when I see Your love, all around me they dazzle before Your light. When I find the joy of presence I surrender to You and to Your will, everything around me is dim, in front of Your light. I adore You, I adore You, this is the reason I live: to adore You. (2))
