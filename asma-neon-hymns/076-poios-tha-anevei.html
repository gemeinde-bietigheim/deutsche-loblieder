076 POIOS ThA ANEVEI

Asma Neon - No. 076 | "ΠΟΙΟΣ ΘΑ ΑΝΕΒΕΙ"
Verse 1
Poios th’ anevei stin ourania Sionpoios tha stathei mpros ston Agio Sou throno.Mono aftos pou echei agni kardiath’ anevei ‘kei psila, th’ anevei ‘kei psila,th’ anevei stin ourania Sion.Dos mou Kyrie agni kardiagemise tin me ourania fotia,dikaio kane me gia na mporo stin ourania Sion n’ anevo.Agios, agios, agios, agios,agios, agios o Kyrios.Tha ‘rtho me pisti ston Agio throno Soumprosta sta Serafeim ,mprosta sta Cherouveim.Kai me latreia, thafmasmotha psallo apo chara.Agios, agios, agios, agios,agios, agios o Kyrios.
(Who will ascend to the heavenly Sionpoios will stand before Your Holy One throne. Only he who has a pure heart will rise up here, will rise up here high, it will ascend to the heavenly Zion. My Lord, pure heart, fill it with heavenly fire, make me righteous so that I may be in heavenly Zion come up. Holy, holy, holy, holy, holy, holy is the Lord. I will come with faith in the Holy throne In front of the Seraphim, in front of Cherubim. And with worship, praise I will sing for joy. Holy, holy, holy, holy, holy, holy is the Lord.)
