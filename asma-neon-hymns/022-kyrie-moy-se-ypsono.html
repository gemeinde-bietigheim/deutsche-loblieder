022 KYRIE MOY SE YPsONO

Asma Neon - No. 022 | "ΚΥΡΙΕ ΜΟΥ ΣΕ ΥΨΩΝΩ"
Verse 1
Kyrie mou Se ypsono.Kyrie mou Se doxazo.Mou ‘feres tosi chara. Ti chara tis sotirias.Katevikes edo sti gi na anoixeis odoap’ ti gi eis to Stafro gia na sothoki apo ‘kei ston tafo, ap’ ton tafo sto TheoKyrie mou S’ agapo. (2)
(Lord I lift You up. Lord I praise You. You have brought me so much joy. Her joy salvation. You came down here to earth to open a way from earth to the Cross for to be saved from here to the grave, from the grave to God, my Lord, I love You. (2))
