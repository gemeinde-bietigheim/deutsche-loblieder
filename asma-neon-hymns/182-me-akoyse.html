182 ME AKOYSE

Asma Neon - No. 182 | "ΜΕ ΑΚΟΥΣΕ"
Verse 1
O ti chara o Kyrios me akousesan ti foni mou ypsosa s’ Afton ,san prosefchithika eskypse ki akouse ,gi’ afto oso zo tha epikaloymai Afton .Ponoi thanatou san me perikyklosan kai stenochoria tou Adi san me vrike ,thlipsi kai ponos otan me synantisan ,m’ ap’ ola afta me lytrose o Theos .Gematos eleos kai dikaiosyni kai me agapi einai o Theos.Talaiporithika m’ Aftos me esose,psychi mou isychase dioti o Kyriosse akouse kai se efergetise. (2)
(Oh, what a joy the Lord heard me, I raised my voice to Him, as if I prayed bend down and hear, therefore as long as I live I will call upon Him.Pains of death as if they surrounded me and distress of Hades as if it found me, sorrow and pain when they met me, God redeemed me from all this. Full of mercy and justice and with love is God. I suffered with Him save, my soul be still because the Lord heard you and benefited you. (2))
