164 EChO ESENA ChRISTE MOY

Asma Neon - No. 164 | "ΕΧΩ ΕΣΕΝΑ ΧΡΙΣΤΕ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1
Otan erthei i nychta, de fovamaiotan erthei skotadi vathy,echo Esena Christe mou pou mou pianeis to cherieisai dipla mou kathe stigmi. (2)Otan echo diogmo, de fovamai otan oloi m΄ afinoun monachoecho Esena Christe mou mou skoupizeis ta dakryai skia Sou me parigorei. (2)Otan vlepo vouna, de fovamaiotan dysvati einai i zoiecho Esena Christe moupou mou deichneis to dromo kai s΄ afton perpatame mazi. (2)Otan oi alloi geloyn de fovamaiotan me theoroyn fanatikoecho Esena Christe moupou glyka m΄ agkaliazeismou gemizeis me fos tin psychi. (2)
(When the night comes, I am not afraid that deep darkness will come, I have You, my Christ when you hold my hand you are by my side every moment. (2) When I have persecution, I am not afraid when everyone leaves me alone, I have You, my Christ you wipe away the tears, your shadow comforts me. (2) When I see mountains, no I was afraid that life is impassable. I have You, my Christ, where You show me road and we walk on it together. (2) When others laugh not I was afraid they would consider me a fanatic. I have You, Christ, sweet me you embrace me, you fill my soul with light. (2))
