165 I PsYChI MOY DIPsAEI GIA SE

Asma Neon - No. 165 | "Η ΨΥΧΗ ΜΟΥ ΔΙΨΑΕΙ ΓΙΑ ΣΕ"
Verse 1
Kyrie i psychi mou dipsaei gia Sei kardia mou pothei Christe ena aggigma Sou.Erimos i zoi mes ton kosmo aftoagonizomai, prospatho thelo tin agkalia Sou.Se zito ela Kyrie na se do kai konta Sou na anapaftho, pare ola ta vari.Steile mou dynami gia na perpato mes to dromo Sou to steno, ela dose mou chari.Kyrie ti zoi Sou Esy prosferes kai to aima sou echyses gia na me lytroseis.Ta vari kratises sto stafro kai tis thlipseis mou.Agio Pneyma mou edoses gia na m΄ endynamosei. Stin kardia echo tora poly chara mesa mou i eirini Sou, tora vasileyei. Ki erchomai na mpo stin parousia Sou kai sto oros Sou to agio i psychi mou n’ anevei.
(Lord my soul thirsts for You my heart longs Christ for a touch Yours. Life with the world is lonely, I struggle, I try, I want the hug You. I am asking you, come Lord, so that I can see you and rest near You, take everything burdens. Send me strength to walk in Your narrow way, come give me grace. Lord You offered Your life and shed Your blood for to redeem me. You kept the burdens on the cross and my sorrows. Saint You gave me spirit to strengthen me. I now have great joy in my heart in me Your peace now reigns. And I come to enter into Your presence and to Your holy mountain may my soul ascend.)
