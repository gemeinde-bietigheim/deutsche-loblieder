063 TO AIMA TOY ChRISTOY

Asma Neon - No. 063 | "ΤΟ ΑΙΜΑ ΤΟΥ ΧΡΙΣΤΟΥ"
Verse 1
To aima tou Christoy ,to aima tou Christoy,to aima tou Christoy gia me.Imoun apololos deinos amartolos,echythin sto Stafro gia me. (2)O Logos tou Theoy, o Logos tou Theoy,o Logos tou Theoy gia me.M’ Afton panta tha zo, Afton tha martyrovadizo pros ton ourano. (2)To Pneyma tou Theoy, to Pneyma tou Theoy,to Pneyma tou Theoy gia me.Afto zoopoiei, i sarx den ofeleiafto mou dinei dynami. (2)I agapi tou Theoy, i agapi tou Theoy,i agapi tou Theoy gia me.Ton Iisoy Christo epano ston Stafrothysia ekane gia me. (2)
(The blood of Christ, the blood of Christ, the blood of Christ for me. I was poor sinner, poured out on the Cross for me. (2) The Word of God, the Word of God, the Word of God for me. With Him I will always live, with Him I will I bear witness to the sky. (2) The Spirit of God, his Spirit God, the Spirit of God for me. This gives life, the flesh is of no use to me gives strength. (2)God's love, God's love, God's love for me. He made Jesus Christ on the Cross for me. (2))
