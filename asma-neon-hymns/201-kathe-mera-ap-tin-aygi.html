201 KAThE MERA AP’ TIN AYGI

Asma Neon - No. 201 | "ΚΑΘΕ ΜΕΡΑ ΑΠ’ ΤΗΝ ΑΥΓΗ"
Verse 1
Kathe mera ap’ tin afgi mechri ti nychtia oli i gi,ton Theo na eflogei. (2)Doxa s’ Afton, psalte straties ton ouranon,doxa ston Thafmasto.Doxa timi, psalte pistoi ki oli i gi n’ antichei.Na i entoli mou:“Agapate allilous” opos sas agapo.Aftos s’ agapa file, ti minyma thafmasto,ki ego s’ agapo file megalo ki alithino.
(Every day from dawn to dusk all the earth bless God. (2) Glory to Him, sing, hosts of heaven, glory to Him Wonderful. Glory, honor, sing ye faithful, and let all the earth resound. Here is the commandment me: "Love one another" as I love you. He loves you friend, what a message wonderful, I love you too my friend great and true.)
