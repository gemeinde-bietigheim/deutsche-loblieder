228 EChO SOThEI

Asma Neon - No. 228 | "ΕΧΩ ΣΩΘΕΙ"
Verse 1
Echo sothei ki echo chara.Echo sothei mesa ap' ti fthora.Me pisti piga konta sto Stafro,me pisti piga sto Christome aima mou 'plyne tin kardiaki echo sothei.De me nika o kosmos pia.De me nika dynami kamia.Konta mou echo Christo dynato,to vracho echo v' akoumpoki otan erchetai to kakode me nika.Panta tha zo me to Christo.Panta tha zo kai tha ton ymno.Gemati tha 'cho tin kardia me charakai me tis pistis ta ftera,panta me to Christo kontapanta tha zo.Ki otan vretho ston ourano.Kai tha Ton do endoxo, lampro.Stefani tote lampro tha forokai ston aggelon to choro,panta me to Christo mou tha zopanta tha zo.
(I have been saved and I have joy. I have been saved through corruption. By faith I went to Cross, with faith I went to Christ, my blood, wash my little heart be saved. The world does not defeat me anymore. No power defeats me. I am close to me Christ is strong, I have the rock to lean on when evil comes to me wins. I will always live with Christ. I will always live and praise him. I will be full I have a heart with joy and wings with faith, always with Christ I'll live forever. And when I'm in heaven. And I'll see Him glorious, brilliant. Stefani then brilliant I will wear even to the angels the dance, always with My Christ, I will live forever.)
