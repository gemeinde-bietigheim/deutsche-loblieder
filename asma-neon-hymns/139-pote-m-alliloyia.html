139 POTE M’ ALLILOYIA

Asma Neon - No. 139 | "ΠΟΤΕ Μ’ ΑΛΛΗΛΟΥΙΑ"
Verse 1
Pote m’ alliloyia, pote m΄ Osannaoi kardies mas smigounsto Theo mprosta.Ypsoste tis kardies saski archiste to glyko tragoydi.“Doxa ston Agio Lytrotias antichei pantoy.Afiste ti chara sasn’ anthisei zontano louloydiki aploste myro ki omorfiatis charis tou Christoy.Gemiste me to Pneymakai xecheiliste ap’ tin agapinotes glykes mes stis kardiesna peftoun san vrochi. Fonaxte: “alliloyia”stou kosmou n’ akoustei tin akri.“Glyke Sotira s’ agapom’ oli mou tin psychi”.
(Sometimes with hallelujahs, sometimes with Hosannas, our hearts are united to God ahead. Lift up your hearts and begin the sweet song. "Glory in Saint Redeemer echoes everywhere. Let your joy bloom alive little flower, spread myrrh and beauty of the grace of Christ. Fill me the Spirit and overflow with love sweet notes in the hearts they fall like rain. Shout: "Hallelujah" for the world to hear end. "Sweet Savior, I love you with all my soul".)
