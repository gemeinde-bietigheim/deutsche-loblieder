Verse 1
Poios ta matia ton tyflon
(Who the eyes of the blind)
kai ta ota ton koufon anoigei;
(and the ears of the deaf opens?)
O Kyrios, o Kyrios, o Kyrios.
(The Lord, the Lord, the Sir.)
Poios giatreyei tous leproys
(Who heals the lepers?)
poios egeirei tous nekroys, poios einai;
(who raises the dead, who is it?)
O Kyrios, o Kyrios, o Iisoys Christos.
(The Lord, the Lord, Jesus Christ.)
Epodos
(Refrain)
Poios ton anemo vastaei
(Who him wind blows)
sti zoi poios se krataei; Pes mou.
(in life who holds you? tell me)
O Kyrios, o Kyrios, o Kyrios,
(The Lord, the Lord, the Sir,)
o Kyrios, o Kyrios, o Iisoys Christos.
(the Lord, the Lord, Jesus Christ.)
Poios katevike sti gi
(Who came down to earth)
ki afise ti doxa Tou gia sena;
(and left His glory for you?)
O Kyrios, o Kyrios, o Kyrios.
(The Lord, the Lord, the Lord.)
Poios edechthike empaigmo
(Who accepted taunt)
poios ypefere stafro gia mena;
(who suffered the cross for me?)
O Kyrios, o Kyrios, o Iisoys Christos.
(The Lord, the Lord, Jesus Christ.)
