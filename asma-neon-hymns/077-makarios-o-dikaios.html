077 MAKARIOS O DIKAIOS

Asma Neon - No. 077 | "ΜΑΚΑΡΙΟΣ Ο ΔΙΚΑΙΟΣ"
Verse 1
Makarios opoios pote ston asevon to dromo de vadise, oyte kathise ston chlefaston tin edra.Alla sto dromo Tou Theoy einai to thelima touki olimeris ki olinychtis afto na meletaei.De tha ’nai san to dentro aftos, pou einai rizomenostis ochthes afthonon neron pou chynontai kai trechoun.Den tha ‘nai etsi oi aseveis, alla san t’ achyro einaipou ap’ tis gis to prosopo o ageras to saronei.Gi’ afto kai tha parastathoyn oi aseveis stin krisiall’ omos oloi oi efseveissti synaxi ton dikaion.Giati gnorizei o Kyrios ton dikaion ton dromo,kai katanta ton asevon to dromo stin apoleia.
(Blessed is he who has never walked in the path of the ungodly, nor sat in it mockers of the seat. Others in the way of God is the will of this All day and all night he should study. He will not be like the tree he, who is rooted in the banks of abundant waters that pour out and they run. The ungodly will not be like that, but they are like straw from her earth's face the wind sweeps it. That's why the ungodly in judgment, but all godly in the assembly of the righteous. Why The Lord knows the way of the righteous, and the way of the wicked in loss.)
