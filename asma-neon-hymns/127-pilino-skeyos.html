127 PILINO SKEYOS

Asma Neon - No. 127 | "ΠΗΛΙΝΟ ΣΚΕΥΟΣ"
Verse 1
Pilino skeyos, ostrakino skeyos, eimai ego.Mesa mou omos echo ena thayma, echo ena thisafro.Echo Esena Patera, echo Esena Christe mou echo Esena Agio Pneyma. Echo Esena Patera, echo Esena Christe mou echo Esena Thee mou.Ti foni Sou akoyo kai Sena niotho na mou milas.Niotho na me koitazeis, niotho na m΄ agkaliazeis, ki eisai toso konta.Plai mou eisai Patera, plai mou eisai Christe mou,plai mou eisai Agio Pneyma.Mesa mou eisai Patera, mesa mou eisai Christe mou,mesa mou eisai Thee mou.S’ agapao Patera, S’ agapao Christe mou,S’ agapao Agio Pneyma.S’ agapao Patera, S’ agapao Christe mou,S’ agapao Thee mou.
(Clay vessel, shell vessel, I am. But within me I have a miracle, I have a treasure. I have You Father, I have You my Christ, I have You Holy Spirit. I have You, Father, I have You, my Christ, I have You, my God. Your voice I hear you and I feel you talking to me. I feel you looking at me, I feel you you embrace, and you are so close. You are by my side Father, you are by my side My Christ, by my side you are the Holy Spirit. In me you are Father, in me you are my Christ, within me you are my God. I love you Father, I love you My Christ, I love You Holy Spirit. I love You Father, I love You Christ my, I love you, my God.)
