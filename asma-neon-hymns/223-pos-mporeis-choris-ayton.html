223 POS MPOREIS ChORIS AYTON

Asma Neon - No. 223 | "ΠΩΣ ΜΠΟΡΕΙΣ ΧΩΡΙΣ ΑΥΤΟΝ"
Verse 1
Pos mporeis choris Afton, na zeis na perpataspou anapneeis, pos milas choris Christo;Pos mporeis se xeni gi, alitheia na zitaspos chairesai kai tragoudas choirs zoi;Pos mporeis makria ap' Afton gia agapi na milas;Afoy monachos sou paleyeis gia ena chadi.Pos les oti echeis fos, o ilios sou egine tholosta matia sou echeis sfalismena sto skotadi.Des ta dakrya pou kyloyn mesa ap' ta matia Toupou eine karfomena stin psychi sou.Pes mou pos de spaei i kardia koitontas sto StafroEkeinon pou egine i lytrosi sou;Pes mporeis s' afti ti gi eirini na zitasafotou vlepeis stin psychi sou tarachi;Pos zitas vero na vreis ti dipsa sou na sviseismes stin erimo tou kosmou tin xeri;Pes apomakrynthikes ap' ton Patera sou;Tin poli sou afises kai agapises tin xeni.Gyrna piso sti chara mes stin patrida souopou asfaleia kai agapi se prosmenei.Des sta cheria Tou pliges akgathia sto kefaliponaei kai den milaei, ma se koitazei.Pes mou pos de lygise i psychi koitontas sto Stafroto aima pou ap' agapi sti gi stazei;
(How can you live, walk, breathe, talk without Him? Christ? How can you, in a foreign land, really ask how you rejoice and sing? lifeless? How can you talk about love away from Him? After being a monk you fight for a caress. How do you say you have light, your sun became domes your eyes are closed in the dark. See the tears that flow through them Tupou's eyes were fixed on your soul. Tell me that the heart doesn't break looking at the Cross of those where your redemption took place? Can you tell her? ask the earth for peace after you see turmoil in your soul? How do you ask vero na find your thirst quenched in the desert of the dry world? Say did you turn away from your Father? You left your city and loved it stranger. He returns to the joy of his homeland where safety and love are in you he waits. Look at his hands wounds thorns in his head he hurts and does not speak, but he is looking at you. Tell me how the soul did not bend while looking at the blood of the Cross that does the earth drip with love?)
