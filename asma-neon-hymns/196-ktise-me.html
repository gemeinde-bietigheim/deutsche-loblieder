Verse 1
Ktise me xana, poso thelo na Sou moiaso,
(Build me again, how I want to be like You,)
plase me xana Thee, thelo na Se vro.
(make me again God, I want You i find)
Krata me sta cheria Sou, krata tin kardia mou,
(Hold me in Your hands, hold my heart,)
mathe mou Patera mou o,ti agapas na agapo.
(teach me my Father whatever you love to love)
Epodos
(Refrain)
Allaxe me Kyrie, ap' ta vathi tis kardias mou,
(Change me, Lord, from the bottom of my heart my)
fotise ta matia mou na mporo na se koito.
(enlighten my eyes so that I can look at you.)
Allaxe me Kyrie, ap' ta vathi tis psychis mou,
(Change me Lord, from the depths of my soul)
dos mou neo Pneyma na mporo na Se agapo,
(give me new Spirit that I may love You,)
o, nai Patera, dos mou neo Pneyma
(oh yes Father give me new Spirit)
na mporo na Se agapo.
(to be able to love You)
Adynamo to soma mou, ma stin kardia mou mesa,
(My body is weak, but in my heart in,)
mono Esy choras Thee, Esy na katoikeis.
(only You live, God, You dwell.)
Stis afles tis polis Sou, tha prosefchitho,
(In the courts of Thy city, shall pray)
oles tis meres tis zois mou ekei na katoiko.
(all the days of my life there to dwell.)
