221 ENAS EINAI O DROMOS

Asma Neon - No. 221 | "ΕΝΑΣ ΕΙΝΑΙ Ο ΔΡΟΜΟΣ"
Verse 1
Enas einai o dromos pou echaraxe Aftos,enas einai o dromos o Iisoys Christos. (2)Ela kai sy apala na afiseis,to fortio pou chronia stous omous krataskai 'cheis pia kourastei.To kratise o Sotiras tou kosmoukai to kouvalise gia sena, nai sto Golgotha.Ela kai sy sti chara tou Kyriou,mes stin eifni Tou, mesa sti doxa Touna anapaftheis.Ela s' Afton pou s' agapise aioniaeinai i alitheia, einai i zoi, s' agapaei Aftos.
(One is the way that He paved, one is the way of Jesus Christ. (2) Come and gently let go of the burden you carry on your shoulders for years you hold on and you're already tired. The Savior of the world held it too carried for you, yes to Golgotha. Come too to the joy of the Lord, mes in His silence, in His glory you rest. Come to Him who loved you truth is eternal, it is life, He loves you.)
