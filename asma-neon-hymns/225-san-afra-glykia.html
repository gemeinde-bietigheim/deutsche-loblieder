Epodos
(Refrain)
Pneyma tou Theoy, Pneyma Agio,
(Spirit of God, Spirit Holy,)
mas agapas alithina.
(you really love us)
Pneyma tou Theoy, Pneyma Agio,
(Spirit of God, Holy Spirit,)
mas agkaliazeis storgika.
(you hug us affectionately.)
