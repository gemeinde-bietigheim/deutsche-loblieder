112 TON YPsOSE O ThEOS

Asma Neon - No. 112 | "ΤΟΝ ΥΨΩΣΕ Ο ΘΕΟΣ"
Verse 1
Ton ypsose o Theos eis ta ypsilakai charise s’ Afton, to yper pan onomakai kathe gonato, tha kleinei sto Christogiati einai Kyrios eis doxa Theoy Patros. (2)Ton ypsose o Theos eis ta ypsila kai charise s’ Afton to yper pan onoma.Tha Se ymniso Thee kai tha psallo pros Sem’ oli mou ti dynami. Tha Se ymniso Thee,tha latreyso Ese oli mera Esena tha aino.Giati eisai Kyrie endoxos ki eflogo t’ Agio Sou onoma,i kardia mou agni se Sena as faneikathos Esena tha latreyo o, Christe. (2)
(God exalted him to the highest and bestowed on him, the best name and all knee, will be closed in Christ because he is Lord to the glory of God the Father. (2) God exalted him to the heights and bestowed upon him the best of everything name. I will praise You God and I will sing to You with all my might. I will I will praise you God, I will worship you all day long I will praise you. For you are Lord glorious and I bless Your Holy name, let my heart be pure in You evidently Thee will I adore O Christ. (2))
