220 I AGAPI SOY ChRISTE MOY EDOSE ZOI

Asma Neon - No. 220 | "Η ΑΓΑΠΗ ΣΟΥ ΧΡΙΣΤΕ ΜΟΥ ΕΔΩΣΕ ΖΩΗ"
Verse 1
I agapi Sou Christe mou edose zoikai tora zo xana kai pali ap’ tin archi.I agapi Sou Christe mou edose charase Se psallo, alliloyia.To fos Sou lampei, lampei, lampei psilafotizei tin kathe mia kardia.Aionia tha zeis tha zoyme kai emeis kai tha psalloume to osanna , alliloyia.Sta cheria Sou kratas t’ asteria kai ti gimazi Sou eimai asfalis.Gi’ afto ki ego tha Sou psallo, tha psallo enoso zo, eis Esena ton Dimiourgo, alliloyia.
(Your love Christ gave me life and now I live again and again from the beginning Your love Christ gave me joy I sing to You, hallelujah. Your light shines, it shines, it shines, it lights up every heart. You will live forever, we will live too we will sing the hosanna, hallelujah. In Your hands you hold the stars and earth I am safe with You. That's why I too will sing to You, I will I sing while I live, to You the Creator, hallelujah.)
