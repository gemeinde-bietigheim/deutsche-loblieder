189 KAThE PROI SAN XYPNISO

Asma Neon - No. 189 | "ΚΑΘΕ ΠΡΩΙ ΣΑΝ ΞΥΠΝΗΣΩ"
Verse 1
Kathe proi san xypniso archizo na psallo, s’ Eseta cheili mou ki i kardia mou ypsonoun Ese.Patera, ein’ gemati i kardia mou m’ efgnomosyni polli gia tin pistotita kai to eleos Sou se me.Tha doxazo Esena Thee mou, gia panta,tha doxazo Esena pantotina.Tha doxazo Esena Thee mou, gia panta,tha doxazo Ese.
(Every morning as soon as I wake up I begin to sing, to Eseta my lips and my heart rise Father, my heart is filled with much gratitude for your faithfulness and Your mercy to me. I will glorify You my God, forever, I will glorify You forever. I will praise You, my God, forever, I will praise You.)
