084 I AGAPI TOY

Asma Neon - No. 084 | "Η ΑΓΑΠΗ ΤΟΥ"
Verse 1
I agapi Tou, mou allaxe ti zoim’ evgale apo skotadi pykno,ki apo ti fylaki.M’ eleftherose kai tora zo konta Toueimai makria apo ti sklavia,kai tin osmi tou thanatou. I agapi Tou, mou charaxe poreiakai sto dromo Tou pia perpatomes stin eleftheria.Mou edose fylakto, gia panta tin prosefchi, m’ othise etsi apla na niko kai na milame mazi.I agapi Tou, me gemise apo galini ki i chara pou mou edose Aftos, mes stin kardia mou tha meinei.Ki an apomakryntho, ap’ ti glykeia Touagkalia, trecho piso amesos s’ Afton kai vrisko parigoria.
(His love changed my life, brought me out of thick darkness, and from prison. He set me free and now I live near him. I am far from him slavery, and the smell of death. His love, marked a path for me on His way now you walk in freedom. He gave me a talisman, forever prayer, prompt me thus simply to overcome and speak together. His love, I was filled with peace and the joy that He gave me, in my heart will stay. Even if I go away, from sweet Tuagalia, I run back immediately to I find solace in him.)
