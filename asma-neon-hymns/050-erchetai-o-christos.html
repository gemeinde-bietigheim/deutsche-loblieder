050 ERChETAI O ChRISTOS

Asma Neon - No. 050 | "ΕΡΧΕΤΑΙ Ο ΧΡΙΣΤΟΣ"
Verse 1
Erchet’ o Christos, erchet’ o Christoski oloi mas Ton karteroyme,ki oloi mas Ton karteroyme.Erchetai xana, erchetai xanaki oloi mas Ton karteroyme. (2)De tha klaime pia, de tha klaime pia,dakrya de tha yparchoun, dakrya de tha yparchoun.Gelio kai chara, gelio kai chara mono afta pia tha yparchoun. (2)Erchet’ o Christos, erchet’ o Christoski otan oloi tha Ton doyme, ki otan oloi tha Ton doyme,m’ oli tin kardia, m’ oli tin kardiaoloi mas tha Ton ymnoyme. (2)
(Christ is coming, Christ is coming and all of us are waiting for Him, and all of us are waiting for Him He is coming again, he is coming again, all of us are waiting for Him. (2)We'll cry no more, we'll cry no more, no more tears, no more tears they will not exist. Laughter and joy, laughter and joy, only these will exist. (2) Christ is coming, Christ is coming and when we will all see Him, and when we will all see Him, with all our hearts, with all our hearts we will all love Him we praise (2))
