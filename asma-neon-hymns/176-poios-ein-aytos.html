176 POIOS EIN’ AYTOS

Asma Neon - No. 176 | "ΠΟΙΟΣ ΕΙΝ’ ΑΥΤΟΣ"
Verse 1
Poios ein' Aftos pou stekei epano,s' afto to lofo to gymno,ta cheria Tou einai karfomenapano s' ena skliro stafro.To prosopo Tou matomeno,na kai to soma Tou gymnokai ap' tin plefra ti logchismenito aima trechei kai nero.Ein' i thysia tou Patera,einai o amomos Amnos,ein' i alitheia tis agapis,einai o Iisoys Christos. (3)Poios einai Aftos pou ein' ston tafo,ki exo stratiotes Ton frouroyn,ki oi myrofores oi gynaikes,prin na charaxei xekinoyn.Vriskoun ton tafo anoigmeno,ki o Iisoys den einai ekei,ta roycha Tou einai diplomena,ki aggeloi stekontai ekei.Ma ti zitate gia na vreite,to zonta mesa stous nekroys,den ein’ edo all’ anestithitora tha zei stous ouranoys. (3)
(Who is He who stands on this bare hill, His hands are nailed to a hard cross. His face bloody, yes His body is naked and from the speared side the blood runs and water. If the sacrifice of the Father is the innocent Lamb, it is the truth of of love, is Jesus Christ. (3) Who is He who is in the tomb, and outside soldiers guard Him, and the women who bear myrrh, before they start. They find the tomb open, and Jesus is not there there, His clothes are folded, and angels are standing there. But what you ask to find, the living among the dead, they are not here but resurrected and will live in the heavens. (3))
