Verse 1
Gennithike, mesa sti fatni egennithike,
(He was born, in the manger he was born,)
ston ourano efanike asteri foteino.
(a star appeared in the sky bright.)
Gennithike, mesa sti fatni egennithike,
(He was born, in the manger he was born,)
kai tapeina efanike o Thafmastos Theos.
(and humbly the Wonder One appeared God.)
Epodos
(Refrain)
Gennithike, gia sena xereis fanerothike
(It was born, for you you know it was manifested)
o Megas Archon Lytrotis
(the Great Archon Redeemer)
pou irthe edo kato sti gi
(who came down here on earth)
gia na sothoyme ego ki esy.
(so that I too can be saved you.)
Gennithike, sto stavlo mesa egennithike,
(He was born, in the stable he was born,)
o vasilias tis doxas elave tou doylou ti morfi.
(the king of glory received his slave form.)
Gennithike, chara megali fanerothike
(He was born, great joy was revealed)
kai fos alithino efanike stou skotous ti sigi.
(and true light appeared in the darkness the silence.)
