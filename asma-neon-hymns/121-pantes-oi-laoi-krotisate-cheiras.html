121 PANTES OI LAOI KROTISATE ChEIRAS

Asma Neon - No. 121 | "ΠΑΝΤΕΣ ΟΙ ΛΑΟΙ ΚΡΟΤΗΣΑΤΕ ΧΕΙΡΑΣ"
Verse 1
Pantes oi laoi krotisate cheiraskamete endoxon ton ymnon Aftoy.Eis ta dexia tis megalosyniseinai o thronos tou aioniou vasilia.Aftos kat’ archas tin gin ethemelioseerga ton cheiron Tou einai oi ouranoi.Aftos diamenei, aftoi apoloyntaikai os imation palaiothisontai.Aineite Afton en kymvalois efichoispasa pnoi as ymnei Ton Kyrion.Aineite Afton en chordais kai organoisepeffimisate ton thriamvon Aftoy.Idoy erchetai endedymenos doxanopsontai Afton pasai ai fylai tis gis.To Pneyma ki i nymfi legousin erchounai, erchou Kyrie, dikaie kriti.To Pneyma ki i nymfi legousin erchou,nai, erchou Kyrie, Eleftheroti. (2)
(All the peoples, clap your hands and make His hymn glorious. To the right of majesty is the throne of the eternal king. He first of all the heavens are the works of His hands. He dwells, they they perish and as a garment grow old. Praise Him with cymbals let every breath of joy praise the Lord. Praise Him with strings and you cheered His triumph. Behold, He comes clothed All the tribes of the earth glorify Him. The Spirit and the nymph say they come, come Lord, righteous judge. The Spirit and the nymph say come, yes, come Lord, Liberator. (2))
