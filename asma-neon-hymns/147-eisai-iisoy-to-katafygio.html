147 EISAI IISOY TO KATAFYGIO

Asma Neon - No. 147 | "ΕΙΣΑΙ ΙΗΣΟΥ ΤΟ ΚΑΤΑΦΥΓΙΟ"
Verse 1
Eisai Iisoy to katafygio mouvoitheia stis thlipseis mou,to froyrio mou ki o Eleftherotis,Pateras mou kai filos mazi.Eisai mazi mou kai ola ta echo,eirini, chara sti zoi.Me xedipsas me zontana neramazi Sou i psychi xanazei.Eisai Iisoy to katafygio mouvoitheia stis thlipseis mou,to froyrio mou ki o Eleftherotis,Pateras mou kai filos mazi.San perpato se koilades thanatoupote de fovamai giati,Se niotho konta me parigoreis,na me arnitheis den mporeis.
(You are Jesus my refuge help in my sorrows, my fortress etc Liberator, my Father and friend together. You are with me and all I have peace, joy in life. You quench my thirst with living waters, with you my soul lives again. You are Jesus my refuge help in my sorrows, the my fortress and the Liberator, my Father and friend together. As I walk in valleys of death I'm never afraid because, I feel you close, you comfort me, yes me you can't refuse.)
