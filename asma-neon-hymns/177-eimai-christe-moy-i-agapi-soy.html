177 EIMAI ChRISTE MOY I AGAPI SOY

Asma Neon - No. 177 | "ΕΙΜΑΙ ΧΡΙΣΤΕ ΜΟΥ Η ΑΓΑΠΗ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1
Eimai Christe mou i agapi Sou.Eimai to kerdos tis thysias Sou.Eimai kommati ap’ to soma Sou,eimai plymenos me to aima Sou. (2)Eisai o vrachos o aionios.O Vasilias mou o ouranios.To froyrio mou i katafygi,Sy ‘sai Christe mou Theie lytroti. (2)Choris Esena adeia i zoi.Adeio trapezi dichos trofi.Eisai to manna ap’ ton ourano,eisai o vrachos t’ athanato nero. (2)Mprosta Sou einai oli mou i zoiDos mou Christe mou Theia dynami.Ta dyo mou cheili otan tha ymnoyn, Se na doxazoun na Se eflogoyn. (2)
(I am, my Christ, Your love. I am the gain of Your sacrifice. I am part of Your body, I am washed in Your blood. (2) You are the rock o eternal. My King is heavenly. My fortress my refuge, You are Christ my God redeemer. (2) Life is empty without you. An empty table without you food. You are the manna from heaven, you are the rock the immortal water. (2) In front of You is my whole life, my Christ, my Divine power my two lips when they will praise You, glorify You and bless You. (2))
