116 AGIA FOTIA

Asma Neon - No. 116 | "ΑΓΙΑ ΦΩΤΙΑ"
Verse 1
Agia fotia panta as kaiei dynatimesa stis kardies o Christos na morfothei,tote tha psalloume psila sto throno Toudoxa sto sfagmeno amno tou Theoy.Agia fotia anapse Pneyma Theoy,na kapseis sti gi kathe plani tou echthroy,kai tote Esena Iisoy oi anthropoi tha dounkai T’ onoma Sou tha epikalestoyn.Agia fotia panta as kaiei dynatimesa stis kardies o Christos na morfothei,tote tha psalloume psila sto throno Toudoxa sto sfagmeno amno tou Theoy.Agia fotia anapse Pneyma Theoy,na kapseis sti gi kathe plani tou echthroykai tote Esena Iisoy oi anthropoi tha dounkai T’ onoma Sou tha epikalestoyn.Kai tote Esena Iisoy oi anthropoi tha dounkai T’ onoma Sou tha epikalestoyn.
(May the holy fire always burn strong in the hearts for Christ to be educated, then we will sing high on the throne Glory to the slain lamb of God.Holy fire kindle the Spirit of God, to burn to earth every error of the enemy, and then You Jesus, people will see and call on Your name. Holy fire always let Christ burn strong in the hearts to be educated, then he will we sing high on the throne Glory to the slain lamb of God. Holy fire kindle Spirit of God, to burn on earth every error of the enemy and then You Jesus people will see and call on Your name. And then You Jesus men shall see and call upon Thy name.)
