012 OPOS TO LOYLOYDI

Asma Neon - No. 012 | "ΟΠΩΣ ΤΟ ΛΟΥΛΟΥΔΙ"
Verse 1
Opos to louloydi, anthizei to proi,opos vgainei o ilios ap’ tin anatoli,opos feggoun t’ astra mesa sti nychtia,etsi o Christos mas xerei n’ agapa.(2)Tou Christoy i agapi einai i pio glykiamono Ekeinos xerei pos na agapa.(2)Opos ta karavia zoun stis thalasses,me proorismo tous chores makrines,zo kai perimeno na ‘rthei i stigmi,pou ki ego tha ftaso sti diki mou gi.(2)Echo ego pyxida ton Iisoy Christo,pou me odigei pros ton ourano.(2)Otan tha salpisei Kyrios o Theos,thelo na petaxo psila ston ourano,thelo na petaxo opos to pouli,pou to eleftherosan mesa ap’ to klouvi.(2)Kai tha Ton doxazo pia pantotina tou Christoy i agapi ein’ i pio glykia.(2)
(Like the flower, it blooms in the morning, like the sun rises from the east, like the stars shine in the night, so our Christ knows loves. (2) Christ's love is the sweetest He knows how to loves. (2) As ships live in the seas, destined for countries distant, I live and wait for the moment to come when I too will reach mine earth. (2) I have Jesus Christ as my compass, who leads me to him sky. (2) When the Lord God will blow the trumpet, I want to fly high to sky, I want to fly like the bird that was released from it cage. (2) And I will forever glorify Christ's love is the most sweety.(2))
