167 KYRIE EIS’ I ZOI

Asma Neon - No. 167 | "ΚΥΡΙΕ ΕΙΣ’ Η ΖΩΗ"
Verse 1
Kyrie Iisoy Christe, Ese i psychi mou ekzita.Mesa se gi xeri, eisai i moni mou drosia.S’ echo dei sto throno Sou, Thee mouti dynami, ti doxa Sou echo deiti allo na gemisei tin kardia mouKyrie, eis’ i zoi. (2)Kai Se doxazo, Se megalynota cheili mou gia Sena tha ymnoynta megaleia Sou Christe mou epainoyn.Zoi mou, fos mou, Gie tou Theoy.
(Lord Jesus Christ, my soul seeks You. In a dry land, You are my only one dew. I have seen Thee on Thy throne, O mighty God, I have seen Thy glory more to fill my heart Lord, you are the life. (2) And I glorify Thee, Thee my enlarged lips for You would praise Your majesty, my Christ praise. My life, my light, Son of God.)
