Verse 1
Kyrie otan Sou milo
(Lord when I speak to You)
chairomai, psallo kai aino
(I rejoice, sing and praise)
i kardia mou gemizei me chara
(my heart fills me joy)
tetoia agapi de vrika pouthena.
(I have never found such love anywhere.)
Epodos
(Refrain)
S' agapo Kyrie mou S' agapo
(I love you my Lord i love)
pote tha 'rtheis na me pareis
(will you ever come to get me)
va me pas ston ourano
(va you take me to heaven)
gia na zisoume aionia mazi
(to live forever together)
sti glykia tin parousia Sou Christe
(in the sweet presence of Your Christ)
pote tha 'rthei ekeini i ora pio poly
(that time will never come again a lot)
giati protos me agapises Esy
(because You loved me first)
panta tha s' efcharisto.
(I will always thank you.)
Dose mou agapi stin kardia
(Give me love in the heart)
dynami na Se akoloytho
(strength to follow You)
odigise ta vimata mou Esy
(guide my steps You)
kathe mera s' afti ti zoi.
(every day in this life.)
Mi m' afiseis pote na klonistho
(Never let me falter)
na 'sai mazi mou se kathe peirasmo
(be with me in every temptation)
stirize me sta podia mou Christe
(keep me on my feet Christ)
t' onoma Sou na omologo.
(I confess Your name.)
