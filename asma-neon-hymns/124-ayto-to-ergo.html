Asma Neon - No. 124 | "ΑΥΤΟ ΤΟ ΕΡΓΟ"
Verse 1
Afto to ergo pou echei archisei me sena o Theos.Afto to ergopou echei archisei me sena o Theos.Einai pistos va to teleiosei,pistos va to teleioseiTo ergo pou echei archisei me sena,tha teleiosei o Theos.Ki an o agonas pou echeis,afaipei tin elpida sou siga, siga.Ki an argei va rthei lysi,kai na ymniseis den mporeis pia.Tote na xereis pos Aftos se krata.Eisai asfalis sti diki Tou agkalia.Eisai i chara sto Theo, eisai gi' Afton thisafros.(2)
(This work that God has begun with you. This work has begun with you God. He is faithful and will finish it, faithful will he finish The work that it has begun with you, God will finish it. What if the struggle that you have, it is taking away your hope slowly, slowly. Even if it takes a while, there will be a solution, so be it you can no longer sing praises. Then know that He holds you. You are safe in His own embrace. You are the joy of God, you are a treasure for Him. (2))
