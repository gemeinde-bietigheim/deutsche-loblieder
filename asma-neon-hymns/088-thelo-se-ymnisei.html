088 ThELO SE YMNISEI

Asma Neon - No. 088 | "ΘΕΛΩ ΣΕ ΥΜΝΗΣΕΙ"
Verse 1
Pou na perpatiso makria apo Senapos na fygo ap’ ti matia Soukai an anevo psila ston ouranoekei Esena synanto.Kai an tis afgis foreso ta fteravathia sti thalassa an meinoki ekei tha m΄ odigisei to cheri to diko Sougiati ekeino me krata.Thelo se ymnisei me oli tin kardia moukai ti zoi mou prosfero me chara s’ Esena, s’ Esena Christe.Ti na fovitho ki an skotadi me skepaseii kardia mou ki an poneseitha lampei gia mena ti nychta tha einai meragiati Esy eisai to fos.Thelo Se ymnisei me oli tin kardia moukai ti zoi mou prosfero me charas’ Esena, s’ Esena Christe.
(Where can I walk away from You How can I get out of Your sight And if I climb high in the sky there I meet You. And if at dawn I put on the wings to the sea, if I am alone, that Suyati's hand will lead me there hold me. I want to praise you with all my heart and I offer my life with joy in You, in You Christ. What should I fear, even if it is dark with me cover my heart and even if it hurts it will shine for me at night it will be day because You are the light. I want to praise You with all my heart and I offer my life with joy to You, to You Christ.)
