098 FONI AGALLIASIS

Asma Neon - No. 098 | "ΦΩΝΗ ΑΓΑΛΛΙΑΣΗΣ"
Verse 1
Foni agalliasis kai sotiriaseinai stis skines ton dikaion. (2)To cheri tou Theoy einai dynato,to cheri Theoy echei ypsothei,to cheri tou Theoy kanei thaymata,tha ziso kai tha leo ta erga tou Kyriou.(2)Ymnoyn to Vasilia, doxazoun to lytroti,ymnoyn to Vasilia, doxazoun to lytroti.Salpigktes salpiste tora, kai tou echthroy ta teichi tha pesoun, giati o Kyrios einai mazi mas, o dynatos.Seiet’ i gi, kai tremei o ouranos,o stratos tou Kyriou diavainei empros. (2)
(A voice of rejoicing and salvation is in the tents of the righteous. (2) His hand God is strong, God's hand is lifted up, God's hand is doing wonders, I will live and tell the works of the Lord.(2) They praise the King, praise the redeemer, praise the King, praise the redeemer. Trumpets blow the trumpet now, and the walls of the enemy will fall, for the Lord is with us, the mighty one. The earth shakes, and the sky trembles, the army of the Lord passes forward. (2))
