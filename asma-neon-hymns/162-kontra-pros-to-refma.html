Verse 1
Kontra pros to reyma, kontra
(Against the stream, against)
pleoume me sena Iisoy
(we sail with you jesus)
tin antistasi apsifoyme tora
(we defy resistance now)
me ti chari Sou, Christe mas.
(by Your grace, our Christ.)
Epodos
(Refrain)
Ki an ypsonetai o echthros mas,
(And if the enemy rises us,)
gigantas, ki emeis mikroi
(giant, and we are small)
kontra tha antistathoyme
(against we will resist)
ton enikises Esy.
(you beat him You.)
Thyelles kai kataigides
(Storms and storms)
reymata kai potamoi
(streams and rivers)
den mporoyn pote na svisoun
(they can never turn off)
ti fotia Sou Lytroti, Christe mas.
(the fire of Your Redeemer, our Christ.)
Me ta matia mas strammena
(With our eyes fixed)
pros Esena o, Christe
(to Thee, O Christ)
trikymies tou aiona
(storms of the century)
tora de fovomaste, Christe mas.
(now we are not afraid, our Christ.)
