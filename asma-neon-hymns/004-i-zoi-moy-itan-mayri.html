004 I ZOI MOY ITAN MAYRI

Asma Neon - No. 004 | "Η ΖΩΗ ΜΟΥ ΗΤΑΝ ΜΑΥΡΗ"
Verse 1
I zoi mou itan mayri, voutigmeni mes sto dakryzoysa mes sto voyrko tis amartias ton frikto.Oi anthropoi mou deichnan perifronisi kai echthra,ospou mia mera ton Iisoy synantisa.Mou ‘pe: “Maria se sygchoro, ta lathi sou ta xechnogia sena th’ anevo sto stafro gia thanato tromero”.Edine parigoria, s’ arrostous ti giatreia,elpida se ftochoys kai fos se tyfloys.Ma mia mera Ton syllavane, san listi Ton dikasane,kai oloi fonazane na Ton stafrosoune.-Kyrie giati se misoyn, giati se chlefazoun;To kalo mas edidaxes, agapi skorpises.Eida na Ton sernoun to Golgotha n’ anevainoun,agkathia Tou foresan sto stafro Ton karfosan.To aima tou etreche, pigoyla schimatize,pliges Ton gemisane kai polloi Ton ftynane.Ma Ekeinos fonaxe dynata to “Tetelestai”. Patera sygchora tous sotiria dose tous”.
(My life was black, drenched in tears in the mire of sin the horrible. People showed me contempt and enmity, until one day him I met Jesus. He told me: "Mary I forgive you, I forget your mistakes I will go up to the cross for a terrible death". He gave comfort to the sick healing, hope to the poor and light to the blind. But one day they arrested Him, They tried Him like a robber, and everyone was shouting to crucify Him. - Lord why they hate you, why do they mock you? You taught us good, you spread love. I saw to drag Him up Golgotha, to climb up, they wore Him on the cross with thorns They nailed him. His blood was running, his chin was forming, wounds filled him and many spat on Him. But He shouted loudly "It is done". Father, I'm sorry give them salvation".)
