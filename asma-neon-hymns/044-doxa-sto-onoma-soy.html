044 DOXA STO ONOMA SOY

Asma Neon - No. 044 | "ΔΟΞΑ ΣΤΟ ΟΝΟΜΑ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1
Thee mou Se doxazo, mesa ap’ tin kardia mou S’ agapo.Sy eisai to pan gia me, ypsono t’ onoma Sou me chara.Doxa st’ onoma Sou, doxa st’ onoma SouSy eisai o vasilias, pou mas odigeis,ta panta Esy mporeis o Kyrios.Doxa st’ onoma Sou, doxa st’ onoma Sou.Sy eisai o ischyros, Sotiras mou,symvoulos mou thafmastos, o Kyrios.Kai doxazo Se, Iisoy Christe, poso S’ agapo.Theos eirinis, Emmanouil, Theos pantotinos, o Kyrios.
(God I praise You, from the bottom of my heart I love You. You are everything to me, I exalt Your name with joy. Glory to Your name, glory to Your name you are the king, who leads us, everything is possible for you Lord. Glory to Your name, glory to Your name. You are the mighty one, my Savior, my counselor wonderful, the Lord. And I praise You, Jesus Christ, how I love You. God of peace, Emmanuel, God forever, the Lord.)
