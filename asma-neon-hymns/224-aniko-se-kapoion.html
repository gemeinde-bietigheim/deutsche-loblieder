Verse 1
Aniko se kapoion kai paradothika s' Afton
(I belong to someone and surrendered to Him)
stafrothike o Agios gia mena ton amartolo.
(the Saint was crucified for me sinful.)
Prospathisa ston kosmo afton monachos na dikaiotho
(I tried in this world as a monk to justify myself)
ma i zoi pou ekana itan makria ap' to Christo.
(but the life I led he was far from Christ.)
Epodos
(Refrain)
Iisoy Christi mou Esy megali Vasilia
(Jesus, my Lord, You are great King)
tis amarties de mou tis logariazeis pia
(you don't impute my sins to her anymore)
den tis thymasai gia Sena einai parelthon
(you don't remember her about you it's past)
gi' afto Christi mou aionia tha Se ymno.
(therefore, my Servant, I will praise You forever.)
Sta chronia pou spatalisa sto diava toytis tis zois
(In the years I wasted in the course of this life)
i kathe mou paravasi eiche to stigma tis ntropis.
(my every transgression had the stigma of shame.)
Ma ola afta xechastikan giati aniko sto Christo
(Ma all these were forgotten because I belong to Christ)
kai den mporei na pei kaneis giati syychorises afton.
(and no one can say why you thanked him.)
