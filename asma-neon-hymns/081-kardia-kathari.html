081 KARDIA KAThARI

Asma Neon - No. 081 | "ΚΑΡΔΙΑ ΚΑΘΑΡΗ"
Verse 1
Kardia kathari, chtise mesa mou Theekai Pneyma efthes pali dos mou.(2)Mi me aporripseis apo tou prosopou Sou kai to Pneyma Sou min afaireseis Thee.Dos mou ti chara tis sotiria Sou kai pali,me Pneyma igemoniko stirixe me.Kardia statheri, chtise mesa mou Theekai exocho Pneyma dos mou. (2)Gia na ektelo ta diatagmata Sou mono kai pote mou na mi Se lypiso Thee.Me to aima Tou Christoy mou Sy katharise me,me Pneyma igemoniko stirixe me.Kardia tapeini, chtise mesa mou Theekai exocho Pneyma dos mou.(2)Gia na ektelo ta diatagmata Sou mono kai pote mou na mi Se lypiso Thee.Me to aima tou Christoy mou, Sy katharise me,me Pneyma igemoniko stirixe me.
(Heart pure, build in me God and Spirit straight again give me. (2) Do not me reject from Your face and do not take away Your Spirit, my God. Dos the joy of Your salvation again, with a sovereign Spirit support me. Heart firm, build in me God and excellent Spirit give me. (2) To perform the Your decrees only and never to pity You, God. With the blood of Christ my You cleanse me, with a sovereign Spirit sustain me. Humble heart, build in me, O God, give me an excellent Spirit. (2) That I may carry out Your decrees only that I may never pity You, God. With the blood of my Christ, Sy cleanse me, with a sovereign Spirit sustain me.)
