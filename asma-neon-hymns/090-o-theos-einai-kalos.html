090 O ThEOS EINAI KALOS

Asma Neon - No. 090 | "Ο ΘΕΟΣ ΕΙΝΑΙ ΚΑΛΟΣ"
Verse 1
O Theos einai kalos, o Theos ein’ agathoskai emeis doxologoyme Tou Theoy o laos.Otan skeftomai tin agapi Tou, pou mou edosethelo n’ alalaxo na to fonaxo kai na akoustei,o Theos mas einai kalos. (2)
(God is good, God is good and we praise God people. When I think of His love, which He gave me, I want to change it cry out and be heard, our God is good. (2))
