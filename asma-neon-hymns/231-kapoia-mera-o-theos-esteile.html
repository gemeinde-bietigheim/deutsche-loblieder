231 KAPOIA MERA O ThEOS ESTEILE

Asma Neon - No. 231 | "ΚΑΠΟΙΑ ΜΕΡΑ Ο ΘΕΟΣ ΕΣΤΕΙΛΕ"
Verse 1
Kapoia mera o Theos esteileTon Yio Tou na pethanei gia meO! ti megalo doro itan aftopio polytimo apo kathe thisafroPatera mou ouranie tin prosefchi mou stelnoela kai lytrose me Christe mou se prosmeno.Sygchorise me Kyrie pou amartisa se Senapos ezisa makria Sou choris tin parousia Sou.Mou charises ta dora t' ouranoymou 'doses aionia zoima pos mporesa na S' arnithotin plati mou na strepso sto Stafro.Patera mou ouranie tin prosefchi mou stelnoela kai lytrose me Christe mou se prosmeno.Sygchorise me Kyrie pou amartisa se Senapos ezisa makria Sou choris tin parousia Sou.Esy Kyrie me perimenestosa chronia na epistrepso se Seti zesti Sou akgalia anoixesti zoi mou pali gemises.S' efcharisto Patera sto throno Sou pigainoproseychomai se Sena ki anoigei i kardia mouo,ti Sou 'cho zitisei esy mou to 'cheis doseiaionia efgnomosyni tha echo mesa mou Christe.
(One day God sent His Son to die for me! what a great gift that was more precious than any treasure, my Father in heaven, grant my prayer send me and redeem me my Christ I am waiting for you. Forgive me Lord that I sinned against You. I lived away from You without Your presence. You gave me the gifts of heaven you gave me eternal life how could I refuse you turn my back to the Cross. My Father, grant my prayer send me and redeem me my Christ I am waiting for you. Forgive me Lord that I have sinned against You as I have lived away from You without Your presence. You Lord me I have been waiting for years to return to Seti. Your warm embrace has opened me my life you are full again. I thank you Father on Your throne I go and pray to You and my heart opens to what I have asked You You have given me eternal gratitude I will have in me Christ.)
