015 KYRIE AFINO TIN KARDIA MOY

Asma Neon - No. 015 | "ΚΥΡΙΕ ΑΦΗΝΩ ΤΗΝ ΚΑΡΔΙΑ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1
Kyrie afino tin kardia mou,sta cheria Sou kai me dakrya zito,plyne tin pligi mou me to aima pou chythike gia mena sto Stafro.Me to aima Sou t’ agio,plyne me sto aima Sou t’ agno.(2)Kyrie sou ferno ti zoi moukai to thelima Sou t’ agio zito,monacha sti foni Sou na ypakoyo,monacha gia ti doxa Sou na zo.Gia ti doxa Sou Kyrie,mono gia ti doxa Sou na zo.(2)
(Lord, I leave my heart in Your hands and with tears I ask, wash the wound with the blood shed for me on the Cross. With Your blood holy, wash me in your pure blood. (2) Lord, I bring my life to you and Your holy will I ask, only to Your voice to obey, only for the glory I live for You. For Your glory Lord, only for Your glory I live. (2))
