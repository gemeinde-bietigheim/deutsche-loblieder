Verse 1
I foni tou Kyriou th' akoustei s' oli ti gi,
(The voice of the Lord will be heard in all the earth,)
neo asma mes sti nychta tha ypsothei.
(new song into the night will rise up)
Sto oros tou Theoy, o laos tha mazeftei
(On the mountain of God, the people will gather)
kai i dynami Tou tha fanerothei.
(and His power will manifested.)
Epodos
(Refrain)
Einai o Agios Theos tis zois,
(He is the Holy God of life,)
o Agios Theos tis zois
(her Holy God of life)
kai i foni tou Kyriou th' akoustei,
(and the voice of the Lord will be heard,)
eine o Agios Theos tis zois.
(he was the Holy God of life.)
Ta matia ton tyflon tha anoixoun kai tha doun.
(The eyes of the blind will be opened and they will see.)
T' aftia ton koufon tha akoyn
(Deaf ears will hear)
kai oi choloi san ta elafia tha pidoyn
(and the lame as deer shall leap)
kai oloi tha doxologoyn.
(and all will praise.)
I endoxi omorfia tou Kyriou tha fanei
(Its glorious beauty Sir, it will be seen)
kai i erimos tha anthisei pantoy
(and the desert will bloom everywhere)
ki osoi fovoyntai ton Agio Theo,
(and those who fear the Holy One God,)
Afton monacha tha akolouthoyn.
(They will only follow them.)
