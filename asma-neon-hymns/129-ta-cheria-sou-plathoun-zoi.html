Verse 1
Ta cheria Sou plathoun zoi,
(Your hands shape life,)
agapi, eirini, chara.
(love, peace, joy.)
Ta cheria Sou plathoun zoi,
(Your hands are forming life,)
gemizoun tin adeia kardia.
(they fill the empty heart.)
Epodos
(Refrain)
Eflogia ta cheria Sou,
(Blessing the hands you,)
galini o Logos Sou,
(peace be Thy Word,)
agalliasi to vlemma Sou,
(delight Your gaze,)
eirini o Logos Sou.
(peace the Word Su.)
O Logos Sou einai sofos
(Your Word is wise)
sofizei to nou, tin psychi.
(enlightens the mind, the soul.)
O Logos Sou einai to fos
(Your Word it is the light)
fotizei tin odo tis zois.
(illuminates the path of life.)
To vlemma Sou agapi skorpa
(Your look spreads love)
chara i zesti Sou thoria.
(joy Your warm light.)
To vlemma Sou agapi skorpa
(Your look spreads love)
i Theia, i zesti Sou agkalia.
(the Divine, Your warm embrace.)
