218 AGIOS, AGIOS, AGIOS

Asma Neon - No. 218 | "ΑΓΙΟΣ, ΑΓΙΟΣ, ΑΓΙΟΣ"
Verse 1
Agios, Agios, Agios Eflogimenos.Kyrios tou pantos, einai o Yios o Timimenos.Eflogimeno as einai t’ onoma tou doxasmenou Iisoy.Axio kai Agio to Aima tou Arniou,pou efere anastasi, mesa stin kardia mas.Agios, Agios, Agios Eflogimenos.O Yios tou Theoy, o aparchis proorismenos.Eflogimeno as einai t’ onoma tou doxasmenou Iisoy.Axio kai Agio to Aima tou Arniou,pou efere anastasi mesa stin kardia mas.Se ymnoyme Kyrie, Se eflogoyme Kyrie,efcharistoyme Kyrie, Se doxazoume Patera,gia ti chari Sou kai mono. (2)Gia ton Yio Sou to Sotira Se doxazoume Patera.
(Holy, Holy, Holy Blessed. Lord of all, is the Son the Honored. Blessed be the name of the glorified Jesus. Worthy and Holy the Blood of the Lamb, which brought resurrection, in our hearts. Holy, Holy, Holy Blessed. The Son of God, the beginning destined. Blessed be the name of the glorified Jesus. Worthy and the Holy Blood of the Lamb, which brought resurrection in our hearts. Se we praise you Lord, we bless you Lord, we thank you Lord, we praise you Father, for Your sake alone. (2) For Your Son the Savior Se we praise the Father.)
