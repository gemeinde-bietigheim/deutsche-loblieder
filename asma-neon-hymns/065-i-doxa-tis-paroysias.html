065 I DOXA TIS PAROYSIAS

Asma Neon - No. 065 | "Η ΔΟΞΑ ΤΗΣ ΠΑΡΟΥΣΙΑΣ"
Verse 1
O i doxa tis parousias tou Kyriou ein’ agia!Fotise tis kardies ton piston Sou Christe,me ti chari tou prosopou Soukai ti doxa tis parousias Sou.Stin endoxi Sou parousia, dino tin timi kai ti latreia.Megaloprepeia kai doxa eisai ntymenos Kyrie,Despoti t’ ouranoy Sy Agie,Vasilia tis gis o endoxe!
(O the glory of the presence of the Lord is holy! Illumine the hearts of Your faithful Christ, with the grace of Your face and the glory of Your presence Your glorious presence, I give honor and worship. Majesty and glory you are clothed Lord, Master of heaven You Holy One, King of the earth oh glorious!)
