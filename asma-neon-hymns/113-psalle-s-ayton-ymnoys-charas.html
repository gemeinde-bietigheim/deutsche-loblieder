113 PsALLE S’ AYTON YMNOYS ChARAS

Asma Neon - No. 113 | "ΨΑΛΛΕ Σ’ ΑΥΤΟΝ ΥΜΝΟΥΣ ΧΑΡΑΣ"
Verse 1
Psalle s’ Afton ymnous charas,apo To Pneyma ki apo tin kardia.Doxase Afton, kai ypsose dose ti thesi pou anikei s’ ena Vasilia, Kyrio, Dimiourgo,dose to throno tis kardias. Giati o Kyrios echei ti dynamina eleftheronei kai na lynei ta desma.Aftos sou dinei niki, thriamvo, chara,ela mazi mas psalle dynata!. (2)Echo foresei imatio doxis.(2)kai to pneyma tou skotous. echei fygei ap’ eme.Echo foresei imatio doxis.Zei o Theos mou nai zei, zei kai vasileyei.Zei kai tha fanei. Nai zei, nai zei, nai zei.Doxa, doxa st’ Agio onoma Tou,Doxa, doxa stin agapi Tou.Doxa, doxa eis ti dynami Tou.Doxa sti megali chari Tou. (2)
(Sing to Him hymns of joy, from the Spirit and from the heart. Praise Him, and raise up give the place that belongs to a King, Lord, Creator, give it throne of the heart. Because the Lord has the power to free and solve the bonds. He gives you victory, triumph, joy, come with us sing loudly!. (2)I have put on a robe of glory.(2)and the spirit of darkness. is gone from me. I have put on a robe of glory. My God lives yes lives, lives and He reigns. He lives and will be seen. Yes he lives, yes he lives, yes he lives. Glory, glory to His holy name, Glory, glory to His love. Glory, glory to the power His. Glory to His great grace. (2))
