Verse 1
Thraystikes san alavastro
(You broke like alabaster)
stou Golgotha ton akmona
(on Calvary the anvil)
myro zois varytimo agapis ki omorfias.
(myrrh of life precious of love and beauty.)
Ki olous mas, mas afypnises
(And you woke us all up)
se mian afgi anoixiatiki,
(on a spring dawn,)
kathos mas eprosferthikes ouranias efodias.
(as well as us you were offered heavenly fragrance.)

sti skoteini nychtia.
