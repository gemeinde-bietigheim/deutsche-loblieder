Verse 1
O Kyrios mas einai Pantodynamos
(Our Lord is Almighty)
einai o Theos o nikitis.
(it is God who is victorious.)
To lao odigei mesa sti machi
(The people lead in battle)
trepei tous echthroys tou se fygi.
(he puts his enemies to flight.)
Epodos
(Refrain)
Fonaxte me dynami ki ischy,
(Shout with power and power)
ypsoste foni.
(raise your voice)
Statheite mprosta sto Vasilia,
(Stand before the King,)
giati kerdise ton polemo gia mas.
(because he beat him war for us.)
O Kyrios mas einai Pantodynamos
(Our Lord is Almighty)
einai o dikos mas archigos.
(it's ours leader.)
Doxazoume, latreyoume kai psalloume
(We praise, worship and sing)
ston Kyrio pou nikise gia mas.
(to the Lord who won for us.)
