207 ZITAS ChARA

Asma Neon - No. 207 | "ΖΗΤΑΣ ΧΑΡΑ"
Verse 1
Zitas chara sta vouna ta psila,se galano pelago ki ourano,omos cheimonia kai varia katachnia,sou kryvei afti ti chara.Zitas chara se chrysi syntrofiame filikes anoichtes syntrofies,omos oi stygnes tis alitheias stigmesse vriskoun sti monaxia.Zitas chara mes tin kathe kardiana deis lampri kathenos ti matia.Omos stin pikri, ti skliri ti zoifoliazei i pagonia.Tragoudistis tis kainoyrias geniastha po pantoy enan ymno charas,tou Christoy i zoi kai diki sou an genei,tha echeis tote chara.Tha vreis chara stou Christoy ti matiastin anoichti matomeni agkalia,opou ki an vretheis o Christos Lytrotis,tha se gemizei chara.

You ask for joy in gold we keep friendly open company, but the sobs of truth moments find you in loneliness. You ask for joy with every heart to see everyone's look is brilliant. But in the bitter, hard life she nestles frost. Singer of the new generation, I will sing a hymn everywhere joy, Christ's life and yours if born, you will then have joy find joy in Christ looking at the open bloody embrace, wherever if you find Christ the Redeemer, you will be filled with joy.)
