166 GLYKE IISOY MOY

Asma Neon - No. 166 | "ΓΛΥΚΕ ΙΗΣΟΥ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1
Kathos koitazo psila, psila ston ourano.Esena agnanteyo, to vlemma Sou niothoto cheri Sou aggizo, mazi Sou milo.Glyke Iisoy mou, mes tin agkalia Sou thelo na kourniaso, na xekourasto.Esena zitao, s’ Esena elpizo,skoupizo ta dakrya kai s’ akoloutho.Glyke Iisoy mou.To vlemma mou strefo pros Seelpida mou vrache pisteTo froyrio mou ki i katafygi mou i petra mou eisai kai stirizomai. Glyke Iisoy mou alitheia kai fos mouchara odigos mou, i odos mou eisai Esy otan fovamai se Sena trecho ki eirini mou dineis vathia stin psychi. Glyke Iisoy mou, mes tin agkalia Sou thelo na kourniaso na xekourasto.Esena zitao, se Sena elpizo,skoupizo ta dakrya kai s’ akoloutho.Glyke Iisoy mou.
(As I look up, high in the sky. You I gaze, Your gaze I touch Your palpable hand, I talk with You. My sweet Jesus, in my embrace I want to perch on you, to rest. I ask you, you I hope, I wipe the tears and follow you. My sweet Jesus. The look I turn to Sheelpida, my faithful fortress, my fortress and my refuge you are my rock and my support. My sweet Jesus truth and light my dear guide, you are my road, when I am afraid, I run to you You give me deep peace in my soul. My sweet Jesus, in Your arms I want to perch and rest. I ask you, I hope in you, I wipe the tears and I follow you. My sweet Jesus.)
