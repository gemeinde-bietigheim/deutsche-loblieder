Verse 1
Ekei sto Golgotha vlepo to sotira
(There on Calvary I see the savior)
ti thesi ti diki mou na pairnei
(take my place)
sto stafro vlepo tis pliges Tou
(on the cross I see His wounds)
to aima Tou na trechei
(His blood to flow)
na pethainei ekeinos, gia na ziso ego.
(let him die, that I may live me.)
Epodos
(Refrain)
Toso o Theos ton kosmo ton agapise
(God so loved the world)
oste edose ton gio Tou ton monogeni
(so that he gave the son His only son)
gia na mi chathei kathenas
(so that no one perishes)
pou s' Afton pisteyei
(who believes in Him)
alla na 'chei aionia zoi.
(but so be it eternal life.)
Thysiase o Theos ton gio Tou gia emena
(God sacrificed His son for me)
ti thesi ti diki mou pire
(the position mine took)
sto stafro plirose gia mena
(on the cross he paid for me)
gia kathe mou amartia plirose
(for every sin of mine pay)
Ekeinos to chreos pou eicha ego.
(He the debt I had.)
