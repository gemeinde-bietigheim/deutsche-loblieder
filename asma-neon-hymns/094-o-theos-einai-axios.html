Verse 1
O Theos einai axios, kai agios Theos
(God is worthy, and a holy God)
kai i poli, i agia poli Tou Theoy
(and the city, the holy city of God)
gia mas einai i chara olis tis gis.
(for us it is the joy of the whole earth.)
O Theos dinei niki ston diko Tou to lao
(God gives victory to His people)
mes sti machi polemaei ton echthro,
(into battle fights the enemy)
gia mas o Kyrios einai nikitis.
(for us the Lord is victorious.)
Epodos
(Refrain)
Gi afto to onoma Sou ypsonoume
(Y this Thy name we exalt)
kai Se efcharistoyme gia ta osa echeis kanei.
(and thank You for what You have done.)
Gi afto o Kyrie! Se latreyoume
(That's why oh lord! We adore you)
giati Esy eisai o Theos mas,
(for You are our God,)
Kyrios tis gis kai tou ouranoy.
(Lord of the earth and of sky.)
