211 GIA SENA ThA ZISO

Asma Neon - No. 211 | "ΓΙΑ ΣΕΝΑ ΘΑ ΖΗΣΩ"
Verse 1
Gia Sena tha ziso glyke mou Sotira,tha S’ akolouthiso sto kathe mou vima.Sou dino ta panta, pare tin kardia mou,mono Esy na kyvernas.Esy kapoia mera epires ton dromoki anevikes pano ston Golgotha,ekei Se stafrosan, karfia Sou karfosan,gia na vro ego lefteria.Dose mou agapi, glyke mou Sotira,na niotho eirini, vathia stin kardia mou,se Sena tha psallo ton kathe skopo moukai tha ‘rtho mia mera na Se do. (2)
(I will live for You, my sweet Savior, I will follow You in my every step. I give You everything, take my heart, only You rule. You someday won You went up the road to Golgotha, there they crucified You, Your nails nailed, that I may find freedom. Give me love, my sweet Savior, to I feel peace, deep in my heart, to You I will sing my every purpose and I will come one day to see you. (2))
