191 ME DEOS STEKOMAI MPROSTA SOY

Asma Neon - No. 191 | "ΜΕ ΔΕΟΣ ΣΤΕΚΟΜΑΙ ΜΠΡΟΣΤΑ ΣΟΥ"
Verse 1
Erchomai sto throno Sou Thee,sta agia ton agion Kyrie,ekei pou to ego mou chanetai,ekei pou oi skepseis oles svinoune.Aggeloi pantoy trigyro Sou,gonatizoun sti megalosyni Sou.Se latreyoun kai Se proskynoyn, kai me eflaveia ti doxa Sou ymnoyn.(2)Me deos stekomai mprosta Soukai liono mesa sti matia Sou.Sta podia Sou mprosta lygizei i kardia,kai chanomai sti fotia tis parousias Sou,tis agiosynis Sou.(2)Me deos stekomai mprosta Sou.
(I come to Your throne, God, in the holy of holies, Lord, where my ego disappears, where thoughts all fade away. Angels all around To You, they kneel before Your majesty. They adore You and worship You, and me reverence Thy glory they praise.(2)In awe I stand before Thee and melt in Your gaze. At Your feet in front the heart bends, and I get lost in fire of Your presence, Your holiness. (2) In awe I stand before Su.)
