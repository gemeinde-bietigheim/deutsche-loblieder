009 TA KALYTERA LOGIA MOY

Asma Neon - No. 009 | "ΤΑ ΚΑΛΥΤΕΡΑ ΛΟΓΙΑ ΜΟΥ"
Verse 1
Ta kalytera logia mou, den ta echo grammenaden yparchoune symvola, den yparchoun fones.Me mia glossa agnoristi i kardia mou se Senapsithyrizei o Kyrie!, myries, myries fores.(2)Einai ores pou stekomai kai koitazo t’ asteria,ti galinia ti thalassa, to vathy ourano.Kai aplono se Sena nai, ta ftocha mou ta cheriame ta matia dakryvrechta me to stoma kleisto.(2)Ki einai ores pou vlepontas to diko Sou to cherina skoupizei ta dakrya n’ akoumpa stin pligi.Niotho mesa mou gyro mou tis pnois Sou t’ agerikai ta logia t’ aneipota, ta akoys stin siopi.(2)
(My best words, I have not written them, there are no symbols, they do not exist voices. With an unknown language my heart whispers to You, O Lord! thousands, thousands of times. (2) There are hours when I stand and look at stars, the calm sea, the deep sky. And I reach out to You yes, the my poor hands with tearful eyes and closed mouth. (2) Ms there are hours when seeing Your hand wipes away the tears to touch in the wound. I feel in me around me the air of Your breath and the words of unspoken, you hear them in the silence.(2))
