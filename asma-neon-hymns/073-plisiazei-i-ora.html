073 PLISIAZEI I ORA

Asma Neon - No. 073 | "ΠΛΗΣΙΑΖΕΙ Η ΩΡΑ"
Verse 1
Plisiazei i ora ftanei i stigmi,makria na fygo ap’ afti ti gi,tis oras ta lepta pernane viastika,ki o Christos tha ‘rthei toso xafnika.Echo etoimastei gia ourania giorti,gia deipno giortino doxa sto Theo.Ekeinos tha me parei ston ourano,tha ziso ekei gia panta tha Ton ymno.Ela kai sy tora na fygoume mazi,etoimasou tora einai i stigmi,san kleftis sti nychtia tha ‘rthei xafnika,tote th’ arpachthei opoios ton dechthei.Echo etoimastei gia ourania giorti,deipno giortino gia mas ston ourano,me ton Iisoy mas pantote ekei,aionia tha zisoume oi pistoi.
(The time is coming, the moment is coming, far from leaving this earth, it's time minutes pass quickly, and Christ will come so suddenly. I am ready for a heavenly feast, for a festive dinner, glory to God. He will take me to heaven, I will live there forever I will praise Him. Come and you now let's go together, get ready now is the time, like a thief in the night he will come suddenly, then whoever receives him will be raptured. I have prepared for heaven celebration, holiday dinner for us in heaven, with our Jesus always there, the faithful will live forever.)
