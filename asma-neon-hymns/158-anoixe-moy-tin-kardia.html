158 ANOIXE MOY TIN KARDIA

Asma Neon - No. 158 | "ΑΝΟΙΞΕ ΜΟΥ ΤΗΝ ΚΑΡΔΙΑ"
Verse 1
Anoixe mou tin kardia mouanoixe mou ta fterana petaxo sta ouraniasti glykia Sou agkalia. (2)Ki otan ftaso ekei pano, kai mazi Sou efrethotha foreso to stefani‘kei psila ston ourano. (2)Kai th’ agalletai i kardia mouprosopo Sou san thorokai me olous tous agiousSe aionia tha ymno. (2)
(Open my heart, open my wings, let me fly in the sky Your sweet hug. (2) And when I get up there, and with You I would find myself wearing the crown high in the sky. (2) And he will rejoice my heart in Your face as a shield and with all the saints In eternity will hymn. (2))
