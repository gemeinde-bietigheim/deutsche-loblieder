Asma Neon - No. 150 | "Σ’ ΕΥΧΑΡΙΣΤΟΥΜΕ ΟΛΟΙ"
Verse 1
S’ efcharistoyme oloi, mesa ap΄ ti kardia masesteiles ton Yio Sou gia mas na stafrothei.Kai tora lytromenoi, Ese doxologoymeanoigoun ta ourania apo tin prosefchi.S’ efcharistoyme Kyrie, gia kathe eflogias’ efcharistoyme oloi, megaloi kai mikroi.Dose mas tin eirini, kai ayxise tin pistina meinoume gia panta, sto Logo Sou pistoi.S’ efcharistoyme oloi, mesa apo tin kardia masesteiles ton Yio Sou gia mas na stafrothei. (3)
(We all thank you, from the bottom of our hearts you sent your Son for us crucified. And now redeemed, we glorify You, the heavens open from her prayer. Thank you Lord, for every blessing we all thank you, great and small. Give us peace, and increase faith to stay for always faithful to Your Word. We all thank You from the bottom of our hearts you sent us your Son to be crucified for us. (3))
