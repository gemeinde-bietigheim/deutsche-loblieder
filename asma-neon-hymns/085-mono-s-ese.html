085 MONO S’ ESE

Asma Neon - No. 085 | "ΜΟΝΟ Σ’ ΕΣΕ"
Verse 1
Psallo doxa sto onoma Sou o Thee!Psallo kai ymno Ese,giati t’ onoma Sou einai thafmasto!Dino doxa sto onoma Sou o Thee!Psallo kai ymno Ese,giati t’ onoma Sou einai thafmasto!S’ Esena anikei Theepou kathesai sto throno,Aionie Amne oli i doxa, kai i timi ,i dynami kai i ischy mono S’ Ese anikei aionia. (3)Amin.Ki emeis o laos tou Theoy, me mia kardia oloi mazitha leme pantoy, pos, oli i doxa kai i timi ,i dynami kai i ischy mono S’ Ese anikei aionia. (3)Amin.
(I sing glory to Your name O God! I sing and praise You, for Your name is wonderful! I give glory to Your name O God! I sing and praise You, for the name It is wonderful to you! It belongs to you, God, you sit on the throne, Eternal Amne all glory, and honor, strength and power belong to You alone forever. (3) Amen. And we the people of God, with one heart we will all say together everywhere, how, all glory and honor, strength and power belong to You alone forever. (3) Amen.)
