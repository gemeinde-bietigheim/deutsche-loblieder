025 EISAI O KALOS POIMENAS

Asma Neon - No. 025 | "ΕΙΣΑΙ Ο ΚΑΛΟΣ ΠΟΙΜΕΝΑΣ"
Verse 1
Eis’ i elpida, chara, iliachtida,mou dineis ti dynami mes stin psychi.Eisai to fos, eisai filos mou pantotinos.Mou dineis tharros, mou dineis kouragiomprosta na vadizo psila na koito.Kai i zoi mou echei noima, echei skopo. Eisai o kalos poimenas, o Sotiras mou Christos,Ti foni Sou anagnorizo kai prochoro.Mes sto dromo Sou vadizo kai Esena akoloutho,thelo na eimai konta Sou na mi chatho, na mi chatho.
(You are the hope, joy, sunshine, you give me strength in my soul. You are it light, you are my friend forever. You give me courage, you give me bravely to walk high to look. And my life has meaning, it does purpose. You are the good shepherd, my Savior Christ, Your voice I recognize and move forward. I walk in Your way and I follow You, I want to I'm close to You so I don't get lost, don't get lost.)
