047 S’ EYChARISTO ThEE

Asma Neon - No. 047 | "Σ’ ΕΥΧΑΡΙΣΤΩ ΘΕΕ"
Verse 1
S’ efcharisto Thee, giati me agapiseskai ton Iisoy thysia prosferes.S’ efcharisto Thee, giati Esy me lytroseski olo mou to einai mou to allaxes.Gi afto tora oi adynamoi as poun, oti einai dynatoigiati o Christos thysiastike gia mas.Gi afto ki oi ftochoi mporoyn na poun,pos echoun ployto t’ ouranoy,giati o Theos zei tora stin kardia. (2)
(I thank you, God, because you loved me and offered Jesus as a sacrifice. I thank you God, why did You redeem me and change my whole being. That's why now let the weak say, that they are strong because Christ was sacrificed for This is why even the poor can say that they have wealth of heaven, for God now lives in the heart. (2))
