018 I want to believe in you

German Hymns - No. 018 (English Translation)
Verse 1:
Ich will an Dich glauben, wie Abraham es tat,
(I want to believe in you like Abraham did,)
was kann dem geschehen, der solchen Glauben hat?
(what can happen to someone who has such faith?)
Seinen Sohn führt' er zum Brandaltar,
(He led his son to the burning altar,)
zu opfern ihn, wie's ihm von Gott befohlen war.
(to sacrifice him, as God commanded him.)
Ich will an Dich glauben, wie Abraham es tat.
(I want to believe in you like Abraham did.)
Verse 2:
Ich will an Dich glauben, wie Daniel es tat,
(I want to believe in you like Daniel did,)
was kann dem geschehen, der solchen Glauben hat.
(what can happen to someone who has such faith?)
Sie warfen ihn den Löwen hin, er betete zu Gott,
(They threw him to the lions, he prayed to God,)
und der beschützte ihn.
(and he protected him.)
Ich will an Dich glauben, wie Daniel es tat.
(I want to believe in you like Daniel did.)
Verse 3:
Ich will an Dich glauben, wie Simeon es tat,
(I want to believe in you like Simeon did,)
was kann dem geschehen, der solchen Glauben hat.
(what can happen to someone who has such faith?)
Geduldig blieb er Jahr für Jahr, bis Christus er geseh'n,
(He remained patient year after year until he saw Christ,)
wie's ihm verheißen war.
(as he was promised.)
Ich will an Dich glauben, wie Simeon es tat.
(I want to believe in you like Simeon did.)
Verse 4:
Ich will an Dich glauben, wie Stephanus es tat,
(I want to believe in you like Stephen did,)
was kann dem geschehen, der solchen Glauben hat.
(what can happen to someone who has such faith?)
Sie steinigten zu Tode ihn, er betete für sie,
(They stoned him to death, he prayed for them,)
und Gott erhörte ihn.
(and God heard him.)
Ich will an Dich glauben, wie Stephanus es tat.
(I want to believe in you like Stephen did.)
